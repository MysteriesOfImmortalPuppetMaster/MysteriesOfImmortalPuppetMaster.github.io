Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 929: Drinking and Getting Drunk

Ning Zhuo took out a cold jade sealed bottle from his Storage Belt. What was held inside the bottle was none other than Ice Jade Wine.

"Try this first." Ning Zhuo poured a small cup of Ice Jade Wine for Tu Yuanzi. "This wine is called Ice Jade Wine. It is cool and crisp on the palate, but within this chill, there is spiritual energy that won't harm your foundation. You haven't been in your transformed state for long, so drink a little at first and savor it slowly."

Tu Yuanzi stared at the wine.

His gaze revealed curiosity. This curiosity was very clean and pure, free of greed or calculation. He was just like a child seeing the scenery outside the mountains for the first time, genuinely finding an unfamiliar object fascinating.

Then, holding the cup with both hands, Tu Yuanzi mimicked Ning Zhuo's actions and sniffed it very seriously.

"It's like snow," he said, stating his first impression.

Ning Zhuo laughed heartily and raised his hand. "Take a sip and see if it tastes even more like it."

Tu Yuanzi stuck out his tongue and licked it. Having just transformed not long ago, and dropping his guard in front of Ning Zhuo, he revealed this tiny flaw.

His brows immediately furrowed, then subsequently smoothed out.

He didn't know how to describe it. He only felt a thread of coolness drop from the tip of his tongue. After entering his throat, it wasn't freezing; instead, it felt as if it gently washed away the chaotic, mundane impurities within his body, followed by a spreading warmth.

Tu Yuanzi's eyes immediately lit up. "It's delicious."

"Then drink some more." Ning Zhuo poured him another cup.

He also raised his cup and drank. As the Ice Jade Wine went down his throat, his divine sea became slightly clearer, and fine ripples formed in his qi sea.

Tu Yuanzi drank faster and faster, gradually becoming immersed in the sensation. Ning Zhuo simply took out a wine jar and let him pour it himself.

"Speaking of which, it's been about a year since I left Fire Persimmon Immortal City," Ning Zhuo couldn't help but sigh inwardly.

This past year, to him, was like a wonderfully diverse storybook, with every page filled with vibrant colors.

His original intention for traveling the world was to resurrect his mother.

This goal was divided into several stages, and Ning Zhuo had already achieved several phased results one after another.

Besides this, he had also become stronger. Although this strength wasn't temporarily reflected in his cultivation base, whether it was his Cultivation techniques, his various realms, or his combat strength, all had seen a qualitative improvement.

When they left Fire Persimmon Immortal City, neither Ning Zhuo nor Sun Lingtong could have imagined that in just about a year's time, they would grow to such an extent. Through combat strength alone, they could match Golden Core cultivators. If they controlled Military Might, they could even fight Nascent Soul cultivators. They had even once defeated a Soul Formation-level Underworld Prefecture Monarch!

Of course, such a battle record was difficult to replicate, as it had relied on the layouts of various powerful experts and almighty figures. But it truly dispelled much of the mystique for Ning Zhuo, greatly boosting his spirit. It broadened his mind, changing him from someone who engaged in petty theft to someone open, majestic, and exuding the aura of a young hero.

It was precisely with this mindset that Ning Zhuo advanced triumphantly after entering the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate. First, with the secret help of the Confucian cultivator faction, he made a name for himself. Then, he defeated strong enemies like Ban Ji and Gu Qing, establishing a firm foothold, before gaining the attention of figures like Zhong Dao, Tuoba Huang, and Wang Yu.

Up until now, with Meng Yaoyin's help, he had thrillingly survived a life-and-death crisis. Using the Naming Fire Furnace to break the deadlock and guiding events to his advantage, he had successfully established an alliance and set up a stronghold.

Now, standing behind him were five Nascent Soul cultivators, even more Golden Core experts, and Foundation Establishment-level genius cultivators!

Ning Zhuo lowered his eyes, looking at the ice-blue liquid in his cup, but his thoughts gradually deepened.

Naming Stronghold seemed bustling today, but in reality, it was only in its nascent form.

The five Nascent Soul cultivators all had their own backgrounds and calculations. Chunyangzi, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, the Red-Robed Guest, and the others were not people who would remain subordinate for long. Although Tu Yuanzi made Ning Zhuo feel close to him, his true loyalty lay with Senior Cangyazi's wishes. Tan Zhu also had his own stance; as a fierce figure of the Demonic Path, his true intentions were unknown.

If the Nascent Soul cultivators were like this, so were the Golden Core cultivators.

Some came because of the Naming Fire Furnace, some because of debt claims, some saw the future potential in Ning Zhuo, and some were merely placing a temporary bet.

Their willingness to sit together today was, in some cases, due to being dispatched by the forces behind them, extending their tentacles into the matter.

For others, it was because Ning Zhuo used debts to assign seats, used rules to restrain people, and used Liu Jinke's defeat to establish his prestige.

But if, in the future, Naming Stronghold distributed benefits unevenly, faced setbacks in charging the peak, or encountered difficulties repairing the furnace, the thoughts of these people would surge like Cloud Layers, inevitably leading to individual changes in allegiance.

The Foundation Establishment geniuses were also very complex.

Situ Xing, Shen Xi, and Lin Jinglong all had super families behind them. Only Mu Yuehua had a clear and clean background, but she was tied to Danxia Peak.

He needed to borrow the momentum of the masses to form the foundation of Naming Stronghold; at the same time, he had to guard against their momentum suppressing him in return.

The balance in this was very difficult to grasp.

Ning Zhuo drank while he pondered.

The cool, crisp alcohol entered his belly, causing his exhausted mind to slowly unwind.

Opposite him, Tu Yuanzi was hugging the wine jar, carefully pouring wine into his cup. His movements were clumsy, his expression earnest, and his mouth slightly open, filled with longing.

Every time, he poured a small cup, then raised the little wine cup, tilted his neck back, and drank it clean. Then, he carefully went back to pouring, afraid of spilling even a single drop.

Ning Zhuo had given him the wine jar with the original intention of letting him drink directly from it. Watching Tu Yuanzi's clumsy and innocent appearance, Ning Zhuo's mood lightened significantly.

"No matter what, the ceremony of establishing the alliance and the stronghold alone has yielded massive gains."

One congratulatory gift after another resurfaced in his mind.

The Nine-Linked Ring Sound Lock sent by Cao Gui looked like a child's toy, but in truth, the nine rings were interlocked, using a musical score as the key to conceal secrets. This item possessed considerable ingenuity in the path of Mechanisms; it was not only usable but also worth studying.

The official paper and bamboo brushes presented by Bai Jiyun and Liu Fushu, as well as the poem Bai Jiyun had composed on the spot, were not just literary treasures, but they also held narrative value. And a good story would inevitably boost Ning Zhuo's reputation even further.

The small Mechanisms gifted by the four - Patch Sun, Little Braid Jiang, Bold Zhang, and Fire Twister Ah Huo - revealed their friendly attitude toward Ning Zhuo.

Yu Heye, knowing what was appropriate, sent a Golden Core-level heavy gift, letting Ning Zhuo understand that he also possessed considerable political acumen.

Gu Huaijiu's Nostalgic Jade was even rarer. By gifting it, the sheer weight of Gu Huaijiu's gesture almost went without saying.

Che Zhuzi sending the Yellow Crystal Spider Nest surprised Ning Zhuo quite a bit. It also confirmed the former's immense sincerity in deepening their cooperation.

Situ Xing gifting a sword embryo, Shen Xi sending the Shen Family's Formation insights, and Lin Jinglong offering the seeds of the Thin-Bark Cloud-Swallowing Birch were all publicly expressed gestures of goodwill.

The Compatibility Token gifted by Lu Zeng could act as an intermediary between Mechanism components; its value was absolutely enormous.

The "Phoenix Singing at the Morning Sun" zither score given by Dong Nishang had already been glanced over by Ning Zhuo. He confirmed it could provide an amplification to a swarm of Mechanism birds, making it unexpectedly suitable for him.

The five portions of Golden Core-level precious materials sent by the people from the Five Elements Peak weren't extremely precious, but they were large in quantity and required painstaking effort to refine. This point alone showed great thoughtfulness.

The profound weapon armor blade, sword, axe, and hook sent by the Armory Cave were a full four pieces of Golden Core-level armaments.

The most valuable might have been the Heart Snake Horse-Tripping Bell brought by Han Muma. This item was of great significance, not limited to the treasure itself, but also for its hidden ties with the Myriad Beasts Peak.

Ning Zhuo thought of Liu Jinke. "This person also brought a 'congratulatory gift'."

"My battle with him proved my worth. The prestige I gained was just right, helping me better face the various cultivators who joined the alliance."

Before this battle, Ning Zhuo's reputation had been somewhat hollow. Only after this battle was he truly taken seriously by others and treated as an equal by many Golden Core-level cultivators.

Ning Zhuo's mind was crystal clear. "In truth... in the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion, what I received were far more than just congratulatory gifts, weren't they?"

It was a web.

A massive web woven from favors, interests, reputation, probes, and debts.

And he stood right at the center of the web.

He could use this web to weave more solid connections and leverage more resources. But at the same time, if he couldn't control it well and overplayed his hand, he would end up dropping a rock on his own foot and being bound by this invisible, massive web.

The Calamity Luck Meng Yaoyin had spoken of surfaced in Ning Zhuo's mind.

He gained a deeper understanding of this.

"Calamity Luck is the combination of disaster and blessing."

"As long as one can endure and digest the calamity, what remains is the blessing!"

"The things I've gained from this, even the combined harvest of several Rising Cloud Minor Trials wouldn't be able to match."

Of course, the minor trial involving the Naming Fire Furnace couldn't be counted here. Such a Cloud-Stirring Minor Trial was exceedingly rare; one might only occur once in over a dozen sessions of the Flying Cloud Assembly.

Tu Yuanzi hugged the wine jar, his cheeks slightly flushed. His eyes were brighter than before, but also more unfocused.

He was already somewhat drunk.

Tu Yuanzi hadn't been transformed for long, and he was tasting many of the mortal realm's flavors for the first time. At this moment, as the alcohol entered his body and his spiritual energy circulated, his thoughts became like clouds blown open by the wind, and he gradually started talking more.

He voluntarily talked about his life by Cangyazi's side, speaking of the deep bonds between him and the other spirit pets.

As he spoke, he laughed out loud.

This laughter held not a hint of concealment; it was clear and bright, like a string of pebbles suddenly tumbling down a mountain stream.

The lingering calculations swirling in Ning Zhuo's mind were gently knocked aside by this string of laughter.

Naming Stronghold.

Flowing Cloud Peak.

Tie Kuang.

Resting Flame Cloud Nest.

Five Nascent Souls, numerous Golden Cores, many creditors...

But on Tu Yuanzi's side, it was trivial matters like who stole whose sleeping spot, or who secretly hid which spirit fruit in a rock crevice.

The mortal world was not just about scheming and playing games.

There was also this kind of companionship.

There was no need for sharp verbal sparring, no need for guarded probing. Just sitting together, drinking a cup of wine, and talking about small things completely unrelated to the grand scheme was enough to bring peace to one's heart.

Ning Zhuo picked up his cup and slowly drank it all.

This time, he didn't subconsciously ponder the situation; he just drank.

After finishing two jars of Ice Jade Wine, Ning Zhuo thought for a moment and took out another kind of spirit wine from his Storage Belt.

As soon as the wine jar was opened, a different fragrance wafted into the air.

It was no longer the clear, cold, and crisp scent of the Ice Jade Wine, but rather a profound, serene fragrance carrying a cold brilliance like moonlight.

"This wine is different," Ning Zhuo said. "It's called Cold Bone Moon Dew Wine*. It nourishes the Soul and benefits the spirit body. You can only drink a little."

Ning Zhuo poured him a cup.

The silver-gray liquid rippled slightly at the bottom of the cup, its luster like moonlight draped over a frigid pool.

As the Cold Bone Moon Dew Wine entered Tu Yuanzi's body, the alcohol didn't swiftly travel through his meridians like the Ice Jade Wine had. Instead, it slowly sank deep into his Soul.

The aura around Tu Yuanzi gradually grew tranquil. The thoughts that had been drifting due to intoxication felt as if they had been washed by the moon dew, becoming clear, moist, and stable.

Tu Yuanzi exhaled a breath of turbid qi and couldn't help but rub his chest. "So comfortable."

Ning Zhuo chuckled lightly. "Your Physical Body is strong, but your Soul is relatively weak. This wine is beneficial to you, but you must proceed gradually and strictly guard against overindulgence."

Tu Yuanzi drank two more cups, and his entire upper body began to sway slightly, completely buzzed.

He let out a hiccup and looked at Ning Zhuo with bleary, drunk eyes. "Young Master Ning Zhuo, you are a good person."

Ning Zhuo laughed out loud.

If someone else had said this, he would only ponder how much was true, how much was false, and what schemes lay beneath. But coming from Tu Yuanzi, it was like a cup of pure water placed in front of him - so clear that there was nothing to guess.

Ning Zhuo suddenly thought of Linghu Jiu.

That guy loved wine!

When drinking with Linghu Jiu, the table was filled with laughter, witty remarks, and genuine, heartfelt joy.

As the wine went down their throats, it wasn't just about the taste of the liquor, but the continuous diluting of the barriers between their hearts. Sometimes, between people, exchanging a thousand words wasn't as effective as sharing a jar of wine.

Friendship was a truly marvelous thing.

It wasn't like an alliance treaty, written in black and white.

It wasn't like a debt, with a ledger of numbers.

It wasn't like a faction, with its ranks of superiority and inferiority.

Once it was born, it allowed people to shed some of their armor, making them willing to bare their true hearts in front of others, and even more willing... to share good wine.

Looking at Tu Yuanzi, a faint warmth welled up in Ning Zhuo's heart.

Spending time with a person like this made him feel exceptionally relaxed.

Tu Yuanzi drunkenly left the study and stayed the night directly inside the cave manor.

Left alone, Ning Zhuo also felt a bit light-headed and floaty.

In such a state, as he reconsidered the current situation, he no longer felt the previous sense of oppression.

"Assaulting the Flowing Cloud Peak won't be as simple as establishing a title in the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion. The old powers on the Flowing Cloud Peak are deeply rooted and complexly intertwined. Cloud beasts, cloud nests, cloud veins, and Cultivators from all sides will all cause ripples due to Naming Stronghold's aggressive intrusion."

"Revolving around the Naming Fire Furnace, competition and scheming both inside and outside the Naming Stronghold will absolutely be unavoidable."

"Heh, I don't need to be anxious."

Impatience exposes fear, and rashness leads to miscalculation.

Right now, the most crucial thing was to wait.

Wait for the probes from all sides, wait for the undercurrents to surface, and wait for the enemies to make the first move.

"Especially that Liu Jinke..." Thinking of this person, the corners of Ning Zhuo's lips curled up slightly.

He had naturally harbored profound intentions when he let that man go.

Liu Jinke sneezed, then stared at the envoy in front of him with an ugly expression.

He subconsciously lowered his voice. "I've already done as you asked!"

"I fought Ning Zhuo, and my head was even cut off by him."

"And now you still want me to go and make trouble for him?!"

Liu Jinke was roaring in a hushed voice.

The envoy, who came exactly from the Demon Subjugation Hall, remained expressionless. "Liu Jinke, calm down. When we previously asked you to trouble Ning Zhuo, we informed you in advance that the other party possessed Golden Core-level combat strength, warning you to be extremely careful."

Liu Jinke glared. "Was that just simple Golden Core combat strength? He has Fire Element Techniques specifically designed to counter me! He only used the inherited Mechanism of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Machine Tube], and he didn't even use a single Mechanism art!"

"He didn't unleash his full power at all!"

"Do you think I'm an idiot? You still want me to challenge him? Wouldn't I just be courting death?!"

Liu Jinke felt that while he had indeed received funding from the Demon Subjugation Hall, money was money, and his life was his life.

Before fighting Ning Zhuo, he hadn't known Ning Zhuo's true bottom line. Now that he had a clearer picture, he understood that this act was trading his life for money. What good would all the money in the world do if his life was gone!

What should he do?

If he agreed to such a thing, wouldn't he be a massive idiot?

The envoy chuckled and shook his head slightly, his eyes filled with pity. "You don't understand your true situation, Liu Jinke."

"No rush. I won't force you. In a few days, you will come looking for me on your own initiative."

Liu Jinke froze. "What do you mean?"

The envoy had already turned to leave. "In the coming days, you will understand."

TL Notes:

*Cold Bone Moon Dew Wine: A Nascent soul level wine, given to Ning Zhuo as a reward from the White Paper City Lord.
