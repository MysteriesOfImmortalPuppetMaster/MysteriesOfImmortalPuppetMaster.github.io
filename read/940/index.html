Chapter 940: The Counterattack of Nanming Stronghold

Lei'en Terrace.

The Red-Robed Guest hovered in midair, slowly revealing himself. He was expressionless, gazing down from above. The moment he appeared, it immediately caused a commotion below. Soon after, cultivators of the Thundercloud Society flew into the sky to confront him.

The Red-Robed Guest’s expression was extremely cold, and he spoke quite directly: "Your Thundercloud Society funded Liu Jinke to make trouble for Ning Zhuo of my stronghold. Stop pretending to look so innocent."

"Since that is the case, take this patch of my red cloud. If you can withstand it, this grievance will be considered settled."

After speaking, he didn't wait for a response. He directly raised his hand and waved it, and a mass of blood clouds surged out from his sleeve.

At first, the blood cloud was nothing but a ball of red rosy mist, but after a few breaths, it swelled into a crimson sea covering the sky.

Fire intent roiled within the cloud, as if tens of thousands of blood-colored lotus flowers were blooming simultaneously, and also like a red river hanging upside down in the sky. Wrapping within it a scorching, bloody, and hot gale, it oppressively surged toward the Thundercloud Society's encampment.

Warning bells exploded within Lei'en Terrace, and the surrounding bronze lightning rods roared in unison.

The formation activated. Streaks of purple-black lightning were drawn out, stacking layer upon layer to transform into an inverted thunder bell. On the bell's walls, electrical light wandered like dragons and snakes, and lightning runes were densely packed, as if a heavenly tribulation had been artificially confined within.

The Red-Robed Guest stood high in the sky, looking down with cold eyes. His sleeves billowed, and a full head of black hair was blown backward by the fiery wind.

The opponent's resistance made him so excited that the corners of his mouth twitched, revealing a ferocious smile.

The next moment, he pressed down his palm. The blood cloud broke like the dam of a heavenly river, crashing thunderously into the Lei'en Terrace grand formation.

Boom!

Crimson fire and purple lightning exploded in midair.

The first layer of the lightning screen was burned red by the blood cloud, like thin paper licked and curled by tongues of fire. The cultivators of the Thundercloud Society were greatly alarmed and hurriedly activated the formation plate. The second and third layers of the lightning screen immediately lit up. Countless streaks of purple lightning tangled into chains, extending from the depths of the grand formation to wrap around the blood cloud in reverse.

The Red-Robed Guest laughed loudly: "With just this little bit of lightning, you dare to show off in front of me?"

He took a step forward, and suddenly nine crimson fiery hands reached out from the blood cloud. The five fingers were ferocious, and each palm had a burning rune. They all grabbed towards the main gate of Lei'en Terrace.

But right at this moment, the sounds of bells echoed loudly from deep within Lei'en Terrace.

The phantom of the thunder bell suddenly shrank, actually swallowing the sky-filling blood cloud along with the Red-Robed Guest!

The outside world only saw the lightning glow surge wildly while the blood cloud was abruptly extinguished.

For a time, the surroundings were in an uproar.

"The Red-Robed Guest has been swallowed!"

"The grand formation of the Thundercloud Society's encampment is indeed no small matter. That is a Nascent Soul cultivator!"

On Lei'en Terrace, Lei Wangyue's expression was cold and stern. His hands firmly pressed on the formation plate. The lightning exploding from his cuffs charred the flesh of his arms black. He gritted his teeth and pushed the formation. Inside the thunder bell, tens of thousands of lightning bolts struck down simultaneously, intending to thoroughly suppress the Red-Robed Guest.

Ten breaths.

Twenty breaths.

Thirty breaths.

The blood cloud was still nowhere to be seen.

Just when everyone thought the Red-Robed Guest had been trapped to death by the formation, a maniacal laugh suddenly echoed from deep within the thunder bell.

The laughter was muffled at first, then quickly grew louder and louder, like an underground volcano blasting open a mountain peak, and also like a sea of blood rising from a pool of lightning.

"Exhilarating!"

Rumble!

A corner of the thunder bell suddenly bulged, followed by a crack that formed a scarlet line of fire.

The fiery line grew thicker and thicker. Blood clouds spewed out from the gap. The Red-Robed Guest was charred all over, half of his red robe completely shattered. On his chest was a hideous lightning scar with flesh torn open, yet he still stepped out with his head held high.

The blood cloud behind him was tattered, but it still blotted out the sky and covered the earth.

The moment he came out, he struck back with a backhand palm, hitting the thunder bell phantom until it shook three times.

The Red-Robed Guest held his head high and chest out, glaring at his surroundings like a tiger. No one noticed that he swallowed a mouthful of fresh blood.

His crimson eyes swept over the cultivators present, and he smiled ferociously: "The Thundercloud Society is nothing more than this."

As soon as his voice fell, Lei'en Terrace collapsed with a loud boom.

Amidst the clamor of the observing cultivators, the Red-Robed Guest rode his red cloud and proudly departed.

Lei Wangyue and the others had ashen faces. They watched helplessly as the Red-Robed Guest left arrogantly, staying in place and choosing not to pursue.

Mu Yuehua personally submitted a visiting card to a certain tea dock of the Green Tea Society.

After the tea dock was silent for a moment, the main doors opened wide. Several female cultivators with cold gazes and fake smiles welcomed Mu Yuehua inside.

Inside the tea dock, the bamboo shadows were clear and sparse, and cloud springs flowed around the steps.

Ye Qingming personally prepared the tea. The teacup was no more than three inches, its glaze a blue-white. The tea liquid inside was as clear and bright as spring water, and its aroma was gentle, like wisps of warm wind through a woman's hair, silently seeping into the mind.

The tea aroma had already risen.

This fragrance did not harm or trap people; it only relaxed the mind, softened the divine sense, and melted away one's guard bit by bit. Often, those who entered the formation would reveal thirty percent of their inner secrets in conversation before even realizing something was wrong.

Mu Yuehua sat properly among them, her moon-white sleeves resting before her knees, her expression tranquil.

Ye Qingming said softly: "Little Daoist Friend Mu, please have some tea."

Mu Yuehua looked down at the tea.

A speck of silver-white firelight drifted from her fingertips.

The firelight was like cold dew under the moon, cold but not rigid, profound but not dark.

As it fell into the teacup, the tea liquid did not boil, but the aroma condensed into a thin layer of frost in an instant. The frost patterns spread along the surface of the tea, actually forcing out the formless tea aroma wisp by wisp.

Ye Qingming's eyes flashed slightly, and her heart stirred secretly: "Taiyin Fire Seed?"

The tea aroma was refined by the cold fire, transforming into pale white smoke threads that hovered above the teacup.

Mu Yuehua reached out and gathered this wisp of smoke aroma into her palm. She held it loosely, making a fist. After rubbing it for a bit, she leisurely opened her hand.

Her fingers were slender. In her cold moon-like palm, there was already an exquisite little pill.

"I also have a tea pill. I invite Senior Ye to taste and appraise it."

As she spoke, she tilted her palm. The exquisite tea pill immediately fell into the tea and quickly dissolved.

The color of the tea water rapidly changed, forming a faint silver light that emitted a clear and cold chill.

A flash of shock passed through Ye Qingming's eyes. The female cultivators beside her could not conceal their reactions, revealing expressions of shock one after another.

This small pill, refined on the spot by Mu Yuehua using the tea aroma as the main material, directly cracked one of the Green Tea Society's signature spirit teas, displaying an extremely powerful level of pill refinement!

Mu Yuehua leisurely reached out, simultaneously using her divine sense to control objects, sending the tea that was originally meant for her through the air to Ye Qingming.

Ye Qingming wore a solemn expression. She reached out to take it and took a gentle sip.

She remained silent for a long time before putting down the cup in her hand. Her gaze towards Mu Yuehua had completely changed: "Good tea."

Mu Yuehua smiled, slowly stood up, and gave a slight bow: "Thank you, Senior Ye, for your hospitality. I shall take my leave."

Ye Qingming sighed and said solemnly: "You all must courteously escort Daoist Friend Mu for three miles before returning."

The one Lin Jinglong challenged was Zhao Ni, a Golden Core beast tamer of the Bird and Beast Manor.

The martial arena.

Zhao Ni released a Golden Core-level Cloud Wing Tiger. The tiger's roar churned the clouds, and as it spread its wings, sky-filling cloud blades surged like a tide of snow-bright sabers.

Lin Jinglong planted a tree seed, retreated thirty zhang first, and then used Wood Element Spells to rapidly grow cloud vines.

The cloud vines did not resist the tiger's claws head-on, but instead grew along the flow of the cloud qi. Every time the Cloud Wing Tiger flapped its wings, it would whip up a cloud current, and the cloud vines would use the cloud current to climb, branch out, and entangle.

Zhao Ni initially held the upper hand. He sneered continuously, advancing fiercely and attacking with all his might. It wasn't until the Cloud Wing Tiger's third pounce that he realized fine vines had already formed a net between the tiger's claws.

"Good boy!" Zhao Ni shouted. The Cloud Wing Tiger roared fiercely, snapping most of the green vines.

However, Lin Jinglong had already planted the tree seed deep.

A young sapling sprang up from the ground, its bark white as snow and its thin leaves transparent. In just a short moment, it actually swallowed a patch of chaotic clouds in the surroundings. Having lost its cloud momentum, the Cloud Wing Tiger's wings sank, and its body dropped half a foot toward the ground.

Under Lin Jinglong's rapid growth stimulation, the spirit tree expanded rapidly, soon becoming thick enough for several people to hug.

From then on, Lin Jinglong gained the upper hand.

An incense stick's time later.

Lin Jinglong raised his hand, and thirty-six green vines closed in simultaneously, stopping just before the Cloud Wing Tiger's neck.

The Cloud Wing Tiger barely managed to stand up before collapsing heavily to the ground.

Lin Jinglong cupped his hands and said: "Thank you for the match."

Zhao Ni's expression was gloomy, but he had nothing to say.

Shen Xi challenged Mister Zhaoxin of the Wenxin Studio.

Mister Zhaoxin had a Golden Core cultivation and excelled in mental formations.

He set up a Mind-Reflecting Wall. Cloud shadows flowed on the wall. Those who entered the formation would have their greed, anger, and ignorance triggered, seeing what they feared, craved, felt guilty about, and loathed in their hearts.

Shen Xi stood before the formation. He unrolled the formation scroll in his hand, and the phantom of the Nine Palaces emerged grid by grid.

Mister Zhaoxin smiled: "Little Daoist Friend Shen, a formation can break a formation, but how can the mind be broken?"

Shen Xi replied: "If the mind is unbroken, the formation will break itself."

He took a step into the Mind-Reflecting Wall.

Within his field of vision on the wall, it first showed the heavy expectations of his clan, then peers and geniuses like Ning Zhuo and Situ Xing, and even the cold glares of the Shen clan's elders. Various pressures pressed down like a mountain. The light and shadows on the Mind-Reflecting Wall shifted, constantly disturbing his mind.

However, Shen Xi did not look at the illusions on the wall; he only looked at the Nine Palaces on the ground.

He used the Nine Palaces to secure his steps, and his steps to secure his mind. With every step he took, a formation rune would fall from his formation scroll. After nine steps, the Nine Palaces returned to their positions. All the illusions on the Mind-Reflecting Wall were divided into nine pieces by the formation runes, and then shattered by a single point of his finger.

Mister Zhaoxin's smile stiffened slightly.

Shen Xi cupped his hands: "Wenxin Studio's mental formation is marvelous. This Shen has been taught a lesson."

He walked out freely and elegantly.

The intensity of Nanming Stronghold's counterattack far exceeded the public's expectations.

Situ Xing, Shen Xi, and Lin Jinglong attacked one after another, fighting above their tiers and winning all their matches, fully demonstrating the Foundation Establishment front-facing capability of a super clan.

Meanwhile, Mu Yuehua's "battle record" was also spread around, eliciting gasps of admiration from bystanders.

The Red-Robed Guest forcefully shattered Lei'en Terrace. He successfully concealed his injuries, and his arrogant combat posture made the crowd even more fearful of the Nanming Stronghold. After all, the Red-Robed Guest was only one of the five great Nascent Souls in the stronghold.

The true Grand Stronghold Lord was Chunyangzi.

Would Chunyangzi make a move?

Many cultivators, who enjoyed watching the commotion grow bigger, held strong expectations for this.

Chunyangzi sat firmly without moving, showing no signs of taking action.

Some mocked him for putting on airs, while others said he was experienced and prudent. Chunyangzi showed absolutely no reaction.

Ning Zhuo also watched coldly from the sidelines, knowing in his heart that Chunyangzi would absolutely not make a move: "He is the leader of a faction, so his political acumen will definitely not be weak. Therefore, he must know - amidst this clamor, Nanming Stronghold still needs a stabilizing pillar of the Righteous Path!"

However, what dissatisfied Ning Zhuo was that Old Man Taoli hadn't moved either.

This person had stayed consistent for decades, hiding inside his pill furnace to cultivate bitterly, practically cultivating the concept of "closed doors" into a Daoist attainment. The outside world was fighting lively, but hearing it, he only felt it was troublesome.

After Ning Zhuo pondered over it, he personally went to ask him to test the Earth Expansion Alliance's encampment. Old Man Taoli used excuses like his furnace fire was unstable and his pill qi was not flowing smoothly, rattling off over a dozen reasons in one breath.

After hearing him out, Ning Zhuo just smiled: "Since Daoist Friend Tao finds it inconvenient, then I'll ask Daoist Friend Baicao Weng to go. If the earth veins of the Earth Expansion Alliance have Earth Element spirit herbs growing alongside them, I presume Baicao Weng will be interested."

Then, he "casually" mentioned that this task could be counted as a new debt.

Upon hearing this, Old Man Taoli instantly couldn't sit still anymore.

"Wait, wait!"

"Who said I'm not going? I just need to regulate my furnace's breath first! How can a place like the Earth Expansion Alliance be left for Baicao Weng to casually try? His combat strength is definitely inferior to mine. If he is defeated, what will happen to the prestige of my Nanming Stronghold?"

In recent days, there had been many rumors, speculations, and evaluations surrounding the Nanming Stronghold outside.

Among them, regarding Old Man Taoli and Baicao Weng, there was a saying about the Two Old Men of the Stronghold, one male and one female "standing shoulder to shoulder."

Initially, neither Old Man Taoli nor Baicao Weng took it to heart, but as the rumors grew louder, it gradually gave both of them the desire to compete with each other.

As for who released the rumors, there is no need to mention it here.

After Ning Zhuo brought out Baicao Weng, Old Man Taoli was instantly goaded into action and decisively left.

The Earth Expansion Alliance's encampment, Leiyun Terrace, concealed a heavy earth vein formation.

Old Man Taoli first delivered a visiting card and openly and honorably charged into the formation.

The Earth Expansion Alliance, restrained by their previous funding of Liu Jinke, had a small weakness grasped by Ning Zhuo. Coupled with their confidence in their own formation, they decisively accepted the challenge.

As soon as Old Man Taoli entered the periphery, he was swallowed by a Spirit-Locking Thick Earth Formation.

Earth walls closed in from all four sides like mountains. Layers of heavy pressure pressed down, as if the entire Flowing Cloud Peak was sitting on his shoulders.

Old Man Taoli cursed loudly and ducked back into his portable treasure furnace, using the furnace to refine himself and forcibly resist the heavy pressure.

The Spirit-Locking Thick Earth Formation pressed heavier and heavier. The outer wall of the pill furnace creaked. Old Man Taoli couldn't escape for the moment, and his predicament continued to deepen. If this continued, there would be absolutely no hope.

Seeing this, the cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance spoke up to persuade him to surrender.

Old Man Taoli was originally hesitating, but upon hearing the calls for surrender, his fighting will paradoxically strengthened: "The previous few people all won, yet I cannot. What face do I have left to return to the Nanming Stronghold? What face do I have to see Ning Zhuo?"

He couldn't think of a way to break the deadlock, so he could only continue to endure forcibly.

Seeing him so stubborn, the cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance secretly cursed him out, but they didn't dare to directly kill him.

The leader thought for a moment and immediately dispatched an envoy to Chunyangzi's side to explain the situation.

Upon learning the details, Chunyangzi hesitated for a moment but still decided to inform Ning Zhuo first.

Ning Zhuo's flying message quickly responded, informing Chunyangzi that he had already made arrangements and deployments, and that he only needed to quietly watch the situation change.

Chunyangzi received the reply, his eyes flashing. While maintaining his attention, he temporarily held himself back.

Tu Yuanzi brought Ning Zhuo's orders and came to the Earth Expansion Alliance to rescue Old Man Taoli.

He shouted a few words from outside the formation. After hearing this, Old Man Taoli's face flushed red. Feeling even more that he had no face to retreat, he simply acted like a turtle shrinking its head, cooping himself up in the pill furnace and continuing his meaningless persistence.

The cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance were all helpless.

Tu Yuanzi thought to himself: "What Young Master Ning Zhuo instructed me was indeed correct. Things are developing exactly as he predicted."

He immediately rolled up his sleeves and stated he would personally enter the formation to fish Old Man Taoli and his furnace out.

The cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance discussed it for a moment and felt troubled. They expressed that the grand formation was used to protect the encampment and it would be difficult to interrupt and stop it.

Tu Yuanzi thought to himself: "This is again just like Young Master Ning Zhuo predicted."

He immediately grinned widely and waved his hands repeatedly: "No need, no need for so much trouble. I'll just go and come right back."

After speaking, he proactively entered the formation.

The encampment's grand formation erupted with even stronger power, but Tu Yuanzi merely frowned, as if he felt an extra wet and heavy piece of clothing had been draped over his shoulders.

The formation runes exploded with blinding yellow light, but Tu Yuanzi moved as if entering an uninhabited land.

He quickly approached the pill furnace, raised his hand to grab it, and then leaped out.

Leaving the grand formation, he casually tossed the pill furnace.

The pill furnace hit the ground, and Old Man Taoli tumbled out along with the furnace, looking in a thoroughly wretched state, with the furnace lid knocked half-askew.

He looked up at Tu Yuanzi, naturally knowing it was this person who had saved him. His face flushed red, and he immediately cupped his fists and bowed in gratitude.

Tu Yuanzi paid it no mind and showed a simple, honest smile.

Meanwhile, the cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance were deadly pale.

"He... he forcibly withstood the Spirit-Locking Thick Earth Formation?"

"That is the Earth Expansion Alliance's encampment formation!"

"This Tu Yuanzi is absolutely an expert among Nascent Soul-level cultivators!"

After this incident, Tu Yuanzi's reputation skyrocketed.

The image of the Nascent Soul cultivator holding the fourth chair of the Nanming Stronghold was truly fleshed out in the hearts of outsiders for the first time, becoming extremely weighty.
