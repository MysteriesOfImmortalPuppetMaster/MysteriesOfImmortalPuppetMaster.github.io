Chapter 941: Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar

On Flowing Cloud Peak, Tan Zhu halted his steps.

This was a deserted mountainside, nothing out of the ordinary. However, Tan Zhu knew that not far below was a Flowing Cloud Ferry.

At the ferry, cloud qi circulated constantly. It was frequently used by White Cloud Village, the Setting Sun Society, and the Floating Life Society as a temporary passageway.

Expressionless, Tan Zhu sat cross-legged, closed his eyes, and began to meditate.

Wisps of purplish-black qi emanated from his body. Carried by the wind, the purplish-black smoke blended into the flowing clouds and spread downwards.

Not long after, the Flowing Cloud Ferry fell into chaos.

The cloud layer at the ferry was dyed a faint purple, and the poisonous gas grew increasingly dense.

This ferry was managed by White Cloud Village. The stationed cultivators immediately sent an emergency report. White Cloud Village dispatched a Nascent Soul cultivator to resolve the issue.

Yet, after several attempts, the clouds could not be shifted, the wind could not blow them away, and the rain could not wash them clean.

The purplish-black poison cloud hung in mid-air, blocking the cloud paths. Any flying boats, cloud beasts, or escaping lights that approached would have their spiritual light corroded by the poisonous gas.

The source of this disaster was quickly discovered.

The Nascent Soul cultivator immediately dispatched an envoy to hold Tan Zhu accountable.

Tan Zhu had a fierce reputation. The Golden Core envoy had to muster his courage to question him face-to-face.

Expressionless, Tan Zhu straightforwardly admitted it, apologized on the spot, and stated he was willing to bear all losses.

The Golden Core envoy's courage grew a bit, and he further demanded that Tan Zhu resolve the current situation.

Tan Zhu, however, shook his head. With a very calm tone, he informed the other party that he too was out of ideas, "The Cultivation Art I practice leaves a deep disaster in its wake, and I myself suffer greatly from it. To tell the truth, my lifespan is nearing its end. I don't have much time left."

"These are not empty words. I've heard that the Myriad Manifestations Sect has a sect-guarding spiritual treasure, the Mantra Bell, which can detect lies and verify the truth."

"The fact that I am approaching my limit can be verified beneath the Mantra Bell!"

Hearing this, the Golden Core envoy was stunned for a couple of breaths. He subconsciously stared at Tan Zhu, his eyes filled with disbelief and bewildered helplessness.

After snapping back to reality, the envoy immediately flew back to report.

When the Nascent Soul cultivator of White Cloud Village heard the news, they were completely numb.

A Demonic Path Nascent Soul cultivator on the brink of death!

Who would dare provoke such a person?

Especially since Tan Zhu was famously a lone wolf - no children, acting alone, without any bindings or attachments.

"Why did this person join Naming Stronghold?"

"Regardless, his actions this time are clearly meant to make things difficult for us, to exact revenge."

"If I go personally, I'll definitely just get my nose rubbed in the dirt."

The Nascent Soul cultivator sent to help by White Cloud Village was secretly alarmed. They had no choice but to request reinforcements from the village while making emergency dispatch arrangements.

In just two hours, three cloud paths were forced to reroute, causing losses to skyrocket.

Old Man Youyun hurried to the scene in a burning rush.

With completely white hair and beard and wearing broad robes, he stood above the ferry. As he raised his hands, a cloud light spread out around him, like thousands of acres of snow-white cotton lifted by an invisible wind.

Talisman Art - White Cloud Gray Dog!

Twelve cloud runes flew out from his sleeves. The pure white runic light condensed in mid-air to form the shape of a massive dog.

The giant cloud dog raised its head and gave a long howl. The sound echoed, shaking the surrounding cloud mountains until they rustled.

Immediately after, the giant dog lunged into the purplish-black poison cloud. With its mouth tearing and its four claws thrashing, it actually ripped apart and swallowed large patches of the poison cloud.

Seeing this, the cultivators at the ferry immediately erupted into cheers.

"Lord Youyun's action is truly extraordinary!"

"No matter how bizarre this poison cloud is, it's still a cloud. All clouds in the world fall under the jurisdiction of our White Cloud Village!"

But just as the cheers arose, it was as if an invisible hand shoved them back down their throats.

The poison cloud torn by the cloud dog did not dissipate. Instead, like an injured living creature, it had a stress response. Threads of purplish-black smoke poured backwards into the cloud dog's mouth, nose, claw crevices, and underbelly.

The pure white cloud dog was instantly stained with purple spots. First, its limbs stiffened, and then its entire body emitted a strange sizzling sound.

Boom!

The cloud dog shattered, turning into a shower of toxic purple rain.

The raindrops fell onto the ferry's formation barrier, burning dark ripples into it.

A few White Cloud Village disciples who couldn't dodge in time got rain on their hems. Their robes immediately corroded, and their protective spiritual light was gnawed open as if by poisonous insects, frightening them into retreating repeatedly.

Old Man Youyun's expression changed slightly, and his chest tightened. He had suffered a backlash from his own talisman technique, and a trace of coppery sweetness rose in his throat.

Shortly after, Madam Caiyun arrived.

She wore a plain white dress with a white jade hairpin tucked into her hair at the temples.

Arriving at the ferry, she unleashed her Lifebound Treasure, the Cloud-Tailoring Scissors.

Snip!

The first cut fell, and the cloud layer was severed like silk, splitting open a white scar hundreds of meters long.

Snip!

The second cut fell, and the white scars crisscrossed to form a massive cross.

Snip, snip...

The Cloud-Tailoring Scissors continuously opened and closed. The white scars spread across the purplish-black poison cloud, tearing it into pieces.

Madam Caiyun's intention was obvious: to sever the poisoned cloud layer from the ferry's cloud vein. As long as she cut off the connection between the poison cloud and the ferry's cloud root, she could toss it into the outer turbulent currents.

The cultivators of White Cloud Village held their breath again as new hope was born.

However, as the cloud scissors plunged deep into the poison cloud, a sudden mutation occurred.

The purplish-black smoke seeped deeper along the cut cloud gaps. The wider the cut, the faster the toxic smoke drilled in. Originally only polluting a section of the ferry, it now spread along the scissor marks, dying all the exposed cuts of the ferry's cloud veins a dark purple.

Madam Caiyun felt a chill in her heart and immediately retracted the scissors.

But it was already too late.

When the Cloud-Tailoring Scissors flew back, the blades were crawling with toxic spots, its spiritual light dimmed.

She reached out to catch it, and a wisp of black smoke immediately rose from her palm. The pain made her eyebrows twitch.

The onlookers lost their voices.

"Just what exactly is this thing?"

"Even Madam Caiyun using her Lifebound Treasure has failed!"

Old Man Youyun's brows knitted into a tight knot. Having acted personally before and now observed this entire process, he had deduced the truth.

"This is no simple poison art, nor is it an ordinary smoke technique."

"It possesses the properties of a Divine Art. It lies dormant within the smoke; it can adapt to techniques and evolve when it encounters techniques."

Grasping the damaged cloud scissors, Madam Caiyun nodded slightly, her voice turning cold, "It isn't a complete Divine Art, but it already has the core foundation of one. For this Tan Zhu, before his death, to have actually touched upon this level?"

The two pillars of White Cloud Village were both inwardly horrified.

The Flowing Cloud Ferry was nearly paralyzed!

In just a few short hours, at least twenty-seven flying boats were forced to reroute. In the past, caravans of cloud beasts came and went endlessly. The tavern guests of the Floating Life Society, the secret couriers of the Setting Sun Society, and White Cloud Village's own cultivators all had to pass through here.

Now, three cloud paths were forced to divert, seven flying boats were stranded, and nineteen cloud beasts were disturbed by the toxic gas, their spiritual light damaged...

Just the costs of rerouting the cloud paths, compensation for delays, and repairing the flying boats' protective formations had already racked up nearly a hundred thousand Spirit Stones in losses.

The longer this dragged on, the greater these losses would become.

Even more fatally, the purplish-black poison cloud was eroding the ferry's cloud root!

The ferry on Flowing Cloud Peak couldn't be operated from just any random patch of cloud qi.

The cloud veins converged here, with fixed tides, stabilized by formation flags, and nourished over many years by White Cloud Village.

If the cloud root were soaked through with toxic smoke, the ferry would rot from the base up. By then, the entire cloud path would collapse, altering the flow of cloud qi for several surrounding minor peaks as well.

The change in cloud flow would create a wider-ranging turbulence. Such turbulence often lasted for months, or even years, until a new point of equilibrium was reached and it stabilized once more.

But by that time, a completely new ferry would be formed in a completely new location.

Very likely, it would no longer be within White Cloud Village's territory.

Upon learning of this situation, the faces of White Cloud Village's Nascent Soul cultivators and Golden Core cultivators became increasingly grim.

Someone said angrily, "Since Tan Zhu admitted to it, make him pay for the damages!"

Pay?

How could a Demonic Path Nascent Soul cultivator nearing the end of his lifespan possibly afford it?

Force him?

He was a dying demonic cultivator, the childless, sectless, and unattached Poison Annihilation Wanderer. If they really pushed him too far, he might go sit in front of White Cloud Village's main headquarters for three days before dying, which would be even more catastrophic!

What's more, everyone knew why Tan Zhu had made a move.

White Cloud Village had sponsored Liu Jinke. The latter's battle with Ning Zhuo was equivalent to White Cloud Village striking at Naming Stronghold.

As a member of Naming Stronghold, Tan Zhu didn't target anyone else, deliberately polluting White Cloud Village's Flowing Cloud Ferry instead. His intent for revenge couldn't be more obvious.

After an emergency discussion, the cultivators were out of ideas. They could only hold their noses and beg Tan Zhu for leniency.

This time, the person who came was no longer a Golden Core envoy, but Old Man Youyun ascending the mountain personally.

He landed a good thirty zhang away from Tan Zhu. Cupping his hands first, his tone was low, "Fellow Daoist Tan, White Cloud Village has offended you previously. Regarding the matter of Liu Jinke, my village indeed acted improperly."

Tan Zhu remained seated cross-legged, his pale-grey face devoid of joy or sorrow, like a stone statue on the verge of weathering away.

Old Man Youyun continued, "I entreat you, Fellow Daoist, to withdraw your Divine Art. My White Cloud Village will swallow the ferry's losses. Furthermore, we have prepared three modest gifts to be delivered to Naming Stronghold as a formal apology."

Only then did Tan Zhu open his eyes.

His eyes were incredibly dark, as black as a toxic pool that had accumulated over ten thousand years.

Tan Zhu offered no further explanation. He stood up, flew above the ferry, slowly raised his hand, and made a grasping motion with his five fingers towards the ferry below.

The purplish-black poison cloud originally suspended above Flowing Cloud Ferry suddenly lit up from within with countless ghostly glimmers. These glimmers were neither firelight nor lightning, but rather resembled ghost lanterns buried deep in rotting soil.

The poison cloud began to reel back. First, its edges collapsed, and then its center started to spin, transforming into a massive purplish-black vortex.

A low whistling sound emerged from within the vortex.

It sounded like wind whistling through dry bones.

Looking up at this scene, the cultivators at the ferry all retreated involuntarily. The protective formations on those damaged flying boats lit up layer by layer, while the cloud beasts lowered their bodies and let out uneasy growls. The disciples of White Cloud Village turned particularly pale.

Tan Zhu clenched his five fingers.

The purplish-black vortex shrank violently.

A large swath of toxic smoke was retracted by him, drilling into his robes and seeping into his body through countless pores.

The cloud qi remaining in mid-air turned gray in patches, fracturing and dissipating like a silk painting licked by flames, exposing large swathes of empty space.

The cultivators of White Cloud Village felt such heartache they were practically speechless.

This was the ferry's cloud qi that they had nurtured for many years, and now not even one-tenth of it remained!

But if the toxic smoke wasn't extracted, the entire ferry would have been destroyed. Although the losses were currently disastrous, they could at least preserve the foundation.

"To think Tan Zhu was so terrifying!"

"Why did the higher-ups have to provoke Naming Stronghold?"

"Hm?" Tan Zhu was about to leave when his expression suddenly shifted.

The cloud layers of Flowing Cloud Ferry circulated day and night. Seemingly scattered but actually connected, they were like a giant cloud ring. While blotting out the sky and sun, they formed some sort of stable cycle and concealment.

At this moment, the cycle here had been completely shattered, allowing a certain "truth" that had long been concealed to reveal itself.

The light of the eastern sky was like a blade, slashing diagonally into the gap in the clouds.

Golden-red sunbeams pierced through the remnant clouds, shining deep into the mountain's belly.

Everyone saw that behind that ordinary mountainside, a hidden cave entrance had actually emerged.

The cave entrance opened halfway into the inner side of a cliff. The cloud qi at its edges hung like a curtain, illuminated by the sunlight so that golden twilight seemed to flow across it.

Deep within the cave, a vertical shaft resembling an ancient well slowly materialized. Its walls were etched with countless dark golden runes. Awakened by the sunlight, the runes lit up one after another, shining like golden scales.

What churned within the well was not water, but golden Yang Qi, accompanied by faint flames.

As wisps of sunbeams fell from the cave entrance into the well, a crimson firelight rose from the well's bottom.

The sunlight and earth fire intersected in the well. Enveloped by the surrounding cloud qi, they astonishingly transformed into clusters of crimson-gold rosy clouds, slowly spreading outwards along the well's rim.

The crimson-gold rosy clouds accumulated more and more, surging in all directions, collapsing the hidden cave entrance. Rocks tumbled and melted away, revealing more of the interior scene.

Because of this, an ancient altar - half embedded in the mountain's belly, half overlooking the sea of clouds - was revealed.

The altar resembled a cloud disc, stacked layer upon layer, with sun-supporting patterns carved into its edges. Beneath its base, a fire vein could faintly be seen coiled like a crimson dragon; with every breath, firelight seeped through the cracks in the stone. The altar's peak received the sunbeams, its waist swallowed and spat out clouds and mist, and its base suppressed the fire vein.

Truly, the mountain was the furnace, the clouds were the lid, the sun was the pill fire, and the fire vein was the firewood.

At this moment, the entire mountainside actually gave birth to a majestic and blindingly radiant phenomenon!

All the cultivators present were left dumbfounded.

By the time White Cloud Village's Nascent Soul cultivator reacted and wanted to conceal it, it was already too late.

By now, the crimson-gold rosy clouds had multiplied so much that they flooded towards the sky.

The sky was illuminated into a brilliant expanse of golden light. Countless cloud phenomena morphed within the glow - at times resembling a Golden Crow spreading its wings in dazzling splendor, at times like a pill furnace opening its lid to overflow with precious light, and at times like a crimson dragon supporting the sun, spiraling upwards.

The nearby cultivators only felt a rush of gentle, pure Yang breath washing over their faces. It wasn't scorching or fierce, but vast and dignified, as if the newly risen sun were illuminating thousands of miles of mountains and rivers.

"Wh-what is this?!"

"To think such a place was hidden within Flowing Cloud Peak?"

"Sunbeams, cloud qi, and fire veins harmonizing together... This is a geomantic treasured land!"

"The Yang Qi on the altar does not leak, the cloud qi does not disperse, and the fire vein does not rampage. How strange, who exactly set this up? This is absolutely not something formed naturally!"

Discussions exploded like a tidal wave.

The news of the geomantic treasured land seemed to sprout wings, rapidly spreading all over Flowing Cloud Peak, and then washing over the peak to reach every corner of the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate.

When Ning Zhuo learned of this matter, he was in his Cave Manor repairing the Spirit-Siphoning Box.

Holding a Jade Slip in his hand, his divine sense swept into it. Astonishment first flashed in his eyes, quickly followed by solemnity.

"Tan Zhu truly lives up to his reputation as a long-established Demonic Path figure. This time, his actions actually left the entirety of White Cloud Village helpless, forcing them to beg for leniency."

"Because of this, Naming Stronghold's prestige has flourished even more!"

"But by causing this commotion, destroying a vast amount of cloud qi, and breaking the original concealment cycle, he actually exposed a geomantic treasured land. It truly is beyond expectations."

Ning Zhuo's brows furrowed slightly as he murmured, "Is this what tribulation-luck is?"

The words Meng Yaoyin had spoken that day surfaced in his mind once again.

Tribulation-luck was a combination of disaster and blessing.

Being at odds with the Myriad Manifestations Sect himself, Naming Stronghold warring with Flowing Cloud Peak, the second battle with Liu Jinke, the various factions placing bets, Tan Zhu avenging against White Cloud Village... This entire journey seemed to be full of tribulations, troubles, and pressure. But if one survived the tribulations, a blessing would actually be overturned from within them.

However, a blessing would never simply drop out of the sky and into one's pockets for free.

Whether he could obtain it still depended on whether he, Ning Zhuo, could digest the tribulations lying within.
