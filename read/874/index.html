Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 874: Behind-the-Scenes Manipulation

News about the Nanming Fire Furnace had spread across almost the entire Flying Cloud Kingdom.

When Xiao Juxia returned to the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate once again, the teleportation formations were packed to full capacity.

Many people, right after stepping out of the teleportation formations, immediately asked the guard cultivators for the exact location of Danxia Peak and headed straight there.

The guard cultivators were overwhelmed with work.

Ever since the news of the Nanming Fire Furnace got out, they hadn't had a moment's rest.

Every round of the teleportation formations brought in a maximum load of people, drawing in a massive sea of cultivators. Even with a three-shift rotation, they were still dead tired.

Thanks to the cover provided by the massive influx of cultivators, Xiao Juxia was not interrogated upon returning to the Myriad Manifestations Sect this time.

He first gathered intelligence, then returned to his rented immortal cave to focus on his calculations. It wasn't until nightfall that he pushed open his door, stepped out of the cave, and gazed at the night scenery.

In the night sky, stars dotted the darkness.

In the direction of Danxia Peak, however, there was a mesmerizing light pink glow, looking like radiant clouds blanketing the sky.

Even at night, it was bustling with noise. Countless cultivators, drawn by the Nanming Fire Furnace, flocked there like ducks, carrying hopes of getting lucky.

Originally, Xiao Juxia had absolutely zero interest in this.

But the results of his calculations made him change his previous mind.

"Returning to the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate this time provides further intelligence. Moreover, being close to the site of the incident makes it easier to acquire Heavenly Secrets."

"The results of the previous Cultivation Art modification still exist; they weren't extinguished just because Qin De died."

"But when I calculated how to obtain these results, the divination didn't point to the Demon Subjugation Hall. Instead, it pointed to this Nanming Fire Furnace minor trial. What's the logic behind this?"

To make this calculation, Xiao Juxia had exerted all his power and expended over twenty years of his lifespan.

Despite paying such a heavy price, he only obtained an ambiguous result.

He quickly accepted it.

Because this was simply the nature of the Divination Method in cultivation; it was often vague and ambiguous. This was especially true when the matter was of great importance.

"Could it be that before Qin De died, he had some sort of interaction with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit?"

"Or perhaps he buried his results somewhere on Danxia Peak?"

"There is another possibility: the cultivator who acquired Qin De's results is also participating in this Rising Cloud Minor Trial?"

Unsure of the answer, Xiao Juxia immediately set off for Danxia Peak.

Upon arriving, he merely observed from the sidelines, waiting to see if he could trigger any spiritual intuition and gather new clues.

A streak of golden light slowly flew over.

It was a carriage made of pure gold, entirely resplendent and shining, like a moving sun in the night sky.

Every inch of the carriage body was carved with exquisite patterns - dragons and phoenixes bringing auspiciousness, the sun and moon shining together, stars circulating, and mountains rolling. Between these patterns were inlaid countless tiny gems that sparkled with various colors in the night, like stars dotting a golden firmament.

In front of the carriage were eight spirit horses.

They were all pure white pegasi, entirely snow-white without a single stray hair. Their manes were like snow, their hooves like the wind, and their eyes sparkled with spiritual energy. The eight pegasi trotted side by side, their paces perfectly uniform, like a thoroughly tempered honor guard.

Behind the carriage were dozens of attendants.

They wore uniform red-gold Daoist robes. The hem of their robes bore the characters "Chunyang" (Pure Yang) embroidered in vigorous, sharp strokes, glittering radiantly in the night. Their paces were uniform, their aura solemn. Each of them held their heads high and chests out, staring straight ahead like a well-trained army.

Seated properly upon the carriage was a single person.

He looked to be in his forties, with a handsome face and an innate noble air about his brows and eyes. His black hair was bound up by a golden crown inlaid with a red-gold gem, within which a faint flame seemed to dance. He wore a red-gold Daoist robe entirely embroidered with the full text of the Chunyang Pill Sutra. The densely packed characters seemed to flow over the fabric like living creatures.

His hands rested in his lap, fingers long and nails neatly trimmed. His eyes were lightly closed, his expression as calm as water, as if he hadn't traveled from afar but was merely taking a stroll in his own backyard.

A Nascent Soul cultivator.

And at the peak of the Nascent Soul stage, no less!

This person's appearance immediately caused a sensation.

Inside the Formation Space, Jiang Ping instantly recognized him: "It's actually Chunyangzi!"

Chunyangzi was a Daoist title. It belonged to the leader of the Pure Yang Palace. Every generation's sect master of Pure Yang Palace was called Chunyangzi.

The Pure Yang Palace was one of the cultivation forces in the Flying Cloud Kingdom. While not a behemoth like the Myriad Manifestations Sect, it still held significant sway within the Flying Cloud Kingdom.

Shen Hongyao stared at the resplendent golden carriage with tightly furrowed brows: "He is the Sect Master of the Pure Yang Palace. How could he personally participate in the Rising Cloud Minor Trial? Once he wins, he'll have to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect. What happens to the Pure Yang Palace then?"

Qingbaizi sat cross-legged on a bluestone. His eyes widened, flashing with a sharp glint: "Interesting! For Chunyangzi, a leader of a faction, to actually participate in Danxia Peak's minor trial... It seems the rumors weren't entirely baseless. The Pure Yang Palace has been declining over the years and is showing signs of merging into the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

With such grandeur and status, the guard cultivators naturally rushed over to escort the entire convoy up to the mountain peak.

Hiding in a corner, Xiao Juxia witnessed the entire process and couldn't help but sigh inwardly: "During the previous clash of destiny, the Nanming Fire Furnace was destroyed as collateral damage. However, Wang Yu, Dong Chen, and the others indeed showed ingenuity by using the nearly destroyed furnace to successfully stoke the opportunism of countless people, creating such a spectacle."

"For this Flying Cloud Assembly, just this Rising Cloud Minor Trial alone has attracted who knows how many outside talents to the Myriad Manifestations Sect!"

"Such grand events and such systems are the fundamental reasons why the Myriad Manifestations Sect stands unshakeable."

"The predecessors who founded the Myriad Manifestations Sect and established such systems are truly respectable, and terrifying."

Before the golden rosy clouds left in the sky by Chunyangzi's carriage had completely dissipated, another anomaly appeared on the horizon.

A speck of scarlet appeared in the sky.

At first, it was merely the size of a pinprick, entirely unnoticeable in the night sky. But in an instant, it rapidly expanded, like a drop of fresh blood dripping into clear water, swiftly spreading, diffusing, and corroding until it dyed half the night sky a dark, bloody red.

From within the scarlet expanse, a figure slowly walked forward.

He walked through the air, his steps so light that they made almost no sound. Yet, with every step he took, dark red ripples spread out through the night sky beneath his feet, like a stone tossed into a pool of blood, sending out rippling waves.

The Red-Robed Guest!

He still wore a scarlet robe embroidered with dark blood patterns. The patterns twisted like snakes, wriggling slightly in the night breeze as if alive. His face was handsome to the point of being almost demonic - eyebrows like distant mountains, eyes like bright stars, a high nose bridge, and lips so deeply red they looked as if they had been smeared with fresh blood.

At this moment, his face was full of displeasure.

He had just returned to the main mountain gate only to receive bad news: Chunyangzi had actually shown up on his own accord to participate in this Rising Cloud Minor Trial!

The Red-Robed Guest and the current Chunyangzi had a deep-seated grudge. The former had been relentlessly hunted by the Pure Yang Palace, cutting a sorry figure until he was forced to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect, finally earning himself a period of peace.

"Eyesore!" The Red-Robed Guest raised his right hand, splayed his five fingers, and lightly clenched them toward the lingering golden rosy clouds in the midair.

The golden clouds flickered violently in the night sky a few times, emitting a "hissing" sound like glowing charcoal doused with cold water, before ultimately dissipating entirely without leaving a single trace.

The Red-Robed Guest coldly snorted, scrutinizing the guard cultivators flying over to receive him.

Bracing themselves, the cultivators escorted the Red-Robed Guest inside.

In the Formation Space, a moment of silence ensued.

Qingbaizi's voice was hoarse and low: "Whether it's Chunyangzi or the Red-Robed Guest, their pill qi is incredibly vast. Even if we added all of ours together, we wouldn't measure up to either of them individually."

Shen Hongyao snorted: "Hmph, if you gave me the same amount of cultivation time, the accumulation I'd build up certainly wouldn't be weaker than theirs."

Qin Ke frowned: "Chunyangzi is one thing. But the Red-Robed Guest uses human blood, human bones, and human souls as medicine... Will the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit accept this kind of person?"

Jiang Ping sighed: "Among the Four Symbols, the Eastern Azure Dragon governs life, overseeing the sprouting of all creation and the flourishing and withering of vegetation. The Western White Tiger governs killing, overseeing armored warhorses and the slaughter of warfare. The Northern Black Tortoise governs concealment, overseeing the Netherworld Prefecture and the return and hiding of all things. As for the Southern Vermilion Bird..."

"It governs growth, oversees summer, belongs to the Fire element, and is red in color."

"Fire is blazing, hot, and dry, yet it shines its light upon all. It can warm hearts, but it can also incinerate everything; it can illuminate the darkness, but it can also swallow the light; it can bring hope, but it can also bring destruction. Fire has no good or evil, no right or wrong; it is simply pure power."

"All things grow and are nurtured in the South. This 'growth' refers to nurturing and strengthening. It represents an upward force, a vigorous vitality."

"Therefore, it is strictly neutral."

Jiang Ping's perspective was broad. Hearing his words, Shen Hongyao and the others couldn't help but frown one by one.

They all realized one thing: even a great Demonic Cultivator like the Red-Robed Guest had the possibility of being recognized by the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

None of these people rushed to participate in the minor trial.

The arrangement Wang Yu had given them was to wait until the final deadline before having them enter the field.

On one hand, it was to expand their horizons. Such an opportunity was rare even in previous Flying Cloud Assemblies.

On the other hand, it was to let the massive tide of cultivators do the trial and error for them.

Ning Zhuo wanted to collect results and compare them to find the hidden patterns. Wang Yu and his group had the exact same intention.

Once the trial and error produced results and revealed the patterns, Jiang Ping and the others could make timely adjustments and the most appropriate arrangements.

Danxia Peak, Main Hall.

Wang Yu held a Jade Token in his hand, currently observing Chunyangzi's performance inside the Formation Space.

Chunyangzi extended his palm, revealing a Fire Seed.

Wang Yu immediately recognized it: "Oh? It's the Vermilion Feather Fledgling Flame."

This Fire Seed appeared red-gold and shaped like a fledgling bird whose wings were not yet fully grown. This fire was born within a phoenix's nest, formed by the condensation of residual flames escaping when a phoenix egg hatched.

The Vermilion Feather Fledgling Flame was incredibly nascent, so much so that it possessed almost zero lethality. However, it had its own unique power, granting it unparalleled advantages in refining certain Medicinal Pills.

Most importantly, the Vermilion Feather Fledgling Flame could grow; it was a lesser tier precursor to many other Fire Seeds.

For example, if it were accepted by the Nanming Vermilion Bird Fire, it could take the Vermilion Bird as its mother and the furnace as its nest. Under the nourishment of the Vermilion Bird's Qi, it would slowly mature, ultimately shedding its fledgling form to transform into a brand new Nanming Vermilion Bird Fire Seed!

Chunyangzi was the helmsman of a Sect. Dipping into his Sect's foundations to produce such a Fire Seed was no problem at all.

Even if it was from his personal collection, it wouldn't be surprising.

But unfortunately, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit had absolutely no reaction to it.

"It doesn't matter, I'll assist you." Wang Yu smiled faintly and lightly brushed his whisk.

He secretly manipulated the Formation, directly driving a Jade Token to fly toward Chunyangzi.

Chunyangzi first stored away the Vermilion Feather Fledgling Flame, then received the Jade Token, a calm smile surfacing on his face.

Wang Yu kept his involvement completely hidden.

Whether the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit could be saved or not was still up in the air. But external forces like Chunyangzi and the Pure Yang Palace were tangible assets.

Even if this Rising Cloud Minor Trial hosted by Danxia Peak ultimately couldn't save the Nanming Fire Furnace, it would naturally be a good thing if it could absorb more outside talents.

"Some people I actively gave Jade Tokens to, but for others, I did not."

"These people will need to be closely monitored later on!"

The scenes of several people passing the trial flashed through Wang Yu's mind. Impressively, Ning Zhuo, Sun Lingtong, and Qing Chi were among them.

Two days and two nights passed in a flash.

Ning Zhuo looked at the intelligence he had gathered in his hands, still pondering the potential hidden rules.

"Zhongli Mei, Qingbaizi, Nine Fires Dragon Lord, Cang Yazi, Chunyangzi, the Red-Robed Guest, Old Man Taoli, Old Man Baicao, Qin Ke, Dong Nishang, and so on are all on the list."

"Not a single Nascent Soul cultivator failed in the first round, and only a few Golden Core cultivators suffered defeat."

"Among the Foundation Establishment stage, Zhu Fenxiang, Situ Xing, Yun Niaoniao, as well as Boss, Qing Chi, Gongsun Yan by my side, are all included."

Ning Zhuo couldn't help but look up at the sky: "This is hard."

"Although the intelligence I've obtained is genuine, the Formation was set up by Danxia Peak. Behind-the-scenes manipulation is bound to exist."

"People like Zhongli Mei, Qingbaizi, Qin Ke, and Jiang Ping are the obvious indicators."

"So, in reality, what the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit is drawn to or approves of is very difficult for an outsider like me to judge."

"I'll just take it one step at a time."

The second round commenced.

The number of people on Danxia Peak decreased by at least eighty percent.

Previously, even the sky had been crowded; now, people were only lining up on the mountain paths.

Although many cultivators weren't qualified, they still came over to watch. Xiao Juxia was one of them.

Ning Zhuo landed at the foot of the mountain and joined the queue.

Upon reaching the peak, the Jade Token he had previously obtained was collected, and he stepped into the Formation Space once again.

The Formation Space was exactly the same as before, and even the Jade Tokens looked identical.

But when Ning Zhuo walked up to a Jade Token, it didn't react at all. He immediately knew that the standard for the test had been raised a notch.

"The Five Elements Qi Regulation Art I primary cultivate also encompasses the Fire Element. I might as well try this first."

Ning Zhuo exhaled his Five Elements spiritual energy, immediately disassembling it. The other four elements progressively generated each other, ultimately converting the spiritual energy into pure Fire Element.

Just by doing this much, the Jade Token trembled and flew over in front of Ning Zhuo on its own accord.

"That's it?" Ning Zhuo instantly caught it.

Just like that, he breezed right through the second round.

Wang Yu was still personally presiding over the array. Seeing Ning Zhuo pass the trial, his heart stirred slightly.

"The Cultivation Art this boy cultivates can actually control the Five Elements spiritual energy with such proficiency? So distinctly separated, yet capable of mutual generation and suppression?"

"With a little cultivation, he'd be an excellent seedling for alchemy!"

"The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit feels an affinity for him. It let him pass just by him channeling out Fire Element spiritual energy."

The Jade Token flying to Ning Zhuo wasn't the result of Wang Yu secretly interfering.

The ones he had to secretly interfere for were people like the Red-Robed Guest, Cang Yazi, and Chunyangzi.

"Tie Kuang's analysis was right. These Nascent Soul and Golden Core cultivators have cultivation bases that are simply too powerful, which inherently triggers the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's vigilance and wariness."

"Sigh... if I hadn't stepped in, all of these people would have been eliminated!"
