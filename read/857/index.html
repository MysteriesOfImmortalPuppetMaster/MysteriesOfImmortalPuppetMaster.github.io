Chapter 857: A Strike from the National Seal

"This is the perfect opportunity to harshly discipline them and completely transform their mental outlook."

Compassion has no place in commanding troops.

Zhong Dao's style in leading the Demon Subjugation Hall had always been this iron-blooded.

From another perspective, the Myriad Manifestations Sect's scale was simply too large. There were too many people within it.

After this Flying Cloud Assembly, a new batch of cultivators would be absorbed into the sect.

Using this battle to carry out an elimination - discarding the dross and keeping the elite - would bring long-term benefits to the Cloud Prison.

"But why hasn't Wu An arrived yet?"

The direct person in charge of this Cloud Prison was Wu An. Although the Cloud Prison as a whole fell under the jurisdiction of the Demon Subjugation Hall, the daily guarding, patrolling, and Formation maintenance were all Wu An's responsibility.

If Zhong Dao intervened now, it would confirm Wu An's dereliction of duty. For a Cloud Prison commander found guilty of dereliction, a light punishment would be dismissal, while a heavy one would mean criminal charges. This was not what Zhong Dao wanted to see.

The elite cultivators did not belong to the Demon Subjugation Hall. Standing there, they felt like every day dragged on like a year.

Eventually, someone urged again, "Hall Master Zhong Dao..."

Zhong Dao snorted coldly and interrupted, "What's the rush? Just how much do you know about that Demonic God?"

The elite cultivators' expressions froze.

Zhong Dao said slowly, "My Demon Subjugation Hall has spent years dealing with these fiends and fighting them as enemies. I understand them far better than you do. I don't blame you for your ignorance."

He paused, his gaze profound. "That Demonic God is fully titled the 'Lord of the Seamless Rift'."

"'Seamless' means having no gaps, a perfect unified whole. To be able to create a rift within the 'seamless' and rule over rifts - that is the Lord of the Seamless Rift."

"He is one of the Demonic Gods, feeding on all physical entities, regardless of whether they are dead or alive. The rifts He creates are both passages and traps."

As soon as he finished speaking, agonizing screams rang out from the frontline.

The first few Demonic Path cultivators who rushed into the void rift were all devoured.

This was the Lord of the Seamless Rift hunting!

In front of the void rift, the Demonic Path cultivators continuously backed away. The brutal reality of the first escapees dying violently right before their eyes instantly extinguished everyone's fanaticism. Freedom was right in front of them, but so was death. No one dared to rashly step into that rift again.

The guards, however, would not give them time to hesitate.

"Kill!" the guard commander roared hoarsely.

The guards swarmed forward. Blades flashed, sword shadows flickered, and battle cries shook the heavens.

The Demonic cultivators retreated step by step, suffering heavy casualties.

The Eagle Claw Butcher's ten fingers dripped with blood, most of his steel claws snapped.

The Ghost-Faced Scholar changed his ghost faces one after another, his injuries growing increasingly severe.

The ordination scars on the Copper Dhuta's head had entirely dimmed, and his physical stamina plummeted to rock bottom.

Under these circumstances, Zhong Dao finally made his move!

Clang!

A bell toll abruptly exploded like the shattering of heaven and earth. It pierced through the Cloud Layers, penetrated the stone walls, broke through everyone's eardrums, and struck straight into the depths of their souls.

The Demonic cultivators' frontline fell into total chaos amid a chorus of wails.

Some bled from their seven orifices, some fell to their knees, and others fainted outright!

Clang - Clang - Clang -

Three bell tolls rang out, each louder, heavier, and more explosive than the last.

The Demonic cultivators who were still struggling were as if struck by an invisible giant hammer. Over a dozen had their souls scattered and dropped dead on the spot!

Zhong Dao slowly floated down.

"It's the Hall Master!!" The guard cultivators' morale skyrocketed.

The Demonic cultivators, on the other hand, were thoroughly intimidated, displaying obvious terror.

With one toll of the bell, evil forces were repelled; with three tolls of the bell, ten thousand ghosts shared in sorrow.

Zhong Dao stood with his hands behind his back. His gaze swept over the still-struggling Demonic cultivators, and he spoke coldly, "Surrender, and you will not be killed."

A deathly silence filled the tunnel.

The Demonic cultivators looked at each other, their eyes full of inner conflict.

"I... I surrender..." one Demonic cultivator's voice trembled as he was the first to kneel.

With the first, came a second, then a third. One by one, the Demonic cultivators knelt. Some clutched their heads with their hands, while others prostrated themselves on the ground.

Of course, there were also Demonic cultivators who preferred death over submission. They jumped straight into the void rift, only to be instantly devoured by the Lord of the Seamless Rift.

However, after five Demonic cultivators died, one actually managed to escape successfully!

"Hahaha." Seeing this, Withered Bone Old Demon laughed heartily. With a leap, he too jumped into the void rift.

Under the gaze of many, he was not devoured either, but was directly teleported and escaped from this place!

This scene instantly sent all the present Demonic cultivators into wild ecstasy!

Like madmen, they surged toward the void rift.

The Eagle Claw Butcher was the first to charge into the rift.

The Copper Dhuta originally followed closely behind.

The Phantom Enchantress released her final wisp of fragrant wind, confusing the Copper Dhuta for a split second, and rushed in ahead of him.

The Copper Dhuta snapped back to his senses, cursed, and chased in after her.

By the time the guard cultivators fought their way to the front of the void rift, over twenty Demonic cultivators had already teleported and escaped through it.

"Hall Master Zhong Dao!" The elite cultivators were highly anxious. "How do we track them now?"

Zhong Dao stood with his hands behind his back, watching the slowly closing rift, and said indifferently, "We can't catch up to them anymore."

The elite cultivators looked at each other and fell into silence, obvious dissatisfaction written on their faces.

In their eyes, even though Zhong Dao had acted personally and utterly routed the Demonic cultivators' frontline, he had been far too overconfident. He hadn't gone all out, nor had he allowed the elite cultivators to strike. This was undoubtedly a delay of military opportunity, which ultimately led to the increased casualties among the guard cultivators and the escape of so many Demonic cultivators.

Prisoners escaping from the Cloud Prison was a massive scandal.

This was especially true right now, when the Myriad Manifestations Sect had opened its main gates to host the Flying Cloud Assembly. This was a grand event. During this period when all eyes were upon them, even the slightest mistake would cause the Myriad Manifestations Sect's reputation to plummet.

Now, the Cloud Prison incident was already a major disaster that had never occurred in the many previous Flying Cloud Assemblies hosted by the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

"Hall Master Zhong Dao, we will report this truthfully to the Sect Master," the leader of the elite cultivators said with a stiff tone.

Zhong Dao kept his back to them and merely replied indifferently, "Go."

Huazhang Kingdom.

The main hall of the Imperial Ancestral Temple was brightly lit.

"Pfft!" The Ruler of Huazhang suddenly spat out a mouthful of blood and immediately collapsed to the ground, withering.

Smack.

The incense burner shattered into several pieces, scattering sandalwood embers all over the floor.

Han Song was already sitting on the floor, his breath weak as a thread. His Literary Qi was severely overdrawn, and his mental energy had been excessively consumed.

Zheng Jing had long since fainted. He lay curled up at the foot of the Sacrificial Altar, his face an ashen hue, his breathing so faint it was almost imperceptible.

Wang Shu leaned against the wall, his face the color of gold foil, his limbs completely limp. His eyes were half-open, his pupils unfocused, looking as if he would pass out at any second.

The one in the best condition was still the Ruler of Huazhang.

His chest heaved violently, every breath carrying a rust-like stench of blood.

He looked up at the Golden Chapter Jade Tome.

The sixty-two-page Jade Tablet had already closed. The four characters "Huazhang Jade Tome" on its cover glowed faintly. However, that light was far dimmer than before.

What was shocking to see was a crack that had appeared at the bottom of the tome's cover.

Although the crack was as thin as a strand of hair, in the eyes of the Ruler of Huazhang, it was distinctly visible and extremely hideous.

The Ruler of Huazhang stared at that crack, his eyes filled with anguish.

"My Lord," Han Song struggled to speak, his voice so hoarse it was barely audible. "The opposing side's destiny... is stronger than we anticipated... This subject begs My Lord... to temporarily cease..."

After taking a moment to recover, Han Song continued with great difficulty, "Wait until we have recovered... we will hold an even larger national sacrifice... summon more Great Confucians... and use the power of the entire nation..."

"It's too late," the Ruler of Huazhang interrupted him.

Han Song was stunned.

The Ruler of Huazhang spoke slowly, "I can sense... our destiny's landing point is already gone."

Han Song's expression drastically changed.

To draw an analogy, the battle of destinies was like a clash between two armies. Losing the landing point of their destiny was equivalent to having their entire frontline, or even their main city, wiped out. What would they use to fight the enemy?

The Ruler of Huazhang stared intently at the Golden Chapter Jade Tome. "We still have a chance for one final strike."

Although the destiny's landing point was gone, relying on the intense destiny clash from just now, the main bodies of both sides' destinies were still entangled. As time passed, the degree of this entanglement would also rapidly decrease.

The Ruler of Huazhang raised his hand and unhooked an item from his waist.

It was a perfectly square jade seal, three inches across, entirely verdant green, and radiating a gentle, warm light. Carved on the seal's knob was a coiled dragon. This was none other than the Huazhang National Seal.

Han Song's pupils contracted sharply. He struggled to sit up but was completely devoid of strength. "My Lord..."

The Ruler of Huazhang raised his hand, signaling him not to speak.

Wang Shu's hoarse voice drifted over. "My Lord, the Royal Seal must not be used lightly! If anything goes wrong, the foundation of the nation will be shaken! Please think twice, My Lord!"

The Ruler of Huazhang merely closed his eyes and took a deep breath.

This was the moment for him to make the decision.

When he opened his eyes again, only absolute resolve remained in them!

He raised his hand, hoisting the Royal Seal high.

That small jade seal emanated a warm glow under the lamplight. The light was soft yet firm, much like the thousand-year literary lineage of the Huazhang Kingdom, like the lifeblood of countless Confucian cultivators, and like the wisdom of generations of past sages.

A dragon's roar resonated!

In the next moment, the coiled dragon on the Royal Seal came alive, transforming into a resplendent dragon-shaped stream of light.

The stream of light poured directly into the Golden Chapter Jade Tome.

The Golden Chapter Jade Tome suddenly trembled violently, and all sixty-two Jade Tablets flipped open simultaneously. The text on every page was glowing, burning.

Boom!

A fierce shockwave of air swept outwards in all directions, centering on the Golden Chapter Jade Tome.

A blinding pillar of light pierced straight through the temple roof and shot into the clouds, drawing the gaze of countless people in the royal capital.

Han Song, Zheng Jing, and Wang Shu were all blown away by the shockwave, smashing into the corners of the room and fainting on the spot.

Only the Ruler of Huazhang remained standing before the Sacrificial Altar, completely motionless. His clothes had been shredded by the shockwave, his face was scratched and bleeding from flying debris, and his body was shaking violently.

The jade seal slowly fell back into the Ruler of Huazhang's hands.

But his gaze remained fixated on the Golden Chapter Jade Tome.

He was waiting.

Waiting for the final outcome of this clash of destinies.

Flying Cloud Kingdom, outside the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

Xiao Juxia, who had been flying rapidly high in the sky the entire way, suddenly experienced a slight change in expression as his spiritual intuition was triggered.

"Something incredibly important must have happened!"

He had a strong premonition and instinctively went to pinch his fingers to perform a divination.

But in the very next second, he stopped this dangerous action.

"Too dangerous!"

"If I rashly divine it now, it's equivalent to observing a clash of destinies. It will definitely implicate my personal destiny. I'm afraid I would become the pitiful little fish caught in the crossfire when the city gate catches fire."

"But..."

Xiao Juxia thought of another possibility - what if the clash of destinies had already ended, and they needed him to clean up the aftermath?

If that were the case, divining it right now would allow him to immediately seize the initiative and gain a preemptive advantage!

Xiao Juxia paused high in the sky. A Cloud Layer drifted over, concealing his figure.

For a time, he fell into hesitation.

The summit of Cloud Canopy Peak.

The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy still hung suspended above the nine-tiered white jade Sacrificial Altar. Its nine-layered canopy surface slowly rotated, with seven-colored clouds continuously flowing around it.

Dong Chen sat cross-legged, his face pale and his aura weak. His left arm was still wrapped in bandages, and the caved-in area of his chest had been stabilized by a Medicinal Pill, but every breath he took still brought a faint pang of pain.

Wei Ji, Tuoba Huang, Wang Yu, and the others were slumped in various places. Some were regulating their breathing, some were healing their injuries, and some were even still unconscious.

A squad of elite cultivators sped over from the direction of the Cloud Prison.

"Greetings, Sect Master!" Their leader fell to one knee, cupping his fists in a salute.

Dong Chen opened his eyes and looked at him.

The leader of the elite cultivators had an ashen face as he reported the entire sequence of events in a low voice, detailing everything exactly as it happened.

Dong Chen frowned slightly. "A spatial rift?"

The leader nodded. "According to reports from the guards on the scene, Song Taosheng sneaked into the Cloud Prison in an attempt to assassinate Qin De. The five death-sworn soldiers he brought with him were all killed in action, and he himself perished in battle with Qin De. Qin De... escaped."

"Escaping alongside him were Withered Bone Old Demon, Eagle Claw Butcher, Copper Dhuta, Ghost-Faced Scholar, Phantom Enchantress, and over twenty other Demonic cultivators."

"This subordinate personally believes that if Lord Zhong Dao had intervened in time..."

Dong Chen shook his head, cutting him off directly, "I know."

"You may step down and stand by."

The middle-aged cultivator opened his mouth, wanting to say something more, but seeing that Dong Chen had already closed his eyes and clearly didn't want to discuss it further, he could only cup his fists in salute and retreat with his squad of elite cultivators.

A moment later.

On a secluded mountain path of Cloud Canopy Peak.

The squad of elite cultivators was marching briskly down the mountain, all of them wearing displeased expressions.

"That's it?" a young cultivator couldn't help but say. "The Sect Master didn't even ask a single question?"

Another cultivator said indignantly, "Over twenty prisoners escaped from the Cloud Prison! Do you know what a massive scandal this is? And during the Flying Cloud Assembly, no less! The Sect Master actually just dismissed us like that?"

A third cultivator guessed, "Could it be that even the Sect Master fears Lord Zhong Dao?"

The leader finally lost his patience. "Enough."

Everyone fell silent.

The leader stopped in his tracks and looked back toward the summit of Cloud Canopy Peak. Up there, the seven-colored rosy light of the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy was still faintly circulating, but it was already much dimmer than usual.

"The Sect Master has his own considerations," he said slowly. "We are only subordinates. We shouldn't speak out of turn."

He then sighed softly. "Let's go. If the Sect Master tells us to stand by, we stand by. The other matters aren't for us to worry about."

At the peak of Cloud Canopy Peak.

Tuoba Huang turned his head and looked at Dong Chen. "Sect Master."

Dong Chen shook his head, instantly cutting him off. "Do not be anxious, and above all, do not underestimate Zhong Dao."

"Besides, our most pressing priority is to assist the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy and continue suppressing the destiny!"

"This time, our united wills acting as a fortress finally turned the tables, securing us a winning advantage. However, we still cannot be careless, because the opponent still has the strength for one last strike."

Tuoba Huang didn't take it seriously. "The opponent couldn't be stupid enough to think that one final strike can turn the tables, right?"

Wei Ji said coldly, "Perhaps the opponent wants to gamble one last time?"

Dong Chen, however, slowly closed his eyes.

Seeing this tiny gesture, the others immediately fell silent.
