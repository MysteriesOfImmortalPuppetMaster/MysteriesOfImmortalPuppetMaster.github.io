Chapter 844: Golden Chapter Jade Book

The Golden Chapter Jade Book was the supreme destiny-suppressing treasure of the Huazhang Kingdom.

It was a jade book, nine inches square and three inches thick, with pages of white jade and bindings of gold wire. Currently, it held sixty-two pages.

For a long time, it had been stored in the center of the main hall of the Huazhang Kingdom's Imperial Ancestral Temple, enshrined atop a three-tiered white jade altar. It suppressed the national destiny, condensed Literary Qi, resonated with the hearts of the people, and issued warnings of national calamities.

At this very moment, the gold wires binding the Golden Chapter Jade Book were all pulled taut and straight, emitting the ear-piercing sound of metal clashing against iron. The noise was sharp and shrill, as if something were tearing at the gold wires, attempting to snap them one by one.

On the cover, the large characters for "Huazhang Jade Book" were also trembling slightly.

The guarding official was terrified to the point of despair. When he rushed out of the Imperial Ancestral Temple, his legs were weak. He stumbled and staggered through three courtyards and past layers of corridors before crashing headlong into the duty room.

In the duty room, Temple Director Zhou Zheng was organizing the list of offerings for the day. Hearing the door crash open, he jerked his head up and saw the pale face of Lu Huairen, causing his heart to sink immediately.

Zhou Zheng asked urgently, "What has happened?"

The guarding official opened his mouth, but no sound came out. He pointed in the direction of the Imperial Ancestral Temple, his entire body trembling like a sieve.

Zhou Zheng stood up abruptly, strode over to him, and grabbed hold of him. "Speak!"

The guarding official finally squeezed out a few words: "Jade... Jade Book... Anomaly... Warning!"

Zhou Zheng's expression changed drastically. Without another word, he rushed out the door.

Moments later, Zhou Zheng dashed out of the Imperial Ancestral Temple. He took a blank jade slip from his robes and used his spiritual energy to carve eight characters: "Jade Book Anomaly, Monarch please arrive urgently!"

In the next instant, the jade slip was infused with spiritual energy, transforming into a streak of flowing light that shot into the sky.

The Huazhang Monarch was currently polishing chess pieces.

His greatest hobby was playing Go. He collected many famous sets, and in his leisure time, rather than lingering in the harem, he spent almost all his time playing with these famous pieces, wiping down the boards, and studying the stones.

The next moment, the jade slip smashed through the window lattice and flew toward him.

The Huazhang Monarch hurriedly caught it, cast his divine sense into it, and his expression changed abruptly.

The Huazhang Monarch immediately stood up, activated the hidden teleportation array in the room, and entered the main hall of the Imperial Ancestral Temple directly.

As the Monarch, his every move affected all parties and carried great consequences. With a major emergency occurring, the Huazhang Monarch entered the temple quietly to prevent causing unrest in the court.

The gates of the Imperial Ancestral Temple's main hall were tightly shut, with only Zhou Zheng inside.

Seeing the Huazhang Monarch arrive, he hurriedly knelt in obeisance.

The Huazhang Monarch waved his hand, signaling him to rise, and immediately walked up to the Golden Chapter Jade Book. He repeatedly activated hand seals and incantations, muttering under his breath.

Heart Seal, Body Seal, and Mouth Seal - the three seals were applied simultaneously, revealing the Huazhang Monarch's nervousness.

Half an incense stick of time later, the Golden Chapter Jade Book slowly calmed down, returning to its usual state of radiating auspicious clouds and colorful light.

However, the Huazhang Monarch's expression was very grim.

He had soothed this national treasure, but he also gained a vague sense of the impending threat: "It is a twisted logic and heresy that can greatly shake the literary and logical foundations of my Huazhang Kingdom! It seems to come from the Demonic Path. Not long ago, the destiny of the Demonic Path became entangled with the destiny of my Confucianism, triggering the warning from the Golden Chapter Jade Book!"

"The Demonic Path..."

The Huazhang Monarch pondered for a moment, then suddenly called out, "Zhou Zheng."

Temple Director Zhou Zheng bowed: "Your subject is here."

"Transmit my secret edict: Summon Han Song, the Libationer of Jixia Academy; Zheng Jing, the Erudite of the Imperial College; and Wang Shu, the Director of Studies at the Directorate of Education. Have them enter the Imperial Ancestral Temple immediately. Do not alert others, and do not leak the news."

Zhou Zheng accepted the order and quietly withdrew.

Half an hour later, the three great scholars entered together.

The leader, Han Song, had white hair and beard, a lean face, and eyes like stars. Behind him, Zheng Jing looked calm, while Wang Shu's brows were slightly furrowed. All three wore plain robes without any accessories - this was the highest etiquette for a secret council within the Confucian school.

"My Lord," the three bowed in greeting.

The Huazhang Monarch raised his hand, gesturing for them to dispense with the formalities. He transmitted his thoughts via divine sense, informing the three of the causes and consequences.

All three expressions changed slightly.

The Huazhang Monarch continued, "This matter is of grave importance. To avoid any personal error in judgment on my part, I ask the three beloved ministers to communicate with the Golden Chapter Jade Book one after another, so we may verify this among ourselves."

Han Song stepped forward to sense it first. He was followed by Zheng Jing and Wang Shu.

"The Lord's sense is correct," Han Song said slowly. "This is a sign of the Demonic Path's destiny entangling with our Confucian destiny. Although the entanglement is shallow, it has already triggered the Jade Book, which is clearly no small matter."

Zheng Jing nodded: "What kind of thing is the Demonic Path's destiny nurturing that it could shake my Huazhang's literary vein?"

Wang Shu frowned deeply: "If allowed to develop, at best it will cause turbulence in the national destiny; at worst... the literary vein could be severed. My Lord, I suggest we immediately initiate a sacrificial rite."

The great affairs of a nation lie in warfare and sacrifice.

The Huazhang Monarch nodded: "Correct. I summoned you three here in secret to assist me in holding a secret National Sacrifice!"

He looked at Han Song: "How long do you think is needed to prepare?"

Han Song frowned, lowered his head in thought for a moment, and replied, "At least three days."

The Huazhang Monarch shook his head: "Two days."

Han Song immediately bowed: "Yes, My Lord."

The Huazhang Monarch's gaze swept over the other two: "Who shall write the sacrificial text for this National Sacrifice?"

Both Wang Shu and Zheng Jing were skilled in this art. After exchanging a glance, Zheng Jing took a small step forward: "My Lord, this subject is willing to undertake this task."

The Huazhang Monarch nodded and said to Wang Shu, "This National Sacrifice needs to be secretive; there is no need for a grand commotion. You shall be responsible for setting up the altar within the Imperial Ancestral Temple."

Wang Shu bowed and accepted the command: "Your subject obeys the decree!"

Myriad Manifestations Sect, Main Mountain Gate.

A high-level discussion was taking place.

Wei Ji, the Vice Peak Lord of Heavy Formation Peak, sat at the head.

He wore a robe of coarse hemp, sitting as still as a mountain. His face was that of an unremarkable man in his forties, with light wheaten skin resembling a riverbed stone polished smooth by flowing water. His pure black pupils turned to the left, his gaze calm and detached, as if observing the world through a layer of transparent glazed glass.

On the left sat Tuoba Huang, the Peak Lord of Ten Thousand Beasts Peak.

His body, burly as a mountain, almost burst the chair. On his bronzed skin, a ferocious claw mark slashed diagonally from his left temple to his right cheek, cutting across the bridge of his nose. Black facial hair, thick as a lion's mane, grew wildly, covering half his face but unable to hide the sharp, falcon-like red eyes.

Heat radiated from his body; even sitting still, the air was filled with a scorching aura.

On the right sat Wang Yu, the current Peak Lord of Danxia Peak, wearing a smile.

Rosy clouds flowed across his cloud-patterned Daoist robe, and a horsetail whisk rested in the crook of his arm, exuding an air of calm and rounded perfection. He looked at Wei Ji, then at Tuoba Huang, a profound glint flashing in his eyes.

The three were discussing the debate between Zhao Hansheng and Qin De.

Tuoba Huang laughed loudly: "That sour scholar named Zhao, with his 'Study of the Mind,' lecturing all day long - I've found him an eyesore for a long time! Now that he's been debated into the ground by Qin De and rolled out of the Myriad Manifestations Sect with his tail between his legs, it’s truly satisfying."

His laughter echoed through the room.

Wei Ji's expression remained unchanged as he nodded slightly.

"Qin De defeating Zhao Hansheng in the debate was indeed unexpected," his voice was level and emotionless. "For my Myriad Manifestations Sect, it is actually a good thing."

Hearing this, Wang Yu nodded with a smile: "Peak Lord Wei speaks the truth. If Confucian cultivators develop too quickly and grow too large within the sect, it may not be a blessing for our various peaks. With Qin De as a ballast stone, they won't be able to stir up big waves."

Tuoba Huang grinned, revealing eerie white teeth: "If you ask me, if we could find the right method to settle it once and for all and drive all those sour scholars out of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, that would be best! Duanmu Zhang, Chu Xuangui, Song Taosheng... kick them all out, how clean that would be!"

Wei Ji and Wang Yu exchanged a glance and smiled bitterly at the same time.

That was impossible.

Years ago, the previous Sect Master of the Myriad Manifestations Sect had personally invited Duanmu Zhang to join.

Everyone knew this, and everyone in the Confucian school praised the previous Sect Master for his courtesy to scholars.

The truth was, the Myriad Manifestations Sect had practically poached Duanmu Zhang from the Flying Cloud Kingdom.

If the Myriad Manifestations Sect were to expel this group of Confucian cultivators, the losses would be too great.

First, it would make the world think this was a mistake by the previous Sect Master. Since he was the one who invited Duanmu Zhang, expelling him now couldn't possibly be the fault of the current Sect Master, could it?

Second, the Myriad Manifestations Sect would lose its credibility among the Righteous Path and be seen as untrustworthy. Anyone wishing to join the sect in the future would surely reconsider. This would shake the very foundation of the sect!

Third, the Myriad Manifestations Sect would be perceived as harboring malice toward the Confucian cultivator community.

The Huazhang Kingdom itself was a powerful nation, and the Confucian cultivators scattered among various cultivation nations were growing in number. It could be said that in the entire cultivation world, they were an increasingly significant force that could not be ignored.

If the Myriad Manifestations Sect expelled the Confucian cultivators within its gates, it would make a very obvious political statement, thereby triggering hostility from all Confucian groups in the cultivation world.

Confucian cultivators scattered across various countries would inevitably use various means, direct or indirect, to encourage nations to distance themselves from the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

The Myriad Manifestations Sect would immediately erect a large number of potential enemies.

And what would be the gain?

Merely expelling a batch of Confucian cultivators, which wouldn't even completely eradicate the Confucian influence on the sect.

The issue was that the current group of Confucian cultivators didn't actually have much influence on the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Thanks to Qin De, the sect had always been successful in suppressing them.

To truly expel Duanmu Zhang and the others would absolutely be penny wise and pound foolish, causing immeasurable negative consequences.

Silence lingered in the secret chamber for a moment.

Wang Yu spoke: "The Confucian group is not the main point; I actually have new concerns regarding Qin De."

Wei Ji and Tuoba Huang looked at him.

Wang Yu continued: "His foundation in Demonic studies far exceeds our previous estimates. In this debate, the demonic theories he utilized were complex and profound, possessing quite a grand atmosphere."

Tuoba Huang was dismissive: "Qin De was a genius to begin with, otherwise he wouldn't have created the Scripture of the Sage Thieves. Being locked up for thirty years, pondering his heretical theories every day - is it strange that he came up with something new?"

Wei Ji shook his head slightly.

He stated, "Zhao Hansheng is a renowned great scholar of the Huazhang Kingdom. Entering the Myriad Manifestations Sect this time, carrying the prestige of the new 'Study of the Mind' school, and giving public lectures several times, his strength was obvious to all. After meeting him just once, Duanmu Zhang was willing to take a subordinate position."

"Indeed, Duanmu Zhang needs help and wants to use the Study of the Mind to move the ballast stone that is Qin De."

"But from this, one can also see Duanmu Zhang's confidence in the Study of the Mind and in Zhao Hansheng!"

Wei Ji looked at Tuoba Huang: "When we discussed the matter of Qin De, it was all based on the premise that 'Qin De must lose.' At that time, we all thought Zhao Hansheng would undoubtedly win. Now, what is the result?"

Tuoba Huang fell silent, then slowly nodded: "Peak Lord Wei makes sense. We do need to re-evaluate Qin De."

Wei Ji looked at Wang Yu: "What is Peak Lord Wang's view?"

Wang Yu smiled faintly, his whisk swaying gently: "Why don't I personally take action and perform a Soul Search on Qin De?"

Wei Ji and Tuoba Huang's expressions shifted slightly.

Although the Soul Search technique had risks, Qin De himself was not a member of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, but a prisoner and a tool.

So what if some damage was left behind?

After pondering for a moment, both Wei Ji and Tuoba Huang expressed their agreement.

With the main business concluded, the three relaxed.

Wang Yu picked up his teacup, took a gentle sip, and suddenly said, "Speaking of which, that junior named Ning Zhuo has been making quite a commotion recently."

Wang Yu looked at Wei Ji and smiled. "That kid swept through numerous formation trials a few days ago: the Nine Curved Corridor Formation, Earth Fire Field Formation, Wind and Thunder Formation, Five Elements Rotation Formation, Fivefold Gate Formation, Ten Thousand Swords Returning to the Origin Formation, Illusion Sea Formation, Tiangang Big Dipper Formation, Universe Shift Formation... one after another, taking first place in almost every single one. I heard he also had contact with Wenren Yan from your peak?"

Wei Ji's face remained impassive as he spoke concisely: "That is true. Wenren Yan is quite interested in him and has met with him privately."

Wang Yu raised an eyebrow: "Oh? Does Heavy Formation Peak intend to recruit him?"

Wei Ji gave Wang Yu a deep look and didn't answer directly. Instead, he looked at Tuoba Huang.

Tuoba Huang's face didn't look good.

Everyone present knew the reason.

Tuoba Huang had publicly sent a batch of heavy gifts to Ning Zhuo and announced openly that they were merely gifts, without the demands that Zhong Dao had placed on Ning Zhuo.

At the time, Tuoba Huang had a favorable impression of Ning Zhuo because he learned that Ning Zhuo had, to some extent, rejected Zhong Dao.

Simultaneously, receiving a hint from Wang Yu, he didn't want to see Ning Zhuo join the Demon Subjugation Hall.

He also wanted to suppress Zhong Dao to avenge the embarrassment Zhong Dao had caused him during the Eight Peaks Meeting.

Who would have thought that the heavy gifts sent out would be returned by Ning Zhuo in public?

Ning Zhuo's action undoubtedly caused Tuoba Huang to lose face, naturally triggering Tuoba Huang's ill will.

But since the other party was merely a junior, while Tuoba Huang could disgust Zhong Dao, if he were to target a Foundation Establishment cultivator, the loss of face would be even greater.

So, he had to hold in this pent-up frustration himself.

Wei Ji said to Tuoba Huang, "The conditions my Heavy Formation Peak can offer Ning Zhuo are far inferior to what Peak Lord Tuoba offered initially."

He paused, his voice remaining level: "Perhaps that is the reason he is unwilling to join my Heavy Formation Peak."

He was rubbing salt in the wound.

Sure enough, upon hearing this, Tuoba Huang let out a cold snort.

Tuoba Huang: "That kid Ning Zhuo is too slippery; he's clearly holding out for a higher price. I don't like him."

Wang Yu laughed heartily at this.

Wei Ji looked at him, his gaze detached and calm: "Peak Lord Wang pays such attention to Ning Zhuo; could it be that Danxia Peak also intends to recruit him?"

Wang Yu stopped smiling and waved his hand hurriedly: "How is that possible?"

"Peak Lord Wei jests," he said. "Although Ning Zhuo has outstanding talent, he has no ability in alchemy. My Danxia Peak needs alchemical talent, not formation talent or mechanical talent, and our requirements for moral standards aren't high. I just thought of it and mentioned it in passing. This child, Ning Zhuo, is not what I seek."

In the secret chamber, the aroma of tea curled upwards.

Just at that moment, the jade pendants on all three of them vibrated simultaneously, emitting a dazzling brilliance.

The expressions of the three changed instantly. Casting their divine sense into the jade pendants, they knew it was an urgent summons from the Sect Master of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

"The sect's destiny-suppressing Spirit Treasure, the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy, has changed!"

"Something is threatening our sect?"

"Let us go together, quickly!"
