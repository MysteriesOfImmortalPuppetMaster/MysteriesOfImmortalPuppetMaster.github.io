Zhu Xuanji had taken all of these.
Ning Zhuo only got this set of tiger bones.
The tiger bones floated in mid-air, wrapped in flames, slowly melting until completely dissolved.
The condensed spiritual essence gathered together, forming a tiger head shape.
Ning Zhuo then took out the spirit storage box and opened a small crack.
The tiger head spiritual essence leaned against the edge of the spirit storage box,
naturally attracting many spiritual essences, letting them float out through the crack in the box.
Like fireflies, dots of spiritual essence merged into the tiger head spiritual essence, rapidly expanding its size.
When first refined, it was only the size of an adult's fist,
but after fully gathering the spiritual essence, it was the size of a human head.
"The spiritual essence is of very high quality," Ning Zhuo's eyes shone with joy.
Life Hanging by a Thread!
He directly used the divine ability.
The tiger demon spiritual essence was what he had refined, naturally close to him and unable to resist the divine ability,
completely controlled by Ning Zhuo. This process was effortless and smooth.
"Father God," the tiger demon's spiritual essence, actively conveyed a message to Ning Zhuo through the Life Hanging by a Thread.
Ning Zhuo immediately felt the tiger demon's spiritual essence's admiration for him.
Although spiritual essence had no memories of its past life, its recognition of its master was determined by its past experiences and personality.
Just as Dasheng regarded Ning Zhuo as his lord, the tiger demon regarded Ning Zhuo as its Father God.
From this, it could be inferred that the Black Wind Tiger Demon had been well cared for by its father, the official Mountain God Tiger God of Mist Concealment Mountain, and had a good father-son relationship.
This information was obtained by Zhu Xuanji after capturing the Black Wind Tiger Demon's soul and searching it.
Unfortunately, after this ordeal, the tiger demon's soul had almost dispersed, its value drastically reduced.
Now, the tiger demon's spiritual essence spoke again: "Please, Father God, bestow a name upon me."
From just these two sentences, Ning Zhuo could feel that it was much more intelligent and sensitive than Dasheng's spiritual essence.
Ning Zhuo thought for a moment, then said:
"Our fate comes from the Meng family, you posses the innate talent of night shadow yin evil,
and in life you were of the tiger species... let's call you Meng Yehu."
The tiger demon spiritual essence shook slightly, very satisfied with this name, and immediately said:
"Meng Yehu thanks Father God for the name."
After conversing with Meng Yehu for a while, Ning Zhuo contained it.
Spirit Cognition level!
Ning Zhuo confirmed that the Black Wind Tiger Demon's spiritual essence was higher in quality than Dasheng's, and more than by just one level.
When spiritual essence reaches the wisdom spirit stage, there are five sub-levels:
Spirit Communication, Spirit Intuition, Spirit Cognition, Spirit Perception, and Spirit Mechanism.
Spirit Communication is Dasheng's current level, having some intelligence and being able to communicate with external objects.
Spirit Intuition means deeper intelligence, easier communication,
and deeper understanding of instructions, almost never making mistakes.
As the saying goes, "hearts with spiritual intuition connect instantly,"
with just a slight hint, it can understand the meaning.
At the Spirit Cognition level, intelligence deepens further.
Not only can it understand meanings, but it can also research and learn about meanings, constantly accumulating and cultivating its own knowledge.
The qualitative change here is very significant!
Because cultivation methods themselves are a form of knowledge.
"At the Spirit Cognition level, spiritual essence can learn cultivation methods!"

Ning Zhuo's lips curled into a slight smile, unable to resist imagining the future.
He had a premonition that Dasheng and Meng Yehu would become his left and right arms, his two main combat forces in the future.

This was a Nascent Soul level formation that consumed a lot of fire essence.

Familiarizing himself with the limits of these mechanical parts and collecting relevant information was extremely valuable to him.
