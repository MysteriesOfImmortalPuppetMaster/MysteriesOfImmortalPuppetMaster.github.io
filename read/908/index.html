Chapter 908: Gradually Arising Ripples

Heavy Formation Peak.

In the secret room, it was brightly lit, illuminating the fragmented formation diagrams covering the wall down to the finest detail.

Wenren Yan and Ouyang Lin both stood facing a wall, deep in thought.

Ouyang Lin suddenly pointed to a spot on the fragmented diagram on the wall. "Your deduction here is wrong. The formation core deviates too much; the ice spiritual energy will dissipate here and won't last half an incense stick's worth of time."

Wenren Yan stared blankly for a moment, then focused his gaze. A moment later, he smiled. "You are right. Ah, inviting you to repair this Profound Mist Ethereal Formation was indeed the right choice."

Ouyang Lin remained expressionless. "I am only relatively familiar with ice formations."

"You excel in illusion formations; the parts you deduced and completed are beyond my capabilities."

"To take this a step further and fully restore it, we will need to hire someone more skilled. There are several critical bottlenecks that we cannot overcome even by joining forces."

Wenren Yan smiled bitterly. "Looking at the entire Heavy Formation Peak, you are the only one I can trust."

This sounded nice, but it was actually about interests. Wenren Yan had high expectations for this formation, hoping to rely on it to help him climb to a higher level within Heavy Formation Peak.

Many cultivators on Heavy Formation Peak had sufficient formation attainments to help Wenren Yan. However, due to direct or indirect conflicts of interest, Wenren Yan couldn't put his mind at ease with them; the only one he could trust was Ouyang Lin.

Ouyang Lin fell silent for a moment before offering a suggestion. "Then you can find outsiders to help. I think among this batch of newcomers, Ning Zhuo is absolutely qualified. His attainment in the Dao of Formations is very high, at least at the Renowned Artisan level."

"If you bring him in, I have a feeling he will definitely bring you immense help."

Wenren Yan sighed. "I thought about that too, but wasn't he participating in the Nanming Fire Furnace minor trial earlier? The competition in the trial was so fierce that I didn't have the face to bother him."

Ouyang Lin said, "Now that the minor trial has ended, and Ning Zhuo even obtained the Nanming Fire Furnace, he must be in a good mood. If you go ask for his help now, your chances of success should be much higher."

Wenren Yan smiled bitterly, "That's because you haven't heard certain rumors."

Ouyang Lin was puzzled: "What rumors?"

Wenren Yan: "Rumors about Ning Zhuo wanting to establish an alliance to occupy Flowing Cloud Peak."

Upon hearing this, Ouyang Lin said in astonishment, "That must be utter nonsense. No matter how much of a genius Ning Zhuo is, he only has Foundation Establishment cultivation. He is a pure newcomer; how could he have the rallying power to summon those Nascent Soul and Golden Core cultivators, who are also his creditors!"

A sharp glint flashed through Wenren Yan's pale purple eyes. "I actually think that creating a faction tied together by debt is possible. You haven't met Ning Zhuo in person, but I've shared a meal with him."

"Based on my deductions, he is the source of this rumor; he released it intentionally."

Ouyang Lin frowned. "Why would he do that?"

Pausing for a moment, Ouyang Lin continued, "Could it be that he really wants to make a push for Flowing Cloud Peak?!"

Myriad Beasts Peak.

Tuoba Huang leaned diagonally against a large black stone chair, one foot resting on the footstool and the other casually draped over the armrest.

At this time every day, he would listen to his subordinates' reports.

A fierce-looking white tiger lay at his feet. It was as large as a calf, completely snow-white with ink-black stripes, and the "王" (King) character on its forehead was exceptionally striking.

Tuoba Huang reached out, placing his hand on the white tiger's head and stroking it leisurely.

The white tiger narrowed its eyes comfortably, emitting a "purring" sound from its throat, no different from an ordinary housecat being petted.

Tuoba Huang glanced at his subordinate. "Stop right there. It's the same things all day long, it's too boring. If there's anything interesting today, tell me that first."

The subordinate thought for a moment and nodded. "There is, my Lord."

He understood his immediate superior's temperament very well, naturally knowing what kind of news Tuoba Huang liked to hear.

He immediately said, "Ning Zhuo has recently released rumors that he intends to use the Nanming Fire Furnace as a basis to gather his creditors and form an alliance. This alliance will march on Flowing Cloud Peak and participate in the struggle for it."

Tuoba Huang's interest was immediately piqued. "Oh? This kid doesn't know the immensity of heaven and earth; he's got quite the guts. His releasing rumors must be a test. What is Zhong Dao's reaction?"

The subordinate continued, "To clear his debts, Ning Zhuo also secretly signed a contract with Danxia Peak's Peak Lord, Wang Yu. The two sides agreed that as long as Ning Zhuo doesn't join the Demon Subjugation Hall, his debt to Danxia Peak will be directly halved!"

"Oh?!" Tuoba Huang's eyes lit up. He sat up straight, no longer petting the tiger.

He burst into laughter, heavily slapping the armrest twice. "Zhong Dao appreciates him so much, but this kid doesn't give a damn about him at all. Interesting! Haha, I really want to see the expression on Zhong Dao's face when he hears this news."

After Tuoba Huang finished rejoicing, the subordinate added, "Peak Lord, please hear me out. While Zhong Dao lost face, I'm afraid our Myriad Beasts Peak is also in danger of the same."

Tuoba Huang frowned. "Explain clearly."

The subordinate said, "Our peak's elder, Cang Yazi, is also Ning Zhuo's creditor and participated in this Southern Ming minor trial. If on the day Ning Zhuo establishes his alliance, Cang Yazi runs over to join, it will look very bad."

Tuoba Huang's face instantly darkened.

He first fell into a period of silence, then said, "Cang Yazi... this old geezer has an eccentric temperament. Although it's unlikely, we can't rule out the possibility."

Cang Yazi was his man. If he really lost his mind and joined Ning Zhuo's side, how would the outside world view it?

Outsiders would say: Tuoba Huang has lost the people's support! Even Cang Yazi isn't willing to follow him, preferring a Foundation Establishment cultivator instead. The current Myriad Beasts Peak Lord is nothing more than this!

Tuoba Huang suddenly issued an order: "Have Han Muma handle this."

"Isn't he Cang Yazi's good friend? When Cang Yazi borrowed money this time, he was one of Cang Yazi's creditors."

"Let him go to Ning Zhuo's alliance establishment ceremony as a spectator. Not in the name of Myriad Beasts Peak, but in his own personal capacity."

"Tell him not to let Cang Yazi's joining happen. I will not allow it!"

The subordinate hurriedly nodded. "Understood, Lord Peak Lord."

Rumors regarding Ning Zhuo's alliance building, under Chen San's secret instigation, spread wider and wider.

When Wang Yu's disciple Jiang Ping learned of it, he revealed an expression of admiration.

"Ning Zhuo's methods are actually so outstanding, using his creditors for his own use. I am inferior!"

"No wonder Master values him so much."

Alchemy Hall, underground Cultivation Chamber.

The stone door was tightly shut. Inside the room, there was only a stone bed and a low table.

Shen Hongyao sat cross-legged on the stone bed, wearing plain clothes with her hair down; her face looked slightly thinner.

Formation runes lit up.

"Deacon Shen, the food has been delivered." A voice came from outside the door, carrying a fawning tone.

Shen Hongyao snorted coldly.

"Lord Deacon, please take your time to enjoy. I will come back to collect it in half a sichen."

A teleportation formation activated, and a food box appeared out of thin air on the low table, containing today's Spirit Food.

Shen Hongyao: "Hold on. Tell me about the recent news happening outside."

Ever since she was eliminated from the Nanming Fire Furnace minor trial, she had been punished with solitary confinement in the Alchemy Hall due to an exploding furnace.

Locked in the stone chamber all day without stepping out, only able to cultivate behind closed doors, Shen Hongyao felt quite depressed and bored.

The cultivator delivering her meals was the only person she could interact with, so naturally, she wouldn't let him go easily.

The cultivator was also willing to seize this opportunity to build a closer relationship with Shen Hongyao. After all, she was a Deacon of the Alchemy Hall.

Their idle chatter couldn't last too long; soon, the meal-delivering cultivator took the initiative to leave.

Shen Hongyao was left alone in the room and began to eat the Spirit Food.

She chewed loudly on purpose, clinking the soup spoon, chopsticks, and rice bowl together to dispel the silence in the stone chamber.

However, the more of these sounds she made, the more irritable Shen Hongyao became.

She threw the spoon into the soup bowl, emitting a crisp clatter as spiritual soup splashed everywhere.

"Ning Zhuo..." Shen Hongyao furrowed her brows. She had just learned from the idle chatter the intelligence that Ning Zhuo was gathering his creditors to establish an alliance to brave Flowing Cloud Peak.

Shen Hongyao's chest heaved erratically; she harbored complex emotions toward Ning Zhuo.

She had pinned her hopes on obtaining the Nanming Fire Furnace to let herself take a step forward in the Alchemy Hall. She had failed, and Ning Zhuo was the sole victor.

Now, Ning Zhuo was relying on the furnace to thrive and continue shining brightly. Meanwhile, she was stuck here, locked in solitary confinement, sidelined on the cold bench.

Why had it turned out like this?

"I merely caused a furnace explosion once. It's not like it hasn't happened in the past. Why is the punishment so severe this time?"

Shen Hongyao's face was gloomy, her heart filled with indignation.

"This is probably Jiang Ping suppressing me. He just found an excuse to lock me in solitary confinement as a warning not to covet his things."

"I really envy you, Ning Zhuo." Shen Hongyao was almost jealous in this moment.

She envied his freedom, envied his lack of scruples, envied that autonomy where he could freely express himself.

In comparison, the Alchemy Hall felt like a cage!

Ancient Tomb Black Market.

Yun Youzi's stall was still as inconspicuous as ever.

His good friend, the middle-aged cultivator who had persuaded him to participate in the furnace minor trial, arrived in front of the stall with a smile on his face. "You came back so soon? Why didn't you stay in the Sect for a few more days?"

Yun Youzi shrugged and lazily replied, "It's too noisy over there; it doesn't suit me. It's much quieter here."

The middle-aged cultivator fell silent for a moment. "Regarding the matter of the furnace, it wasn't that your abilities were lacking, but your financial power was insufficient. I heard you also emptied out your life savings and poured them into it?"

Yun Youzi: "It was just restoring the furnace a little. Being able to make some contribution to that alchemy furnace makes me happy."

"As for that Ning Zhuo, although I haven't interacted with him, he should be quite alright. A cultivator who can wield the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone cannot be bad. With the furnace in his hands, it has found a wise master."

The middle-aged cultivator hesitated, wanting to say something but stopping himself.

Yun Youzi chuckled. "I know what you want to ask. Yes, the Alchemy Hall contacted me and expressed their intention to have me return. But I refused."

"You..." The middle-aged cultivator was stunned.

Yun Youzi revealed a knowing smile. "I understand what you're thinking. Even if I failed in the competition for the furnace, I could use my performance to re-enter the sights of those people, reminding them that the Alchemy Hall still has a figure like me."

"However, old friend."

"The way I see it, the Alchemy Hall is no different from a cage. The rules are too strict, and the higher the position, the fewer the slots. Too many people are fighting over them."

"Sigh! You, oh you." The middle-aged cultivator shook his head and sighed deeply.

After a few more exchanges, right before the middle-aged cultivator left: "Oh, right. That Ning Zhuo is using the furnace as a selling point to gather his creditors, form an alliance, and occupy Flowing Cloud Peak. To think a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator is actually forging ahead so valiantly. You are simply too much of a free-spirited hermit. If only you could have a little bit of his enterprising spirit."

"You are also one of his creditors. Do you want to go join the fun?"

Yun Youzi shook his head and said weakly, "Too much of a hassle, not going, not going."

"I'm leaving, leaving." The middle-aged cultivator waved his hand, not looking back.

In his heart, however, he had firmly decided to dispatch a messenger to represent Yun Youzi and contact Ning Zhuo. At the very least, they had to secure the debt.

The Confucian cultivator group of the Myriad Manifestations Sect had continuously kept a close eye on Ning Zhuo.

The incident regarding Zhao Hansheng and Qin De had dealt them a rather heavy blow. The public sentiment stirred up by the Nanming Fire Furnace minor trial had shifted the masses' attention away from them, which also somewhat eased their pressure.

Chu Xuangui: "This intelligence is so detailed and abundant in particulars; it shouldn't be fake. Is Ning Zhuo really going to do this?"

Li Guanyu: "This is indeed something he is capable of doing."

Nangong Zhi: "This is a major matter. I have already sent a letter to Daoist Wen Ruanyu."

Situ Gu: "At the end of the day, Ning Zhuo is not a Confucian cultivator. Why, from the sound of your intentions, do you still want to get involved?"

Duanmu Zhang: "Ning Zhuo possesses youthful spirit and has entered the game himself; his courage is commendable. When the day of establishing the alliance arrives, we only need to send one representative to attend the ceremony."

As the day drew closer, more related news spread.

"Ning Zhuo has already booked the entire Heavenly Treasure Pavilion to hold the alliance establishment ceremony there?" After Ban Jie learned of this intelligence, her brows furrowed deeply.

At both of her temples, there were black copper eyebrow piercings embedded as gear pivots, whirring as they rotated autonomously.

"With such movements, I'm afraid this matter is practically certain."

"The problem now is, just how many creditors has he secretly reached agreements with, such that he now has the confidence to make a move like booking the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion?"

"And how should our clan face this?"

The more Ban Jie thought about it, the more her head ached.

Ning Zhuo was Ban Ji's Royal Mandate Human Tribulation. If he really succeeded in establishing this alliance, wouldn't Ban Ji have to face a massive horde of creditors when dealing with Ning Zhuo in the future?

This move played by Ning Zhuo was simply too brilliant, turning numerous Nascent Soul and Golden Core cultivators into his protective umbrella.

Ever since Ban Jie obtained this intelligence, she had hoped from the bottom of her heart that this was false news, merely a rumor.

But as the day drew nearer, she understood that this hope was futile; she had to face this cruel reality!

"With Ning Zhuo establishing an alliance, can our side form coalitions to counter it?"

Ban Jie had just formed her first thought when she quickly dispelled it.

Ning Zhuo had done very well in this aspect. Although he stole the spotlight, he didn't provoke disgust. There weren't many people who truly had a grudge against him.

The most likely cultivator Ban Jie could think of teaming up with was Pi Fujie from the Pi Clan. But persuading Pi Fujie to deal with the alliance stirred up by Ning Zhuo? This possibility was miniscule, not to mention others.

"It's better to seek help from the clan first." Just as Ban Jie thought this, a messenger from the clan visited.

The messenger brought a heavy treasure, as well as the Ban Clan's intent: "Our clan is currently devoting all its efforts to repairing the Clan Destiny Pivot Chain; this is a matter of paramount importance. Leave Ning Zhuo for Ban Ji to resolve. This treasure will assist Ban Ji in growing stronger!"

And so.

When Ban Jie brought the heavy treasure to find Ban Ji, Ban Ji wore a smug smile: "Strange, how did you know that yesterday I used up the last of the Hundred Refinements Golden Body Pills, thoroughly digesting them, and that my strength has surged significantly?"

"How is Ning Zhuo?"

"This time, if I battle him, my certainty of victory will increase by twenty percent!"

Ban Jie fell silent for a moment: "The situation is a bit complicated..."
