Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 877: The Great Serpent Scythe's Abnormal Movement!

Ning Zhuo almost paled in shock.

For the Mechanism Ring to give such a warning, it had to be a massive crisis threatening his very life!

"But what exactly is going on?"

Ning Zhuo looked completely bewildered, utterly clueless about this life-threatening crisis.

The last time, he did what he did because a mysterious Nascent Soul cultivator attacked, giving him obvious clues.

This time, he was just sitting at home when disaster suddenly fell from the sky.

This left him with no idea where to start.

"Could it be because I borrowed the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone and hung it in front of my cave dwelling? Should I take it down?"

The ring continued to shrink.

"Or is it a lingering consequence of that mysterious Nascent Soul cultivator? Do I need to extinguish the spiritual energy as well?"

The ring was still shrinking.

In just a few breaths, cold sweat began to seep from Ning Zhuo's forehead.

Just as he was at a loss for what to do, he felt an unusual fluctuation coming from his Storage Belt.

Ning Zhuo swept his divine sense over it and found it was a red flower.

"The Red Flower Dharma Aspect, Wu Hen?" Ning Zhuo couldn't help but feel both shocked and delighted.

He had taken two captives in the Two Note Kingdom, one named Wu Hen and the other named Lin You. The former cultivated the Strange Path and had become entirely mutated, completely losing his human form. If not for the sealing suppression of the Red Flower Dharma Aspect, he would have undergone Dao assimilation on the spot.

Ning Zhuo was pleasantly surprised because he felt he had found the source of his life-threatening crisis.

"Wu Hen cultivates the Strange Path; the power of his Dao assimilation would be immense. If I hadn't noticed, I definitely would have fallen victim to it."

"To think that at this critical juncture, the Red Flower Dharma Aspect would be unable to hold on and is about to shatter."

Ning Zhuo had already decided on a plan. He directly left the Cultivation Chamber and entered the Formation Space.

The Bluestone Cave Dwelling had a martial drill formation. The resulting Formation Space was weak on the outside but had strong inner walls, making it suitable for martial practice.

Of course, its durability for practice was limited, so Ning Zhuo couldn't exert his full strength. This place had once been reduced to an arena for Qing Chi and Snow-Colored Maiden Hui to compete against each other in talisman crafting.

Ning Zhuo immediately ordered Qing Chi to leave. He placed the Red Flower Dharma Aspect imprisoning Wu Hen in the martial drill arena and then promptly retreated as well.

"This way, even if Dao assimilation occurs, the martial drill formation can intercept it for a moment."

"After the incident, I'll let the people from the Myriad Manifestations Sect handle the mess."

"I'm willing to pay for the damages!"

The explanation Ning Zhuo had long prepared for outsiders was very simple: he had stumbled upon a fortuitous encounter, but unexpectedly, this Strange Path treasure suddenly mutated, endangering the surroundings.

That was just how Strange Path treasures were - unreasonable and defying common sense.

This actually made it exceptionally easy for Ning Zhuo to explain himself.

However!

Even after Ning Zhuo abandoned the Red Flower Dharma Aspect containing Wu Hen, the Mechanism Ring on his finger continued to shrink, crushing his finger until it ached.

Ning Zhuo narrowed his eyes. "Could it be that the power of this Dao assimilation is too strong, and I must retreat much further away?"

But just as Ning Zhuo was about to pack his things and call his subordinates to flee together, the ring tightened even more.

Ning Zhuo immediately stopped his thoughts of retreating, and the ring actually loosened slightly.

"Fleeing is a mistake?"

"Could there be an external enemy?"

"But this is inside the main headquarters of the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Private fights are forbidden here!"

"Or could my retreating actions leak some crucial information, or give an enemy some kind of opening?"

Near the Bluestone Cave Dwelling, Xiao Juxia hid his figure, continuously capturing Ning Zhuo's lingering aura. He smiled faintly. "That's enough."

His last divination had cost a massive price.

To perform consecutive divinations this time, even if not to a profound degree, required careful calculation since his lifespan was limited.

The more clues Xiao Juxia collected, the smaller the price he would have to pay.

Thus, he hadn't hesitated to come near the Bluestone Cave Dwelling to collect Ning Zhuo's residual aura as a medium.

"For this boy to actually advance to the third round, it shows he has some kind of affinity with the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"I just don't know if it's a good affinity or a sinful one."

"It matters not. This kid doesn't have the means to conceal his own destiny. With a little calculation, I will find out."

Xiao Juxia formally began his divination.

Inside the Bluestone Cave Dwelling, Ning Zhuo frantically tried various ideas, but the Mechanism Ring only tightened, showing no other anomalies.

"In any case, I'll have Boss and the others retreat first and stay away from me. Even if I meet with disaster in the future, they'll have a higher chance of surviving. Or more likely, they can lend a hand to save me."

Ning Zhuo pondered over and over, his thoughts consuming rapidly within his divine sea. His mind churned almost like a storm, yet he still couldn't guess the reason.

If the Supreme Clan Elders of the Ban Family used the Clan Destiny Pivot Chain to observe at this moment, they would be horrified to find that Ning Zhuo's destiny was once again in a precarious situation, extremely dangerous. Because of this, Ning Zhuo's secondary fate star was stimulated!

This fate star corresponded to the Strange Path.

The Great Serpent Scythe began to show abnormal movement!

Ning Zhuo noticed it immediately, almost thinking it was an illusion.

But soon, he confirmed, "It's the Great Serpent Scythe showing abnormal movement!"

He couldn't help but feel a mix of surprise and joy!

The main goal of Ning Zhuo's travels across the world was to save his mother. But Meng Yaoyin's injuries involved the Strange Path. During her most recent awakening, almost as if leaving her last words, she had told Ning Zhuo many things and plans.

She asked Ning Zhuo to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect, find her belongings from when she was alive, and when the Great Serpent Scythe showed abnormal movement, use the Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra to burn them to replenish her spiritual energy.

Ning Zhuo put all his effort into it and completed it beautifully. Not long after initially joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he established a firm foothold, opened up the situation, sneaked into the Shuyu Studio, and directly completed the most important task assigned by his mother.

Afterward, while participating in the Rising Cloud Minot Trial to scheme for future layouts, he secretly waited for the Great Serpent Scythe's abnormal movement.

How many times had he hoped for this situation to occur, yet he never expected that when it finally did, he would be in such a moment of crisis.

"I can't find a way to avoid the danger, so I might as well wake up my mother first."

"Mother should have a way."

"Even if she doesn't, I will at least do everything in my power to help her before I die!"

Regardless of how much the ring dug into his hand, Ning Zhuo immediately took Sun Lingtong and the others into the Ten Thousand-Li Traversing Dragon.

Then, Ning Zhuo stayed alone in an empty cabin. It had long been set up, filled with Meng Yaoyin's relics from her past life.

Ning Zhuo took out Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin and carefully placed her in the center.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin was severely damaged.

Half of her body was missing; half an arm and a left leg had completely disappeared. From the massive damage, the internal mechanisms could be seen, completely twisted and deformed. At many of the wounds, the spiritual networks were broken into pieces, hanging down like torn silk ribbons.

The Jade Marrow Spirit Liquid, condensed with faint Buddhist Light, had entirely leaked out through the cracks.

There were scorched black holes, cracks formed by freezing ice, and several terrifying penetrating wounds.

What frightened Ning Zhuo the most was that he couldn't perceive any spiritual energy within Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin's body at all.

Ning Zhuo had previously thought about repairing her, but after considering her spiritual energy, he suppressed the urge and refrained from acting.

On one hand, Meng Yaoyin's last words hadn't specifically instructed him to do so. On the other hand, Ning Zhuo's concerns were similar to the situation with the Nanming Fire Furnace.

To repair the Nanming Fire Furnace, he needed the acknowledgment and cooperation of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

But the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit had been severely frightened. A Cultivator needed to rebuild trust with it and guide it to cooperate.

Methods like sealing the artifact spirit were unadvisable.

A Nanming Fire Furnace repaired in such a manner would be incompatible with the artifact spirit. At best, it would weaken the spirit; at worst, it would obliterate it.

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was originally born from the Nanming Fire Furnace; they were an integrated whole. This was different from Yuan Dasheng, Meng Yehu, and the others. Their spiritual energy came from external sources, merely lodging within mechanical bodies.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin's situation was rather unique.

Back then, she threw herself into the furnace within the Lava Immortal Palace, and upon emerging, she became Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin.

Her connection to her mechanical body was far tighter than that of Yuan Dasheng or Meng Yehu. At the very least, Ning Zhuo had never seen his mother's spiritual energy escape to inhabit other objects.

If Ning Zhuo casually repaired her, causing drastic changes to the mechanical body, it would likely endanger Meng Yaoyin's spiritual energy.

This possibility had stayed his hand until now.

After placing Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin down, Ning Zhuo immediately sat cross-legged and impatiently activated the Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra.

Since cultivating this Buddhist scripture, he had performed many cremations, but this was the first time he felt such urgency.

Ning Zhuo's heart beat rapidly, and even his hands were trembling slightly.

Flames ignited, enveloping all the relics in the cabin.

The Fire Element Heart Temple also shone brightly, precisely controlling every single wisp of flame.

The flames danced within the cabin. The intense burning didn't produce foul smoke, but rather a faint, warm aura akin to sunlight.

Tiny specks of spiritual light began to drift up.

They were very weak, so faint they were almost invisible. Like fireflies in the dark night, they flickered on and off, seemingly ready to extinguish at any moment.

The spiritual light drifted mid-air, like a lost child searching for the way home in the darkness.

They floated for a while, then slowly, very slowly, drifted towards the central Mechanical Puppet - Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin.

More and more spiritual light consecutively descended into the puppet, illuminating Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin so that her entire body sparkled with spiritual light, like a Bodhisattva statue bathed in Buddhist Light.

The relics were quickly completely incinerated. A massive amount of spiritual light fell into Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin, entirely swallowed up as if plunging into a dark abyss.

Both the spiritual light and the firelight dissipated.

The cabin returned to tranquility, with only a faint, warm aura lingering in the air.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin sat in the center of the cabin, completely motionless.

Ning Zhuo's heart couldn't help but sink. Infinite panic filled his eyes. "Impossible... it couldn't have failed, right?!"

However, the next moment, like a breeze brushing across a lake, a dewdrop sliding off a lotus leaf, or a flower petal gently falling...

A very, very soft voice subtly lingered by Ning Zhuo's ear, rippling through his heart.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin slowly opened her eyes. "Little Zhuo."

In an instant, Ning Zhuo burst into tears, crying out, "Mother!"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin weakly lifted her only remaining arm, and her entire mechanical body floated into mid-air.

She flew in front of Ning Zhuo, extending her palm to gently stroke his large head. "My son, you did well. Much better than Mother expected."

Ning Zhuo immediately revealed a proud expression, slightly raising his head. "Mother, I..."

"Don't move." Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin interrupted him, and then a burst of strange spiritual energy erupted from her palm.

The spiritual energy shrouded Ning Zhuo's entire body, permeating into his Physical Body and Soul, before disappearing without a trace.

However, Ning Zhuo's greatest sensation came from the Mechanism Ring.

To his amazement, he found that the Mechanism Ring, which had been shrinking all this time, suddenly loosened and returned to its original state.

Ning Zhuo looked up at Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin again, his large eyes wide with curiosity. "Mother, what on earth is going on?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin said, "You've been targeted by someone who is divining your fate."

"Fortunately, the person hasn't calculated to the core yet, only reaching the surface layer."

"Coincidentally, I am also skilled in this Path."

"The timing was perfect. The other party calculated a phased result and will naturally believe the subsequent divination content. But in reality, the answers have already been distorted by me."

Meanwhile, Xiao Juxia chuckled and shook his head slightly. "Ning Zhuo's Qi Luck is robust. Such a figure is naturally favored by spiritual treasures. No wonder he easily cleared the rounds twice in a row."

"If anyone were to obtain Qin De's legacy, even with a Royal Mandate, they would be weighed down by such a heavy treasure, causing their destiny to plummet."

"Therefore, Ning Zhuo is not the target I am looking for."

Xiao Juxia came quickly and left just as hurriedly.

He had dozens of other figures that he needed to approach and divine one by one.

Bluestone Cave Dwelling.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin slowly withdrew her palm. "I've successfully deceived them. No one is calculating you anymore."

Ning Zhuo asked curiously, "Mother, who was calculating me? Why did it trigger a fatal crisis?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin replied, "The person has an extremely powerful background. I only sensed it faintly and didn't dare to trace it back at all, for fear of alerting them."

Pausing for a moment, she added, "It should be someone from the Demonic Path."

Hearing this, Ning Zhuo sighed. "When I grow up in the future, I will definitely be a pillar of the Righteous Path, utterly irreconcilable with Demonic Cultivators. By not killing me today, he missed a golden opportunity. Once my strength becomes formidable, I will certainly repay him."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin chuckled softly. "Little Zhuo, having taken the Buddha Heart Demon Seal, you will inevitably be entangled in the karma and destiny of the Buddhist Sect and the Demonic Sect. In the future, you won't be able to avoid dealing with these two types of cultivators. From now on, whether you choose to destroy the Buddha and support demons, or eliminate demons to defend the Dao, there will be plenty of opportunities."

Ning Zhuo was astonished. "Mother, what are you saying? How could we of the Righteous Path support Demonic Cultivators?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin shook her head. "Sometimes, you will find that those monks of the Buddhist Sect are far more detestable than Demonic Cultivators."

Ning Zhuo was more concerned about Meng Yaoyin herself. "Mother, your body is broken, but how is your spiritual energy? Why can I still not perceive any of your spiritual energy inside your mechanical shell?"

Meng Yaoyin said, "This time I found a blessing in disguise and comprehended the profound mysteries of the Strange Path. It's completely normal that you cannot perceive my spiritual energy."

"Little Zhuo, tell Mother everything you've experienced recently... Hm? My spiritual intuition has been triggered. Why is there a fortuitous encounter suited for me nearby?"

Ning Zhuo paused for a moment, then immediately led Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin out of the Ten Thousand-Li Traversing Dragon and into the Formation Space of the martial drill formation.

The Red Flower Dharma Aspect imprisoning Wu Hen was on the verge of undergoing Dao assimilation in the very next second.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin laughed out loud. "Wonderful! This fortuitous encounter was actually brought by you, Little Zhuo."

She moved her divine sense, and the Great Serpent Scythe swirled and danced, cutting through the air before landing on the Red Flower Dharma Aspect. Its sharp tip tapped lightly.

The Red Flower Dharma Aspect instantly collapsed, but Wu Hen was compressed into a chaotic, muddled bead. Dark shadows flowed within it, bizarre and inexplicable.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin opened her mouth and inhaled, sucking the Wu Hen bead into her stomach.

Reaching out with a grasping motion, the Great Serpent Scythe swirled and danced once more, landing firmly in her palm.
