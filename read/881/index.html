Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 881: Ning Zhuo Makes His Move

The ownership of the Nanming Fire Furnace involved many significant interests. But in Shen Hongyao's heart, it was more about a thirst for progress.

Today, Shen Hongyao still wore a red dress, looking like a cluster of burning flames.

Her pace was fast, her skirt fluttering behind her like a butterfly.

She walked up to the pink pillar of light, stopped, reached into her sleeve, and directly took out an alchemy furnace.

The furnace wasn't large, about half a person's height, with three legs and two handles. It was dark red all over, its body engraved with red peony patterns. Two bronze bells hung from the handles, and vine-like patterns coiled around its legs.

There were several scorch marks on its body, a few cracks on the handles, and patches of repairs on the legs.

In truth, this was a weather-beaten alchemy furnace that had followed Shen Hongyao for decades. It had experienced countless furnace explosions and had been repaired by her time and time again.

Under everyone's watchful eyes, Shen Hongyao set up the alchemy furnace, took a top-grade Fire Element Spirit Stone from her sleeve, and tossed it into the bottom of the furnace.

The Spirit Stone was activated, transforming into a ball of crimson flame that licked the bottom of the furnace.

Then, she began to take out the medicinal herbs.

Hundred-year Reishi mushroom, thousand-year Fleeceflower root, Dragon Blood Grass, Phoenix Tail Flower, Ice Soul Lotus Seeds, Fire Dates, Jade Dew, Snow Ginseng...

Her movements in retrieving the medicine were fast and crisp, like a military doctor bandaging wounds on a battlefield - without any sluggishness or hesitation.

She threw these medicinal ingredients into the alchemy furnace one by one and began the refining process. Her actions were so straightforward they almost seemed rough.

"She's going to refine a Life-Sustaining Pill," a voice flashed in Jiang Ping's heart.

Qingbaizi narrowed his eyes slightly, thinking, "The Life-Sustaining Pill is indeed Deacon Shen's most proficient pill recipe."

Qin Ke also observed carefully, pondering secretly, "Shen Hongyao's refinement of the Life-Sustaining Pill is absolutely top-notch; no one in the Alchemy Hall can match her. Even though it's the exact same pill recipe, her yield is inexplicably thirty percent higher, and the medicinal efficacy is three times better."

"Of course, the probability of her furnace exploding is also more than three times higher."

With the ingredients mostly processed, Shen Hongyao began refining the pills.

She grabbed a handful of Fire Element Spirit Stones and threw them directly into the bottom of the furnace to intensify the spiritual fire.

Then, she formed hand seals. Her ten fingers fluttered like butterflies as she executed over a dozen alchemy spells in rapid succession.

The flames beneath the furnace grew fiercer. The furnace walls were burned a bright red, the bronze bells on the handles jingled loudly under the impact of the heatwaves, and the vine patterns on the legs twisted slightly in the high temperature, looking as if they were melting.

Shen Hongyao had refined the Life-Sustaining Pill so many times that the entire process was ingrained in her bones. She could practically refine it with her eyes closed.

"Ordinary alchemy methods are unremarkable, but this time, I'm using my self-created 'Hongyao Pill Compression' technique."

This was an alchemy spell named after herself.

The core was not "refining," but "compressing." By utilizing the furnace to compress the medicinal power to its absolute limit, both the pill success rate and the pills' quality would see a massive increase.

The harder the compression, the better the medicinal efficacy, but at the same time, the easier it was for the furnace to explode.

Bang.

Without any surprises, this alchemy session ended with the furnace exploding.

A large chunk burst open. The bronze bells on the handles were shaken loose, rolling across the floor with crisp ringing sounds. Several scorched black cracks appeared on the furnace body, radiating outward from the hole.

Shards of the furnace scattered and flew out, but in an instant, they were caught by the Formation and easily suppressed.

A thick plume of smoke rose from the hole in the furnace, and the air was quickly filled with a mix of burning and medicinal smells.

Several holes were burned into Shen Hongyao's red dress, her hair was singed by the flames, and her arms, face, and neck were covered in burn marks. A trace of fresh blood leaked from the corner of her mouth. Although she looked wretched, the corners of her mouth curled up, revealing a clear smile.

Her injuries were not light.

It didn't matter.

Under everyone's astonished gazes, she retrieved the freshly refined Life-Sustaining Pills from the furnace.

The surfaces of the pills were still scalding hot to the touch.

Shen Hongyao picked one up, popped it into her mouth, and swallowed it on the spot.

The moment the pill entered her stomach, a surge of warm medicinal power was digested and gushed forth, spreading throughout her body along her meridians.

Wherever the medicinal power reached, Shen Hongyao's burned skin began to heal, her singed hair fell out and regenerated, her severed meridians directly reconnected, and her depleted spiritual energy also began to recover rapidly.

Shen Hongyao's complexion turned from pale to rosy, and her breathing went from ragged to steady.

Her injuries were healed.

She stood up straight, patted the dust off her skirt, smoothed out her messy hair, and then stared intensely at the pink pillar of light with burning eyes.

She was running a tally in her heart: "This batch produced a total of thirty-nine Life-Sustaining Pills. I only ate one. If I sell the remaining thirty-eight, even factoring in the cost of repairing the furnace, the funds I earn will be more than enough to buy three furnaces like this one."

"Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit, did you see that?" she communicated, transmitting her thoughts via her divine sense.

"Did you see my alchemy furnace? I have never abandoned it, sticking with it through thick and thin."

"Furnace explosions are no big deal."

"Don't be afraid."

As if responding to her, ripples once again appeared on the pink pillar of light.

"Could it be that acting like this can also move the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit?" Mu Yuehua narrowed her eyes.

Chunyangzi: "Don't tell me she's going to surpass me?"

Chunyangzi's worries did not come true.

Ultimately, when Shen Hongyao stepped down, the hosting Cultivator announced that the best results still belonged to Chunyangzi and Mu Yuehua.

Shen Hongyao sucked in a breath of cold air and walked back into the crowd of participants, looking thoroughly depressed.

This demeanor angered Wang Yu so much that he wanted to hit her.

"I really want to crack open your skull and see what exactly is going on in your head!"

"The Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit is absolutely terrified specifically because of alchemy furnace explosions. Yet you just had to reenact one right in front of it?"

"And you purposely showed off a self-created alchemy technique - the exact kind that increases the probability of a furnace explosion?"

Wang Yu didn't know what to say.

There was absolutely no way Shen Hongyao could resonate with the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit, but Wang Yu's consideration was that she was a member of the main team and needed to be protected.

"The third round isn't enough; the fourth round can determine the final victor."

"I'll do my best to ensure our own people advance to the next round first."

This was what Wang Yu was considering.

As for Chunyangzi, the previous second-place participant, it was also his behind-the-scenes manipulation that allowed Chunyangzi to take "first place."

The reason Chunyangzi tied with Mu Yuehua was also that Wang Yu had developed a deep appreciation for Mu Yuehua's talents.

Hosting Cultivator: "Next, Daoist Friend Ning Zhuo."

Ning Zhuo was exactly the person who had drawn the fifth jade tally.

A massive amount of attention focused on the big-headed youth.

Ning Zhuo slowly stepped out of the crowd and walked toward the central pillar of light.

In the shadows, Wang Yu cast his gaze over: "For Ning Zhuo to draw the fifth position, his personal Qi Luck isn't bad. Hmm... The fact that he's highly regarded by Zhong Dao and Tuoba Huang also proves this point."

"For him, there's no need for me to act in secret."

"This kid does have talent in the field of alchemy. But if our Danxia Peak wants to recruit him, we'll inevitably have to offer more chips than Zhong Dao."

"If he joins the Demon Subjugation Hall and grows in the future, he's bound to become one of Zhong Dao's capable generals. Just like Yang Sanyan."

Thoughts continuously churned in Wang Yu's mind.

Poaching Ning Zhuo and cutting off the possibility of him joining the Demon Subjugation Hall in advance wasn't out of the question.

But the price to be paid for that required Wang Yu's careful consideration.

After all, Ning Zhuo wasn't the only genius here. In Wang Yu's eyes, Mu Yuehua was a genius Cultivator far more suited for Danxia Peak.

If he were forced to choose between the two, he would definitely choose Mu Yuehua.

The reason he would consider Ning Zhuo was more about weakening Zhong Dao. And this weakening would only manifest in the future. A much more efficient method, in reality, was to cultivate their own disciples, such as the likes of Mu Yuehua.

Wang Yu's attitude toward Ning Zhuo had always been one of standing by and watching.

The fact that Ning Zhuo had passed the first two rounds was also entirely due to his own abilities.

Today, Ning Zhuo wore a white Daoist robe. His mid-stage Foundation Establishment cultivation base was considered at the bottom among the crowd participating in the minor trial.

Upon seeing him, relevant intelligence surfaced in the divine seas of many people.

With this minor trial for the Nanming Fire Furnace advancing to the third round, basically everyone's general intelligence was well understood by one another.

Ning Zhuo was no exception to the others.

"This kid is one of the top-tier genius Cultivators to emerge from this session of the Flying Cloud Assembly?"

"Indeed young and promising."

"I heard this kid possesses quite a few Cultivation arts, but he's only at the mid-stage of Foundation Establishment... That point is his flaw."

"He gained Zhong Dao's favor but hasn't joined him; clearly, he has his own ideas."

"Although his alchemy skills still lack a lot of refinement, the fact that he made it to the third round means there's something to be said about him."

"His background is mysterious and seems to run deep. If he takes out some sort of Fire Seed, it might be possible for him to surpass Chunyangzi."

"Right now it's only the fifth place; it's still way too early to tell."

For a time, all sorts of thoughts and ideas swirled in the hearts of the crowd.

Ning Zhuo walked up to the pink pillar of light and stopped.

He took out an item from his Storage Belt.

It was a segment of bone.

It was about three inches long. It wasn't an eerie white, withered bone, but instead exhibited the texture of warm, smooth white jade. Resting there, it looked like a section of white jade bamboo - its lines were straight and upright, its joints distinct.

The expressions of everyone present underwent slight changes.

And further out, the many spectators watching this minor trial began to discuss fervently.

"Is that... the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone?"

"That's a heavy treasure of the Demon Subjugation Hall! How did it appear in Ning Zhuo's hands again?"

"You're ill-informed, aren't you? Last night, someone saw this profound treasure hanging above the entrance of the Blue Stone Cave Dwelling."

"Doesn't that mean the Demon Subjugation Hall is standing behind Ning Zhuo?"

"To put it another way might be more accurate - the Demon Subjugation Hall is also interested in the Nanming Fire Furnace, so they sponsored Ning Zhuo to vie for this Spirit Treasure-level alchemy furnace!"

Zhu Fenxiang pressed her lips tightly together.

To win this third round, she had paid a heavy price last night to successfully execute a Divine Summoning.

She had only secretly formed an alliance with Ning Zhuo, while outwardly maintaining a strict distance from him. At the same time, she didn't want to see him gain a massive advantage either.

The appearance of the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone also drew Mu Yuehua's gaze.

This genius female cultivator of the Yuehua Sect also felt tense.

Currently, she was tied for first place with Chunyangzi. In reality, if calculated strictly, she was still slightly weaker. Fortunately, Chunyangzi respected the Myriad Manifestations Sect and didn't make an issue of it.

If Ning Zhuo's performance overshadowed hers, then she would obviously be eliminated. This, of course, wasn't the result Mu Yuehua wanted.

As soon as Ning Zhuo took out the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone, he quickly dispelled any feelings or thoughts of underestimation from everyone present.

The big-headed youth's expression was placid as he poured his spiritual energy into the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone.

A platinum radiance gushed forth, like the warm sun on a winter's day, quickly enveloping Ning Zhuo's entire body.

The light was soft yet resolute, neither dazzling nor overbearing, but possessing a presence that could not be ignored. It was like a lamp lit in the darkness, or a flag raised on a wasteland.

More and more platinum radiance surged, almost forming a spherical cluster of light that completely encased Ning Zhuo's figure within.

Within the light cluster, Ning Zhuo's silhouette could be faintly seen - he had straightened his back, standing proudly and unyielding like a green pine tree in a snowstorm.

When Situ Xing saw this scene, his eyes contracted slightly, concealing the wariness in the depths of his heart.

Dong Nishang, on the other hand, revealed a smile. She was one of the pillars among the Confucian cultivators, with a Golden Core cultivation base, and she held genuine fondness and goodwill for Ning Zhuo from the bottom of her heart.

Especially seeing that Ning Zhuo did not major in Confucian methods, yet was able to utilize the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone so outstandingly, it vastly increased her appreciation for this junior.

"This kid's temperament is indeed not bad."

"A rising star of the Righteous Path... No wonder Zhong Dao regards him so highly."

"Mhm, he's a youth of good upbringing."

Even though they were in a competitive relationship with Ning Zhuo, many Cultivators let go of much of their wariness upon seeing this scene.

Wang Yu had seen much more exaggerated situations: "Ning Zhuo hasn't even used his full strength yet. But looking at it right now, even if he did, it would be to no effect."

Wang Yu controlled the Formation, so his understanding of the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit's reactions was as clear as looking at a fire.

He was extremely clear that although Ning Zhuo had unleashed an aura of Noble Righteousness, highlighting his underlying character and his alignment with the Righteous Path faction, it had not earned the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit's approval.

Wang Yu guessed: "An artifact spirit is still an artifact spirit, its way of thinking is different from a human's. It might even disdain the Noble Righteous Qi for being overly rigid and vast, posing a different kind of threat."

Wang Yu found it somewhat amusing.

At this moment, in his eyes, Ning Zhuo was pretty much the same as the cold-faced Cultivator who went first earlier.

"To be able to reach this step can already be considered quite good."

Seeing that the pink pillar of light remained motionless, several Cultivators in the arena cast aside their worries and withdrew their gazes.

Jiang Ping looked thoughtful, suddenly realizing something: according to the order of drawing lots, the Cultivators positioned later actually had a slight advantage. This was because the earlier Cultivators had tried various methods.

All these trial and errors provided those who were in line later with a wealth of precious intelligence, allowing them to form clearer and more accurate judgments regarding the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit's likes and dislikes.

"Unless, the preceding Cultivators were able to make the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit directly acknowledge them as its master. If that happened, no matter what methods the succeeding Cultivators had, they would be powerless to do anything."

Thinking up to this point, Jiang Ping laughed in his own heart first: "But given the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit's current state, how could that be possible?"

Ning Zhuo didn't panic in the slightest in his heart.

His expression was calm, and his mood was tranquil, reflecting on the encounter from last night: "Mother's deductions were not wrong. The Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit has no reaction to my Noble Righteous Qi."

It wasn't that Ning Zhuo had no other methods.

But under the watchful eyes of the public, it was impossible for him to openly cast the Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra.

If this were exposed, Ning Zhuo would be in immense trouble. Then, to subsequently obtain an alchemy furnace that, despite being on the verge of shattering, was still Spirit Treasure-level, would make Ning Zhuo the target of even more covetousness!

"Since that's the case..."

Ning Zhuo closed his eyes and poured his full power into the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone.

Having successfully used it before, he was extremely familiar with it right now, directly causing the Noble Righteous Qi shrouding his body to erupt outwards.

Boom!

A pillar of platinum qi shot straight past the ninth heaven, propping up the sky and connecting to the earth, drawing countless cries of amazement from the Cultivators and spectating crowds present.

"So strong!"

"Is this the sky-piercing pillar of qi mentioned in the intelligence?"

"This youth possesses the persistence of the Righteous Path; he is a good person!"

Wang Yu's eyes didn't show the slightest fluctuation, sighing in his heart: "Alas, being so overbearing is actually more likely to incur the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit's antipathy, wariness, and even fear."

"Good thing the artifact spirit is surrounded by a dreamscape. If it were directly exposed to the outside world, I'm afraid it might just die of sheer terror from this majestic aura."

The Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit gave no reaction.

Ning Zhuo let out a sigh, and taking advantage of the exact moment everyone's attention was captivated by the majestic pillar of qi, he ferociously made his move.

Divine Art: Life Hanging by a Thread!

Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit: "Awoo?!"
