Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 966: The Red-Robed Demonic Might

Daoist Yunhu's expression relaxed slightly.

The White Wave Sky-Hanging Batfish's white wave cloud cavity was specifically meant for swallowing fire, filtering smoke, and rolling up blood qi. Although the Red-Robed Guest's blood-path spiritual energy was ferocious, as long as a portion could be diverted, it would relieve the pressure on the cloud formation.

However, in the next moment, the corners of the Red-Robed Guest's lips curled up.

He slightly hooked his five fingers.

The blood-colored spiritual energy that had been inhaled into the white wave cloud cavity suddenly transformed into tiny blood snakes, swimming madly within the batfish's belly.

The White Wave Sky-Hanging Batfish immediately let out a muffled wail.

Its massive belly bulged high, and a swath of scarlet seeped through its snow-white cloud body. Its cloud-barbels trembled violently as dense sounds of tearing and biting echoed from within the white wave cloud cavity.

The Red-Robed Guest sneered, "Did you think you could swallow my blood arts so easily?"

He clenched his palm.

Bang!

A massive bloody hole burst open in the White Wave Sky-Hanging Batfish's belly. White clouds and blood qi spewed out together like a scarlet blizzard. Losing its balance, its giant wings plummeted diagonally, skimming the cloud lake and crashing down, sending cloud waves surging skyward.

Daoist Yunhu's face instantly paled, and his chest felt stifled.

Although the White Wave Sky-Hanging Batfish didn't die on the spot, it was heavily injured. Its aura was linked to the White Jade Pot Heaven Lamp; with the cloud beast wounded, Daoist Yunhu also suffered a backlash.

"Return!" Daoist Yunhu hurriedly formed a hand seal, wanting to withdraw the batfish back into the pot.

But the Red-Robed Guest's figure suddenly vanished.

In the next instant, he appeared atop the White Wave Sky-Hanging Batfish's head.

His blood robe fluttered.

He stomped his foot down!

Boom!

The batfish's head was stomped into the cloud lake. White rings of waves exploded across the lake's surface. The batfish's cloud body cracked inch by inch, and the cloud-barbels under its belly collapsed into countless scattered wisps.

The Red-Robed Guest stood atop its head, looking down at Daoist Yunhu. "Did I permit you to take it back?"

Sweat beaded on Daoist Yunhu's forehead. With a flick of his fingertips, the Thirty-Six Fold Cloud Sluices immediately closed.

Several cloud sluices lay between the Red-Robed Guest and the White Wave Sky-Hanging Batfish, attempting to cut off the battlefield.

A thread of blood light flew out from the Red-Robed Guest's sleeve.

The blood light was long and slender, dark red like a blade.

Slash!

The first cloud sluice was cleaved open.

The second cloud sluice was cleaved open.

The third, the fourth... they shattered in quick succession!

Every time a cloud sluice snapped, it emitted a crisp crack like shattering jade. Shredded clouds scattered, and white jade light-motes splashed into the cloud lake like pale, broken snow.

Daoist Yunhu's face grew even paler.

"Eight-Armed Cloud-Moving Octopus!" Cloud qi surged from the pot's mouth once again.

An octopus-shaped cloud beast drilled out of the pot. Its body was half-ethereal, half-solid, with eight long arms like flowing cloud ropes. As soon as it appeared, all eight arms extended in unison, wrapping toward the Red-Robed Guest.

It didn't seek to heavily injure him, only to delay the Red-Robed Guest for half a breath of time.

The eight cloud arms coiled around the Red-Robed Guest's wrists, ankles, waist, and back. The cloud arms were soft yet tough, like long ropes in water, or living white silk.

The Red-Robed Guest glanced down, a flash of hostility in his eyes.

"Petty tricks!" The blood veins all over his body suddenly lit up.

The dark patterns on his scarlet robe seemed to come completely alive, transforming into densely packed blood snakes that counterattacked along the arms of the Eight-Armed Cloud-Moving Octopus.

The Eight-Armed Cloud-Moving Octopus trembled violently. Its eight cloud arms were rapidly dyed red.

It tried to sever its arms to survive, but the Red-Robed Guest had already raised his right hand. His five fingers curled like hooks, and he viciously tore!

Riiiiip!

All eight cloud arms broke off simultaneously.

The breakages weren't smooth cross-sections of dissipated cloud qi, but large, dark-red jagged chunks bitten away by the blood snakes.

The Eight-Armed Cloud-Moving Octopus let out a shrill shriek, its body tumbling backward and crashing into the cloud lake, kicking up a massive white mist.

Unwilling to let it go, the Red-Robed Guest leapt forward, transforming into a blood shadow. He caught up to the Eight-Armed Cloud-Moving Octopus and thrust his hands into its half-ethereal cloud body.

He pulled his hands apart!

The Eight-Armed Cloud-Moving Octopus was actually torn apart alive by him!

Instantly, cloud qi violently dispersed, and white light flew wildly.

Daoist Yunhu cried out in alarm, "Eight-Armed Cloud-Moving Octopus!"

A thread of blood leaked from the corner of his mouth. He hastily channeled the White Jade Pot Heaven Lamp to draw the residual cloud nature of the octopus back into the pot. If he was even a moment slower, this cloud beast would likely have been completely obliterated by the Red-Robed Guest on the spot.

The Red-Robed Guest stood in the center of the cloud lake, hands hanging low, his fingertips still entwined with a few wisps of residual cloud.

He raised his hand and flicked. The blood-stained residual clouds fell into the cloud lake, transforming into a few red spots in the blink of an eye.

The Red-Robed Guest laughed loudly, "Whatever else you have, bring it all out! Today, I want to see just how much foundation this White Cloud Village truly possesses!"

His laughter echoed throughout the Cloud Marsh Arena.

Demonic qi overflowed!

At the same time, streaks of scarlet blood light spread out from beneath his feet, dyeing the nearby cloud lake into a marsh of blood. The white clouds, originally pure, were now tainted by blood. Dark red ripples spread from the edges, as if the entire Cloud Marsh was slowly bleeding.

In the seating area of Naming Stronghold, the cultivators' spirits were lifted.

Tu Yuanzi secretly clicked his tongue in awe.

Ning Zhuo's eyes shone brightly. He hadn't benefited much from Tan Zhu's poison arts in the previous battle, but watching the Red-Robed Guest strike frequently triggered his comprehension of the blood path. From a new angle, it gave him a deeper reflection on the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art.

Anyone with discerning eyes could see that the Red-Robed Guest held the absolute upper hand.

But Daoist Yunhu hadn't collapsed.

His face was pale, his mouth stained with blood, and his fingers even trembled slightly, but he was still laying out the cloud formation.

He had been doing this since the beginning of the battle, never once stopping.

Several of the Thirty-Six Fold Cloud Sluices had shattered, but the remaining sluices were still moving.

The cloud qi spewed by the White Jade Pot Heaven Lamp had quietly seeped into the entire cloud lake.

A Sinking Smoke Pot-Locking Formation slowly unfurled beneath the cloud lake, causing the surrounding cloud qi to grow heavier and heavier.

In the arena, Daoist Yunhu suddenly took a deep breath.

He bit the tip of his tongue and spat a mouthful of essence blood onto the Cloud-Feeding Beast Platter.

The nine compartments of the round platter lit up simultaneously.

From the mouth of the pot, three more auras burst out one after another.

The Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch!

The Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane!

The Wandering Smoke Lamp-Carrying Deer!

The Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch hovered at the edge of the Cloud Marsh. Its shell patterns were pale green and white, the opening facing outward. The fine sound of rain immediately began, a continuous pattering of pitter-patter.

The sound of rain wasn't jarring; instead, it felt like countless fine needles quietly piercing into the circulation of blood qi.

The Red-Robed Guest frowned slightly.

He could feel the flow of his blood qi slow by a fraction.

Though it was only a fraction for now, it showed a trend of gradually intensifying. A faint hypnotic intent also began to well up within his divine sea and Upper Dantian.

The Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane spread its long wings, the tips trailing sleeve-like white clouds. Its body was slender, its long neck held high. As its wings spread, a folded sky curtain surprisingly appeared above the cloud lake.

Within the sky curtain, there was a Daoist Yunhu.

Then a second Daoist Yunhu.

A third, a fourth, a fifth...

In the blink of an eye, over a dozen Daoist Yunhus appeared all around the cloud lake. Each one carried a large white jade pot on his back, each had a round face and short beard, and each wore a tense expression.

The Wandering Smoke Lamp-Carrying Deer tread upon the clouds, carrying a cyan-white cloud lamp on its back. Wherever the lamp shone, the layers of blood smoke became distinct, and the changes in the aura within the blood marsh beneath the Red-Robed Guest's feet were clearly illuminated bit by bit.

Leveraging the light from the Wandering Smoke Lamp-Carrying Deer, Daoist Yunhu immediately saw clearly the flow of the Red-Robed Guest's blood arts around his body.

Daoist Yunhu infused his spiritual energy, forcefully channeling the Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch.

The sound of rain abruptly thickened.

Pitter, patter, pitter, patter...

The Red-Robed Guest's footsteps paused, feeling a heavy wave of drowsiness wash over him.

But in the next moment, killing intent surged in his eyes: "Courting death!"

He suddenly opened his mouth and spat out a blood-colored pill.

The pill was only the size of a thumb, yet it was completely translucent, as if hiding a miniature sea of blood within.

The pill detonated, and scarlet blood light shot into the sky, forcibly illuminating the white clouds above the Cloud Marsh a bloody red. Under the impact of the blood qi, the rhythm of the Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch's rain instantly fell into chaos.

The Red-Robed Guest took a step forward.

Boom!

The cloud lake exploded.

His figure moved like bloody lightning, instantly appearing in front of the Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch.

The patterns on the Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch's shell flared brilliantly as it tried to retract into its cloud shell. But the Red-Robed Guest raised his hand and threw a punch.

Blood light converged at the peak of his fist, forming a blood-colored wolf head that fiercely slammed against the conch shell.

Thump!

A heavy, dull boom echoed throughout the martial arena.

A crack split open on the surface of the Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch.

A second punch.

Thump!

The crack spread.

A third punch!

Boom!

Half of the Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch's shell was smashed to pieces, and the conch's sound came to an abrupt halt. Countless sounds of rain turned into fractured musical notes, scattering into the sky above the cloud lake.

Daoist Yunhu suffered a backlash once again. A sharp pain struck his chest, and he couldn't hold back a mouthful of blood.

He hastily retreated.

The dozen-plus Daoist Yunhus also retreated simultaneously, covering up the location of his true form.

Having dealt with the Rain-Listening Sleeping Conch, the Red-Robed Guest looked up. Seeing this scene, he couldn't help but sneer, "Hiding your head and showing your tail."

Blood light swirled in his eyes as his scarlet gaze swept over the Daoist Yunhus one by one.

The cloud curtain woven by the Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane was extremely profound, overlapping truth and falsehood, even imitating auras. An ordinary Nascent Soul cultivator caught in it would need at least a considerable amount of time to distinguish them.

For a moment, the Red-Robed Guest couldn't identify him either.

"Then there's no need to identify." He stuck out his tongue and gently licked the corner of his lips.

He violently threw his sleeves wide open.

A sea of blood surged backward from behind him. An overwhelming scarlet tide pounced toward the dozen Daoist Yunhus simultaneously.

Rumble!

The blood waves crashed into the Heaven-Folding cloud curtain.

The cloud curtain twisted violently.

One by one, the Daoist Yunhus were swallowed by the blood waves. Some shattered on the spot, some turned into white mist, and some let out fake screams.

The Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane let out a long cry, continuously flapping its wings in an attempt to maintain the cloud curtain.

The Red-Robed Guest raised his eyes within the sea of blood.

"Found you."

The Red-Robed Guest's figure suddenly vanished!

A flash of blood light appeared before Daoist Yunhu's eyes.

Greatly alarmed, he hastily channeled the White Jade Pot Heaven Lamp to protect himself.

But the Red-Robed Guest's palm was already striking down.

Bang!

The White Jade Pot Heaven Lamp vibrated fiercely.

Daoist Yunhu was sent flying by a single palm, crashing into the cloud lake and tumbling for tens of meters. The large pot on his back hummed, and a hairline crack surfaced on its body.

The Red-Robed Guest's eyes flickered, his savage grin pausing slightly.

Something was wrong.

The tactile sensation was wrong.

The "Daoist Yunhu" he had struck rapidly stretched out in the cloud lake. His arms turned into wings, his round face became a crane's head, and the large white jade pot transformed into a clump of folded cloud curtain.

The Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane!

This cloud beast had actually disguised itself as Daoist Yunhu's true body to block this strike for its master.

Half of the Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane's body had been shattered by the blood palm, yet it still turned its head to look at the Red-Robed Guest. Its crane eyes held a trace of spiritual resoluteness.

The Red-Robed Guest was taken aback for a moment before laughing loudly, "Good beast, quite loyal!"

He flashed over, his five fingers forming a claw, and grabbed the Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane's neck in one swoop.

Blood light flashed explosively.

The Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane's neck snapped, directly crushed to pieces. Its body turned into a massive expanse of white cloud feathers, plummeting into the blood marsh.

In the seating area of the White Cloud Village, the expressions of the cultivators turned very ugly.

Daoist Yunhu, hidden behind another cloud sluice, saw the demise of the Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane, and his eyes reddened slightly.

That Heaven-Folding Sleeve Crane had followed him for nearly a hundred years.

Ordinarily, it loved standing on the shoulder of the White Jade Pot Heaven Lamp, preening its cloud feathers. Whenever Daoist Yunhu occasionally failed in cloud refinement, it would stretch its long neck and peck at his cuff, as if mocking him.

Now, it was dead.

Obliterated alive by the Red-Robed Guest's palm and claw.

Daoist Yunhu's mind was thrown into turmoil.

The Red-Robed Guest instantly caught this sliver of fluctuation.

He jerked his head around, his gaze piercing through the cloud sluices, cloud mist, and sinking smoke, locking onto the location of Daoist Yunhu's true body.

The Red-Robed Guest smiled savagely, "This time, let's see where you can hide!"

The sea of blood beneath his feet rolled up, forming a long blood-colored bridge.

The Red-Robed Guest walked upon the bridge!

With every step he took, a swath of the cloud lake was dyed red. The Sinking Smoke Pot-Locking Formation tried to suppress his movement, but with the Blood Demon Pill in his stomach, his vitality and blood were boiling, and his demonic intent surged wildly. He charged forward, forcibly resisting the pressure of the sinking smoke.

Daoist Yunhu retreated in a panic.

He released a swarm of Snow Sprout Fragrant Cloud Butterflies.

A swarm of pale green butterflies flew out from the pot. Their wings bore the patterns of snow-white tea sprouts, and an elegant tea aroma spread through the air.

The tea aroma entered the blood, attempting to dilute the Red-Robed Guest's blood fiend aura.

The Red-Robed Guest took a breath, and an intoxicated look actually appeared on his face.

The Red-Robed Guest said, "What a good tea fragrance. These aren't your cloud beasts, are they?"

In the next moment, he opened his mouth and spat out a ball of blood fire.

The blood fire plunged into the butterfly swarm.

The Snow Sprout Fragrant Cloud Butterflies began to burn one after another. Their pale green wings curled up in the blood fire, and the tea aroma turned into a bitter, charred scent. Countless butterfly silhouettes fell, like a batch of spring tea destroyed by blood fire.

Daoist Yunhu then released the Heart-Questioning Shadow Cloud Deer.

Its antlers were like ancient smoke-cloud trees, its eyes gentle and warm, attempting to shine into the gaps of the Red-Robed Guest's heart, tugging at his old grudges towards Chunyangzi, the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and the Jade Blood Mountain Sect.

The Red-Robed Guest's footsteps paused slightly.

Before his eyes, the old memories of cultivators from the Pure Yang Palace hunting him down seemed to surface.

Golden rosy clouds filled the sky.

Sword light formed a net.

He fled injured through the valley, his blood spilling all along the way.

Then the desolate years at the Jade Blood Mountain Sect surfaced. Sect missions pressed down on him one after another, yet the rewards were thin as paper. He couldn't enter the main sect gates, nor could he climb the ranks of the sect's higher-ups. The ferocious man of the Demonic Path of the past had actually been trapped in that cold and gloomy sect, like a chained blood beast.

The antlers of the Heart-Questioning Shadow Cloud Deer glowed faintly.

Hope appeared in Daoist Yunhu's eyes.

But in the next breath, the Red-Robed Guest laughed.

That laughter was incredibly bone-chilling.

The Red-Robed Guest declared, "All of these things are but nourishment for my Dao!"

He struck out with a palm.

The blood fire transformed into a massive scarlet python, swallowing half of the Heart-Questioning Shadow Cloud Deer's body in one bite.

The Shadow Cloud Deer wailed mournfully.

Its antlers snapped, and the branches that resembled ancient smoke-cloud trees shattered into a floor of gray light.

The Red-Robed Guest grabbed with his five fingers, forcibly dragging the Shadow Cloud Deer before him. Spitting blood flames from his palm, he burned its head into nothingness.

Chills shot up from the depths of Daoist Yunhu's heart.

"I can't stop him."

"I absolutely can't stop him!"

Daoist Yunhu fought as he retreated. The cracks on the White Jade Pot Heaven Lamp on his back grew more and more numerous. Inside the pot, the cloud beasts were continuously heavily injured or killed. The backlashes crashed into his body and mind wave after wave, causing the circulation of his spiritual energy to fall into severe disarray.

The Red-Robed Guest still hadn't stopped.

He charged deep into the cloud formation, flattening everything in his path!

He continuously shattered twenty-one of the Thirty-Six Fold Cloud Sluices.

He crushed three formation cores of the Sinking Smoke Pot-Locking Formation underfoot.

The Cloud-Feeding Beast Platter was swept by the aftershock of the blood fire. The demon blood inside exploded, and the resulting blood mist was conveniently inhaled by the Red-Robed Guest through his nose, further bolstering his imposing aura.

Daoist Yunhu's heart ached so much it felt like it was bleeding.

These treasures and cloud beasts, aside from his personal accumulations, were actually largely the foundation of White Cloud Village, funded by various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak. For every single one lost, there was a massive debt of gratitude and trade cost behind it.

But now, the Red-Robed Guest was like a blood demon, rampaging wildly without any scruples.

He wasn't having a battle of techniques.

He was slaughtering beasts and tearing apart formations, using the crudest, most barbaric, most Demonic Path methods to rip apart, layer by layer, the battle tactics painstakingly laid out by Daoist Yunhu and the factions backing him!
