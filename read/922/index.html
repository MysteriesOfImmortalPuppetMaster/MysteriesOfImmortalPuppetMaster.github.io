Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 922: Have You Eaten Your Fill of Your Last Meal?

"Three Nascent Souls." Baicao Weng sighed, his restless and unwilling emotions settling down considerably.

Just as many had guessed, he had indeed been dispatched by Baicao Peak, acting as a tentacle of the main peak to participate in the Nanming Stronghold.

Naturally, Baicao Weng was not very willing, but seeing three Nascent Soul cultivators join, he also began to accept his fate.

Han Muma took a light sip of his tea. As long as it wasn't Cang Yazi joining, he didn't care about the Red-Robed Guest.

Outside.

Ban Jie gritted her teeth, her face entirely ashen: "The Red-Robed Guest has joined! That's three Nascent Souls. Ban Ji has to deal with a faction that has three Nascent Souls?!"

"This is too difficult."

Tie Kuang pondered in silence in the Artifact Refining Hall.

The joining of the Red-Robed Guest was one of the possibilities he had predicted, and now it had become a reality.

"The grand momentum of Nanming Stronghold has been established!"

"However, there is still a slight blemish on this perfection."

Inside the lobby of Heavenly Treasure Pavilion, Dong Nishang watched all of this: "The imperfection lies in its composition. Three Nascent Souls: two Demon Cultivators and one from the Demonic Path. Externally, Nanming Stronghold's image won't be very good. Ning Zhuo's Righteous Path image is deeply rooted in people's hearts, but in the end, he is only at the mid Foundation Establishment stage."

A slight imperfection?

Where was there any imperfection!

A pure gold carriage slowly drove out from a golden light.

Pulling the golden carriage were eight pure white Heavenly Horses. Their manes were like snow, their hooves trod on the wind, and their eyes shone with the light of spiritual energy.

Chunyangzi stepped out of the carriage, walking on thin air step by step. Under Gongsun Yan's respectful invitation, he stepped into the first-floor lobby of Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

"How could Nanming Stronghold be without me, Chunyangzi?" He chuckled, not at all surprised to see the shocked expressions of the Cultivators in the lobby.

Ye Qingming and Jin Mantang were both dumbfounded.

Others joining was understandable, but Chunyangzi?

If he were to join, what about his Pure Yang Palace?

He brought his Sect to submit to the Myriad Manifestations Sect, yet he actually wants to attach himself to a newly founded mountain faction?

Am I under an illusion?

This is outrageous beyond belief!

Ning Zhuo had a look of astonishment on his face and hurriedly asked about the matter of Pure Yang Palace.

Chunyangzi stated that he had already left Pure Yang Palace and was merely the previous Sect Master. This time, he was joining in a personal capacity.

Ning Zhuo immediately welcomed him and invited Chunyangzi to take a seat.

The worry in Dong Nishang's heart was completely swept away: "With Chunyangzi joining, Nanming Stronghold's image is no longer a concern!"

Situ Xing, Lin Jinglong, and Shen Xi exchanged glances, all seeing the joy in each other's eyes. The families behind them were all willing to see this outcome.

Kong Ran mentally applauded and cheered.

Niu Cheng's mouth hung wide open, his heart filled with shock: "A fourth Nascent Soul lord has already joined!"

Nanming Stronghold's grand momentum had risen to yet another level!

Ban Jie, on the mountain peak outside the pavilion, already felt numb.

But the one feeling even more miserable was none other than Liu Jinke. At this moment, he felt as if he were sitting on a bed of nails, with thorns in his back and a fishbone stuck in his throat!

Jiang Ping watched Chunyangzi sit at the main table, looked at the fire-breathing expression the Red-Robed Guest directed at Chunyangzi, and then looked toward Ning Zhuo.

"Ning Zhuo, oh Ning Zhuo..." Jiang Ping felt a complex mixture of emotions in his heart.

And in the very next moment, Mu Yuehua, who was sitting beside him, suddenly stood up.

Jiang Ping blanked for a moment, then immediately felt a strong sense of foreboding.

Mu Yuehua had already beaten him to it, bowing to Ning Zhuo: "Daoist Friend Ning Zhuo, this little woman is also willing to join Nanming Stronghold. Please take me in."

She spoke very politely.

Jiang Ping, on the other hand, felt his vision go dark.

Both Danxia Peak and his master, Wang Yu, thought very highly of Mu Yuehua, feeling that the latter was an excellent seed for pill refining.

He came to observe the ceremony this time and took the initiative to get close to Mu Yuehua precisely because he wanted to recruit her. In the end...

In the end, with just a moment's inattention, Mu Yuehua was snatched away by Ning Zhuo!

Ning Zhuo also felt quite surprised, doubting in his heart: "What's going on? Could it be that Mu Yuehua was also dispatched by Danxia Peak to infiltrate the Nanming Stronghold?"

But Ning Zhuo quickly dismissed this immature guess.

"Probably not."

"Mu Yuehua's situation is different from Baicao Weng's. Baicao Weng's path is already set, but Mu Yuehua still has immense room to grow and potential. She is absolutely a Nascent Soul seed, and her pill refining possesses both innate talent and brilliance."

"Danxia Peak would never dispatch such an outstanding junior elsewhere. That would simply be a reckless waste of a heavenly treasure!"

"So, if there's no other reason, Mu Yuehua genuinely wants to join my Nanming Stronghold? What... is she after?"

Exactly. Instead of joining a perfectly fine place like Danxia Peak, she went out of her way to join Ning Zhuo's Nanming Stronghold.

What was Mu Yuehua after?!

This was also the shared doubt of many Cultivators in the lobby.

But evidently, the cold and aloof Mu Yuehua showed no sign of explaining herself to the public.

"I, I will also join," Tao Liweng stepped forward.

Ning Zhuo no longer needed his declaration of stance and simply waved him off lightly: "Take a seat."

Tao Liweng: ?!

Ning Zhuo's casual attitude stunned him for a moment, making him feel greatly displeased.

But when he looked at the four Nascent Souls who had joined, his feelings of displeasure were swiftly diminished.

Unlike when he previously conversed with Ning Zhuo in private, the current Ning Zhuo gave Tao Liweng a completely different feeling!

"It's about time." And under these circumstances, Zhongli Mei made his entrance.

No one in the lobby recognized him. At this moment, he had already changed his appearance.

"My name is Tan Zhu. I am quite interested in the matter of assaulting Flowing Cloud Peak, so I have come specifically to join," he said straightforwardly.

"Tan Zhu? That name sounds somewhat familiar."

"Hiss..."

"Why are you gasping?"

"Poison... Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation! He is Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation, Tan Zhu!"

"Hiss..." The Cultivator who had just asked the question also drew a sharp breath.

Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation, Tan Zhu, wore a grayish-black long robe. The fabric was thick with a strong sense of drape, and over it, he wore a short-sleeved jacket of the same color. The collar stood half an inch high, concealing his neck.

His eyebrows were thin and faint, the ends slanting upward into his temples. He had a high, straight nose bridge with narrow nostrils, and his breathing was extremely light and slow.

His complexion was unnaturally white, a pallor caused by prolonged deprivation of sunlight or a severe deficiency in vital energy and blood. However, under the illumination of the lobby's lights, others could vaguely see a few extremely faint grayish-black lines beneath his skin.

He was a Demonic Cultivator, a great Demonic Cultivator who was wanted everywhere by the Flying Cloud Kingdom and other Cultivation nations!

His sudden appearance, and the fact that he directly joined Nanming Stronghold the moment he showed up, exceeded everyone's expectations.

Ning Zhuo communicated with Situ Xing and the others via divine sense, obtaining intelligence on Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation.

He thought for a moment, hesitating for just a second, before immediately breaking into a smile and expressing his welcome to Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation.

Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation, however, said: "I have a question. Since I am joining Nanming Stronghold, which seat of authority shall I take?"

The Cultivators in the hall were instantly startled.

Jin Mantang and Ye Qingming exchanged a glance, both feeling the other's eyes light up.

Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation's words clearly carried a suspicion of sowing discord. At the same time, they bluntly exposed a hidden danger.

Anyone who saw the tense atmosphere between Chunyangzi and the Red-Robed Guest at the main table would know that these two would inevitably fight over the seating arrangement.

Ning Zhuo said, "Senior Tan, the seating arrangement is very simple. It is ranked according to the size of one's debt."

"If you have the intention to sit in a higher seat, this junior suggests that you provide funds to repair the Naming Fire Furnace, striving to surpass others in terms of debt."

As soon as these words came out, Kong Ran and the others relaxed their furrowed brows, inwardly praising the brilliance of the answer.

Ning Zhuo had directly defused the crisis, and the solution he brought out was quite convincing - after all, Nanming Stronghold was established precisely to repay debts.

Under this concept, if you are the biggest creditor, then there is absolutely no problem with you taking the highest seat!

Tan Zhu nodded, "Good, I accept this arrangement. But neither he nor I are creditors. How should we rank?"

Tan Zhu pointed his finger at Tu Yuanzi.

Tu Yuanzi's heart tightened, and he waved his hand directly, "I don't mind. I concede that you are stronger, yes, stronger."

Tan Zhu: ...

Ning Zhuo: ...

Everyone: ...

Thus, the tense atmosphere that had suddenly rippled through the lobby completely vanished into thin air due to Tu Yuanzi's concession!

Some people began to look thoughtful.

In their eyes, Tu Yuanzi didn't have the air of a Nascent Soul expert at all and was likely afraid of putting Ning Zhuo in a difficult position. This further corroborated the speculation that Tu Yuanzi was dispatched by Ning Zhuo's family for support!

In the end, the number of Nascent Soul Cultivators joining settled at "five."

Five Nascent Souls!

The newly founded Nanming Stronghold was not favored at first, but after forming its alliance, it leaped into becoming a massive faction with five Nascent Souls. This left many people astonished, doubtful, and completely dumbfounded.

And the "mere mid Foundation Establishment" Ning Zhuo, who had made the initial call to action, single-handedly stirring up a storm to create this mountain faction, once again became the center of attention!

Ning Zhuo waited for a moment. Seeing that no more guests were arriving and the sun was already high in the sky, he announced the start of the banquet.

He didn't lack money, so the banquet booked at Heavenly Treasure Pavilion was of a very high standard, allowing the guests to enjoy themselves to the fullest. Especially Jiang Xiaobian, Niu Cheng, and the others, who all ate until they were stuffed. Meanwhile, Ao Pan, Lu Zeng, and others silently devoured their food, their mouths quickly glistening with oil.

Ye Qingming, Jin Mantang, and Li Ku were extremely heavy-hearted, unable to taste their food.

The most unique person among them was Liu Jinke.

He stared at the table full of delicacies without moving his chopsticks at all.

How could he eat this last meal?!

During the banquet, many people chatted as they ate, frequently casting evaluating glances his way.

This was practically another form of public execution!

Liu Jinke was the most glaring example of what happens when one offends Ning Zhuo!

This gave all the Cultivators in the lobby a strong psychological suggestion - Look, this is the consequence of offending Ning Zhuo. How terrible.

Liu Jinke actually really wanted to leave the banquet, but considering it, doing so would make his evasion too obvious. He would face even more criticism!

Ultimately, he chose to sit as still as a statue with an ashen, rigid face.

He bitterly endured this period of time, waiting as the guests exchanged toasts and gradually ate and drank to their fill.

Ning Zhuo had been maintaining the atmosphere at the main table all along.

Seeing that the time was ripe, he decisively stood up and walked toward Liu Jinke with a calm and unhurried demeanor.

This scene immediately drew the attention of all the Cultivators.

Liu Jinke abruptly sprang to his feet. He stared fixedly at Ning Zhuo, his expression no longer possessing its previous aloof arrogance, replaced entirely by vigilance and wariness.

He squeezed a voice through his teeth: "Ning Zhuo..."

Just as he uttered Ning Zhuo's name, Liu Jinke was startled by his own voice - since when had it become so hoarse and weak?

Ning Zhuo bowed to him: "Senior Brother, have you eaten your fill of your last meal?"

"You!" Liu Jinke took a deep breath. "A mere mid Foundation Establishment cultivator, how arrogant. You must know that although your Nanming Stronghold has many Nascent Soul experts, they cannot help you fight."

Ning Zhuo offered no explanation. He simply extended his palm, pointing toward the lobby entrance, and smiled faintly: "Please."
