Chapter 920: What Exactly is Ning Zhuo's Background?!

Under Ning Zhuo's guidance, the youth in yellow took a seat at the main table. He sat just a few seats away from the Nine Fires Dragon Lord.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord stared at him, and everyone else's gazes were also firmly glued to this youth in yellow.

This was a highly mysterious, unfamiliar Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator!

More crucially, just like the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, he wasn't here to just observe the ceremony; he was here to join directly!

Where did he come from?

He wasn't Ning Zhuo's creditor. What was his purpose for inexplicably joining the Nanming Stronghold?!

Countless questions rose and fell, continuously swirling in the minds of the cultivators in the hall.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was more direct. He transmitted his voice via his divine sense, asking the youth in yellow, "Fellow Daoist, how should I address you? I am very curious, you are not Ning Zhuo's creditor, so why are you joining the Nanming Stronghold?"

Towards the youth in yellow, who was also a Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord felt a sense of kinship but also a great deal of vigilance. From the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's perspective, he was forced against his will to join the Nanming Stronghold. But what reasoning did the youth in yellow before him have for actively participating?!

The youth in yellow responded via his divine sense: "Dragon Lord, you can call me Tu Yuanzi. Why join? Hehe, I am here for Ning Zhuo."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's pupils immediately shrank. He pressed, "What do you mean?"

Tu Yuanzi chuckled, revealing a bit of foolishness, and didn't reply further.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord fell silent. He turned his head to look at Ning Zhuo, a look of suspicion growing in his eyes.

Ning Zhuo had also been observing the youth in yellow. Seeing the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's expression, he immediately guessed that the two had likely communicated via their divine sense.

Ning Zhuo's thoughts spun, and he suddenly said to the youth in yellow, "Senior Nascent Soul, I have yet to ask for your esteemed name."

"Oh, you can just call me Tu Yuanzi," the youth in yellow replied.

He looked just like someone who had cultivated in bitter seclusion for many years and was poor at socializing, only revealing his name now.

Ning Zhuo glanced at the Nine Fires Dragon Lord and added, "Senior Tu Yuanzi, it is an honor for us to have you join the Nanming Stronghold and be regarded so highly by you. However, there is one matter that must be clarified in advance to show my sincerity-"

Tu Yuanzi looked at Ning Zhuo earnestly.

Ning Zhuo carefully weighed his words, "All fellow Daoists who join the Nanming Stronghold will be ranked in terms of seating, and this ranking is tied to debts."

"That is to say, according to the current debt situation, I do not owe you anything, but I owe Lord Nine Fires Dragon Lord quite a bit of money."

"The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's debt exceeds yours, so he must be ranked ahead of you."

"Please do not take offense. This was my original intention in establishing the Nanming Stronghold: to repay debts and not be someone who owes money."

These words were a probe by Ning Zhuo; he couldn't figure out this mysterious Demon Cultivator's origins or purpose.

These words were also meant to placate the Nine Fires Dragon Lord. The big-headed youth directly stated his position.

As expected, once these words were spoken, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's expression immediately softened.

After hearing this, Tu Yuanzi simply nodded, "No problem, Ning Zhuo... Little Friend, I will follow your arrangements."

This mysterious Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator's attitude was actually so amiable!

His simple response and straightforward attitude, however, once again dropped a massive boulder into the mindscapes of everyone in the hall.

"This Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator, Tu Yuanzi, is actually so easy to talk to?"

"Hehe, have you ever seen a Nascent Soul so easy to talk to? It simply varies from person to person."

"Exactly, that hits the nail on the head! Tu Yuanzi only has this attitude towards Ning Zhuo."

"Brother Ning is not simple."

"Ning Zhuo's identity is definitely not simple at all!"

"Tu Yuanzi was very likely dispatched by the faction behind Young Master Ning Zhuo."

"That is highly likely. Otherwise, how could Ning Zhuo have such confidence to form an alliance and charge the peak using the Nanming Furnace? The every move of a scion of a major clan carries profound meaning! How could he make such a risky move?"

"Exactly! Lord Ning Zhuo must have made arrangements long ago."

The crowd transmitted their thoughts via divine sense, communicating frantically, and quickly reached a consensus.

More and more gazes shifted from Tu Yuanzi to Ning Zhuo. Some people's eyes gleamed as they thought of another circumstance surrounding Ning Zhuo.

"Speaking of which, hasn't our sect investigated Ning Zhuo's background and origins yet?"

"That's also too strange. Given the sect's efficiency and capabilities, it shouldn't have taken this long, right?"

"Could it be that the Foreign Affairs Hall has actually already investigated it, and the higher-ups know, but they just haven't revealed it?"

"If that's the case, then Ning Zhuo's background is very special and powerful."

"This time, an unfamiliar Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator appeared to hold the fort. Just what kind of faction is this?"

"Tu Yuanzi is a Demon Cultivator... could it be that Ning Zhuo's background is related to the Demon Race? Is that why the higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect covered up his background information?"

The more the crowd thought, the deeper their imaginations ran, and they couldn't help but gasp in shock.

Thus, the soft hissing sounds of sharp intakes of breath echoed endlessly throughout the hall.

Ning Zhuo maintained a calm expression with a smile on his face, but his heart was filled with doubts: What exactly is the deal with this Tu Yuanzi? Why is he so pleasant to me? This is clearly our first time meeting. What on earth is his objective?

The youth in yellow, Tu Yuanzi, was actually Huang Yue, the pet beast companion of Cang Yazi.

Cang Yazi did not want to involve himself, but he wanted to use the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to bestow Spirit Awakening upon his other pet beast companions, so he arranged for Huang Yue to join the Nanming Stronghold.

He instructed Huang Yue, "With the various magic treasures I've given you, you can easily hide your identity and cultivation, pretending to be a Nascent Soul-level cultivator."

"This will be beneficial to you."

"Firstly, the Nascent Soul-level will allow you to avoid much covetousness and trouble, while simultaneously commanding respect. Secondly, it will provide an external explanation for why your combat power far exceeds that of a Golden Core cultivator."

Huang Yue possessed the Earth Dragon bloodline; even if he transformed into human form, his physical constitution far surpassed that of the Human race.

Even in human form, his combat power could rival a Nascent Soul. This was an innate advantage of demonic beasts.

Cang Yazi further instructed Huang Yue, "This tempering experience also holds many benefits for you. You must look more, listen more, think more, and speak less. The more familiar you become with human nature, the faster and more abundantly your wisdom and spirituality will grow."

"Although I have only met Little Friend Ning Zhuo a few times, he is different from ordinary people. He treats Demon Cultivators and artifact spirits equally. Though young, he possesses the demeanor of the Righteous Path and the magnanimity of a gentleman."

"You can give him more trust. I have also written you a letter of recommendation. Remember, you must hand it to him secretly and covertly."

Huang Yue nodded.

Cang Yazi sighed and said with earnest gravity, "You must cooperate more with Little Friend Ning Zhuo. We need the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to bestow Spirit Awakening upon us."

After saying that, Cang Yazi patted Huang Yue on the shoulder.

Huang Yue's expression became extremely earnest and solemn as he nodded heavily, "Master, I will definitely complete the mission!"

He treated his other companions like family. Having successfully transformed into human form himself, he understood the difficulty and pain of transformation better than anyone. For his family, he was willing to sacrifice and exert all his strength to fight for opportunities to help them!

Therefore, Huang Yue arrived outside the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion early on.

He arrived even earlier than Ning Zhuo!

But he obeyed Cang Yazi's words. Cang Yazi had told him not to stick out too much or be too conspicuous, but to wait until two Nascent Soul cultivators had attended before showing up to join.

Huang Yue did exactly that.

This resulted in the current situation.

He transmitted a message to Ning Zhuo via divine sense, telling the latter that he had a letter of recommendation in his hand and needed to hand it over secretly.

Ning Zhuo secretly responded via divine sense, making an appointment with Tu Yuanzi (Huang Yue) for tonight.

Han Muma was also at the same table. After quietly observing Tu Yuanzi for a long time, he finally sent a divine sense transmission to secretly communicate and probe him.

Tu Yuanzi strictly followed Cang Yazi's instructions: speak little, but show his stance more. In terms of attitude, he naturally supported Ning Zhuo.

After probing for a while, Han Muma was secretly alarmed.

Ning Zhuo's calm expression added a lot of profound meaning in the eyes of this Nascent Soul major cultivator.

A Nascent Soul-level Demon Cultivator came specifically for Ning Zhuo; the significance of this is too monumental!

Could it be that Lord Peak Master Tuoba Huang knows some hidden secrets? He knows his background? That's why he placed such importance on and treated Ning Zhuo with such courtesy, rather than doing it just to embarrass Zhong Dao?

Han Muma also thought of Ning Zhuo's Spirit Communication Art.

His Spirit Communication Art is far from simple. He can actually communicate with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit; this is a method that even the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate cannot produce.

If they could produce it, the Danxia Peak faction would absolutely not have organized this Rising Cloud Minor Trial.

Han Muma also began to overthink: With such a Spirit Communication Art, wouldn't it be extremely convenient to communicate with demonic beasts and spirit beasts?

If Ning Zhuo possesses such a Spirit Communication Art, the faction behind him must be even stronger in this method. Therefore, his clan probably controls many demonic beasts and spirit beasts.

Consequently, at this critical juncture, they deliberately dispatched one to come and assist him.

Excellent! The actions of the scions of major clans are truly flawless!

No wonder Peak Master Tuoba Huang personally asked me to present the Heart Snake Horse-Tripping Bell. This was to probe to what extent Ning Zhuo's Spirit Communication Art could reach.

I understand. I see it clearly now.

These people are all shrewd foxes; behind every single action, there are a hundred hidden motives.

Sigh, raising and taming horses still suits me best, without all these convoluted twists and turns.

This time, I'll just stick to observing the ceremony, and guard against my old friend Cang Yazi acting irrationally and suddenly joining the Nanming Stronghold. As long as I ensure this, I can return and complete my task perfectly.

Tu Yuanzi's participation once again altered the atmosphere in the hall.

As a disciple of Wang Yu, Jiang Ping also had the backing of a Nascent Soul cultivator himself.

The look in his eyes when he looked at Ning Zhuo completely changed.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord supporting Ning Zhuo did not carry much weight, because everyone knew that the Nine Fires Dragon Lord was Ning Zhuo's creditor; his support for Ning Zhuo carried a strong undertone of being forced into it.

But Tu Yuanzi was different!

Jiang Ping knew all too well the significance of having a Nascent Soul cultivator wholeheartedly backing someone!

This matter of forming an alliance to charge the peak, Ning Zhuo actually pulled it off.

He didn't join or defect to an existing faction, but rather forged a new one completely from scratch.

He has the backing of his clan's Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator, which is great, but that is not the key.

Ning Zhuo successfully broke the deadlock by relying on the Nanming Furnace.

Such attainments and methods in the Righteous Path are truly remarkable!

At this moment, Jiang Ping inevitably felt a rise of admiration, as well as heavy envy, for Ning Zhuo from the bottom of his heart.

Ning Zhuo had accomplished what Jiang Ping desired but couldn't do!

Pi Fujie was also envious, but mostly he felt relieved.

Ning Zhuo must be a scion of a major clan! And highly valued by his clan at that!

This Tu Yuanzi is probably his clan's doing.

I made the right choice!

I reconciled with Ning Zhuo, turning an enemy into a friend!

What does it matter if I sacrificed a bit of personal face for this?

I'm so wise!

In the hall, people's hearts were undergoing drastic changes in the shadows.

Situ Xing, on the other hand, was conflicted.

He also thought of Ning Zhuo's background.

He developed an even stronger desire to join the Nanming Stronghold. But...

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord is a Demon Cultivator, and Tu Yuanzi is also a Demon Cultivator. Although they are both Nascent Souls, they are also simultaneously Demon Cultivators.

If I join, how will my clan view this matter?

His dilemma was the exact same dilemma shared by Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong.

"Two Nascent Soul... Demon Cultivators." Ning Zhuo's gaze was deep. He, of course, also saw this drawback.

The human heart was profound and subtle, but Ning Zhuo saw through it.

"I need to reduce these concerns." Ning Zhuo cast his gaze toward the Immortal within the Furnace, Tao Liweng.

Tao Liweng was also looking at Ning Zhuo, so their eyes met smoothly.

"Good." Ning Zhuo smiled at Tao Liweng, a look of encouragement on his face.

Tao Liweng stared at Ning Zhuo with shining eyes, his face full of excitement.

He had previously signed a contract with Ning Zhuo to join the Nanming Stronghold. However, he wasn't completely willing; he was forced by Ning Zhuo's plans and had to defend his own interests.

But now...

Heavens above, two Nascent Souls have joined!

If I join the Nanming Stronghold, I will have the backing of two Nascent Soul cultivators.

This is fantastic!

I was so wise, I made the right choice. I have great foresight, deciding early on to join the Nanming Stronghold.

Hahaha...

Tao Liweng was extremely exhilarated; his previous hesitation, helplessness, and suppressed anger completely dissipated as if they had never existed.

Ning Zhuo: "..."

He could see Tao Liweng's excitement: But do something, join! I need you riht now.

Tao Liweng's political acumen made him blind to the drawbacks of having two Nascent Souls, and he failed even more to grasp Ning Zhuo's meaning of encouraging him to take action.

He just continued to passionately lock eyes with Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo's smile faded a bit, and he ultimately had no choice but to shift his gaze helplessly.

The impact of Tu Yuanzi's appearance and participation immediately spread outwards from the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

On a nearby mountain peak, Ban Jie had a look of shock on her face: "A second Nascent Soul joining? So fast!"

"And it's an unfamiliar Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator... could it be?!"

Smart people just love to overthink.

She also thought of Ning Zhuo's background and origins.

"So, in order for Ban Ji to resolve his Human Tribulation, it's highly likely that he will have to face the super faction behind Ning Zhuo?"

"I don't really understand the Royal Mandate. Is the Human Tribulation of the Royal Mandate really this terrifying?"

Thinking of this, fine beads of sweat couldn't help but seep out on Ban Jie's forehead.

"There are already two Nascent Souls." Ban Jie sincerely hoped it would just be these two, and that no more Nascent Souls would be added!

Zhong Limei had already "transformed" into another person, and he was also silently observing the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

According to his plan, he would also show up today to join.

"An unfamiliar Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator? Hehe, how interesting."

"This kid Ning Zhuo always pulls off unexpected performances."

Zhong Limei was glad to see this situation happen.

Because having changed his appearance, he was also a stranger and also a Nascent Soul cultivator. With Tu Yuanzi paving the way, he wouldn't be too conspicuous and would be much easier for people to accept.
