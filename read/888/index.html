Chapter 888: Dragon Pearl and Wisdom Teeth

This was a pearl.

The pearl was not large, about the size of an adult's fist, and was entirely crimson-gold.

On the surface of the pearl, the faint light and shadow of dense dragon scale patterns could be seen, overlapping layer by layer like the scales of a true dragon.

Looking closely, there was a dragon-shaped phantom inside the pearl. Its head was held high, its claws spread wide, and its tail coiled. It was incredibly lifelike, as if it could break out from the pearl at any moment.

Tiny flames burned on the surface of the Dragon Pearl, carrying an eternal majesty within their blazing heat. This revealed the Dragon Pearl's deep fire nature. The giant dragon it belonged to in life must have been a fire dragon.

The crowd was astonished.

"A Dragon Pearl?!"

"Looking at the dragon-shaped phantom inside the pearl, it has at least ten thousand years of cultivation. The master of this Dragon Pearl was at least at the Soul Formation-level."

"In other words, this is a Spirit Treasure-grade Dragon Pearl that carries its own Divine Art?!"

A Dragon Pearl was the gathering of a true dragon's entire essence, the anchor of its life, and the condensation of its Dao fruit.

In the Cultivation World, it was often said: "A dragon without a pearl is like a human without a soul." Thus, there was the proverb: "Slaying a dragon but not taking its pearl is like entering a treasure mountain and returning empty-handed."

The preciousness of a Dragon Pearl lay not only in the power of a true dragon it contained, but also in the dragon clan's Great Dao it carried. Refining a Dragon Pearl was tantamount to inheriting a true dragon's entire cultivation foundation.

Especially since the original master of this Dragon Pearl had a cultivation base of at least the Soul Formation-level, it meant the Dragon Pearl carried one or even several of its master's Divine Arts!

Besides this, a Dragon Pearl also possessed the miraculous effects of suppressing destiny and warding off all evil.

The discussions from behind reached the ears of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, but he remained focused on the Dragon Pearl in his hand.

His fingers trembled slightly as he murmured in his heart, Father.

This was his father's relic.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord had always kept it close. As he grew up, he comprehended the Dragon Pearl and gained much insight. His currently superb fire-controlling skills were largely thanks to the Divine Arts within the Dragon Pearl.

It could be said that this was the only relic his father had left him in this world, but at this very moment, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord was going to use it here.

Father, you always told me that a dragon must rise above the rest!

When I was young, I didn't understand the profound meaning behind your words. Now I understand.

The world has long been the domain of the Human race. We Demon Cultivators, even if we are dragons, must compromise for the sake of survival.

But now, there is an opportunity here that will allow me to hold my head high as a dragon from now on!

If you were me, you would definitely make the same choice, right?

A fierce light burst from the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's eyes as he resolutely threw the Dragon Pearl in his hand into the damaged Nanming Fire Furnace.

The moment the Dragon Pearl entered the furnace, the nine types of flames in the Nanming Fire Furnace fiercely flared up. Earth fire surged like a tide, wood fire rose like a forest, lightning fire exploded like the sky collapsing, heart fire beat like drumbeats, true fire blazed like the scorching sun, pill fire was as pure as jade, spirit fire was as nimble as the wind, demon fire was as wild as a beast, and dragon fire was as majestic as an emperor.

A nine-fire swimming dragon first danced around the Dragon Pearl before swallowing it in one gulp.

It rushed out of the furnace and coiled around it.

A dragon's roar rang out, stirring the sky's Cloud Layer into a boiling churn.

At this moment, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord felt his father's aura. The majestic yet gentle aura made him involuntarily recall the memories of being protected by his father during his childhood.

His eyes slightly reddened as he clenched his jaw, guiding the fire dragon power within the Dragon Pearl toward the Nanming Fire Furnace.

The fire dragon had already finished processing the inserted precious materials, merging the molten liquid into the furnace along with itself.

After the flames dissipated, a missing piece of the Nanming Fire Furnace had been repaired.

This repaired area was not small at all.

More crucially, there was a faintly discernible fire dragon mark on this restored section.

Low exclamations sounded once again.

"He refined the entire Dragon Pearl."

"He used the Dragon Pearl as a precious material and merged it with the Nanming Fire Furnace. He really put everything on the line."

"Does this mean that from now on, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit will also be able to use the power of the fire dragon?"

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord, meanwhile, stared at the furnace with a gaze full of expectation.

In the next moment, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit poked out its fluffy little head, looked at the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, and chirped three times.

Three times. Only three times.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was stunned.

"Doesn't this mean I got the same result as Cang Yazi, Chun Yangzi, and the Red-Robed Guest?"

"How is this possible?!"

"I... I used a Dragon Pearl!"

He had made a painful sacrifice, taking out his father's only relic to use here, yet all he got were three chirps.

Deep down, he felt that it should have been at least four chirps to do justice to what he had paid!

"Why?"

"Why were there only three chirps?!"

For a moment, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord stood frozen on the spot, his bloodshot eyes staring fixedly at the Cultivator hosting the event.

He didn't say a word, but his gaze seemed to be demanding - Are you kidding me? Is this rigged? Are you deliberately suppressing my performance?! Are you trying to hold me down?!!

The hosting Cultivator's expression darkened.

He naturally didn't know why either, but the prestige of Danxia Peak could not be lost.

The atmosphere in the venue turned heavy. Even though the hosting Cultivator only had Golden Core cultivation, he fearlessly met the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's gaze and said coldly, "The result is out. Lord Nine Fires Dragon Lord, please step back for now and let the subsequent Cultivators attempt it."

These words were like a basin of ice water poured over the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's head.

He took three deep breaths before finally calming down. With an ashen face and intense unwillingness, he walked back into the crowd.

The crowd participating in the minor trial was also discussing it.

"This shouldn't be."

"This is a Dragon Pearl, a Dragon Pearl carrying Divine Arts!"

"What exactly are the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's standards?"

"Could it be that..."

Some things were hard to say out loud in front of the organizers, Danxia Peak, but many people had thoughts similar to the Nine Fires Dragon Lord - perhaps, there really was some inside rigging. After all, this minor trial was hosted by Danxia Peak from start to finish.

The truth of the matter was that there was indeed an inside story!

But Wang Yu felt very wronged at this moment: I really didn't do anything.

Ever since Ning Zhuo took the stage and used the Spirit Communication Mirror to "successfully soothe" the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, he hadn't interfered with this minor trial again. He didn't secretly help anyone, didn't show favoritism to anyone, and naturally didn't cheat in any way.

These Cultivators pulling out their life savings and eagerly rushing to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace was something Wang Yu was happy to see.

But the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit indeed only chirped three times.

What was going on?

Wang Yu was also confused.

Ning Zhuo knew the reason - from beginning to end, all the actions of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit were controlled by him.

He would not let anyone receive four chirps. The maximum would only be three.

This, of course, involved his own careful consideration.

"I think I might know the reason," Ning Zhuo suddenly spoke. His voice was normal in volume, but it immediately attracted the gaze of everyone in the venue.

"Repairing the pill furnace is equivalent to repairing the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's body. Naturally, it will trigger its goodwill and closeness."

"But as we all take turns to act, the degree of repair gets higher and higher. From the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's perspective, for the same degree of repair, the later it happens, the less it will feel it."

"There is a very obvious depreciation involved."

"I'm afraid Senior Nine Fires Dragon Lord's situation is not an exception."

The crowd immediately looked thoughtful.

"Furthermore," Ning Zhuo added, "the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was soothed by my Spirit Communication Art."

"Perhaps as time passed, it has begun to develop feelings of fear and alarm again."

"After all, every time you seniors repair the pill furnace, the momentum is imposing, and any one of your abilities could destroy the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit."

"Therefore, the one that truly played the key role was still my Spirit Communication Art!"

Everyone's gazes were quickly withdrawn. They all believed that Ning Zhuo had spoken such a long paragraph just to build up to that final sentence.

"However, it does sound quite reasonable."

"This kid is also getting anxious. But it's understandable; after all, he was the one who broke the deadlock."

"Even so, when it's my turn, I must go all out. Otherwise, I won't even qualify to enter the next round!"

The Cultivators all had their own calculations in their hearts.

When it was Cang Yazi's turn to act, he indeed went all out.

Artifact refining was not his strong suit, so he directly summoned three spirit beast companions.

A Blue-Eyed Water Ape, a Frost-Horned Heavenly Deer, and a Red-Crowned Black-Feathered Crane all took the field to assist him in refining.

Flames rose up, and Cang Yazi pulled out a variety of precious materials.

Toward the end, he even reached out, put his hand in his mouth, and forcefully pulled out one of his own teeth.

This tooth was the color of white jade, with the texture of warm fat. It felt delicate to the touch and carried a golden halo.

He threw the tooth into the furnace and swiftly refined it.

"Is this... a wisdom tooth?"

Mortals' teeth are primarily for chewing and grinding, classifying them as acquired instruments. But wisdom teeth are singularly different - they do not occlude with the opposing jaw and do not govern the duty of eating. Instead, they are buried deep within the furthest recesses of the gums, like unpolished jade hidden in stubborn stone, or stars concealed in a sea of clouds.

The Supreme Cave Profound Spiritual Treasure's Record of Heaven-Opening Relics stated: "Heaven and earth give birth to humans, who have thirty-two teeth. Among them, twenty-eight belong to the number of mortal bones; the remaining four, where the root of wisdom is concentrated, are called wisdom teeth."

Wisdom teeth were the remnants of the root of wisdom.

Cang Yazi pulling out his own wisdom tooth to use as a consumable material was equivalent to fusing a part of his own wisdom and comprehension into the Nanming Fire Furnace.

One tooth was still not enough.

Immediately after, he pulled out his second wisdom tooth and threw it into the furnace to be refined.

Unable to produce a Dragon Pearl, he adopted a strategy of winning by sheer quantity.

Chirp, chirp, chirp.

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit let out three cries.

Cang Yazi sighed, put away his beast pets, and returned to the crowd.

The duration of the fourth round of the minor trial far exceeded everyone's expectations. It only ended at dawn the next day.

The entire Nanming Fire Furnace had been repaired by a small half. The Cultivators participating had consumed their foundations, or rather, emptied their life savings.

Wang Yu was quite satisfied with this and began to look forward to the next round of the minor trial: "Perhaps, after the next few rounds, the Nanming Fire Furnace will be fully repaired!"

He transmitted his divine sense, having the hosting Cultivator announce the list of Cultivators advancing to the next round.

Anyone who had managed to provoke a reaction from the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was allowed to advance and participate in the next round.

But the expressions of the Red-Robed Guest, Cang Yazi, Chun Yangzi, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, and the others were all solemn. They had already gone all out in this round. What else could they possibly bring out in the next round to earn the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's goodwill again?

Watching their departing figures, countless Cultivators who were just there for the show felt a sense of heaviness. Meanwhile, the experts and high-ranking faction members hidden within the crowd were already itching to make their moves.

The moment Chun Yangzi returned to his temporary cave dwelling, he summoned his subordinates: "Report all of our palace's treasury contents to me! Everything that can be utilized must be utilized!"

The Red-Robed Guest was a lone wolf. Although he still had many precious materials on him, none of them were suitable for repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace. Naturally, he lacked the foundation of the Pure Yang Palace, but it didn't matter.

He took the initiative to seek out the Commerce Hall: "Didn't you want to sponsor me earlier?"

Cang Yazi immediately went to Myriad Beasts Peak to find three or five of his old friends. Beings who could be his friends naturally had excellent cultivation and sufficient foundations.

"I am willing to use Form Transformation Pills to purchase your precious materials! As long as it can evoke the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's goodwill, I'm willing to pay a high price."

"Easy to say, easy to say." The Nanming Fire Furnace minor trial had generated massive momentum; his old friends naturally knew the whole story.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord, however, had the door slammed in his face several times.

He was a Demon Cultivator.

At this critical juncture, although he had some connections, helping him meant helping a demon. What if the Nine Fires Dragon Lord actually won? Wouldn't that mean they would be labeled as part of the Demon Cultivator faction?

Even halls like the Commerce Hall didn't actively approach the Nine Fires Dragon Lord to offer sponsorship.

"I cannot lose!" The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's eyes were bloodshot.

He had sacrificed too much; he had thrown his father's Dragon Pearl into it! Thinking about it another way, it was like scattering his father's ashes into the furnace. In truth, a Dragon Pearl was vastly more precious than mere ashes!

"The more difficult the situation becomes, the more I cannot admit defeat!"

"Who else, which faction can help me?!"

Not long after, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord arrived at the Black Market.

A contract was handed to him.

The behind-the-scenes Cultivator spoke with a deep tone: "Nine Fires Dragon Lord, you must think this through. Do you know what you'll have to pay for signing this contract?"

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord snorted coldly with bloodshot eyes: "As long as I win the Nanming Fire Furnace, what do these debts amount to?"

Once the contract was signed, the Cultivator behind the scenes warned: "Even with our sponsorship, you might not necessarily have a chance of winning."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord: "I know. The others have also practically emptied their foundations, but it's much easier for them to secure sponsorships than for me. I fear no competition! If I lose, I'll accept it!"

The behind-the-scenes Cultivator shook his head: "Since you're betting your future, I'm also worried about not being able to collect my debt. Let me give you a reminder. Among these people, there is one who is quite special. You must be careful, extremely careful of him."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was stunned at first, and then a bolt of lightning-like thought flashed across his divine sea: "Ning Zhuo!"

Similar scenes were also playing out for Chun Yangzi, Cang Yazi, the Red-Robed Guest, and the others.

When Zhu Fenxiang and Zhu Guizhi were secretly discussing the matter, they also felt that Ning Zhuo was in a perilous situation. Tonight would probably not be easy for him!

Because the reason the deadlock was broken in the previous round was entirely due to Ning Zhuo's Spirit Communication Mirror!

This was a method the other Cultivators did not possess.

The Cultivators who acted afterward hadn't completely foregone trying to communicate with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, but none of it worked, which was why they had to resort to repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace.

In their view, the reason Ning Zhuo's performance was poor was merely that his background was too thin; he hadn't produced enough sufficiently precious materials.

Ning Zhuo's artifact-refining foundation was not low at all!

True, he still had the weakness of his low Foundation Establishment Stage cultivation. But he could have "demon pets"! Cang Yazi had used demonic beasts to assist him in refining.

Even if Ning Zhuo didn't have one himself, did the people or factions sponsoring him lack them?

Therefore, Ning Zhuo was their biggest enemy!!!

With this consensus, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, Cang Yazi, and the others, along with the factions and cliques sponsoring them, stirred up turbulent undercurrents.

But countless flying letters and envoys arrived at the Bluestone Cave Dwelling, only to find it already empty.

Upon inquiring...

"What? Ning Zhuo went directly to the Demon Subjugation Hall after the minor trial ended? And the Demon Subjugation Hall even sent a special squad to escort him?"

Upon further inquiring...

"What? Ning Zhuo took all of his subordinates with him as well? They even ended their lease on the Bluestone Cave Dwelling?"

Inquiring even further...

"What? Ning Zhuo was publicly kept behind by Zhong Dao, declining all visitors to go all out for the next round?"

Countless people threw up their hands, feeling utterly helpless.

Since Ning Zhuo had single-handedly orchestrated this situation, he had naturally foreseen this scenario and made arrangements in advance.

But Zhong Dao's name could not deter absolutely everyone.

That very night, Wang Yu, holding his fly whisk and wearing a bitter smile, secretly arrived in front of the main hall of the Demon Subjugation Hall.
