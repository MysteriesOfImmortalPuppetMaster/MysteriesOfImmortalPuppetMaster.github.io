Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 901: Situ Xing vs. Ning Zhuo - Big Dipper Seven Luminaries Sword Formation

The time had come.

Starlight burst from Situ Xing's eyes as he suddenly spoke. His voice, ringing out like clashing metal and stone, echoed endlessly within the martial arena.

"Ning Zhuo."

"Next, I will make you pay the price for underestimating me!"

He floated in mid-air, his silver hair fluttering and his deep blue dharma robes flapping wildly. Around him, the phantoms of seven astral swords formed the shape of the Big Dipper, their sword qi rising and falling like the turning tide.

The Big Dipper Seven Luminaries Sword Formation was complete.

At this moment, Situ Xing's aura climbed to its absolute peak. Silvery-white starlight continuously surged from his body, making him look like a true star descending into the mortal realm.

He pointed with the Star-Guiding Sword.

The Big Dipper Seven Luminaries Sword Formation activated.

The phantoms of the seven astral swords simultaneously erupted with blinding brilliance - Tianshu, Tianxuan, Tianji, Tianquan, Yuheng, Kaiyang, and Yaoguang - and shot forward.

The seven luminaries resonated together, their astral power vibrating in harmony. Wherever the seven swords passed, heaven and earth were torn apart, transforming into a starry domain.

Ning Zhuo continuously waved the Five Elements Generation and Destruction Fan, unleashing wave after wave of Five Elements Spells.

But facing the assault of the seven swords, earthen walls were washed away, golden shields were ripped apart, and vine pythons were shredded. Even the red and blue vortex blending water and fire only lasted a few breaths before collapsing.

This was undoubtedly Golden Core-level combat power!

Ning Zhuo's expression remained calm as he reached into his robes with his left hand, pulling out two magic treasures.

One was a golden light collar, round as a full moon, with flowing golden rays. Its ring emitted a golden halo, rendering it indestructible.

The other was a cyan-white jade ring, smooth and gentle as solidified fat. Clear qi circulated within the ring, capable of generating overlapping cyan light barriers to neutralize attacks from all directions.

Both were Golden Core-level defensive magic treasures!

Clang, clang, clang...

The seven swords struck, colliding with the golden light and the cyan-white light barrier. The barrier trembled violently but never shattered. The golden light dimmed, but it did not extinguish.

Ning Zhuo then retrieved several pieces of Mystic Weapon Armor from his robes. There was a saber, a spear, a shield, gauntlets, and even a mechanical leg.

The big-headed youth chuckled softly. "You have a sword formation, and I have a battle formation. Watch."

Shouzhuo Rotary Slash Formation!

This was Ning Zhuo's self-created battle formation - offense and defense rotated smoothly, the core remained stable, the hovering weapons were adaptable, and they attacked alternately.

The several pieces of Mystic Weapon Armor continuously revolved around Ning Zhuo's body. Just as the saber slashed down, the sword cleaved in; just as the spear swept across, the gauntlets followed up. Using offense as defense, it merged the two into one, forming a seamless and endless cycle.

Watching this scene, Situ Xing furrowed his brow deeply, the flames of anger in his eyes burning even brighter.

He was intimately familiar with the intelligence on Ning Zhuo and naturally knew that these Mystic Weapon Armors were newly acquired items. They were spoils from Ning Zhuo's participation in the Rising Cloud Minor Trial.

In Ning Zhuo's arsenal, they were merely scraps; they couldn't be considered his trump cards at all.

Feeling severely underestimated, Situ Xing's indignation was hard to quell. He pushed the seven swords to unleash an even stronger offensive.

The Mystic Weapon Armor - Old Cold Leg clashed against the Tianshu Sword. Ice shards scattered in all directions as it was knocked flying.

A gauntlet met the Tianxuan Sword. The bronze on the gauntlet cracked as it was sent flying back.

The shield blocked the Tianji Sword. After enduring several heavy blows, it finally gave way, splitting open with a large gash.

Under the onslaught of the seven swords, the Shouzhuo Rotary Slash Formation tottered on the verge of collapse.

Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong were both rendered speechless. They both sensed it: Even now, Ning Zhuo was still holding back, not taking this seriously!

Soon, the Shouzhuo Rotary Slash Formation was left battered and torn, prompting Ning Zhuo to voluntarily withdraw it.

Under the siege of the seven swords, the magic treasures - the golden light collar and the cyan-white jade ring - swayed violently. Both the golden light and the light barrier were severely worn down.

The real key issue was Ning Zhuo's spiritual energy.

Supporting two Golden Core-level magic treasures simultaneously with only mid-stage Foundation Establishment spiritual energy was still quite a stretch.

And this was only possible because Ning Zhuo was using the Five Elements Generation and Destruction Fan to continuously amplify his spiritual energy through the mutual generation of the Five Elements.

"Trifling tricks, mere trifling tricks!" Situ Xing, who clearly held the upper hand, growled in anger. "Ning Zhuo, bring out your real skills!"

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly. "Since you insist, take a look at this."

Ning Zhuo tossed out an Mechanism Token.

The token disassembled, transforming into a set of mechanical armor. It was none other than the Black Iron Han Armor.

"Golden Swallow Tailoring Clouds, Han Armor Covers the Body," Ning Zhuo murmured.

The next moment, the Black Iron Han Armor disassembled on command.

The Five Elements Golden Core Wheel and the mechanical spine spun out in succession, firmly attaching themselves to Ning Zhuo's chest and back, respectively.

Twelve Divided Spirit Mirrors orbited Ning Zhuo, simultaneously firing beams of silver light that guided the various mechanical components onto his body.

Ning Zhuo stretched his arms out, puffed his chest, and raised his head. The sound of rapid clacking echoed in his ears.

Very quickly, the breastplate, leg armor, arm guards, and even the Golden Wings on his back assembled together. Finally, the cables around his abdomen coiled twice like green snakes, locking into place and rapidly projecting a membrane of light that covered his entire body.

Black Iron Han Armor - Golden Swallow Form!

Ning Zhuo's aura drastically transformed. He was no longer at the mid-stage Foundation Establishment, but at the Golden Core stage!

After all, the Five Elements Golden Core Wheel contained three Golden Cores within it.

"Oh! Oh! Oh!" Seeing this scene, Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong both cried out in excitement. Their eyes shone brightly, their gazes suddenly becoming blazing hot.

Ning Zhuo's voice was perfectly calm. "Fellow Daoist Situ, here I come."

In the next instant, he fiercely launched his attack, turning into a bolt of golden lightning that constantly ricocheted among the seven swords.

The Five Elements Golden Core Wheel extracted the power of the Golden Cores. The Golden Wings spread from his back, and the Li Fire Tongue Array was pushed to its absolute limit, spewing out bright orange-red flames.

The buffering qi membrane formed by the Dual Polarity Convergence Formation was compressed until it was as thin as a sheet of paper.

But this time, Ning Zhuo felt... perfectly fine.

It was tolerable!

Several months had passed since the battle at the White Paper Immortal City. During this time, his physical body had continued to strengthen. Thus, while wearing the Black Iron Han Armor - Golden Swallow Form and pushing it to its maximum speed, he was now much more capable of enduring the immense pressure.

However, the greatest change wasn't this, but rather Ning Zhuo's control and execution.

Previously, when he piloted the Black Iron Han Armor and flapped its wings, he relied on brute force and reckless charges, moving in straight lines. But now, he was agile and unpredictable. As his wings unfurled, his twists, turns, and maneuvers were incredibly fluid, exuding a kind of natural beauty.

This wasn't the result of Ning Zhuo's arduous practice. Rather, it was due to the Boat-Burning Soul-Crossing Technique combined with his innate talent, which had allowed Ning Zhuo to absorb the flight memories and experiences of many avian creatures.

Ning Zhuo controlled his wings as if it were instinct, utilizing the lifetime accumulations of countless birds!

Ning Zhuo took the initiative to meet the seven swords head-on.

Wearing the Mystic Weapon Armor gauntlets on both hands, he reached out directly and forcibly grabbed the blade of a flying sword. The flying sword struggled and twisted under his grip, but it couldn't break free.

Ning Zhuo applied a sudden burst of strength with his hands.

Bang.

He actually crushed the flying sword to pieces.

The essence of the Big Dipper Seven Swords was spiritual energy, condensed through the methods of the sword formation; they were not tangible, physical objects.

However, the previous Golden Swallow Han Armor absolutely couldn't have achieved this. The reason was that after Ning Zhuo's skills in artifact refining and formation arrangement had improved, he had modified and optimized the Golden Swallow Han Armor, elevating its quality to fully match the Golden Core level.

Having crushed one sword, Ning Zhuo set his sights on the remaining flying swords.

Situ Xing desperately controlled the remaining six swords to keep him at bay.

With two more bangs, Ning Zhuo shattered two more flying swords.

Situ Xing's pupils shrank slightly, all his previous majesty and fury vanishing. "Ning Zhuo's speed clearly still isn't as fast as the flying swords, yet he can grab them with absolute precision. What is going on?"

"Could it be..."

A terrifying hypothesis surfaced in his mind.

After consecutively crushing three flying swords, Ning Zhuo felt the resistance plummet, exposing a massive opening.

He called out in a clear voice, "Fellow Daoist Situ, please receive this move."

Ignoring the remaining four swords, Ning Zhuo charged straight at Situ Xing.

Situ Xing retreated explosively!

At the same time, he formed hand seals, recalling the remaining four flying swords back to his side.

The four swords orbited him once, consuming his spiritual energy to condense a brand-new flying sword. After circling three times, the number of flying swords was replenished, returning to a total of seven.

Big Dipper Seven Luminaries Sword Formation!

Bang, bang, bang...

Yet Ning Zhuo successfully struck out time and time again, detonating the flying swords one by one.

Situ Xing's heart sank to rock bottom. This scene thoroughly confirmed his earlier hypothesis - Ning Zhuo had seen through his Big Dipper Seven Luminaries Sword Formation.

That was why Ning Zhuo could catch the flying swords even though his speed was inferior. He wasn't catching up to them; he was calculating their next trajectories, moving ahead of time, and successfully intercepting them!

Ning Zhuo's attainment in the Path of Formations had reached the Master-level, an incredibly rare achievement.

Furthermore, Situ Xing had used the Big Dipper Seven Luminaries Sword Formation during his battle with Zhu Fenxiang, so Ning Zhuo had already done some homework in advance.

Knowing oneself and knowing the enemy ensures victory in a hundred battles.

Ning Zhuo valued this battle just as much. His apparent contempt for Situ Xing was merely a part of his plan.

A trace of profound shock appeared on the faces of Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong.

This was their first time seeing Ning Zhuo utilize the Black Iron Han Armor - Golden Swallow Form, and they both assumed this was his ultimate trump card.

The moment Ning Zhuo revealed a trump card, the tides of the battlefield were completely overturned.

Situ Xing steadily lost ground, while Ning Zhuo claimed the upper hand.

The seven swords were continuously shattered, but fortunately, the main body of the Star-Guiding Sword was still present and could assist Situ Xing.

Ning Zhuo swung his fists and kicked, doing his utmost to force Situ Xing into close-quarters physical combat.

Under Ning Zhuo's fierce assault, Situ Xing appeared thoroughly battered on the surface, but his footing remained remarkably steady.

The complete set of gear he wore - the Seven Star Crown, Starlight Dharma Robe, Stardust Skin, Star Vortex Bracers, and Star Track Boots - all resonated with one another, providing substantial defensive power.

Thanks to the interception of the seven swords, very few of Ning Zhuo's attacks actually landed on Situ Xing. However, almost every strike that did land sent Situ Xing flying.

Blood trickled from the corner of Situ Xing's mouth, and he grimaced in pain, his heart trembling. "This guy Ning Zhuo has immense physical strength!"

He was a Sword Cultivator, but he did not excel at close-quarters combat.

There was still a difference between Sword Cultivators, Martial Cultivators, and Body Cultivators. Naturally, there were Sword Cultivators who specialized in melee combat. But Situ Xing was definitely not one of them.

This could easily be seen just from his Starlight Dharma Robe.

Star Escape Talisman!

Seeing himself taking beating after beating without being able to shake off Ning Zhuo, Situ Xing decisively activated a talisman.

The talisman rapidly took effect, causing Situ Xing's speed to skyrocket along a star track, widening the distance between him and Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo pursued him relentlessly.

Star Lock Talisman!

Situ Xing ignited another talisman, triggering the dense starlight particles scattered across the battlefield to boost its power and delay Ning Zhuo's movements.

Situ Xing finally stabilized his footing.

While trying his best to maintain his distance from Ning Zhuo, he continuously pulled out Medicinal Pills and consumed them on the spot.

Healing pills caused his injuries to rapidly recover.

Swallowing a Star Gathering Pill allowed him to swiftly restore his spiritual energy.

Clang, clang, clang...

At the same time, Situ Xing controlled the Star-Guiding Sword to hack at the Black Iron Han Armor, sending sparks flying everywhere as he did everything he could to slow Ning Zhuo's approach.

Ning Zhuo's voice rang out indifferently. "Fellow Daoist Situ, if I continue my fierce assault, do you still have the spare energy to unleash any other sword formations?"
