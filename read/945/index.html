Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 945: Gold-Swallowing Beast God

The Red-Robed Guest and Chunyangzi harbored a deep and bitter grudge; this task could only be entrusted to Ning Zhuo.

If it were handed over to Chunyangzi, the Red-Robed Guest would undoubtedly become the biggest obstacle!

"The Red-Robed Guest is a Demonic Cultivator. He has an unruly and rebellious temperament and hates being ordered around. If I am completely blunt, he will definitely turn hostile."

Ning Zhuo pondered all the way as he personally headed to the Cave Manor the Red-Robed Guest was temporarily renting.

The clouds outside the Cave Manor were dyed red, looking as if they had been splashed with strong liquor.

Ning Zhuo submitted a formal calling card.

Before long, the Cave Manor's doors opened.

As Ning Zhuo stepped inside, he immediately felt a wave of heat wash over him. The entire place was marked by smoke and fire, and the surrounding stone walls were faintly blackened, filled with a rough, blazing, and almost fierce fire intent.

Soon, deep within the Cave Manor, he met the Red-Robed Guest.

The Red-Robed Guest sat diagonally on a fiery couch, his crimson robe half-open, and the color of his lips slightly pale. The knuckles of his right hand tapped lightly against his knee from time to time, making him seem a bit impatient.

"Little Friend Ning Zhuo, why have you come this time?" The Red-Robed Guest revealed a slight smile.

He personally had a very good impression of Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo bowed and respectfully offered an intelligence Jade Slip with both hands. It contained information regarding the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar.

The Red-Robed Guest scanned it with his divine sense. "I already know the news about this treasured geomantic land. I just didn't expect it to be connected to the Land Expansion Alliance. Which main peak is backing them?"

Ning Zhuo replied, "Let us set the matter of the main peaks aside for now. If our Nanming Stronghold wants to assault Flowing Cloud Peak, this is the absolute best location."

The Red-Robed Guest frowned. The moment he first received news of the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar, he had thought of Chunyangzi.

Now, Ning Zhuo had suddenly arrived to tell him that Nanming Stronghold wanted to fight for this treasured geomantic land, which inevitably made him think of Chunyangzi again, wondering if Ning Zhuo had come under Chunyangzi's orders.

Ning Zhuo continued, "The Land Expansion Alliance has managed this place in secret for so many years, only for it to suddenly be exposed. Both the Land Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Township are famous, major factions on Flowing Cloud Peak; there will likely be a fierce struggle between them."

"The value of this treasured geomantic land is no weaker than the main headquarters of any major faction. Yet, ironically, its level of protection is currently the weakest."

"Furthermore, it has now fallen into chaos."

"This is the absolute best opportunity for our Nanming Stronghold."

The Red-Robed Guest's frown deepened. He stared at Ning Zhuo, a trace of coldness hidden in his gaze. After thinking for a moment, he said, "Mm, go on."

Ning Zhuo observed his expression and understood in his heart. "If our stronghold fights for this place, Senior Chunyangzi will definitely participate with full force. I can deduce from the intelligence that this place is extremely beneficial to Chief Chunyangzi's cultivation."

The Red-Robed Guest immediately snorted coldly, displeasure showing on his face. "I share a deep and bitter grudge with him, and it's not like you don't know that. Little Friend Ning Zhuo, did you come here just to ask for a snub?"

Ning Zhuo slowly shook his head. "Senior Red-Robed Guest, it is precisely because Chunyangzi wants to fight for this place that you must fully support this endeavor."

The Red-Robed Guest was astonished and raised an eyebrow. "What do you mean by that?"

He was an itinerant cultivator and walked the Demonic Path. He had been a lone wolf for most of his life, and even after joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he was ostracized and pushed out to other mountain branches, which showed his lack of political acumen.

Ning Zhuo explained, "Nanming Stronghold cannot restrain Chunyangzi. Even if we do not make a move, Chunyangzi will probably act on his own. Don't forget, he is not a solitary figure; he brought his entire sect to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect in one fell swoop."

"Therefore, if Chunyangzi wants to fight for this place, we cannot stop him."

"He could completely break away from Nanming Stronghold, lead his Chunyang Sect to directly assault Flowing Cloud Peak, and occupy this place!"

"But if our Nanming Stronghold acquires it, then you and I will have a share in this treasured geomantic land."

"In the future, if Senior Chunyangzi wants to borrow this land for cultivation, he will still have to consider the opinions of the others in the stronghold."

The Red-Robed Guest fell into silence. Upon careful thought, he tasted the logic hidden within Ning Zhuo's words.

Ning Zhuo continued, "If you make a move this time, you will definitely earn a share of the merit."

"When that time comes, this place will not only be Chunyangzi's opportunity, but it will also become Senior's authority within Nanming Stronghold."

"If he gains power, Senior will have merits to leverage. If he tries to monopolize it, Senior will have the grounds to contest it."

A look of deep thought appeared on the Red-Robed Guest's face.

Ning Zhuo added, "Moreover, the fire veins at the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar have been gathered and restrained by the Yang-Locking Well for many years. The fire energy is extremely abundant, making it highly suitable for you, Senior. This treasured geomantic land could also be used by Senior to assist in refining Medicinal Pills."

Despite the Red-Robed Guest being a Demonic Cultivator, his attainments in alchemy were very profound. Ning Zhuo's words had struck a chord right in his heart.

The Red-Robed Guest revealed a hint of hesitation. "What you say is not without reason, it's just... cough, cough."

He suddenly turned his head to the side and began to cough.

At first, it was just a low cough, but quickly, the coughing grew heavier.

He pressed one hand against his chest and gripped the edge of the fiery couch with the other. Beneath the cuffs of his crimson robe, his knuckles turned slightly pale from the exertion.

The next moment, a streak of dark red spilled over his lips and landed on the back of his hand.

The color of the blood was very deep.

It wasn't ordinary blood, but carried an aura of lightning and fire.

Ning Zhuo's gaze focused.

He immediately and sharply perceived the truth behind the Red-Robed Guest's injuries. "It seems the Red-Robed Guest didn't emerge completely unscathed when he charged through the Formation earlier."

"This is a good time to negotiate!" Ning Zhuo immediately and keenly realized this point.

Although the Red-Robed Guest was a Nascent Soul, he was not made of flawless iron and stone. His injuries would wear down his patience and make him more acutely aware of his own circumstances.

Ning Zhuo felt he could appear a bit more assertive and conduct a high-pressure negotiation.

"The Red-Robed Guest is already on the verge of hesitation, lacking only the final push. Perhaps this pressure is exactly what's needed to finalize the matter!"

Meanwhile.

On Flowing Cloud Peak, inside a hidden cavern.

A cultivator of the Goldstone Alliance held a blade-testing short knife and viciously slashed down at Liu Jinke's shoulder.

Clang!

The blade bit half an inch into the flesh but was unexpectedly blocked by something beneath the skin. The sharp edge slid to the side, ultimately only carving a slanted gash across Liu Jinke's shoulder.

Golden blood surged out, immediately sealing and mending the wound.

Liu Jinke's eyes lit up slightly. "This Golden Netted Garment is not bad."

This garment was no ordinary robe; it was a cluster of crimson-gold threads as fine as hair, resembling a handful of spiderwebs dyed by the morning glow.

After Liu Jinke refined it, he caused the golden threads to enter his body.

The golden threads drilled beneath his skin, spreading along his fascia, bone gaps, and muscle fibers, densely weaving around his internal organs to provide inner defensive power.

Yet, on the surface of his skin, there was nothing to be seen except faintly emerging pale cyan-gold patterns, like a large-scale tattoo.

"If I were to face the blades, swords, axes, and hooks of the Shouzhuo Rotary Slash Formation again..." Liu Jinke muttered.

The Goldstone Alliance cultivator retreated. Liu Jinke then took out a Blood Body Talisman.

The talisman was crimson red, its paper as thick as leather, and it even emanated a warm pulse when touched.

Liu Jinke forcefully activated it. Instantly, half of the vital essence blood in his body was forcibly extracted and poured into the talisman.

The talisman flew from his hand, transforming into a crimson-gold blood shadow.

The blood shadow was initially blurry, but its facial features, figure, and aura gradually solidified until it looked completely identical to Liu Jinke. Even its chest emanated a faint golden light, as though it also concealed a core bead within.

Liu Jinke's face abruptly turned pale.

Having half his vital essence blood drawn out at once, even with his Golden Liquid Reverting Core Physique, made his vision darken. He felt utterly hollow inside, weak and exhausted.

But the next moment, a golden blood core bead inside his abdomen dispersed, turning into a cloud of deeply nourishing golden mist that surged and circulated throughout his body.

The depleted blood qi was rapidly replenished. His meridians reacted like drought-stricken land encountering a sweet rain, and his chest swelled with power once more.

The Blood Body avatar stepped forward and threw a punch, causing the air to explode with a booming sound.

Liu Jinke stared at the avatar and broke into a smile.

To others, this item was a life-risking talisman that would break their bones and tear their sinews. But to him, it was incredibly suitable. As long as he still had his golden blood core beads, he could use them to replenish his vital essence blood, and then use that vital essence blood to create avatars.

After practicing combat with the Blood Body, Liu Jinke then tried swallowing a Fire Warding Pill.

The pill entered his mouth as cold and clear as a blade of ice. The medicinal efficacy did not harm his body; instead, it surfaced from the inside out, covering Liu Jinke's skin in a pale white-gold membrane.

A cultivator activated a fire technique to burn Liu Jinke.

The white membrane on the outside of Liu Jinke's body glowed slightly, blocking the flames on the outer layer. Although there was still a burning pain, it was difficult for the heat to penetrate deeply into his blood.

Liu Jinke couldn't help but clench his fists, his heart surging with excitement. "This pill is excellent! With this pill, why should I fear Ning Zhuo's Fire Element Spells?!"

The Blood Body Talisman and the Fire Warding Pill were of high quality, but as consumables, Liu Jinke only tested them briefly and didn't dare overuse them.

Finally, he took out the Chaotic String Rending Tuning Fork.

Just like the previous treasures, the tuning fork was also sponsored by the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak.

The Chaotic String Rending Tuning Fork was entirely dark gold, its body slightly twisted, not resembling a proper weapon. The outer edges of its prongs were densely covered in fine sound runes - patterns that looked like snapped strings, cracked porcelain, and shattered gold.

Normally, when held in hand, it was dead silent, but the moment Liu Jinke poured a strand of golden blood into it, the fork trembled lightly.

Zheng-

The sound was shrill, ear-piercing, and fragmented, as if someone had suddenly snapped a taut golden string right next to one's ear.

Liu Jinke gripped the fork and swept it through the air. Instantly, layers of crimson-gold sonic waves exploded out. Wherever the sonic waves passed, the flight trajectories of several mechanical birds used for testing were instantly thrown into chaos. Their feathers rattled loudly, and one of them even crashed directly into a stone pillar, shattering into a pile of bamboo bones and iron fragments on the ground.

Golden light flared brightly in the depths of Liu Jinke's eyes. "Ning Zhuo, with this tuning fork in my hand, will you still be able to keep playing your zither?"

At this moment, a cultivator arrived to report. "The Altar has been set up. Lord Flowing Gold Guest, please head over immediately."

"Oh? It's already prepared?" Liu Jinke, knowing the inside story, departed at once.

The Altar was located in the deepest part of the cloud cavern.

The area was piled high with metals and stones. Great White Golden Essence, Cold Edge Golden Essence, White Bin Iron, and Geng Metal Sand were layered to form golden sacrificial steps. In the center of the Altar stood a beast statue. The beast resembled a Pixiu, yet its belly was as large as a furnace, its mouth split all the way to its ears, its maw full of golden teeth, and its eyes half-closed as if in deep slumber.

Jin Mantang personally hosted the ceremony.

He normally had a smile plastered on his face, but at this moment, his expression was solemn. He had taken off all the treasure rings from his ten fingers, and all the precious light radiating from his body had been restrained - hiding whatever could be hidden.

He stood in front of the Altar, his usually obese body standing uncharacteristically straight.

With a wave of his hand, the surrounding cultivators backed away one by one. Even Lei Wangyue, the old woman with the golden hairpin, and the others no longer made a sound.

The depths of the cloud cavern suddenly became frighteningly quiet, leaving only the countless metals and stones gently vibrating beneath the Altar. They emitted a fine, fragmentary ringing, as though tens of thousands of copper coins were gently clinking against one another.

Under guidance, Liu Jinke walked onto the Altar.

Jin Mantang took out a roll of golden silk that was so old it had turned black.

The moment the golden silk was unfurled, low beastly roars actually resounded within the cloud cavern. The sound was muffled, seeming to come from deep within the mountain. Liu Jinke almost felt as if he could see a massive beast lying beneath the earth, using its breath to sniff the numerous Sacrificial Offerings on the Altar.

Jin Mantang began to recite the invocation.

His tone was strange, unlike the elegant speech of the current era. It carried the ancient tones of merchants offering sacrifices to gods. Every word was deep and low, like a copper hammer striking golden bricks.

"Gold is born from the mountains, wealth circulates through the world."

"The beast's mouth swallows gold, the god's belly hoards wealth."

"With what I offer, I breach the god's teeth."

"With what I lose, I exchange for the god's gaze."

"All gold with spirit, returns to its belly."

"All wealth with masters, heeds its name."

"God of Swallowing Gold, Beast of the God of Wealth."

"Open your mouth to accept the offerings, lower your eyes to bestow the domain!"

As the first recitation of the invocation ended, the treasure rings at the four corners of the Altar lit up simultaneously.

Spirit Stones, precious materials, and fragments of Magic Artifacts flew out from the rings one after another, falling onto the golden steps beneath the Altar. As soon as those items touched the sacrificial steps, it was as if they had been bitten by an invisible maw. The spiritual light on their surfaces rapidly dimmed, and their golden qi transformed into fine streams that drilled into the belly of the beast statue.

The Gold-Swallowing Beast statue kept its eyes closed.

Jin Mantang's expression remained unchanged as he continued to recite the second time.

This time, the surrounding cultivators each took out their own offerings.

The Goldstone Alliance offered Great White Golden Essence.

The Thunder Cloud Society presented Purple Lightning Golden Sand.

The Land Expansion Alliance brought out Earth Lung Hidden Gold Soil.

The Floating Life Society contributed three boxes of Golden Essence.

...

The offerings piled higher and higher, and the golden light of the Altar shone ever brighter. Yet, the Gold-Swallowing Beast statue's belly only glowed slightly; it still did not open its eyes.

Jin Mantang looked at Liu Jinke. "It's your turn."

Liu Jinke took a deep breath.

He raised his hand and pressed it against his chest. His quasi-Divine Art "Golden Liquid Reverting Core" slowly began to circulate. For a moment, the golden blood inside his body surged like a rushing river, violently whirling along his meridians.

The first golden blood core bead was forced out of his chest.

The golden blood core bead fell into a golden cup.

Thud.

The sound was very light, yet it made the entire Altar tremble slightly.

Jin Mantang's third recitation of the invocation followed, his tone pitched even higher than before.

"Golden blood becomes the core, within the core lies life."

"Offering a portion of life, pleading for a portion from the god."

"God of Swallowing Gold, Beast of the God of Wealth."

"Open your mouth to accept the offerings, lower your eyes to bestow the domain!"

The light in the beast statue's belly grew brighter, but it still did not open its eyes.

Liu Jinke's heart tightened. Gritting his teeth slightly, he forced out a second bead.

The second golden blood core bead fell into the cup.

The beast statue's mouth full of golden teeth seemed to click together lightly.

Crack.

The sound was extremely faint.

Yet, hearing it sent a chill through Liu Jinke's heart.

He suddenly realized that he wasn't making a trade with a god, nor was he bargaining with Jin Mantang.

He was offering food to a deity.

And the Gold-Swallowing Beast God was currently picking and choosing his flesh and blood.

Jin Mantang did not rush him, only saying in a low voice, "It is still lacking."

A flash of hesitation and heartache passed through Liu Jinke's eyes, but he immediately recalled the humiliation Ning Zhuo had brought him, as well as his own current circumstances.

Having reached this point, could he still turn back?

Absolutely not!

Thus, the third, fourth, fifth...

The golden blood core beads fell into the golden cup one by one.

With every bead that fell, the Altar trembled once. The spiritual light of the mountain of metals and precious materials piled up around it dimmed by a fraction. The golden-black light in the beast statue's belly grew thicker and thicker, as if a bottomless abyss was slowly opening within the golden beast's stomach.

After more than a dozen golden blood core beads were thrown in, the Gold-Swallowing Beast statue on the Altar suddenly twitched slightly.

Jin Mantang abruptly raised his voice, almost roaring:

"Gold is born from the mountains, wealth circulates through the world!"

"All gold has a master, but returning to the belly makes it true!"

"Offering ten core beads, offering ten parts of blood!"

"I beseech the God of Swallowing Gold to open its mouth!"

"I beseech the Beast of the God of Wealth to bestow the domain!"
