Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 930: What Exactly is the Foreign Affairs Hall Doing?

Ning Zhuo fell asleep with a hint of tipsiness.

He slept easily and sweetly that night. By the time he woke up, it was already the morning of the second day.

He began his daily routine.

First were the Three Sects' Supreme Methods, especially the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art, which had always been his focal point. Cultivating this demonic art required accumulating progress over time. Unless he obtained sufficiently suitable demonic souls, the art wouldn't see a sudden leap in progression.

Next was the practice of his Cultivation arts and crafts.

Finally, he reviewed his combat methods.

As Ning Zhuo recalled his two recent battles, his thoughts became clearer.

"In terms of cultivation, Situ Xing is merely at the Foundation Establishment Stage, below Liu Jinke."

"But in terms of the completeness of his combat system, the complexity of his methods, and the pressure of his adaptable mid-battle changes, Situ Xing is actually above Liu Jinke."

Behind Situ Xing stood the Situ Clan.

He had his lifebound flying sword, the Star-Guiding Sword, as well as a complete set of star-path equipment: the Seven Stars Crown, Starlight Dharma Robe, Stardust Cape, Star Vortex Bracers, and Star Track Boots. He possessed the Big Dipper Seven Luminaries Sword Formation and the Cosmic Starry Sky Formation Diagram. He even had three Golden Core-level demon beasts as external support: the Star-Maned Sword Wolf, the Seven Stars Sword Butterfly, and the Star Luminary Sword Falcon.

Situ Xing himself was also a genius. He was a sword cultivator with razor-sharp sword momentum and a methodical approach to setting up formations. In the early stages of a fight, he would suppress his opponent with flying sword techniques, using the residual power to prepare the battlefield. Once his sword formation was complete, his combat power surged straight to the Golden Core level.

In contrast, while Liu Jinke was undoubtedly strong, his strength lay entirely in his Innate Talent.

The Golden Liquid Returning Pill Physique made his golden blood his source. His blood contained metal-attribute spiritual energy, allowing him to absorb metal qi to regenerate his golden blood.

His spiritual energy reserves far exceeded those of his peers, and he was fierce in close combat. He could transform his golden blood into needles, cover his body to form armor, and turn objects into gold. Even after being decapitated, he could use his golden liquid to reattach his head and return from the dead.

Such an Innate Talent was truly astonishing.

But because of it, Liu Jinke's combat style relied far too heavily on his Golden Liquid Returning Pill Physique.

His Midas Touch, golden needles, and golden armor all stemmed from his golden blood. He was like a naturally sharp blade, but the blade's trajectory was too straight, lacking variation. Once Ning Zhuo used the Flame Washing Golden Taint Art to cleanse the Midas Touch's corruption, Liu Jinke's most crucial advantage in a war of attrition was rendered useless.

After experiencing both battles, Ning Zhuo clearly felt that fighting Situ Xing brought him far more pressure.

If Ning Zhuo hadn't had the secret assistance of the Luo Shu, supported by his realm in the Dao of Formations, and dismantled his opponent's moves successively with multiple mechanical constructs, he absolutely would not have won so easily.

The battle with Liu Jinke - which appeared to be the slaying of a Golden Core cultivator and thus far more shocking to the world - was, in essence, just a battle of attrition.

He was an independent cultivator lacking a cohesive system.

Situ Xing, however, was a clan genius with a complete set of methods.

"These two battles also exposed two of my own weaknesses."

First, my mechanical arts are insufficient.

He had numerous mechanical constructs and a profound foundation, but he had not yet truly mastered the mechanical arts within the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder]. Without the enhancement of mechanical techniques, many of his mechanical constructs could only unleash their base power and couldn't be exponentially amplified.

Second, my spiritual energy consumption is too great.

After all, he was only at the mid-Foundation Establishment Stage.

Although the Five Elements Generation-Destruction Fan could rotate the Five Elements and expand his spiritual energy, and the Black Iron Han Armor contained a Golden Core Wheel; continuously activating Golden Core magic treasures, Golden Core mechanisms, and Five Elements Techniques in battle consumed an immense amount of energy. If an enemy was more capable of dragging out a fight, or if opponents attacked in waves, Ning Zhuo might not remain so composed.

"I need a method to replenish my spiritual energy mid-battle."

Medicinal pills, spirit wine, energy-storing mechanisms, and special talismans could all be considered.

"Mechanical techniques can be found within the inheritance of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder] and practiced."

"At the same time, the zither score [Phoenix Sings to the Morning Sun] gifted by Dong Nishang can also assist my mechanical bird swarm."

"Lastly, the poem gifted by Bai Jiyun also bolsters the mechanical bird swarm."

During the previous battle against Liu Jinke, this poem had already revealed its wondrous utility by amplifying Ning Zhuo's offensive power. Although the poem had lost its effect, it was only temporary. As long as he replenished and nurtured it with Literary Qi in the future, it could be triggered again. It wouldn't permanently lose its effect until the poem's characters were thoroughly worn away.

"Next, my opponent should still be Liu Jinke."

"After all, I deliberately left an opening, announcing in public that I would challenge Liu Jinke again to fulfill my promise of beheading him!"

This Innate Talent, the Golden Liquid Returning Pill Physique, had its drawbacks.

The Golden Liquid Returning Pill Physique allowed Liu Jinke to reattach his severed head because his Upper Dantian had already vanished. His divine thoughts, divine sense, and even his soul's perception were deeply integrated with his physical body, golden liquid, and blood. This method formed the foundation of his near-immortality, but it also caused him to lose many of the conveniences enjoyed by normal cultivators.

Ordinary cultivators required the precise manipulation of their divine sense to refine and control magic treasures.

However, because Liu Jinke's divine sense was fused with his flesh, blood, and golden liquid, it certainly turned his flesh and blood into a weapon, but it also made it difficult for him to equip the vast majority of conventional magic treasures. Even if he forced himself to equip them, he could hardly command them as easily as moving his own arm.

Therefore, the external enhancements Liu Jinke could receive were bound to be heavily restricted.

If enemies wanted to use him to deal with Ning Zhuo, due to the Myriad Manifestations Sect's rules, Liu Jinke was indeed the most suitable pawn - an already unsheathed blade.

They could give him metal-attribute precious materials to help him recover his golden blood.

Give him targeted talismans to make up for his mobility and defense.

Give him external objects that counter mechanical bird swarms and suppress fire-element purification.

They could even find special magic treasures compatible with his Golden Liquid Returning Pill Physique for him.

However...

Ning Zhuo's lips curled up slightly.

None of this would be easy.

Conventional magic treasures would be of very little help to Liu Jinke!

Ning Zhuo looked forward to Liu Jinke coming at him again.

Their next battle would outwardly feature Liu Jinke, but in reality, it would be a probe from the enemy forces after doubling down in the shadows. As long as Ning Zhuo withstood it, he would be able to discern much more.

Who gave him the magic treasure?

Where did the resources come from?

Which faction did the methods lean toward?

Was the person behind him part of the old guard from Flowing Cloud Peak, or was another high-ranking Sect official making a move?

In Ning Zhuo's arrangements, Liu Jinke had already become his bait, carrying Ning Zhuo's fishing line.

Ning Zhuo gathered his thoughts and continued his cultivation.

Meanwhile, unbeknownst to him, the news of the establishment of the Naming Stronghold alliance had already spread to many parts of the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate through a series of flying letters, recorded images, and spoken accounts.

Heavy Formation Peak.

Dong Chen sat upright deep within the formation pavilion, a water mirror suspended before him.

Within the water mirror, the sequence of events was playing on repeat: Ning Zhuo establishing the alliance at the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion, slaying Liu Jinke to establish his prestige, and using debt rights to determine the hierarchy.

This Peak Master of Heavy Formation Peak, who was also the current Sect Master of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, initially just watched in silence. Only when the image showed Ning Zhuo calmly wrapping up the situation did Dong Chen finally nod slowly.

He praised softly, "He possesses the finesse of the Righteous Path."

In his eyes, Ning Zhuo wasn't just a young genius who only knew how to fight. He knew how to build momentum, lay down rules, and leverage situations to achieve his goals.

Establishing an alliance and setting up a stronghold was something most Golden Core cultivators couldn't manage well. Yet Ning Zhuo, a mere mid-Foundation Establishment Stage cultivator, was able to navigate among five Nascent Soul cultivators, multiple Golden Core experts, and numerous geniuses, successfully weaving a network of power.

This was extremely rare.

"With this child joining our Myriad Manifestations Sect, if properly guided, he will become a pillar of support in the future."

"However," he slightly furrowed his brows, "how is the Foreign Affairs Hall's investigation into Ning Zhuo's background going?"

The subordinate beside him bowed his head. "There is no conclusive report yet."

Dong Chen's gaze darkened. "No conclusive report yet?"

"This child has been in the Sect for quite some time now, repeatedly causing major incidents. The Foreign Affairs Hall still can't dig up the roots of a foreign cultivator?"

The subordinate did not dare to say more and could only lower his head further.

Dong Chen stopped looking at him and merely said indifferently, "Pass on my words. If the Foreign Affairs Hall continues to be this sluggish, have its Hall Master, Xiang Ji, come to Heavy Formation Peak and explain himself personally."

Ten Thousand Beasts Peak.

After reading the intelligence, Tuoba Huang laughed heartily.

Previously, he had been worried that Cang Yazi might have a lapse in judgment and join the Naming Stronghold. But as it turned out, Cang Yazi never showed up, which put Tuoba Huang in an excellent mood.

"This kid is interesting. At the mid-Foundation Establishment Stage, he actually dares to stir up wind and rain on Flowing Cloud Peak. Let's see how far he can go from here."

As he spoke, he remembered the Heart Snake Horse-Tripping Bell he had sent via Han Muma.

"If he uses this bell, sooner or later he'll expose the true depth of his Spirit Communication Art."

Tuoba Huang had always been highly fixated on the fact that Ning Zhuo had pacified the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

Since the Spirit Communication Art could soothe an artifact spirit, it naturally could also communicate with demon beasts and spirit beasts. How could Ten Thousand Beasts Peak not be tempted by this?

But at this thought, Tuoba Huang also frowned and asked, "Has the Foreign Affairs Hall still not dug up Ning Zhuo's background?"

Upon receiving a negative response, he sneered. "Has Xiang Ji been sleeping on the job at the Foreign Affairs Hall all these years? It's just an outsider junior, and yet they still haven't figured it out."

Pill Sunset Peak.

When Wang Yu received the news, his expression showed little surprise.

His hands had long since reached into the Naming Stronghold. The execution of the Poison Annihilation Wanderer was his biggest pawn. Aside from that, there were many loose threads formed by debts and favors that he could pull whenever necessary.

"The remaining twenty percent of the Naming Fire Furnace is the key moving forward. Tie Kuang..."

Hundred Herbs Peak.

The Peak Master, Heng Zifang, had no reaction whatsoever to this intelligence.

Spirit herbs lay scattered across the desk before her; the fragrance of medicine, the scent of grass, dewdrops, and roots entangled to form a small world of its own.

She was immersed in her own world.

Purple Lightning Peak.

After Meng Wuyan finished reading the intelligence, a flash of regret passed through his narrow purple eyes.

"It truly would have been wonderful if this child had joined Purple Lightning Peak."

But Ning Zhuo had already been fought over by multiple parties and was deeply tied to the Naming Fire Furnace. Now that he had established the Naming Stronghold, it was equivalent to setting up his own foundation. How could a person like that easily submit to Purple Lightning Peak?

Thinking of this, Meng Wuyan also asked about Ning Zhuo's background.

His subordinate reported in a low voice, "The Foreign Affairs Hall has no concrete results yet."

Meng Wuyan chuckled softly. "The Foreign Affairs Hall truly knows how to get things done."

Sword Cry Peak.

Ling Juejian was currently wiping his sword.

The blade, as cold and clear as frost, reflected his stern brow and eyes.

When the recording of Ning Zhuo slaying Liu Jinke was delivered, Ling Juejian only watched it once.

When he saw the slash Ning Zhuo used to decapitate his opponent, sword intent flared slightly in his eyes.

A desire for battle arose, but vanished just as quickly.

"His cultivation is too low," Ling Juejian said indifferently.

Ning Zhuo could slay Golden Core cultivators, but he was ultimately only at the mid-Foundation Establishment Stage. Ling Juejian was at the Nascent Soul Stage; if he harbored the desire to challenge Ning Zhuo, he would be suspected of bullying the weak.

However, his interest in Ning Zhuo had not vanished.

"How is the Foreign Affairs Hall's investigation going?"

After receiving the answer, Ling Juejian sneered. "Just one Ning Zhuo, and they still can't find anything?"

The Foreign Affairs Hall.

In front of Hall Master Xiang Ji, flying letters were piled high.

These flying letters had all come because of Ning Zhuo.

Xiang Ji's scalp tingled with stress. "Ning Zhuo again!"

He had already dispatched many extra personnel on short notice, but bizarrely enough, there were truly no results. Many of his teams encountered various difficult obstacles and all sorts of accidents; they either got stranded halfway or vanished without a trace like stones dropped in the ocean.

It wouldn't be surprising if just one team failed, but for nearly all of his personnel to encounter such situations made it highly suspicious.

Xiang Ji could already anticipate that the demands for accountability would only increase from here on out.

He rubbed his temples and gritted his teeth. "Send more people! Expedite it!"

Demon Subjugation Hall.

Lu Xu'an shook his head. "I am determined to join the Demon Subjugation Hall, how can I attend to everything else at the same time?"

Ning Zhuo had once extended an invitation to him, but he hadn't gone to watch the ceremony.

Yun Niaoniao was filled with regret.

"If I had known, I would have gone!"

"Sigh, I shouldn't have hesitated."

Alchemy Hall.

Shen Hongyao watched the segment where Ning Zhuo severed Liu Jinke's head over and over again.

The flaming great saber spun, and the Golden Core cultivator's head fell to the ground!

As she watched it repeatedly, her eyes grew brighter and brighter.

She admired Ning Zhuo, and not just for his combat strength; she admired him even more for his courage to act and his capability to get things done. She herself was tied down in the Alchemy Hall by Pill Sunset Peak, Wang Yu, and numerous rules, rendering her unable to act independently in many matters. Ning Zhuo, however, could leverage situations to break out of deadlocks, establish an alliance and a stronghold, and actively seize his own destiny.

This freedom made her envious, and it made the fire burning deep in her heart blaze even brighter.

Inside a cave manor.

When Xiao Juxia learned of the intelligence regarding the Naming Stronghold, he suddenly felt a stir of inspiration.

Upon re-entering the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he had constantly been choosing clues related to Qin De.

Despite searching bitterly, he had found nothing.

Now, he suddenly received a burst of spiritual intuition.

"I'll divine it once more."

He took out his divination tool and lit a Lifespan Incense.

The smoke curled gracefully, but instead of rising, it sank downwards.

Xiao Juxia's face rapidly paled, and several fine lines appeared at his temples as he aged several years out of thin air.

The price of divination was naturally very heavy.

However, a sudden flash of spiritual intuition was also extremely rare.

It wasn't until the Lifespan Incense burned out that Xiao Juxia abruptly opened his eyes, a trace of blood trickling from the corner of his lips.

"The Myriad Manifestations Sect, the Naming Stronghold?!" Xiao Juxia muttered in a low voice, his gaze gloomy.

Ignorance Peak.

The Blind Foolish Old Man handed the intelligence in his hands to his son and daughter.

His pair of children both kept their heads lowered in silence, unable to conceal the shame on their faces.

Back then, they had accepted bribes and persuaded the Blind Foolish Old Man to prevent Ning Zhuo from participating in the minor exam, pushing the opportunity toward Ban Ji instead. At the time, they thought they were securing benefits for their family.

Now, Ban Ji's reputation had plummeted, while Ning Zhuo was climbing higher every step of the way, even establishing an alliance and stronghold with five Nascent Soul cultivators gathered behind him.

Whether their choice was right or wrong was already abundantly clear.

Outside the Myriad Manifestations Sect, the super factions - the Shen Clan, the Lin Clan, and the Situ Clan - were also paying close attention to this matter.

Shen Xi, Lin Jinglong, and Situ Xing were all the Foundation Establishment Stage figureheads of their respective clans. Their decision to join the Naming Stronghold was absolutely no small matter.

They would continue to observe and wait.

And remain ready to provide funding at a moment's notice!

Right now, the Naming Stronghold was just a bustling spectacle within the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion, not yet worth them placing massive bets. But if the Naming Stronghold truly succeeded in assaulting Flowing Cloud Peak, securing a foundation on the peak, and gaining a firm foothold, then the Naming Stronghold would no longer be an empty title!

By then, increasing their aid and support would be the safest and most cost-effective move.

The higher-ups of the Pi Clan, meanwhile, breathed a sigh of relief.

"Pi Fujie did very well this time."

"Being able to bend and stretch according to the situation is the true mark of our Pi Clan's disciples."

"Perhaps we can also fight for a chance to have Pi Fujie join the Naming Stronghold and attach himself to Ning Zhuo?"

The higher-ups engaged in lively discussion.

Moonflower Sect.

"How mighty is Pill Sunset Peak? The Naming Stronghold has only just been established and its foundation is not yet stable, yet she actually chose Ning Zhuo?"

"While they haven't officially and fully joined the Sect, this matter might still be turned around."

The cultivators of the Moonflower Sect had shifting thoughts.

Mu Yuehua had once come close to becoming the current generation's Fairy Moonflower; the Moonflower Sect had never truly let go of her inherent value. Seeing her lean toward Ning Zhuo now, many felt not only regret but also a desire to intervene.

Over at Qing Huangzi's location, it was an entirely different scene.

When he received the news of Ning Zhuo successfully establishing the alliance and the mechanical bird swarm shining brilliantly in the battle against Liu Jinke, he was at first pleasantly surprised, but subsequently filled with worry.

He was surprised because Ning Zhuo indeed hadn't let down the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder].

He was worried because the greater Ning Zhuo's momentum became, the harsher the wind and waves he would face.

"What he faces next won't just be one Liu Jinke."

Qing Huangzi worked diligently to rush the production of mechanical flying birds, occasionally coughing softly, his complexion looking a shade more ashen than usual.
