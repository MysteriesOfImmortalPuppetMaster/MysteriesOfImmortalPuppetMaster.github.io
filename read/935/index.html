Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 935: The More He Fights, The Stronger He Gets

"Ning Zhuo, accept your defeat!" Liu Jinke's explosive shout was like metal striking a bell, vibrating the air until it rippled.

He lunged forward to kill, like a mass of raging flames cast from golden blood!

His arms were covered in armor, and blades sprouted from his fingertips. The Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Embryo in his chest throbbed rapidly, its golden-red light pulsing through his flesh. His imposing aura was already inhuman.

At this moment, his sharp edge as a Golden Core-level body cultivator was fully revealed!

The golden blades tore through the air with a shrill whistle like ripping silk, aiming straight for Ning Zhuo's chest and abdomen.

Cao Gui's face turned deathly pale, and Jiang Xiaobian paled from nervousness.

Situ Xing's eyes narrowed. Staring at Ning Zhuo, he thought to himself, "Is he going to reveal his trump card and release that Golden Core-level mechanical puppet?"

Ning Zhuo sat upright on the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot. Though there was solemnity in the depths of his eyes, there was no panic.

The Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment on his body suddenly lit up with a layer of faint silver patterns.

Usually, this Dharma garment only showed flowing cyan patterns of clouds and thunder, appearing elegant and refined. But at this moment, as Liu Jinke lunged close, the silver threads on the garment acted like countless tiny lute strings, plucked by an invisible mechanical melody.

Buzz...

A low hum emanated from the depths of Ning Zhuo's robes.

The sound was not loud, but extremely peculiar. It didn't sound like a zither, a bell, or a drum. It sounded more like thousands of tiny mechanisms starting up simultaneously - gears meshing, reeds vibrating slightly, copper shafts rotating. Myriad subtle noises merged into a faint yet dense mechanical melody.

Liu Jinke's golden blades had already thrust to within three feet of Ning Zhuo.

But this thrust suddenly deviated by a hair.

It wasn't that Liu Jinke was holding back; it was that the sonic waves emitted by the Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment disrupted the circulation path of the golden blood within his body at an extremely close range, ruining the rhythm of his movements.

The Golden Liquid Returning Core Body was indeed tyrannical. Golden blood was spiritual energy, and golden blood was the physical body. Every movement of Liu Jinke was closely linked to the flow of golden blood within him. However, precisely because of this, when the sonic waves caused close-range disturbances, affecting the microscopic rhythms of his blood flow, these tiny impacts accumulated, ultimately forming a disruption to his entire movement.

Liu Jinke's pupils contracted slightly.

"The Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment!" He naturally knew the inheritance intelligence regarding the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder].

Therefore, without any hesitation, Liu Jinke swept the golden blades on his other hand horizontally, forcefully slashing apart this layer of sonic disturbance.

Ning Zhuo, however, seized this split-second opportunity to control the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot and jerked it backward violently.

However, with the Thousand Catty Fall pressing down on the arena, the earthly gravity across the entire martial arts field was immense. The black wheels beneath the mechanical chariot emitted a dull friction sound, and its retreat speed was far slower than usual.

Liu Jinke sneered, "Can you hide?"

The Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Embryo in his chest throbbed again, and the blood gold prime liquid fed back into his whole body. The golden blood rhythm that had just been disrupted by the sonic waves quickly smoothed out.

He stomped heavily on the ground beneath his feet. Bang! The black stone bricks cracked. Dragging a ferocious afterimage in midair, he closed in on Ning Zhuo once more.

Ning Zhuo raised his right hand, and four wooden boxes flew out from his Storage Belt.

The wooden boxes opened simultaneously.

Four Mystic Weapon Armors - a saber, a sword, an axe, and a hook - burst out from the boxes!

Clang!

Clang, clang!

The clanging sounds exploded one after another, like an armory bursting open and cold blades being unsheathed.

The great saber's edge was ghostly white, bringing with it an arc of light as it flew out. The longsword's chill was condensed, its sword light as thin as a starlit thread. The great axe was as heavy as a mountain; the moment it appeared, it pressed down until the air felt solid. The short hook was sinister and eerie; with a turn of its curved blade, it looked like a cold fang under the moon, specifically seeking out the enemy's flaws.

These four Mystic Weapon Armors were all Golden Core-level, heavy gifts sent by the Weapon Armor Cave. As soon as they emerged, the aura of Golden Core-level magic treasures suppressed the martial arts arena, forcefully blocking in front of Ning Zhuo.

Liu Jinke slammed his fist onto the axe weapon.

Boom! Golden light and the dark cyan axe phantom collided. Airwaves exploded outwards, shaking the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot so hard that it slid backward for several zhang.

The axe weapon was smashed backward, a golden dent appearing on its blade. Liu Jinke's arms also went slightly numb, and countless sparks flew from the surface of his golden armor.

The viciousness in his eyes grew even stronger: "Mere Mystic Weapon Armors think they can stop me?!"

Ning Zhuo didn't answer, simply changing his hand seals.

The four Mystic Weapon Armors didn't fight independently; instead, they began to revolve around Ning Zhuo.

The saber was in front, the sword at the side, the axe taking up the rear, and the hook lurking in the diagonal blind spot. The auras of the four linked together, their cold lights shuttling back and forth to form a ring of chilling weapons.

Not only that.

Ning Zhuo also released several of his older Mystic Weapon Armors.

One of them was shaped like a human leg - it was the Mystic Weapon Armor: Old Cold Leg. The moment this leg appeared, frost instantly formed. Fine ice patterns covered the surface of the leg armor, and its joints clicked and clacked, as if a martial artist with frozen bones was stretching their limbs.

Several other Mystic Weapon Armors, such as fists, shields, and short blades, also flew out one after another, each taking up a formation position.

A strange glint flashed through Situ Xing's eyes: "The Shouzhuo Rotation Formation!"

Ning Zhuo had created this formation under the watchful eyes of the public during Young Lord Qingwu's Rising Cloud Minor Trial, so this piece of intelligence was widely known.

The primary feature of the Shouzhuo Rotation Formation was the seamless transition between offense and defense, and the mutable nature of the suspended weapons.

With the four Golden Core-level Mystic Weapon Armors as the main axis, and older ones like the Old Cold Leg as the auxiliary ring, they revolved around Ning Zhuo to form inner and outer weapon wheels.

The saber, sword, axe, and hook primarily handled the killing and attacking.

The fists, shields, legs, and blades primarily handled absorbing and receiving blows.

Once the formation was established, it was as if an invisible array thread connected the Mystic Weapon Armors together. If Liu Jinke wanted to get close to Ning Zhuo again, he would first have to break through this swirling vortex of cold light made of Golden Core-level magic treasures and mystic weapon killing intent.

"Bring it!" Liu Jinke roared furiously.

The Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Embryo in his chest pumped violently, coating his body in a layer of golden blood three times thicker than before. Swinging his fists like a tide of golden light, he crashed head-on into the Shouzhuo Rotation Formation.

In an instant, a dense barrage of booming explosions erupted in the martial arts arena, almost blurring into one continuous roar.

Clang clang clang clang clang!

The saber armor slashed horizontally, but was parried by Liu Jinke's arm, leaving a white scratch on his golden armor.

The sword weapon thrust diagonally, its sword light like a point of cold starlight, aiming straight for Liu Jinke's ribs, but was forcibly blocked by a golden shield condensed in his palm.

The axe weapon sank down like a crushing mountain. Liu Jinke crossed his arms to block it head-on, the black stone bricks beneath his feet shattering inch by inch.

Seizing the momentum, the hook weapon circled around to his back and flicked its cold blade, hooking onto the golden armor at his waist in an attempt to drag him into the eye of the formation.

Liu Jinke let out an angry roar. Golden blood spurted from his waist and transformed into golden blades, forcibly knocking the hook weapon away.

The next instant, the Mystic Weapon Armor: Old Cold Leg kicked fiercely from below!

Bang!

This kick struck the side of Liu Jinke's knee. The power of frost drilled inwards through the gaps in his golden armor. Liu Jinke's leg stagnated and his expression changed slightly, but immediately, his golden blood heated up, forcefully dispersing the cold.

The crowd on the sidelines watched with unblinking eyes.

Ning Zhuo, a mere mid-stage Foundation Establishment cultivator, had actually withstood Liu Jinke's fierce close-quarters onslaught!

One must know, this was entirely different from the previous battle. At this moment, Liu Jinke was utilizing the combat style he excelled at the most.

Yet Ning Zhuo resolutely held his ground.

He wasn't fighting alone; it was more like he was commanding a special army composed of various weapons!

Clang clang clang, crash crash crash...

Sparks flew everywhere, and the noise assaulted the eardrums.

With his golden blood, Liu Jinke fiercely clashed against the swarm of mystic weapons. Every move was heavy and powerful, like a golden-armored fierce general charging into enemy lines.

Ning Zhuo, on the other hand, sat upright on the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot, his body motionless. His hands formed seals rapidly as his divine sense and spiritual energy were intensely consumed, directing more than ten Mystic Weapon Armors to advance and retreat in turns.

Saber light like snow, sword light like thread, axe phantoms like mountains, hook gleams like the moon. The fist armors roared, the shield armors shifted sideways, and the Old Cold Leg would occasionally kick out with a sinister burst of freezing force from a bizarre angle.

Golden light and mystic light intertwined into a net.

Wave after wave of air slammed into the restrictions surrounding the martial arts arena, causing the protective light curtain to tremble wildly.

The expressions of the observing cultivators from all factions became solemn.

A Beast Tamer from the Bird and Beast Manor murmured, "Even with his mechanical birds countered, he can actually bring out a battle formation of Mystic Weapon Armors."

Cold light flashed in the eyes of a saber cultivator from the Water-Severing Saber Pavilion. "This Shouzhuo Rotation Formation has very interesting transitions between offense and defense. To think it was something he merely drafted in the Mystic Armor Cave, yet today it displays such immense practical combat ability!"

A cultivator from the Thunder Cloud Society stared at Ning Zhuo and snorted coldly. "How much longer can his spiritual energy hold out? These are Golden Core-level Mystic Weapon Armors, and he's using four at once! Besides those, there are quite a few low-grade Mystic Weapon Armors, making the numbers even higher."

All of these were rapidly depleting Ning Zhuo's spiritual energy reserves.

Behind Ning Zhuo, the Spirit Siphoning Box operated continuously. The mechanisms inside emitted an endless series of clicking sounds, as if a miniature mechanism workshop was spinning desperately on his back.

The surrounding spiritual energy was absorbed by the Spirit Siphoning Box, filtered through the Five Elements Spirit Talismans, converted into usable spiritual energy, and then channeled into his Qi Sea to circulate.

The Cloud Tide Origin-Returning Talisman inside his lapel was also continuously burning.

The talisman's light was like water, guiding the cloud energy to transform into tiny cloud waves that surged into Ning Zhuo's body layer by layer. From a distance, it looked as if a faint cloud tide constantly swirled around him, with faint five-colored lights bobbing uncertainly within the tide.

The Dharma Pools were also activated one after another.

After the first Dharma Pool was exhausted, the second five-colored Dharma Pool at Ning Zhuo's waist lit up. The central earth-yellow light stabilized his Qi Sea, while the four energies of wood, fire, metal, and water successively fed back into him. His face had just turned pale, but the color was forcefully brought back.

When all else failed, Ning Zhuo would take out a Five Qi Origin-Returning Pill, pop it into his mouth, tilt his head back, and swallow it.

Situ Xing thought to himself, "Dharma Pools, the Spirit Siphoning Box, the Cloud Tide Origin-Returning Talisman, the Five Qi Origin-Returning Pills... These methods can all restore spiritual energy. Brother Ning has indeed made a lot of pre-battle preparations."

Liu Jinke's offensive grew fiercer and fiercer.

The effects of the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Embryo were simply too strong!

Every time Liu Jinke was knocked back by the axe weapon, the golden-red light on his chest would pulse once; every time his golden armor was slashed open by the saber or sword, the blood gold prime liquid would surge out and rapidly mend the cracks; every time the Old Cold Leg kicked a freezing chill into him, his golden blood would counterattack like a hot current, expelling the cold.

The more Liu Jinke fought, the more ferocious his expression became.

The initial cold sternness on his face was long gone, replaced by the madness of someone pushed to their absolute limits.

"I must win this battle!"

"If I lose, how will those factions that sponsored me treat me?"

"I cannot possibly lose! With so many treasures supporting me, what right does Ning Zhuo have to defeat me?"

"Ning Zhuo!" Liu Jinke's eyes erupted with golden light as he suddenly opened his mouth and spat out a mouthful of golden blood.

This mouthful of golden blood didn't turn into needles or armor; instead, it transformed into a tiny golden seal that crashed violently into the Thousand Catty Fall.

Rumble!

The power of the Thousand Catty Fall surged again!

The already oppressive earthly gravity multiplied several times over. On the martial arts arena, the invisible pressure descended like a mountain, causing the black stone bricks to emit popping sounds as they gave way under the unbearable weight.

The entire frame of the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot groaned and creaked, its movements grinding to a near halt.

The revolving speed of the Mystic Weapon Armors surrounding Ning Zhuo was also suppressed and slowed by a fraction.

Grasping this brief moment, the golden armor on Liu Jinke's arms suddenly expanded. Taking a slash from the saber armor and a thrust from the sword weapon head-on, he forcefully crashed through the outer weapon wheel and lunged into the inner circle!

Bang!

He punched the shield armor.

The shield armor flew backward, colliding three feet in front of Ning Zhuo. It was only after being slowed by the sonic waves emitted by the Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment that it avoided smashing directly into Ning Zhuo.

Liu Jinke's second punch followed closely, aimed straight at Ning Zhuo's face!

Ning Zhuo's eyes sharpened as the Old Cold Leg kicked fiercely from the side.

Incredibly, Liu Jinke neither dodged nor evaded, allowing the Old Cold Leg to kick him under the ribs. His golden armor dented, and frost spread, but his punch still hurtled towards Ning Zhuo.

This punch carried the momentum of a golden mountain crushing down.

At this moment, Ning Zhuo almost threw out Snow-Colored Maiden Hui.

If he released Snow-Colored Maiden Hui, her ice-sealing methods could instantly block this punch and even counter Liu Jinke.

He also thought of Snow Pivot Guard Xie.

This was also a powerful trump card that could stabilize the situation.

Ning Zhuo suppressed this thought, forcefully stifling the urge to summon Snow-Colored Maiden Hui.

The silver patterns on the Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment blazed brightly!

The mechanical melody suddenly spiked in volume. Thousands of tiny mechanical sounds merged into a billowing wall of sound, clashing head-on with Liu Jinke's punch.

Boom!

The sonic waves shattered, and the golden fist was deflected.

The edge of the fist grazed past the side of Ning Zhuo's face. The fist wind cut his cheek with a slight sting, and a few strands of hair were severed by the golden energy, fluttering down through the air.

Exclamations erupted from the sidelines.

Cao Gui almost lost his voice, "Young Master!"

But the rage in Liu Jinke's heart only intensified. "Just a little bit more!"

Just a little bit more, and he could have truly hit Ning Zhuo! He couldn't wait to see his fist make intimate contact with Ning Zhuo, beating the latter until he vomited blood, his face deformed, sending him flying!

Liu Jinke lunged again, his punches like a frenzied tide, his golden blood like a boiling sea.

Ning Zhuo had no choice but to open his third Dharma Pool.

Five Elements spiritual energy poured back in.

But the Spirit Siphoning Box let out a sharp, abnormal screech.

It had been pushed to a high intensity since the battle began, and it was subjected to intermittent external influxes. Therefore, at this moment, several internal mechanical components blew out, and green smoke plumed from one side of the box.

Lu Zeng's expression changed. "The Spirit Siphoning Box is about to give out!"

Ning Zhuo's face darkened slightly.

There was no panic in his heart; instead, under extreme pressure, his thoughts became clearer than ever before.

The pages of the Luo Shu began to activate secretly.

Ning Zhuo comprehended while he fought.

Every operation of the Shouzhuo Rotation Formation reflected in his mind. The rotation angle of the saber armor, the timing of the sword weapon's thrust, the residual recoil after the axe weapon took a hit, the trajectory of the hook weapon's flank, the rhythm of the Old Cold Leg filling the gaps, the formation's tremors after the fist and shield armors absorbed an impact... All of these, originally as complex as tangled hemp, gradually transformed into clear array lines in Ning Zhuo's eyes.

The light of mathematical principles shone into the battle formation.

Waves of enlightenment surfaced one after another in the depths of Ning Zhuo's heart.

Gradually, every Mystic Weapon Armor in the formation began to steadily borrow strength from one another.

The axe weapon bore the weight, transferring the residual force to the saber armor.

The saber armor shed the sharpness, guiding the sharp intent into the sword weapon.

The sword weapon thrust at specific points, forcing out the enemy's footwork.

The hook weapon used the flaws in his footwork to drag his body.

The Old Cold Leg would then strike from the blind spot created by the drag, injecting freezing force.

Repeating this cycle endlessly, continuously making micro-adjustments to the trajectory and timing of every Mystic Weapon Armor.

The saber armor no longer slashed head-on; instead, it cut at an angle, slicing away the outer edge of Liu Jinke's punches.

The sword weapon no longer rushed to attack; instead, it hid its blade behind the saber light, exclusively thrusting into the cracks of the golden armor.

The axe weapon no longer simply pressed down; instead, after receiving Liu Jinke's heavy punches, it used the momentum to spin, directing the recoil force towards the hook weapon.

The hook weapon no longer rushed to hook onto him; instead, it waited three inches ahead of where Liu Jinke's foot would land, laying down traps in advance.

The Old Cold Leg completely transformed into an insidious weapon that filled the gaps. Whenever Liu Jinke's blood and qi transitioned or his golden armor repaired itself, it would kick a freezing chill from an extremely tricky angle.

The tide of the battle quietly shifted.

The observing cultivators did not immediately notice, but as the person involved, Liu Jinke sensed it very quickly.

At first, relying on the endurance provided by the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Embryo, he took the slashes of Ning Zhuo's Mystic Weapon Armors head-on. But gradually, every heavy punch he threw had a portion of its power absorbed by the axe weapon, which then transformed in the very next instant into a faster, diagonal slash from the saber weapon reflecting back at him.

With every dodge, his steps were snagged by the hook weapon more and more often.

Every time he mended a crack in his golden armor, the Old Cold Leg would invariably kick over.

"What is going on?" Liu Jinke was filled with shock and rage. "This battle formation is getting stronger?!"
