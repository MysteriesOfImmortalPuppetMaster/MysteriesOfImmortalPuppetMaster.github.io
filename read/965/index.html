Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 965: Red-Robed Guest vs. Daoist Yunhu

Yue Qingshan was dead.

Although the contract for the Five-Win Martial Contest clearly stated the four words "life and death permitted," and although it was signed by all three parties, verified by a spirit contract, and witnessed by a Sect Deacon - leaving no one able to pick out a single fault with the rules -

Rules were rules, and human nature was human nature.

The death of a Nascent Soul cultivator was not the same as the death of ten Golden Core cultivators.

With existences like Soul Formation and Void Refinement in year-round seclusion and rarely showing themselves, a Nascent Soul cultivator was the pillar of a region, the master of its winds and rains, and the very sky for countless disciples.

Now, such a figure had actually died on the spot in the martial arena!

His corpse was already shriveled and ashen, his flesh and skin appearing as if all vitality had been drained away, leaving behind only a stiff, withered husk. That earth cloud had also completely darkened, the remaining earth-cloud essence within its core corroded to pieces by the poison annihilation, resembling rotten wood hollowed out by insects.

The cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance returned carrying Yue Qingshan's corpse.

Qiu Lei's knuckles turned slightly white. He didn't speak, only placing his palm over Yue Qingshan's face. Earth-yellow spiritual energy slowly seeped in, barely managing to close those eyes that had died with lingering grievances.

In the Earth Expansion Alliance seating area, someone whispered, "This grudge cannot go unavenged."

Another gnashed their teeth, "This demon Tan Zhu is too murderous! It's just a martial contest, was there a need to go this far?"

The cultivators of White Cloud Township had complex expressions. Standing on the same side as the Earth Expansion Alliance, they naturally shared a common enemy. However, after witnessing Tan Zhu's three quasi-Divine Arts and his completely crushing combat stance, a layer of deep chill was buried beneath this shared enmity.

"Wanderer Poison Annihilation... truly lives up to his reputation," Wandering Cloud Elder sighed deeply.

On the side of Nanming Stronghold, there was similarly little joy. Pure Yangzi's gaze was profound. Ning Zhuo was equally aware: this battle was won, but the storm had only just begun.

Just as everyone expected, once this news spread, it immediately sparked widespread discussion throughout the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate. The matter of Yue Qingshan's death quickly acted like a massive boulder crashing into a lake, stirring up layer upon layer of waves.

"Did you hear? In the third match of the martial contest, the Earth Expansion Alliance's Yue Qingshan died."

"It's not just a death! It's said he had five layers of purification methods ready: a cyan jade round mirror, short white incense, a silver-white sand screen, a water-blue light shield, and platinum talismans - all treasures specifically meant to counter poison. And the result? He was still swallowed whole by Tan Zhu's poison annihilation quasi-Divine Art."

"How is this a scheduled duel? This is clearly a Demonic Cultivator committing murder!"

"The contract explicitly states 'life and death permitted.' How can you blame Tan Zhu?"

"Hmph, be that as it may, this was a martial skill exchange, he could have just stopped at the point of victory. He clearly had the power to hold back, yet he deliberately poisoned Yue Qingshan to death. How dare Nanming Stronghold recruit such a demonic thug into their ranks?"

"Nanming Stronghold is so desperate for the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar that they aren't picky about what they eat."

"But that was a Nascent Soul cultivator!"

"Indeed, a Nascent Soul cultivator. Placed in many other regions, they would be figures capable of establishing their own Sects. Only a massive enterprise like the Myriad Manifestations Sect could lose a Nascent Soul cultivator and still continue the martial contest. If it were an ordinary Sect, the mountain gate would have been shaken long ago."

"That's exactly why it's the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

Some of these words were merely sighs of emotion, while others carried obvious provocation.

Soon, more voices of condemnation and written attacks began to point toward Tan Zhu.

Some spoke with veiled sarcasm, "Tan Zhu's lifespan is nearing its end, so naturally he isn't afraid of making enemies. Once he finishes killing, he can just dust off his sleeves and go into the ground. But Nanming Stronghold still has to remain on Flowing Cloud Peak, and Ning Zhuo still has to get by in the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Sooner or later, this debt will have to be repaid."

The rumors shifted several times a day.

On Danxia Peak, after listening to his subordinate's report, Wang Yu sat alone for a long time.

The rosy clouds of Danxia Peak were always brilliantly colorful, with fire vein qi rising from deep within the mountain's belly, casting a warm red glow on the pavilions halfway up the mountain.

But today, Wang Yu felt these rosy hues were somewhat heavy.

He stood by the window, watching the floating fire clouds in the distance, his expression complex.

The subordinate whispered, "Peak Master, the outside world is verbally attacking Tan Zhu, and many voices are already implicating Nanming Stronghold. Should we secretly intervene and quell some of it?"

Wang Yu did not answer immediately.

What surfaced in his mind was the image of Zhongli Mei drawing out three hundred years of pill qi in front of the Naming Fire Furnace that day.

Three hundred years of pill qi! Not three hundred days, nor thirty years.

This was almost the most profound accumulation of an alchemist's entire life!

But when Zhongli Mei drew the pill qi and injected it into the pill furnace, his expression was very calm. It was as if what he was extracting wasn't the foundation of half his life, but a handful of ordinary furnace ash.

Wang Yu sighed softly in his heart.

Only he and Tan Zhu (Zhongli Mei) knew that the order for Yue Qingshan to die was given by Wang Yu himself.

"So, what will your reaction be next?" Wang Yu subconsciously shifted his eyes, looking in the direction of Heavy Formation Peak.

Heavy Formation Peak.

Inside the formation pavilion at the peak, Dong Chen sat in silence.

Before him lay a dark-cyan formation plate. Light and shadows flowed across the formation plate, repeatedly replaying the scenes from the miasma poison martial arena.

Yue Qingshan stepped on the earth cloud, mud and stone overwhelming the sky.

Tan Zhu raised his hand, and the poisonous smoke petrified them.

Yue Qingshan's five layers of protection formed.

Tan Zhu squeezed his five fingers, and poison rose from within the defenses.

Yue Qingshan fell.

The image stopped at the exact moment Yue Qingshan crashed into the mud.

Dong Chen's fingers rested on the edge of the formation plate, his fingertips gently rubbing against it. The skin on his knuckles was slightly white from excessive force.

He was not a man to show his joy or anger, but at this moment, the depths of his eyes remained terrifyingly dark.

On the surface, Yue Qingshan was a cultivator of the Earth Expansion Alliance, but in reality, he was Dong Chen's friend, and they had a life-and-death bond.

Back then, both were merely at the early stage of the Golden Core. During an earth vein collapse, Yue Qingshan guarded a formation core for Dong Chen for three days. In those three days, earth fire surged upward, and the mountain ranges poured in. Yue Qingshan's body was scorched halfway black, yet he forcibly held that formation core.

Afterward, Dong Chen also risked his life, going all out with his techniques to carve out a sliver of hope in a desperate situation, allowing the two of them to escape by a hair's breadth.

Later on, the two cultivated on their own, and their interactions weren't considered frequent, but the friendship remained.

After Dong Chen took on a high-level position at Heavy Formation Peak, he secretly invited Yue Qingshan. Yue Qingshan, all alone, actively joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect. On the surface, he was a member of the Earth Expansion Alliance on Flowing Cloud Peak, but in truth, he was Dong Chen's most capable and trusted ally!

But now, Yue Qingshan was dead.

Dead in the third match of the Five-Win Martial Contest.

Dead by Tan Zhu's hands.

Dong Chen closed his eyes, and only after a long while did he slowly exhale a breath of turbid qi.

"Tan Zhu." His voice was very soft, yet intensely heavy.

Tan Zhu's lifespan was nearing its end.

Killing him held little meaning.

The killing intent deep in Dong Chen's eyes slowly sank, like dark clouds pressing into the earth, ultimately transforming into an even colder hatred.

"Investigate."

"Find out exactly who is behind Tan Zhu. Find out why he joined Nanming Stronghold!"

Thinking of this, Dong Chen brought out a fishhook.

The hook's body was extremely thin, like a crescent cold moon folded into a frosty blade. It was a dark, ghastly white with no trace of blood; ordinary people who saw it would feel a chill in their hearts.

At the tip of the hook was a point of gloomy blue, cold starlight, looking as if it could illuminate the very depths of a soul.

The fishhook landed in the palm of Dong Chen's hand without the slightest weight.

Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor.

Ning Zhuo sat alone in the quiet room, his eyes slightly closed.

He was reviewing the battle from the day, secretly mobilizing the Luo Shu to assist in his calculations.

"It's not that I haven't seen Divine Art-level poison techniques. Tan Zhu's poison annihilation was clearly just a quasi-Divine Art."

"There's actually an uncrossable chasm between a quasi-Divine Art and a true Divine Art. Why was it so tyrannical in this battle?"

In another cave manor.

Old Man Taoli sat cross-legged on a futon, also recalling this battle.

"A conventional quasi-Divine Art excels in quality. A Technique excels in quantity. When quality and quantity oppose each other, if a quasi-Divine Art has not truly reached perfection, it is possible for it to be forcibly drowned out by an ocean of Techniques."

"Just like a piece of gold and iron; although it is harder than mud and sand, if there are thousands of layers of mud and sand, it will still be buried."

"So, although a quasi-Divine Art is strong, it isn't invincible."

Old Man Taoli narrowed his eyes: "But Tan Zhu is different."

"His quasi-Divine Art doesn't just have quality, it also has quantity."

"A massive quantity."

"An ocean-like quantity!"

"It's just that it is too restrained. From the outside, one only sees a wisp of smoke. But in reality, it is a mountain of poison, a sea of poison!"

"Moreover, this kind of poison seems to be... pill poison?"

The figure of Tan Zhu appeared in Old Man Taoli's heart: "This old demon has hidden himself very deeply."

"But ultimately, he is a member of our Stronghold."

"We won this match. As long as we win one more, we can station ourselves on Flowing Cloud Peak and occupy the fengshui treasure land that is the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar."

"Hehe!"

"Looking at it now, joining Nanming Stronghold was indeed a wise choice."

For the fourth match, it was White Cloud Township's turn to send someone out first.

White Cloud Township did not hesitate in the slightest, directly choosing the Cloud Marsh Martial Arena.

Here, there was no solid ground, nor was there a completely empty sky.

In all directions, up and down, there were endless expanses of cloud qi. The cloud qi was like a marsh; the thick parts were like white mud bogs, while the thin parts resembled silver mist and flowing springs. In the distance, layers of clouds stacked like islands floating in mid-air. Nearby, the cloud tide slowly undulated, each rise and fall bringing forth moist spiritual energy and fragmented cloud light.

In the center of the martial arena lay a vast cloud lake.

The cloud lake wasn't made of water, but was formed from the compaction of extremely fine and dense white clouds. When a cultivator stepped onto it, they would feel the softness beneath their feet, yet they wouldn't sink into it. Beneath the cloud lake lay countless cloud veins resembling undercurrents, crisscrossing like an invisible giant net.

The cultivators from the three parties - Nanming Stronghold, White Cloud Township, and the Earth Expansion Alliance - successively took their seats.

Every cultivator's expression was exceedingly solemn.

The Five-Win Martial Contest had already seen Nascent Soul blood.

From the moment Yue Qingshan died, this scheduled duel was no longer just a superficial contest. It had turned into a bloody gamble between Nanming Stronghold, the Earth Expansion Alliance, White Cloud Township, and even the various factions on Flowing Cloud Peak!

Inside the martial arena, a cultivator slowly appeared.

He had a round face with a short beard, wasn't tall in stature, had a mild complexion, and carried a giant white jade kettle taller than a person on his back.

The kettle's body was pale and lustrous, with thirty-six layers of cloud sea patterns painted on its surface. A cyan jade cloud stopper was stuffed into the kettle's mouth, and faint white cloud qi continuously seeped out from the stopper's edge. That cloud qi did not disperse; instead, it slowly drifted around his shoulders, cuffs, and waist.

Daoist Yunhu!

Ning Zhuo's eyes flashed slightly, a wisp of doubt rising in his heart: "This Nascent Soul cultivator is famous for raising cloud beasts and is not adept at combat. Why would they dispatch him?"

It was Nanming Stronghold's turn to send someone.

Before this battle, the cultivators had held discussions.

According to common sense, sending Tan Zhu naturally would have been the safest bet. Anyway, the agreement for the Five-Win Martial Contest did not make clear restrictions in this regard. However, Tan Zhu had already explicitly stated that because of his poor condition from the previous battle, he could not make a move.

"Alright, let me deal with him," Red-Robed Guest let out a sinister laugh and slowly stood up.

As soon as he stood, his scarlet robes billowed without wind. Dark blood patterns on the magic robes crawled from the hems all the way to the cuffs, and from the cuffs, they swam toward the back of his hands, as if thirsting for slaughter.

Tan Zhu was withered, silent, and obscure, like a deep well concealing poison.

Red-Robed Guest, however, was extroverted, flamboyant, and bloody, like a boiling pool of blood, making absolutely no effort to conceal his demonic nature.

"A life-and-death battle like this is something I've looked forward to for far too long," Red-Robed Guest licked his lips.

Red-Robed Guest stepped into the arena.

With every step he took, a ring of dark red ripples would spread across the cloud lake. The white clouds were tainted by his blood qi, their edges taking on faint red lines, like pristine silk stained with fresh blood.

Seeing that it was Red-Robed Guest, Daoist Yunhu immediately revealed a trace of nervousness.

He knew exactly who he was facing: Red-Robed Guest, a Nascent Soul Demonic Cultivator, a ferocious blood-path villain who was once hunted by the Pure Yang Palace. He later joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect, only to be marginalized to the Emerald Blood Mountain Gate. This person cultivated the Blood Demon Great Art and also dual-cultivated the Blood Pill Scripture, feeding on blood and proving his Dao through slaughter. His demonic qi was extremely heavy.

Facing such a person, Daoist Yunhu naturally couldn't feel at ease in his heart.

What's more, Yue Qingshan had just died at Tan Zhu's hands in the previous match.

"White Cloud Township, Yunhu," Daoist Yunhu cupped his hands and said.

Red-Robed Guest narrowed his eyes, joking with a smile, "You came to see this seat carrying a kettle on your back. Are you planning to stuff this seat inside it?"

Daoist Yunhu's smile stiffened slightly, but he still replied, "Fellow Daoist jests. This poor Daoist is merely someone who herds clouds and raises beasts. Entering the arena today, I am only here to do my best."

Red-Robed Guest threw his head back and laughed loudly: "Do your best? Good, very good!"

He raised his hand and flicked his blood-colored sleeve: "In the last match, Tan Zhu killed one. If you don't do your best, wouldn't it make this seat look inferior to him?"

Daoist Yunhu's heart sank.

Red-Robed Guest's words sounded like he was competing with Tan Zhu. But to Daoist Yunhu's ears, they sounded like a death knell tolling prematurely.

The jade chime sounded.

The fourth match, begin!

Almost at the exact moment the jade chime rang, Daoist Yunhu pulled the cyan jade cloud stopper from the giant white jade kettle on his back.

Boom.

Cloud qi surged from the kettle's mouth.

The originally calm Cloud Marsh Martial Arena seemed to be stirred by an invisible giant hand. The cloud veins beneath the cloud lake lit up simultaneously, and thirty-six streaks of cloud light flew out from the kettle's mouth, landing in all directions and transforming into thirty-six thin jade cloud gates.

The cloud gates hovered in the air, echoing one another.

White clouds rolled, and the cloud veins converged.

A Pot Heaven Cloud Formation rapidly unfolded!

Daoist Yunhu shook his sleeve and took out another round plate. The round plate was divided into nine grids, each grid holding a different type of cloud grain, rosy mist powder, cold dew, and demon blood.

A feeding tray for rosy mist beasts!

As the round plate turned, all nine grids lit up together.

From deep within the kettle's mouth, a low buzzing sound simultaneously echoed.

The first cloud beast charged out.

A White Wave Heaven-Draping Manta Ray!

It was shaped like a massive manta ray, its wings spreading out to cover several zhang of the cloud sky. It was snow-white all over, with streaks of water-ripple cloud whiskers drooping from its belly. It didn't fly by flapping its wings, but glided along the cloud qi, resembling a tide of white sea sweeping across the firmament.

The moment it appeared, the cloud lake kicked up white waves.

The turbulent white waves swept towards Red-Robed Guest.

Red-Robed Guest stood in place, his scarlet robes fluttering loudly. He looked at the White Wave Heaven-Draping Manta Ray, a hint of mockery appearing in his eyes.

He raised a hand and launched a palm strike.

Blood light spewed from his palm, transforming into a giant scarlet handprint that pushed horizontally across the cloud lake.

Boom!

The bloody palm collided with the white waves; cloud qi exploded, and blood light splattered. The White Wave Heaven-Draping Manta Ray opened the cloud cavity on its belly and actually sucked the shockwaves of the bloody palm into its stomach.

The cloud whiskers beneath its belly all turned red, as if they had drunk blood water.
