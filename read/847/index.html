Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 847: Soul Search

Song Taosheng's gaze turned out the window, as if piercing through the heavy night to see the distant Cloud Prison.

"That day, I audited the sutra debate," he said, his eyes couldn't help but flicker, actually revealing a hint of fear.

"I witnessed Zhao Hansheng's defeat with my own eyes. Whether it was the School of Mind or traditional Confucian studies, no matter what move Zhao Hansheng made, Qin De had a countermeasure."

Song Taosheng continued, "I can confirm that for the majority of the debate, Qin De was intentionally holding back. In many instances, he could have pressed his advantage to secure victory, but he chose not to strike or relentlessly pursue. He was using Zhao Hansheng to hone his own scholarship."

"Qin De has already developed an unfathomable aura!"

Saying this, Song Taosheng let out a deep sigh.

"Zhao Hansheng and Qin De made a three-year pact, agreeing to debate again in three years. But to my ears, that is nothing more than an idiot's daydream."

Kong Ran's heart jolted. "Senior's meaning is..."

Song Taosheng looked at him, a trace of pain flashing in his eyes. "In three years, Zhao Hansheng will undoubtedly lose!"

Kong Ran: "..."

Song Taosheng continued, "Rather than facing defeat again in three years, it is better to eradicate Qin De right now. He is a scourge, a scourge that should have been eradicated thirty years ago!"

"Back then, after the incident with the Sage's Great Thief Scripture, I strongly advocated for his eradication! What is the use of keeping such heretical teachings? Kill him, burn it, and let it completely disappear from the world!"

"But Duanmu Zhang... Sigh, the benevolence of a woman!"

"Duanmu Zhang pitied Qin De's talent and wanted to give him a chance to turn over a new leaf. He said the Confucian Sect is rooted in benevolence and should not recklessly harbor murderous intent."

Song Taosheng smiled bitterly. "I once pinned my hopes on Zhong Dao. The Hall Master of the Demon Subjugation Hall is strictly impartial and enforces the law like a mountain. If he acted, Qin De would undoubtedly die."

"But I didn't expect... the higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect would actually use Qin De for their own agenda."

Kong Ran frowned slightly. "Their own agenda?"

Song Taosheng nodded. "Qin De is more useful alive than dead. He is a stain on Confucian cultivators, a massive mountain pressing down on our heads. With him around, Confucian cultivators cannot hold their heads high, which is why the development of our group has been extremely limited over the years."

"This is also why Duanmu Zhang voluntarily stepped aside and was willing to let Zhao Hansheng take charge of the situation. He - no, we all want to use the School of Mind to refute Qin De and move away this mountain that suppresses us."

In order to seek help, Song Taosheng revealed these secrets to Kong Ran.

He continued, "Qin De was sentenced and has been imprisoned until now. During this period, he has fully grown in prison, contemplating day and night, continuously perfecting and innovating the Sage's Great Thief Scripture."

"To the point that he relied on it to easily defeat a great Confucian scholar like Zhao Hansheng in debate."

"Zhao Hansheng's own attainments in traditional Confucian studies were already profound, and he had also grasped parts of the essence of the School of Mind. I didn't expect that in the end, he would actually suffer defeat at Qin De's hands."

"Qin De's sheer talent truly sends chills down my spine whenever I think about it even slightly."

"And now, Duanmu Zhang and the others still want to wait for Zhao Hansheng, waiting for him to return to debate the sutras in three years!"

"Three years!"

"After three years, what kind of monster will Qin De grow into?"

"This is absolutely rearing a tiger to invite calamity!"

Kong Ran could only remain silent.

After a long while, Song Taosheng continued, "Therefore, I must eradicate him."

"As long as I can kill him, even if I die in the Cloud Prison, I will be completely willing!"

"But... I also know that relying on myself alone, I absolutely cannot achieve this. I even find it extremely difficult to sneak into the Cloud Prison. That is why I came to find you."

Kong Ran nodded.

He knew the underlying meaning behind Song Taosheng's words - the latter had outwardly come to find Kong Ran, but was actually seeking the help of the person behind him: Kong Ran's father, Kong Zhaoming.

Kong Zhaoming held a high position and great authority, being a trusted aide to the current Monarch. His own strength was formidable enough, and at the same time, he was a Confucian cultivator!

Kong Ran raised his eyes to look at Song Taosheng. The latter's thin, clear-cut face was filled with absolute resolve.

With just one glance, Kong Ran could genuinely feel Song Taosheng's determination - facing death unflinchingly and disregarding all consequences.

Kong Ran's voice was dry. "Senior, have you developed the resolve to die?"

Song Taosheng smiled faintly. "Without the resolve to die, how could I accomplish such a grand undertaking?"

Kong Ran sighed deeply with emotion. "Life is what I desire; righteousness is also what I desire. If I cannot have both, I will sacrifice life and choose righteousness."

"Senior..." Kong Ran stood up, took a step back, and gave a deep, formal bow to Song Taosheng.

This bow came entirely from the bottom of his heart.

It was respect for a true warrior, a tribute to a man willing to march to his death.

Song Taosheng raised a hand to vaguely support him. "There is no need for this. I am merely doing what must be done."

Kong Ran straightened up, his eyes slightly red.

He still couldn't hold back and tried to persuade him once more. "Senior, why must you do this? If the sky falls, the tall ones will hold it up. Mister Duanmu Zhang and Mister Zhao Hansheng, they are both far more anxious than you are."

Song Taosheng shook his head. "For this kind of matter, Duanmu Zhang and Zhao Hansheng are not suitable. Me doing it is the most appropriate. As the saying goes, the rise and fall of the world is the responsibility of every ordinary man!"

"Although I have hopes of reaching the Nascent Soul stage, I am ultimately just a mere Golden Core cultivator. If I die, although it will be a loss for the Confucian cultivator community, it will only be temporary. It could even be considered insignificant."

"But if we allow Qin De to continue growing, that will be the true disaster."

"This is a very worthwhile trade!"

Kong Ran: "But..."

Song Taosheng continued, "Sigh, little friend Kong Ran, let me tell you another truth."

"Ever since the day I audited the sutra debate, my Cultivation has been greatly disturbed."

"Whenever I try to calm my mind and focus my spirit, various thoughts constantly rise and fall. They question the Confucian classics I have read my entire life, distort my understanding of the ancient sages, and shake the beliefs I have built up over decades."

"Ordinary demonic scriptures, I can naturally resist. But the Sage's Great Thief Scripture created by Qin De is different."

"It cites Confucian classics, uses Confucian principles, and debates the very foundation of the Confucian school. If you do not understand Confucian studies, reading it will only feel baffling; but if you are proficient in Confucianism, reading it is like drinking wine - spicy at first taste, but revealing deep, complex flavors after a long savor."

"Even now, I am starting to feel that the Sage's Great Thief Scripture actually makes sense!"

Kong Ran's pupils shrank fiercely.

Song Taosheng gritted his teeth and said in a deep voice, "Previously, although this scripture was heretical, I could still resist it. But in this debate, the new version of the Sage's Great Thief Scripture that Qin De revealed is far more profound and terrifying! It has shed its mortal body and exchanged its bones; it is now an exceptionally outstanding heretical scripture."

"If this Cultivation Art, or this branch of learning, is allowed to develop further, it is very likely to even become... the Enemy of Confucianism!"

Kong Ran's heart shook violently.

The Enemy of Confucianism, the enemy of all Confucian cultivators and the entire Confucian school!

How highly Song Taosheng valued Qin De and the Sage's Great Thief Scripture!

Song Taosheng believed that if this was not taken seriously, a fully matured Qin De, bearing his Sage's Great Thief Scripture, would very likely severely damage the vitality of the entire world's Confucian cultivator community, and even shake their very foundations.

"Se-Senior..." Kong Ran's voice trembled slightly. "That debate... what exactly was discussed?"

Song Taosheng shook his head. "It is better for you not to know."

"Even I am like this, suffering severe interference in my daily Cultivation. Chu Xuangui, Situ Gu, and the others are definitely not faring much better."

"Gu Qing's situation is even worse."

"If he cannot overcome this difficult hurdle, his cultivation level will hardly advance an inch in the future, and might even regress."

"Little friend Kong Ran, how do you compare to Gu Qing?"

Kong Ran's small face turned pale, and he hurriedly shook his head. "Fellow Daoist Gu Qing is a talent of the nation, of course I cannot compare."

"I won't listen, I won't listen!"

"This junior's cultivation is still shallow, and my foundation is unsteady. I cannot listen to such demonic teachings!"

"Senior, I will write a letter to my father right now."

Song Taosheng nodded. "Many thanks for your help, little friend Kong Ran!"

Kong Ran revealed a troubled expression. "It's just that I cannot be sure of how my father will decide."

Song Taosheng revealed a faint smile. "Although this old man has never met Lord Kong even once, regarding this matter, I have absolute confidence."

Cloud Prison.

Qin De sat cross-legged by the wall of his cell.

His hair was disheveled, his eyes tightly shut, and his breathing was steady.

His hands, feet, and even his neck were bound in shackles. Runes were carved all over the shackles and chains, constantly suppressing his spiritual energy and Physical Body.

However, wisps of withered yellow qi were continuously being produced, permeating, and lingering within his body.

The Myriad Methods Demonic Degeneration Art was circulating!

Qin De's divine sense sank into his dantian, carefully sensing the changes in his body.

Within his dantian, the surface of his originally pure Golden Core was now covered in dense yellow-brown patterns. The patterns spread like a spiderweb, eroding the origin of the Golden Core bit by bit.

The spiritual energy flowing through his meridians was similarly tainted with a hint of brown.

Entering the Demonic Path!

"The Myriad Methods Demonic Degeneration Art is indeed worthy of being a supreme-grade Cultivation Art. The confinement and suppression of the Cloud Prison cannot completely stop me from circulating this demonic art."

Qin De was racing against time!

He was very clear about his current situation: the higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect were not fools. With Zhao Hansheng defeated, they would inevitably investigate the cause. Soul Search - had always been the most direct method.

"Perhaps in the next moment, a powerful Cultivator from the Myriad Manifestations Sect will appear before me."

"I need to save myself; I need to escape this cage!"

"Once I regain my freedom, I will also spread my teachings. If Wang Xinyue could found the School of Mind, why can't I found the School of the Sage's Great Thief?"

"I am not a Demonic Cultivator."

This phrase lingered in Qin De's heart.

Xiao Juxia had brought him a turning point and allowed him to win the debate, but Qin De had never truly trusted Xiao Juxia. Moreover, he merely treated the Myriad Methods Demonic Degeneration Art as a stepping stone, or rather, a tool to help him escape his cage.

"Although the Myriad Methods Demonic Degeneration Art is supreme-grade, Xiao Juxia just stood by and watched me. I can use this to step into the Demonic Path, but I must not completely fall into it!"

The Demonic Path?

Qin De had no desire to be part of it. All along, he had considered himself a cultivator of the Righteous Path and had always wanted to walk the Righteous Path.

He created the Sage's Great Thief Scripture merely because he felt that this, too, was the essence of a Confucian cultivator.

He wanted to carve out a path that belonged solely to him.

What he wanted had always been to leave his mark in history, not to become a Demonic Cultivator, be hunted down, live in constant fear for his life, and flee from place to place.

Therefore, at the core of his Confucian Dao Golden Core, Qin De always maintained a patch of purity. Unless absolutely necessary, he would not abandon it.

Xiao Juxia had already returned to the Myriad Manifestations Sect's mountain gate.

Although he was far from the Cloud Prison, Qin De's every move was under his surveillance.

Xiao Juxia was aware of Qin De's persistence and knew he was deliberately holding back on his Golden Core.

"A proper young man..." Xiao Juxia murmured softly.

Qin De was indeed a child of a respectable family. He read books since childhood and adhered strictly to the rules. He cultivated the Righteous Path and had smooth sailing all the way.

He loved reading, had a quick mind, and was good at thinking. He read extensively, memorized countless Confucian classics, and after smoothly cultivating to the Golden Core Stage, he forged a new path.

Only after writing the Sage's Great Thief Scripture did he begin to practice stealing to verify what he had learned.

Prior to that, he had not committed murder or arson, had not bullied men or oppressed women, nor had he committed countless evils.

After experiencing decades of imprisonment and enduring endless loneliness and torment, he still wanted to maintain his identity on the Righteous Path.

"But just because you don't want to walk it, does it mean you won't have to?" Xiao Juxia chuckled.

This smile was highly complex - it contained coldness, understanding, lamentation, pity, and also mockery.

"The moment you learned the Myriad Methods Demonic Degeneration Art, you were already swept up by the Destiny of the Demonic Path."

"The more you cultivate it, the deeper you go, the deeper you will be swept up!"

"The Destiny of the Demonic Path wants to incubate the complete Myriad Methods Demonic Degeneration Art. You will certainly be arranged by fate to cultivate a Myriad Methods Demonic Degeneration Art that belongs uniquely to you."

"All your struggles and unwillingness are nothing but useless naivety!"

Thinking of this, Xiao Juxia's expression shifted slightly.

He looked far into the distance toward the Cloud Prison, sensing a powerful presence rapidly flying in that direction.

His mind was stirred by this, and he immediately pinched his fingers to divine. "Oh, it's Wang Yu, the Peak Master of Danxia Peak. His trip to the dungeon this time must be to investigate Qin De."

"Hehe, see, Qin De? It's not that you can hold your ground just because you want to."

"Fate will sweep you up and constantly push you forward. Even if the direction forward is not according to your will."

Xiao Juxia immediately sent a voice transmission to Qin De, informing him about Wang Yu.

Reminded of this, Qin De instantly grew tense.

"To actually alarm the current Peak Master of Danxia Peak! This person is deeply shrewd and quite skilled in negotiation. I absolutely cannot let him see any flaws."

"I hope the Myriad Methods Demonic Degeneration Art will once again display the mighty power and wondrous uses of a supreme-grade Cultivation Art!"

The time had still been too short.

Qin De had converted to the demonic art, but he had accumulated too little demonic power. It was fundamentally insufficient to free himself, let alone fight someone.

He could only continue to pretend.

Wang Yu descended from the clouds and was immediately guided into the Cloud Prison.

His steps were unhurried as he slowly walked forward. Before long, he stood in front of Qin De's cell door.

Qin De was leaning against the corner of the wall, resting with his eyes closed. Hearing the noise, he slowly opened his eyes.

The two locked eyes.

"Qin De," Wang Yu spoke, his voice not loud, but clearly echoing in the silent cell. "I must still congratulate you on your victory in the debate."

Qin De sneered, "What an honor it is for me, Qin, to actually trouble Lord Wang Yu to personally come and investigate me?"

Wang Yu quietly stared at Qin De and said bluntly, "I am going to perform a Soul Search on you."

Qin De coldly snorted. "Then bring it on."

With a light wave of his horsetail whisk, Wang Yu pulled Qin De's body right up to the cell door.

He didn't say another word. He raised his right hand, splayed his five fingers, and suspended it three inches above Qin De's head.

Soul Search!

Qin De's body jerked violently. His eyes rolled back, and his limbs convulsed intensely. The pain of a Soul Search far exceeded any torture in the world. In an instant, Qin De's Soul was forcibly torn open, and his memories were casually flipped through by Wang Yu.
