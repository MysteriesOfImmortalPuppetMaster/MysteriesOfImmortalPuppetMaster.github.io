Chapter 894: The Red-Robed Guest is Moved (Make-up Update +5)

Ning Zhuo had already considered situations like Tie Kuang's before signing the secret contract with Zhongli Mei.

However, he hadn't expected it to happen so quickly.

Ning Zhuo's actions were not on a whim, but the result of careful deliberation. This was the growing pain of transition, a price Ning Zhuo had to bear.

He quickly calmed his emotions and began a detailed discussion with the next person.

The next person was the Red-Robed Guest.

"Senior Red-Robed Guest, this is the list of debts between us." Ning Zhuo handed over a Jade Slip.

The Red-Robed Guest scanned it with his divine sense and nodded slightly. "No errors."

Immediately after, he asked, "You little brat, when do you plan to repay this debt?"

Ning Zhuo cupped his hands with a bitter smile and explained, "Senior knows my situation completely. I had no choice but to take on these debts. Logically, the Nanming Fire Furnace belongs to me, and the other cultivators should just accept their loss. But if my clan learns of my actions in the future, they will definitely punish me severely for violating our clan's style."

"But how can I alone repay such a massive debt?"

"Furthermore, I must guarantee my own cultivation; I cannot abandon myself and prioritize all my resources for repaying debts. Therefore, for at least the next hundred years, it is definitely impossible."

The Red-Robed Guest snorted coldly and nodded.

Ning Zhuo immediately showed a look of surprise, then praised, "Senior's magnanimity is like the sea! Earlier, when I hosted Senior Chunyangzi, he doubted me and thought I would delay the repayment indefinitely. In contrast, Senior deeply trusts me. This truly leaves me amazed and full of admiration."

The Red-Robed Guest snorted again. "You Righteous Path people are exactly like this. Suffering just to save face! Since you want to mingle in the Righteous Path, you naturally have to keep your promises. Not to mention, you made this promise in public, and everyone knows it. How could you back out when you're aiming to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect?"

"Chunyangzi is narrow-minded and jealous of the capable. His cultivation of the Pure Yang Cultivation Art and his position as the leader of the Pure Yang Palace require him to strictly maintain his virgin boy pure yang body. Inevitably, his essence fire burns fiercely without an outlet. Blocking it is worse than channeling it, resulting in a twisted temperament."

"It's not surprising that such a person would doubt you."

Ning Zhuo narrowed his eyes, adopting a contemplative expression.

After a long while, he carefully weighed his words and slowly spoke, "Although Senior Chunyangzi doubted me, I still respect him. He has cultivated for over a millennium, maintaining a pure yang body and guarding the pure yang Dao without ever overstepping."

"The pure yang fire is inherently blazing. If it were vented, the fire would scatter and the Dao would weaken. He restrains it within his body, nourishing his core with fire and his Dao Path with the core - this is exactly his Dao Path."

"The Pure Yang Palace has been established for thousands of years and has weathered countless storms. For hundreds of years, Senior Chunyangzi has governed with unwavering dedication, preserving the Pure Yang Palace's orthodoxy and its sparks."

The Red-Robed Guest's lips twitched as he mocked, "You Righteous Path little brat sure have a silver tongue. The Pure Yang Palace is clearly crumbling and in grave danger under his leadership, yet you claim he 'governed with unwavering dedication.' He’s fallen so far that he has to merge his entire sect into the Myriad Manifestations Sect, yet you say he 'preserved the orthodoxy' as if he's enduring humiliation for a greater cause."

"Hahaha!" The Red-Robed Guest laughed loudly. "Don't worry, as a dignified Nascent Soul cultivator, I won't go gossiping behind anyone's back."

The Red-Robed Guest patted Ning Zhuo on the shoulder. "Relax, don't be so tense. I'm actually quite thankful to you."

"The Nanming Fire Furnace forced Chunyangzi to empty his pockets and consume the Pure Yang Palace's accumulated foundational treasures. You made him suffer such a massive loss; that's far better than anything I could have done."

"As long as you promise me not to repay his debt, I can forgive your debt to me as well!"

Ning Zhuo immediately shook his head, his attitude firm. "Senior underestimates me. I would never do such a thing!"

A flash of anger crossed the Red-Robed Guest's face, and killing intent surged in his heart. However, remembering that this was Danxia Peak and that the Demon Subjugation Hall stood behind Ning Zhuo, he spoke in a commanding tone, "Hmph. Then you will make Chunyangzi the very last person you repay!"

Ning Zhuo shook his head again, but this time his movements were several times slower. Then he smiled bitterly. "Actually, I wanted to use another method to repay Senior Chunyangzi's debt early. But unfortunately, Senior Chunyangzi rejected my proposal."

The Red-Robed Guest initially grew even angrier, but upon hearing that Chunyangzi had refused, his expression eased up a bit. Unable to hold back his curiosity, he asked, "What proposal?"

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath and, with a sincere and regretful expression, said, "I invited Senior Chunyangzi to join us and seize Flowing Cloud Peak together!"

The Red-Robed Guest's pupils contracted, revealing a look of shock.

Having originated as a rogue cultivator and joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect for many years, he naturally understood the Sect's structure of eight peaks and sixteen halls.

"You little brat, you have huge ambitions. You're merely at the Foundation Establishment Stage, yet you're scheming for a main peak?!" The Red-Robed Guest was truly stunned by Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo laughed heartily. "Whether I can do it or not doesn't matter. But if I don't even dare to think about it, wouldn't that make me a coward?"

The Red-Robed Guest fell silent.

He was exactly someone who didn't dare to think about it.

Ning Zhuo cupped his hands. "Senior Chunyangzi has the foundation of the Pure Yang Palace, so he might need to deliberate carefully and choose cautiously. But I am fighting on my own; the barefoot have nothing to fear from those wearing shoes. Senior is daring and courageous, you must definitely understand me!"

A complex emotion flashed in the depths of the Red-Robed Guest's eyes.

Back when he was a rogue cultivator mingling in the Demonic Path, he was indeed fiercely combative and dared to do anything. But after surviving to become an old veteran of the martial world, he had learned too many lessons and lost his courage. It perfectly matched the old saying: The older you get in the martial world, the smaller your courage becomes.

Even after joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he was constantly ostracized. He even had to apply just to enter the Sect's main gate, living a very stifling life.

Otherwise, he wouldn't have come for the Nanming Fire Furnace this time.

He saw the Nanming Fire Furnace as his hope of ascending to the Sect's upper echelons!

Now, his hope was gone, or rather, it had fallen into Ning Zhuo's hands.

Ning Zhuo's current background made him too afraid to make a move.

Ning Zhuo's public promise to acknowledge and repay the debt had made him change his mind - he wanted to demand his repayment, and ideally, teach Chunyangzi a lesson in the process!

Now, hearing that Ning Zhuo wanted to seize Flowing Cloud Peak and being flattered by Ning Zhuo saying he "must definitely understand me," the Red-Robed Guest was momentarily filled with profound emotion. The complexity of his feelings was truly difficult to put into words.

Although this kid mingles in the Righteous Path, he really resembles me... just like how I was when I was young. Fearing neither Heaven nor Earth, heh!

Thinking of this, the Red-Robed Guest couldn't help but vastly change his impression of Ning Zhuo, secretly developing a lot of fondness for him.

But on the surface, the Red-Robed Guest still coldly snorted, "You, a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator, actually invited a Nascent Soul cultivator, and someone who is a sect master like Chunyangzi? Of course he would refuse! What were you thinking? Do you think your prestige is that great?"

Ning Zhuo burst into laughter, then cupped his hands. "Senior, please forgive this kid's presumptuousness."

"I actually do think I have quite a bit of face."

"Senior, do you know that Lord Zhong Dao of the Demon Subjugation Hall and Peak Master Tuoba Huang of Myriad Beasts Peak both hold me in high regard? The former lent me the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone twice, and he offered it on his own initiative. The latter even offered me a heavy gift in public, but I rejected it."

The Red-Robed Guest nodded; this intelligence was already well known to everyone.

Ning Zhuo continued, "Senior, do you know that I also have accomplishments in Formation setups? I have long been in secret contact with the upper echelons of Heavy Array Peak."

The Red-Robed Guest grunted in agreement. Previously, for the sake of the Nanming Fire Furnace, he had also bought a lot of intelligence from the Commerce Hall. This exact piece of information was in Ning Zhuo's file.

Ning Zhuo held his head high and said, "With my Innate Talent and brilliance, in this current Flying Cloud Assembly, it's not just the other Peak Masters and Hall Masters choosing me; I am also choosing them. Isn't that right?"

The Red-Robed Guest coldly snorted, "Not just you, every generation's top-tier geniuses are like this."

Although his expression was cold and stern, Ning Zhuo's high-profile, high-spirited attitude actually suited his appetite more. When he was young, he was exactly like this!

Ning Zhuo said, "Although I'm only at the Foundation Establishment Stage, what's so great about the Golden Core or Nascent Soul stages? As long as I'm given time, I will definitely be able to reach them too! But I only have one chance to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect through the Flying Cloud Assembly. I must seize this opportunity and make the most of it."

"What's the point of joining other halls, or even a main peak? Many things would lack freedom; I wouldn't be able to make my own decisions, and I'd still have to act on orders!"

The Red-Robed Guest was already nodding in his heart.

After joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he had felt extremely stifled. He couldn't do this, and he couldn't touch that. There were also certain missions assigned from above; if he didn't do them, he wouldn't receive the corresponding treatment!

Ning Zhuo raised his voice, "But seizing Flowing Cloud Peak... that would be different!"

"Senior is definitely aware of the situation on Flowing Cloud Peak."

"I only need to form a faction and participate in the struggle for Flowing Cloud Peak. If my luck is good and the leader I support ultimately succeeds... in the outside world, that would be the monumental merit of assisting a rising dragon! I could also assume a high-level position. Wouldn't that be much more comfortable than taking orders under powerhouses like Zhong Dao and Tuoba Huang?"

If these words reached the ears of Zhongli Mei or Chunyangzi, they would definitely scoff at them. Even Foundation Establishment cultivators like Shen Xi or Situ Xing would shake their heads repeatedly upon hearing this.

But the Red-Robed Guest was different!

He completely understood; Ning Zhuo's line of thought perfectly echoed the voice in his own heart.

Ning Zhuo pressed on, "Senior, wouldn't you agree?"

The Red-Robed Guest snorted coldly. "You little brat, you are indeed a talent of the Righteous Path, to be so proficient at scheming!"

Ning Zhuo said, "Without scheming, how can one progress? If I hadn't obtained the Nanming Fire Furnace, none of this would be worth mentioning. But now that I have the Nanming Fire Furnace, this is an absolutely perfect opportunity!"

"I owe everyone massive debts, and I have no ability to repay them within a hundred years. Everyone won't feel at ease with me and will constantly worry about the progress of the repayment. Why don't we all act together, using the Nanming Fire Furnace in my hands - this is a Spirit Treasure-level alchemy furnace, after all. In the future, when cultivation-aiding spirit pills come rolling out, do we have to worry about lacking influence and appeal?"

"Furthermore, we are all creditors of the Nanming Fire Furnace. What a great twist of fate this is, forming the perfect alliance of interests. As long as we pool our efforts, it's not impossible to seize Flowing Cloud Peak. Once occupied... that's one of the eight great main peaks!"

The Red-Robed Guest was momentarily lost in thought.

He was moved by the prospect Ning Zhuo painted.

The eight great main peaks... His original intention had only been to use the Nanming Fire Furnace to become a high-ranking member of Danxia Peak, thereby improving his awkward situation within the Sect.

His original plan was now unrealistic.

But Ning Zhuo had just thrown out a far more practical plan!
