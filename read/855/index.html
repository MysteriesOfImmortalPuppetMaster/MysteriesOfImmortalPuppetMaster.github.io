Chapter 855: Sacrificing Life for Righteousness

"Good materials, rise for me!" A Demonic Cultivator immediately made a move, wanting to refine the corpses of the deathsworn into zombies.

"His bones are mine!" This sparked a scramble. A Demonic Cultivator skilled in the bone path had shining eyes as his demonic qi surged wildly.

"It's you, you're Qin De! You're not dead!!" Song Taosheng roared, charging over to kill him.

As it turned out, after regaining his mobility, Qin De had quietly eliminated Zhou Sumo. He needed to race against time to refine the Blood Mist Demonic Seed as quickly as possible.

Therefore, he made arrangements, placing Zhou Sumo's corpse in his own cell and activating a demonic technique to disguise it.

The Myriad Methods Demonic Fall Art was a supreme-grade Cultivation Art, and the various Demonic Path techniques recorded within it were extremely exquisite. During Song Taosheng's first assassination attempt, even though he used detection methods, he failed to see through the truth.

The "Qin De" that Song Taosheng had killed was merely Zhou Sumo's corpse. After being crushed into a bloody paste and bone fragments, it was mutilated beyond recognition, making it even more impossible to identify.

After Song Taosheng and the others "succeeded," they wanted to break out of the Cloud Prison and fight for their lives. Even the deathsworn, when circumstances permitted, preferred life over death.

However, right as they reached the eighth floor, they were trapped by a Formation.

They managed to break out of the Formation with great difficulty, only to be forced back down to the ninth floor. It was truly despairing.

But right now, Song Taosheng felt incredibly fortunate. He was glad they had returned to the ninth floor, otherwise, how could he have discovered that Qin De was still alive!

Seeing Song Taosheng lunging at him, Qin De felt both angry and helpless. In his view, he had clearly passed off his disguise successfully; he never expected the other party to stumble upon his true identity by sheer coincidence.

Left with no choice, he had to fight Song Taosheng.

Boom! Boom! Boom!

The two sides clashed fiercely, sending out rolling shockwaves like a surging tide.

Literary treasure - Righteous Heart Slip!

Song Taosheng unleashed his literary treasure. It was completely translucent white, radiating a gentle, warm light. Engraved on the Jade Slip were two ancient seal characters - Righteous Heart.

The Jade Slip specifically countered Demonic Qi and could suppress and kill all evil demons.

Although Qin De had the Blood Mist Demonic Seed protecting him, for a time, he was still beaten back repeatedly by the Righteous Heart Slip, suffering several wounds!

"Dammit, I've only refined forty percent of the Blood Mist Demonic Seed. If I had completely converted it, relying on the grade of the Myriad Methods Demonic Fall Art, I wouldn't fear this literary treasure in the slightest!"

Taking advantage of the momentum, Song Taosheng attacked furiously, trying everything possible to deal Qin De a fatal blow. "Qin De, today you will undoubtedly die!"

"That's hard to say."

"Hmph, Confucian cultivators are far too arrogant."

"That Righteous Path hypocritical face is truly sickening!"

Several Demonic Cultivators suddenly intervened, intercepting Song Taosheng and giving Qin De a chance to catch his breath.

Song Taosheng's expression darkened sharply. "This is a matter of our Confucian School. If you heretical demons don't take this chance to escape, will you even have the time later?"

"Hehe, this fellow Daoist is clearly a Demonic Path cultivator. How is he someone from your Confucian School?" The Ghost-Faced Scholar pulled a bizarre smile.

The Bronze Head Monk looked at Qin De, rubbed the ordination scars on his head, and laughed heartily. "Betraying Confucianism to join the demons. Little brother, not bad!"

He himself was a Buddhist cultivator. Unable to strictly observe the precepts, he constantly violated them and ultimately became a traitor to the Buddhist Sect. Therefore, he found Qin De, someone with similar experiences and choices, quite to his liking.

The Eagle Claw Butcher, however, stared at Qin De. His voice was cold and gloomy as he questioned, "Why is Zhou Sumo's Blood Mist Demonic Seed on you?"

Zhou Sumo was different from most of the prisoners; he was a Righteous Path cultivator, a member of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

If it was Zhou Sumo who gave the demonic seed to Qin De, then even though Qin De was using a demonic seed, he would still be considered someone of the Demonic Path.

"Of course I am a Confucian cultivator!" Qin De had always considered himself as such in his heart, but at this moment, he didn't mind putting on an act.

Qin De chuckled darkly. "Naturally, it's because Senior Zhou Sumo was too old, and the demonic seed didn't fancy him. If we want to revitalize the Demonic Path, we have to rely on young people like me, right?"

Saying so, he forcefully activated the demonic seed within his body, causing an even denser blood mist to surround him and his Demonic Qi to surge.

The Eagle Claw Butcher coldly snorted and turned his head to look at Song Taosheng.

The Bronze Head Monk and the Ghost-Faced Scholar glared at Song Taosheng like tigers eyeing their prey!

Song Taosheng pondered inwardly: "Qin De has completely fallen and become a Demonic Cultivator. By slaying him, for the nation and the people, who knows how many innocent lives I will save!"

"However, the situation before me has become thorny."

Song Taosheng was quite clear about the strengths and weaknesses of himself and the enemy. His advantage was possessing magic treasures and talismans, while the opponents had the advantage of numbers but were completely empty-handed.

However, having just gone through a fierce battle and broken out of a Formation, his condition was already far from optimal.

If a real fight broke out, the outcome was truly hard to say!

If I'm not careful, I'll be delayed and lose this final opportunity. Thoughts flashed like lightning in Song Taosheng's divine sea.

This was what he worried about the most.

This time, he had abandoned everything, embracing the resolve to die in order to assassinate Qin De. Although many twists and turns had occurred along the way, there was still a chance right now.

Qin De had personally tried earlier, so he was very certain that even if he released many Demonic Cultivators, he wouldn't be able to escape.

What Song Taosheng feared now was failing to kill Qin De before the high echelon of the Myriad Manifestations Sect intervened to stop his final assassination attempt.

The higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect definitely did not want Qin De to die. After all, the latter was the best tool for suppressing the Confucian cultivators within the Sect!

"Since that's the case!" Song Taosheng gritted his teeth and without hesitation, unleashed his final resort.

Confucian technique - Sacrifice Life for Righteousness!

Mencius: "Life is what I desire; righteousness is also what I desire. If I cannot have both, I will let life go and choose righteousness."

Once this Confucian technique was cast, it could no longer be stopped.

It was similar to the Heavenly Demon Disintegration; it was a Cultivator's final swan song in the journey of life!

Song Taosheng's aura abruptly changed.

Subsequently, his body began to glow.

At first, the light was very faint, like the first gleam of dawn. But soon, it grew brighter and more intense, gradually turning into a blazing ball of white light that completely enveloped him.

Within the white light, his figure began to blur, as if he was merging with the light itself.

"This is?!" Qin De's pupils shrank slightly.

The Eagle Claw Butcher's expression changed drastically. "Sacrificing Life for Righteousness! This old thing doesn't want to live anymore!"

The smile on the Ghost-Faced Scholar's face instantly froze.

The Bronze Head Monk's hand, which had been rubbing his ordination scars, also paused.

The white light gradually dissipated, revealing Song Taosheng's body.

His entire body was wreathed in a faint white glow, and he naturally floated into the air.

He opened his eyes, the depths of which also lingered with white light, and his voice was peaceful yet firm: "Qin De, prepare yourself. I have come to take your life."

Half-step Nascent Soul!

Song Taosheng discarded his life in exchange for an explosive surge in combat power.

Qin De's expression was solemn.

"Attack together!" he shouted in a low voice.

The Eagle Claw Butcher, the Ghost-Faced Scholar, and the Bronze Head Monk attacked simultaneously.

The Eagle Claw Butcher swung all ten fingers, and countless steel hook-like claw phantoms flew from his fingertips, forming a massive net that enveloped Song Taosheng.

The Ghost-Faced Scholar's face constantly shifted. With every change in expression, an illusion art assailed Song Taosheng. The illusions layered over one another, blurring the line between truth and falsehood, enough to plunge anyone into chaos.

The Bronze Head Monk brought his hands together in prayer. The eighty-one ordination scars on his massive head lit up simultaneously. He lowered his head and charged fiercely at Song Taosheng. This ramming attack carried tremendous momentum, powerful enough to shatter mountains.

The three Demonic Cultivators deployed their respective methods, attacking Song Taosheng at the same time.

Song Taosheng slightly raised his hand. The Righteous Heart Slip hovered above his palm, radiating streaks of pure white radiance. Wherever the radiance passed, the claw phantoms instantly melted away, the layers of illusions shattered, and even the Bronze Head Monk's charging momentum was stalled.

Song Taosheng activated another Confucian technique, and a white light arrow shot out, striking the Eagle Claw Butcher squarely in the chest.

The Eagle Claw Butcher screamed in misery, flying backward to crash into the wall, vomiting fresh blood.

Song Taosheng tossed out another literary treasure, which shot like lightning toward the Ghost-Faced Scholar.

The Ghost-Faced Scholar's bizarre smiling face abruptly shattered, revealing his true, featureless face underneath. Clutching his face, he retreated repeatedly, hiding in a corner.

Song Taosheng then sent a talisman flying, striking the Bronze Head Monk dead on.

The Bronze Head Monk didn't dodge or evade. Upon taking it head-on, a clang of clashing metal erupted. He staggered backward, his balance unsteady, and collapsed onto the ground, his face deathly pale.

In the first round of exchanges, Song Taosheng directly repelled the three Demonic Cultivators, severely intimidating the others.

The other Demonic Cultivators were originally restless and eager to strike, but seeing this scene, they successively discarded any thoughts of joining the fray.

Having cleared the obstacles, Song Taosheng sighed lightly and charged at Qin De.

Qin De gritted his teeth, mobilizing the demonic seed within his body with all his might.

Blood mist surged out madly, coalescing into a barrier in front of him.

Song Taosheng's entire body radiated white light once again, transforming into a sphere of light that violently crashed into the blood mist barrier.

The barrier trembled violently, and streaks of cracks spread across its surface.

Qin De's face flushed red as he poured all his strength into maintaining it.

Three breaths.

Five breaths.

Ten breaths...

Crack!

The barrier shattered with a boom.

The white sphere of light drove straight in, aiming directly for Qin De.

Qin De let out a miserable shriek as his entire body was sent flying backward, smashing against the wall.

The radiance dissipated, revealing that his chest had been pierced through by the white light, leaving a bloody hole the size of a bowl. Fresh blood spurted out, dyeing the wall and the floor red.

He slumped to the ground, his aura feeble.

Song Taosheng dispersed the white light and reverted to human form. He slowly walked up to him and looked down.

"Qin De, I know you are definitely unresigned. But for the sake of the big picture, please die."

Qin De lay on the ground, gazing up at this condescending old Confucian.

Blood trickled from the corner of his mouth, and his face was deathly pale, but a bizarre glint flashed in his eyes.

"Hehe." A bizarre and sinister smile surfaced on the corners of Qin De's mouth. "Why don't you look closer, Mister Song?"

As the voice entered Song Taosheng's ears, it suddenly became ethereal and illusory.

Song Taosheng immediately sensed something was wrong. At the edges of his vision, it was as if a curtain had been lifted, revealing the truth.

He was still struggling against the blood mist barrier; he had not actually pierced through this obstacle.

Everything that just happened was merely an illusion.

Not only that, Song Taosheng's entire body had been infiltrated by a massive amount of demonic energy. He had clearly used the Sacrifice Life for Righteousness Confucian technique, and his power should have held the absolute advantage, but the result was that Qin De was entirely a notch superior.

"What is going on?!" Just as Song Taosheng was reeling in shock and doubt, Qin De suddenly struck.

Pfft!

A demonic hand condensed from blood mist directly pierced through Song Taosheng's defenses and tore into his chest. It actually reached all the way into his middle dantian and ruthlessly gripped the Golden Core within!

The next moment, the blood mist demonic hand retracted, forcibly ripping Song Taosheng's Golden Core out with its bare hand!

Song Taosheng was dumbfounded.

Having lost his Golden Core, he no longer had a source of spiritual energy, and he instantly crumbled.

The majestic blood mist demonic energy swallowed him whole.

In the next moment, his corpse, now sucked dry into a withered husk, fell directly onto the ground, while the heavily swollen blood mist returned into Qin De's body.

The Eagle Claw Butcher, the Ghost-Faced Scholar, and the Bronze Head Monk stared blankly at this scene.

In their eyes was shock, wariness, and also - greed.

Song Taosheng had just been caught in a demonic magic illusion and failed to see clearly, but the other three Cultivators had witnessed the entire process.

It turned out that at the start of the clash, Qin De had indeed fallen at a clear disadvantage. But soon, he executed his demonic art, converting the boundless Confucian energy Song Taosheng had crushed down upon him into his own demonic energy. By fighting so fiercely, Song Taosheng was effectively aiding the enemy, delivering a massive gift package of spiritual energy to Qin De.

With this ebb and flow, Qin De rapidly reversed the situation, gaining the upper hand for himself.

By the time Song Taosheng woke up, this old Confucian had already missed the most critical window. The momentum was long gone, and he was ultimately slain by Qin De!

The Eagle Claw Butcher licked his lips. A cold glint flashed in his eyes as he stared at Qin De. "You brat... what kind of Cultivation Art is this?"

The skin on the Ghost-Faced Scholar's face writhed slightly. He had just captured a ghost and fashioned it into a mask, wearing it on his face. At this moment, he said, "To actually be able to convert another's attack into one's own demonic energy so rapidly... Such efficiency is unheard of."

The Bronze Head Monk rubbed the ordination scars on his head, his gaze looking like it wanted to devour someone. "And there's that demonic illusion art, capable of actually fooling a Half-step Nascent Soul!"

At present, the state of these Demonic Cultivators was completely identical - penniless and stripped bare.

The three Demonic Cultivators could see clearly: the reason Qin De was able to overcome Song Taosheng was entirely due to his Cultivation Art being exceedingly profound and superior, allowing him to completely crush Song Taosheng.

This was the supreme-grade Myriad Methods Demonic Fall Art!

The three Demonic Cultivators exchanged glances, each seeing the covetousness and greed in the others' eyes.

Although they all had their primary Cultivation Arts and it was difficult to change them, even just using such a profound demonic art as a reference might allow them to comprehend their own Spells.

However, none of the three Cultivators made a move.

Right now, they were all grasshoppers tied to the same string. The most urgent matter was still breaking out of the Cloud Prison!

Qin De clearly sensed the thoughts of the three Cultivators, but he merely pretended not to know. Playing the part of a Demonic Cultivator, he merged into the group and charged toward the arriving guard Cultivators.

As for the deathsworn who had returned together with Song Taosheng, they had already all lost their lives.

Cloud Canopy Peak.

The atmosphere surrounding the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy had clearly eased down.

Dong Chen sat cross-legged. His face was still pale, but his breathing was much more stable than before.

Wei Ji's coarse hemp Daoist robe was stained with blood, but those pure black eyes had regained their luster.

Tuoba Huang sat sprawled on the ground, fiery energy roiling around him. The wounds on his body had already been treated, wrapped in numerous bandages.

Wang Yu leaned against a piece of crushed rubble, his fly whisk having been replaced with a new one.

The rest of the people had also improved somewhat.

Flowing light flashed in the depths of Dong Chen's eyes. "I have already investigated it. The focal point of this destiny clash lies deep within our Sect's Cloud Prison!"

"The Cloud Prison?" Tuoba Huang was surprised, then he pulled a grin. "Old man Zhong Dao's territory actually had such a huge screw-up?"

Wang Yu: "The situation hasn't been determined yet. We should immediately send people to investigate!"

Dong Chen nodded slightly. "Naturally."

At the moment he revealed this information, he had already issued an order via divine sense transmission.

An elite squad had already teleported over through the emergency teleportation formation.

The bottom of the Cloud Prison.

The Demonic Cultivators had organized a few attacks. Twice, they managed to break out of the ninth floor and fight their way to the eighth floor, only to be driven back down by the guard Cultivators.
