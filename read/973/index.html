Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 973: Dao and Principle

This person was named Xiang Sishui. He primarily cultivated the middle dantian in his Qi Sea using the Cultivation Art Hundred Calamities Convergence Sutra. His spiritual energy was the most pure and boundless. He excelled in prolonged combat, spiritual energy recovery, and spell conversion. Hanging from his waist was a Dead Spring Jade Urn. The spring water inside was taken from a secret ground of the Xiang Sect's Deathward Gate. It could bear his companions' injuries, and it could also channel vitality and spiritual energy back into others.

The person on the right had shoulders and a back as broad as iron walls. Around the side of his neck was a faint ring of bone patterns, looking as if a shackle forged of white bone was deeply carved into his flesh.

This person was named Xiang Zican. He primarily cultivated the lower dantian in his Essence Sea using the Cultivation Art Corpse King's Mountain-Moving Bone Sutra. His physical body was as tyrannical as a ferocious beast, and his vitality circulated like an erupting furnace. He constantly wore the Three Disasters Body-Locking Armor and wielded the Fate-Weeping Long Lance. When charging enemy lines, he delighted most in trading life for life and injury for injury.

The Cultivation Arts practiced by these three were extremely radical, specializing solely in one specific dantian while completely abandoning the other two.

If such a cultivation method were used by an ordinary cultivator, it would have long become a fatal flaw: one specializing in the Divine Sea would inevitably have extremely weak spiritual energy and a frail physical body; one specializing in the Qi Sea would have massive vulnerabilities in their divine soul and physical body; and one specializing in the Essence Sea would have their divine sense and spiritual energy easily exploited by others.

The Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect did this intentionally because they had successfully cultivated a heaven-defying Divine Art -

Shared Life and Death!

The Jade Slip explained it in detail: This Divine Art allowed the three to evenly share and circulate their states, whether positive or negative. For instance, injuries, spiritual energy, soul power, lifespan, and so on could all cycle back and forth within their three bodies.

If one person was heavily injured, the injury would balance out among their flesh and blood like flowing water.

If one person's spiritual energy was exhausted, the other two's energy would reverse course like a roaring river, instantly filling them back up.

If one person's divine soul was damaged, the soul power of the other two would immediately fill that void.

In the most extreme case, even if one of them died on the spot, as long as the other two were willing to pay a sufficient price in lifespan and vitality, they could forcefully drag their companion back to the mortal realm and directly resurrect them!

Therefore, the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect never spared their lives in combat and were exceedingly valiant and ferocious.

"Shared Life and Death..." Ning Zhuo's expression was grave, the light in his eyes sinking bit by bit.

There were also several visual recordings within the Jade Slip, detailing the combat scenes of the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect.

Night rain in the barren mountains.

A Nascent Soul sword cultivator, hair disheveled, stepped on sword light and retreated wretchedly through the curtain of rain.

Half of his Daoist robe was soaked in blood, yet the flying sword in his hand remained as clear as autumn waters. Every time the sword light swept horizontally, it shaved off a section of the mountain ridge, slicing the sky-filling rain into countless watery lines with its unparalleled, fierce sword qi.

Xiang Zican stood at the very front. His Fate-Weeping Long Lance had already been sheared in half by the sword light. A deep, bone-exposing wound split his chest open, flesh rolling outward, and one could even see his heart - grazed by sword qi - still struggling to beat.

A ruthless glint burst from the Nascent Soul sword cultivator's eyes. His flying sword abruptly split into seven solid sword shadows, like seven life-claiming talismans, aiming straight for Xiang Zican's skull, throat, heart, and dantian.

"Die!"

The sword whistled shrilly, shattering the sky-filling curtain of rain into white mist.

Yet at this moment, Xiang Zican grinned. His bloodstained teeth appeared as pale as bone in the night rain.

Instead of retreating, he advanced, brazenly crashing head-on into the seven sword shadows that were powerful enough to split gold and shatter stone!

The sword shadows entered his body. Blood exploded like fireworks, dyeing half the cliff face red.

Xiang Zican's body convulsed violently; he almost collapsed.

The ruthless expression on the Nascent Soul sword cultivator's face had just begun to ease when it froze at the corners of his eyes.

Xiang Sishui uncapped the Dead Spring Jade Urn. Gray-white spring water surged out in reverse, wrapping around those terrifying wounds like a living creature.

The lantern-shaped mole between Xiang Hundeng's brows suddenly lit up. The flame within his Life-Illuminating White Lantern flickered, and Xiang Zican's gaze - which had lost focus due to intense pain - refocused.

Tiny wounds simultaneously tore open on the bodies of Xiang Hundeng and Xiang Sishui - they had shared the majority of the sword injuries Xiang Zican suffered.

Taking advantage of the split-second the Nascent Soul sword cultivator's mind trembled, Xiang Zican viciously hurled his half-broken long lance!

The Nascent Soul sword cultivator's protective sword aura was smashed into a depression, and the sword light beneath his feet faltered by an inch.

The Life-Illuminating White Lantern's light immediately fell upon his face!

The visual recording abruptly ended here.

A crimson fire purgatory.

A Nascent Soul fire cultivator stood proudly in midair. Behind him, a Dharma Idol resembling the massive maw of a volcano spewed blackish-red magma, melting the entire valley into flowing glass.

The air warped and distorted. Even the cultivators observing the battle from afar had their protective spiritual light crackling from the extreme heat.

Xiang Hundeng was swallowed by a wave of fire.

The Life-Illuminating White Lantern flickered in the crimson fire purgatory. Its lampshade melted, yet the wick stubbornly burst forth with a blinding ray of white light.

The Nascent Soul fire cultivator clapped his hands together. The volcanic Dharma Idol crashed down, and the blackish-red magma transformed into a hideous giant python lunging at Xiang Hundeng.

Sparks sprayed simultaneously from Xiang Hundeng's seven orifices. The white light in his eyes quickly dimmed, like a guttering candle in the wind.

Xiang Sishui's face turned ghastly pale, and the spring water in his Dead Spring Jade Urn boiled and churned under the high temperature.

Bulging veins throbbed on Xiang Zican's forehead. Charred black cracks surfaced on his Three Disasters Body-Locking Armor as the armor let out a groan, unable to bear the heavy burden.

The three suffered the attack simultaneously. Like three zither strings being roasted by a raging fire, it was unknown which one would snap first.

The Nascent Soul fire cultivator continued to drive his Dharma Idol. The magma giant python's offensive grew fiercer and fiercer, almost dragging Xiang Hundeng completely into an eternal purgatory of fire.

However, right at this moment, Xiang Hundeng suddenly lifted his head. His eyes were empty, yet his lips curled into a bizarre smile.

The nearly melted Life-Illuminating White Lantern flew from his chest. The lantern's flame abruptly turned a deathly gray-white.

Deep within the volcanic Dharma Idol behind the Nascent Soul fire cultivator, a flickering lantern shadow unexpectedly emerged. It illuminated an old wound hidden for hundreds of years deep within his divine soul - a Heart Demon fracture left behind from when he attempted to break through to a higher realm.

Roaring across the sky-filling sea of fire, Xiang Zican hurled his half-lance. The body of the lance burned glowing red, yet it carried an indomitable, tragic ferocity as it accurately nailed the lantern shadow located at the old wound of the volcanic Dharma Idol.

The volcanic Dharma Idol shook violently. The spewing magma abruptly rolled back, pouring over the Nascent Soul fire cultivator himself.

In a mix of shock and rage, he retreated, wanting to extinguish that lantern shadow. However, he discovered it was using his own soul fire to burn brighter and brighter, clinging to him like maggots to a bone.

The visual recording froze at this moment.

A boundless expanse of snowfields.

Wind and snow blanketed the earth, dyeing the world in a desperate shade of pale.

A female Nascent Soul cultivator fled while treading on a snow lotus. Her left arm was already gone, the severed stump crusted in thick blood-ice.

Behind her, three figures sprinted across the snowfield as if in flight, their speed unexpectedly not the slightest bit slower than her escape light.

Xiang Sishui chased in the middle. His face was pale as paper, and shockingly, an ice-crystal long spike was embedded in his chest, piercing straight through the location of his Golden Core. Cold qi diffused from the wound, freezing half his meridians into ice sculptures.

Had it been any other Golden Core cultivator, their spiritual energy would have been severed long ago and they would have collapsed to await death. Yet, that cold qi continually circulated among the three bodies, evenly dispersed by the "Shared Life and Death" Divine Art, making it bearable.

The Nascent Soul female cultivator constantly looked back, her face fraught with tension and terror.

She raised her hand and struck out with a snow lotus Dharma seal. Thousands of ice petals transformed into the sharpest hidden weapons, slicing toward the three.

Xiang Zican let out a beast-like roar. Using his broad chest and scar-riddled body, he forcefully crashed into the storm of ice petals. The ice blades sliced into his flesh, but the moment blood seeped out, it was frozen into a new layer of bloody ice armor.

He directly tore open the rain curtain of ice petals.

Taking this opportunity, Xiang Hundeng's white lantern flickered. Its light pierced the sky-filling snowstorm and shone upon the snow lotus under the Nascent Soul female cultivator's feet. The snow lotus's escape light visibly stagnated for half a breath.

Xiang Sishui violently ripped the ice-crystal long spike from his chest, bringing along a large chunk of frozen, mangled flesh. Coughing up a mouthful of purplish-black coagulated blood mixed with ice shards, he pressed his hands heavily onto the Dead Spring Jade Urn.

The gray-white spring water turned into a roaring river, surging from the cracks in the snowfield and entangling the roots of the snow lotus.

The Nascent Soul female cultivator turned deathly pale and screamed, "You're crazy! If you keep fighting like this, all three of your Golden Cores will shatter!"

Xiang Zican laughed boisterously amidst the wind and snow: "The Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect sell our lives! Since when have we feared shattering our cores!"

Xiang Hundeng's voice was very soft, yet bore an indifference that saw through life and death. It clearly pierced the howling wind: "If the employer can pay the price, we can afford the death."

The Nascent Soul female cultivator plunged into utter terror.

She no longer harbored any delusions of luck. A Nascent Soul phantom floated from the top of her head, attempting to abandon her physical body and flee. But the light of the Life-Illuminating White Lantern immediately landed upon her Nascent Soul phantom.

From deep within the snowfield, a miserable shriek, so shrill it sounded inhuman, echoed out.

...

These precious and rare recordings thoroughly verified the sheer power of the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect!

Singled out, every one of them possessed combat strength at the peak of the Golden Core stage, bearing an extreme superiority in a specific aspect.

The Shared Life and Death Divine Art closely bound the three into one entity, achieving a terrifying qualitative leap!

The three covered each other's weaknesses, making them even more formidable. Especially their fearless combat style, which was extremely fierce, making even Nascent Soul-level experts find them a headache.

After reviewing the intelligence on the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect, Ning Zhuo sat quietly for a long time.

He slowly raised his right hand, his fingertips resting on the Mechanism Ring.

He deduced in his mind, "If I were to fight one against three, facing the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect head-on..."

The Mechanism Ring abruptly constricted!

The force was so fierce it nearly crushed that segment of bone alive.

Ning Zhuo's expression remained unchanged. He shifted his thought process: "What if I managed to split the battlefield and fight one against two..."

The Mechanism Ring constricted again. The force lessened slightly, but the intense pain felt like icy iron teeth biting into his finger bone.

Ning Zhuo continued to test it: "What if I set a trap, such as a Dao Field, and only strike at one of the Three Valiants..."

The Mechanism Ring contracted once more! This time, the pain was minimal, yet it still felt like a red-hot iron needle piercing into his flesh.

Ning Zhuo slowly exhaled a breath of turbid qi.

He was not at all surprised by this result.

Xiang Hundeng's divine soul was like an abyss, Xiang Sishui's spiritual energy was like a river, and Xiang Zican's physical body was like a beast. Soul, Qi, and Essence - the three mutually supported each other like a perfect equilateral triangle.

The three shared their injuries, and positive buffs applied to one could also be distributed to the other two.

Attacking any one of them was equivalent to facing all three simultaneously. The idea of dividing the battlefield was rendered invalid under the Shared Life and Death Divine Art!

What made them even more dread-inducing was their incredibly rich combat experience. It was hard to expect them to make a mistake in an intense battle filled with fatal crises.

In this regard, during the three battles, Liu Jinke was visibly mentally unbalanced, eager to prove himself, which allowed Ning Zhuo to gradually seize the initiative.

Ning Zhuo estimated that if he fought one against three, he would basically be suppressed the entire time!

Ning Zhuo examined himself.

His foundation in Five Elements Spells was profound. The Five Viscera Divine Art could forcibly elevate his spells to the Golden Core-level, but his cultivation ultimately remained at the mid-Foundation Establishment Stage. His total reserve of spiritual energy was like rowing a boat in shallow water; it couldn't withstand prolonged attrition.

His Mechanical Arts were formidable, with numerous mechanical constructs. Snow-Colored Maiden Hui, Snow Pivot Guard Xie, and the Void-Embracing Broken-Blade Guards were all regularly available Golden Core-level combat units. However, Yuan Dasheng and Meng Yehu were still recuperating. The mechanical bird swarm suffered tremendous losses in the battle with Liu Jinke, and the Mystic Weapon Armor, Spirit Extraction Box, and the Void-Embracing Broken-Blade Guards all required time for repairs.

Automaton cultivators had always been able to stack combat power using constructs, but their greatest fear was high-intensity consumption leading to unsustainable logistics.

The Defense-Keeping Rotational Slash Formation could protect the body as well as cycle between offense and defense. However, it relied on the Mystic Weapon Armor as the formation core, borrowing the combination of the principles of weapons, the variations of formations, and the momentum of close combat. Yet, it was ultimately a new method that had just matured. Faced with the siege of the Three Valiants, it would be absolutely far from enough!

The true path of the Military Strategist lay in leading troops into battle, utilizing military might to replace one's own spiritual energy, soul power, and so on, to leverage a higher tier of combat strength. His Qing-Jiao Army was far away in White Paper Immortal City, and it was absolutely impossible for the Myriad Manifestations Sect's martial arena to allow him to lead a large army to besiege single opponents.

As for the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Ability, the Buddha Heart Demon Seal, and the Luo Shu pages, these were his absolute deepest trump cards. Whether they could be used, how great the cost would be, and what repercussions would follow their use, all had to be precisely weighed on a scale.

The Blood Ember Fire Seed had also been exposed, losing its effectiveness for a surprise attack.

"Although my battle record is impressive, it actually has major limitations. In the Thousand Peaks Forest battle, I leveraged Military Might. In the battle at the Forgetful River Prefecture, I benefited from the arrangements of my predecessors, only delivering the final strike."

It was like a giant stone boulder pushed to the edge of a cliff, only a hair's breadth away from plummeting. At that moment, a mere poke from a toddler's finger could trigger a terrifying, tumbling crash.

"Besides that, there is also the suppression from the Buddha Heart Demon Seal, allowing me to preserve my destiny."

Ning Zhuo was humble and cautious, always maintaining a clear understanding of himself.

Although he was only at the mid-Foundation Establishment Stage, his repeated victories over higher-level cultivators were all conditional. It was because he could leverage stronger combat power.

This was the golden age of Cultivation!

People's profound comprehension and ingenious application of principles and Dao reasoning had reached a certain peak.

Cultivation level was just one factor of combat strength. In ancient times, it was the primary determinant, but with the prosperity of the Cultivation World, it had long been relegated to the background.

The strength of the military path lay in substituting spiritual energy with military might, bypassing weaknesses in cultivation level. Excellent mechanical constructs were self-sufficient; they didn't even require their masters to have any cultivation!

The Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect frequently slaughtered Nascent Soul cultivators above their realm because they too leveraged greater combat power through their Divine Art.

What was a Divine Art?

A Divine Art was the ultimate truth of Heaven and Earth!

It was entirely possible for Ning Zhuo to defeat the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect. For example, he could deploy seventeen or eighteen Nascent Soul-level spiritual mechanical combat puppets. Or, he could command several powerful Soul Formation-level demon pets and spirit beasts.

However, doing so involved Mechanical Arts and Beast Taming Arts.

These cultivation skills were simply the practical application of adhering to principles and reasoning.

If Ning Zhuo were the only son of some major figure with an unfathomable background, where even the family servants were Soul Formation-level, that would work too.

But that was a matter of fate, or luck.

That, too, was a manifestation of the principles of Heaven and Earth.

The divination arts among the hundred cultivation skills researched precisely this aspect.

Ning Zhuo had cultivated to this point and reached where he was today because he was favored by destiny, his realm of comprehension was profound, and his nature was humble and meticulous. It was because he commanded the Dao and its principles.

His weak cultivation level didn't matter. He could frequently leverage higher combat strength and put up a fight!

But now, the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect he faced possessed a Divine Art and an equally profound advantage in Dao principles. Furthermore, each one of them had a peak Golden Core cultivation level. In this aspect, they completely eclipsed Ning Zhuo's shortcoming in cultivation base.

"If Yuan Dasheng and Meng Yehu could fight, my pressure would decrease significantly. Even so, they have almost no experience fighting cooperatively, let alone tacit understanding."

Although Ning Zhuo won the great battle at the Forgetful River Prefecture, he was still suffering from the battle damages to this day.

"To fight one against three and win is simply too, too difficult."

"Fortunately, people have already taken the initiative to sponsor me. And the ones sponsoring me will absolutely not be limited to just this one faction."

"This is the manifestation of tribulation and luck."

"If I can pass this hurdle, I wonder to what extent I can digest this tribulation and luck?"
