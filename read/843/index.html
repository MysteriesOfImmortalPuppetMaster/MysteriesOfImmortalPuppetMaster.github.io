Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 843: Dao Struggle!

Qin De first felt a surge of intense surprise, followed by a tidal wave of horror.

"Is it really a supreme-grade cultivation art?!!"

Without a doubt, this was an immense fortuitous encounter.

"But how can this person hear my inner thoughts? What method is he using exactly?!"

Qin De was completely unable to discern how Xiao Juxia had achieved this.

In his heart, Xiao Juxia became even more unfathomable, his intentions hard to read.

However, in the next moment, Qin De spoke decisively: "The Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art is a supreme-grade art. Why would you teach it to me for no reason? What exactly do you want to do?"

He asked directly.

Xiao Juxia appreciated his candor and directness. He laughed and chose to be frank as well: "That question itself is the answer."

"The Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art is not perfect; it still needs completion."

Qin De’s eyes widened: "A demonic art like this isn't perfect yet?"

He couldn't see any imperfections at all. He felt the demonic art was boundless and exquisitely profound. Just a brief contact with it gave him the feeling that he wouldn't be able to fully comprehend it even if he spent his entire life studying it.

Xiao Juxia said, "Of course it isn't perfect. Accurately speaking, it has a long way to go before perfection."

"This is merely the initial version."

"This art allows all methods in the world to be transformed into demonic techniques, converting any type of spiritual energy into demonic power. In this process, what is truly consumed is the cultivator's Demonic Nature!"

"In nature and heaven and earth, there are never two identical leaves. Therefore, every person's Demonic Nature is different."

"Different characters' Demonic Natures can transform their original cultivation bases and techniques into different variations of the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art."

"And I intend to collect these transformed versions of the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art, find the commonalities among them, and ultimately complete this supreme-grade cultivation art!"

"So that's how it is." A sharp light suddenly flourished in Qin De's eyes.

He immediately laughed: "In that case, Senior might as well use your divine abilities to rescue me."

"Once I escape this cage, I will certainly cultivate the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art diligently and go all out to help Senior achieve your goal!"

Qin De possessed amazing talent. After winning the scriptural debate against Zhao Hansheng, he was very clear about his current situation.

He had revealed profound attainments in demonic studies. From now on, he would be even more effective as a tool to suppress the Confucian cultivator faction of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, preventing their influence from growing.

Because of this, Qin De's value to the Myriad Manifestations Sect had increased. The latter would inevitably guard him more strictly; he might have to spend the rest of his life in a cell.

For Qin De, this was naturally terrible news.

He did not want to be imprisoned until death!

Facing Qin De's request, however, Xiao Juxia chuckled and refused: "I will not take action."

Qin De narrowed his eyes slightly: "Why?"

Xiao Juxia: "Choosing you and teaching you a supreme-grade art is already the limit. Why should I help you more?"

"The person I selected, having received the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art, actually still needs my help to escape?"

"Then where is the value of my judgment?"

Xiao Juxia revealed an astonishing arrogance.

He continued: "Right now, the Myriad Manifestations Sect is holding the Flying Cloud Assembly. Look, there are so many Rising Cloud Minor Trials outside; you might as well view this escape attempt as a trial I am giving you."

"Only by passing this trial can you gain further recognition from me."

Qin De fell silent for a moment: "Then what is the reward for passing this trial?"

Xiao Juxia’s gaze was distant, seeming to pierce through everything to the heavens and earth: "Freedom... isn't that the greatest reward?"

Qin De was struck dumb, then grinned, emitting a silent laugh: "Senior's words are brilliant. Indeed, it is so!"

"However..."

"This junior still has one matter I haven't figured out."

"If I fail to escape and continue to be imprisoned by the Myriad Manifestations Sect, aren't you afraid your supreme-grade Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art will be leaked?"

"You must know, after debating down Zhao Hansheng and suddenly displaying such shocking demonic attainments, I will definitely arouse the suspicion of the Myriad Manifestations Sect's higher-ups."

"After the fact, they will certainly send people to search my soul and gather evidence and clues."

"At that time, can Senior and Senior's Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art deceive everyone?"

Qin De was not easily fooled. His thoughts were agile, and he immediately switched angles to threaten Xiao Juxia again.

Unexpectedly, Xiao Juxia didn't care at all: "Even if it leaks, what does it matter?"

"You are still too young and have experienced too little. You do not understand the profound mysteries of Qi Luck."

Qin De hurriedly asked for guidance: "How is this involved with Qi Luck?"

He had searched for knowledge on this esoteric subject many times but had never learned a single thing. Whenever he encountered a sliver of opportunity, he would pursue it with all his might.

Xiao Juxia said: "Do you think I alone could create a complete supreme-grade art like the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art?"

"This is not my affair, but the affair of the Demonic Dao."

"It is the Demonic Dao that wants to give birth to a supreme-grade art to deal with the chaos of the world in the future. I am merely a pawn of Heaven, the one laying the foundation."

"Do you know? This is a Dao Struggle!"

Qin De’s heart skipped a beat: "Dao Struggle?"

Xiao Juxia said: "In the prosperous age of cultivation, there are Ten Thousand Methods, One Thousand Schools, One Hundred Arts, and Ten Daos. The Ten Daos correspond to the Three Sovereigns and Five Emperors, the only eight dynasties in cultivation history. During the Blood Slaughter Dynasty, the Demonic Dao and Monster Dao were at their strongest. During the Martial Emperor's era, the Martial Dao swept through heaven and earth. During the Ban Dynasty, the Divine Dao and Military Dao dominated the world..."

"Like this, these Ten Daos were introduced one after another. As one finishes singing, another takes the stage. Now, entrenched between heaven and earth, they are the mainstream principles, competing for victory against each other, waxing and waning, coming and going endlessly."

"For the next great era, the Demonic Dao is nurturing a supreme-grade cultivation art. In this movement, you and I simply happened to be there; we are merely pawns participating in it."

Qin De couldn't help but shake physically and mentally.

In the end, he was only a Golden Core cultivator.

Moreover, shortly after creating the Sage's Great Theft Scripture, he had been imprisoned in the depths of the Cloud Prison, cut off from the world.

His cultivation base and experiences were limited.

Yet now, Xiao Juxia had lifted a corner of the curtain revealing the truth of the world.

At this moment, Qin De was incredibly certain that Xiao Juxia was the highest-level cultivator he had ever met in his life.

As for how high that level was, Qin De could not judge!

Xiao Juxia continued: "In the art of Qi Luck, there is a saying: fortune and misfortune depend on each other."

"Qin De, you created the Sage's Great Theft Scripture. While achieving something astonishing, you also nearly exhausted your good luck, thereby attracting a Human Tribulation."

"Because of this, you were imprisoned in the Cloud Prison. For so many years, you lost your freedom, but at the same time, you gained stability. Your tribulation Qi has been constantly dissipating, while your luck has been constantly accumulating. It is precisely the state of extreme misfortune paving the way for prosperity!"

"The luck you accumulated erupted all at once, landing right on me, forming 'Noble Luck'."

"Therefore, I am teaching you the supreme-grade Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art!"

"I intend to borrow your Qi Luck to nourish a 'Qin De version' of the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art."

A touch of arrogance flashed across Xiao Juxia's face: "I'll speak plainly, Qin De. You were originally a Confucian cultivator. Confucian studies cannot yet be called a 'Dao'. Although the Huazhang Kingdom is strong, it cannot push Confucian studies, the Confucian School, or the Confucian Sect to the level of the Ten Daos."

"But you still possess a portion of Confucian Qi Luck."

"By converting you into the Demonic Dao, it is equivalent to digging out a portion from the Confucian Qi Luck. This isn't using my Qi Luck, nor the Demonic Dao's own Qi Luck, but using Confucian Qi Luck to nourish the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art!"

"This is your current situation."

A sharp glint flashed in Qin De's eyes.

Xiao Juxia spoke the truth, appearing extremely candid.

Qin De: "I only knew that families have family luck, clans have clan luck, and nations have national luck. Every super sect has a spirit treasure to suppress their luck. Every cultivation nation has even more than that."

"I didn't expect the Ten Daos to have Dao Luck as well!"

"A supreme-grade cultivation art arouses the jealousy of heaven and earth; it is no small matter. Therefore, it requires the consumption of unparalleled Qi Luck."

"This seems like an investment with extremely high risk and unknown returns."

"But the Demonic Dao acts without consuming too much of its own Qi Luck, instead digging into the Qi Luck of the other nine Daos or the Thousand Schools to perform the gestation."

"This really is the style of the Demonic Dao!"

Qin De sighed from the bottom of his heart. At the same time, he understood why Xiao Juxia did not fear his threats at all.

Because this was a matter of Dao Luck.

The Demonic Dao's Qi Luck would not allow him, Qin De, to ruin this situation.

This was one of the Ten Daos.

How many Demonic cultivators were there under the heavens?

Countless.

The enormity of the Qi Luck of the Ten Daos was unimaginable.

Therefore, Qin De was certain he could not take any action that would disrupt the strategic goals of the Demonic Dao. He was just a prisoner, just a Golden Core cultivator; everything he did would be severely influenced by the Demonic Dao's Qi Luck.

Thus, it was better to borrow this east wind to achieve his own goals, rather than disobey the Demonic Dao's Qi Luck, causing accidental mishaps and inviting destructive calamity.

Xiao Juxia had actually experimented many times and seen this many times. He had nothing to fear and laughed loudly: "Qin De, I did not misjudge you. You are a smart man!"

Qin De's expression became solemn and grave.

He understood the implication behind Xiao Juxia's words.

Xiao Juxia was reminding Qin De that he didn't have much time left.

"Indeed, not much left."

"My defeating Zhao Hansheng in the debate and severely damaging the prestige of the Mind School will definitely arouse all sorts of suspicions among the Myriad Manifestations Sect's higher-ups."

"They will definitely come to investigate me."

"Once they discover the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art, they will certainly destroy me and take the art into their own pockets!"

Xiao Juxia had no doubt the Myriad Manifestations Sect's leadership would do this.

Because this was a supreme-grade cultivation art, a rare treasure of the world! The benefits it represented were far greater - far, far greater than a tool used to suppress the development of the Confucian cultivator faction!

After learning the truth about the Demonic Dao's Qi Luck, Xiao Juxia had already clarified his course of action - not to oppose the Demonic Dao's Qi Luck, but to go with the flow to achieve his own ends.

This in itself was beneficial to him!

"So, I must race against time to use the Myriad Methods Demonic Descent Art to convert my entire cultivation base."

"I am trapped in prison, subjected to all kinds of seals and bans, but these are all targeted at my original Confucian strength."

"After converting to a demonic art, most of these seals will become ineffective. This is my once-in-a-lifetime chance to escape!"

"I must seize it!"

"It is very likely there will only be this one chance."

Deeply aware of the severity of the situation, Qin De immediately closed his mouth and eyes, executing the demonic art with all his might.

Inside the cave dwelling.

Zhao Hansheng stood before Duanmu Zhang and bowed deeply: "Brother Duanmu, I have failed you. I am leaving now, but I will return in three years. I will definitely give you an explanation, Brother. Of course, I must also give an explanation to myself!"

Duanmu Zhang slowly raised his hand and returned the bow: "Fellow Daoist Zhao, go ahead."

Zhao Hansheng turned around and walked away step by step.

As for Gu Qing, he had already fallen into a coma and was temporarily taken into Zhao Hansheng's storage; the situation was getting worse.

If delayed any longer, Gu Qing might turn from Confucian to Demonic.

Zhao Hansheng had tried many times, even asking Chu Xuangui and other Confucian cultivators for help, but none could persuade Gu Qing.

This situation was extremely dangerous. Zhao Hansheng had to rush back to the Huazhang Kingdom as soon as possible to seek the help of other Great Confucians, or even ask Wang Xinyue, the founder of the Mind School, to take action personally!

Zhao Hansheng's departure made Duanmu Zhang look visibly older and weaker.

Just as great as Duanmu Zhang's hope and anticipation had been before, his disappointment and depression were now equally massive.

Some Confucian cultivators tried to comfort him, saying that since Qin De had officially turned from Confucian to Demonic, he was no longer a Confucian cultivator but a Demonic cultivator. The reason for him suppressing the development of the Confucian faction no longer existed.

But Duanmu Zhang knew very well that as long as Qin De possessed Confucian spiritual energy for a single day, he would remain a suppression device over the heads of the Confucian cultivators, firmly suppressing their development.

The game between Duanmu Zhang's group and the Myriad Manifestations Sect's higher-ups revolved around Qin De as the key point of attack and defense.

The key to this matter had always been the sect's higher-ups.

"The Myriad Manifestations Sect high command will never let go easily. If you ask me, we should act directly and eliminate Qin De!" another Confucian cultivator proposed, his expression agitated.

"Originally, we didn't kill Qin De because we cherished his talent and knew his foundation was in Confucian studies. possessing a Literary Palace, Literary Heart, and Literary Qi - he was one of us."

"But now, although he still has this Confucian cultivation, he has turned into a Demonic cultivator in his bones. What is there to hesitate about?"

Chu Xuangui immediately shook his head, his attitude resolute: "This absolutely cannot be done!"

Once the Confucian faction took action and killed Qin De, it would be equivalent to their political death.

This consequence was even more serious and terrible than losing their lives.

"In fact, if the sect's higher-ups learn that we are doing this, they might even deliberately let go and allow us to succeed!" thought Chu Xuangui.

He saw things more clearly than most of the Confucian cultivators.

Situ Gu said in despair: "This won't work, that won't work... how can we Confucian cultivators ever raise our heads? Will there ever be a day for us to stand out within the Myriad Manifestations Sect?"

Everyone was speechless, their expressions miserable.

After a long while, Song Taosheng suddenly laughed long and loud, the sound desolate.

He stopped laughing and let out a long sigh: "A tree moved dies, a person moved lives. Gentlemen, I am leaving."

"What?!" The Confucian cultivators were greatly shaken and hurriedly tried to persuade him.

Song Taosheng publicly stated that the future within the Myriad Manifestations Sect was bleak; even the Mind School couldn't succeed. Even if Zhao Hansheng returned after studying for three years, would it really work?

Why hang oneself on a single tree?

Since the Myriad Manifestations Sect wouldn't work, why not leave?

Take a step back, and the sea and sky are vast!

Unable to persuade Song Taosheng, the crowd turned to look at Duanmu Zhang.

Song Taosheng also looked at Duanmu Zhang, a final trace of expectation hidden in the depths of his eyes.

Duanmu Zhang was disheartened; he looked at Song Taosheng and did not say a word.

In the deathly silence, Song Taosheng's final expectation completely dissipated.

He chuckled, turned, and left. His figure stumbled, but he did not hesitate. Until he disappeared from everyone's view, he did not look back once.

Song Taosheng's departure was a huge blow to Duanmu Zhang and all the Confucian cultivators.

"Is there really no day for us Confucian cultivators to stand out in the Myriad Manifestations Sect?"

Huazhang Capital, Tai Temple.

The guardian official pushed open the vermilion doors of the Tai Temple's main hall as usual.

In the next moment, he froze in place, staring in shock at the scene before him.

The national treasure that suppressed the country's destiny, the Golden Chapter Jade Scroll, was emitting a strange light!

The glow was cyan tinged with purple, and the purple was flushed with black, as if some ominous object were breeding within it. The entire main hall of the Tai Temple was illuminated in a bleak pallor.

Cold sweat dripped from the guardian official's forehead as he was suddenly startled awake: "This is a warning from the National Treasure! What is threatening our entire Huazhang Kingdom?!"
