Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 911: Trading Mechanical Birds and Discussing Flowing Cloud

The newcomer was none other than Qing Huangzi.

Qing Huangzi did not have much time left. Therefore, taking advantage of the current Flying Cloud Assembly, he hosted the Rising Cloud Minor Trial to select a suitable cultivator so that his lineage's inheritance, the Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder, could be smoothly passed down.

A few months ago, Ning Zhuo participated in Qing Huangzi's minor trial and gained his approval midway through. The latter wanted to directly announce the results and hand over the inheritance to Ning Zhuo. However, Ning Zhuo refused, strictly following the procedure to defeat his opponents fair and square, ultimately taking the inheritance into his own hands.

Because of this matter, Qing Huangzi appreciated Ning Zhuo even more, and even deeply respected him.

He had personally said to Ning Zhuo, "Go on, go on. Do as your heart desires. I will hold down the fort for you at Green Bamboo Peak for my final watch."

Afterward, having fulfilled his lifelong wish, he lived in seclusion on Green Bamboo Peak, wanting to peacefully spend the final period of his life.

However, this period was far more "lively" than he had anticipated; it was not peaceful or serene at all.

Qing Huangzi paid attention to Ning Zhuo, constantly hearing about his progress - how he won first place in those minor trials, what level of assembly status he had exchanged for, and how he had gained the appreciation of various bigshots.

While gratified that he had found such an outstanding inheritor, Qing Huangzi was also somewhat on edge. He often sighed that this junior, Ning Zhuo, really loved to stir things up.

This time, he first heard the rumors and then received Ning Zhuo's invitation.

Qing Huangzi could no longer sit still.

Ning Zhuo was stirring up way too much trouble this time. It was equivalent to giving up the opportunities at the Demon Subjugation Hall and Myriad Beasts Peak. He wanted to start from scratch and lay his hands on Flowing Cloud Peak, one of the eight major peaks.

What grand ambition this was!

While sighing in admiration, Qing Huangzi also worried that Ning Zhuo was taking too big of a step this time. The faster one walked, the easier it was to stumble, and the harder the fall would be.

This time, Ning Zhuo actively invited him over and clearly stated that he had a request.

Qing Huangzi decisively left Green Bamboo Peak and arrived at Ning Zhuo's newly rented Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor.

"Senior!" Ning Zhuo walked into the reception hall, cupped his hands in greeting, and smiled brightly, giving off a strong sense of intimacy.

Qing Huangzi stood up, looked him up and down, and smiled. "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, we haven't seen each other for a few months, and the things you're doing are getting bigger and bigger."

Ning Zhuo walked up to Qing Huangzi, proactively held his hand, and shook it twice. "Senior, I must still thank you for gifting me the Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder inheritance. A few days ago, I used it to defeat Situ Xing head-on."

After speaking, Ning Zhuo gestured for Qing Huangzi to take a seat.

Qing Huangzi's eyes immediately lit up. He laughed heartily twice and sat back down.

"I have a premonition: handing the inheritance over to you is absolutely one of the most correct things I've done in my life!"

"Up on Green Bamboo Peak, I hear news about you every day; my ears are getting calluses. Winning first place in multiple minor trials, standing out among first-rate geniuses, the Dao-Bearing Jade Page, the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone, the Naming Fire Furnace, the conflict with the Ban Clan, gaining the appreciation of Zhong Dao and Tuoba Huang..."

"Especially this time, you want to establish an alliance and charge the peak. If this matter is truly accomplished, you will be considered a top-tier figure across all past Flying Cloud Assemblies."

Qing Huangzi sighed with deep feeling.

Ning Zhuo replied, "Senior overpraises me. This junior just encountered the right opportunities and went with the flow. To be honest, I wasn't confident in many of the minor trials I participated in; I was merely striving my hardest to be first."

"In terms of results, my luck has been quite good."

"This time is the same. I only employed this strategy because I want to repay my debts."

"Acting this way will inevitably draw more and larger conflicts. Therefore, I wish to purchase some mechanical flying birds from Senior in advance to bolster my combat power."

Ning Zhuo had invited Qing Huangzi over primarily for this matter.

If he were to craft the mechanical flying birds himself, the quantity needed would be too large, wasting too much time and energy, making it very uneconomical. Being new to these mechanical flying birds, he needed to familiarize himself with the blueprints and accumulate crafting experience, which was highly troublesome. Most importantly, there wasn't enough time.

Ning Zhuo was making ample preparations for the worst-case scenario, guarding against anyone causing trouble during the alliance establishment ceremony.

Therefore, he sought to purchase these mechanical flying birds from Qing Huangzi.

Ning Zhuo didn't lack combat power; he had still held back quite a few trump cards during his battle with Situ Xing. However, he lacked conventional combat power.

These mechanical flying birds were something he could display in public, and when combined with his current mechanical arts, the resulting combat power would be quite impressive.

Seeing Ning Zhuo bring it up proactively, Qing Huangzi directly handed him a storage bag.

Ning Zhuo swept it with his divine sense and found the bag completely full of the mechanical flying birds he wanted.

Neck-Spear Cranes, Joint-Hugging Pheasants, Wind-Facing Orioles, Gold-Thread Caged Sparrows, and Sleeve-Piercing Cranes. The first two were the most numerous, while the Wind-Facing Orioles and Gold-Thread Caged Sparrows had the smallest scale. The poison paired with the Sleeve-Piercing Cranes had dedicated bottles for storage.

However, the grades were generally not high; Foundation Establishment-level ones only accounted for a small portion. The vast majority were only at the Qi Refining Stage.

Qing Huangzi said, "These mechanical flying birds will definitely shine in your hands. But, is this all you need? I also have Golden Core-level mechanical puppets on hand."

Ning Zhuo shook his head, "I only need these for now. Senior, please accept this."

Ning Zhuo handed Qing Huangzi a jade box.

Qing Huangzi opened the jade box and saw several Ghost Path Golden Cores resting inside.

Qing Huangzi was briefly surprised, then chuckled helplessly. "I've heard intel saying that when you first entered the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain, you liked to 'smash' people with Golden Cores, acting extremely wealthy when discussing trades. It was only after receiving my inheritance that you switched to using Spirit Stones for trading."

"I didn't expect that today, I'd get to experience it firsthand."

As he spoke, he closed the jade box and pushed it back across the table to Ning Zhuo.

"These mechanical flying birds are just minor items; I'll simply give them to you. Golden Cores are precious; one is more than enough, let alone the four here."

Ning Zhuo shook his head. "Senior, passing the Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder to me is already an immense favor. If I accept your mechanical flying birds for free, this junior's heart will not be at ease."

"Moreover, such is my family tradition, and this junior dare not violate it."

If anyone else said this, Qing Huangzi would think they were just being polite or saying superficial words.

But who was Ning Zhuo?

He was a righteous gentleman who could use the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity to summon a pillar of vast righteous qi!

"I know. The family tradition of not accepting a reward without merit." Qing Huangzi laughed heartily.

Ning Zhuo's family tradition, coupled with the stories of him consecutively gaining the appreciation of Zhong Dao and Tuoba Huang but politely declining their offers, had already spread widely throughout the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain.

"Alright. We'll consider these mechanical flying birds as you buying them from me. But the value of the Ghost Path Golden Cores far exceeds them. I must compensate you with something else."

Ning Zhuo smiled and said, "Senior, there is no rush."

"It is best that you keep your Golden Core-level mechanical puppets by your side for self-defense."

"I am confident in my alliance establishment and peak charge this time. I only fear that the impact will grow larger in the future and implicate you. After all, I am currently the sole inheritor of the Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder inheritance. We have a close relationship and are, through and through, our own people."

"With your old foundation keeping you safe, this junior can also feel a bit more at ease."

"Our inheritance and the prestige of Green Bamboo Peak must not be easily diminished."

These words immediately moved Qing Huangzi.

Especially Ning Zhuo's mention of the term "our own people," which made Qing Huangzi feel secretly gratified.

Observing his expression and seeing Qing Huangzi in a good mood, Ning Zhuo voiced his request: "Senior, these five types of mechanical flying birds are of great use to me. But I do not have enough time or energy to craft them myself."

"Let these Ghost Path Golden Cores serve as this junior's deposit. Could I beseech Senior to step in and help me craft a few more? Preferably Foundation Establishment-level ones, produced in large quantities."

Qing Huangzi nodded first. "Alright, I promise you."

He agreed very decisively, without a shred of hesitation.

According to his previous life plans, Qing Huangzi wanted to enjoy his twilight years peacefully, not wanting to toil at all in his limited remaining life.

But since this matter concerned Ning Zhuo, it was a completely different story.

Today, the talent, righteousness, destiny, and bright future that Ning Zhuo displayed all made Qing Huangzi willing to change his life plans to exert some effort for Ning Zhuo.

To someone approaching their limit, no matter how many Ghost Path Golden Cores there were, they actually held no appeal.

The main factor was Ning Zhuo himself; in Qing Huangzi's eyes, he was worth the sacrifice!

Qing Huangzi added, "Although I can mass-produce these mechanical flying birds, there are distinctions among them."

"The Neck-Spear Crane, Joint-Hugging Pheasant, and Sleeve-Piercing Stork - these three types of mechanical flying birds are no problem for me, and the production speed will be fast."

"But the Wind-Facing Kite and the Gold-Thread Caged Sparrow are different."

"The former is like a kite and involves papercrafting techniques. The latter has a much more ingenious structure and requires formidable weaving skills."

"You also have the complete contents of the Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder inheritance, so you must have anticipated this, right?"

Ning Zhuo nodded, "I understand. To be honest, the crafting of the Wind-Facing Kites and Gold-Thread Caged Sparrows is quite a headache for me as well. Then I will trouble Senior to first mass-produce the Neck-Spear Cranes, Joint-Hugging Pheasants, and Sleeve-Piercing Cranes. Focus primarily on the Neck-Spear Cranes, with the latter two making up twenty percent each."

Qing Huangzi nodded, "Done."

He then encouraged, "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, you are still young. With such strong learning ability and such high comprehension, when you dabble in papercrafting and weaving techniques in the future, these troubles will be no problem for you at all."

Ning Zhuo had intentionally displayed his learning and comprehension abilities to the outside world, especially during the minor trial in the Black Armor Cave. Seeing Qing Huangzi's reaction now, he knew that his previous actions had yielded excellent results.

Qing Huangzi added, "Actually, for the Neck-Spear Cranes, Joint-Hugging Pheasants, and Sleeve-Piercing Storks, you can hire others to do the work. The Wind-Facing Kites and Gold-Thread Caged Sparrows, however, are difficult to divide labor for."

The contents of the Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder inheritance needed to be kept strictly confidential.

Therefore, the blueprints could absolutely not be leaked.

Compared to conventional artifact refining, crafting mechanisms had the convenient aspect of being able to be broken down into countless mechanical parts. Some of the unimportant mechanical components could be outsourced for others to produce.

But the structure of the Wind-Facing Kite was very simple, utilizing papercrafting techniques. The true focus of its production lay in the related formations and talismans. It was not easy to disclose these formation diagrams and talismans, making it inconvenient to casually hire formation masters or talisman masters as subcontractors.

As for the Gold-Thread Caged Sparrow, if reduced to its core, it was essentially a single bamboo strip. Through weaving, the bamboo strip ultimately formed the mechanical shape of a bird in a cage.

The crafting requirements for this mechanical flying bird were the highest, especially regarding weaving techniques.

Therefore, in Qing Huangzi's lifelong accumulation, the quantity of Wind-Facing Kites and Gold-Thread Caged Sparrows was the smallest, especially the latter. Back then, Qing Huangzi's weaving was solely to train his mechanical crafting abilities; his efficiency was extremely low, and he produced them through massive amounts of trial and error, simply grinding out the time.

"Actually, if you could turn weapons into jade and silk and make peace with the Ban Clan, it might be possible to borrow their clan's foundational Dao Vessel, the Gold Thread Silver Shuttle Plate."

"This Dao Vessel can allow even a cultivator with zero weaving foundation to weave a Renowned Artisan-level magical net."

"Using it to assist in crafting the Gold-Thread Caged Sparrow would cause your speed to soar, covering a thousand li in a single day!" Qing Huangzi thought for a moment before speaking with deep meaning.

"The Dao Vessel, the Gold Thread Silver Shuttle Plate?" Ning Zhuo frowned slightly.

Being enemies with Ban Ji, he naturally had investigated the Ban Clan. This Dao Vessel was one of the Ban Clan's openly known foundations, so Ning Zhuo was certainly aware of it.

"Senior's reminder is correct; one more friend is better than one more enemy. Especially since I am establishing an alliance to charge the peak, I'm afraid layer upon layer of trials will come unceasingly." Ning Zhuo understood the underlying meaning in Qing Huangzi's words.

Qing Huangzi nodded and smiled, feeling that his admonition had been effective.

Little did he know that what Ning Zhuo was actually thinking in his heart was: "If I can draw the Ban Clan into taking action, that wouldn't be bad either. Wouldn't that accelerate my digestion of this current calamity?"

"However, Ban Ji has yet to officially join the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Mother told me that this calamity should manifest within the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

"Those who will be useful to me are the members who have already long joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

The people on Flowing Cloud Peak were exactly Ning Zhuo's targets!

Thus, next, after storing away the storage bag, Ning Zhuo took the opportunity to ask Qing Huangzi for guidance regarding the various large and small factions on Flowing Cloud Peak.

Qing Huangzi was an old member of the Myriad Manifestations Sect and knew much more deeply about the matters of Flowing Cloud Peak than Ning Zhuo did.

Towards Ning Zhuo, Qing Huangzi naturally shared everything he knew without reservation: "Flowing Cloud Peak has always been extremely chaotic. Even when there are few, there are still dozens of large and small factions. When there are many, the number exceeds a hundred."

"These factions are like a revolving door, constantly rising and falling. The competition is extremely fierce. In my memory, it has always been this turbulent."

"Even if a faction stands out and a strong individual temporarily takes the position of Peak Lord of Flowing Cloud Peak, it is only temporary. After a while, they will be beaten down or pushed out."

"However, over the last hundred years or so, the strongest faction on Flowing Cloud Peak is undoubtedly the Thunder Cloud Society."

"The Thunder Cloud Society is extremely powerful. Their President is a peak Nascent Soul-level great cultivator who specializes in lightning methods, possessing immense lethality."

"Under him, there are also four Nascent Soul-level elders."

"The cultivation of the rest of the members is, at the very least, Golden Core-level."

"The Thunder Cloud Society occupies the summit of Flowing Cloud Peak, where the spiritual energy is the most abundant. The terrain is perilous, easy to defend and hard to attack. The Thunder Cloud Society set up the 'Heavenly Thunder Attraction' Formation there. Once activated, it can summon nine bolts of heavenly thunder, the power of which is comparable to a Nascent Soul strike."

In reality, Ning Zhuo already knew this intelligence; he had long since purchased it from the Commerce Hall.

However, Ning Zhuo still quietly listened for a while before consulting Qing Huangzi about which factions the Thunder Cloud Society was closely related to on Flowing Cloud Peak, and which of the other seven peaks and sixteen halls they were closer to.

This intelligence was the real focal point; it could reveal the secrets of power regarding camps, alliances, and so on.

This was intelligence that Ning Zhuo could not buy from the Commerce Hall; only an old timer like Qing Huangzi would know a little about it.
