Chapter 873: The Jade Token Delivers Itself

This was a neat and tidy circular space.

The four walls were condensed from transparent spiritual light. The slightly pink walls of light were like an eggshell, isolating everything outside. The ground was made of smooth bluestone, engraved with densely packed formation patterns. Filled with cinnabar, they gleamed with a dark red luster under the warm light, resembling a massive spider web.

In the center of the space floated a moon-white Jade Token.

The Jade Token slowly rotated. With every revolution, a faint ring of spiritual light spread out, rippling like water.

A stream of information traveled along the spiritual light, reaching every cultivator who arrived in the Formation Space, informing them of the specific rules.

As a member dispatched by the Transmission Hall to contend for the Nanming Fire Furnace, Hong Lie entered the Formation Space, looked around, and couldn't help but exclaim in praise, "What an exquisite formation!"

He was immediately drawn to the Jade Token in the center: "Three chances, huh..."

He wasn't surprised.

The Transmission Hall was one of the sixteen halls, an internal organization of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, so they naturally obtained the inside information early on.

Moreover, Danxia Peak's organization of this Cloud-Rising Minor Trial was too rushed, forced by circumstances to meet deadlines, making much of the information difficult to keep secret.

Hong Lie walked up to the Jade Token and stopped.

He took out a futon, sat cross-legged, and began to execute his Cultivation Art.

His main cultivation was the Three Yang True Fire Incantation. Cultivating it to a profound level allowed him to summon the Three Yang True Fire.

As Hong Lie executed his Cultivation Art, the temperature in the space began to rise rapidly.

It wasn't a scorching, suffocating heat, but a gentle warmth like the morning sun. As the saying goes, the three yang bring prosperity, warming people's hearts and bringing entirely new hope.

The Jade Token had no reaction; one chance had been used up.

Hong Lie frowned slightly and continued to execute his Cultivation Art.

His entire body emanated an orange-yellow light as he pushed the Cultivation Art to its absolute limit.

A moment later, the Jade Token was still slowly rotating without any change.

The second chance was also used up.

Hong Lie grew nervous: "There are only three chances, and I only have my last chance left. It's time to use my trump card."

He gave a dark shout in his heart, going all out, not hesitating to frantically consume his spiritual energy. Turning his palm upward, he coaxed a cluster of flame from his palm - the Three Yang True Fire!

Filled with hope, he presented the flame in his hand to the Jade Token.

The Jade Token extinguished its own faint light, slowly stopped rotating, and became perfectly still.

"Did I... did I succeed?" Hong Lie's eyes widened.

Then, in the very next moment, he was teleported out.

A guarding cultivator hollered beside him: "Hurry up! Those who have been eliminated, don't linger. Fly away as quickly as possible, there are still people waiting behind you."

Hong Lie: "..."

He subconsciously complied, riding the clouds to fly away from the cliff, before finally snapping back to reality.

"I was eliminated!"

"All three chances used up, even though I summoned the Three Yang True Fire."

"How difficult it is to gain the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's approval!"

He observed his surroundings and found that the majority of cultivators were also carrying the frustration and despondency of being eliminated.

These people came to try with a sliver of hope, but exhausting their three chances completely killed off any fluke they had in their hearts.

Seeing so many people in the same boat as him, Hong Lie felt slightly better, sighing in his heart: "It's exactly because the difficulty is so high, to the point that Danxia Peak couldn't handle it, that they resorted to treating a dead horse as a living one and organized this Cloud-Rising Minor Trial. Me being eliminated is entirely reasonable."

Another figure stepped into the Formation Space.

He was an herb farmer from Hundred Herbs Peak, with Foundation Establishment cultivation. He didn't know how to refine pills or understand formations; he only knew one kind of work - planting, picking, and refining herbs.

He walked under the Jade Token, sat cross-legged, and first circulated his Cultivation Art.

A fresh aura, a mix of green grass and soil, lingered in the space, making one unconsciously feel close and relaxed.

The Jade Token had no reaction; one chance used.

The herb farmer took out the medicinal herbs he carried with him and began to refine medicine.

The medicinal herbs from Hundred Herbs Peak were naturally of high quality, mostly rarely seen on the market.

Cinnabar Red Fleeceflower, Six-Six Snow Lotus, Golden Scale Ginseng...

The Jade Token ultimately stopped rotating, and the herb farmer was eliminated.

"Sigh..." Following the crowd, the herb farmer flew away from the cliff. His state of mind quickly returned to peace. "I've already given it my all. I only hope my companions will succeed."

Old Chef carried a food box and entered the Formation Space.

Coming before the Jade Token, he opened the food box.

The food box had three distinct tiers.

The first tier contained various seasonings - Sichuan peppercorns, chili peppers, ginger, garlic, cinnamon bark, star anise, bay leaves... a dazzling array of a full twenty to thirty types.

The second tier contained fresh ingredients - spirit beast meat, spirit bird eggs, spirit vegetables, spirit mushrooms, spirit rice... every single one a meticulously selected high-grade item.

The third tier contained a set of kitchen utensils - a cleaver, a small wok, an iron ladle, and a pair of long chopsticks. The cleaver's edge was sharpened to a gleam, the wok's bottom was thick and smooth, the ladle's handle was polished shiny from use, and the tips of the long chopsticks were slightly blackened, traces left from years of constant use.

A moment later, Old Chef had set up a simple kitchen within the Formation Space.

He first took out a piece of spirit beast meat, his hand rising and falling with the knife. The cleaver made rhythmic "thwack, thwack, thwack" sounds on the cutting board, as lively as a war drum. The spirit beast meat was sliced into uniformly thick pieces.

Subsequently, he took out a large handful of dried chili peppers, chopping them into fine segments. He smashed the ginger and garlic, and chopped the scallions into sections. Everything was done cleanly and smoothly like flowing water.

Then, he set up the small wok, flipped his palm, and a cluster of flames rose from it, licking the bottom of the wok.

Old Chef tossed the ginger and garlic into the wok.

Sizzle -

White smoke billowed, and a rich aroma permeated the space. The fragrance released by the ginger and garlic, stimulated by the high heat, was spicy and intense, so stimulating it rushed straight to the head.

Then, Old Chef tossed the spirit beast meat into the wok, quickly stir-frying it. The meat slices tumbled in the wok, their edges curling slightly. Their color changed from bright red to golden yellow, forming a layer of fragrant, crispy crust on the surface.

Next, the chili peppers went into the wok.

In an instant, the aroma explosively intensified.

Overbearing, intense! It was like a ball of flame exploding in the nasal cavity.

The spiciness of the chili peppers intertwined with the umami of the spirit beast meat, forming a complex, distinctively layered scent that pervaded the space, filling every corner.

After turning off the fire, Old Chef transferred the dish from the wok onto a celadon plate. The dish on the plate was bright red and glistened with oil. The red of the chilies and the golden yellow of the meat slices complemented each other, and its mouth-watering aroma assailed the nose.

He reached out his hands, offering the celadon plate to the Jade Token.

The Jade Token slowly rotated.

No reaction.

Old Chef's gaze slightly narrowed: "The chili peppers I chose are of the Fire Element, they should be the closest to the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's disposition. Did it still fail to move it...?"

Old Chef's strategy was different from the others; he used his trump card right from the start.

Since the trump card failed, Old Chef immediately made a new decision: "In that case, the most important thing for me to do next is to try two other directions to gather more intelligence for the Young Master."

A moment later, Old Chef also flew out from the back cliff, becoming one of the eliminated members.

Ning Zhuo's vision suddenly lit up; he had entered the Formation Space.

The four walls were transparent spiritual light, slightly pink, like an eggshell.

The ground was smooth bluestone, completely covered in dense formation patterns. Filled with cinnabar, they gleamed with a dark red luster under the warm light, like a giant spider web spreading outward from the center.

Ning Zhuo's gaze first fell on the walls of light: "This should be a variant application of the Light Wall Formation."

Then, he swept his eyes over the bluestone floor tiles, his gaze pausing briefly on the cinnabar: "A triple formation using cinnabar as the conduit."

"The first layer is the Azure Void Earth Reversal Formation. The earth is reversed to become the sky, using the earth as the sky. This perfectly fits the unconstrained nature of the dreamscape. Excellent."

"The second layer is the Constellation Formation. No, it's just the Twenty-Eight Constellations formation patterns." Ning Zhuo's divine sense penetrated into the interior of the bluestone floor tiles.

"My previous judgment was wrong; it's only a double formation. The constellation patterns in the middle don't form a complete formation, but they are still able to filter out messy, meaningless information."

"The innermost layer is the Four Symbols Formation. Azure Dragon, White Tiger, Vermilion Bird, Black Tortoise - the four symbols each occupy a direction, with the Vermilion Bird serving as the primary one. That's a given, after all, the artifact spirit is in the form of a Vermilion Bird, perfectly corresponding to the Nanming Pill Furnace."

Ning Zhuo possessed a realm in formation dao that ordinary people could hardly reach; naturally, what he observed was much deeper than others.

He raised his head slightly, focusing his gaze on the Jade Token in the air.

The big-headed youth furrowed his brow slightly, pondering inwardly: "This Jade Token should be a formation artifact, attuned to the most core dreamscape methods. But at the same time, it is also the Formation Core of this Formation Space. One artifact serving multiple purposes, truly ingenious."

Wanting to take a closer look, Ning Zhuo stepped towards the Jade Token.

The next moment, the Jade Token lit up and flew directly toward Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo instinctively reached out and caught it. In just a split second, he was teleported away, outside the Formation, finding himself inside a pavilion halfway up the mountain.

"Congratulations to Your Excellency - ah, it's Young Master Ning Zhuo."

"Congratulations. The methods you displayed have obtained the preliminary approval of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit."

"Possessing this token grants you the qualification to enter the next round of the Minor Trial."

Ning Zhuo was a little dumbfounded.

He had merely gone in and observed for a few glances. He hadn't even unleashed his prepared methods!

How did he succeed just like that?

"Is the difficulty this low? I passed without doing anything." Ning Zhuo pondered in his heart. The surprise that had just surfaced quickly dissipated, replaced by a sense of doubt.

He looked at the guarding cultivator: "There should be quite a few cultivators who gained approval, right?"

The guarding cultivator looked perplexed for a moment: "Uh, from what this lowly one has seen, the number is not large."

Ning Zhuo nodded slightly and then inquired about the start time of the next round.

The guarding cultivator: "Judging by the current results, Young Master should be able to participate in the next round of the Minor Trial in about two days."

Ning Zhuo flew away from Danxia Peak and returned to the bluestone cave dwelling.

Not long after, Old Chef, Sun Lingtong, and the others also successively returned.

Ning Zhuo asked about their experiences and encounters.

Sun Lingtong spread his hands: "I saw the Jade Token looked pretty good, reached out and grabbed it, and was teleported away."

Qing Chi also said: "It was strange. Before I even unleashed my Azure Flame, the Jade Token came to me, acknowledging that I had received the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's approval."

Hearing the words of the two, Old Chef found it somewhat hard to believe.

He recounted his own experience, failing three consecutive times.

"But for the final attempt, Young Master, I tried making a bowl of lotus seed soup. My thought was - lotus seeds have a neutral nature and a sweet taste, entering the heart, spleen, and kidney meridians. They can nourish the heart and calm the mind, tonify the spleen and benefit the kidneys. Perhaps it could soothe the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit."

"After it was made, the Jade Token trembled slightly twice, but in the end, it still didn't fly into my embrace."

Ning Zhuo nodded, committing this precious piece of information to heart.

He looked at everyone and stated that he was the same. Like Sun Lingtong and Qing Chi, he hadn't displayed any methods; he simply got close to the Jade Token and acquired it.

As to why this happened, Ning Zhuo was still filled with doubt and had absolutely no answers.

"Let's see what Gongsun Yan has to say when he returns."

A short while later, Gongsun Yan also returned to the bluestone cave dwelling.

"That was close. In the final third round, I had no choice but to display my Innate Talent, Heart Fire. Only then did the Jade Token acknowledge me and fly into my hands."

Gongsun Yan also felt that the difficulty of this Minor Trial was extremely high.

Having already used his biggest trump card, he had absolutely no confidence in the second round of the Minor Trial.

"Bizarre, truly bizarre." Ning Zhuo had originally hoped to find some sort of pattern in this, but instead sank into deeper confusion.

Sun Lingtong suggested: "Little Zhuo, just looking at the number of people eliminated, this Minor Trial is indeed extremely difficult. Our three situations are definitely a bit odd. Why not ask others?"

Ning Zhuo nodded, immediately agreeing with Sun Lingtong's suggestion.

By expanding the sample size for reference, perhaps they could discern a pattern within it. Applying it in the next round could increase their chances of success.

It had been a while since Ning Zhuo arrived at the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and because he was kind to others and possessed brilliant tact, his social circle constantly expanded, earning him quite a few friends.

Ning Zhuo cast a wide net, sending flying letters to Shen Xi and the others, and used the divine statue to secretly communicate with Zhu Fenxiang.

While sending out flying letters, he also received them.

The first letter came from Yun Niaoniao.

This female cultivator, only at the Foundation Establishment cultivation level, was born into a family with a long lineage of medicine. She spent years treating impoverished commoners and injured cultivators free of charge, earning her the title of "Little Bodhisattva."

She had cooperated with Ning Zhuo during the Demon Subjugation Hall's Cloud-Rising Minor Trial. Having long heard of Ning Zhuo's reputation, she held him in high esteem.

Ning Zhuo unfolded the letter to read: "Yun Niaoniao also passed the first round, but she was only acknowledged by the Jade Token during her second chance when she displayed her medical skills."

"I remember... she also performed outstandingly during the Sink Karma Swamp trial. When stepping onto the swamp, the mud only reached her ankles."

Not long after, Shen Xi replied, informing Ning Zhuo that he himself had been eliminated, but Situ Xing and Lin Jinglong both got selected and advanced to the next round.

When night fell, Ning Zhuo checked the divine statue and found that Zhu Fenxiang had also replied to him.

"Zhu Fenxiang also passed the first round. She summoned a god beforehand, bringing forth a burst of boundless vitality, which earned her the approval of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit."

"For the next step, she plans to summon a more fitting god to assist her. Her ultimate goal is one of the Four Symbols, the Southern Vermilion Bird."

"If she really can summon It to grant her blessings, I'm afraid the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit might be drawn to her out of natural adoration?"

Ning Zhuo pondered for a moment, parsing out the hidden meaning behind Zhu Fenxiang's words.

On one hand, Zhu Fenxiang demonstrated her own strength, expressing her determination to contend for the Nanming Fire Furnace. On the other hand, Zhu Fenxiang was also hinting to Ning Zhuo that if he also wanted the help of the gods, he could come and "beg" her for assistance.

"Once I take the initiative to speak up, Zhu Fenxiang will hold the initiative in setting the price," Ning Zhuo chuckled softly.

Soon after, he restrained his smile, his brow slightly furrowing. He had asked around but still hadn't found any obvious pattern.
