Chapter 868: The Furnace Contention

Danxia Peak, Assembly Hall.

In the exact center of the hall sat a massive incense furnace.

At this moment, wisps of green smoke curled upward from the furnace, lingering beneath the domed ceiling and suffusing the entire hall with a quiet, solemn atmosphere.

Twelve purple sandalwood chairs were arrayed on both sides. The reliefs carved into their backs depicted various pill patterns, resembling clouds and glowing rosy twilight.

The main seat in the center was slightly elevated above the rest. Draped across it was an intact, snow-white fox fur pelt. Soft and thick, it made the person sitting upright upon it appear even more handsome and otherworldly.

Wang Yu sat very upright, his back straight. Under the illumination of the lamps, the rosy-cloud patterns on his cloud-robe shimmered with a faint crimson-gold luster.

His left hand rested on the armrest, five fingers slightly curled. His posture seemed casual, but concealed a stiffness that was hard for outsiders to detect.

In his right hand, he held a brand-new white jade fly whisk. The whisk's threads were as white as snow and hung motionless by the side of the chair.

Wang Yu's complexion was normal, perhaps even a bit rosier than usual. His eyes were deep like the stars, save for a thin, dim, grey film that would flash only in the brief moments when he shifted his gaze or blinked.

This hint of dimness was perfectly concealed by his composed demeanor. Without careful observation, it was completely imperceptible.

The hall was already full of people.

All were core figures and important high-ranking members of Danxia Peak.

Wang Yu coughed lightly, making the hall seem even quieter.

Following that, the current Peak Master of Danxia Peak slowly spoke: "I have invited you all here today for the matter of the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"The Nanming Fire Furnace is one of the major foundations of our Danxia Peak. Now, the furnace is destroyed and its spirit frightened, teetering on the brink of death."

"Ordinary methods of pacification are completely ineffective. Forcibly repairing it is extremely inappropriate; if the artifact spirit is frightened to death, the Nanming Fire Furnace will no longer be a spiritual treasure. Restoring it in the future would be incomparably difficult."

"This is not merely an internal affair of our peak; the Sect Master is also paying close attention."

"The relevant news regarding the Rising Cloud Minor Trial has already been proactively announced. But I would much prefer that 'the meat rots in our own pot'."

"Just like the Demon Subjugation Hall. When an incident occurred in the Cloud Prison, they were able to handle it themselves."

"The Nanming Fire Furnace is inherently an asset of our peak. It would be best if it could remain in our own hands."

"As for how to achieve this, if any of you have ideas, please speak your minds freely!"

As his voice faded, a dead silence fell over the hall.

This silence lasted for about three breaths.

An old alchemist suddenly stood up, drawing many gazes. "Peak Master! I cannot escape blame for the destruction of the Nanming Fire Furnace! Over the past few days, my colleagues and I have perused the archives of our peak and the Alchemy Hall, and made numerous inquiries to draft a list of names."

"The cultivators on this list are all exceptional talents of the current era. Perhaps they can regain the trust of the Vermillion Bird artifact spirit and keep the Nanming Fire Furnace on our peak."

The old alchemist's hair and beard were grizzled, his figure trembling slightly. His voice was hoarse, and his expression carried an almost pleading urgency - an urgency to atone for his mistakes.

Wang Yu quietly glanced at the old alchemist, then slowly swept his gaze across the entire hall. Only after a full ten breaths did he nod. "Since no one objects, we might as well discuss this list first."

Having received the Peak Master's permission, the old alchemist immediately spoke: "First on the list is the Vice Hall Master of the Alchemy Hall, Jiang Ping."

"He is three hundred and twenty years old, with a cultivation at the Mid Golden Core stage. In his one hundred and ten years since joining the sect, he has produced a high volume of pills with excellent quality, and is especially skilled at refining 'Spirit-Nourishing Pills'. During a Sect Grand Competition, he once took first place with a batch of 'Six-Cycle Spirit-Nourishing Pills', achieving a 73% success rate, six pill patterns, and a pill fragrance that lingered for three days without dissipating."

"This person possesses an incredibly steady temperament while concocting pills and exerts exquisite control over the heat. He once set a record of refining pills continuously for thirty-seven days and nights without rest, during which a furnace did not explode even once. In my view, he is truly one of the most outstanding Golden Core-level alchemists to emerge from our peak in the last hundred years."

The cultivators present remained silent.

More accurately, no one objected.

The Myriad Manifestations Sect was the number one sect in the Flying Cloud Kingdom, teeming with talent. Danxia Peak, in particular, was founded on alchemy and had complete control over the Alchemy Hall. Jiang Ping, placed among them, did not have particularly outstanding alchemy attainments, yet he was recommended first.

The reason for this was simply that Jiang Ping was one of the disciples taken in by Wang Yu.

The old alchemist putting Jiang Ping at the top of the recommendation list was entirely a matter of political correctness.

Not a single person present raised an objection.

Wang Yu's expression was calm. "Continue."

The old alchemist then recommended the second person: Shen Hongyao, one of the deacons of the Alchemy Hall.

"This woman is known as 'Fairy Hongyao' and also as 'Half-Yama'. She is two hundred and thirty years old but is already at the Late Golden Core stage. The pills she specializes in fall into two distinct extremes. The first is the 'Life-Sustaining Pill'; she once used this pill to save over thirty thousand people during a plague. The second is the 'Life-Detonating Pellet', which, when consumed, allows one to erupt with power far beyond their limits..."

This time, the old alchemist had barely finished speaking when someone voiced a dissenting opinion.

"Shen Hongyao's alchemy techniques are indeed unconventional, often featuring shocking moves. She once added 'Yin Fiend Spring Water' during her pill concoction, which increased the efficacy of the Life-Sustaining Pills by thirty percent, but this also caused her furnace to explode three times, injuring seven people."

"She acts audaciously and is frequently punished with 'Furnace Bans'. Her disregard for convention is both a strength and a weakness. The Vermillion Bird artifact spirit is currently frightened; if she were to perform another 'shocking move', it would likely add fuel to the fire."

The old alchemist argued strongly: "However, this woman's sincere desire to save people cannot be faked. Artifact spirits are the most sensitive and can discern the truth of a human's heart. Shen Hongyao's utmost sincerity might be able to move the Vermillion Bird artifact spirit."

Someone else agreed: "That makes sense. Besides, is there such a thing as alchemy without exploding furnaces? Have you and I never blown up a furnace in our lives?"

The sounds of discussion gradually rose, and the atmosphere in the hall became somewhat noisy.

Wang Yu listened quietly from the sidelines. After a moment, he spoke mildly: "Move on to the next person."

The old alchemist nodded hastily. "Yes."

He continued his recommendations.

There was Qing Baizi, a Guest Cultivator of Danxia Peak with a Nascent Soul cultivation. He was originally a rogue cultivator who had traveled widely and was especially skilled at refining 'Soul-Nourishing' and 'Mind-Calming' pills.

There was Qin Ke, an alchemist from Danxia Peak itself. Having been in Danxia Peak for ninety years, he apprenticed under Elder Sun and was an alchemist nurtured by the peak itself. Known for "Pulling Chestnuts from the Fire," he had once used precise spiritual energy control to forcibly separate the pill liquid inside a furnace at the very moment it was about to explode, saving sixty percent of the pills.

There was Gu Lianjie. Originally an Outer Sect disciple, he was exceptionally promoted into the Alchemy Hall due to his outstanding alchemy talent. His pill completion speed was extremely fast. He once set a record of refining forty-nine furnaces of pills in a single day, averaging less than half an hour per furnace, while maintaining a success rate above sixty percent.

There was Zhongli Mei, the former Hall Master of the Alchemy Hall, one of the Grand Elders of Danxia Peak, and one of the most senior veteran alchemists alive today. He had once refined the Water-Fire Dual Element Pill, shocking the entire Flying Cloud Kingdom.

Apart from the first person, everyone who followed was heavily discussed and even became the center of swirling arguments.

The voices in the main hall grew increasingly chaotic.

When the old alchemist finished his recommendations, Wang Yu did not give a final verdict. Instead, he asked the other cultivators if they had any alternative candidates to recommend.

Instantly, the cultivators in the hall spoke up one after another, expressing their views.

Wang Yu sat steadily in the main seat, his expression remaining perpetually placid.

No matter who was making the recommendation, they always placed Jiang Ping first. Even if they didn't, their very first sentence would express that they had no objections regarding Jiang Ping.

Wang Yu committed every person's behavior and words to heart, constantly analyzing them.

He knew very well: on the surface, this was about recommending talent to preserve the Nanming Fire Furnace, but beneath the surface, it was about a shift in the political landscape of Danxia Peak and the Artifact Refining Hall!

After the Nanming Fire Furnace exploded, the several old alchemists in charge of pill refining bore heavy blame and were inevitably going to have to yield much of their political standing. The space they left behind had already allowed many cultivators to smell blood in the water. Right now, they were ostensibly arguing on the merits, but in reality, it was a "bloody slaughter."

Wang Yu quietly observed for a moment, and his mind gradually eased.

Regardless of who it was, they all endorsed Jiang Ping. This proved that his control over Danxia Peak and the Alchemy Hall remained ironclad, without any issues.

Wang Yu's thoughts couldn't help but drift. His eyes flickered slightly, concealing a hint of worry.

"The root cause of the furnace explosion this time was the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy being overturned. We were personally involved, and as a result, the Qi Luck of the various main peaks and halls were all dragged down with it."

Wang Yu knew the truth and knew that these old alchemists had been terribly wronged. But he absolutely would not speak up to reverse the situation for them.

"One must comply with the hearts of the people and follow the flow of the current."

The initiative was firmly grasped in Wang Yu's hands.

By finalizing each candidate, he could effortlessly steer the political shift in the direction he desired.

"The Sect Master handled this brilliantly, pulling in almost all the core and high-ranking members to collectively share the burden of this depletion and turbulence in Qi Luck. Only with such impartial treatment can the scales be balanced."

What worried him was not himself, nor the political situations of Danxia Peak or even the Myriad Manifestations Sect, but rather the Nanming Fire Furnace.

"The explosion of the Nanming Fire Furnace was caused by the ripple effect of the Qi Luck. Even though our peak encompasses the power of the Alchemy Hall and is teeming with talent, and even if we go all out with the single-minded goal of retaining the Nanming Fire Furnace, I fear that in the end, it will just be drawing water with a bamboo basket - all in vain."

Although Wang Yu did not cultivate the esoteric art of Qi Luck, he was a senior with rich experience and held a high position, so he had at least some understanding of it.

His personal Qi Luck and the Qi Luck of Danxia Peak were strongly linked, and both were implicated by the incident with the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy. Their Qi Luck had plummeted significantly and was clearly at rock bottom.

With insufficient Qi Luck, they could not save the Vermillion Bird artifact spirit. Even if they managed to save it, they would likely be unable to retain the Nanming Fire Furnace.

"If it is like this for our peak, the other main peaks and halls are in a similar state regarding their Qi Luck."

"Only Purple Thunder Peak, Flowing Cloud Peak, and the Demon Subjugation Hall suffered the least reduction."

"But I fear that the one who can ultimately save the Vermillion Bird artifact spirit and retain the Nanming Fire Furnace is still somewhere on the periphery."

Thinking of this, he immediately formulated a plan.

"The truly important candidates to examine are newcomers, or the marginal figures of the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

"However, the timing of recruiting them must be grasped perfectly!"

If recruited too early, a target who would have otherwise gained the trust of the Vermillion Bird artifact spirit might experience unforeseen accidents due to their declining Qi Luck, ultimately making them very likely to fail.

But if recruited too late, it would be too late. More factions and cliques would swarm in to recruit them. Although Danxia Peak was strong, its competitiveness was only relative.

"Even if the internal list is finalized this time, they won't be the main force."

"We must gather those among the periphery or sect marginals who are most likely to seize the Nanming Fire Furnace, and strive to recruit them at the most opportune moment!"

"Of course, the actions to investigate and gather information must be carried out in secret."

Old Man Baicao was in his medicinal garden, watering his spiritual herbs.

From the moment a seed entered the soil and sprouted, to the seedling growing branches, flowering, and bearing fruit, he tended to every type of herb personally, never delegating the work to others.

He worked at sunrise and rested at sunset, keeping company with the herbs and neighboring the soil. He enjoyed this kind of life.

A Daoist boy trotted over, pushed open the fence gate, and barged in.

"In such a rush, what kind of proper behavior is this?" Old Man Baicao criticized mildly. His wrist was steady, and the stream of water flowing over the herbs remained perfectly consistent, neither too large nor too small.

The boy panted heavily as he informed Old Man Baicao of the news regarding the Nanming Fire Furnace and the Vermillion Bird artifact spirit.

The stream of water Old Man Baicao maintained trembled slightly. His divine sense fell into disorder, causing the stream to split into a fork.

But quickly, the fork disappeared, and the small stream of water returned to its original state.

"The Nanming Fire Furnace... that is one of Danxia Peak's foundations! To think it is actually only a hairline away from destruction."

"It's already incredibly rare for an alchemy furnace to give birth to an artifact spirit. To generate one in the form of a Vermillion Bird is even more difficult."

Old Man Baicao glanced at the boy and tested him on the spot: "You've also read the Commentary on the Ancient Text of the Dragon and Tiger Classic. Do you know why the Vermillion Bird form is so exceedingly rare?"

The boy wiped the sweat from his forehead, thought for a moment, and replied in a crisp voice: "I know, My Lord."

"Within the divine chamber, the golden liquid is born from fire. And the Vermillion Bird is the god of fire, steaming from below..."

The boy eloquently recited a large section. Upon reaching a part he was deeply familiar with, he couldn't help but sway his head back and forth.

"Mhm." Old Man Baicao waved his hand. "That's enough."

The boy stared with shining eyes. "My Lord, since the Vermillion Bird artifact spirit is so rare, are you going to go?"

Old Man Baicao set down the water gourd, crouched down, and plucked a weed from the herb bed. He brought it to his nose, sniffed it, and tossed it aside. Standing up, he dusted the dirt off his hands.

"I'm going."

Nine different colors of firelight reflected on the face of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord.

He was a Demon Cultivator possessing the bloodline of a Fire Dragon. He excelled at controlling fire, and his greatest specialty was having nine fires simultaneously in a single furnace - a signature skill he had practiced for over forty years.

Earth fire, wood fire, thunder fire, heart fire, true fire... At this moment, under the control of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, nine different flames burned fiercely inside a single alchemy furnace, each performing its own function without interfering with the others.

A flying letter suddenly barged in.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord swept it with his divine sense, and his hands instantly trembled slightly. The hand seals he was holding lost their form, causing the nine fires in the furnace to fall into disorder for a split second.

In just that split second, the leaked fiery energy caused the flying letter to spontaneously combust.

Two breaths later, the flying letter turned into a cloud of flying ash.

A brilliant light filled the eyes of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord. "The Nanming Fire Furnace! The Vermillion Bird artifact spirit!"

"It has been over two hundred years since I joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect and became a Guest Elder of Danxia Peak."

"I have never been qualified to touch the Nanming Fire Furnace. Yet now, I actually have the opportunity to become its master?!"

Even though the Nine Fires Dragon Lord possessed the required skills and profound alchemy attainments, due to his identity as a Demon Cultivator, he was still subjected to disdain despite having a dragon bloodline. Ostracized year-round, he had always been a peripheral figure within the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

"If I can gain the trust of the Vermillion Bird artifact spirit and master the Nanming Fire Furnace, I will be able to catalyze the Nanming Vermillion Bird Fire."

"This fire is a divine fire, and also a stellar fire, with extensive uses. It ranks eleventh on the Fire Seed Rankings and possesses extreme value."

Although the Nine Fires Dragon Lord could control nine types of flames simultaneously, none of them were precious Fire Seeds comparable to the Nanming Vermillion Bird Fire.

The more this Demon Cultivator thought about it, the more excited he became. He couldn't help but stick out his tongue and lick his lips.
