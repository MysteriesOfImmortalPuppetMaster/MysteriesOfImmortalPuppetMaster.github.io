Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 936: I'll Take Them All On!

"What is going on with Ning Zhuo's weapon battle formation?"

"He's modifying the formation in the middle of the battle!"

"Under such pressure, he's actually still improving his battle formation?"

The spectating cultivators immediately realized this truth. For a moment, they were all moved, the shock in their hearts beyond words!

The longer Liu Jinke fought, the more unbearable it became.

His Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus was perfectly suited to him, continuously feeding energy back into his body. This single item alone could rival the sum of all of Ning Zhuo's various replenishment methods.

But no matter how strong the energy feedback was, it couldn't make up for the tilt of the battle.

Liu Jinke gradually fell into a disadvantage. Under the watchful eyes of the crowd, he was caught in a pincer attack from the front and back by the rotational slash formation, besieged from left and right. He was beaten until his skin split and his flesh tore, gradually forced into defending far more than he attacked.

"No, I absolutely cannot lose!" Liu Jinke's will to fight burned like fire. Suffering setbacks and falling into a disadvantage only made him even more unwilling to give up.

"How could I, a dignified Golden Core cultivator, lose to a mid Foundation Establishment cultivator twice in a row?"

"Especially the second time, when I possess so many powerful treasures!"

Liu Jinke almost bit his teeth to pieces, desperately holding on.

The crowd watched in stunned silence.

Before the battle began, Liu Jinke had been aggressively imposing like a fierce tiger descending a mountain, but now he had become like a stray dog fleeing in all directions, being chased and beaten all over the place by Ning Zhuo.

Especially the cultivators from the Flowing Cloud Peak factions; seeing this scene, every single one of them wore a gloomy expression, or ground their teeth in anger.

They had fully armed Liu Jinke specifically to target Ning Zhuo's various mechanical constructs and methods, yet right now, Ning Zhuo was using the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation to beat Liu Jinke into running for his life.

This greatly exceeded their expectations.

"The Splitting Metal and Stone Art can disintegrate ordinary mechanical constructs, but these Profound Weapon Armors are integrated as a whole and cannot be dismantled."

"Can mechanical arts even be used like this?"

"Ning Zhuo's Formation Dao level is difficult to estimate! Such a method requires extremely profound mastery in both mechanical arts and formation arrangement."

"Some other day, I'll also go to the Profound Armor Cave to buy some Profound Weapon Armors to try out."

As the battle continued, the radiance of the Dharma Pool at Ning Zhuo's waist gradually dimmed.

This was already the third one.

Continuously taking the Five Qi Return to Origin Pill yielded strong medicinal effects, but it also made Ning Zhuo's meridians throb with a slight swelling pain. The Cloud Tide Origin Returning Talisman had burned out, turning into ashes that drifted down from within his robes.

As for the Spirit Absorbing Casket, it finally let out a crack. Its internal mechanisms overheated and burst, flipping the casket lid open and spewing out a puff of blue smoke.

Ning Zhuo directly stored it into his Storage Belt.

The spectating cultivators' eyes lit up one after another, feeling that the battle situation had changed again.

"Ning Zhuo prepared many methods to sustain the battle, but now these methods are about to be exhausted."

"The Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation has opened my eyes, but this method consumes too much spiritual energy."

"Exactly. There is no lack of Golden Core-level Profound Weapon Armors in there, while Ning Zhuo is merely at the mid Foundation Establishment Stage. If he were a Golden Core-level cultivator, Liu Jinke wouldn't have a single shred of hope to win. But the fact is that Ning Zhuo is only at Foundation Establishment!"

"He's out of spiritual energy!"

"Liu Jinke still has the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus!"

"As long as he drags it out a little longer, Ning Zhuo probably won't be able to hold on."

Liu Jinke naturally noticed this point as well.

His spirits lifted. The Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus on his chest beat wildly once again, its golden-red light illuminating his face, making him look extremely ferocious.

He roared in his heart: "My persistence was right!"

At the same time, he shouted out loud: "Ning Zhuo, your spiritual energy is about to run out! Ultimately, I will be the one who wins this battle!"

Ning Zhuo raised his eyes to look at him. There wasn't a trace of panic in his eyes; instead, they revealed a touch of regret.

"Is it finally going to end after all?" he sighed softly.

The next moment, he completely activated his final Dharma Pool.

Five-colored brilliance fiercely poured into his qi sea.

At the same time, the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation suddenly contracted!

The saber, sword, axe, hook, fist, shield, and 'Old Cold Leg' Profound Weapon Armors all snapped into their precise positions. The inner and outer rings overlapped in an instant. The originally dispersed and supportive formation suddenly transformed into a spinning meat grinder of blades.

The axe unit fell first.

Liu Jinke forcefully blocked it with both arms. His forward momentum abruptly stopped, and his golden armor cracked open once again.

The saber armor used the aftershocks of the axe's momentum to slash diagonally, striking the edge of the crack and ruthlessly expanding the rift.

The sword unit, like a streak of cold starlight, pierced deep into the crack, immediately sending golden blood splattering.

The hook unit circled behind, snagging Liu Jinke's shoulder armor. With a fierce yank, it caused his upper body to lose its balance.

The 'Old Cold Leg' kicked the side of his knee from below. A burst of freezing energy erupted, causing his lower stance to stagnate as well.

The fist armor and shield armor crashed into him from the left and right, cutting off all avenues of retreat.

Finally, the four Golden Core Profound Weapon Armors - the saber, sword, axe, and hook - executed a rotational slash simultaneously!

The cold light was like a ring!

The shadows of the weapons were like the moon.

Liu Jinke's pupils contracted sharply. That crisis, so familiar it made his Divine Soul turn cold, struck once again.

His neck!

It was his neck again!

He wanted to retreat, but the Thousand Catty Drop inversely dragged down his footsteps.

He wanted to erupt with his golden blood, but the sword unit had pierced into his golden armor's crack, causing the circulation of his blood to stagnate for a moment.

He wanted to urge the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus, but just as his chest lit up, the freezing energy from the Old Cold Leg rushed up from his lower stance, making the transition of his qi and blood half a breath too slow.

Half a breath was already enough.

Clang, clang, clang, clang!

The four Golden Core-level profound weapons alternately slashed onto his neck.

This time, it wasn't a flaming broadsword like the Blood-Burning Gold-Breaking Slash, but rather the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation.

The saber armor cleaved open the golden armor, the sword unit locked down his blood pathways, the axe unit scattered his protective golden power, and the hook unit yanked his center of gravity askew.

The four combined their forces, their strikes stacking together, forcefully carving a cold, biting line across Liu Jinke's neck.

Pfft!

Golden blood spurted out.

Liu Jinke's head flew up once again.

The arena fell into a split-second of dead silence. Following that, an uproar erupted like a tidal wave!

"Decapitated again!"

"Ning Zhuo chopped off Liu Jinke's head again!"

"I didn't expect that this time it wouldn't be a Fire Technique, but a battle formation! A battle formation formed by Profound Weapon Armors!"

Liu Jinke's head rolled onto the ground, his eyes wide open, still bearing a look of utter disbelief.

Meanwhile, his headless body staggered backward two steps before freezing in place, completely motionless.

The Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus on his chest throbbed madly, its golden-red light flashing frantically as if out of control.

It was useless.

Liu Jinke had been defeated once again!

Ning Zhuo, however, did not press his advantage to attack. He slowly raised his hand, making the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation halt.

The Profound Weapon Armors hovered in mid-air. The cold light of the saber, sword, axe, and hook flowed, like a group of silent executioners quietly waiting.

The spectating cultivators revealed looks of confusion.

According to the challenge agreement between Ning Zhuo and Liu Jinke, this was a life-and-death battle. But why didn't Ning Zhuo deliver the killing blow?

Ning Zhuo was waiting.

Sure enough, a moment later, the golden blood that Liu Jinke had sprayed out flowed backward like a living creature. His head dissolved into a puddle of golden liquid and rapidly flowed back to the stump of his neck. The golden liquid churned, condensed, and shaped itself, finally reforming back into Liu Jinke's head.

He abruptly opened his eyes and violently stumbled backward several large steps. Holding his head with his hands, he gasped heavily for air.

"I-I'm alive again? I didn't die?!"

But this time, he felt no pleasant surprise, only a pale, extreme terror.

The first time he was decapitated, he could still console himself that he had underestimated his enemy.

The second time he was decapitated, he had absolutely no more excuses.

He had obtained the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus, the Flock-Startling Whistle, the Thousand Catty Drop, the Shattering Thunder Beads, the Fate-Severing Blade Talisman, and the Splitting Metal and Stone Art. He had obtained numerous Medicinal Pills and the secret sponsorship of the Flowing Cloud Peak factions. Yet, in this battle, his head was still chopped off by Ning Zhuo.

And this time, Ning Zhuo hadn't even used the Fire Techniques that he had dreaded so much.

Instead, he had forcibly used a battle formation to utterly crush him!

Liu Jinke stared at the Profound Weapon Armors hovering in mid-air, then looked at Ning Zhuo's calm and indifferent face, completely unable to muster any more will to fight.

"I concede," Liu Jinke's voice was hoarse.

As these three words left his mouth, it was as if all the strength had been drained from his body, and his whole person hunched over slightly.

Ning Zhuo nodded, a smile appearing on his face, his tone gentle. "If Senior Brother wants to concede, that's not unacceptable."

Liu Jinke's cheeks twitched.

Right now, he was most terrified of hearing Ning Zhuo speak so gently.

Because he knew clearly that beneath the gentleness of these scions of major clans lay cold, sharp blades.

Liu Jinke said, "This was a life-and-death duel. For you to spare my life, you must have other plans. Spit it out, what do you want from me?"

Ning Zhuo thought for a moment, then swept his gaze across the spectating cultivators. He said plainly, "Senior Brother lost this battle. You must hand over everything you gained for this fight - the techniques, talismans, and magic treasures specifically used to target me - as a closing gift for the promise of your decapitation."

A commotion immediately broke out on the sidelines. Especially the cultivators from the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak, whose expressions all changed drastically.

Liu Jinke didn't dare to look at these people. Instead, he looked up at the sky and let out a long sigh, "My skills are inferior to others. What else can I do?"

Following that, gritting his teeth, he handed over the treasures, techniques, and other items that Ning Zhuo requested, one by one.

The Flock-Startling Whistle, the Thousand Catty Drop, the Fate-Severing Blade Talisman, the unused Shattering Thunder Beads, the remaining Golden Marrow Life-Renewing Pills, Flame-Evading Blood-Protecting Pills, Iron Bone Stiffening Pills, and so on.

Finally, there was the transcribed copy of the Jade Slip for the Splitting Metal and Stone Art.

When it came to the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus, Liu Jinke hesitated for a moment. But ultimately, he began to dig open the flesh of his right chest, enduring the pain to take out this bizarre precious material, and handed it over to Ning Zhuo with trembling fingers.

No matter how good the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus was, it was Liu Jinke's path and was not suitable for Ning Zhuo.

When Ning Zhuo took the Jade Slip containing the Splitting Metal and Stone Art, an imperceptible gleam flashed in his eyes. The youth valued this more.

This technique had once caused his mechanical birds to disintegrate in large numbers, but in his hands, it would be a different story. Mechanical dismantling, mechanical corrosion resistance, improvising repairs on the battlefield, and counter-acting others' mechanical constructs - he could draw inspiration for all of these from this spell.

Ning Zhuo stored away the spoils of war one by one, finally looking at Liu Jinke. "Senior Brother, our agreement regarding the decapitation shall be considered settled today."

Liu Jinke's face was ashen. He didn't want to say anything and turned to leave the arena.

However, Ning Zhuo suddenly raised his head. His gaze bypassed Liu Jinke and fell upon the cultivators from the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak on the sidelines.

His voice was not loud, but it clearly spread throughout the martial arena. "I, Ning, have witnessed the methods you all sent over."

The sidelines instantly went completely silent.

Many people's expressions changed drastically.

A strong ominous feeling arose in Liu Jinke's heart. He stopped in his tracks, turned around, and looked at Ning Zhuo.

A faint smile rested on Ning Zhuo's face. His white clothes gently fluttered in the wind, his posture standing as tall and straight as a sword.

"The Flock-Startling Whistle disrupted my mechanical birds, the Splitting Metal and Stone Art dismantled my mechanisms, the Thousand Catty Drop suppressed my speed, the Shattering Thunder Beads broke my defenses, and the Fate-Severing Blade Talisman slashed my hanging threads."

"There were also Medicinal Pills and powerful treasures to help Senior Brother Liu Jinke sustain his combat endurance."

"Very good." He nodded lightly, his tone actually carrying a bit of sincerity.

"You've all gone to great lengths."

As these words fell, many people's hearts sank.

Ning Zhuo didn't name names. But he had implicitly called out almost every single faction that had indirectly participated in this martial drill by sponsoring Liu Jinke.

Ning Zhuo's gaze remained calm as he continued, "If there are still any other methods, you might as well continue to bring them out."

"I will take them all on!"

At this moment, his imposing aura erupted, making his white robes flutter fiercely.

The four Golden Core Profound Weapon Armors hovered behind him. The saber, sword, axe, and hook all bore battle scars, yet their sharp edges remained undiminished. The older Profound Weapon Armors like the Old Cold Leg were damaged to varying degrees - some densely covered in cracks, others with dim spiritual light - yet they still maintained their formation without scattering, like a group of battle-hardened veterans standing tall in blood.

Ning Zhuo's aura was actually not very strong; he was merely at the mid Foundation Establishment Stage.

However, he had defeated a Golden Core-level cultivator twice. Especially the second time, it was a fully armed Liu Jinke, a Liu Jinke who had fought to his heart's content in close combat and still lost.

Many people present looked at Ning Zhuo, yet an indescribable sense of oppression welled up in their hearts.

Ning Zhuo looked around, his tone changing slightly, carrying the indignation of a victim. "I originally had no enmity or grudges with any of you. I merely wanted to repay my debts."

"But since this feud has already been forged, I, Ning Zhuo, am absolutely no coward, and there is no one from top to bottom in Naming Stronghold who is afraid of trouble!"

"Flowing Cloud Peak, I am determined to charge through it!"

"And I have left this person's life for you all."

As Ning Zhuo said this, he pointed his finger at Liu Jinke.

Hearing these words, Liu Jinke sucked in a breath of cold air, utterly panicked in his heart. Why... why am I still involved in this?!

Ning Zhuo said, "Didn't you want to stop me? Didn't you want to plot against me?"

"Come on."

"Do it again!"

"No matter how you arm Senior Brother Liu Jinke, no matter how you strengthen him, I can chop off his head once again."

Ning Zhuo's voice and tone were calm, yet resolute and resounding.

The female cultivator from the Green Tea Society lowered her eyes, her fingertips trembling slightly.

The beast tamer from the Bird and Beast Manor remained silent. The canine beast at his feet whimpered lowly, having its head pressed down by him.

The cultivator from the Expand Earth Alliance looked gloomy. The mountain range patterns on his cuffs glowed faintly before quickly extinguishing.

Lightning flashed in the eyes of the disciple from the Thunder Cloud Association.

The saber cultivator from the Severing Water Saber Pavilion slowly gripped his saber hilt, but ultimately let go.

Cao Gui's eyes were slightly red, while Patch Sun and the others were so excited that their faces flushed crimson.

Situ Xing stood in the crowd, looking at Ning Zhuo, speechless for a long time.

"In this second battle, Liu Jinke still failed to force out more of Ning Zhuo's trump cards."

"At the very least, I have yet to see that Golden Core puppet."

"He is treating Liu Jinke as a chess piece, provoking almost all the major factions on Flowing Cloud Peak."

"The key is, he's not a lone wolf; he has Naming Stronghold backing him. He similarly has a Nascent Soul-level existence supporting him from behind."

"Impressive. His combat power is impressive, and his methods are even more impressive!"

"Ning Zhuo, as expected of you. As expected of the man who defeated me."

In the center of the martial arena, Ning Zhuo stored away the Profound Weapon Armors.

Liu Jinke stood rooted to the spot, glaring at Ning Zhuo, his face filled with intense grief and indignation.

He had been spared his life by Ning Zhuo, but he was still being ruthlessly utilized by him.

Of course, he wanted to break free from this vortex, but at this very moment, his heart was filled with despair.

Impossible. Those factions of Flowing Cloud Peak won't let me off.

And Ning Zhuo, that damned brat, will absolutely not let me off easily either!

Sinister. Too sinister.

These scions of major clans truly eat people without spitting out the bones. Why did I ever provoke such a person?!

In this second battle, Liu Jinke had been utterly beaten into submission by Ning Zhuo.

He had come back to life, but he no longer had any courage left. All that remained was deep terror and endless remorse.
