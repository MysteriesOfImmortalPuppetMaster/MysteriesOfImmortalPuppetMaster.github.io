Chapter 328: The Ancestor is Too Biased Towards Ning Zhuo

Ning Clan.

Council Hall.

The spiritual light lamps emitted a bright glow.

A group of Clan Elders gathered here, but the main seat, as well as the seat immediately to its left, were empty.

They were missing the Ning Clan Leader and the Young Clan Leader.

The Clan Elders looked gloomy and were in a bad mood as they began to discuss official affairs.

"The Crimson Flame Beast Horde that besieged the Lava Immortal Palace has retreated by sixty percent. In a few days, the Crimson Flame demonic beasts around Fire Persimmon Immortal City will also begin to withdraw. When that time comes, the City Lord's Mansion will likely release an announcement. Setting aside disaster relief and the extermination of wild demonic beasts, garrisoning the border outposts is where we should intervene the most."

The words of the Talisman Hall's Clan Elder immediately elicited agreement from the majority.

Four days had already passed since Ning Zhuo voluntarily yielded the position of Palace Master to Meng Kui.

In fact, on the very day Meng Kui became the Palace Master, three great Nascent Soul cultivators joined forces, and Zhu Xuanji, Shu Zhongjun, and others launched a counterattack together. The uncalculated slaughter directly snuffed out the rampant arrogance of the Crimson Flame demonic beasts.

The three great Nascent Soul cultivators were naturally Meng Kui, Meng Hu, and the cultivator from Purple Dawn Pavilion.

The Purple Dawn Pavilion cultivator used the excuse of "our disciple being mistakenly executed by the Zheng Clan" to seek an opportunity to claim the Lava Immortal Palace.

After Meng Kui became the Palace Master, he actively displayed his authority, making the Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator immediately realize that his schemes had fallen through and his plan had failed.

Looking at Meng Hu and Meng Kui, he gritted his teeth in anger: "What formidable two generals of the Meng Clan."

Meng Hu's gaze was solemn, his attention largely focused on the Ill-Fated Shield in the hands of the Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator. This Night Rain demonic weapon was truly strong; he had fought hard for a long time without seeing any hope of breaking the shield. Instead, he suffered backlash and was riddled with wounds, the power of any defensive methods severely weakened.

Meng Kui, however, smiled and indicated that he would not let the Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator make a wasted trip; he would hand over the entire Zheng Clan!

This made the Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator quite astonished. Only after asking for details did he learn - the only two Golden Core ancestors of the Zheng Clan had fallen, and core strengths like Zheng Hua, as well as junior talents like Zheng Jian, had all lost their lives.

He immediately understood the reason why Meng Kui was able to hand over the Zheng Clan.

Without the strength to protect themselves, the Zheng Clan was nothing more than a chess piece that could be sacrificed at will!

After understanding the details, the Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator's attitude softened: "I need the corpses of the Zheng Clan's twin Golden Cores."

Meng Kui nodded, saying meaningfully: "Zheng Danlian can be given to you, but Zheng Shuanggou has already been killed by a Demonic Cultivator, and his corpse's whereabouts are unknown."

The Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator replied: "The corpse of just Zheng Danlian alone is not enough!"

Meng Kui sneered: "There's no rush. Once we kill those two Demonic Cultivators and snatch back Zheng Shuanggou's corpse, we can also satisfy your needs."

The ones who killed Zheng Shuanggou were precisely the two Golden Core cultivators of Purple Dawn Pavilion, except they had disguised themselves as Black Shadow Demonic Cultivators.

The Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator's momentum stalled, and he could only take a step back, his attitude softening further as he negotiated with Meng Kui via voice transmission.

He indeed needed the Zheng Clan captives to take back to his Sect to complete his mission. If he didn't cooperate with Meng Kui, he wouldn't be able to get Zheng Danlian's corpse. He would also be jointly expelled by the two great Nascent Soul cultivators of the Meng Clan, which would look very bad for him.

After the two had mostly reached an agreement, Zhu Xuanji also flew into the sky, representing the Southern Bean Royal Family to express sincerity in cooperation. If the Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator could help, it would be regarded as a great merit, and he would receive generous rewards from the Southern Bean Kingdom.

Just like that, the two sides that had been fiercely fighting called a truce. The Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator, Meng Kui, and Meng Hu attacked together, sweeping across the battlefield and slaughtering the Crimson Flame demonic beasts until their corpses littered the field.

The three great Nascent Souls among the Crimson Flame demonic beasts were either dead or injured; the Flame-Treading Lizard fled in a panic with a gaping hole in its head.

The Misfire Snake was imprisoned by Meng Kui.

The Demonic Ape fell into a coma.

The Crimson Flame demonic beasts suffered heavy casualties, but the beasts lacked intelligence. Provoked by death and gore into boundless ferocity, they continued to charge and kill endlessly.

The three Nascent Souls first easily reclaimed the lost territory, then counterattacked into the magma, killing all the way deep into Fire Persimmon Mountain. They slaughtered until their hands were somewhat numb before finally stopping.

During this period, another Nascent Soul-level Crimson Flame demonic beast was enraged and exchanged blows with the three Nascent Soul cultivators, retreating with heavy injuries.

Meng Hu chased it all the way, only returning resentfully when he saw the demonic beast delve deep into the boundless fire poison and earth fire.

Without the support of a demon monarch with Nascent Soul-level combat power, the Golden Core-level elite demonic beasts naturally suffered heavy losses.

However, despite the Nascent Soul cultivators attacking frequently, the Crimson Flame Beast Horde remained endless. It wasn't until the third day that the Crimson Flame demonic beasts emerging from the peak of Fire Persimmon Mountain showed a noticeable decrease.

"This slaughter was too great, offending Heaven. After returning to the palace, I must fast for seven days and seven nights and bathe in the Qi of Supreme Purity to wash away the slaughter and bloodlust." The Purple Dawn Pavilion Nascent Soul cultivator sighed and stopped attacking from then on.

Meng Kui also immediately ceased his attacks.

Only Meng Hu continued to strike out in all directions. Fighting to his heart's content, he roared at the sky, his howl stirring the winds and clouds and enveloping the entirety of Fire Persimmon Mountain.

The Crimson Flame Beast Horde was suppressed by the joint efforts of the three Nascent Soul cultivators!

The Ning Clan's Clan Elders continued to discuss numerous family matters.

Taking inventory of the losses, repairing the clan grounds' defensive formation, strictly distributing compensation funds...

Finally, they had to address a topic. And this topic was something everyone in the hall did not want to face.

"Ning Zhuo is our real threat! This time, he is going to publicly sacrifice to the Ning Clan members who died in battle. Are we really not going to stop him?" The one who brought this up was Ning Qingqiao.

He possessed the Innate Talent 'Frost Descent Without Sound' and had a Foundation Establishment cultivation base. Originally, he wasn't qualified to participate in discussions. But during the demonic beasts' siege, it wasn't just the clan leader who died in battle; Clan Elders had also lost their lives.

With vacancies in the upper echelons of the family, Ning Qingqiao was promoted as a result, becoming a new face in the Council Hall.

The Clan Elders all fell silent.

The Academy's Clan Elder sighed faintly: "The great affairs of a state are only military and sacrifices. The same is true for a family."

Ning Qingqiao immediately added: "This time, if we really let Ning Zhuo host the public sacrificial rites, the hearts of our clan's branch members will probably all turn to him. With this ebb and flow, how will we compete with the branch family in the future?"

The group of Clan Elders all frowned.

It wasn't that they lacked a view of the big picture, but rather that they were familiar with administration and very clear about the current situation: the main family and the branch family, due to their blood ties, were like a lever supported in the middle. If one side was high, the other was low.

Either the East Wind prevails over the West Wind, or the West Wind prevails over the East Wind. The suppressed side would be continuously drained of blood by the dominant side; the weak would become weaker, and the strong would become increasingly stronger.

The Battle Hall's Clan Elder threw up his hands: "Ning Zhuo has already become the head of the branch family. This matter even obtained the Ancestor's approval; it is already irreversible."

"Although he is only a minor cultivator at the mid-stage of the Qi Refining Stage, his status is high enough. There is no problem with him publicly hosting the sacrificial rites, so how can we stop him?"

The crowd fell into a moment of silence.

"Ning Zhuo, sigh... who could have predicted that such a freak would appear in our clan's branch?"

"A few months ago, I never would have imagined that our massive Ning Clan would be split in two because of a mere sixteen-year-old junior from the branch family!"

"Hmph, if it weren't for the emergence of the Lava Immortal Palace, how could it be possible?"

Seeing that the Clan Elders were only focused on the surface, Ning Qingqiao felt anxious: "Hmph, in my opinion, if we can't stop it openly, then let's employ some underhanded methods. If we make Ning Zhuo fail the sacrificial rites or make the scene look ugly, it will definitely deal a blow to his prestige, winning some advantage for our Ning Clan's main branch."

*Pfft.*

Just as Ning Qingqiao finished speaking, his expression suddenly changed drastically. He spat out a mouthful of fresh blood, falling directly from his chair and kneeling on the ground.

The Clan Elders were shocked and wanted to stand up, but were firmly suppressed in their respective seats by boundless pressure.

On the main seat of the Ning Clan leader, Ning Jiufan suddenly materialized.

He coldly swept his gaze across the hall and scolded: "We all stem from the same origins of the Ning Clan, yet you lot want to fight amongst yourselves? With such dispositions, how can the family possibly prosper!?"

"Ancestor!" the Clan Elders cried out one after another, making excuses for themselves. Some even went as far as to clear their names, stating that this was merely Ning Qingqiao's personal opinion and did not represent theirs.

Hearing these claims, Ning Qingqiao immediately spat out another mouthful of blood from sheer anger.

Ning Jiufan harshly criticized the crowd, finally looking at Ning Qingqiao and directly stripping him of his Clan Elder status, ordering him to face the wall and reflect on his mistakes for three years.

Having dealt with the matter, Ning Jiufan immediately left.

Only then did the Clan Elders regain their mobility; each one was drenched in sweat, feeling as if they had been given a new lease on life.

Looking at Ning Qingqiao, they felt contempt in their hearts; one couldn't become a Clan Elder with just combat power alone, one also needed brains.

Yet they couldn't help but feel a sense of sympathy, like a fox mourning the death of a hare.

Ning Qingqiao possessed an Innate Talent, yet he ended up like this. For Clan Elders like them who had no Innate Talent, if they tried to deal with Ning Zhuo, their fate would surely be even more miserable than Ning Qingqiao's!

"I agree with Ning Zhuo hosting the sacrificial rites, there's nothing wrong with it."

"Indeed, he is the genius of our clan. It is such a great thing that an outstanding junior like him can step up and courageously take responsibility."

"This is a sign of our Ning Clan's prosperity. I think that on the day of his sacrificial rites, we should step forward to help."

"Agreed."

"I second that."

"Seconded."

Seeing the Clan Elders behave like this, Ning Qingqiao was so angry that his vision went black.

"The Ancestor is biased!" he wailed, fainting dead away on the spot.

The rest of the Clan Elders deeply agreed with Ning Qingqiao's lamentation in their hearts, but they all acted as if they hadn't heard his words at all.

Ning Jiufan sighed faintly.

Although he had left, his divine sense still shrouded the Council Hall.

If the Clan Elders of the Ning Clan's main branch knew the truth about the Lava Immortal Palace, they would certainly deeply understand Ning Jiufan.

The combat power that Ning Zhuo displayed at the end left Ning Jiufan utterly shocked!

He was able to frontally repel Zhu Xuanji using Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin.

He had the Milk Butterfly Maiden who could deploy a quasi-Golden Core-level Formation.

Heavy Armored Blood Ape - Dasheng, Netherworld Envoy - Qi Bai, and Weather Balloon - Ice Crystal Snow.

With these mechanical constructs by his side, who would dare to treat Ning Zhuo merely as a minor cultivator at the mid-stage of the Qi Refining Stage?

Such formidable combat power had already surpassed him, Ning Jiufan, alright!

And the tactics Ning Zhuo displayed when facing Zhu Xuanji and Meng Kui made Ning Jiufan look at him in a new light, forcing him to completely acknowledge him.

He felt remorse in his heart for not discovering Ning Zhuo's excellence earlier, having tried to win him over too late.

He also secretly rejoiced that Ning Zhuo had voluntarily split off into the branch family, not staying behind to wreak havoc on the main branch. Given his Righteous Path tactics, stratagems, and terrifying combat power, who in the main branch could possibly be his opponent?

Didn't everyone see that both the leader and the young leader of the Ning Clan had died right in front of him?

Ning Jiufan's public support for Ning Zhuo, throwing his full weight behind him, was actually to protect the Ning Clan's main branch. After all, he had once promised to safeguard these descendants.

Ning Jiufan's punishment of Ning Qingqiao was also out of hope that the latter would completely grasp reality, cultivate properly during his three years of facing the wall, and strive to use his Innate Talent to provide new momentum for the Ning Clan's main branch.

The current Ning Qingqiao, having suddenly obtained the status of a Clan Elder, was too full of himself. If he truly provoked Ning Zhuo into making a move, his life would be hanging by a thread!

Unfortunately, Ning Zhuo, Zhu Xuanji, and Meng Kui had already agreed to keep the final truth of the Lava Immortal Palace struggle a strict secret. Although Ning Jiufan was informed of it after the fact, he could not leak it out.

The crowd did not know the truth and found it difficult to understand his well-intentioned efforts.
