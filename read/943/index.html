Chapter 943: Golden Blood Pill Bead

Ye Qingming gently pushed over a cup of tea.

The tea soup was clear, but three golden tea leaves rested at the bottom. The edges of the leaves were sharp as blades, yet the veins were as fine as clouds.

"Golden Bud Dao-Comprehension Tea," she said softly. "One of the secret treasures of the Green Tea Society! This tea does not increase spiritual energy, but it can clear the mind and boost comprehension by more than tenfold. It prevents you from fainting amidst extreme pain while making it easier to touch the underlying laws deep within your Innate Talent."

Finally, Jin Mantang took out a jade box.

When the jade box was opened, a dim golden-red bead was unexpectedly floating inside.

Liu Jinke took just one look, and the golden blood throughout his body involuntarily trembled.

He exclaimed in astonishment, "Could this be?!"

The bead seemed to be calling out to him.

Jin Mantang put away his smile, revealing a rare look of seriousness. "This is a Golden Blood Pill Bead left behind by a Senior with the Golden Liquid Returned Pill Physique after they passed away in meditation. To obtain it, our Fusheng Society used three hidden channels, spent 230,000 Spirit Stones, and owed two massive favors."

Liu Jinke stared fixedly at the Golden Blood Pill Bead.

It shared an aura of the exact same origin.

It felt closer to him than the Nine-Aperture Blood Gold Fetus, resembling his future more than any Metal Element treasure!

The old woman with the golden hairpin spoke slowly, "On the path to a Quasi-Divine Art, relying on treasures alone is not enough. You must also comprehend."

Qiu Lei took over the conversation, "Therefore, we have modified a Formation for you."

The golden platform in the center of the cloud cavern vibrated.

The surrounding Cloud Walls peeled off layer by layer, revealing the Grand Formation hidden behind the clouds. The formation patterns were not flat but suspended in three dimensions. Three hundred and sixty golden formation nails hovered in all directions, and twelve lifespan candles stood at the corners of the formation. The lifespan candles were completely pale white, but the Candlewick was pure crimson-gold. Even before being lit, a heart-palpitating aura of time was already permeating the air.

Wen Suzhen said in a low voice, "This formation is called the Lifespan-Burning Comprehension-Ascending Formation."

Liu Jinke's expression changed. "Burning lifespan?"

Qiu Lei remained expressionless. "If you want a Quasi-Divine Art, how could there be no price to pay? Once the formation activates, it will burn your lifespan and elevate your comprehension. You are originally a Golden Core, so you still have lifespan to spare. Sacrificing thirty years for the prototype of a Quasi-Divine Art is a very good deal."

Liu Jinke's eyelids twitched wildly.

Thirty years of lifespan!

Just as he was about to speak, Lei Wangyue said coldly, "You are afraid of Ning Zhuo, afraid of losing your lifespan, yet you still want a Quasi-Divine Art. Where in the world is there such a cheap bargain?"

Liu Jinke's expression was incredibly ugly.

Qiu Lei delivered the final blow, "Don't forget, you've already signed the contract."

Liu Jinke remained silent for a long time before finally gritting his teeth and walking into the formation.

He was afraid of death, and he was terrified of Ning Zhuo.

But without a Quasi-Divine Art, he would undoubtedly lose the third battle. And if he lost to Ning Zhuo a third time, even if his life was spared again, his Dao Heart would probably be completely crippled!

"Everything for the future!" Liu Jinke clenched his jaw.

The Formation activated.

The twelve lifespan candles ignited simultaneously.

There were no flames soaring into the sky, only twelve tiny points of crimson-gold candlelight standing quietly at the corners of the formation.

The moment the candles lit up, Liu Jinke let out a muffled groan. He felt as if thirty years of time within his body had been sliced off by an invisible blade, turning into boiling grease that poured into the Grand Formation.

Boom!

The Earth Lung Hidden Gold Sand beneath the golden platform suddenly boiled. The dark golden sandy soil turned into thin streams, crawling up Liu Jinke's legs along the formation patterns.

Wen Suzhen spread her sleeves, and three hundred and sixty-five life-sustaining golden needles flew out simultaneously. The gleam of the needles fell like rain, sinking into the blood apertures all over Liu Jinke's body. With every needle that entered his body, Liu Jinke felt as if a blood aperture had been fastened by a golden lock. In the end, the golden blood throughout his entire body had nowhere to vent and could only surge madly within him.

Lei Wangyue flicked his finger, and Purple-Gold Lightning Marrow transformed into a streak of lightning liquid, violently pouring into the crown of Liu Jinke's head.

"Ah-" Liu Jinke threw his head back and screamed miserably, his expression becoming incomparably ferocious.

The lightning marrow entered his blood, acting like countless lightning blades hacking within his blood vessels. The golden blood was chopped apart, then locked back by the life-sustaining golden needles; it was pulverized into mist, then drawn by the Great White Pill Essence Gold outside the formation, re-condensing into fine pill patterns.

The old woman with the golden hairpin waited for a moment. Seeing that the timing was right, she threw the Great White Pill Essence Gold directly into the formation.

The pale golden stone floated in front of Liu Jinke's chest and slowly melted, turning into rings of platinum light patterns that merged into his flesh and blood.

Beneath the skin on Liu Jinke's chest, a tiny pill phantom gradually surfaced.

That was not the Nine-Aperture Blood Gold Fetus, but the very first Golden Blood Pill Bead condensed from his own golden blood.

Of course, it had not fully formed yet; it was merely an illusory pill.

Ye Qingming lifted the Golden Bud Dao-Comprehension Tea. The tea soup transformed into a wisp of Pure Light and landed on the space between Liu Jinke's eyebrows. Liu Jinke's consciousness, which had been almost knocked out by the pain, abruptly became clear.

Liu Jinke bared his teeth. This sense of clarity actually made him feel double the agony!

He could clearly feel every drop of golden blood in his body being locked, struck by lightning, transformed into a pill, and reshaped.

He could feel the extreme emptiness seeping out from the marrow of his bones as his lifespan violently burned away.

He also felt that after the Golden Blood Pill Bead left by the previous generation's Golden Liquid Returned Pill Physique merged into his body, it transformed into an ancient, weathered, and homologous golden-red torrent.

In that instant, Liu Jinke seemed to see another person.

That person was similarly covered in golden blood, similarly survived being decapitated, and similarly struck by countless weapons, yet remained standing tall in a pool of blood without falling. Finally, when that person passed away in meditation, the golden blood across their entire body gathered into a bead, leaving behind a sliver of comprehension bordering on a Divine Art.

Blood can act as the source.

The source can condense into a pill.

The pill can return to the body.

The golden liquid flows and circulates endlessly; this is the Returned Pill!

Liu Jinke's body and mind underwent a severe tremor.

He finally saw the hidden path in the deepest part of his Innate Talent, one that had always been obscured by flesh and blood.

"So the Golden Liquid Returned Pill Physique isn't just about having abundant golden blood, isn't just about the golden transformation of the Physical Body, and isn't just about reconnecting a severed head."

"The true 'Returned Pill' is refining the golden blood of the entire body into a self-circulating Dao of Alchemy."

"Every Golden Blood Pill Bead is a temporary Golden Core."

When the pill beads scatter, they become a greatly nourishing medicine. They can repair fractures in the Physical Body, reconnect severed meridians, replenish depleted spiritual energy, and gently nourish the divine sense blended within the golden blood.

Every condensed pill bead acts as a life-sustaining core. It can replace the heart to pump golden blood, and after losing limbs or being decapitated, it can pull the Physical Body back together. If the body parts are separated too far, the pill bead can even actively transform into bones and blood, growing into skin and flesh!

The golden light within the formation grew brighter and brighter.

Forcing himself to endure the pain, Liu Jinke finally and completely condensed the first Golden Blood Pill Bead in his chest!

It was only the size of a fingertip but shone with a brilliant crimson-gold light. Pill patterns vaguely circulated on its surface, like a tiny golden sun sunken deep within his flesh and blood.

Immediately after, a second point of golden light lit up in his abdomen.

The second Golden Blood Pill Bead condensed much slower, but it was far more steady, resembling a golden star rising from the depths of the Earth Lung.

The third one condensed right beneath his throat.

It was the smallest, but also the sharpest. Its pill light flickered on and off, like a golden blade held within his blood.

The three Golden Blood Pill Beads connected with one another, forming a golden blood circulation cycle.

Liu Jinke's miserable screams gradually lowered, replaced by heavy panting.

Streaks of blood patterns cracked open on the surface of his skin, but inside the blood patterns wasn't fresh blood, but crimson-gold light.

The light flowed like liquid pill essence returning to a furnace.

The deficits, hidden injuries, and the shadowy fear in his heart left behind from his two battles against Ning Zhuo were all forcefully washed away at this moment.

His aura began to skyrocket!

Although he hadn't truly broken through to the next realm and reached Nascent Soul, his aura had visibly and forcefully shot up by a large margin.

"Ahhhh..." Liu Jinke roared at the sky as waves of qi surged out from his body in all directions.

Instantly, the four walls of the cloud cavern vibrated, the lightning nails chimed in unison, and the golden sand churned!

The Cultivators watched Liu Jinke in the formation with varying expressions.

Lei Wangyue's eyes held a look of satisfaction.

The old woman with the golden hairpin, however, felt a sharp pang of financial loss.

Wen Suzhen frowned, clearly estimating how much longer this Physical Body could endure.

Jin Mantang muttered softly, "This time, we really bled our capital dry."

Ye Qingming looked at Liu Jinke and said softly, "I hope he doesn't lose again."

These words were very light, but they made everyone's hearts sink at the same time.

"Ning Zhuo..." In the minds of all the Cultivators present, the figure of the big-headed youth emerged.

Even though the other party was merely in the mid Foundation Establishment Stage, all these Golden Core cultivators felt a heavy sense of oppression in their hearts.

The formation's light gradually dissipated.

Three of the twelve lifespan candles had completely burned out, and the remaining nine had also shortened by a margin.

Liu Jinke knelt on the golden platform, his head hanging low, not moving for a long while.

His back was drenched in sweat, and he was dripping with blood.

After an extreme state of exhaustion, a brand new strength surged from the deepest part of his body, and soon his entire body responded.

He slightly raised his head, the corners of his mouth twitching as he smiled.

First came a low chuckle.

Then, a hearty laugh.

His laughter echoed through the cloud cavern, shaking the golden sand until it rustled and danced.

He fully raised his head. His eyes no longer simply shone with golden light; instead, deep within each eye, a pill phantom was spinning.

At the same time, the three largest Golden Blood Pill Beads at his chest, abdomen, and beneath his throat lit up simultaneously. The pill light shined through his flesh like three little suns hidden inside his body.

He reached out his right hand.

His palm split open, and golden blood gushed out, condensing into a tiny pill bead.

Immediately, the pill bead dispersed, transforming into a golden medicinal mist that fed back into his palm.

That hand, which had previously been struck black and cracked by the lightning marrow, recovered at a visible rate. New flesh and skin formed, and the golden light retracted inward.

Watching this scene, Liu Jinke's breathing grew heavier and heavier.

Powerful.

Unprecedentedly powerful!

He felt that his golden blood was no longer just blood, but rather endless, surging golden rivers. The three largest Golden Blood Pill Beads were three floodgates - able to store, release, scatter, and condense.

As long as the pill beads remained uncrushed, he could continuously recover.

As long as his golden blood did not run dry, he could fight endlessly.

"And this is just the beginning. I can condense even more Golden Blood Pill Beads and store them in my body!"

What else could Ning Zhuo do?

Decapitate him?

He now had a Golden Blood Pill Bead right beneath his throat. The moment his head was severed, the pill bead could disperse into a highly nourishing golden mist, stabilizing both his head and torso.

Drain his spiritual energy?

The two main pill beads in his chest and abdomen rotated, giving him an endurance that far surpassed the Nine-Aperture Blood Gold Fetus.

Mechanical Bird Swarm?

Although his Gold and Stone Splitting Spell was stolen by Ning Zhuo, his Quasi-Divine Art was complete. Its power would skyrocket drastically, its upper limits so high that even Liu Jinke found it hard to predict.

Liu Jinke stood up.

His Physical Body was unprecedentedly powerful, but the fear in his heart hadn't completely dissipated.

Ning Zhuo's two saber strikes had already been deeply etched into his mind.

But right now, the fear was suppressed by an even more surging sense of power. It was like a shadow sunk at the bottom of the water, forced to cower in the most obscure corner under the illumination of a crimson-gold sun.

"Ning Zhuo..." Liu Jinke muttered the name under his breath.

This time, his voice finally lacked that trembling edge.

It held greed, resentment, and a rekindled will to fight!

He looked up at the crowd and grinned, a golden light seeming to flow between his teeth.

"In the third battle, I will definitely win!"

Lei Wangyue laughed loudly, "Good!"

The old woman with the golden hairpin leaned on her cane and stood up. "Remember, you are not simply alone. You are with us. You must force Ning Zhuo to flee in a sorry state, letting everyone see that Flowing Cloud Peak is not a place for Southern Brightness Stronghold to trample over."

Wen Suzhen reminded him, "Although the three Golden Blood Pill Beads have formed, they are still only the prototype of a Quasi-Divine Art. Do not underestimate your enemy."

Liu Jinke snorted coldly.

He clenched his fists.

Golden blood surged within his fists, bringing a sense of power akin to a roaring tsunami and crumbling mountains.

Quasi-Divine Art, Golden Liquid Returned Pill!

He couldn't imagine how, with such immense power, there was any possibility he could lose?

Thousand Stoves Peak.

Underground Black Market.

The gray-robed Cultivator saw the newcomer clearly and paused his fingertips slightly. "You've arrived."

In recent days, Sun Lingtong hadn't bothered to attempt any Rising Cloud Minor Trials. Instead, he had visited here multiple times to discuss the procurement of demonic souls with the gray-robed Cultivator, making both parties much more familiar with each other than before.

After chatting for a few sentences, Sun Lingtong once again displayed his generosity. The immense number of demonic souls he wanted to purchase left the gray-robed Cultivator both shocked and worried.

"Are you trying to buy souls, or are you planning a prison break?" the gray-robed Cultivator sighed.

Sun Lingtong snorted coldly. "This transaction is clear as day. Where does this talk of a prison break come from? You worry far too much."

The gray-robed Cultivator shook his head. "Nascent Soul-level demonic souls aren't wild wandering ghosts. Every single one here has a substantial background. Even if their soul bodies have been ground down and damaged, they are still figures with a name and reputation. Asking for so many in one breath - are you trying to refine some grand Demonic Art, or are you trying to rescue someone?"

"You've been here quite a few times, elder, but I can only sell you one Nascent Soul-level demonic soul."

Sun Lingtong said, "Hmm?"

The gray-robed Cultivator continued, "But there's no limit on the number of Golden Core-level demonic souls."

Sun Lingtong snorted, "You don't have that many Golden Core-level demonic souls in your hands either."

The gray-robed Cultivator handed over a Jade Slip. "Bear with me, elder. I'm just here to do business. If anything goes wrong, wouldn't I be courting death?"

"If several Nascent Soul-level Demonic Cultivators appear in the hands of a single person at the same time, that news alone would prompt the Demon Subjugation Hall to immediately track me down."

Sun Lingtong wasn't willing to give up just like that. "I can increase the price."

The gray-robed Cultivator shook his head continuously. "No matter how much you add, it's a no."

Sun Lingtong sighed. "Isn't the black market business all about the bold stuffing themselves full? With such little courage, when will you ever strike it rich?"

The gray-robed Cultivator sighed deeply, showing unprecedented persistence. "The bold ones are already locked up in the Cloud Prison."

Sun Lingtong was just about to speak when another Cultivator walked over.

He wore a black cloak, was of short stature, and had a silver half-mask on his face. His exposed lips were incredibly thin, like paper cut out by a single stroke of a blade.

"Is the Seven Fiends Blade Demon still available?"

The gray-robed Cultivator frowned. "Mr. Shadow Ledger, you're a little late. Whether this Nascent Soul demonic soul is available or not depends on this elder."

The Cultivator in the black cloak with the silver mask looked at Sun Lingtong and smiled faintly. "Fellow Daoist has also taken a liking to the Seven Fiends Blade Demon?"

Sun Lingtong's heart skipped a beat. "Mr. Shadow Ledger. It's him..."

Sun Lingtong knew a bit about this person; he was a merchant in the black market who specialized in selling intelligence.

The Commerce Hall sold vast amounts of information, but all of it consisted of intelligence that could be traded openly. Things like inside information regarding the Myriad Manifestations Sect, power struggles, hidden past secrets, and so on, were absolutely unattainable from the Commerce Hall. However, with Mr. Shadow Ledger, it was possible.

Thinking of something, Sun Lingtong gave a light chuckle. "I did take somewhat of a liking to it, but if you want it that badly, letting you have it isn't completely out of the question."

Mr. Shadow Ledger understood immediately. "What do you want in exchange?"
