Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 924: Decapitation!

Ning Zhuo first took out a Medicinal Pill and ate it in public.

Fire Element - Flame Washing Golden Filth!

His divine sense mobilized and his spiritual energy infused. Most importantly, in his Fire Element Heart Temple, the Fire God statue abruptly opened its eyes.

On the surface, Ning Zhuo took out the Five Elements Creation and Destruction Fan and gently fanned himself.

Under the Five Elements Creation and Destruction Spell, his Five Elements spiritual energy rapidly transformed into the pure Fire Element, and the scale of his spiritual energy instantly expanded by more than six times.

The next moment, his spiritual energy plummeted, consumed in massive quantities, and transformed into a burst of flame.

The flame was blazing and pure. Born from within Ning Zhuo's body, it quickly surrounded his entire form. Wherever it passed, the impurities in his meridians were burned to nothing.

The flames permeated out of his body. The orange-red flames climbed onto the golden light collar and the green-white jade ring.

At a speed visible to the naked eye, the golden "rust" on the golden light collar quickly melted away.

The gold paint on the green-white jade ring was also washed away by the flames.

The orange-red flames spread downward, quickly covering the entirety of the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot. A large number of components that had previously turned to gold were now baptized, restored to their original appearance.

Liu Jinke: ??!!

Seeing this scene, he was so shocked that his eyes nearly popped out of their sockets.

What was going on?!

A Fire Element purification Spell had been executed by Ning Zhuo? And it perfectly countered his Gold-Turning Technique!

Ning Zhuo thus turned danger into safety.

Cao Gui let out a long breath of relief.

Being of low status and holding little weight, he didn't dare to make a commotion, only daring to rejoice in his heart.

Sharing his mood were Jiang Xiaobian, Patch Sun, Zhang Dadan, A'Huo, Lu Zeng, Che Zhuzi, Gu Huaijiu, and others.

"Good!" At this time, however, someone voiced the thoughts of the crowd.

For a moment, it drew sidelong glances.

It was Pi Fujie!

Pi Fujie's eyes shone brightly. Seeing Ning Zhuo unleash this Fire Element Technique, he felt quite pleasantly surprised, while simultaneously confirming that his action of turning an enemy into a friend - even at the cost of his own face - was correct.

The more beautifully Ning Zhuo fought now, the more it proved his own brilliance, and he would be able to give an explanation to his family.

Therefore, Pi Fujie's joy came straight from the heart!

Shen Xi, Kong Ran, and the others saw Pi Fujie acting like this, and their faces carried a hint of eccentricity.

Shen Xi, restrained by his status as a scion of a major clan, had to maintain his reserve, while Kong Ran had the style of a Confucian cultivator and would not express his stance easily. As a result, Pi Fujie, someone with a distant relationship to Ning Zhuo, was actually supporting him the most enthusiastically from the sidelines.

Ning Zhuo used the Fire Element Technique to wash his surroundings, and then controlled the orange-red flames to spread across the battlefield.

Wherever the flames went, the Metal Element was directly burned away and faded. The gold-brick ground, golden branches, and golden leaves were all restored to their original states.

Liu Jinke's eyes twitched, and he couldn't help but question, "What kind of Spell is this?"

Ning Zhuo replied, "Easy to say, this is Flame Washing Golden Filth. Even I did not expect that this obscure Spell I painstakingly learned would help me so much at this moment in my life!"

This, of course, was just rhetoric.

How could Ning Zhuo not know to use Mechanical flying birds to wear him down?

He had collected Liu Jinke's intelligence in advance and deliberately let his defensive magic treasure get hit by the Gold-Turning Technique in the early stages of the battle, purely to set up this countermeasure.

Ning Zhuo possessed an extremely profound Five Elements realm, which was his greatest reliance. Not only could it be used to design Fire Element Techniques, but it could also be used to comprehend Liu Jinke's Metal Element methods.

But unlike before, Liu Jinke was casting techniques through his Innate Talent; the cornerstone of his Gold-Turning Technique was his Innate Talent: Golden Liquid Returned Core Physique!

Therefore, under normal circumstances, it would take Ning Zhuo a very long time to conceptualize a Fire Element Technique to counter it.

But that was only under normal circumstances.

Ning Zhuo, who possessed the Luo Shu, did not fall under normal circumstances.

A fragmented Dao Artifact was ultimately still a Dao Artifact, fundamentally an existence surpassing a Spirit Treasure!

With the help of the Luo Shu, Ning Zhuo's calculation speed was quite terrifying, and he quickly designed the Flame Washing Golden Filth Technique that perfectly countered the Gold-Turning Technique! Of course, achieving just this step was not enough.

Because this Technique was freshly created, Ning Zhuo had never used it before.

But he had the Fire Element Heart Temple. With his mind residing in the temple, once activated, his control was refined down to the smallest detail. Any Fire Element Technique, even if completely unfamiliar, could be executed correctly and rapidly.

At this moment, Ning Zhuo was a god of the Fire Element!

Executing Fire Element methods was an instinct as natural as breathing or drinking water, as if he were born with it!

All these factors stacked together meant that the moment Ning Zhuo's Flame Washing Golden Filth Technique came out, it directly cleared the obstacles and turned the tide of the battle.

The Nascent Soul cultivators outside the arena had been watching the battle. They sighed with emotion in their hearts, while some used their divine sense to communicate secretly.

"This Flame Washing Golden Filth Technique is actually the perfect bane to the Gold-Turning Technique. This kid Ning Zhuo's luck is very strong."

"If he didn't have strong luck, how could he become the master of the Naming Fire Furnace?"

"According to common sense, he is only at the mid-Foundation Establishment stage and primarily cultivates Mechanical Arts. His Fire Element Techniques shouldn't be this powerful, capable of rapidly washing away the might of the Gold-Turning Technique. What Medicinal Pill did he just eat?"

"I just caught a quick glimpse. It looked like an ordinary Spirit-Nourishing Pill? Hehe."

Speaking of this, the Nascent Soul cultivator couldn't help but chuckle softly. In his view, this was impossible! This was merely Ning Zhuo's misdirection.

Thus, he sighed, "Ning Zhuo has ample means; the establishment of Nanming Stronghold is the best proof. He uses these little tricks of disguise in battle with unparalleled proficiency!"

Little did he know, the Medicinal Pill Ning Zhuo consumed actually was a Spirit-Nourishing Pill. It was quite ordinary, used for regular healing and nourishing of the mind.

This was Ning Zhuo doing the exact opposite of expectations, acting as an outward explanation for why the Fire Element Technique he unleashed this time had such great might.

There was no other way around it. Once the deity statue in the Fire Element Heart Temple opened its eyes, it provided an extremely high amplification to Fire Element Techniques!

"He actually has a Fire Element purification Spell. My luck is a bit poor," Liu Jinke tried hard to calm the ripples in his heart. This was a very rare situation, but it was reality.

He had to accept that his Gold-Turning Technique had failed, and he could no longer use time to erode his opponent and increase his own advantage.

Ning Zhuo also secretly breathed a sigh of relief.

Surveying Liu Jinke's intelligence, the thing he cared about the most was his Gold-Turning Technique. If it couldn't be resolved, he would have had to meet offense with offense and race against time with his opponent.

That would have caused many hidden dangers and spawned a massive amount of variables.

That would not do.

Therefore, Ning Zhuo deliberately fought like this just to disable this card of his opponent's.

Now, the big-headed youth had successfully done so, and he began to shift from defense to offense.

He once again released a batch of Mechanical flying birds.

This time, they were the ones he had purchased from Qing Huangzi.

They were respectively: Neck-Spear Cranes, Joint-Embracing Pheasants, Wind-Riding Kites, Gold-Thread Caged Finches, and Sleeve-Thorn Gulls!

Liu Jinke's expression changed slightly, but he quickly returned to a sneer. "Mere Qi Refining-level Mechanical birds want to deal with me?"

He jolted his arms, and once again, ten thousand golden needles shot out from the pores all over his body, blasting toward the flock of Mechanical birds in the sky!

Ning Zhuo handled his ancient zither, strumming wildly, supplementing his divine sense to control the flock of Mechanical birds.

The flock of birds suddenly scattered.

The Neck-Spear Cranes first climbed upwards, and then rapidly dive-bombed downwards.

During their dive, they continued to flap wildly. Every flap brought a strong thrust, making their speed faster and faster.

The air itself was pierced through, erupting with sharp whistling sounds.

Liu Jinke redirected the spray of his golden needles, sweeping them across the crane flock.

Ning Zhuo's zither music became melodious. The Neck-Spear Cranes adjusted their direction, and their diving path changed from a straight line to a curve, avoiding the golden needles to the greatest extent possible.

The golden cascade swept past, often only striking the air or grazing the edges of the cranes' wings, sparking a few embers, but failing to land precise hits.

However, as the distance between the two sides closed, Liu Jinke's hit rate skyrocketed. Even though Ning Zhuo pushed his control to the absolute limit, it couldn't be avoided - a large number of Neck-Spear Cranes were hit. Their riddled bodies either disintegrated mid-air on the spot or crashed powerlessly to the ground, falling apart.

Meanwhile, the necks of the remaining dozen or so Neck-Spear Cranes violently contracted, and then - suddenly catapulted out!

Over a dozen slender, black-and-white crane necks shot forth like spears, accompanied by sharp bird screeches. During their flight, the spiral patterns and runes on the surface of the crane necks drove the neck-spears to spin at high speeds, generating streaks of azure, swirling air currents that made their projectile speed even faster and gave them more penetrating power.

Liu Jinke spat out a mouthful of golden blood.

The golden blood transformed into a golden shield in front of his body.

Clang, clang...

The crane neck-spears stabbed into the golden shield, emitting crisp sounds as dense as rain hitting plantain leaves!

The golden shield blocked the crane neck-spears. It only continuously retreated due to the impact force, but the shield's surface remained completely intact without a single crack.

Ning Zhuo sighed in his heart, knowing the reason - the grade of the Neck-Spear Cranes was ultimately too low. If they were at the Golden Core level, he guaranteed that Liu Jinke wouldn't be able to handle it.

If it were Situ Xing, he would definitely be in a completely wretched state. But Liu Jinke was, after all, at the Golden Core level, and the golden shield relied on his Innate Talent, making it quite extraordinary.

"If I execute a matching Mechanical Art, I can also greatly increase the power of this attack," Ning Zhuo thought to himself.

It wasn't that he didn't have such a Mechanical Art; the inheritance of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Machine Cylinder] was quite mature and comprehensive, but Ning Zhuo couldn't execute it.

Mechanical Arts were different from Five Elements Techniques. For Ning Zhuo to master the former, he absolutely had to go through a period of bitter practice.

In addition to this, the Mechanical Arts of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Machine Cylinder] were mostly expressed through musical instruments. This added another layer of translation, making it rather inconvenient for a cultivator to master.

But just at this moment, golden light suddenly burst from Ning Zhuo's body.

The golden light diffused, covering a vast area and shining onto the Neck-Spear Cranes.

More accurately, it shone onto the neck-spears.

In an instant, the offensive power of the neck-spears soared. As the subsequent strikes stabbed into the golden shield, cracks rapidly appeared on it.

Soon, the cracks grew densely.

Liu Jinke's expression abruptly changed. The golden shield was pierced through, and unable to dodge in time, his body was directly impaled by three crane neck-spears.

Liu Jinke stumbled back a step, his posture staggering slightly, but he immediately stabilized himself.

"This is a Confucian technique enhancement?" Liu Jinke revealed a look of surprise.

Even Ning Zhuo, the party involved, blanked slightly before nodding in sudden realization. "Exactly so."

Outside the arena, many informed individuals successively looked towards Bai Jiyun.

Bai Jiyun wanted to maintain his reserve, but ultimately couldn't help but wear a faint smile on his face.

Thoughts on Watching Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo Establish the Alliance at the Heaven's Treasure Pavilion!

This was the poem he had composed; he didn't expect it to be triggered at this exact moment.

"Moon-white robes with bamboo as bones, the new furnace's warm fire reflects the flowing clouds. Do not say that Foundation Establishment lacks heroic ambition, just watch as a dragon's roar rises from this place!"

"Good poem, a great poem indeed."

Kong Ran had already muttered it aloud, praising it in public.

Liu Fushu nodded repeatedly. He originally had considerable admiration for Bai Jiyun's poetic talent, and even more so at this moment.

Others were analyzing the poem.

"'Moon-white robes with bamboo as bones, the new furnace's warm fire reflects the flowing clouds'.... This is a poem written specifically for Ning Zhuo, no wonder it is so fitting."

"When a Confucian cultivator composes a poem, the more fitting it is, the more it can trigger powerful and wondrous effects."

"Literature has no fixed form, so regardless of whether it was Bai Jiyun or Ning Zhuo, both felt surprised."

"Looking at it right now, this poem can boost Ning Zhuo's offensive! The question is, does it have any other effects?"

Ning Zhuo was riding the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot. Because of the flame purification, his speed had climbed back up.

His long robes were white as snow. Accompanied by the maneuvering of the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot, they fluttered like clouds. His posture was calm, and at this moment, he didn't forget to tease Liu Jinke, "Senior Brother, your luck really is quite bad. This poem gifted by a good friend of mine just had to take effect exactly at this moment."

Liu Jinke's face was ashen as he complained in his heart, "What kind of luck do I have?"

On the surface, he forced himself to cheer up, acting as if he didn't take it to heart at all.

"Hahaha, no matter how lucky you are, it still can't overcome the gap in strength between you and me! You think some broken copper and rotten bamboo can actually hurt me? Watch closely!" He reached out his hand and directly pulled out the short crane neck-spears piercing his body.

No blood splattered out. Golden liquid sprang up at the wounds, quickly filling in the holes. The golden light faded, restoring the color of flesh and blood.

This was also an application of his Innate Talent: Golden Liquid Returned Core Physique!

Liu Jinke spread his arms wide, and a massive amount of golden blood spewed out from his body.

The golden blood condensed, transforming into a set of heavy battle armor that completely enveloped him from head to toe.

Innate Talent - Golden Armor Covering the Body!

Liu Jinke shouted explosively and stomped his feet fiercely against the ground. The stone slabs beneath his feet shattered with a boom as his entire person charged toward Ning Zhuo like a cannonball fired from a barrel.

The golden battle armor trailed golden afterimages in the air. At this moment, Liu Jinke erupted with even faster speed.

Ning Zhuo was well-prepared. His hands continuously swept across the ancient zither, the music resounding throughout the martial arena!

Countless Mechanical flying birds moved in response.

The Neck-Spear Cranes flew about in all directions, intermittently firing their neck-spears, striking the golden armor and sending sparks flying wildly.

The Joint-Embracing Pheasants burrowed out from underground, attacking the soles of Liu Jinke's feet before immediately detonating with a bang.

Higher up in the sky, the Wind-Riding Kites hovered, continuously absorbing spiritual energy and spiritual qi from the air.

The Gold-Thread Caged Finches burst open, turning into tangled silk bamboo that completely enveloped Liu Jinke within.

Liu Jinke brandished his arms, tearing the silk nets apart. But the silk nets were endless and layered; every time he tore through one layer, two more would fill its place.

In short, the various Mechanical flying birds exhausted themselves, doing their utmost to entangle him, successfully eroding Liu Jinke's offensive momentum again and again.

Liu Jinke was, after all, at the Golden Core cultivation level. His full-body defense had no weak points. Just as he was about to break out of the flock of birds, the golden light on Ning Zhuo suddenly transformed into a dragon's roar that augmented the entire flock.

The Mechanical flying birds' speed increased drastically, and their defenses skyrocketed.

The golden trim on Ning Zhuo's body was no longer visible, proving that Bai Jiyun's poem had unleashed its full might.

Liu Jinke was entangled by the flock of birds. He dashed left and right, yet resembled a trapped beast.

"Ning Zhuo has gained the upper hand."

"He's lucky, someone gifted him a poem."

"Golden Liquid Returned Core Physique... Although this Innate Talent is strong, his golden blood is limited. The longer this drags on, the more disadvantageous it is for Liu Jinke."

"This situation, this scene..." Seeing such a sight, Situ Xing in the crowd felt a deep sense of empathy!

Liu Jinke also felt like a trapped beast and resolutely used a talisman.

The talisman made him vanish from his spot. When he reappeared, Liu Jinke was already standing on the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot, only a few steps away from Ning Zhuo!

A Void Teleportation Talisman!

And for it to be positioned so precisely was incredibly rare.

The precious talisman was already consumed, and Liu Jinke had no time to feel heartache over it. He lunged directly at Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo remained unpanicked in the face of danger. In the next moment, he released the Void-Embracing Blade-Breaking Guard.

A Golden Core-level puppet!

The Guard counter-charged, tightly hugging Liu Jinke, and then jumped off the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot.

Ning Zhuo's gaze was like fire. In his Fire Element Heart Temple, the deity statue opened its eyes once more, assisting him in executing yet another brand-new flame Techique.

Fire Element - Blood-Incinerating Gold-Breaking Slash!

A massive flaming broadsword spun forward, chopping heavily down toward Liu Jinke's neck.

Boom.

The flames exploded, surging and roaring. Liu Jinke's head and body were separated, tragically decapitated!

Ning Zhuo won. Still sitting upright on the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot, he flicked his sleeve. "Thank you for testing my strength, Senior Brother. I might as well let you know: that executioner's meal was eaten quite appropriately."
