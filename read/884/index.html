Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 884: Countermeasures

Ever since Ning Zhuo received the divination and guidance from the Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin, he paid more attention to Flowing Cloud Peak.

Just as he had analyzed before, going head-to-head with Danxia Peak would definitely mean having shit for brains.

However, Danxia Peak firmly controlled the situation. Even though Ning Zhuo forcefully controlled the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, he was still in a passive position.

Sun Lingtong said, "These Righteous Path cultivators won't stop until they secure a satisfactory amount of benefits. Three days' time is definitely not enough!"

Ning Zhuo's expression was heavy. He nodded slightly, just about to say something, when his Storage Belt suddenly vibrated.

His divine sense penetrated it, and he immediately found it was the idol inside the Storage Belt.

Ning Zhuo and Zhu Fenxiang both used the idol to communicate secretly.

"Is it Zhu Fenxiang contacting me? Wait a moment, Boss." Ning Zhuo gave a heads-up, then sent his divine sense into the idol, retrieving a stream of information.

In the message, Zhu Fenxiang tried to recruit Ning Zhuo again. She indicated she could help him perform the Divine Summoning Art to amplify the might and Divine Power of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

This way, Ning Zhuo would definitely have a better performance in the fourth round of tomorrow's minor trial.

The price was that Ning Zhuo had to sign a secret contract with the Zhu Clan. If he obtained the Nanming Fire Furnace in the future, the Zhu Clan would also possess a portion of its ownership.

"But Zhu Fenxiang doesn't know that it was you, Little Zhuo, who single-handedly elevated her status. If we're really talking about her strength, at most she could only make the pink light pillar ripple. She has misjudged the true situation," Sun Lingtong said.

Ning Zhuo nodded slightly, "However, this move can be considered quite seasoned."

"On the surface, Zhu Fenxiang, Situ Xing, and I are all tied for first place. If Zhu Fenxiang secretly forms a contract with me, she turns a competitor into an ally. Not only does it reduce the number of strong foes, but it also increases her own chances of winning."

"Her mother has also been by her side this whole time and hasn't gone back."

"If she truly acquires the Nanming Fire Furnace, an artifact-level alchemy furnace like this would definitely cause her branch's status within the entire Zhu Clan to skyrocket."

Ning Zhuo already had a clear guess regarding Zhu Fenxiang's demands and interests.

Danxia Peak.

Inside a teahouse.

Wang Yu sat on the eastern side of the table, wearing a Danxia-colored Daoist robe. The alchemy furnace patterns embroidered on the hem of his robe flowed with a faint luster under the reflection of the candlelight.

Chunyangzi sat on the western side of the table. His hair was tied up with a golden crown. Within the crimson-gold gemstone embedded in the crown, flames faintly danced, reflecting the light in his eyes.

Wang Yu lifted the teapot and poured a cup for Chunyangzi first, then one for himself. The tea flowed from the spout like a thin red thread, falling into the cup and stirring up ripples. The tea's fragrance rose with the steam, diffusing between the two.

"Danxia Peak's Xiaguang Tea truly lives up to its reputation. The tea soup is like a rosy cloud, its fragrance like light; drinking it is like bathing in the morning dawn," Chunyangzi praised.

Wang Yu seized the opportunity to say, "If Palace Master Chunyang joins my peak, there will definitely be no shortage of Xiaguang Tea."

Chunyangzi picked up his teacup, gently blew on the hot steam on the surface, and took a sip, tasting it carefully. The tea left a sweet aftertaste in his mouth, its lingering charm stretching out, just like the rolling cloud sea of Danxia Peak.

"Peak Master Wang, you should know that I did not come alone." Chunyangzi set down his teacup, his gaze landing on Wang Yu's face. "I brought my entire sect this time. Thousands of disciples up and down Chunyang Palace are waiting for my news."

Chunyangzi's voice was neither rushed nor slow, enunciating every word clearly: "Therefore, this matter is of great importance and requires careful consideration."

Wang Yu naturally understood the implication behind Chunyangzi's words, but simply smiled without saying anything.

Chunyangzi continued, "Danxia Peak and the Alchemy Hall both have profound foundations and impressive strength. However, within the Myriad Manifestations Sect, it's not as if there are no factions stronger than Danxia Peak."

"So, Peak Master Wang." Chunyangzi lifted his teacup and slightly raised it toward Wang Yu in a toast. "If I choose to join Danxia Peak, what can you give me?"

Wang Yu calculated in his heart.

If Ning Zhuo, Situ Xing, Zhu Fenxiang, and the others hadn't appeared, and if the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit hadn't reacted at all during the third round of the minor trial, he would have had the best negotiating chip right now - internally guaranteeing the Nanming Fire Furnace to Chunyangzi directly!

As long as the contract was signed, whether Chunyangzi could repair the Nanming Fire Furnace in the future would no longer be his, Wang Yu's, problem.

But things were different now.

He never expected that so many people could generate goodwill with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit and show a trend of intimacy.

This proved that the Nanming Fire Furnace could still be saved.

With such value, naturally, he couldn't let it go so easily.

Wang Yu thought for a moment before speaking slowly, "Danxia Peak can provide a subordinate peak, designated as Chunyang Peak, to settle Palace Master Chunyang's subordinates. This way, Chunyang Palace can maintain its independence and legacy."

"At the same time, members of Chunyang Peak can have priority access to Danxia Peak's alchemy resources and spiritual materials compared to other factions. Any Medicinal Pills purchased from the Alchemy Hall will uniformly receive a twenty percent discount for three years."

"Daoist Friend Chunyangzi, you can take on the role of Vice Peak Master of Danxia Peak and enjoy the treatment of an elder!"

Hearing this, Chunyangzi only chuckled.

"Peak Master Wang." Chunyangzi put down his teacup, leaned forward slightly, and looked straight into Wang Yu's eyes. "My main purpose in bringing my entire sect here this time is for the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"With this furnace, it wouldn't be impossible for me to join Danxia Peak along the way. You should also know that my performance in front of the formation today already held a lot of promise."

"Perhaps the Peak Master can offer some suggestions in this regard?"

Wang Yu almost rolled his eyes.

He knew all too well and couldn't help but curse inwardly, "Promise, my ass! Your cultivation is as high as the Nascent Soul Stage; you are completely repelled by the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, so there's absolutely no need for you to even try. The person who truly carried you all the way to the fourth round is sitting right across from you."

Wang Yu obviously couldn't agree to Chunyangzi's conditions.

Next, Wang Yu attempted to propose alternative conditions to continue deep discussions with Chunyangzi.

The two went back and forth, dragging the conversation here and there, continuously burning through time.

A stick of soul-soothing incense in the censer burned down, and another was swapped in.

A pot of Xiaguang Tea was brewed, and another was swapped in.

The candlelight flickered countless times, casting their shadows on the wall - sometimes long, sometimes short, sometimes bright, sometimes dim.

Wang Yu's gaze fell on the bronze incense burner in the corner. Watching the curling smoke rise, he silently calculated the time in his heart.

He had originally wanted to talk to a few others tonight - Cangyazi, the Dragon Lord of Nine Fires, the Red-Robed Guest, and that Ning Zhuo. But evidently, Chunyangzi saw right through this and was deliberately stalling for time.

One cup of tea after another; one topic after another. Beating around the bush, neither rushed nor slow, simply not giving him a chance to get away.

"Chunyangzi is holding out for the highest bidder; it's hard to nail this down. Tomorrow at the minor trial, I can suppress him a bit to damage his confidence and use that as a breakthrough," Wang Yu thought to himself.

He was also well aware that those truly helpful for repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace were Foundation Establishment cultivators like Ning Zhuo, Zhu Fenxiang, and Situ Xing.

That Yunyouzi could also be counted among them.

"Sigh, I originally wanted to step out personally to speak with them. Tonight... let's just forget it."

Bluestone Cave Dwelling.

Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong's discussion was constantly interrupted.

From time to time, flying letters or messengers would arrive, either inviting Ning Zhuo, proposing negotiations, or even issuing threats.

"And this is under the condition where I deliberately elevated Zhu Fenxiang and Situ Xing to make their performance look on par with mine."

"If it were just me, I'm afraid the pressure right now would be at least ten times greater. There would definitely be no peace," Ning Zhuo sighed.

Sun Lingtong said, "We've waited and waited, but we haven't seen a single messenger from Danxia Peak or the Alchemy Hall."

"It seems their main focus is placed on those Nascent Soul and Golden Core cultivators. Especially Chunyangzi. He brought his entire sect with him this time to participate in the Flying Cloud Assembly."

Ning Zhuo pondered, "By falsifying results for Zhu Fenxiang and Situ Xing, I shared the pressure and lessened the covetous gaze from Danxia Peak."

"But these three days are my final opportunity. I have to go all out and take a gamble, Boss."

Sun Lingtong nodded, "Mn, Little Zhuo, just do it according to your earlier thoughts! I support you!"

Ning Zhuo then sent out a flying letter.

A moment later, Cao Gui arrived at the Bluestone Cave Dwelling with an excited heart.

Ning Zhuo had taken the initiative to contact him, expressing his desire to cooperate with the Commerce Hall: "In today's competition, many people kept pace with me. Especially Zhu Fenxiang, she even faintly surpassed me."

"I have already used the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone. Tomorrow's test will inevitably be more difficult. If I want to truly win, I must bring in outside help."

"I have already obtained Lord Zhong Dao's permission to have a partial, limited cooperation with the Commerce Hall. But let me state this up front: the Commerce Hall cannot have any share in the ownership of the Nanming Fire Furnace."

Cao Gui immediately smiled bitterly, "Is there any room for discussion regarding this point?"

Ning Zhuo also smiled bitterly and spread his hands, "This isn't something you can negotiate with me. This matter requires Lord Zhong Dao's nod, do you understand?"

Cao Gui nodded repeatedly, "Understood, understood."

He paused for a moment. "Partial ownership rights is a core clause. Young Master Ning Zhuo, I cannot make the call on this matter. I'll have to send a flying letter first."

"Please." Ning Zhuo extended his hand in a gesture.

Cao Gui sent the flying letter on the spot and quickly received a response.

Cao Gui nodded, even directly handing the flying letter to Ning Zhuo to read: "My Hall has agreed. Please explain in detail, Young Master."

Ning Zhuo smiled inwardly.

He was alone and lacked power, with no way to negotiate with the Commerce Hall on equal footing. Therefore, by bringing up Lord Zhong Dao, he easily made the domineering partner proactively understand and make concessions.

Ning Zhuo's expression froze slightly, and he lowered his voice, "Since things have reached this point, I will be completely honest with you."

"I have a method to communicate with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, making it cooperate to slightly repair the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"During tomorrow's minor trial, I plan to use this cultivation method to publicly repair parts of the furnace to express my sincerity to the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit. Once the artifact spirit knows I am helping it, it will definitely become extremely close to me. It might very well allow me to snatch first place in one fell swoop!"

"If I truly obtain the Nanming Fire Furnace, I will definitely engage in deep cooperation with the Commerce Hall."

Cao Gui was stunned, revealing a look of doubt as he dragged out his tone, "To think the Young Master possessed such astonishing means?"

Ning Zhuo nodded, "I know you have questions, but listen to me explain slowly."

"First, the reason I reserved this method is because I must physically touch the furnace itself and face the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit directly. This was an environment not provided in the previous minor trials."

"Second, once I communicate with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, it will inevitably expose the roots of my Cultivation Art, which is not something I want to see."

"Third, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit is extremely terrified, deeply afraid of being harmed by others. This is my opportunity. It is also everyone else's opportunity. However, once I use this method, it's highly likely to greatly alleviate the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's state of terror, allowing more people to take advantage!"

After listening, Cao Gui showed a look of sudden realization.

Ning Zhuo continued, "I know Danxia Peak also has deep control over the Commerce Hall. Cooperating with you is, to some extent, cooperating with Danxia Peak."

"I need Danxia Peak to modify the environment of tomorrow's minor trial to provide me with the right conditions."

"If possible, I hope to be the last one to make a move."

Cao Gui cupped his fists, "This is a grave matter. Young Master, please allow me to send another flying letter."

"Please." Ning Zhuo smiled and extended his hand.

The reply quickly flew back to the Bluestone Cave Dwelling.

After reading the letter, Cao Gui revealed an excited expression, "Young Master, Danxia Peak has basically approved it all. Further development of the Formation is feasible. Because there are no restrictions in the minor trial's rules against it."

"However, your turn order cannot be changed. Because it was publicly announced at the end of the third round."

"If orders are changed overnight, I fear it would damage Danxia Peak's reputation in the Righteous Path."

Ning Zhuo gritted his teeth and thought for a moment with a hesitant expression, before finally nodding, "Fine, let it be so."

He then looked at Cao Gui, "Repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace won't be simple. I don't have any handy materials on hand, so I need you to provide them."

Cao Gui laughed heartily, "I can make the call on this point. Young Master, I can promise you this right now on behalf of the Commerce Hall!"

The two conversed for a while longer, and Ning Zhuo ordered Old Chef to see Cao Gui out.

He then picked up the idol and secretly contacted Zhu Fenxiang.

Zhu Fenxiang was extremely surprised upon receiving the message. Not daring to make a decision on her own, she hurriedly sought out her mother to discuss it.

Her mother analyzed, "Ning Zhuo has a bright future ahead of him. He is a rising star of the Righteous Path, capable of releasing such a vast pillar of righteous qi. He wouldn't stoop to deceiving us about this. What he said should all be true."

"Since that's the case, why not give it a shot? I will contact the clan right away and have them send over the corresponding precious materials!"

The next day.

At the summit of Danxia Peak.

The sea of clouds churned as if boiling. The morning glow pierced through the cracks on the horizon, dyeing the entire sea of clouds a resplendent crimson-gold.

In the ethereal eaves and open courtyard, yesterday's pink pillar of light had dissipated, replaced by a dilapidated alchemy furnace - the true body of the Nanming Fire Furnace.

The furnace body was shattered, with not even a tenth remaining. Charred black cracks covered it like a spiderweb, extending all the way from the furnace's mouth to its base. There were a few spots that had even completely snapped apart; it was simply a tragic sight to behold.

Around the alchemy furnace, Formation Masters from the Formation Hall had worked through the night to set up a Formation. The Formation runes were inscribed with spiritual liquid, flowing with a faint golden light in the morning sun.

The Formation's function was to provide protection for the alchemy furnace and the artifact spirit. At the same time, cultivators could directly touch the furnace fragments and the main furnace body to confront the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit face-to-face.

In the venue, the cultivators gathered once again.

Even further out, a massive number of cultivators crowded over, clinging to various cloud tops to watch the spectacle once more.
