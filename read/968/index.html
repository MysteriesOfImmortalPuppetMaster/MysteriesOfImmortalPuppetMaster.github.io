Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 968: My Righteous Path

A faint golden light flashed in Chun Yangzi's eyes, but he did not speak immediately.

He was also extremely vexed.

Just like Ning Zhuo, he was a newcomer who wanted to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

The Cave Manor was quiet for a moment.

The lamplight burned silently, casting somewhat gloomy expressions on both their faces.

Chun Yangzi suddenly asked, "What about Tu Yuanzi?"

Ning Zhuo remained expressionless.

Chun Yangzi said, "Of the five Nascent Souls in the Naming Stronghold, Nine Fire Dragon Lord was defeated, Red Robe Guest is dead, I have fought one round, and Tan Zhu's asking price is too high. Now, only Tu Yuanzi is left to make a move."

His tone was calm, but his meaning was very clear.

In Chun Yangzi's view, Tu Yuanzi should be a Nascent Soul sent by the faction behind Ning Zhuo to assist him.

Since they had reached the final juncture, it was time for him to take action.

Ning Zhuo shook his head.

His expression became extremely stern. "No."

Chun Yangzi looked at him. "Why?"

Ning Zhuo said, "The final match is too crucial. Tu Yuanzi is not suitable."

Chun Yangzi did not speak.

Ning Zhuo continued, "His combat strength is far inferior to Red Robe Guest's. If we send him into the arena, he will undoubtedly lose!"

Chun Yangzi looked deeply at Ning Zhuo, his gaze carrying a scrutinizing intent.

Ning Zhuo understood in his heart: Chun Yangzi suspected him of holding back his strength.

Ning Zhuo immediately stood up, meeting Chun Yangzi's gaze without flinching at all. "Senior Chun Yangzi, this is absolutely not a case of this junior intentionally preserving strength. I can swear an oath, or even sign a contract, to prove that these words are true!"

Chun Yangzi looked at him for a long time.

Finally, he slowly nodded. "There is no need."

Ning Zhuo maintained his serious expression, "Tu Yuanzi possesses a cultivated aura of mystery, but this mystery does not equate to strength. This battle concerns the ownership of the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar, the foundational establishment of Naming Stronghold, and the death of Red Robe Guest. This junior would absolutely never hide things for selfish reasons in this matter!"

Chun Yangzi's expression softened slightly.

He sighed, "Then only Tan Zhu is left."

Ning Zhuo remained silent for a breath, then nodded, "If there really is no other way, we will just have to give up some benefits."

Ning Zhuo looked at Chun Yangzi and said, "At the end of the day, it's just some debt claims. As for the position of Head Camp Master, could it be that Senior Chun Yangzi still cannot see past it?"

Chun Yangzi pondered in silence, a layer of golden light slowly flowing in the depths of his eyes.

After a long time, he said, "My concerns still lie with Tan Zhu."

Ning Zhuo: "Oh?"

Chun Yangzi: "I always feel that his schemes go far beyond this. If we seek his help, what if it's yet another trap?"

"If he deliberately asks for a high price, lures us into agreeing, and then in the fifth match... is defeated, or admits defeat..."

The atmosphere in the Cave Manor suddenly turned cold.

After a moment of silence, Ning Zhuo's expression was solemn as he said in a deep voice, "Although Red Robe Guest is dead, his Soul remains in this world."

"As long as his Soul is still there, I will find a way to rescue him."

Chun Yangzi frowned slightly. "You want to save Red Robe Guest? You should understand: his Physical Body and Nascent Soul have already perished; only his Soul is left. Even if we ransom him back, he won't be of any assistance to our Naming Stronghold."

Ning Zhuo nodded. "Of course I understand. But I collected Senior Red Robe Guest's corpse. His body, along with the many treasures on it, have been left behind. I will strictly safeguard them. When Senior Red Robe Guest's Soul is rescued in the future, they will all be returned to him."

"Besides that, while he was alive, he lent me a Fire Seed. This Fire Seed, I also must return to him!"

Red Robe Guest was killed by the Heaven-Draping Single Line Hook, and his Soul was hooked away, but his corpse and belongings were preserved. It wasn't that White Cloud Village and the Territory Expansion Alliance didn't want to take them, but rather that the contract for the Five-Victory Martial Contest had long stipulated it.

The Five-Victory Martial Contest did not forbid life and death, but it forbade the plundering of corpses and remains.

If a participating Cultivator died, their corpse and accompanying treasures were to be collected by their own side. If a magic treasure was shattered, refined, or seized control of on the spot during the spell battle, that was a matter of victory and defeat in combat, and treated differently. But looting a corpse, stealing treasures, or cutting away Nascent Soul relics post-battle was considered a rule violation.

This was also one of the prerequisites for all three parties to accept "not forbidding life and death."

Primarily, the Territory Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village wanted to dispel the concerns of their backers, encouraging them to boldly lend out magic treasures and the like.

Ning Zhuo sighed deeply. "Even though Senior Red Robe Guest is a Demonic Cultivator, he was ultimately a member of our Naming Stronghold."

Chun Yangzi's face had already darkened.

Ning Zhuo raised his head, his gaze looking straight at Chun Yangzi. "Senior, as the Head Camp Master, you should support me, right?"

Chun Yangzi coldly snorted in his heart.

He and Red Robe Guest had a personal grudge. Red Robe Guest's defeat and death in battle wasn't entirely a bad thing for him.

Ning Zhuo's words contained hidden barbs; he was using the title of Head Camp Master to pressure Chun Yangzi.

Chun Yangzi naturally understood. He had led the Chunyang Palace for who knows how many years; within the entire Naming Stronghold, his political acumen was absolutely second to none.

Chun Yangzi chuckled first. "If we agree to Tan Zhu's demands and invite him to make a move, then he should be the Head Camp Master, not me."

Pausing, Chun Yangzi continued, "Under the watchful eyes of the public, you, Ning Zhuo, personally entered the arena, collected his corpse, and preserved his dignity. Going this far is already enough."

"Red Robe Guest is responsible for his own death. He did not fight entirely for Naming Stronghold; he had his own beneficial calculations."

"War is perilous, and spell battles carry the risk of death. The contract does not forbid life and death; he was very clear about this before taking the stage."

Ning Zhuo slowly shook his head. "Everything you said, Senior, is correct."

"Red Robe Guest indeed had selfish motives, and he indeed fought for profit. But Naming Stronghold itself was gathered due to mutual interests; we all inherently have selfish motives."

"Regardless of his motives, Senior Red Robe Guest fought for Naming Stronghold, and Naming Stronghold was established under my singular advocacy. Reasoning it this way - he died because of me."

"Because of you?" Chun Yangzi's brow furrowed tighter. "Ning Zhuo, you think too highly of yourself."

Facing a Nascent Soul powerhouse at close range, Ning Zhuo felt no pressure at all. "Naming Stronghold is newly established; what is its foundation? It is not a sect gate, nor a spirit vein, nor years of inheritance. It is debt, it is promises, it is the Naming Fire Furnace, and it is the various Nascent Souls being willing to believe that I, Ning Zhuo, will at least acknowledge my debts."

"Today Red Robe Guest died in battle. If I immediately turn around and divide up his corpse, treasures, dharma robes, and furnace vessels to cover battle losses, what will the others think?"

"What will Nine Fire Dragon Lord think?"

"What will Tao Liweng think?"

"What will those Golden Core and Foundation Establishment creditors think?"

Chun Yangzi said coldly, "When a person dies, their debts are extinguished; this is an inherent truth of heaven and earth. Ning Zhuo, you are a smart person. You should know that if you truly do this, it is tantamount to handing leverage over to our enemies."

Ning Zhuo sighed deeply. "I know."

Chun Yangzi snorted coldly. "You don't know!"

His voice suddenly deepened. "You are the new master of the Naming Fire Furnace!"

"The entire Naming Stronghold was established because of the Naming Fire Furnace. If you are schemed against by our enemies, the entire grand scheme of Naming Stronghold will be overturned!"

"With Red Robe Guest's Soul falling into enemy hands, your eagerness to rescue him allows the enemy to use this to set a trap."

"The methods that can stem from this are too numerous."

Ning Zhuo remained silent for a moment. "Even so, I must still do it."

A sharp glint flashed in Chun Yangzi's eyes.

Ning Zhuo looked up, staring fixedly at Chun Yangzi. "Senior, if I only calculate pros and cons, only protect the big picture, and abandon those who die in battle, Naming Stronghold might be able to survive the fifth match, but it will not survive what comes after."

"Senior, Naming Stronghold is not Chunyang Palace. It has no ancestral tablets, no millennium-old sect rules, and no hierarchy of superior and inferior. The reason it can gather together right now relies entirely on the fact that I, Ning Zhuo, am willing to acknowledge my debts, am willing to take responsibility, and will not give up easily."

"Red Robe Guest's Soul, I will save."

"Red Robe Guest's corpse, I will protect."

"Red Robe Guest's treasures, I will also seal away."

The youth's words were resolute and resounding. "On this matter, there is no room for negotiation!"

As the last sentence fell, the Cave Manor became completely quiet.

Chun Yangzi stared at Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo also looked at Chun Yangzi.

The atmosphere was as heavy as a mountain.

After a long time, Chun Yangzi suddenly let out a laugh.

There was absolutely no warmth in his laughter.

Chun Yangzi said, "Ning Zhuo, you have a lot of courage to take responsibility."

Ning Zhuo cupped his hands. "Senior overpraises."

The two parted on bad terms.

The night had already deepened.

Ning Zhuo looked out toward the mountains.

Cloud energy slowly floated around the waists of the mountains; the scattered lights of the distant peaks looked like cold stars embedded in the night sky. The mountain wind blew past, bringing a faint, damp chill from the clouds, as well as the lingering scent of blood that had yet to dissipate from the martial arena.

Red Robe Guest was dead.

This fact pressed heavily on his heart like a block of cold iron.

If Red Robe Guest had won, everything would be easy to handle.

Naming Stronghold would have three wins and one loss, and the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar would be in their hands. Red Robe Guest would have made a great contribution, and Tan Zhu's exorbitant demands would lose the leverage to exploit their crisis. Chun Yangzi's position as Head Camp Master could also be stabilized, and Ning Zhuo could calmly deal with all parties.

Naming Stronghold was newly established, and morale was already scattered. Such a loose alliance was best suited for fighting battles with the wind at their backs.

When things go smoothly, everyone is motivated, and all issues can be suppressed.

Once adversity hits, everyone schemes, and all hidden troubles float to the surface.

The reality was that Red Robe Guest not only lost, but also died.

What was even more troublesome was that his Soul was hooked away by the Heaven-Draping Single Line Hook, falling into enemy hands.

"A Dao Artifact..." Ning Zhuo sighed deeply.

Yue Qingshan died at Tan Zhu's hands first, and then the Territory Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village killed Red Robe Guest with the Heaven-Draping Single Line Hook; one could well say - you set the precedent, and we just follow suit.

"Therefore, the root cause still lies with Tan Zhu." A cold light flashed in Ning Zhuo's eyes.

The Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar was "accidentally" excavated by Tan Zhu. Yue Qingshan was killed by Tan Zhu. All of this was moving to his rhythm.

What made Ning Zhuo feel troubled was that he knew absolutely nothing about Tan Zhu's intentions.

The other party was someone whose lifespan was about to end!

Tan Zhu had publicly declared that his impending end of lifespan could be verified under the precious bell of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, so it was definitely not false.

"A dying man asking for debt claims on the Naming Fire Furnace is clearly paving the way for someone else. Who could this person behind the scenes be?"

"Was Red Robe Guest's death also part of this calculation?"

As this thought arose, the chill in Ning Zhuo's heart deepened.

Ning Zhuo raised his hand and gently rubbed his glabella.

He wrote a flying letter and gave it to Chen San.

When Chen San entered, he still maintained a respectful posture, but his expression was clearly much more tense than usual. With the Five-Victory Martial Contest having reached this point, everyone knew that Naming Stronghold was already standing on the edge of a blade.

Ning Zhuo said, "Two things."

Chen San immediately cupped his hands. "Young Master, please instruct."

Ning Zhuo said, "First, vigorously investigate the origins of the Heaven-Draping Single Line Hook."

Chen San's eyelids twitched slightly.

Ning Zhuo continued, "It appeared in the hands of Cloud Pot Daoist, but it absolutely does not belong to Cloud Pot Daoist. Which faction on Flowing Cloud Peak lent out this artifact, whether they are just a middleman, and whether there is an even deeper mastermind behind the scenes - all of this must be investigated."

Chen San nodded solemnly. "This subordinate understands."

Ning Zhuo said, "Second, thoroughly investigate Tan Zhu!"

Chen San's heart chilled.

Ning Zhuo's gaze was calm, but his words were heavy: "Investigate his background, investigate who he came into contact with before joining Naming Stronghold, investigate whether he has hidden connections with Danxia Peak, the Commerce Hall, and Flowing Cloud Peak. Sigh... just find out as much as you can."

Chen San replied solemnly, "Yes."

Ning Zhuo provided sufficient funds, and Chen San received his orders and withdrew.

Ning Zhuo then used Life Hanging by a Thread to contact Sun Lingtong across space. "Boss, I want to investigate the origins of the Heaven-Draping Single Line Hook, and I also want to investigate Tan Zhu."

Sun Lingtong: "I'll do my best, but don't hold your breath for the results."

Ning Zhuo: "Yeah, I know. We've just entered the Myriad Manifestations Sect; our foundation is far too shallow."

Neither of these two targets was easy to investigate. One was a Dao Artifact, the other was an old demonic figure. There would definitely be people covering up their tracks.

Ning Zhuo then laid out his upcoming plans for handling Red Robe Guest's corpse, relics, and Soul.

Sun Lingtong said, "Let me give you a piece of advice: just don't bother with it. The Five-Victory Martial Contest was something they agreed upon themselves, not forbidding life and death. Red Robe Guest took the stage himself, acted ruthlessly himself, and was hooked to death himself. In the end, this was his own choice."

"Hehe, Chun Yangzi being unwilling to rescue him is a massively good thing for you, Little Zhuo."

"If even he, as the Head Camp Master, won't mount a rescue, then you, Little Zhuo, who rank last in seniority, shouldn't be worrying about it either."

At this point, Sun Lingtong subconsciously lowered his voice: "You're not the Head Camp Master. Chun Yangzi is."

"If necessary, we can leak this truth. Hehe."

"Since Chun Yangzi is unwilling to take this responsibility, let him take the blame instead."

"As for Red Robe Guest's relics, the worst that happens is we take a bit less profit."

Ning Zhuo sighed and said, "Boss, Red Robe Guest lent the Blood Ember Fire Seed to me."

Sun Lingtong: "The prerequisite for him lending you the Fire Seed was that you healed his injuries. Moreover, when he lent you the Fire Seed, he wasn't straightforward at all and even had to test you."

Ning Zhuo fell silent on his end.

Sun Lingtong: "Then let me put it another way. Let's wait and see which way the wind blows first, alright?"

"Red Robe Guest's Soul was hooked away, and it might not last long. The power of a Dao Artifact like the Heaven-Draping Single Line Hook is too overwhelming; without spiritual energy, it's completely impossible to control its retraction and deployment freely. Maybe in a few days, his Soul will just perish."

"By that time, Red Robe Guest's Soul will be extinguished, and nothing can save him. We'll inherit his final wishes and continue dealing with the Territory Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village. That is also the Righteous Path!"

Ning Zhuo shook his head: "Boss, that is indeed the Righteous Path, but it is not my Righteous Path. My mind is set!"

"Hmph, you brat, asking for trouble!" Sun Lingtong was resentful and scolded Ning Zhuo, but there wasn't much anger in his tone.

"When do you plan to announce your decision?" Sun Lingtong asked.

Ning Zhuo: "Tomorrow morning. No, the sooner, the better."

The youth's eyes flashed with a sharp light, suddenly realizing that time was of the essence.

That very night, Ning Zhuo invited Shen Xi, Lin Jinglong, Situ Xing, Mu Yuehua, several Confucian cultivators of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and others. In public, he preserved Red Robe Guest's corpse, sealed the relics on his body one by one, and openly admitted the matter of the Blood Ember Fire Seed.

Ning Zhuo solemnly announced: He would find a way to rescue Red Robe Guest's Soul, and he would return all these relics to him!

At that time, many passersby were drawn over to witness it together.

As soon as Ning Zhuo made this declaration, it immediately spread like the wind throughout the main sect gates of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, sparking widespread discussion.
