Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 845: Deduction

Three streaks of flowing light descended upon the summit of Cloud Canopy Peak.

The peak was as flat as a whetstone, housing a palace that served as the residence for the successive Sect Masters of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

The palace gates opened automatically. The three streaks of light slowed slightly, passing through the gates and entering the palace.

In the main hall, a white jade altar, nine zhang tall, stood towering and majestic. The altar was divided into nine levels, each three chi and three cun high, carved from whole blocks of Kunlun jade. The entire structure was a translucent white, emitting a warm, soft glow.

Above the altar, the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy hovered in mid-air.

Its nine-layered umbrella surface rotated slowly, with seven-colored clouds and rosy mists flowing ceaselessly across it.

Countless tiny cloud ribbons hung from the edge of the canopy, swaying with the wind like gently dancing tassels.

Eighty-one umbrella ribs spread out like fan bones. Suspended at the end of each rib was a cloud-patterned jade pendant. As the pendants struck against one another, they produced crisp, melodious chimes.

Nine main cloud ribbons hung down from the heart of the canopy. At the end of each ribbon hung a main pendant - representing Qianyuan, Kunyuan, Zhenyuan, Xunyuan, Kanyuan, Liyuan, Genyuan, Duiyuan, and Zhongyuan. When these nine pendants collided, the sound resonated for a hundred li.

The flowing light dissipated, revealing the figures of Wei Ji, Tuoba Huang, and Wang Yu.

The three fixed their gazes on the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy and immediately noticed something was wrong.

Although the nine-layered surface was still rotating slowly, it was no longer stable. It moved erratically, fast then slow, as if something were interfering with it.

The colors of the seven-colored clouds were no longer pure; a faint darkness permeated them, as if some filth had been mixed in.

The nine main cloud ribbons were pulled taut, no longer swaying with the breeze. The sound of the main pendants striking each other was rapid and shrill, carrying a sense of anxiety.

Tuoba Huang’s pupils constricted, and he blurted out, "The Cloud Canopy is sending a warning!"

Beside the altar, a figure stood with hands clasped behind his back.

He wore a green-grey Daoist robe embroidered with faint formation patterns that flowed slowly with his breathing. His hair was tied high and secured with an ebony hairpin. His face was lean, his eyebrows thick and straight, and his eyes were as deep as an abyss.

It was the current Sect Master of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, the Master of Heavy Array Peak - Dong Chen.

Dong Chen said, "The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy is one of the destiny-stabilizing Spirit Treasures of our Myriad Manifestations Sect. This warning implies that a major event concerning our entire sect is imminent."

"Subtly enough, aside from this one, none of the other destiny-stabilizing treasures in the sect have reacted."

Treasures that stabilized destiny were extremely rare because Qi Luck itself was an esoteric study. Crafting a destiny-stabilizing treasure required the use of destiny-related techniques.

In this regard, cultivation kingdoms actually had it much easier.

Because cultivation kingdoms possessed their own majestic destiny, condensing and harnessing the dragon vein of a region, using national destiny to create National Artifacts for stabilizing destiny was a natural process.

As a super sect, the Myriad Manifestations Sect did not possess National Destiny, so naturally, it could not employ national destiny. Even if they seized a destiny-stabilizing National Artifact, it would be difficult to utilize.

Although Ning Zhuo had taken the Forgetful River Prefecture Lord's Seal, he could not use it. Because it was a National Artifact, it was primarily driven by national power.

The solution for super sects was to accumulate destiny-stabilizing magic treasures, spirit treasures, and so on.

These could be driven by spiritual energy.

A super sect like the Myriad Manifestations Sect had a scale rare in the world, with a massive number of personnel. Consequently, their requirements for destiny-stabilizing treasures far exceeded those of equivalent forces.

Just as Ning Zhuo possessed the Buddha Heart Demon Seal as a destiny-stabilizing treasure, its efficacy would be diluted as his own faction expanded.

Since the Myriad Manifestations Sect had such a massive, world-rare scale, it followed that this super faction must possess a large quantity of destiny-stabilizing treasures, or perhaps one extremely powerful destiny treasure.

Wei Ji, Tuoba Huang, and Wang Yu learned from Dong Chen that the other destiny treasures had not reacted, and only the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy was showing abnormalities. At first puzzled, they soon realized a key point, and their expressions turned grave.

Wei Ji’s voice was low. "Is it that prophethic poem?"

He had spoken the answer.

There were differences in quality and specialization among destiny treasures. Among the many destiny-stabilizing Spirit Treasures of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, the task entrusted to the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy was to mask Qi Luck and delay the arrival of calamity, like sheltering one from wind and rain.

Many years ago, a mighty figure within the Myriad Manifestations Sect had deduced the sect's future and obtained a terrifying prophethic poem.

The poem described a doomsday-like future, with the Myriad Manifestations Sect being the origin of this catastrophe.

Since the prophethic poem was deduced, the Myriad Manifestations Sect had attached great importance to it, constantly trying to avoid the prophecy.

The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy bore this heavy responsibility; it had been masking the Myriad Manifestations Sect, delaying the calamity represented by this prophethic poem.

But now, it was showing strange movements.

No other destiny treasure was reacting. Clearly, the truth pointed directly to this prophethic poem!

Dong Chen said, "I have only cultivated a few destiny techniques. I summoned you urgently so we can join forces to cast a technique and explore the truth within."

"Let us begin."

Immediately, the four took up positions at the east, south, west, and north of the white jade altar and cast their techniques together.

The next moment, the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy trembled slightly.

The rotation of the nine-layered surface accelerated, and the seven-colored clouds boiled over. The eighty-one small pendants vibrated simultaneously, emitting a continuous sound of gold and jade clashing.

The nine main cloud ribbons swayed violently, and the nine main pendants at their ends gradually shone with brilliant light.

The Qianyuan Pendant was pure azure, Kunyuan pure yellow, Zhenyuan deep green, Xunyuan pale green, Kanyuan mysterious black, Liyuan crimson red, Genyuan earthy yellow, Duiyuan silvery white, and the Zhongyuan Pendant flowed with seven colors.

For a moment, colorful brilliance filled the enclosed space of the main hall.

Dong Chen and the other three closed their eyes tight, their minds drawn into a vast sea of clouds where various images rose and fell in succession, appearing endlessly.

A moment later, the four concluded their casting.

Through the clues provided by the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy, they had deduced many things.

Wang Yu remained silent.

Wei Ji looked at Dong Chen, waiting for orders.

Tuoba Huang, impatient by nature, looked furious. "Someone actually infiltrated our sect intending to draw calamity upon us? They deserve death!"

Dong Chen shook his head slightly. "In every Flying Cloud Assembly of our Myriad Manifestations Sect, there are always those with ulterior motives mixing in. But to find the key figure among them, we must continue casting techniques."

Wang Yu agreed. "Qi Luck is entangled and profoundly mysterious. Perhaps this person doesn't even know they will trigger the calamity upon our sect. It might be their unintentional actions, or a deliberate plan, that just happened to trigger this situation."

Wei Ji sighed. "This is the mystery of Qi Luck. Heaven and Earth are the chessboard, and we living beings are merely pieces."

After resting for a while, the four continued to cast techniques, intending to use the Qi Luck entanglement of the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy to drag this key figure out!

Meanwhile.

Moonlight filtered through the window lattice, falling upon Xiao Juxia, causing the ancient scroll in his hands to reflect a faint light.

Suddenly, Xiao Juxia frowned.

A strange sensation rose from the bottom of his heart, attracting his attention.

He immediately put down the scroll, closed his eyes, and performed a calculation with his fingers.

Three breaths later, he opened his eyes.

He calculated the result: His secret instruction of the Ten Thousand Methods Falling into Demon to Qin De had only made slight progress. If he lingered in the Myriad Manifestations Sect now, these small gains would come to naught. Conversely, leaving now would be more conducive to achieving his goals.

Xiao Juxia couldn't figure it out for a moment.

From his perspective, Qin De was currently cultivating the Ten Thousand Methods Falling into Demon, converting his entire cultivation base into this demonic art.

This was a critical moment.

He needed to watch constantly so that if there were any changes, he could intervene in time to help Qin De, or to observe and learn from Qin De's version of the Ten Thousand Methods Falling into Demon.

This was also the time for harvest.

But now, the result of the deduction was telling him to leave immediately.

"Then I shall leave now." Xiao Juxia immediately put away the ancient scroll, left the room, and his figure quickly disappeared into the night.

During the Flying Cloud Assembly, the main gate of the Myriad Manifestations Sect was open freely.

Xiao Juxia hid his strength and, with his exceptional means, passed through the teleportation array with ease, leaving the main mountain gate.

A thousand li away, two streaks of light were flying side by side.

It was Zhao Hansheng and Gu Qing.

The master and disciple had left the Myriad Manifestations Sect five hours ago. Along the way, both remained silent, simply burying their heads in travel.

Although they had a carriage, they still chose this method of travel that drained spiritual energy and tortured the physical body as a way to silently vent the depression and grief in their hearts.

Gu Qing was, after all, only at the Foundation Establishment Stage and was showing signs of exhaustion.

Zhao Hansheng, observant of the smallest details, slowed down in advance and was the first to descend.

The master and student landed on a solitary peak.

Zhao Hansheng looked at the bright moon in the sky, sighed bitterly, and then said, "Qing'er, as your teacher, I have something to tell you."

Gu Qing quickly said, "Teacher, please speak."

Zhao Hansheng's tone was low but firm. "I intend, upon returning to our country this time, to thoroughly study under Master Wang Xinyue and dedicate myself to researching the School of Mind."

Gu Qing was stunned.

Zhao Hansheng was a Great Confucian Scholar, famous throughout Huazhang. If he did this, it would surely cause a sensation.

In the Huazhang Kingdom, although the School of Mind had emerged suddenly and attracted attention, its development was still arduous. Every step forward faced enormous pressure.

This was because the traditional Confucian schools occupied too many interests. Court official positions, academy teaching posts, disciples' tuition, the printing of classics... all were controlled by these people.

These resources and benefits had long been carved up by traditional Confucianism. For the School of Mind to develop and grow, it inevitably had to take a share from them.

Some people had vision and foresight, willing to accept new learning. Zhao Hansheng was one such person. But the majority - they only cared about their own interests. Since the School of Mind threatened their livelihood, how could they easily let go?

Therefore, if the School of Mind wanted to prosper, it had to go through "battles" one after another. Debating scriptures with the old schools, discussing the Dao with the orthodox, maneuvering with those in power, and fighting against conservatives. Only with every victory could they win a fraction of space to grow.

Under these circumstances, what would happen if Zhao Hansheng, who had achieved the status of Great Confucian Scholar through traditional schools and was famous nationwide, switched to the School of Mind?

It would inevitably shock the entire nation!

Putting aside the School of Mind's reaction, the traditional schools would surely hate Zhao Hansheng to death, viewing him as the greatest traitor!

Previously, Zhao Hansheng had only exchanged ideas with Wang Xinyue and obtained some essentials of the School of Mind, which he admired.

Only after leaving the Huazhang Kingdom and traveling the world did he feel free enough to teach the School of Mind.

Relying solely on these essentials of the School of Mind would not be enough to defeat Qin De.

Since there was a three-year pact, Zhao Hansheng knew perfectly well that relying on traditional Confucianism offered little assurance. Only by mastering more essentials of the School of Mind would there be a chance to wash away the shame.

His mind was made up. He would thoroughly switch to the School of Mind, not hesitating to stand at the very center of this vortex, not hesitating to face the condemnation of old friends!

Gu Qing was deeply moved, knowing that a significant part of Zhao Hansheng's switch to the School of Mind was for his sake. Immediately, Gu Qing gritted his teeth. "Teacher, why don't you let your student go and join..."

Zhao Hansheng interrupted directly. "How could I, Zhao, let my own student charge into the line of fire!"

As the master and student spoke, a faint, almost imperceptible aura quietly approached.

The aura slowly permeated, seeping into Gu Qing's divine sea, leaving an extremely faint mark deep within his soul.

Gu Qing suddenly shivered.

He didn't pay it any mind, thinking it was just mental agitation combined with the fatigue of travel.

Zhao Hansheng sighed. "Let's go. We'll travel a bit further; there's a town ahead where we can rest for the night."

In the night sky, two streaks of light drifted further and further away.

Behind them, a grey figure quietly emerged.

Xiao Juxia watched their retreating figures, a faint smile playing on the corners of his mouth. "This Gu Qing is also a good seedling for cultivating the demonic path. It is a pity his teacher is by his side; it's inconvenient to strike directly."

Just now, he had planted a demonic thought in Gu Qing.

"Wang Xinyue is far from simple."

"The current ruler of the Huazhang Kingdom has grand strategies and foresight, playing chess with the world, fully promoting Confucianism, causing several cultivation kingdoms to change their systems."

"The Confucian foundation is not what it used to be; it can be called robust. Wang Xinyue might just be the person derived from Confucian Destiny to deal with the coming Era of Great Strife."

Xiao Juxia's gaze was deep and dark.

Therefore, he decided to let Gu Qing go for now, allowing the latter to become a pawn to approach Wang Xinyue. Thereby helping Xiao Juxia probe the background of this founder of the School of Mind, and even deduce the strength of the Confucian Destiny.

"The Era of Great Strife..."

Xiao Juxia murmured softly, then pinched his fingers to calculate again. "I can go back now."

Having walked out of the main gate of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he had nothing to do. On a sudden whim, he had caught up with the master and disciple, Zhao Hansheng and Gu Qing, and planted a small device.

Huazhang Capital.

Imperial Ancestral Temple.

"Hmm?!" Wang Shu, who was making arrangements, suddenly looked up, his pupils constricting violently.

He was shocked to discover that a golden thread on the Golden Chapter Jade Scroll had suddenly snapped.

"Detrimental to the state!" Wang Shu's heart tightened, and he immediately hastened his arrangements. As for this scene, Zhou Zheng would naturally report it.

Myriad Manifestations Sect.

Cloud Canopy Peak Summit.

Dong Chen and the other three stopped casting their techniques, all physically and mentally exhausted.

"We cast techniques with all our might, yet we couldn't deduce it?!"

"It's not that we couldn't calculate it, but that it landed on empty space."

"The Eight Peaks are all within the main mountain gate. For the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy to block this calamity, the range is compressed and limited to the main mountain gate. Perhaps this key figure has left the mountain gate; that is also possible."

Wei Ji, Tuoba Huang, and Wang Yu discussed among themselves.

Dong Chen took a light breath and slowly stood up.

His divine sense penetrated the main hall, seeing the scene of dawn on the horizon.

It was almost daybreak.

During the Flying Cloud Assembly, the four cultivators present were all busy with myriad affairs, entangled in various duties.

Dong Chen sighed, thinking to himself: We can only postpone this matter for now. After some time, we will gather the four of us again to cast the technique together.

Zhao Hansheng and Gu Qing arrived at the town at night and rested for a couple of hours.

At sunrise, just as the two were about to depart, the station master of the post station came forward with clasped fists, saying there was a letter specifically delivered for Zhao Hansheng.

Zhao Hansheng opened the letter and saw it was from Duanmu Zhang.

After reading it, he handed it to Gu Qing.

Gu Qing scanned it and found that Duanmu Zhang was expressing his intentions to continue standing guard at the Myriad Manifestations Sect, waiting for Zhao Hansheng to return after achieving success in his studies.

At the same time, Duanmu Zhang made a small request.

He hoped that when Zhao Hansheng and Gu Qing returned to their country this time, they could take Song Taosheng with them.
