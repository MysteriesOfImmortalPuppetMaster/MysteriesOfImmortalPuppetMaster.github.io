It was one of the trump cards for the White Jade Camp to resist Lord Long and hold on. If it couldn't be retrieved and returned to its original place, the sustained combat capability of the White Jade Camp would be greatly weakened.

"After spiritual energy is purified, it is more efficient to use. Otherwise, if there are too many 'impurities,' the loss is too great, and the effect will be very poor."

Ning Zhuo's fingers moved, constantly activating techniques, stirring up the talismans in the spirit energy pool.

"There are more than three thousand talismans on the pool wall, forming a magical restriction together, which is... the 'Earth Mother Nourishing Milk Essential Art'!"

Sun Lingtong immediately showed a look of joy; Ning Zhuo's cracking speed exceeded his imagination.

Ning Zhuo took out more than a dozen staff heads and then took out a wooden hammer, hammering them hard, forcibly driving the staff heads into the pool wall.

He then used mechanical arts to manipulate the staff heads, successfully interfering with the operation of the spirit energy pool, forcing the three formations to gradually come to a halt.

The magical restriction of the 'Earth Mother Nourishing Milk Essential Art' was also propped open with many gaps and openings by the staff heads, allowing Ning Zhuo to peer into the core within.

He infused his spirit energy with all his might.

This spirit energy followed the staff heads, accurately finding the core of the spirit energy pool, washing it like a flood!

After a dozen breaths, Ning Zhuo had washed away most of the connection between the spirit energy pool and its original owner. The rest would require slow work and would take a long time.

Having achieved this, Ning Zhuo had reached his goal.

He immediately stopped infusing spirit energy and withdrew his divine sense.

Huff, huff...

Ning Zhuo was gasping for breath, his face pale, his divine sense greatly consumed, and his spirit energy urgently needing replenishment.

Sun Lingtong was in an even worse state than him.

Sensing that the spirit energy pool was no longer shaking violently, he immediately revoked the stealing technique 'Real Copy Hand,' collapsing to the ground exhausted.

But he was very excited, his eyes shining, staring intently at the spirit energy pool.

Ning Zhuo was doing the finishing work; he was circling the spirit energy pool, now the size of a small table, using various materials to set up a sealing formation.

Sun Lingtong observed Ning Zhuo's techniques and nodded with satisfaction, immediately praising: "Little Zhuo, it wasn't in vain that I trained you since you were young. This set of methods to suppress treasures, although you haven't used it for a long time, you practiced it so many times before that you're already thoroughly proficient; it's practically ingrained in your bones! You did great!"

Although Ning Zhuo was physically and mentally exhausted, seeing the spirit energy pool in front of him also brought a flush of joyous redness to his face.

"I have refined most of it and learned that this spirit energy pool is called the 'Earth Milk Pure Spirit Pool.'"

"According to estimation, the market price is four hundred and four hundred fifty high spirit stones!"

"We've made a fortune, we're rich!"

The Earth Milk Pure Spirit Pool was of the magic treasure level.

And the price range of magic treasures was extremely large!

A spirit energy pool at the magic treasure level was a heavy treasure; the cost of refining it was often a huge sum, qualified to be used only by medium and large forces.

Therefore, spirit energy pools at the magic treasure level were expensive.

Usually, a Golden Core-level force had an annual output value of about 1.5 million low-grade spirit stones.

Note that this was the gross output value, not net profit.

Such an Earth Milk Pure Spirit Pool would require a Golden Core-level force to not eat or drink for three consecutive years to afford it.

For an individual Golden Core cultivator, assuming he had a strong earning ability, working hard on his own, not eating or drinking and spending no money, only making money, it would take at least forty-seven years!

When Sun Lingtong heard the quoted price from Ning Zhuo, he miraculously squeezed out a bit of strength from his exhausted body.

He stood up: "Quick, Little Zhuo, this is a rare opportunity!"

"There are at least two more spirit energy pools in the White Jade Camp."
