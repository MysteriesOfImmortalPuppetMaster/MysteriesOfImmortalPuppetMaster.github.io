Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 895: Cangya's Leverage

The Red-Robed Guest pondered for a moment. "Little Kid Ning Zhuo, there is a massive flaw in your plan."

Ning Zhuo replied, "If Senior has any concerns, please feel free to voice them."

The Red-Robed Guest said, "Don't forget, your Nanming Fire Furnace is not completely repaired; it is still lacking by twenty percent. We emptied our coffers and borrowed external funds just to reach the current repair progress. How do you plan to repair the remaining twenty percent? Don't tell me you intend to ask the clan behind you for a loan?"

Ning Zhuo snorted lightly. "If I always reach out to my family whenever I encounter difficulties, how could I demonstrate my own abilities and methods?"

"The reason I am exceptional is because I am Ning Zhuo, not because of where I come from."

These words made the Red-Robed Guest, who came from a rogue cultivator background, look at Ning Zhuo much more favorably.

Ning Zhuo continued, "Senior might not be aware, but just a moment ago, the current Hall Master of the Artifact Refining Hall, Tie Kuang, paid me a personal visit."

The Red-Robed Guest had actually seen Tie Kuang leaving before he arrived, but at this moment, he feigned ignorance. "Oh? What did the two of you discuss?"

Ning Zhuo replied, "Lord Tie Kuang offered to sponsor me on his own accord. He is willing to mobilize the power of the entire Artifact Refining Hall and provide sufficient precious materials to help me completely repair the Nanming Fire Furnace."

The Red-Robed Guest's pupils shrank.

Ning Zhuo continued, "But I refused him."

The Red-Robed Guest was stunned.

Ning Zhuo added, "Lord Tie Kuang demanded twenty percent ownership of the furnace. Heh, does he really think I'm that easy to trick? My cultivation might be on the weaker side, but that's about it."

The Red-Robed Guest fell silent.

Putting himself in Ning Zhuo's shoes, he thought he probably would have agreed to the deal. After all, it only cost twenty percent, leaving him with eighty percent of the ownership, and in return, he would get a fully functional Nanming Fire Furnace. Why wouldn't anyone jump at that offer?

Seeing the Red-Robed Guest's silence, Ning Zhuo deliberately said, "If Senior doesn't believe me, you are more than welcome to ask around afterward."

The Red-Robed Guest said, "It seems you already have a plan in mind. Since it's neither your clan nor the Artifact Refining Hall, could it be the Demon Subjugation Hall?"

Ning Zhuo burst into laughter. "Not them either. Senior, please take a look!"

He took out the secret contract he had previously signed with Zhong Limei and showed it to the Red-Robed Guest.

The Red-Robed Guest lacked political acumen. After reading the contract, he simply congratulated Ning Zhuo for having the opportunity to clear his debts.

Ning Zhuo said, "If my plan succeeds and I become an upper echelon of Flowing Cloud Peak, why would I bother joining some hall just to be bossed around?"

The Red-Robed Guest thought to himself, "This kid is still too young. Within the Myriad Manifestations Sect, although the Eight Great Main Peaks appear to sit above the Sixteen Halls, in reality, every hall has historically been a contested territory for the Eight Peaks. The Demon Subjugation Hall is an impenetrable fortress; it is perhaps the most powerful and influential department among all the Sixteen Halls."

"However... Zhong Dao enforces incredibly strict rules, demanding absolute obedience in all actions. Given Ning Zhuo's arrogant and rebellious genius nature, his refusal to be disciplined would indeed generate immense resentment towards such an environment."

The Red-Robed Guest handed the secret contract back to Ning Zhuo and pressed, "Then how do you plan to resolve the final twenty percent shortfall and fully repair the Nanming Fire Furnace? Don't tell me you expect us creditors to foot the bill again?"

At this thought, the Red-Robed Guest grew wary.

Ning Zhuo replied, "Of course not!"

He spoke without hesitation, his attitude resolute.

"I will use the repair of the Nanming Fire Furnace as an excuse to invite a few other seniors and borrow more funds. I'm already in debt anyway, and I definitely won't be able to pay off these massive sums within a hundred years regardless!"

The Red-Robed Guest was momentarily rendered speechless.

He looked at Ning Zhuo, unable to help but think to himself, "The sheer shamelessness of this kid... He really reminds me of myself back in the day!"

Ning Zhuo asked, "Senior, would you say my method is viable?"

The Red-Robed Guest thought: Of course it is viable! The Nanming Fire Furnace is just one step away from being completely repaired. Once that happens, won't spiritual pills come rolling out in endless waves? If I were an outsider looking in, I would definitely want a piece of the action.

Ning Zhuo asked again, "With the Nanming Fire Furnace in my hands, I am now going to interfere in the struggle over Flowing Cloud Peak. War is treacherous and fraught with danger. Would you creditors feel at ease? Wouldn't you worry about my safety? Wouldn't you fear the Nanming Fire Furnace falling into someone else's hands?"

The Red-Robed Guest pondered: Of course we would worry! You little brat just need to rest well and work hard to pay off your debts; stop stirring up trouble!

As if he could hear the Red-Robed Guest's inner thoughts, Ning Zhuo immediately added, "Senior, don't try to talk me out of stirring things up. I am a dignified man, a peerless genius. What's wrong with having a little ambition? Factions like the Demon Subjugation Hall and Danxia Peak - if I join them, how much time will I have to waste climbing the ranks? How many years will I spend being bossed around? What's wrong with wanting a little freedom?"

The Red-Robed Guest remained silent.

Ning Zhuo continued, "I dared to sign this secret contract, and I even rejected Tie Kuang. No one can stop my forward momentum! So what if I'm only at the Foundation Establishment Stage? It's not like I want to be the Peak Master; I obviously can't handle that yet. But I want to become an upper echelon - why can't I?! Man makes his own destiny! I just want to gamble on this!"

"If I succeed, I will laugh proudly at the world. If I fail, what's the big deal?!"

The Red-Robed Guest took a deep breath, still remaining silent.

Ning Zhuo asked one final question: "Senior, my mind is set on this! As my creditor, you're worried about my safety, so what do you think is the best course of action? Shouldn't you put in a little effort to protect me at critical moments? Even if you don't fight, just publicly announcing that you are leading me to fight for Flowing Cloud Peak to boost my prestige would be good enough."

"Of course, if you truly join the fray, you would be the leader. I would assist you with all my might to help you ascend to the position of Peak Master of Flowing Cloud Peak!"

"Though my cultivation is low, I have the Nanming Fire Furnace. I can refine pills for you! With so many precious pills, would we ever have to worry about recruiting talents or exchanging them for resources?"

The Red-Robed Guest pressed his lips tightly together, his heart pounding with temptation!

He was well aware of the situation at Flowing Cloud Peak. Although it was one of the Eight Great Main Peaks, it had lacked an official Peak Master for a long time.

Various factions were entangled there in a chaotic mix, constantly taking turns staging their plays for power.

"The title of Peak Master of Flowing Cloud Peak might be in name only, but that is only temporary."

"And even a temporary Peak Master is far better than my current status as a marginalized outcast!"

"But are there any traps or schemes hidden within all this?"

The Red-Robed Guest was already tempted and wanted to get involved, but his past life on the Demonic Path still kept him on high alert.

Unable to figure it out at the moment, he simply said to Ning Zhuo, "Kid, your eloquence is truly remarkable. Remarkable."

Ning Zhuo's eyes lit up. "Does that mean Senior has agreed?"

The Red-Robed Guest flicked his sleeve and turned to leave. "How could that be? Keep dreaming! I'll hold onto this list of debts, and we will slowly settle accounts in the future."

Ultimately, he rejected Ning Zhuo and departed from Danxia Peak.

"Little Zhuo, it seems the Red-Robed Guest was somewhat tempted." As soon as the Red-Robed Guest left, Sun Lingtong's divine sense traveled along the Life Hanging by a Thread and transmitted into Ning Zhuo's divine sea.

Ning Zhuo responded with his divine sense, "More than just tempted. The Red-Robed Guest joining us is already a near certainty."

"This person is a rogue cultivator who has always fought alone. Relying on his ruthless combativeness, thievery, pillaging, and a few fortunate encounters, he managed to cultivate to the Nascent Soul level."

"However, he offended an increasing number of people, including Chun Yangzi. As it gradually became impossible for him to survive out there, he ended up joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

"His political acumen is utterly lacking. Even after joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he still tried to walk his old path, only thinking about fighting and killing without an ounce of self-awareness. Therefore, once his true colors were revealed, he was continuously marginalized by the Sect. For the past few decades, he has been stuck at the Sect's Jade Blood Mountain Gate. Essentially, he's in a state of near exile."

"For a Nascent Soul cultivator like him, despite living for a thousand years, he has always existed in his own little world. He survives and cultivates according to an antiquated set of rules. Because he never adapted, he is bound to suffer losses."

"This time, he saw an opportunity during the minor trial of the Nanming Fire Furnace and decided to take a risk. But the way I see it, with his level of attainment on the Righteous Path, even if the Nanming Fire Furnace fell into his hands, he wouldn't be able to utilize its true potential."

"Using the Nanming Fire Furnace to refine pills is actually secondary. Its true value lies in the fact that it is a heritage-level treasure, the very foundation upon which a sect can be established!"

Sun Lingtong nodded. "You're absolutely right. If placed in the outside world, any cultivator using this furnace to establish a faction would undoubtedly attract a massive number of cultivators eager to cling to them."

The Nanming Fire Furnace represented a never-ending stream of cultivation resources.

It was just like a spiritual spring or a Spirit Stone vein - it naturally attracted cultivators to gather nearby and practice. Over time, various clans and sects would inevitably spring up around it.

Ning Zhuo said, "By solely relying on the Nanming Fire Furnace, I can attract talents within the Myriad Manifestations Sect and establish my own faction. The fact that I am in so much debt right now presents the perfect opportunity. I can use these debts to pull us closer, form an alliance, and make them work for me! Hahaha."

Sun Lingtong said in a gratified tone, "Little Zhuo, you were born to thrive on the Righteous Path. Ordinary people would only see this debt as a burden, yet you've weaponized it into a lucky chance!"

"But do you really plan to attract new creditors? Although Tie Kuang exaggerated a bit, in my eyes, he was stating a fact. We aren't lacking the precious materials for repairs; we're lacking capable artifact refiners. Furthermore, if you bring in other creditors, what will you do when they demand ownership rights? Handing the Nanming Fire Furnace over to the Artifact Refining Hall is still the safest bet."

Ning Zhuo nodded inwardly. "Boss, we are in complete agreement on that point. However, even though I rejected Tie Kuang, won't he eventually lower his conditions on his own accord to help me repair it?"

Sun Lingtong was stunned. "What do you mean?"

Ning Zhuo laughed heartily. "Chun Yangzi and the Nine Fire Dragon Lord have both rejected me, but I believe the chances of them joining our side remain extremely, extremely high."

Sun Lingtong scratched his head. "Little Zhuo, do you still have other methods up your sleeve?"

Ning Zhuo feigned a profound tone, "Boss, just watch how things unfold in the future."

The fifth Nascent Soul cultivator that Ning Zhuo received was Cang Yazi.

Cang Yazi was an elder of Myriad Beast Peak - not merely a guest elder, marking a fundamental difference between him and mid-way joiners like the Red-Robed Guest.

While the Red-Robed Guest had been ostracized, Cang Yazi simply had no patience for cultivating alongside other Humans. He much preferred the company of pure and sincere spirit pets. He had relocated on his own initiative and spent the last few centuries cultivating in solitude within the White Mist Valley of the Northern Frost Mountain Gate.

Given someone with his status, Ning Zhuo hadn't held any expectations.

Surprisingly, before Ning Zhuo even had a chance to speak, Cang Yazi got straight to the point: "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, the debt you owe me can be repaid in another form. It will entail almost no loss for you."

Ning Zhuo's heart skipped a beat, and he hurriedly asked for guidance.

Cang Yazi then said, "Among the spirit pets by my side, there are avian types. The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit is perfectly suited to awaken their sentience. Combined with my Form Transformation Pills, it will allow their transformations to go much more smoothly."

Feeling a great surge of pleasant surprise, Ning Zhuo shook his head and flatly refused, "No! Absolutely not!"
