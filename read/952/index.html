Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 952: Ning Zhuo, You Will Undoubtedly Lose!

The double-pupiled golden eagle behind Zhao Ni was so startled by the zither music that it violently shook its feathers. Zhao Ni pressed down on the eagle's body. Having already figured it out, a heavy, strange gleam flashed in his eyes: "This kid's musical skills have improved too fast!"

Ye Qingming gently blew away the foam on the surface of her tea while watching the battle, feeling puzzled: "His musical skills are actually this proficient? His attainments are this profound?"

She understood music theory. After listening for a moment, she discerned that Ning Zhuo hid order within chaos, possessing a unique rhythm. Amidst the frenzy, there was a distinctive musical beauty!

Listening to the frenzied zither notes, Liu Jinke's inner irritability sharply increased.

The Chaotic-String Sound-Rending Fork was originally meant to break Ning Zhuo's zither notes and disrupt the mechanical bird swarm.

But at this moment, he found that although his rending sound could still suppress the mechanical birds, it could not completely sever Ning Zhuo's coordination. It even forced Ning Zhuo's zither notes to become increasingly fierce.

This feeling made him feel both familiar and uncomfortable.

He realized that he was once again being treated as a whetstone, used by his opponent to sharpen a blade.

"A mere Mid Foundation Establishment Stage, yet he dares to look down on a Golden Core cultivator like me!" Liu Jinke's anger grew further, and he no longer hesitated.

Beneath the robes at his chest, a black-and-gold divine pattern suddenly lit up!

It was a pattern resembling a beast's maw, its edges lined with interlocking golden teeth, and its center bottomlessly black.

Accompanied by a low shout from Liu Jinke, the divine pattern opened and closed like a living creature. Black-gold light spread directly in all directions from his entire body, instantly sweeping over half of the martial arena.

Gold-Devouring Divine Domain!

The golden threads embedded in the cloud-marble floor, used to stabilize the formation patterns, suddenly dimmed a fraction. Inside the mechanical birds' bellies, the metallic springs, gears, and clasps simultaneously let out fine, mournful creaks. The metal qi within these metallic spiritual constructs was fiercely extracted, flowing like small streams returning to the sea, converging toward the beast-maw divine pattern on Liu Jinke's chest.

The situation on the martial arena drastically changed.

The black-gold radiance surged like a tide, swallowing shattered bamboo, iron scraps, golden needles, and mystic wheels. It wasn't as blazing as lightning and fire, nor as sharp as a blade's gleam, but it possessed an unsettling, ultimate greed. It was as if an invisible giant maw had opened in the center of the arena, slowly chewing up all metallic objects.

The mechanical bird swarm instantly descended into chaos.

Liu Jinke charged forward once again. Wherever he passed, the mechanical birds were utterly routed.

Ning Zhuo's gaze turned a fraction heavier.

He patted his Storage Belt, and the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard dropped into the arena.

As soon as this Golden Core-level puppet appeared, it blocked the path directly in front of Ning Zhuo. Its chest and abdomen were hollow, its qi bearing an empty vastness, as the folded blades on its arms slowly extended, the blade surfaces glinting with a pale, cold light.

Liu Jinke rushed in, bringing the Chaotic-String Sound-Rending Fork crashing down on its head. The Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard parried with its left arm and smoothly countered with a backhanded slash from its right arm. The folded blade sliced like a returning wind snapping snow, aiming straight for Liu Jinke's wrist bone.

Clang!

The clash of metal striking metal made the cloud-marble floor tremble.

Clang! Clang! Clang!

The Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard successfully blocked Liu Jinke's charges three times in a row. Every time Liu Jinke tried to bypass the guard and approach Ning Zhuo, he was intercepted by the folded blades at strange angles. The blades did not seek to meet force with force; instead, they targeted the transition points of his power, narrowly missing slicing Liu Jinke's wrists open on several occasions.

In the seating area of the Naming Stronghold, Situ Xing's eyes lit up: "Worthy of being Golden Core-level."

Over at Flowing Cloud Peak's seating area, Qiu Lei shook his head instead. He had scouted out the inheritance intel regarding the [Empty Valley Syllable Cyan Mechanism Cylinder] and felt secretly smug: "It also has metallic spiritual constructs."

Inside the Gold-Devouring Divine Domain, the metal qi from the metallic nodes inside the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard's body began to be extracted.

At first, only the luster of the blades dimmed a fraction. Subsequently, the rotation of its shoulder and elbow joints began to display a slight sluggishness.

That level of sluggishness was minuscule, but with Liu Jinke right in front of it, he immediately noticed.

He directly changed his fighting style.

No longer trying to bypass the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard, he forced it into head-on clashes again and again.

The Chaotic-String Sound-Rending Fork smashed down continuously. The rending sounds fell like rain, while the divine domain ceaselessly devoured, extracting metal qi like a tide.

Every time the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard blocked a strike, the metallic nodes within its body were knocked loose. With every rotation, a layer of the blade's spiritual light was peeled away by the divine domain.

Ning Zhuo was forced to allocate more magical power and more divine sense to control the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard in its clash against Liu Jinke.

While borrowing the brief breath of time fought for by the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard, he raised his hand and cast out a swathe of firelight.

Fire Element Spell support attack!

Liu Jinke sneered. Golden blood sprayed from his palm onto the ground, activating the Gold-Transmutation Art.

A layer of golden filth floated up from the cloud-marble floor. Several fallen mechanical components were tainted by the golden blood, immediately twisting and stiffening on the ground as they turned a golden color.

The golden light spread towards Ning Zhuo, as well as onto the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot.

Liu Jinke was repeating his old trick!

Ning Zhuo took out a Medicinal Pill and swallowed it.

As the pill's qi entered his throat, his fiery intent skyrocketed along with it. To outsiders, it looked like the Medicinal Pill was boosting his fire, but in reality, it concealed the true secret of the Fire Element Heart Temple within his body.

Flame Cleansing Golden Filth Art!

Crimson firelight poured out from Ning Zhuo's palm, washing over his own body and the surrounding martial arena like a river of fire.

The flames did not merely burn; they carried a refining intent, scouring inch by inch along the patterns of the golden filth. The mechanical components tainted by the Gold-Transmutation Art let out sizzling sounds as the golden filth was gradually burned into black smoke, slowly revealing the true colors of the cloud-marble floor.

In their previous two battles, the Flame Cleansing Golden Filth Art had heavily countered Liu Jinke. This time, although the effect was still present, it was no longer instantaneous.

The Golden Blood Core Bead inside Liu Jinke's body trembled, and a new force was suddenly born. The golden filth poured out endlessly, scattering all around.

In the martial arena, the golden filth that had just been cleansed by the flames was replenished once again.

Seeing this, Ning Zhuo substituted defense with offense, executing Fire Element Techniques to fiercely attack Liu Jinke's main body.

Liu Jinke opened his mouth and swallowed a Fire-Warding Pill. The pill melted as soon as it entered his mouth, its clear, cold medicinal power spreading against the surface of his golden blood, forming a faint white-gold film.

The flames burned onto his forearms, only causing the golden film to sizzle; they found it difficult to instantly penetrate his golden-blooded body.

Liu Jinke then consumed a Flame-Evading Blood-Protecting Pill.

The pill's qi entered his blood like a cold blade, protecting the bloodline circulation deep within his golden blood. With the two medicinal powers stacked together, the flames could only scald his flesh and make the golden blood boil slightly. They could no longer sever his foundation like they had in the previous two battles!

"The counter is still there," Wen Suzhen stared at the slight scorch marks on Liu Jinke's body surface, "but it has been suppressed by the medicinal power."

Lei Wangyue's tense jaw finally relaxed a bit: "That's how it should be! This is exactly what we wanted to see!"

In the arena, Liu Jinke's offensive became increasingly ferocious.

He swallowed a Golden Marrow Life-Renewing Pill. The pill turned into a thick golden liquid in his belly, spreading through his meridians, filling the depths of his bone marrow, and repairing his foundation. Another pill, an Iron Bone Stiffness-Fixing Pill, was crushed between his teeth. Its medicinal power sank into his skeleton, causing a series of low popping sounds from his joints, as every single bone was temporarily forged into iron bone by the medicinal power.

Liu Jinke's body weight skyrocketed, his physical prowess surged wildly, and the power behind his momentum multiplied several times over. It was as if he had transformed from a spearman into a heavy-armored infantryman.

The Chaotic-String Sound-Rending Fork smashed into the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard. The guard was forced back half a step, while Liu Jinke, upon suffering a counterattack, merely slid back an inch.

The next moment, the mechanical bird swarm lunged from all directions, submerging Liu Jinke.

A Golden-Thread Caged Sparrow exploded into entangling bamboo wires, wrapping around his arm, only to be forcefully torn apart by him. A Bamboo-Clasping Pheasant charged low across the ground, attempting to shake his stance, but his lower body was as steady as a mountain range. Even as the Bamboo-Clasping Pheasants self-destructed in succession, they only caused Liu Jinke's body to sway a few times.

The vast majority of the attacks were blocked by his golden armor. Even when occasional armor-piercing strikes pierced his skin, the Golden Netted Garment hidden beneath the surface effortlessly deflected them all.

The entire defensive system perfectly withstood the attacks of the mechanical bird swarm.

Countless golden needles sprayed from the acupoints all over Liu Jinke's body, while the tuning fork in his hand continuously vibrated with soundwaves, exterminating this flock of mechanical birds.

"Liu Jinke has practically been reborn," Lin Jinglong whispered.

Shen Xi watched Ning Zhuo open his first Spell Pool. His hand, which had been stroking a jade pendant, paused: "Ning Zhuo has started to replenish his magical power."

A five-colored Spell Pool lit up.

The earthy yellow in the center first stabilized his sea of qi, followed by the sequential backflow of wood, fire, metal, and water. Five Elements magical power poured into Ning Zhuo's meridians, slightly filling the deficit caused by simultaneously maintaining the mechanical swarm, the personal guard, the fire techniques, and the zither music.

Liu Jinke saw this and coldly snorted. The Golden Blood Core Beads at his chest spun, and he directly dispersed one of them.

Golden mist surged from his body. His vital essence blood, magical power, injuries, and physical exhaustion were fully restored almost simultaneously! The scorch marks that had just been burned by the flames rapidly peeled off, revealing newly formed golden skin. The golden blood's radiance on the Sound-Rending Fork grew even brighter, and even the Gold-Devouring Divine Domain expanded outwards by half a zhang.

Ning Zhuo's replenishment was like a trickling stream.

Liu Jinke's replenishment, however, was like a gushing waterfall!

Comparing the two, the superior and inferior were immediately obvious.

Many cultivators from Flowing Cloud Peak understood the inside story, and quite a few wore smug smiles on their lips, looking forward to the "good show" Ning Zhuo was about to put on.

Liu Jinke chiseled his way forward with fierce attacks, advancing steadily. The Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard retreated step by step, and the mechanical bird swarm could not contain him. Ultimately, he managed to close the distance to just over ten zhang away from Ning Zhuo.

Liu Jinke took out a Blood Body Talisman.

The crimson talisman paper was like leather, its surface veins throbbing gently like blood vessels.

He slapped it against his chest, and in an instant, half the vital essence blood in his body was forcibly extracted by the talisman!

Blood light burst out from his body, condensing into a crimson-gold blood shadow by his side. At first, the blood shadow's face was blurred, but in the blink of an eye, its facial features, body shape, and aura all took form, becoming almost identical to Liu Jinke's true form.

Liu Jinke's face instantly paled a shade, his lips went dry, and his rigid body experienced a momentary bout of weakness.

But the next moment, another Golden Blood Core Bead dispersed, and a surge of nourishment rushed up, causing his chest and abdomen to swell with power once again. The golden light in his eyes, which had just dimmed, immediately regained its brilliance!

"Ning Zhuo, come and block me!" Liu Jinke roared. The blood body and his main body flanked left and right, pouncing towards Ning Zhuo simultaneously.

Ning Zhuo's fingers moved so fast they became a blur, and a loud burst of zither music echoed out.

Mechanical birds constantly flew out to reinforce the battlefield. The frenzied zither notes, like a violent storm, drove the mechanical bird swarm into a furious offensive, sweeping over like a tidal wave.

The two Liu Jinkes charged upstream against the current, their offensive power astonishing!

Ning Zhuo mobilized the pages of the Luo Shu, trying his utmost to find flaws in the blood talisman body.

The consumption of his divine sense reached its absolute peak at this moment.

Ning Zhuo had to play the zither, steer his mount, the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot, coordinate a massive number of mechanical birds, control the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard, and manage the Five Elements Spell Pools flowing back into his sea of qi.

Ning Zhuo felt a continuous, faint stinging sensation coming from the center of his brow. The foundation of his Mid Foundation Establishment Stage divine sense was, at this moment, stretched somewhat thin.

"Ning Zhuo, take my fork!" Liu Jinke's main body roared, charging right in front of Ning Zhuo and bringing the Chaotic-String Sound-Rending Fork smashing down fiercely.

Meanwhile, the blood talisman clone tightly entangled the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard.

Ning Zhuo sighed softly in his heart. His divine sense mobilized the Golden Light Collar and the Cyan-White Jade Ring, causing golden halos and a cyan-white curtain of light to unfurl layer by layer.

Clang!

The rending sound crashed into the curtain of light, violently shaking the Golden Light Collar.

Liu Jinke's blood body temporarily knocked back the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guard, closed in from the other side, and threw a punch towards Ning Zhuo's shoulder.

The mechanical bird swarm lunged to block it, but the blood body neither dodged nor retreated. Allowing a few Sleeve-Thorn Sparrowhawks to slash open its chest and abdomen, its fist continued to crash into Ning Zhuo's curtain of light.

Ning Zhuo had no time to parry. He could only endure the blow head-on, then freed one hand from the zither to wield the Langgan Hundred-Jointed Whip, lashing out at the blood talisman body.

At the same time, he controlled the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot into an emergency retreat.

Liu Jinke and his blood talisman clone heavily struck Ning Zhuo a few times, pounding the big-headed youth's curtain of light until it dimmed. Only after the body of the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot was left battered and a massive number of mechanical birds were destroyed did Ning Zhuo manage to temporarily break free and reopen the distance.

"Hahaha, hahahaha!" Liu Jinke looked up at the sky and burst into laughter.

This bout of laughter released all the pent-up frustration in his heart.

Thrilling, absolutely thrilling!

Ever since Liu Jinke had started clashing with Ning Zhuo, this was the first time he had seen Ning Zhuo in such a wretched state.

At this moment, the fear of Ning Zhuo in Liu Jinke's heart had completely dissipated. All his lack of confidence had crumbled, leaving behind only the thirst for victory!

Sitting on the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot, Ning Zhuo seized the time to rest and reorganize.

He activated his second Spell Pool.

The five-colored spiritual light flowed back once again, his meridians swelling under the scouring of magical power.

Ning Zhuo's face paled slightly, but the depths of his eyes remained calm.

He was not in a hurry to counterattack. Instead, he gathered the surviving mechanical birds on the field, scattering the remaining flock around him, shifting to a skirmishing tactic.

A skirmishing style, naturally, was even less capable of stopping Liu Jinke.

Liu Jinke and his blood talisman clone lunged right before Ning Zhuo's eyes once more.

Ning Zhuo immediately deployed his Mystic Armament Armors!

The four Golden Core-level Mystic Armament Armors - saber, sword, axe, and hook - flew out first. The saber's gleam was heavy, the sword's gleam was cold and clear, the axe's gleam was profound and fierce, and the hook's gleam was dark and soft. Afterwards, the older Mystic Armament Armors, such as the old cold legs, spear, shield, and gauntlets, also appeared one after another. The battle damage left over from their second battle had all been repaired. Guided by Ning Zhuo's magical power, they rapidly took their positions.

Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation!

In the viewing stands, many cultivators subconsciously sat up straight when they saw this scene.

In their previous battle, Liu Jinke had been defeated precisely by this combat formation. This formation cycled between offense and defense - first defending oneself, then slashing the enemy. It could borrow the momentum of an enemy's close-range fierce attacks and reverse it into a rotational slashing killing intent. During their second battle, Liu Jinke had been forcefully driven to decapitation by this formation, a scene that was still repeatedly played back on many Image Transmission Jade Slips to this day.

"Come!" Liu Jinke roared as he smashed into the formation.

The saber armor slashed at his neck and shoulders first, the sword armor thrust towards his heart, the axe armor pressed down on his waist and abdomen, and the hook armor wrapped around the back of his knees. The four Golden Core-level armors coordinated flawlessly, their sharp edges forming a wheel, their cold gleams intertwining into a singular mass.

Liu Jinke was instantly slashed with several wounds. Golden blood splattered, spraying onto the cloud-marble and turning into speckled, crimson-gold stains.

The Golden Netted Garment lit up.

The cyan-gold silk netting beneath his skin suddenly pulled taut, as if countless fine golden threads were tugging at his flesh and blood.

Next, the saber blade cut half an inch into the flesh before being pulled aside at a slant by the golden netting. The sword tip, originally meant to pierce his heart, was deflected off course, sliding past and scraping against his ribs. The axe blade slashed into his back, splitting flesh and blood but failing to break bone. The hook armor entangled the back of his knees, but with a tightening of the golden netting, the hook blade was shoved aside.

Liu Jinke was torn open and bathed in blood by the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation, but he completely ignored it.

At this moment, his battle intent pierced the heavens, burning ceaselessly like a raging fire!

Gold-Devouring Divine Domain!

Liu Jinke channeled the divine pattern on his chest with all his might, bringing out the absolute strongest might of the divine domain.

The sharp edges of the Mystic Armament Armors immediately dimmed at a rate visible to the naked eye.

The golden light on the saber's edge was drawn out into thin threads, the spine of the sword armor emitted a strained, low hum, grey-black spots surfaced on the edges of the axe armor's heavy spiritual light, and the rotational speed of the hook armor slowed by a fraction.

Liu Jinke gave a smug, low roar: "Ning Zhuo, your combat formation won't last much longer! In today's battle, you will undoubtedly lose!"
