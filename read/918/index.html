Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 918: Nascent Soul Joins the Alliance!

As soon as Liu Jinke arrived at the door, he provoked them directly and completely unreasonably.

Ah Huo glared, Cao Gui frowned, and Gu Huaijiu, holding a teacup, narrowed his eyes at Liu Jinke.

Situ Xing and the others remained expressionless. Everyone at their table, including Kong Ran, sensed a deeper implication - it was highly likely that Liu Jinke was acting under someone else's instructions!

Ye Qingming looked at Ning Zhuo, observing how this striking, brightly dressed youth would respond.

Li Ku also silently cast a glance his way.

Jin Mantang felt a surge of pleasant surprise in his heart. The previous few Golden Core cultivators had delivered gifts one after another, leaving him repeatedly disappointed. Suddenly, a cultivator from Flowing Cloud Peak arrived. Even though he was an independent cultivator, he issued a fierce challenge directly to Ning Zhuo.

Under the watchful eyes of the crowd, Ning Zhuo pondered for a moment before suddenly cupping his hands toward the sky. "The Myriad Manifestations Sect is the number one sect in the Flying Cloud Kingdom! With its Eight Peaks and Sixteen Halls, it stands as majestic as a mountain. For a lowly mid-Foundation Establishment cultivator like myself to have the fortune of joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect during the Flying Cloud Assembly is truly a peerless opportunity!"

Some people were surprised, not understanding why Ning Zhuo was suddenly singing praises to the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

Others looked thoughtful, and some even secretly praised his brilliance.

Shen Xi's eyes lit up, internally applauding him. "Brother Ning acts with such composure. With just a few words, he has proactively resolved a hidden danger, preventing others from framing him as an enemy outside the Sect."

Ning Zhuo withdrew his distant gaze and turned to look at Liu Jinke. "For Senior Brother Liu Jinke to condescend to come here and personally weigh my worth, I feel deeply honored. Senior Brother is a dignified Golden Core cultivator, yet you still deigned to visit and guide a soon-to-be fellow disciple of the Myriad Manifestations Sect like me. Senior Brother's magnanimity is truly admirable."

Kong Ran almost burst out laughing, thinking to herself, "Brother Ning is too wicked. How is this praising Liu Jinke? It's pure sarcasm."

Liu Jinke obviously heard it too, and his expression darkened. "Enough nonsense! Just tell me, do you dare accept it? Do you have the guts?"

Ning Zhuo smiled, his gaze clear and resolute. "Since Senior Brother wishes to test my worth, I will accept."

Liu Jinke was immediately delighted, while the expressions of Ah Huo, Cao Gui, and the others changed slightly. Jiang Xiaobian looked up, revealing a look of concern.

"However-" Ning Zhuo suddenly shifted his tone.

"Today is the day I establish my alliance, which is fundamentally meant to better repay my debts to the major creditors. Fellow Daoists have traveled far to be here. If my personal matters were to ruin everyone's mood, it would be my fault. Why don't we wait until the ceremony is over before I spar with Senior Brother? What do you say?"

Liu Jinke frowned. "Hurry it up, kid! You speak a bunch of pretty words, but you're still just stalling for time."

"If I were you, I'd prefer short pain over long pain. Just fight me, let me beat you up, and be done with it."

"After that, whether you continue doing what you're doing now is entirely up to you."

Hearing these words, Ning Zhuo's heart stirred slightly.

Liu Jinke seemed completely unaware that he had leaked a lot of crucial intelligence through his words.

This made Ning Zhuo suspicious: "It seems the other party doesn't harbor deep malice; he just has a mission to beat me up. If someone behind the scenes is instructing him, do they merely want to suppress me and damage my reputation? He said he wanted to test my worth - could that be the truth?"

"Of course, this could also be a smokescreen, a disguise for their true objective."

Thinking of this, Ning Zhuo took out a scroll of silk cloth from his Storage Belt and handed it to Liu Jinke. "Since Senior Brother wishes to test my worth, why not take a look at this?"

"This is a life-and-death duel contract drafted by your Junior Brother. Please look it over, Senior Brother."

Liu Jinke's pupils shrank slightly.

All the cultivators in the hall also fell dead silent upon hearing the words "life-and-death duel."

The atmosphere abruptly grew heavy as everyone's eyes locked onto the contract.

Liu Jinke snorted coldly. "Bring it here."

The crowd's gazes followed the contract as it moved from Ning Zhuo's hand to Liu Jinke's.

Liu Jinke swept his divine sense over it and found that it was indeed a life-and-death duel contract. It clearly stipulated: Both parties volunteered to fight, life and death would be left to fate, and afterward, there would be no pursuit of accountability, no revenge, and no implicating others. In the event of death or injury, the deceased's family could not claim compensation, and the injured could not seek redress...

Is this kid serious? Liu Jinke looked up and stared at Ning Zhuo. In that moment, the disdain on his face had completely vanished.

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly, appearing ever more nonchalant. "Do not blame me, Senior Brother. My cultivation level is low; fighting against you is already fraught with peril. If we don't sign this contract, I won't feel at ease. And what if I accidentally injure Senior Brother? Wouldn't that be a sin? By signing this contract, neither of us will have any worries. Isn't that better?"

Liu Jinke narrowed his eyes.

Ning Zhuo spoke very politely and humbly, but Liu Jinke wasn't an idiot. He deeply felt the overflowing confidence hidden deep within Ning Zhuo!

The atmosphere in the hall turned solemn.

Situ Xing felt it the most profoundly. He looked at the smiling Ning Zhuo and had already realized, "Brother Ning is truly enraged. He actually intends to deliver a lethal blow to Liu Jinke."

Cao Gui, on the other hand, was full of worry. "Why must Young Master Ning Zhuo do this? You have a boundless future. Given time, your cultivation will definitely surpass this malicious guest. There is no need to fight to the death right now!"

Lin Jinglong sighed internally, "Brother Ning is doing the right thing. He must strike down the enemy as harshly as possible at the very first opportunity to display his strength. Otherwise, if he is looked down upon, it will draw an endless barrage of sieges, and he won't be able to get any real business done."

Ye Qingming looked at Liu Jinke, well aware that Ning Zhuo's astonishing move had already backed Liu Jinke into a corner.

Jin Mantang, unable to bear the anxiety, couldn't help but secretly transmit his divine sense: "Fellow Daoist Liu Jinke, good on you! You stood up representing our Flowing Cloud Peak. How can we let a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator look down on us? Before this battle, I will gift you a magic treasure to bolster your prestige!"

Liu Jinke's expression had originally hidden a trace of apprehension, but upon hearing this, his eyes lit up. He responded via divine sense transmission: "Then it's a promise!"

Jin Mantang replied without hesitation, "Of course!"

Liu Jinke then flew into a rage, pointing his finger at Ning Zhuo. "Good, good, good! Arrogant enough, brave enough. I'll sign this life-and-death contract!"

He immediately signed the contract.

Ning Zhuo laughed heartily and gestured with his hand. "Please, Senior Brother, take a seat and observe the ceremony. Once this ceremony concludes, I will definitely fight you to the bitter end."

The contract was made in triplicate. One copy was burned to ash as an offering to God. The other two were kept by each of them respectively.

Liu Jinke sneered. Clutching his copy, he found a table in the corner and sat down alone.

Ning Zhuo smiled at him and suddenly called out, "Innkeeper, please serve this Senior Brother his own table of wine and dishes first, so he can properly savor them. I, Ning Zhuo, am a cultivator of the Righteous Path. Even if Senior Brother acts this way, I must still properly arrange a sumptuous last meal for him."

Bang.

Hearing this, Liu Jinke slammed his hand on the table and abruptly stood up. "Ning Zhuo! You are too arrogant! Come on then, bring it on right now! Let me properly teach an ignorant brat like you a lesson!"

Ning Zhuo smiled. "Senior Brother, we have already signed the contract and reached a public agreement. Are you going to break your word right now?"

Liu Jinke immediately froze in place, his face flushed with anger. He glared furiously at Ning Zhuo, his eyes bloodshot. Pointing at Ning Zhuo, he squeezed a sentence out from between his teeth: "Very good. You're very good, kid."

Ning Zhuo cupped his hands. "Thank you for the praise, Senior Brother!"

Situ Xing shook his head and chuckled, finding this rather familiar. "Brother Ning truly has a knack for infuriating people."

Liu Jinke felt utterly stifled. He had a strong urge to storm off in anger, but ultimately restrained himself. Forcing down his fury, he reluctantly sat back down.

Right at that moment, the synchronized sound of footsteps echoed from outside the door.

The footsteps were steady and powerful, like drumbeats, striking rhythmically against everyone's hearts. The crowd turned to look and saw four figures filing in through the door. They wore matching green-grey short robes with tightly bound cuffs. Suspended at their waists were palm-sized iron tokens engraved with the character for 'Military'.

People from Profound Armory Peak!

Each of the four carried a long wooden box. The boxes were made of red sandalwood, their surfaces as smooth as mirrors, with copper strips inlaid along the edges. Finely detailed Runes were carved into the copper strips.

The wooden boxes varied in size, differing in both length and width.

The four men approached Ning Zhuo. The leader stepped forward and bowed slightly. "We of Profound Armory Peak have come under orders to offer our congratulations!"

Ning Zhuo cupped his hands. "The four fellow Daoists have traveled far. Please forgive me for my failure to greet you at the door."

The four men opened the wooden boxes in their hands one by one, revealing four Mystic Weapon Armors - a saber, a sword, an axe, and a hook. Each one emitted the aura of a Golden Core-level magic treasure.

Ning Zhuo immediately showed a look of joy. "Many thanks to the profound favor of your esteemed Peak's Lord, and my thanks to the four fellow Daoists for delivering this gift."

The leader remained expressionless. "There is no need for politeness, Young Master. When Profound Armory Peak acts, we only ask whether it is worth it. Young Master is worthy of this gift, so Profound Armory Peak bestows it. There is no need for thanks, nor is there a need to decline."

Ning Zhuo gestured. "Please take a seat first, four fellow Daoists, and await the alliance ceremony."

The leader nodded and led the other three away to find their seats.

Ning Zhuo stowed away the four Mystic Weapon Armors. He had just been feeling that the Mystic Weapon Armor in his hands was of a lower grade; he never expected that by inviting the representatives of Profound Armory Peak to observe the ceremony, they would deliver a gift that struck right at the heart of his needs.

He subconsciously looked toward Liu Jinke and thought, "I can perfectly test out the new Mystic Weapon Armors on him."

Liu Jinke had been keeping an eye on Ning Zhuo. Instantly detecting the ill intent in Ning Zhuo's gaze, he glared back angrily.

Ning Zhuo gave him a faint smile.

Soon, more people arrived.

Five figures filed in through the door.

Each person's Daoist robe was embroidered with a different pattern: the cyan-robed one had green pines and cypresses; the red-robed one had blazing fire lotuses; the yellow-robed one had vast earthen mountains and rivers; the white-robed one had sharp, glinting swords; and the black-robed one had surging ocean waves.

People from the Five Elements Peak.

"The Rotation Hall of the Five Elements Peak comes under orders to offer our congratulations." The leader of the five bowed.

"The Five Elements Peak congratulates Young Master Ning on the establishment of your alliance. We have prepared a meager gift; please accept it as a token of our regard."

First was the Essence of Great White, refined from metal and stone ore veins using Metal Element magical power.

Second was the Essence of Profound Water, gathered from a deep-sea spring and nourished with Water Element magical power.

Third was the Essence of Abundant Earth, fourth was the Essence of Crimson Flame, and fifth was the Essence of Azure Wood.

All five precious materials were of the Golden Core level, corresponding respectively to the Five Elements.

After Ning Zhuo collected the precious materials, the leader cupped his hands again and said, "Our Peak Lord still intends to hire you, Young Master, as the Vice Hall Master of the Rotation Hall."

Ning Zhuo shook his head slightly, politely refusing: "I am intent on establishing an alliance to charge the Peak, so I can only painfully decline your noble Peak's kind offer."

The leader also shook his head. "The Peak Lord has said that the position of Vice Hall Master of the Rotation Hall will forever remain open for you, Young Master!"

Ning Zhuo let out a long sigh. "What virtue or ability do I possess to earn such high regard from the Peak Lord? Please take a seat first, five fellow Daoists, and enjoy some tea."

The five men cupped their hands and went down to find seats.

One by one, they cast strange glances toward Liu Jinke. This was entirely because all the other tables in the hall were devoid of dishes; Liu Jinke's table was the sole exception.

Liu Jinke: "..."

The five conversed via divine sense transmission and wisely chose an unoccupied table to sit at.

Pi Fujie looked at Liu Jinke with a gaze tinged with pity.

Ye Qingming and Jin Mantang's gazes constantly shifted from the four people of Profound Armory Peak to the five envoys of the Five Elements Peak.

Both were inwardly amazed at how highly Ning Zhuo was valued. Although this had been hinted at in their intelligence reports, witnessing the concrete situation unfold right before their eyes was still deeply impactful.

Even an ascetic cultivator like Li Ku could infer: "Ning Zhuo is so highly regarded by these two Peaks, and intelligence also stated he received the admiration of Zhong Dao and Tuoba Huang. This is the treatment of a first-rate genius, which is not uncommon in past assemblies, but usually, it comes from powers within the same category. However, the factions that favor Ning Zhuo have obvious differences, showing that his extraordinary strength spans multiple domains."

"Indeed, only a genius like this would have the audacity to establish an alliance to charge the Peak!"

The continuous arrival of guests successfully highlighted and proved Ning Zhuo's excellence. Even though the youth hadn't lifted a finger, relying solely on his connections was enough to make the cultivators in the hall take him increasingly seriously, constantly raising their internal estimations of him.

"However, so much time has passed, and the cultivators in the hall are at most at the Golden Core stage. Still, no Nascent Soul-level cultivators have arrived."

"And this is merely for observing the ceremony."

"From this, it can be seen that Ning Zhuo's rallying power is only so-so. His influence this time isn't that broad or deep."

"A Golden Core-level faction doesn't pose much of a threat."

Just as they were making these analyses, the first Nascent Soul cultivator abruptly descended upon the venue!

He had fiery red long hair that fell loosely over his shoulders, looking like a burning flag.

He wore a crimson-gold long robe embroidered with nine Fire Dragons. Their heads were raised proudly toward the sky, their claws flared like hooks, and their tails intertwined like vines.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord!

He strode into the hall and stood firmly in the center. With his hands clasped behind his back, he swept his gaze around the room before locking eyes on Ning Zhuo.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's expression was gloomy, his brows tightly furrowed. He scrutinized Ning Zhuo with a heavily condescending demeanor.

Ning Zhuo, however, looked at the Nine Fires Dragon Lord with eager expectation. Without the slightest fear, he said faintly, "For the Dragon Lord to grace us with your presence, I presume you have thought things through thoroughly?"

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord snorted coldly and said through gritted teeth, "Ning Zhuo, regarding your establishment of an alliance this time, I have decided to join."

As soon as these words were spoken, the entire hall was stunned!

The guests looked at one another in dismay. Some had their eyes wide open, others their mouths agape, and some tilted their teacups, spilling tea.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord!

A Nascent Soul-level Demon Cultivator, a Guest Elder of Danxia Peak, a powerhouse possessing the Fire Dragon bloodline. Such an existence actually wants to join Ning Zhuo's alliance?

This wasn't observing the ceremony, nor was it offering congratulations. This was enlisting!

Ye Qingming, Jin Mantang, and Li Ku all saw the solemn expressions on each other's faces.

The moment a Nascent Soul cultivator joined, the Nanming Stronghold instantly became one of the formidable factions within Flowing Cloud Peak, no longer allowing for even the slightest bit of underestimation.

Ning Zhuo, however, did not show the slightest hint of joy. He cupped his fists and said, "I am overwhelmed by the Dragon Lord's favor. If no other Nascent Soul cultivators join, then you, Dragon Lord, will be the Grand Chieftain of my Nanming Stronghold."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord snorted coldly and said nothing, silently acquiescing.

He hadn't wanted to join this tiny stronghold, but who told him to owe money to the black market?
