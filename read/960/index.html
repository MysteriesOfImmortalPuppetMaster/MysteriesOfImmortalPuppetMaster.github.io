Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 960: Hidden Reward

Ning Zhuo had made tremendous progress compared to before he participated in the minor trial.

His fingertips landed on the small cloud nursery formation plate, infusing it with spiritual energy.

The next moment, the formation plate levitated.

Patterned cloud sand paved the bottom layer, resembling a circle of dense formation lines. Ning Zhuo used his Water Element spiritual energy to support the water vapor at the base, and then used shape-setting cloud ink to outline the cloud paths. Soon, a miniature cloud nursery took shape in mid-air.

Ning Zhuo's small cloud nursery was divided into three layers.

The bottom layer gathered water; the water vapor was steady, like the base of an alchemy furnace.

The middle layer nurtured clouds; several nascent clouds inhaled and exhaled cloud grain, with cold, warmth, moisture, and dryness each in its proper place.

The top layer transformed attributes; a white cloud slowly shifted between the three states of cloud, rain, and mist. Beside it, a wisp of rosy twilight tentatively merged into it without destroying the cloud core.

The outer ring was a grazing path where ten small clouds drifted in sequence, advancing and retreating in an orderly manner.

The entire cloud nursery resembled a void alchemy furnace, yet also looked like an exquisite Mechanism formation plate.

Clouds were born within it, nurtured within it, transformed within it, and returned within it.

The entire venue was utterly silent.

Yun Sanggu looked at that small cloud nursery, remaining silent for a long time.

After a long while, she finally announced, "Ning Zhuo, having passed all twenty trials, ranks first in this Rising Cloud Minor Trial."

Some were envious, some marveled, and others had complex expressions.

Ning Zhuo retracted his hand and bowed to Yun Sanggu, "Thank you for your guidance, Senior."

Yun Sanggu replied, "You passed the trials relying on yourself."

The standard rewards were quickly distributed.

One Clear Dew Cloud-Generating Plate, one box of Morning Mist Water Droplets, three Light Rain Breath-Listening Talismans, three clumps of Cloud Womb Cotton, one bottle of Warm Rosy Powder, one Small Cloud Nurturing Nursery Plate, three Weather-Tuning Water Droplets, one portion each of Rosy Dye Powder, Ink Mist Liquid, and Frost White Cloud Fluff, a strand of Rain Mother Cloud Silk, a Cloud-Herding Fine Cord, a strand of Sleeve-Returning Cloud Silk, ten sticks of Cloud-Attracting Incense, a segment of White Bridge Cloud Bone, one Cloud Sealing Box, and three scrolls of Cloud Grain recipes.

These rewards piled up in front of Ning Zhuo, making many participants watch with burning envy.

Especially the White Bridge Cloud Bone and the Cloud Sealing Box, both of which were highly practical items. The White Bridge Cloud Bone could be refined into cloud steps or cloud bridges, and could also be combined with Mechanical arts. The Cloud Sealing Box could store living clouds, perfectly suiting Ning Zhuo's future needs to nurture his own cloud materials.

Ning Zhuo collected them one by one, his expression neither arrogant nor impatient.

Yun Sanggu publicly announced the dismissal, but secretly transmitted a message to him via divine sense.

Ning Zhuo pretended to leave but quietly returned halfway.

Guided by a disciple of the Cloud Nursery Courtyard, he detoured into a cloud corridor in the rear.

The cloud corridor was quiet and secluded.

Cloud curtains hung low on both sides, isolating the sounds of the outside world. Walking deep into it, an inconspicuous small courtyard appeared before him. There was no plaque above the courtyard door, only an old stone carved with blurry cloud patterns.

Yun Sanggu was already waiting there.

At this moment, there were no other organizers beside her; she was alone.

Ning Zhuo cupped his fists and said, "Senior called this junior here. Is there another trial?"

Yun Sanggu looked at him and said, "The Rising Cloud Minor Trial has a hidden tradition. It is not activated every time, nor is it guaranteed for the first-place winner. Today, you passed all twenty trials, and instead of merely copying the old methods, you formed your own understanding. According to the old rules, you may attempt it once."

Ning Zhuo's heart stirred. "Attempt what?"

"Cloud Nature," Yun Sanggu replied.

Ning Zhuo's eyes slightly narrowed.

Yun Sanggu explained, "Cloud materials can be bought, cloud artifacts can be refined, and cloud grains can be mixed. Only Cloud Nature cannot be forced. Cloud Nature is the root of a living cloud. When fire nature enters a cloud, it becomes a fire cloud; when lightning nature enters, it becomes a thundercloud; when water nature enters, it becomes a cumulonimbus rain cloud; when cold nature enters, it becomes a frost cloud; when fiendish nature enters, it becomes a fiend cloud."

Pausing here, Yun Sanggu looked at Ning Zhuo, "Do you know what 'Nature' is?"

Ning Zhuo thought for a moment and wanted to answer, but held back the impulse. With a slightly solemn expression, he cupped his hands and said, "Please enlighten me, Senior."

Yun Sanggu said, "Nature refers to one's intrinsic properties. All things are born between Heaven and Earth, taking form from Qi and deriving their Nature from the underlying laws. Stone has the nature of stone, thus it is dense and heavy; water has the nature of water, thus it moisturizes and flows downward; fire has the nature of fire, thus it flares upward and refines; lightning has the nature of lightning, thus it is explosive, swift, deadly, and destroys evil."

"In the Cultivation World, what we call 'Nature' does not merely refer to the good and evil of human hearts, nor just to one's temper and emotions. Rather, it refers to the most fundamental, habitual, and hardest-to-defy tendency within an entity."

As she spoke, she flipped her palm.

A tiny thundercloud floated out from her sleeve. The thundercloud was only the size of a fist, but within its grayish-white core, fine streaks of lightning wandered. With every flash of lightning, the edges of the cloud would slightly explode before rapidly gathering back together.

Yun Sanggu said, "This is lightning nature. The nature of lightning and electricity is shocking, rapid, fierce, and capable of shattering turbidity. If you force it to be nurtured into a rain cloud, it can still drop rain, but the water will inevitably contain the intent of electricity. A moment of carelessness could startle the cloud and cause it to blast apart. This is because at its very foundation, it holds lightning nature."

She then pinched out a wisp of pale red rosy cloud.

The rosy cloud had a warm and gentle hue, not fierce at all, yet it constantly drifted upward. Its edges were lined with a fine golden light, like the evening glow reflecting on a mountain.

Yun Sanggu said, "This is fire nature. Fire nature flares upward, loves to ascend, excels at refining, can illuminate objects, and can also burn them. If fire nature enters a cloud, it becomes a fire cloud. Fire clouds are not necessarily all ferocious. Some only illuminate the cloud core, warming and nourishing the rosy veins; while others scorch the cloud and damage its womb, easily incinerating all its water vapor. The difference lies not in the name, but in the depth of the fire nature and the mastery of the fire's intensity."

Ning Zhuo's heart trembled slightly as he suddenly thought of the Nine Fires of the Nine Fire Dragon Lord.

Earth fire is heavy and dense; wood fire brings growth; lightning fire shocks and trembles; heart fire disturbs the spirit; pill fire gently refines; demonic fire corrodes; dragon fire crushes from above. The so-called Nine Fires were, in fact, flames containing nine different types of natures!

Seeing the change in his eyes, Yun Sanggu knew he was moved and continued, "Daoist School texts often speak of the dual cultivation of nature and life. 'Life' refers to the physical body, qi and blood, lifespan, and foundation. 'Nature' refers to the true essence of the divine soul and the tendency of one's spiritual clarity. Applied to cloud materials, the principle is the same. The cloud body is its 'life', and the cloud nature is its 'nature'. If the cloud body scatters, it can still be reformed. But if the cloud nature is ruined, it is like a human heart veiled in dust. Even if the physical form remains, it is no longer the original living cloud."

She paused, then added, "The Confucian school speaks of innate nature and also of acquired temperamental nature. Innate nature is the foundation before it is dyed by the world; temperamental nature is the bias formed by the acquired environment, the contamination of spiritual energy, and the cultivator's nurturing. Cloud materials are the same. A newborn white cloud might just be a pure cloud womb. If nourished by lightning qi for a long time, it slowly develops a lightning nature. If smoked by the medicinal fumes of an alchemy furnace day after day, it might transform into a medicinal smoke cloud or a rosy pill cloud. If trapped in a land of yin fiends for ages, it will become a turbid cloud or a fiend cloud."

Ning Zhuo's heart stirred, and he whispered, "So, nature is not immutable?"

Yun Sanggu glanced at him and said, "Good question. Nature has its roots, but it also flows and changes. In the dharma discussions of the Buddhist Sect, it is often said that all phenomena lack fixed identity and arise from dependent origination. If you only look at an object's nature today, you easily become rigid. If you simply say that all things have no nature, it becomes too vague and empty. When a cultivator observes nature, they must see where it comes from, how it is nourished, and where it is going."

Ning Zhuo nodded, profoundly shaken. The way he looked at Yun Sanggu became completely different from before, thinking to himself, "This female cultivator is absolutely not simple!"

Yun Sanggu only had a Golden Core cultivation base, but her understanding of the Great Dao far exceeded Ning Zhuo's imagination. Ning Zhuo had his own understanding of "Nature," but hearing Yun Sanggu's explanation was like ascending to a whole new level.

Yun Sanggu had elaborated from the perspectives of the Daoist School, Confucian, and Buddhist Sect respectively. She spoke with assurance, operating from a high vantage point, with the three philosophies illuminating one another to provide a penetrating analysis that allowed Ning Zhuo to see to the very core!

Ning Zhuo cupped his fists and earnestly asked for guidance, "Then, Senior, what about spiritual energy?"

Yun Sanggu replied, "Spiritual energy is also like this. Spiritual energy is the nature of the spirit. Spirit is not merely the abundance or scarcity of spiritual qi, but the capacity for sensation, transformation, clarity, and autonomy. If a Mechanism possesses spiritual energy, it is not just a dead object operating, but can make subtle adjustments according to the situation. If a cloud possesses spiritual energy, it is not merely the gathering and dispersal of water vapor, but it can feel fear, understand hunger and fullness, and recognize its home."

Ning Zhuo's soul was touched once again.

Yun Sanggu steered the topic back, "Therefore, Cloud Nature is the deepest tendency of a cloud, without physical form. If a Cultivator obtains Cloud Nature, they must nurture that nature with their own body! Cloud Nature will reflect back upon oneself and amplify the traits within oneself that are similar to it. Those who obtain lightning cloud nature will see their decisiveness grow, but will easily become rash and fierce. Those who obtain fire cloud nature will see their imposing aura grow, but will easily become impetuous and hasty. Those who obtain cumulonimbus rain cloud nature will see their nurturing capacity grow, but will easily overthink. Those who obtain hidden cloud nature will see their forbearance grow, but will easily become overly secretive."

Having said this, Yun Sanggu stepped forward and led the way, "Follow me."

Ning Zhuo followed her for a distance until they arrived outside a small courtyard.

Yun Sanggu pushed open the courtyard door. The courtyard was empty, containing only an old formation.

The formation lines were carved into the bluestone floor. The traces were extremely faint, having weathered an unknown number of years. At the formation core was a shallow well. There was no water in the well, only faint white mist. The white mist rose but did not dissipate outside the courtyard, as if gently held by an invisible hand.

"This formation is named the Myriad Phenomena Cloud-Attracting Formation, and what it connects to is precisely the Myriad Phenomena Cloud Sea! After you enter the formation, you absolutely must not force anything; simply display your own disposition. If a Cloud Nature acknowledges you, it will approach you on its own."

"Go in."

Ning Zhuo's heart jolted, thinking to himself, "It connects to the Myriad Phenomena Cloud Sea?"

He rubbed his Mechanism Ring, which remained completely unresponsive.

With that, Ning Zhuo walked into the formation.

The bluestone formation lines lit up slightly.

The next moment, the scenery before his eyes drastically changed.

The small courtyard vanished, Yun Sanggu vanished, and the bluestone floor also disappeared.

Ning Zhuo stood alone amidst a boundless sea of clouds.

The cloud sea was vast and ethereal, stretching to the ends of the sky. In the distance, lightning snaked around, and purple-white electric arcs flashed within the heavy cloud layers. On another side, rosy clouds surged, with crimson-gold, scarlet, and orange-yellow interweaving into a brilliant cloud tide. Beneath his feet, the sound of rain echoed softly, as if countless drizzles were falling into an unseen abyss. Even further away, frost condensed into clouds, baleful energy floated and sank, and hidden clouds flickered in and out of sight.

Ning Zhuo did not move.

He knew that everything here was not ordinary cloud materials, but the echoes of Cloud Natures!

The first wisp to approach him was a lightning cloud nature.

Within the grayish-white cloud clump hid fine streaks of lightning. It circled Ning Zhuo's palm once, emitting a low thunderous rumble. It seemed to be attracted by Ning Zhuo's decisiveness and the slaughtering intent of his Mechanisms. Lightning flashed across Ning Zhuo's knuckles, bringing a numbing sting.

The second wisp to approach was a fire cloud nature.

Crimson cloud qi surged over, its edges appearing thoroughly burnt by the evening glow, carrying a warm fiery intent. It sensed the Fire Seed 'Blood Ember' within Ning Zhuo's body, and also sensed his newly comprehended fire techniques. When the cloud qi drew near, even Ning Zhuo's sleeves grew slightly hot.

The fire cloud nature was very suitable for cultivators of fire techniques.

If the Nine Fire Dragon Lord came here, he might be favored by this type of cloud nature.

The third wisp was a cumulonimbus rain cloud nature.

It approached silently, carrying a deep and heavy water intent. The water intent within Ning Zhuo's Water Element Kidney Temple faintly responded, almost instinctively wanting to accept it. The cumulonimbus rain cloud nature hovered around Ning Zhuo's knees, its core damp, as if hiding an unfallen downpour.

For a moment, Ning Zhuo was tempted.

The cumulonimbus rain cloud nature best matched his Water Element realm. If he obtained this nature, he would definitely make leaps and bounds in dealing with rain clouds, wet clouds, and gathering water into clouds.

Afterwards, hidden cloud nature, rosy cloud nature, and cold cloud nature also approached one after another.

The hidden cloud nature liked his forbearance, circling his sides three times, almost attaching to his shadow. The rosy cloud nature was attracted by his alchemy fire mastery, gently illuminating the edges of his sleeves. The cold cloud nature lingered at the edge of his sea of consciousness, seemingly admiring his calmness.

Countless cloud natures were attracted to him, surrounding his body. Yet, it seemed that after seeing through him, they suddenly departed!

Coming and going.

The Myriad Phenomena Cloud Sea, myriad kinds of Cloud Natures!

For a moment, it was as if Ning Zhuo was viewing flowers while riding a galloping horse - a dazzling and chaotic blur before his eyes.

After the many cloud natures retreated, Ning Zhuo stood alone in the center of the cloud sea, his surroundings completely empty.

In the outside world, within the old formation of the small courtyard, Yun Sanggu watched the changes in the formation lines, her brows furrowing slightly.

"It's been so long, hasn't he succeeded yet?" This was beyond her expectations.

Within the formation, Ning Zhuo's surroundings were completely empty. He looked at the cloud sea, somewhat at a loss, not knowing why all these cloud natures had left him.

Suddenly, a wisp of white was born from the depths of the cloud sea.

That white was extremely faint.

It had no lightning, no rosy hue, no trace of rain, no cold frost, and no baleful energy. It did not even possess any particularly strong imposing aura. It was just a small clump of ordinary white cloud - soft, thin, and clean, like an idle cloud that could be seen anywhere halfway up a mountain on a sunny day.

Ning Zhuo's mind stirred, and he subconsciously reached out his palm.

The white cloud naturally fell into his palm, so light it was almost weightless.

The next moment, it transformed into a soft wisp of white qi and sank into Ning Zhuo's meridians.

There was no thunderous roar.

There was no boundless rosy light.

There was no violent agony.

In a daze, Ning Zhuo returned to reality, finding that he was already standing outside the edges of the formation.

Yun Sanggu looked at him in astonishment, "You actually obtained the white cloud nature!"

Ning Zhuo was an absolute novice in this aspect and hurriedly asked for guidance.

Yun Sanggu did not hold back in her teaching: "Simply put, it is a blank slate. It lacks the lightning nature of a thundercloud, the fire nature of a fire cloud, and the water nature of a cumulonimbus rain cloud. It is impartial and unbiased, just like white paper. Precisely because it is blank, it is like drawing on white paper; it is extremely easy for it to be tainted by other properties, and subsequently transform into other cloud natures."

Ning Zhuo asked again, "Senior, how should I nurture this nature with my body?"

Yun Sanggu showed a rare moment of hesitation. "Cloud nature is closely connected to a cultivator's temperament. For example, if a cultivator is quick-tempered, they will attract a lightning cloud nature. If a cultivator is gloomy and overthinking, they will attract a rain cloud nature. As for your white cloud nature..."

"In the future, if your heart becomes turbid, it will become turbid. If your heart becomes restless, it will become restless. If your foundation is mixed, it will become chaotic. If your temperament loses balance, it will lose its true form."

"Alas, this matter is extremely rare, so it's hard for me to teach you. You might as well take a look at this Jade Slip."

Ning Zhuo took it and cast his divine sense into it. He discovered it was a roster and various records of past cultivators who had passed the formation test and obtained their respective cloud natures.

Wen Jinglei, a former outer disciple of the Heavenly Dipper Sect. In his youth, his temperament was upright and unyielding, never retreating from challenges. He participated in the Flying Cloud Assembly, entered the Myriad Phenomena Cloud-Attracting Formation, and a lightning cloud nature actively threw itself into his embrace. Afterwards, he cultivated lightning cloud methods for thirty years, forging the [Three-Strike Lightning Cloud Drum], which could hide lightning within clouds to be released upon hearing the drum. Once, during a night of demon tide, he sealed a mountain pass with three acres of lightning clouds, shocking and killing over seven hundred demonic beasts, saving twenty-nine fellow sect members.

After obtaining the lightning cloud nature, his decisiveness became even firmer and his spirit fiercer. In his twilight years, because he refused to yield, he had a dispute with his Dao Companion and they parted ways. Repenting on his deathbed, he left his dying words: "Lightning nature can shatter evil, but it can also shatter bonds of affection. Let future scholars be cautious."
