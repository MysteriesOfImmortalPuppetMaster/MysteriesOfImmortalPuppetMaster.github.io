Chapter 898: The First to Join

Tao Liweng did not think highly of Ning Zhuo at all.

Flowing Cloud Peak of the Myriad Manifestations Sect had always been the most fiercely contested and chaotic area within the sect.

As the saying goes, a gentleman does not stand under a collapsing wall, yet this hotheaded Ning Zhuo was actively smashing his head right into it!

"You say you are so highly regarded by Zhong Dao and Tuoba Huang, so wouldn't it be great to join the Demon Subjugation Hall or Myriad Beast Peak? It would be great to quietly cultivate and accumulate resources like me. Although you owe a lot of money, you're still young. What's the rush?"

"Look, even I haven't urged you to repay your debt right now! Just pay it back slowly, but whatever you do, don't be reckless."

These were all Tao Liweng's heartfelt thoughts.

He really wanted to voice them out, but seeing Ning Zhuo's implicitly unruly and confident expression, he knew that speaking bluntly would be useless, so he could only try his best to hold them in.

Suddenly, a flash of inspiration hit Tao Liweng's mind. "Nine Fire Dragon Lord? Little Friend Ning Zhuo, could that Nascent Soul cultivator be the Nine Fire Dragon Lord?"

"Do you know what situation he's in right now?"

"He previously participated in the Minor Trial of the Fire Furnace, not hesitating to borrow heavily from the black market and taking on an extremely high quota. His current situation is very troublesome, so he's egging you on to occupy Flowing Cloud Peak."

"Is he even human? No, he's not."

Even as Tao Liweng said this, he was trying his best to exercise restraint.

Ning Zhuo raised his eyebrows, deliberately provoking him, "I know. He's a Demon Cultivator. He's a dragon."

Tao Liweng almost rolled his eyes. His Adam's apple bobbed as he narrowly suppressed the words screaming in his heart: "Is that what I meant?! My point is, he's just a gambling addict!"

Tao Liweng panted a few times and earnestly advised, "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, you're still too young. Sometimes, you may know a person's face, but not their heart. You and the Nine Fire Dragon Lord are different; he has no clan, but you have the backing of your clan."

Because Ning Zhuo's background had still not been investigated thoroughly, it resulted in everyone currently overestimating the Ning Clan.

Ning Zhuo coldly snorted, "Let the clan repay the debt? I've figured it out long ago! In the end, I'll still be the one paying the price. The clan might even take the opportunity to demand my Nanming Fire Furnace."

"Why do you think I gave up the good life of a Young Master in my clan to travel thousands of miles to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect?"

"Isn't it because there's no freedom at home? Here, I can be completely free, start from scratch, and prove myself! Especially now that I have the Nanming Fire Furnace. This is Heaven favoring me, a special arrangement made just for me."

"If one does not take what Heaven gives, they will be punished for it!"

"I, Ning Zhuo, will definitely carve out my own world on Flowing Cloud Peak!!"

When Ning Zhuo said that last sentence, his pitch was high, almost shouting.

Tao Liweng was so angry that his lips trembled. He thought to himself, "This brat's head is in the clouds! He's completely lost it; he has zero self-awareness!! Go ahead and crash! Only when his head is cracked and bleeding will he truly recognize himself and realize that occupying Flowing Cloud Peak and participating in the Flying Cloud Assembly are two entirely different things!"

But unfortunately...

Unfortunately, Ning Zhuo held the Nanming Fire Furnace in his hands!

And he owed a massive debt!

More than half of Tao Liweng's life savings were tied up in this.

If they had no relationship, Tao Liweng would have flicked his sleeves and left after exchanging a few words. But now, he had to think of a way to salvage his losses.

Just as he was racking his brain trying to figure out how to persuade Ning Zhuo to abandon this unrealistic plan, Ning Zhuo spoke again.

"Tao Liweng." This casual tone immediately made the corner of Tao Liweng's eye twitch.

Previously, Ning Zhuo still addressed him as "Daoist Friend Tao." Now, he was directly calling him by his name, becoming increasingly impolite.

"I know you want to persuade me, and thank you, but drop it. I've already made my decision, and there's no turning back."

"Look at this."

Tao Liweng subconsciously took the Jade Slip. "What is this?"

Ning Zhuo replied, "This is the secret contract I signed with Zhong Limei; it's an agreement I reached with Danxia Peak. Look, I'm fully prepared. I'm not even going to the Demon Subjugation Hall anymore; I'm going to occupy Flowing Cloud Peak!"

Tao Liweng's heart skipped a beat upon hearing this. He quickly scanned it with his divine sense, causing his pupils to shrink slightly, and his hands to tremble faintly in anger.

How infuriating.

Ning Zhuo didn't understand anything at all and acted on his own!

Yet Tao Liweng couldn't directly intervene to stop him.

It was clearly a secret contract, but instead of hiding it away, Ning Zhuo was showing it off everywhere.

Showing it to Tao Liweng this time served as an extremely heavyweight piece of evidence, thoroughly demonstrating his firm determination to dive headfirst into the vortex that was Flowing Cloud Peak!

After reading the contents of the Jade Slip, Tao Liweng no longer had any doubts about Ning Zhuo's plan regarding Flowing Cloud Peak!

"It's over, I can't stop him." A wave of despair washed over Tao Liweng's heart.

Ning Zhuo observed his expression and felt quite disappointed inside - as a rogue cultivator who had always cultivated in seclusion, Tao Liweng's political awareness was simply too low. He still hadn't noticed the deeper implications.

Ning Zhuo needed him to see those deeper implications in order to proceed with his plan.

However, Tao Liweng said, "Ning Zhuo, have you ever thought that jumping into the vortex of Flowing Cloud Peak is easy, but getting out will be difficult?"

"Don't just look at how happily the Nine Fire Dragon Lord is chatting with you right now; he's asking a favor of you. In the future, when you've repaired the Nanming Fire Furnace and finally realize the truth, do you think the Nine Fire Dragon Lord will allow you to back out?"

"Have you ever considered that you might even lose your Nanming Fire Furnace?!"

These words carried an underlying meaning - If the Nanming Fire Furnace is lost, what will happen to me, your creditor? You acknowledge these debts, but the new owner probably won't.

Despite Tao Liweng's earnest advice and continuous persuasion, from beginning to end, he only ever cared about his own interests.

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, revealing a look of hesitation for the first time.

Observing his expression, a look of joy instantly appeared on Tao Liweng's face. "Thank Heaven, this big-headed kid is finally starting to think seriously."

Seeing Tao Liweng's joyful expression, Ning Zhuo instantly knew that relying on Tao Liweng to figure it out himself would be too difficult.

He sighed deeply and had no choice but to drop a hint, "Tao Liweng, you keep saying 'Nine Fire Dragon Lord' this and 'Nine Fire Dragon Lord' that, but who told you he was that mysterious cultivator?"

Tao Liweng froze instantly. "If not him, then who?"

Subconsciously following Ning Zhuo's gaze, he looked at the Jade Slip in his hand and scoffed internally. "Zhong Limei? Hehe, how could that... be, be possible?"

As soon as this thought surfaced, it immediately gave rise to even more suspicions.

"Why is Ning Zhuo so confident about occupying Flowing Cloud Peak?"

"Why would Danxia Peak voluntarily waive so much debt? Half of it was slashed away just like that. Is it really just to prevent Ning Zhuo from joining the Demon Subjugation Hall?"

"What if, what if Danxia Peak still wants the Nanming Fire Furnace, but Ning Zhuo refuses to sell? Could it be they saw through Ning Zhuo's true nature, so they stoked his ambition and encouraged him to occupy Flowing Cloud Peak?"

"That way, signing the secret contract is just a lure to make Ning Zhuo stop thinking about the Demon Subjugation Hall and focus solely on causing trouble at Flowing Cloud Peak!"

"But in reality, Danxia Peak has already laid a trap there, waiting for Ning Zhuo to dive right in and ultimately lose the Nanming Fire Furnace. No, it's likely even more ruthless. Using this method, Danxia Peak will pocket Ning Zhuo from another angle, acquiring both the person and the furnace!"

Thinking up to this point, Tao Liweng suddenly felt that Danxia Peak was highly likely the mastermind behind the scenes, and Zhong Limei, precisely because he was an elder of Danxia Peak, had been ordered to lead Ning Zhuo to storm Flowing Cloud Peak.

Seems like he finally thought of that layer. Seeing Tao Liweng's expression fluctuating, Ning Zhuo let out a sigh of relief internally.

He counted silently for a few seconds before suddenly saying, "Tao Liweng, I've already said what I should say, and I definitely won't say what I shouldn't."

"Am I stupid? Would I risk going to Flowing Cloud Peak if the prospects were unclear?"

When these words landed in Tao Liweng's mind, they were instantly "translated" into a completely different meaning.

First, there were indeed many things Ning Zhuo couldn't say out loud. Because it concerned Danxia Peak, Danxia Peak certainly had its own schemes and must have made Ning Zhuo keep his mouth shut.

Second, Ning Zhuo felt his future was bright and had strong confidence precisely because he was cooperating with a main peak like Danxia Peak. Sigh, this kid has lost himself; he's already been completely seen through.

Ning Zhuo said in a tone of exasperation, "Joining me will really benefit you! For me, I can also use this method to repay my debt. It's a win-win for everyone."

"If you don't have this intention, then forget it. But when the time comes, don't say I dragged out my payments and didn't repay you."

"I've already warned you in advance!"

"So, before we part ways, we must also sign a secret contract stipulating these matters, lest you ruin my reputation in the future."

Tao Liweng was so angry he wanted to curse, feeling quite furious in his heart. "How could I possibly sign this kind of secret contract with you?!"

He glared at Ning Zhuo: "For this matter, count me in!"

Ning Zhuo revealed a look of surprise. "What do you mean? You changed your mind?"

If it were the Nine Fire Dragon Lord, Tao Liweng wouldn't have much confidence. The other party wasn't human; he was a gambling addict. He might even use his own subordinates as stakes.

But if it was Danxia Peak, one of the Eight Peaks, pulling the strings behind the scenes, that was a completely different story!

Tao Liweng still didn't think highly of Ning Zhuo's prospects, but he felt the waters here were very deep, and there was something fishy going on.

For the sake of the bulk of his life savings, he decided to join. He didn't genuinely want to do any work; at most, he just wanted to "get a closer look."

But this was enough.

Immediately, before leaving, Tao Liweng signed a secret contract with Ning Zhuo. The secret contract was very loose, even vague. Both parties merely agreed to form an alliance and then team up to occupy Flowing Cloud Peak.

Tao Liweng left with a heavy heart, utterly unaware that he was actually the very first person to join the cause.

Afterward, Ning Zhuo successively had detailed discussions with several Golden Core cultivators: Baicao Weng, Jiang Ping, Shen Hongyao, and Qin Ke, confirming the specific amounts of his debts.

Ning Zhuo informed all of these people about his plan to fight for Flowing Cloud Peak, but not a single one agreed to join.

Not long after, Ning Zhuo successfully negotiated with almost all the Golden Core cultivators. The reason it was "almost" was that Ning Zhuo couldn't contact Yun Youzi. The latter had already left the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate and continued his wandering.

It was now the Foundation Establishment cultivators' turn.

The first one was none other than Situ Xing.

Situ Xing had been waiting for a long time. The very first time he laid eyes on Ning Zhuo, he said, "Let's forget about calculating debts for now and have a fight. Whoever wins is the boss!"
