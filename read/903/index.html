Chapter 903: Meteor Slash

Situ Xing sank into darkness.

"Wake up, Xing'er, wake up." From within the boundless and endless darkness, an ethereal call echoed.

"Wh-where am I?" Situ Xing's consciousness gathered.

He looked around and saw the night sky like ink, spreading for ten thousand miles with neither sun nor moon in sight. There was only the boundless darkness pressing heavily down upon him, making him feel exceedingly suffocated and hopeless.

All was dead silent.

"I... I seem to have forgotten something." Situ Xing tried hard to remember.

Just then, within the deep night, a faint glimmer of light suddenly lit up.

The light was extremely weak, like a flickering candle in the wind, liable to go out at any moment.

Immediately after, a second point, a third point, a fourth point...

Countless specks of light lit up one after another against the dark canopy, densely packed and covering the sky.

It was as if someone had sequentially ignited tens of millions of lamps in the darkness, one after another, patch by patch, spreading from nearby to the distance, extending from one end of the horizon to the other.

In the blink of an eye, the originally empty night sky was filled with these countless specks of light.

At first, they hung quietly high above, utterly motionless, twinkling with a cold light. Then, they began to slowly grow larger.

A low, rumbling sound echoed, exceedingly distant and soft, but soon, as the points of light expanded, the sound also grew louder, like a grand army of thousands of horses galloping forth.

At first, it was just two or three specks of light slipping from the highest point of the firmament, trailing slender tails of light as they slowly plummeted downward.

Immediately following that was an overwhelming barrage!

Tens of thousands of meteors broke free from the sky's restraints at the exact same moment, crashing down like a torrential downpour.

Trailing blazing light-tails of varying lengths, they carried world-destroying might, tearing through the vast sky and cutting open the darkness!

Thus, the entire deep night was illuminated by this magnificent meteor shower.

The starlight grew ever more brilliant - explosive, scorching, and maddening!

Innumerable meteors dragged out countless pillars of light, plunging straight down from the depths of the Nine Heavens, lighting up everything as bright as day, leaving not even the darkest corner a place to hide.

Situ Xing floated, looking up, feeling as though the entire firmament was collapsing and falling!

A familiar voice rang in his ears once more: "Little Xing, comprehend this well. This is one of our clan's foundation inheritances - the Heavenly Meteor Starfall Chart. Visualize it carefully. According to clan rules, before the age of ten, you only have this one chance."

Situ Xing's consciousness gave a light jolt. "Right, I am currently visualizing the Heavenly Meteor Starfall Chart."

He hurriedly focused his attention, swept away all distracting thoughts, and looked closely.

The air was torn apart, emitting sharp whistling sounds that converged into a deafening wave of noise, like ten thousand ghosts crying at once, like a hundred beasts roaring together!

The entire universe was trembling.

Situ Xing was also swaying, the world spinning around him!

This meteor shower was so massive; the nearby meteors were as large as mountains, while the distant ones were the size of millstones. But what attracted Situ Xing the most was the one charging at the very forefront.

It was only medium-sized, its entire body burning fiercely with azure flames, trailing a brilliant tail of fire.

Its surface was covered in cracks, even oozing a magma-like slurry. As it fell, fragments continually peeled off from the main body, turning into a sky full of sparks before dissipating into the night.

Its speed was the fastest, its charge the fiercest.

It was shrinking at a speed visible to the naked eye.

Situ Xing felt that it cared not at all. Or rather, it intended to die on the path of its charge, erupting with the most resplendent radiance of its life!

Situ Xing's gaze was firmly drawn to it, and an indescribable emotion suddenly surged in his heart.

"Little Xing, what have you comprehended?" that voice asked him again.

Situ Xing replied, "It is a flying sword technique called the Meteor Slash."

The voice fell silent for a moment. "Little Xing, to execute this flying sword move, you must achieve unity of body and sword. Although its power is formidable, it is a self-sacrificing strike, putting all your eggs in one basket. If you use it in the future, you must be extremely cautious."

Situ Xing was just about to respond.

Boom!

A meteor smashed down, blowing his world to pieces.

He returned once more to the final moments of his clash with Ning Zhuo.

Exhaustion, an intense exhaustion, made his vision go black.

His physical stamina was nearly depleted, and his spirit was constantly approaching its limits. Every time he was on the brink of collapse, he pulled himself back by swallowing Medicinal Pills and activating treasures.

Every inch of his bones was wailing, every strand of his meridians was trembling.

He knew he could hold on no longer.

But Ning Zhuo was still sitting upright on the shaft of his mechanical chariot, unleashing an endless barrage of attacks, showing not the slightest sign of discouragement or exhaustion.

Admit defeat and step down with dignity?

Or persist, as the opponent might also be an arrow at the end of its flight, merely feigning it convincingly?

After a lightning-fast thought, Situ Xing chose to gather his final strength into his body, achieve unity of man and sword, and unleash the Meteor Slash!

This was his strongest strike, and also his final strike.

Succeed, or die trying.

He charged forward!

"If I strike Ning Zhuo, I might just seize this victory!"

However, walls of ice rose before him one after another.

Bang, bang, bang.

He had no desire to change direction at all, nor was he able to.

The flying sword technique Meteor Slash was exactly like this - forging ahead without turning back!

The first beat of the drum rouses the spirit, the second weakens it, and the third exhausts it.

In the end, he was stopped in his tracks, trapped by a wall of ice.

"No!" He wanted to charge again, but there was not a trace of spare energy left in his body.

With intense unwillingness, he watched helplessly as he was frozen in ice.

Not far away, the image of Snow-Colored Maiden Hui was so clear. And Ning Zhuo's face was blurred behind Snow-Colored Maiden Hui, appearing completely out of reach.

Situ Xing had gone all out, burning himself to exchange for his strongest strike, yet it had stopped halfway.

"I didn't even touch the corner of Ning Zhuo's clothes!"

Indignation.

An intense indignation burned like a ball of fire within Situ Xing's heart.

He struggled fiercely with all his might -

He suddenly sat up from the bed, his eyes glaring and his teeth clenched!

Hearing this commotion, Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong hurriedly rushed into the room, both of them overjoyed.

The latter blurted out, "Brother Situ, you're awake!"

Situ Xing froze for a few breaths before finally realizing: his battle with Ning Zhuo had already ended, and he had just woken up from a coma.

"I... lost." Situ Xing's expression was no longer ferocious, his clenched fists were forced to loosen, and he began to pant heavily.

Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong came to the bedside, neither speaking.

The room fell into silence for a time.

Situ Xing's mind spun faster and faster as he quickly recalled the battle between himself and Ning Zhuo.

"This battle is perhaps the most intense clash of my entire life."

"I fought with all my strength. Even though I lost... I have no regrets."

"Ning Zhuo's combat strength..." At this point, Situ Xing couldn't continue.

During the battle, immersed in it, he hadn't realized it. Now, reviewing it in hindsight, he discovered that Ning Zhuo had deliberately yielded in far too many places. The entire flow of the battle had actually been controlled by Ning Zhuo the whole time.

Situ Xing looked up at Lin Jinglong, a rare trace of resentment in his eyes. "Brother Lin, didn't you tell me earlier that you and Ning Zhuo fought to a draw?"

Lin Jinglong coughed twice and smiled awkwardly. "We only exchanged pointers, just stopped before going too far."

Situ Xing: "..."

Shen Xi interjected, "Brother Situ, what are your plans moving forward?"

Situ Xing relaxed and leaned back against the headboard, letting out a bitter smile. "I prepared for this battle with everything I had, borrowing numerous treasures and beast pets from my clan in advance, but the result was that I was still no match for Ning Zhuo."

"Ning Zhuo yielded to me in many places, leaving me with absolutely no excuses after my defeat."

"He achieved his goal and also proved that his previous actions were neither him being arrogant nor underestimating me, but that he truly possessed the strength."

"I accept my defeat and will honor the bet; I will establish my own independent faction."

Saying this, he looked at Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong. "You two, what are your thoughts?"

A trace of nervousness arose in Situ Xing's heart.

He, Shen Xi, and Lin Jinglong were both competitors and friends of many years. If he had their help, he would have much greater certainty in completing the various missions he took on in the Flying Cloud Assembly moving forward, and his efficiency would greatly increase.

As the saying goes, a great hero needs three good helpers. If one were to fight completely alone, they might as well join the Demonic Path - what was the point of mingling in the Righteous Path?

Situ Xing, Shen Xi, and Lin Jinglong all had extraordinary backgrounds; they were the Foundation Establishment figureheads of their respective clans. Their relationship was one of mutual competition and mutual success.

Faced with Situ Xing's inquiry, Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong exchanged a glance and indeed did not respond directly.

Seeing this, Situ Xing's heart suddenly grew cold.

Shen Xi said, "Brother Situ, I heard from Ning Zhuo that when you challenged him, you said, 'Whoever wins is the boss.' If you truly want to honor the bet, just separating from us and starting your own faction isn't enough."

Situ Xing let out a long sigh. "In front of you two, I'll speak the truth."

"I prepared so thoroughly before the battle, so when I challenged Ning Zhuo, I never thought I would lose, which is why I said that."

"As a result of losing, I must strike out on my own."

"My personal reputation is a small matter, but I am the Foundation Establishment figurehead of my clan. Submitting to another and dragging down my clan's reputation is unacceptable."

"Ning Zhuo's background is currently unknown. If I were to serve under him, how would outsiders view me? How would they view the Situ Clan?"

Shen Xi furrowed his brows slightly. "Then how will you handle your promise to Ning Zhuo? If he exposes it, I'm afraid you'll be seen as a treacherous and untrustworthy person, which will damage your clan's reputation even more."

Situ Xing's brows locked together tightly. "Ning Zhuo is an upright gentleman, a fellow member of the Righteous Path. He will definitely be able to understand me. Thus, if I discuss it with him, I can probably settle the matter by paying him some resources."

Shen Xi sighed softly. "That is your assumption, but in all things, you must consider the worst-case scenario. What if Ning Zhuo stubbornly refuses?"

Situ Xing revealed a look of worry. "Then it would be difficult."

He suddenly realized something. "Does Brother Shen have another idea, or are you trying to persuade me? It's actually quite simple. Am I someone who breaks promises on a whim? What I worry about is my clan's reputation. If Ning Zhuo has a profound background, then serving as his subordinate wouldn't be impossible."

He thought for a moment. "Even if Ning Zhuo's background isn't deep and he isn't from a super faction, as long as there is a Nascent Soul expert holding the fort, it's acceptable."

Lin Jinglong let out a sigh.

Situ Xing looked at him.

Lin Jinglong's gaze was profound. "Brother Situ, Brother Ning has already anticipated your reaction. But the current situation is somewhat complicated. You should probably speak with Brother Ning first."
