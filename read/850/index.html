Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 850: Demonic Path Destiny

It is not easy to attain a Demonic Path realm.

To be precise, elevating any realm is very difficult.

"I successively received the True Intent infusions from the Five Elements Divine Lord, Lord Qingwu, and my Boss. These were all immense, once-in-a-century fortuitous encounters."

"Now, acquiring a Demonic Path realm will be extremely difficult."

"On one hand, Demonic Path cultivators usually act alone and consider themselves supreme in the world. How could they possibly make such a sacrifice to infuse their True Intent into someone else?"

"On the other hand, my own disposition is upright, and I am filled with righteous qi. I have an exceptional talent for Buddhist Sect Cultivation Arts. The cultivation methods I am least adept at are those of the Demonic Path."

As Ning Zhuo cultivated along his journey, traveling the world and expanding his horizons, he also gained a deeper understanding of himself.

"A Demonic Path realm cannot be extravagantly hoped for."

"The most stable and reliable method is to seek the life experiences of Demonic Path cultivators."

"Then, by summarizing those experiences and accumulating them over time, I can strive for a quantitative change to trigger a qualitative transformation!"

Ning Zhuo thought again of the White Paper Immortal City and the Underworld.

Deep in his heart, he couldn't help but feel an impulse to return to the Ghostly Black Marshlands, harvest demonic souls on a massive scale, eradicate evil, and uphold the Righteous Path!

But he immediately suppressed this urge.

He knew clearly in his heart: joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect this time was the correct choice. Without putting down roots here, even if he took over the White Paper Immortal City in the future, he would face constant attacks and countless hardships, making it entirely impossible to cultivate in peace.

"Joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect and finding the most suitable entry point for myself is what the overall situation requires!"

"However, I still hold a captive in my hands who cultivates a Blood Path Demonic Art."

Ning Zhuo took out a Red Flower Dharma Aspect.

He had three captives; two of them were restricted and imprisoned by the Red Flower Dharma Aspect.

Among them, Wu Hen had already undergone a mutation and was essentially ruined, becoming a hidden danger that Ning Zhuo monitored at all times.

The other was Lin You.

At this moment, the flower bud had become so thin it was nearly transparent.

Through the petals, Ning Zhuo could faintly see a figure curled up inside. Lin You remained motionless, huddled within the flower bud in a twisted posture, like a corpse. His complexion was as pale as paper, his lips devoid of blood, his eyes tightly shut, and his chest showed almost no signs of rising and falling.

When Sun Lingtong entered, he saw Ning Zhuo studying the Red Flower Dharma Aspect.

"Boss," Ning Zhuo revealed a smile.

"Here, for you." Sun Lingtong tossed a papercraft substitute face directly to Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo didn't catch it immediately. "Boss, you should keep this to protect yourself."

Sun Lingtong shook his head. "My Theft Path Methods are endless, and my ability to dodge attacks is much higher than yours. Moreover, you'll be in the limelight more and more, and countless people will be keeping an eye on you. Having a few more backup measures is definitely the right move!"

Ning Zhuo never intended to stand on ceremony with Sun Lingtong, so he nodded right away. "That makes sense. When you have any major operations, Boss, I'll return this to you."

Ning Zhuo carefully examined the papercraft substitute face.

The mask was very light, almost weightless. The material was white paper, but it felt smooth and warm to the touch, with faint patterns flowing across its surface.

This was exactly the result of papercraft techniques.

It reminded Ning Zhuo of the Profound Primordial Scholar, the Great Primordial Form-Tailoring Manual he had obtained, and those Paper Puppet armies secretly stationed in the Underworld by the White Paper Immortal City.

"I also need to learn this papercrafting technique; it will definitely be very useful to me in the future."

"Sigh, it's a pity my time is so limited. Papercrafting will have to be pushed to the back of the line."

Ning Zhuo put away the mask.

He didn't need Sun Lingtong to explain; he already knew all about Sun Lingtong's numerous experiences today. This was all because Life Hanging by a Thread allowed Ning Zhuo to observe in real-time. If a crisis occurred, Ning Zhuo could use the Spirit Communication Mirror, aided by the Divine Art, to rapidly pull Sun Lingtong back to his side.

Sun Lingtong said, "I encountered a Cloud-Stirring Minor Trial today, it was very interesting. The participating cultivators have to step onto the Talisman Dueling Platform, take turns making moves, craft talismans on the spot, and use them right there."

"I do have some talisman-crafting methods, but my level isn't high, so I need someone else to assist me."

"Little Zhuo, Qing Chi only listens to you. You should make some arrangements and send her out to tackle the trial and have some fun with me."

Ning Zhuo had been cultivating these past few days, actively utilizing his soaring Formation Dao realm state while also striving to explore the widespread applications of the Luoshu in his daily cultivation.

Ning Zhuo was slightly taken aback and asked, "What is Qing Chi doing?"

Sun Lingtong replied, "Didn't you teach her talisman crafting to pass the time? This girl has been burying her head in studying talisman making, and she's made great progress."

Ning Zhuo let out an "Oh" and said with gratification, "Before she lost her memory, she was impatient and always in a fiery rush. This amnesia has actually made her more steady. It truly is a case of blessings and misfortunes coming together. This will be quite beneficial for her future cultivation."

At this point, a look of hesitation appeared on his face.

Sun Lingtong knew very well what Ning Zhuo was worried about, so he immediately spoke up, "Little Zhuo, if you're afraid of Qing Chi encountering an accident, just plant Life Hanging by a Thread on her. When the time comes, you can use the Spirit Communication Mirror to pull her back to your side too."

Ning Zhuo's eyes flickered slightly. "During the battle with the Forgetful River Prefecture Lord last time, my Life Hanging by a Thread was broken by Qing Chi. It's likely because of her mysterious Innate Talent."

Sun Lingtong nodded, affirming with an "Mm."

He agreed with Ning Zhuo's speculation.

Qing Chi's Innate Talent was also very mysterious. The Azure Flame possessed a tyrannical might, able to forcefully refine even a National Treasure like the Nether Dragon Scissors. This battle record was simply unimaginable!

In contrast, even though Ning Zhuo had obtained the National Treasure, the Lord of the Forgetful River Seal, he couldn't find any way to use it.

He had also considered using Qing Chi's Azure Flame to work on the Lord of the Forgetful River Seal. However, after losing her memory, Qing Chi had also forgotten how to activate her Innate Talent Azure Flame.

Therefore, planting Life Hanging by a Thread on Qing Chi might not necessarily guarantee her safety.

She had merely forgotten how to use her Innate Talent; the talent itself still existed. It was highly likely to trigger passively and snap the thread of Life Hanging by a Thread once again.

Ning Zhuo sought stability and did not want any accidents to happen to Qing Chi.

On one hand, he was filled with gratitude and guilt towards Qing Chi, unwilling to let anything bad happen to this comrade-in-arms. On the other hand, Qing Chi's identity was special; she was intimately connected to the important force that was the Qing-Jiao Army.

"I am already too busy to take care of myself right now, so how can I look after Qing Chi? What if she encounters danger outside, but I am tied up with my own affairs and fail to pull the thread in time, letting her fall into a perilous situation? What then?"

"There's no need to create unnecessary complications at this moment."

Thinking of this, Ning Zhuo was about to open his mouth and tactfully decline Sun Lingtong's proposal.

Right at that moment, the Mechanism Ring on his finger slightly contracted.

Ning Zhuo's heart instantly jolted. The Mechanism Ring had the effect of issuing warnings, but ever since arriving at the Myriad Manifestations Sect, it had gone silent.

Ning Zhuo and Sun Lingtong speculated that this was because the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate was suppressed by a Luck-Suppressing Magic Treasure, which restricted the Mechanism Ring's effectiveness.

He hadn't expected the long-dormant Mechanism Ring to take effect again at this moment.

However, this time the contraction was extremely faint and fleeting, making Ning Zhuo start to doubt whether it was just an illusion.

The big-headed youth was naturally sharp-witted. He preferred to believe it was real rather than dismiss it.

He immediately changed his train of thought, deliberately thinking: "I won't arrange for Qing Chi to leave and participate in the trial."

The Mechanism Ring did not react.

He pondered for a moment, then thought again: "I won't plant Life Hanging by a Thread on Qing Chi."

The next moment, the Mechanism Ring slightly contracted!

It was warning him again. Ning Zhuo's heart skipped a beat, instantly understanding: Planting Life Hanging by a Thread on Qing Chi was the key!

Divine Art - Life Hanging by a Thread.

Ning Zhuo acted resolutely and swiftly, making his move directly.

Qing Chi was right inside the Formation Space. Even though she wasn't in Ning Zhuo's line of sight, he held the token for the Bluestone Cave Manor, meaning everything within the cave was under his control.

The thread of Life Hanging by a Thread descended precisely towards the top of Qing Chi's head.

Qing Chi remained completely unaware, still burying herself in crafting talismans.

Halfway down, the thread naturally deflected and couldn't land!

Ning Zhuo's pupils shrank slightly. He thought for a moment, then sent a sound transmission to Qing Chi.

Upon hearing Ning Zhuo's instructions, a hint of pleasant surprise appeared on Qing Chi's face.

She immediately put down her talisman brush, left the Formation Space, and went to pay her respects to Ning Zhuo. Seeing Sun Lingtong there, her mind stirred with anticipation.

Ning Zhuo said to Qing Chi, "We have been at the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate for some time now."

"There are countless Rising Cloud Minor Trials here; it can be said that fortuitous encounters are everywhere."

"Qing Chi, you've studied diligently and performed excellently, but you cannot always work behind closed doors. Why not go out and participate in a few minor trials to try your luck? Even if you gain nothing, broadening your horizons would still be good."

Qing Chi immediately agreed, casting an expectant gaze toward Ning Zhuo. "Young Master, I'm willing to go with you."

Ning Zhuo shook his head. "I've been in the limelight quite a bit. It's better if you aren't associated with me for now."

The later his connection with Qing Chi was exposed, the better. Because this was a very important clue that pointed to Ning Zhuo's relationship with the White Paper Immortal City.

Ning Zhuo estimated that this secret of his was about to be exposed. That wasn't a huge problem either.

Qing Chi immediately revealed obvious disappointment.

Ning Zhuo pointed at Sun Lingtong. "Go with my pageboy; you two can look out for each other."

Qing Chi obediently nodded. "Yes, Young Master."

Ning Zhuo added, "I will plant another countermeasure on you. You must cooperate with me completely and allow it. If you encounter any danger, I will be able to pull you out of peril, helping you escape swiftly and pulling you back to my side."

Qing Chi couldn't help but feel moved, thinking to herself, "Young Master holds me in his heart!"

She nodded repeatedly. "Everything is at the Young Master's discretion."

Ning Zhuo cast the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Art once again. This time, there was only a slight resistance before it successfully settled.

Qing Chi stared intently at Ning Zhuo, her bright eyes unblinking.

Ning Zhuo smiled. "It's done."

Qing Chi was momentarily stunned. "That's it? Young Master."

She hadn't felt a thing.

Ning Zhuo took out the Spirit Communication Mirror. "Let's test it out, so you can familiarize yourself with the feeling."

He had Qing Chi leave the Cultivation Chamber and re-enter the Formation Space. Then, he used Life Hanging by a Thread and the Spirit communication technique to pull her out of the Formation Space, bringing her in front of him in an instant.

Qing Chi retched. The intense dizziness caused her to kneel on the ground, supporting herself with her hands.

This feeling was very unpleasant.

Ning Zhuo walked over, patted Qing Chi's shoulder, fed her a Medicinal Pill, and helped her up.

"Young Master, it's unbearable. I can't stand," Qing Chi leaned closely against Ning Zhuo, nestling against him.

Sun Lingtong chuckled mischievously.

Ning Zhuo only supported her for about a dozen breaths before letting her leave to rest and recover on her own.

Sun Lingtong led the way, enthusiastically explaining to Qing Chi how they should coordinate for tomorrow's trial.

Left alone in the room, Ning Zhuo still felt a bit uneasy, so he took out the relevant intelligence regarding the Talisman Dueling Platform trial.

"My background hasn't been uncovered yet. As long as they don't know about the Ning Clan, they won't link me to talismans."

"Therefore, these kinds of Rising Cloud Minor Trials related to talismans are most likely safe."

Ning Zhuo had a clear understanding of his own situation.

He had many opponents. The Ban Clan, at least, would secretly set ambushes for him. Situ Xing, Pi Fujie, and others also had motives.

In previous Flying Cloud Assemblies, it was common for competitors to use Rising Cloud Minor Trials to scheme against each other and set elaborate traps. After all, making a direct move inside the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate was the ultimate taboo!

"It's somewhat embarrassing to say. Among the four major cultivation arts, my attainment in talismans is actually the lowest."

Ning Zhuo pondered for a moment. "For now, I should focus on Medicinal Pills."

The efficacy of the Embryonic Breath Spirit Boat would continue to decrease in the future.

Before that happened, Ning Zhuo had best find a substitute.

Medicinal Pills were the most orthodox solution.

The vast majority of Cultivators took this path; it was just that Ning Zhuo's personal circumstances were somewhat special.

Therefore, Ning Zhuo wanted to mend this shortcoming.

"Although I lack the foundation of a pill refinement realm, I possess Jia Luanzhen's lifelong experiences. His knowledge was vast, and having countless practical memories of pill refinement will save me a massive amount of effort."

"By participating in pill refinement trials, I can avoid potential traps while also applying theory to practice, allowing me to digest Jia Luanzhen's pill refinement experiences much faster."

"Sigh, if only I had the Unity of Knowledge and Action Art, my efficiency would skyrocket!"

Unfortunately, Zhao Hansheng and Gu Qing had just left.

When Ning Zhuo heard this news, he was somewhat dumbfounded. He had no idea about what had transpired surrounding Qin De in the Cloud Prison.

Zhao Hansheng's sudden departure caught Ning Zhuo completely off guard.

The big-headed youth had still been planning to acquire the Unity of Knowledge and Action Art from Zhao Hansheng. Now that the key person had run off, Ning Zhuo felt deeply disappointed and could only choose to give up.

After all, joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect was the crux of the grand scheme. The Unity of Knowledge and Action Art would be like giving wings to a tiger or adding flowers to brocade, but it wasn't essential.

When it came to priorities, Ning Zhuo naturally had a clear mind.

Under the moonlit night, all was silent.

On the nine-tiered white jade platform, the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy slowly rotated, just as usual.

Suddenly.

A muffled tremor echoed from deep within the cloud canopy.

The sound was extremely low and deep, like the heavy breathing of an ancient behemoth, or a rumble from the depths of the earth. Had the grand hall's formation not been constantly active, intercepting this sound, the entire Sect would have inevitably been shaken!

The nine layers of the cloud canopy abruptly came to a halt.

Following that, the canopy began to shudder violently up and down and sway side to side, as if some demonic orb was ramming it from within, seeking to break free of its cage and seize its freedom.

At this moment, the colors of the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy's seven-hued clouds began to shift wildly. Nine-colored radiances alternated and flickered, flashing erratically.

When Dong Chen rushed over in an emergency, the nine main cloud ribbons of the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy were all pulled taut, whipping about violently and causing the air to crackle with sharp pa sounds. The nine principal pendants at the ends of the ribbons smashed against each other frantically, emitting the ear-piercing sound of clashing metal, a shrill and urgent noise like an alarm.

Dong Chen hastily made his move, pouring in his spiritual energy to help the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy maintain its stability.

As the current Sect Master, Dong Chen held a special status and bore a portion of the Myriad Manifestations Sect's destiny. His personal intervention was naturally extraordinary.

However, the effects were only superb at first; soon, the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy began to quake again.

Dong Chen's expression turned solemn as he realized, "It's the calamity foretold by the prophetic poem. It has suddenly intensified infinitely, to the point that even the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy is struggling to suppress it!"

"Relying on me alone, it cannot be suppressed!"

Dong Chen immediately shot out three Flying Letters.

The Flying Letters streaked out like lightning, and then, passing through the Sect's formation, were swiftly transmitted away.

The next moment, one of them appeared in front of Wang Yu.

Wang Yu handled matters thoroughly and smoothly. He was just about to conduct a third soul search on Qin De when he was interrupted by the Flying Letter.

Scanning it with his divine sense, his expression immediately changed. "An anomaly has occurred with the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy again; this matter is not trivial!"

Qin De had already been soul-searched twice in a row, and there weren't any suspicious points to begin with. Wang Yu instantly threw Qin De to the back of his mind, formed a hand seal, and vanished from his spot.
