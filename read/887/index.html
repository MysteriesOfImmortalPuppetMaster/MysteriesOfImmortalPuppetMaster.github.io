Chapter 887: Rushing to Repair the Treasured Furnace

If Ning Zhuo's stunning performance made people take him seriously and feel pressured, then Zhu Fenxiang's subsequent attempt brought continuous pleasant surprises to everyone.

"Zhu Fenxiang previously wanted to use the Divine Summoning Art for the South Ming Vermilion Bird again, but resolutely changed her mind halfway and summoned Lord of a Thousand Hammers instead."

"Although this girl is young, she has great vision and decisiveness. She actually saw that the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit had already been pacified, allowing her to continue artifact refining, repairing the medicinal pill furnace to gain the artifact spirit's favor!"

"Excellent, truly excellent. In that case, couldn't I do the same?"

Some people shifted their gazes to Ning Zhuo. Seeing his ugly expression, they couldn't help but sigh in realization - thanks to Ning Zhuo using his lifebound magic artifact to pacify the artifact spirit, the situation was blown wide open. At the same time, they rejoiced that Ning Zhuo was ordered first rather than last. If he were last, Ning Zhuo might have been the sole victor.

"The third, fellow cultivator Situ Xing," the presiding cultivator called out softly.

Under everyone's watchful eyes, Situ Xing took out a jade slip from his sleeve. He engraved his thoughts with his divine sense and immediately poured his spiritual energy into it.

The next moment, the jade slip transformed into a streak of white light, flying into the sky and disappearing into the Cloud Layer.

Situ Xing himself walked slowly toward the center.

He stood in front of the pill furnace, pausing for a moment until a subordinate of the Situ Clan arrived.

"Young Master, the precious materials you requested are all here." This subordinate directly brought over a treasure chest.

Situ Xing took the iron chest and opened the lid. Instantly, a treasure light shot into the sky.

Situ Xing first took out three flags from the treasure chest and set them up in the arena, forming a simple refining formation.

He then took out various precious materials from the treasure chest.

There were Stargrid Stones. The stone body was silvery-white and shaped like a chessboard, its surface covered in dense starlight patterns, resembling the starry night sky.

There was Stardust Sand. Silver light gleamed within the deep blue, fine as powder, flowing in the chest like the Milky Way.

There were even Star Core Fragments. Their color was golden-white, shaped like hearts, with starlight flowing inside as if they were living things.

As a scion of a major clan, Situ Xing's basic skills were solid. But to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace, just these basic skills were not enough.

Therefore, he had temporarily borrowed the banners and directly established a refining formation. These banners were all magic treasures, allowing him to refine above his own level.

Situ Xing stood in the center of the refining formation, mobilizing his spiritual energy and spitting out vital essence blood, causing the banners to wave and sparks of starlight to dance.

The flames burned, melting all the precious materials together into one entity.

Situ Xing poured out the precious liquid.

The precious liquid seeped into the charred cracks. From within the pill furnace's cracks, fine, silvery-white spiritual veins surprisingly emerged, flowing across the furnace walls like miniature galaxies.

Soon, the galaxies solidified and took shape, repairing another small portion of the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Situ Xing retracted his hand and exhaled a long breath.

His face was pale and his aura unsteady, yet his eyes flashed with sharp light.

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit curled up into a ball.

Ning Zhuo secretly exerted strong control over it: "Come on, put on a show."

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit once again revealed its head, chirping "Chirp, chirp" at Situ Xing twice, just like a baby bird.

Seeing this, obvious joy immediately surfaced on Situ Xing's face.

"I bet right too!" Situ Xing excitedly clenched his fists. As he turned to return to the crowd, he looked at Ning Zhuo, his gaze filled with banter and mockery.

"It's all thanks to your methods, Ning Zhuo."

"You paved the way for everyone!"

After Situ Xing was Zhongli Mei.

This former Hall Master of the Alchemy Hall and one of the Supreme Elders of Danxia Peak, upon seeing Situ Xing's results, decisively changed his original plan as well.

Zhongli Mei walked up to the fire furnace and stopped. He reached out and gently stroked the furnace wall, feeling the spiritual light flowing within those repaired cracks.

Then, he sat down cross-legged.

He took out various precious materials and also began to repair the pill furnace.

At the final critical juncture, he let out a soft cry and unexpectedly drew a strand of pill qi from his own body, injecting it into the pill furnace.

The crowd couldn't help but let out a collective low gasp.

Qing Baizi, in particular, felt his body and mind tremble fiercely.

When Zhongli Mei finally made his move, he directly extracted three hundred years' worth of his accumulated pill qi.

He injected all of it into the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Zhongli Mei's pill qi was warm, rich, and enduring. After being injected into the fire furnace, it could elevate the furnace's pill-refining effectiveness by several tens of percent.

This wasn't repairing, but "nourishing"! It was something only extremely seasoned alchemists could accomplish - nourishing the pill furnace with pill qi to make its spiritual energy more abundant and the artifact spirit's intellect clearer.

Zhongli Mei's face turned pale. Once the time limit was reached, even his movements as he stood up were somewhat shaky.

Witnessing the entire process, Ning Zhuo sighed in his heart, "Zhongli Mei's contribution is truly immense. Come on, little baby, give him a chirp."

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, under strong control, poked its bird head out again and chirped twice.

Seeing this scene, Zhongli Mei revealed a weak smile and subconsciously reached up to stroke his beard twice.

Chunyangzi walked out from the crowd.

His steps were firm and powerful. The red-gold Daoist robe swayed gently with his strides.

He walked up to the fire furnace and stopped, his gaze falling onto the Nanming Fire Furnace that had already been partially repaired.

His eyes were filled with a look of absolute determination to obtain it.

"What was the purpose of me bringing my entire Sect to participate in the Myriad Manifestations Sect's Flying Cloud Assembly?"

"Isn't it exactly to fight for the Nanming Fire Furnace?!"

"By obtaining this Spirit Treasure, I can take advantage of the momentum to secure a place on any major mountain peak within the Myriad Manifestations Sect, saving the effort of fighting to establish my Sect's status."

At this moment, Chunyangzi recalled his secret negotiations with Wang Yu last night.

"Therefore, I must have the Nanming Fire Furnace!"

"In terms of foundational wealth, who can surpass me? I have the entire Pure Yang Sect backing me."

Chunyangzi cautiously took out a jade box and opened the lid. Lying inside the box was a red-gold pearl. Inside the pearl, flames danced, resembling a living creature.

The Pure Yang True Fire Pearl!

This was a supreme treasure condensed from Pure Yang True Fire by the successive generations of patriarchs of the Pure Yang Palace. Each pearl took hundreds of years to condense, and the entire Pure Yang Palace only possessed three of them.

Chunyangzi tossed the Pure Yang True Fire Pearl into the core of the furnace.

The moment the pearl entered the furnace, it suddenly transformed into a ball of flame. Accompanied by Chunyangzi continuously channeling his spiritual energy into it, the flame changed from crimson to red-gold, and grew from a tiny flicker into a raging inferno the size of a millstone.

Chunyangzi took out a jade bottle. The precious liquid contained within the bottle numbered exactly forty-nine drops. This was the entire accumulation of the Pure Yang Palace from its founding until now. It could be said to be incomparably precious!

Chunyangzi used his divine sense to extract the precious liquid, sending it drop by drop flying into the Nanming Fire Furnace.

The spiritual liquid entered the furnace and turned into red-gold mist, permeating the furnace body. Wherever the mist went, the incomplete parts of the furnace wall were rapidly repaired at a speed visible to the naked eye. Meanwhile, the texture of the rest of the furnace body became more sturdy, and the operation of the formation runes became smoother.

The crowd behind him let out collective low gasps, one after another sighing in awe at Chunyangzi's grand generosity.

Chunyangzi's expression was calm, but anyone could tell his intense determination and fighting spirit!

It wasn't until the time limit was exhausted that Chunyangzi regretfully stopped.

"He really brought out his life savings." Ning Zhuo was pleased to see that after Chunyangzi's refining, the degree to which the Nanming Fire Furnace had been repaired was the largest.

"Come, little artifact spirit, give Chunyangzi a few chirps as encouragement."

Under Ning Zhuo's strong control, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit poked out its head and let out three chirps: "Chirp, chirp, chirp."

Chunyangzi's spirits were instantly lifted: "Good, good, good. It chirped three times, because of me it chirped three times. Everyone else only got two!"

At this moment, all his previous sacrifices seemed to have been worth it.

Chunyangzi returned to his original spot with his head held high. His posture irritated one of the people present.

"Chunyangzi." The Red-Robed Guest's pupils contracted slightly. "Just you wait."

This Nascent Soul powerhouse of the Demonic Path walked out of the crowd, feeling immense pressure in his heart.

His scarlet robe fluttered like a flag in the morning breeze. Dark-black blood patterns writhed violently on the robe, as if living creatures were struggling. His face was gloomy and cold, while an extremely resolute voice echoed in his heart: "The Nanming Fire Furnace belongs to me. No matter what, I must suppress Chunyangzi!"

He walked briskly to the Nanming Fire Furnace and stopped.

He directly brought out his own pill furnace - the Blood Sea Venomous Snake Furnace!

The furnace body was about the height of a person, with three legs and two handles, and entirely deep dark red in color. Engraved on the furnace body was a Blood Sea picture: the blood sea surged, corpses and bones bobbed up and down, and countless twisted faces struggled, wailed, and sank within the bloody waves.

Hanging from the left and right furnace handles were human bone bells.

Three completely crimson venomous snakes were coiled and entwined around the three furnace legs.

As soon as this furnace appeared, a thick bloody stench quickly permeated the entire venue.

Even Wang Yu was startled.

Of course, he wasn't afraid of the Red-Robed Guest, but rather worried that the Red-Robed Guest's demonic and heretical arrogance would scare the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to death.

"Don't be afraid, don't be afraid," Ning Zhuo quietly comforted it, while simultaneously exerting strong control to make the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit merely shrink its body slightly.

Wang Yu set his heart at ease.

The Red-Robed Guest sat down cross-legged, his hands forming incantation seals as he activated the Blood Sea Venomous Snake Furnace.

Flames surged up from within the furnace. The three blood venom snakes simultaneously raised their heads and spat out three streams of blood venom flames to fan the furnace fire.

For a time, blood flames soared into the sky, dyeing the entire Illusory Eaves Open Courtyard a dark red.

The Red-Robed Guest took out various items from his accumulated wealth and threw them one by one into the pill furnace to be refined.

He threw the Blood Soul Essence he had accumulated over a lifetime - the supreme treasure he had used when breaking through to the Nascent Soul stage - into his own fire furnace.

He took out a jade box, opened the lid, and brought out his bottom-of-the-trunk Blood Soul Pearl. This, too, was tossed into the pill furnace as a consumable material.

"Refine for me!" the Red-Robed Guest exerted himself fiercely.

The moment the pearl entered the furnace, a mournful shriek rang out from within, sounding as if thousands upon thousands of Wailing Souls were crying at the same time.

Among the cultivators in the arena, some covered their ears, their hearts shaken, while others frowned deeply, revealing expressions of disgust...

A moment later, the pill furnace was completely opened. The bloody stench had unexpectedly transformed into a rich fragrance.

A streak of blood light refined by the Red-Robed Guest directly attached itself to the Nanming Fire Furnace.

When the blood light dissipated, the missing sections on the fire furnace had been filled in a bit more.

The Red-Robed Guest's body swayed; his aura had weakened by more than tenfold. He put away his pill furnace and stared fixedly at the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Ning Zhuo inwardly sighed in admiration: "He used so many Spirit Treasure-level materials! Especially that Blood Soul Pearl, it's truly incomparably rare. I'll give you three praises as well."

The next moment, he controlled the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to chirp three times: "Chirp, chirp, chirp."

The Red-Robed Guest took a deep breath. Failing to suppress Chunyangzi made him somewhat dissatisfied, but at least the two sides were tied.

The Red-Robed Guest slowly stood up and returned to his original spot with his head held high. On the way, his fierce gaze locked firmly onto Chunyangzi.

Chunyangzi coldly snorted, meeting the Red-Robed Guest's gaze without dodging or averting his eyes.

"It's finally my turn." Nine Fires Dragon Lord had already been waiting somewhat impatiently.

He walked briskly forward and stood firm in front of the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Nanming Fire Furnace, you are mine! Nine Fires Dragon Lord harbored wild ambitions in his heart. You are my stepping stone for advancement.

It had been more than two hundred years since he joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect and became a Guest Elder of Danxia Peak. However, because of his identity as a Demon Cultivator, even with his Fire Dragon bloodline, he suffered disdain. He was constantly marginalized year-round and had always been a fringe figure in the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

In the past, how could he possibly have had the chance to touch the Nanming Fire Furnace?

"Now, repairing it and becoming the final victor is my perfect opportunity to obtain the Nanming Fire Furnace!"

"In my entire life, this is very likely the only chance I'll ever have."

In Nine Fires Dragon Lord's heart, there was excitement, but also pressure.

The later a cultivator went, the greater the competitive pressure. It was all because the preceding Ning Zhuo, Zhu Fenxiang, Situ Xing, Zhongli Mei, Chunyangzi, and the Red-Robed Guest... everyone had fought with all their might, and everyone had utilized their bottom-of-the-trunk trump cards.

Thus far, no one had been eliminated; everyone had made the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit chirp.

"I must make you chirp at least four times!"

Nine Fires Dragon Lord sat cross-legged. Whether casting techniques or taking out Fire Seeds, he ignited nine different types of flames.

This was his signature technique, renowned throughout the entire Sect.

Earth Fire, Wood Fire, Lightning Fire, Heart Fire, True Fire, Pill Fire, Spirit Fire, Demon Fire, and Dragon Fire - nine types of flames surged simultaneously. Each performed its own role without interfering with the others as they intertwined, coiled, and danced together within the furnace chamber.

Nine Fires Dragon Lord used his divine sense to control objects, taking out several dragon scales.

These were parts of his own body, originally intended for refining his lifebound Spirit Treasure. But now, he directly threw them all into the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Each dragon scale was the size of a palm. The edges of the scales were as sharp as blades, and the surface of the scales featured fine patterns that looked like burning flames. Contained within them was the most primal Fire Dragon bloodline power in his body!

The dragon scales entered the furnace and rapidly turned into nothingness.

The nine flames flared up simultaneously. The Earth Fire surged, Wood Fire ascended, Lightning Fire exploded, Heart Fire pulsated, True Fire blazed, Pill Fire was pure, Spirit Fire was nimble, Demon Fire was wild, and Dragon Fire was majestic - the nine flames intertwined and surprisingly transformed into a small, red-gold Fire Dragon that spiraled and danced within the Nanming Fire Furnace.

This miraculous scene made everyone subconsciously hold their breath.

An artifact refining technique that fused nine flames was rarely seen in this world! This was the Dragon Lord's bottom-of-the-trunk method. If Nine Fires Dragon Lord hadn't displayed it this time, the world would have remained completely in the dark.

Just this display alone made everyone present start to believe - among all the cultivators participating in this minor trial, the one with the strongest artifact refining attainments was undoubtedly Nine Fires Dragon Lord!

Nine Fires Dragon Lord's face flushed red, and the veins on his forehead bulged. He clenched his jaw tight, his mind tied entirely to the Fire Dragon. Evidently, controlling a dragon made of nine flames was not easy for him, either.

He emptied his amassed wealth, taking out various precious materials.

There was White Essence Dragon Saliva, Sub-Prime Dragon Bone, Dragon Peony Blood Stone, Ten-Thousand-Year Dragon Crystal...

Under the dance of the Fire Dragon, all sorts of precious materials melted into one, rapidly repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace.

"Still not enough, it's still not enough!"

At the very last moment of the time limit, Nine Fires Dragon Lord gritted his teeth and took out an object, causing everyone's expression to tremble in shock.
