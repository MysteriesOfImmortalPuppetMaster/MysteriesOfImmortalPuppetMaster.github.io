Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 931: You Must Fight Ning Zhuo Again

Liu Jinke jolted awake from a nightmare.

He subconsciously reached out and touched his neck. His expression gradually calmed, and after a long time, he exhaled a breath of turbid qi.

Although he possessed the ability to reattach his severed head, the sensation and experience of being decapitated were truly horrifying, leaving Liu Jinke with profound psychological trauma.

In his divine sea, the figure of Ning Zhuo couldn't help but surface once again.

Even though he had lost to Ning Zhuo, he harbored little desire for revenge.

Hate, naturally, he felt hate.

Ning Zhuo had severed his head in public and used the "Severed Head Promise" to force his miserable exit. This was humiliation! Humiliation from a mid Foundation Establishment cultivator toward a Golden Core cultivator.

But beyond the hate, there was fear.

The Golden Liquid Reverting Core Body was not truly immortal. Although that blade hadn't taken his life, it had severed a layer of his false sense of security.

Before exposing this trump card, Liu Jinke had psychological confidence no matter who he fought - the fact that he could regenerate severed limbs was a secret.

But now, this secret was exposed.

Liu Jinke felt as if a layer of his clothes had been stripped off, leaving him completely naked.

This made him exceptionally uncomfortable, as tension and anxiety continuously bred in the depths of his heart.

Right now, he only wanted to cultivate behind closed doors, replenish his golden blood, and wait for the storm to pass.

For a rogue cultivator to survive until the Golden Core stage, it never relied on mere hot-blooded bravery, but on knowing when to advance, when to retreat, and when to lower his head and hide.

Liu Jinke disguised his appearance, concealed his tracks, and arrived at the black market.

He stopped in front of a vendor's stall.

"How much are you selling this ore for?" A peculiar glint flashed in his eyes as he deliberately lowered his voice to ask.

Possessing golden blood, he felt a resonance and recognized that although this ore looked ordinary on the surface, it actually contained a precious Metal Element treasure material called the "Sunken Vein Crimson Gold Nodule."

Sure enough, the stall vendor was unaware of this and quoted a low price.

Still harboring some suspicion, Liu Jinke crouched down and picked up the ore to inspect it. Upon direct physical contact, his golden blood's resonance with the treasure grew even stronger.

Liu Jinke's heart couldn't help but stir.

His depleted golden blood felt an intense hunger. Holding this piece of ore, he was like a starving man seeing food, wishing he could absorb it on the spot.

Liu Jinke swiftly paid the Spirit Stones.

Having snagged this bargain, he immediately headed back to Flowing Cloud Peak.

Upon returning to his cave manor, Liu Jinke sealed the restrictive formations, immediately sat cross-legged, and circulated the Golden Liquid Reverting Core Body.

Golden blood seeped from his palm, slowly soaking into the Crimson Gold Nodule.

The Sunken Vein Crimson Gold Nodule rested obediently in his palm like a piece of dead gold. The gray shell peeled away, and a dark red-golden light shone through the cracks. Wisps of heavy, thick metal qi were drawn by his golden blood, drilling into his body through his palm.

A long-lost look of relaxation appeared on Liu Jinke's face.

But the next moment, an abrupt change occurred!

Deep within the Crimson Gold Nodule, several earthy-yellow fine lines suddenly lit up. Like venomous snakes awakening from the ore's core, they drilled against the flow of his golden blood.

Liu Jinke's chest fiercely bulged.

The originally smooth-flowing golden liquid suddenly surged backward, like a river flowing in reverse, a mighty golden tide crashing backwards into his heart apertures.

"Pfft!"

What he spat out was not fresh blood, but a line of scalding golden plasma. The golden plasma hit the ground, causing the stone bricks to immediately sizzle and scorch into a web of cracks.

The cave manor's restrictions were triggered, creating a series of explosive sounds.

Golden blood seeped from Liu Jinke's pores and flowed along the cracks in the ground. Wherever it passed, the stone veins turned gold, and the spiritual energy became corrupted. The entire cave manor looked as if it had been smeared with a layer of sickly gold paint.

Suddenly, the main doors of the cave manor were smashed open.

Several cultivators stepped through the dust and barged in.

Liu Jinke turned pale with shock. Pressing one hand to his chest and bracing the other against the ground, he tried to stand up, only to find his knees feeling as heavy as if they were filled with cold iron.

He could only glare angrily at the intruders, looking up and shouting, "Who are you?!"

The leading cultivator glanced down at the earth vein cracks, then looked at the golden corruption covering the ground. His face was as stern as iron.

"Liu Jinke!" His voice was like a stone hammer striking a table. "How bold of you! To practice heretical arts in your cave manor, causing your golden blood to lose control and nearly corrupting the mountain path's spirit vein!"

Liu Jinke's pupils rapidly shrank. "There was a problem with the Metal Element treasure I bought! It's not my fault!"

"No, you guys are plotting against me!!"

He was not stupid and quickly realized what was happening.

The intruder sneered, "Still making excuses! The evidence for this matter is irrefutable. Save your complaints for the others when you face trial."

By now, Liu Jinke had recognized the identities of the intruding cultivators. "Earth Expansion Alliance! When did I ever form a grudge with you? For you to frame me like this?"

The Earth Expansion Alliance was known for surveying earth veins and expanding mountain peaks, and they were most skilled at making an issue out of spirit veins. Now that they firmly claimed Liu Jinke had corrupted the mountain path's spirit vein, the matter had become exceptionally troublesome.

Several hours later, Liu Jinke stood outside his sealed cave manor, his face ashen green.

His breathing was labored, and his chest still ached from the backlash of the reverse-flowing golden liquid.

The clouds and mist of Flowing Cloud Peak pressed down against the mountainside, damp, cold, and gloomy, hanging over his head like an unannounced verdict.

"Why would the Earth Expansion Alliance frame me?" He puzzled over it but couldn't find an answer.

Unable to figure it out, he could only push the thought aside. Highly vigilant, Liu Jinke dragged his injured body toward the Commerce Hall to purchase Metal Element treasures.

His golden blood was depleted, and the backlash had yet to settle. If he delayed any longer, his Golden Liquid Reverting Core Body would likely fall into a state of "Golden Rigor" on its own. By then, his limbs would be heavy and his movements sluggish; forget fighting Ning Zhuo again, he'd struggle even to defend himself.

The shopkeeper, who used to be on friendly terms with him, suddenly turned a cold shoulder.

"Daoist Liu Jin, let's settle your old tab first."

Liu Jinke frowned. "What old tab?"

The shopkeeper brought out a ledger and flipped it open page by page.

Three catties of Geng Metal Sand, two pieces of White Bin Iron, and half a tael of Cold Edge Refined Gold.

These were previous credits, and their payment period had arrived.

Liu Jinke's face sank. "Impossible! The deadline is still months away."

Unhurriedly, the shopkeeper pushed the ledger in front of him.

The ledger's pages glowed with a faint copper light, and the magical seals were distinct, piercing into Liu Jinke's view like an iron nail.

The seals were real.

The ledger was also real. Only the deadline was fake.

From behind the counter, the shopkeeper's voice was so polite it bordered on callousness. "If you feel wronged, Daoist, you are free to file a complaint. But today, this tab must be settled."

A cold chill ran through Liu Jinke's entire body.

Ultimately, he was forced to settle the old tab.

The majority of the Spirit Stones on him were emptied out, yet he hadn't managed to buy a single piece of Metal Element treasure.

His cave manor was sealed, and old debts weighed him down.

He could only take out his few remaining Spirit Stones and stay in a remote inn.

In inns, taverns, and even on the streets, numerous Jade Slips suddenly began circulating.

The images recorded within the Jade Slips were precisely the disgraceful scene of him, Liu Jinke, being decapitated by Ning Zhuo.

A flaming greatsword flashed.

A head rolled.

Golden blood sprayed!

Following that was the footage of his terrified resurrection, his hasty concession, and his rushed departure.

Someone had even slowed down the playback, repeating that exact moment, as if afraid others wouldn't see clearly how his head was severed, how he reattached it, and how he admitted defeat.

"The Severed Head Guest, hahaha."

"The head-reattaching rogue cultivator from Flowing Cloud Peak."

"The number one coward among Golden Cores!"

Mocking laughter drifted down from the second floors of teahouses, slipped through the window cracks of taverns, and surfaced from the hushed voices of pedestrians.

Overcome with fury, Liu Jinke rushed out and crushed several transmission Jade Slips in succession.

The inn's lobby fell silent for a moment, and then someone whispered, "If I were him, I'd rather just bash my head in and die."

Liu Jinke snapped his head around.

That person immediately shut their mouth, but showed little fear; their eyes were filled with the schadenfreude of someone enjoying a good show.

Another person lowered their voice, "Is it possible the image in the Jade Slip isn't real?"

Liu Jinke stood frozen in place, his back completely rigid.

Unable to shop at the Commerce Hall, he still needed high-grade Metal Element treasures to mend his body.

After resting for a night, Liu Jinke decided to seek help from a friend.

Just as he stepped out onto the street and turned a corner, Liu Jinke's expression drastically changed.

He reached out to check - his storage bag felt more than half empty.

He spun around, staring behind him, his eyes practically spitting fire as he desperately searched for the culprit.

He found no suspicious individuals.

Instead, the surrounding cultivators, annoyed by Liu Jinke's aggressive demeanor, glared back at him.

Liu Jinke stood stiffly in place, completely out of options.

Time passed swiftly. He painfully accepted the reality. Touching the empty storage bag at his waist, his anger was like a fire doused with cold water, instantly reducing to a mere wisp of smoke.

Gritting his teeth, he headed toward a friend's cave manor on another peak.

Midway there, the Cloud Layer hung very low.

Suddenly, beastly roars echoed out.

The clouds and mist tore open as several demonic beasts with red eyes charged out, drool hanging from their fangs and their fur a scarlet red, rushing straight at Liu Jinke.

"Quick, dodge!"

"The spirit beasts from the Bird and Beast Villa have lost control!"

In the distance, beast tamers shouted loudly, their tone anxious, but their movements were neither fast nor slow.

Liu Jinke forcibly summoned his golden armor.

However, the armor's surface had only half-formed when it dimmed inch by inch due to the lack of golden blood.

Boom!

A Cloud Bull slammed into his chest.

He was sent flying backward, clearly hearing the sound of his ribs snapping.

As he lay on the mountainside, a Cloud Eagle tore at his shoulder, forcefully ripping away a chunk of flesh.

Overcome with pain, Liu Jinke let out a low growl, "Bird and Beast Villa!"

The beast tamers of the Bird and Beast Villa finally arrived, apologizing profusely. Claiming it was their responsibility, they insisted on taking Liu Jinke away to heal his injuries.

Naturally, Liu Jinke gave them no pleasant looks and initially refused. However, despite being a grand Golden Core cultivator, he struggled even to stand up at this moment. He could only allow the beast tamers to support him as they left the area.

The beast tamers brought him back to Flowing Cloud Peak, bringing him to Hanging Gourd Clinic for treatment.

The head physician spoke with a gentle tone: "Daoist Liu Jin, your injuries are not light, and your golden blood is severely depleted. If we delay further, I fear it will damage your foundation."

Liu Jinke wanted to sneer, but he couldn't even lift the corners of his mouth.

The medicinal liquid was warm and soothing, the acupuncture needles cool and refreshing; his wounds closed inch by inch. Yet, Liu Jinke felt not a sliver of gratitude in his heart, only a deeper chill.

A day later, Liu Jinke's injuries had stabilized and were mostly healed.

But lacking golden blood, he remained incredibly weak.

The physician sat by his bed. "Daoist Liu Jin, how are you feeling?"

Liu Jinke stared at him. "What do you want me to do?"

The physician smiled faintly. "It seems you already understand, Daoist. Take a look at this."

He took out a black-gold box.

The moment the lid opened, even the medicinal fragrance in the room was suppressed.

Lying within the box was a golden-red, round fetus. It looked like a pill, but wasn't; it looked like a heart, but wasn't. There were nine orifices on the fetal wall, and each orifice expelled fine blood-gold mist. With every breath, the faint sound of crashing tides could be heard. It was truly bizarre.

This was the Nine-Orificed Blood Gold Fetus!

Liu Jinke's heart jolted, and the residual golden blood in his body embarrassingly boiled in response.

The physician said, "It seems you've already recognized it. That's right, it is the Nine-Orificed Blood Gold Fetus. To others, it is heretical, bizarre, and hard to tame. But to you, it's like having a second heart."

Liu Jinke firmly gritted his teeth, but his gaze was inevitably drawn and glued to the Nine-Orificed Blood Gold Fetus.

It was a material he had once desperately searched for to no avail, the most suitable treasure for the Golden Liquid Reverting Core Body!

Using it to convert more golden blood would be highly efficient. It was simply a match made in heaven.

Liu Jinke hated himself for being tempted.

At the same time, he hated even more that he had no choice but to be tempted.

"What exactly do you want me to do?" Liu Jinke stared fixedly at the physician.

The physician's expression was indifferent. "Accept Ning Zhuo's challenge."

Liu Jinke's pupils rapidly shrank. For a moment, it was as if he forgot to breathe. Amidst the silence, he suddenly spoke, "I knew it."

Having been framed continuously, Liu Jinke had realized it by now.

This was a setup. The catalyst was Ning Zhuo's declaration to continue his challenges, giving him, Liu Jinke, a chance to fight again.

And this opportunity was seized by other forces, who banded together to coerce Liu Jinke.

Ning Zhuo had now been confirmed to have Golden Core-level combat strength, but exactly how high it was had not been tested in the previous battle.

Liu Jinke was the perfect chess piece to test Ning Zhuo!

Because Ning Zhuo had already put the word out, if he didn't challenge him now, he would surely suffer a backlash and a massive drop in reputation.

And Liu Jinke now understood Ning Zhuo's intention. "I've been played by him!"

Liu Jinke finally understood why Ning Zhuo was so confident.

Even if he was unwilling himself, circumstances would force him to clash with Ning Zhuo once more.

"But when I clash with Ning Zhuo, I'm truly risking my life!" Liu Jinke roared. "You might as well just kill me!!"

The physician patted him on the shoulder. "It won't come to that."

"Liu Jinke, we've prepared many things for you. I guarantee your strength will skyrocket."

Liu Jinke gnashed his teeth. "Where are they?"

The physician replied, "Naturally, they aren't with me. I am merely a physician; it's not strange for me to use the Nine-Orificed Blood Gold Fetus on you."

"There are several other cultivators in the same situation as me."

"However, I suggest you visit the Golden Hairpin Crone first."

"She is a Guest Elder of the Metal and Stone Alliance and an expert of our era. She has mastered a specific technique. If you go and learn it from her, dealing with mechanical constructs will be extremely convenient."

Liu Jinke ground his teeth until they creaked, but ultimately he nodded. "I'll go!"

The Golden Hairpin Crone was as thin as a stick, the ten fingers of both her hands gleaming with the color of metal and iron.

She tossed out a dilapidated golden book, golden dust rustling down from the edges of its pages.

"The Dao of Mechanisms relies on the word 'unity'. Axles unite with springs, tenons unite with mortises, talismans unite with formations, and qi unites with mechanisms. As long as you disrupt this unity, even a thousand birds and ten thousand beasts are nothing more than a pile of wood and iron that will plummet to the ground."

"This technique is the Metal and Stone Cleaving Art. Listen closely..."

The Golden Hairpin Crone had clearly been instructed in advance. Upon seeing Liu Jinke, without a second word, she directly imparted the technique.

Liu Jinke was quickly entranced by the lecture. He couldn't help but beam with joy, feelings of pleasant surprise and delight gradually swelling to fill his chest.

"If I master this technique, I can guarantee that all of Ning Zhuo's mechanical flying birds will fall apart, completely disintegrating in mid-air."

"Even that Golden Core-level puppet will suffer disaster!"

Liu Jinke's mindset was gradually undergoing a subtle shift.

After all, he was now personally experiencing the benefits of being sponsored.

After playing with the Yellow Crystal Spider Nest for a while, Ning Zhuo began his daily cultivation routine.

Once his routine was finished, Ning Zhuo frowned, sinking into deep thought. Currently, replenishing his spiritual energy remained a difficult problem, requiring him to employ methods to plan ahead.

"With Liu Jinke being buffed and sponsored, the first thing those behind him will think of should be the cultivation advantage a Golden Core has over a Foundation Establishment."

"Therefore, in the next battle, my endurance will be severely challenged!"
