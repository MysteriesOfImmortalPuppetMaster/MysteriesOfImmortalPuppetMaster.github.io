Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 967: A Thread Hanging from the Sky!

In the spectator stands, the cultivators of the Territory Expansion Alliance all fell silent.

The cultivators of White Cloud Township had ashen faces.

Elder Wandering Cloud furrowed his brows tightly.

They had made extremely sufficient preparations for Daoist Yunhu's battle. The White Jade Heaven-in-a-Pot Lamp, the Thirty-Six Fold Cloud Sluice, the Sunken Smoke Pot-Locking Formation, numerous cloud beasts, coupled with the sponsored methods from factions like the Setting Sun Society, Green Tea Club, and Heart-Questioning Studio - it was more than enough to besiege and kill the vast majority of Nascent Soul cultivators.

Unexpectedly, the Red-Robed Guest was actually this powerful, far exceeding what their intelligence had stated!

"Exhilarating!" The Red-Robed Guest laughed loudly. His blood-red robes fluttered wildly as his demonic qi soared to the heavens.

Provoked by Yue Qingshan's death, stimulated by Tan Zhu's merits, and irritated by being suppressed by Chunyangzi taking the head seat, his actions now indirectly poured out years of pent-up frustration onto the battlefield.

How satisfying!

Daoist Yunhu's face was deathly pale.

He had already retreated to the edge of the Cloud Marsh Arena.

If he stepped back any further, he would crash into the protective formation's light curtain.

The White Jade Heaven-in-a-Pot Lamp floated in front of him, its body crisscrossed with cracks. The few remaining cloud beasts guarding him all had sluggish auras.

"Don't force me any further!" Watching the Red-Robed Guest closing in, he had no choice but to release the Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow.

A piercing crow's caw rang out.

This was not a cloud beast, but a demon beast.

It was covered in black feathers, the roots of which gleamed with the dark red hue of a setting sun. Its eyes were like small beads of coagulated blood, its beak was long, slender, and sharp, and the tips of its claws carried the color of dried blood.

As soon as it appeared, it locked its gaze on the blood qi surrounding the Red-Robed Guest.

The Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow let out a sharp cry and turned into a black and red shadow, diving into the edge of the Red-Robed Guest's sea of blood and giving a vicious peck!

It actually pecked away a large swath of blood light.

As the blood light entered its mouth, the red hue at the roots of the Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow's feathers grew even brighter.

Its aura suddenly surged by several degrees.

The Red-Robed Guest raised an eyebrow, feeling a slight sense of surprise for the first time since the battle began.

The Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow lunged forward again, pecking down a second mouthful of blood qi.

The more it ate, the faster and fiercer it became. The thicker the sea of blood around the Red-Robed Guest, the more excited it grew. Blood mist, blood threads, blood drops, and even the residual light of blood techniques all became its food.

This demon beast perfectly countered the Blood Path!

A trace of invigoration finally appeared in Daoist Yunhu's eyes.

The Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow was sponsored by the Setting Sun Society. Its ferocity was simply too great; Daoist Yunhu couldn't control it at all and was quite terrified of suffering a backlash.

But releasing it now yielded unexpectedly effective results!

The Red-Robed Guest's blood techniques were only making the Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow stronger as it ate.

The Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow pecked at him once more.

Shockingly, the Red-Robed Guest did not dodge or evade. Instead, he raised his arm, allowing it to peck through his protective blood light.

Overjoyed, the Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow inhaled viciously.

A massive amount of blood qi was swallowed into its belly.

Its body rapidly expanded, its black feathers standing on end as the setting sun coloration burned all the way from the roots to the tips.

The next moment, the Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow's cries changed.

From excitement, they turned into agony.

Strange gurgling sounds came from its belly, like a pot of blood boiling over.

The Red-Robed Guest raised his hand and tapped gently with his fingertip. "Is my blood tasty?"

The Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow's eyes suddenly bulged.

The blood qi it swallowed had transformed into countless blood-colored pill patterns within its belly. Those patterns intertwined with each other, rapidly condensing into a tiny Blood Pill.

The moment the Blood Pill was formed, it immediately reversed the process, devouring the Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow's demon blood.

The Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow struggled madly, shedding large patches of black and red feathers. It tried to vomit out the Blood Pill in its belly, but it was already too late.

Boom!

The Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow's belly exploded, raining down demon blood.

The Red-Robed Guest opened his mouth and inhaled.

The sky full of demon blood rolled backward and was swallowed into his mouth.

The blood light around him flourished even more. His scarlet robes looked as if they had been soaked in fresh blood, and the dark blood patterns emitted faint, satisfied hisses.

The joy on Daoist Yunhu's face froze.

The Setting Sun Blood-Devouring Crow was dead.

Not only was it dead, but it had turned into nourishment for the Red-Robed Guest!

At this moment, Daoist Yunhu's will to fight plummeted to rock bottom.

Stepping on blood clouds, the Red-Robed Guest walked over step by step.

He wore a smile on his face, but his eyes held no warmth whatsoever.

The Red-Robed Guest slowly asked, "Is there any more?"

Daoist Yunhu's lips went dry.

The Red-Robed Guest added, "If you don't have any more, then I will send you on your way."

As his voice fell, the Red-Robed Guest's figure abruptly shot forward.

A large, blood-colored hand pierced through the last few layers of cloud qi, viciously grabbing toward Daoist Yunhu's head.

Daoist Yunhu shrieked, "Open the pot!"

The White Jade Pot swelled with the wind, becoming as large as a tower ship. Its mouth opened wide, revealing the final layer of the Heaven-in-a-Pot Cloud Chamber. Inside the chamber, layers of white light folded over each other, attempting to suck the Red-Robed Guest into the pot.

The blood light around the Red-Robed Guest skyrocketed, and he unleashed another large, blood-colored hand.

The two hands gripped the mouth of the White Jade Heaven-in-a-Pot Lamp. The ten fingers curled like hooks and gave a violent tear!

The mouth of the pot burst apart.

The originally tiny cracks on the pot's body instantly expanded, and more than half of the thirty-six layers of cloud sea patterns snapped. The cloud chamber inside shook violently as massive amounts of cloud qi sprayed out from the fissures.

Daoist Yunhu was struck as if by lightning, blood seeping from all seven of his orifices simultaneously.

With his Lifebound Treasure torn apart, he suffered a heavy blow.

The Red-Robed Guest dashed out of the white light at the pot's mouth in a single step. His left hand seized the White Jade Heaven-in-a-Pot Lamp, while his right hand pressed toward Daoist Yunhu's chest.

Daoist Yunhu tried to dodge.

He couldn't!

The Red-Robed Guest's palm landed.

Bang!

Daoist Yunhu's chest caved in. His entire body was sent flying backward, slamming into the protective light curtain.

Massive ripples washed across the light curtain.

Pressed against the formation's light curtain, Daoist Yunhu slowly slid down.

The entire arena was deathly silent.

Anyone could tell that Daoist Yunhu had lost.

Elder Taoli murmured, "He lost! We won!"

"No, he still has a trump card." A sharp gleam flashed across Ning Zhuo's eyes.

This was because he noticed that on Daoist Yunhu's face, there was fear, pain, wretchedness, and the deathly pallor of critical injury - yet, uniquely, there was no despair.

In the arena, the Red-Robed Guest also sensed something.

His gaze turned cold, and he was just about to retreat.

But Daoist Yunhu raised his head, his face covered in blood and filth, and hissed, "Take my hook!"

Above the Cloud Marsh Arena, a single thread suddenly dropped down.

It was a thread so thin it was nearly invisible.

Dangling from an extreme height.

Silent and traceless.

There was no startling thunder, no rosy clouds of light, no tremendous coercive pressure.

There was only a single thread.

A thread hanging from the sky.

The Red-Robed Guest's pupils contracted sharply.

He tried to transform into blood and escape.

But the moment this thread descended, the space across the entire Cloud Marsh Arena seemed to be pinned in place. The Blood Shadow Evasion Art had just been activated when it was forcefully suppressed back into his physical body by an indescribable power.

A Dao Artifact!

For the first time, a strong sense of crisis welled up in the Red-Robed Guest's heart.

At the lower end of that thread was tied a fishing hook.

The hook's body was extremely thin, like a crescent cold moon bent into an icy edge. It was entirely pale-black, showing no sign of blood color. At the tip of the hook lay a speck of dim blue, cold starlight. The starlight wasn't bright, yet it felt as if it could illuminate the very depths of one's soul.

The Sky-Hanging Thread Hook!

The Red-Robed Guest roared in fury. His sea of blood surged in reverse, pushing the power of his Blood Demon Pill to the absolute limit.

Scarlet blood light shot into the sky, transforming into a giant blood shadow that reached out to grab that fishing hook.

The fishing hook swayed gently.

The blood shadow's palm was sliced open by the thread, effortlessly.

In the next instant, the hook fell.

The Red-Robed Guest's body suddenly stiffened.

It was as if the tip of the hook hadn't touched his physical body, yet simultaneously felt as though it had pierced through his entire being.

He lowered his head and found his blood robes were still fluttering, his hands could still lift, and his sea of blood was still roaring behind him.

Monstrous fury surfaced in the Red-Robed Guest's eyes. "Ah!"

The Sky-Hanging Thread Hook jerked upward violently.

The Red-Robed Guest trembled violently in body and mind, as if struck by lightning.

His face twisted, his handsome, demonic features revealing a hideous expression for the first time.

Frozen at this very moment.

This was the last expression he wore before his death.

He was dead!

In the spectator stands, Chunyangzi abruptly stood up.

Ning Zhuo's expression changed drastically.

The cultivators of Naming Stronghold broke into an uproar.

"A Dao Artifact!"

"This is a Dao Artifact!"

"White Cloud Township actually has a Dao Artifact as part of their foundation?!"

Many of the cultivators from White Cloud Township also bore looks of shock. Clearly, not everyone knew about this trump card.

Daoist Yunhu knelt on the cloud lake, his face covered in blood and filth, his entire body trembling.

He was also afraid.

The Dao Artifact wasn't his.

To activate this Dao Artifact, the conditions were extremely strict - Daoist Yunhu himself had to be in a life-threatening situation where his life hung by a thread!

If the Red-Robed Guest hadn't forced him into such a state, and if he hadn't retained just enough breathing room to mobilize this Dao Artifact with his divine sense, he would have undoubtedly died a tragic death at the Red-Robed Guest's hands by now.

"I survived."

"I ultimately survived after all!"

Daoist Yunhu's eye sockets turned red, his lips quivering.

The Sky-Hanging Thread Hook took the Red-Robed Guest's life, secretly hooking his soul and casting it into Daoist Yunhu's embrace.

The Red-Robed Guest's physical body levitated in mid-air. The blood patterns on his scarlet robes had lost their vitality; what originally squirmed like snakes was now entirely stiff and dead.

Daoist Yunhu, covered in blood and filth, was almost completely drained. His hand movements were clumsy and fumbling as he continuously consumed Medicinal Pills to heal his injuries.

The Cloud Marsh Arena quieted down once again.

The sound of the jade chime delayed in ringing out.

Even the presiding cultivator on duty was stunned by this scene.

A moment later, the adjudicating voice finally sounded, carrying an unconcealable gravity: "In the fourth match, White Cloud Township's Daoist Yunhu is the victor."

He had won.

In the end, it was actually Daoist Yunhu who won!

This outcome exceeded the expectations of almost everyone present.

Daoist Yunhu had been at a disadvantage the entire time. In just another breath, he would have been slapped to death by the Red-Robed Guest's palm.

But in the end, that single thread fell from the sky, the Dao Artifact hooked its prey, and forcibly fished the Red-Robed Guest from the pinnacle of victory straight into the jaws of death.

The seating area for Naming Stronghold was dead silent.

Chunyangzi stood there, golden light flashing in his eyes like cold lightning.

He gazed at the Red-Robed Guest's corpse. What surfaced in his mind was the Sky-Hanging Thread Hook, and layers of cold chills rolled up in his heart.

"War is perilous and battles are fraught with danger. Truly fraught with danger."

"Such a Dao Artifact... exactly which faction's foundation on Flowing Cloud Peak does it belong to?!"

On the Cloud Marsh Arena, the residual blood qi gradually dissipated.

The Red-Robed Guest's scarlet robes lay spread over the cloud lake. They were no longer bright, leaving behind only a dead, dark red.

It was Naming Stronghold's turn to go down and collect the corpse.

Chunyangzi looked at Ning Zhuo. Ning Zhuo slowly nodded and, under everyone's watchful eyes, walked into the martial arena.

His heart was extremely heavy-

The Red-Robed Guest was dead.

The Five-Win Martial Contest had completely turned into a bloody game!

The score was two to two.

The final match would decide the ownership of the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar, and would also decide whether Naming Stronghold could truly establish a foothold on Flowing Cloud Peak.

"Ning Zhuo, let's talk in a bit," Chunyangzi transmitted via divine sense.

Shortly after, inside Chunyangzi's Cave Manor.

Inside the Cave Manor, the lamplight was faintly yellow.

Chunyangzi sat upright in the seat of honor. The old wound on the webbing of his right hand had not yet fully healed, and golden light faintly circulated within his sleeves. There was no anger on his face, but it was more terrifying than if he were furious.

Ning Zhuo sat below him, his expression solemn.

The two remained silent for a long while.

Chunyangzi was the first to speak. "We cannot lose the final match."

Ning Zhuo nodded. "We cannot lose."

If they lost, there was no hope for the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar. The momentum Naming Stronghold had built over the previous three matches would also be thoroughly suppressed. The Red-Robed Guest would have died in vain, and Tan Zhu's deterrence would become a laughingstock for others.

Chunyangzi slowly nodded. "Therefore, we must win."

He paused for a moment before adding, "The safest candidate is Tan Zhu."

Ning Zhuo nodded, not finding this the least bit surprising.

In the previous battle, Tan Zhu forcefully killed Yue Qingshan; his fierce prestige remained. If they could invite him to fight again, it was indeed their most reliable move.

It was just that...

Ning Zhuo's gaze flickered as he said with deep meaning, "I feel that he might not be willing to take action so easily."

Chunyangzi stood up. "Let's go see him together."

Tan Zhu sat deep inside his Cave Manor.

He was still wearing a gray-black deep robe. His skin was pale, his eyebrows thin and faint, and his breathing was so light it was almost non-existent. He looked like an old man who had been suffering from a prolonged illness, sitting in the shadows. The only difference from before was that a cup of cold tea sat beside him.

Seeing Chunyangzi and Ning Zhuo enter, he raised his eyes.

Chunyangzi's gaze was heavy. "For the fifth match, we would like to ask you to fight, Fellow Daoist."

Tan Zhu coughed softly. "My consumption in the last match was massive."

Chunyangzi said, "If you are willing to fight, Naming Stronghold will definitely compensate you."

Tan Zhu asked, "Compensate?"

He seemed to smile slightly.

Tan Zhu continued, "I want the creditor's rights."

Tan Zhu slowly said, "The creditor's rights related to the Naming Fire Furnace. I want to purchase the share in your hands, Chunyangzi. At the same time, Little Friend Ning Zhuo must also take out at least a portion of his."

The Cave Manor abruptly fell silent.

The golden light in Chunyangzi's eyes condensed. "Exactly how much?"

Tan Zhu quoted a number.

The asking price was so high that it made the corner of Ning Zhuo's eye twitch slightly.

Ning Zhuo and Chunyangzi couldn't help but exchange a glance, a familiar feeling rising in both of their hearts once again.

Tan Zhu's quotation was so high that it didn't seem like a spur-of-the-moment idea; rather, it seemed like it had been calculated long ago, specifically waiting for them at this very moment.

Chunyangzi remained silent.

Tan Zhu said, "My lifespan is nearing its end, and I suffered massive consumption in the previous match. If you want me to fight in the fifth match, I would be gambling my remaining life one more time. This price is not excessive."

Chunyangzi asked, "What does Fellow Daoist want the creditor's rights for?"

Tan Zhu didn't answer.

He merely picked up the cold tea and took a sip.

As the tea entered his throat, he coughed softly twice more.

Tan Zhu said, "If you two feel this is inappropriate, you are naturally welcome to find someone else more capable."

With things said to this point, there was no room left for further negotiation.

Chunyangzi stood up, his sleeves shifting slightly. "This matter is of grave importance. Please allow us time to discuss."

Tan Zhu nodded. "As you wish."

After leaving Tan Zhu's Cave Manor, Chunyangzi and Ning Zhuo remained silent the entire way.

It wasn't until they returned to their own Cave Manor and activated the formation that Chunyangzi spoke coldly, "So this is what Tan Zhu was waiting for."

Ning Zhuo's expression was grave. "What he wants isn't Spirit Stones or precious materials, but the Naming Fire Furnace creditor's rights. If we actually agree to it, he will instantly become the Grand Chief of our Naming Stronghold."

Chunyangzi's gaze was dark and profound.

Since the initial establishment of Naming Stronghold, the seating hierarchy had been inextricably linked to debts, contributions, and creditor's rights. Chunyangzi being able to sit in the position of Grand Chief was partly due to his strength and prestige, and partly because of his heavy debt relationship with Ning Zhuo.

Chunyangzi said, "As expected, he took the initiative to join Naming Stronghold solely for the Naming Fire Furnace."

Ning Zhuo said, "Not only that, he also wants to use this opportunity to truly secure his position within Naming Stronghold!"

Chunyangzi looked at Ning Zhuo. "Who do you think is behind him?"

Ning Zhuo smiled bitterly and shook his head. "I'm just a newcomer, you know."

Chunyangzi furrowed his brow tightly.

Ning Zhuo's expression was similar to his as he sighed softly, "Tan Zhu's entry into Naming Stronghold was quite sudden. His lifespan is near its end, yet he was still willing to risk his life in such a situation. In the last battle, he killed Yue Qingshan too cleanly, and too decisively. Reflecting on it now, it seems there was a hidden motive."
