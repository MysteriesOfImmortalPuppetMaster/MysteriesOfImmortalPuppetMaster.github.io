Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 932: The Dharma Pool Minor Trial

Although Ning Zhuo was only at the mid Foundation Establishment Stage, relying on the mutual conversion of his Five Elements Techniques, one portion could expand to more than six. This allowed him to handle opponents with higher cultivation bases with ease.

It was also by relying on this that he had defeated Liu Jinke.

However, this method was also exposed as a result.

Just as Liu Jinke's trump card of being able to resurrect even after decapitation was exposed, Ning Zhuo naturally suffered a similar loss in this regard.

Ning Zhuo had to guard against any traps the opposing side might set up targeting this point.

He pondered over his own methods and found that he actually already possessed two countermeasures.

The first was the Ice Jade Wine.

After drinking this wine, it could use spirit treasures to drive qi treasures, using the spirit to replenish qi. It was the optimal cultivation resource for practicing the Ice Heart Art.

For others, drinking it could make their qi sea cultivation soar, and replenishing spiritual energy on the battlefield was naturally not an issue.

But this method had a huge drawback: replenishing spiritual energy meant consuming divine sense!

Divine sense was equally important to Ning Zhuo, perhaps even much more important.

Because in normal combat, Ning Zhuo would control a large number of mechanical constructs. Even casting Five Elements Techniques required the consumption of divine sense.

His consumption in terms of divine sense was also massive!

Therefore, this option was not adopted.

Besides this, the Headwind Kite excelled in prolonged combat and, under ideal conditions, had infinite endurance.

Ning Zhuo currently had this kind of mechanical flying bird on hand; it was a previous stock belonging to Qing Huangzi.

But the problem was that the Headwind Kite was large and moved relatively slowly. When flying high in the sky, it required protection. This flaw was quite obvious, and it belonged to the inheritance of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder].

Since the latter had already been exposed, the Headwind Kite was also an open card.

For Ning Zhuo to rely on it carried a great risk. The enemy would very likely target this point.

After a moment of deep thought, Ning Zhuo ordered Gongsun Yan to forge mechanical parts and had Old Chef go out to buy talismans and Medicinal Pills. Then, he personally stepped out to participate in a Rising Cloud Minor Trial.

The organizer was the Artifact Refining Hall.

As one of the sixteen halls of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, the Artifact Refining Hall was managed by Tie Kuang. During every Flying Cloud Assembly, they would host Rising Cloud Minor Trials related to artifact refining.

Every round of this minor trial presented a different test, requiring cultivators to refine artifacts. As long as they met the standard, there were material rewards. Those who stood out would naturally receive the attention of the Artifact Refining Hall, or perhaps even an invitation to join them.

This time, the test topic for the Artifact Refining Hall's minor trial was - the Dharma Pool.

A Dharma Pool was a type of storage treasure, but it only stored one thing: a cultivator's spiritual energy.

The special thing about the Dharma Pool was that it uniquely matched and bound to a specific cultivator. During refinement, the user had to act personally and could not have someone else do it for them.

The pool embryo had to be repeatedly tempered by one's own spiritual energy. The pool walls needed to be engraved with four types of Formations: energy storage, energy locking, energy recovery, and pressure release, which consumed the cultivator's divine sense. Finally, when opening the pool, one had to drip in vital essence blood so that the Dharma Pool would recognize its master's aura.

A Dharma Pool refined in this way would be closest to the cultivator, akin to another organ. Only then could it store their spiritual energy on normal days, and when needed, the pool would open its gates to feed back into the qi sea, sustaining crucial spiritual energy.

In battle, this was often the key to victory. During artifact refining, it could also help cultivators overcome difficult hurdles, providing critical endurance to ultimately succeed in their refining.

And for Ning Zhuo, this was exactly what he lacked the most right now.

This Rising Cloud Minor Trial was set up on the Earth Fire Square.

The square was octagonal, and the ground was paved with black iron bricks. Every brick had fire patterns on it, and a dark red luster seeped from deep within the lines, as if a slumbering sea of fire was hidden underground.

Around the square stood twelve bronze statues of furnace beasts - some dragons, some tigers, some turtles, and some vermilion birds. Faint fiery breaths intermittently spewed from their bronze mouths, filling the air with a mixed scent of rust and charcoal fire.

On the high platform, several deacons of the Artifact Refining Hall sat upright.

"Wait, look who's here?"

"It's actually Ning Zhuo!"

"Didn't he establish the Naming Stronghold and is preparing to challenge Flowing Cloud Peak? Why would he come here to participate in a minor trial?"

Ning Zhuo's arrival quickly caused ripples of commotion.

His reputation was currently booming.

The recent founding of the Naming Stronghold, the joining of five Nascent Souls, and the execution of Liu Jinke to establish dominance - these deeds set Ning Zhuo apart from other genius cultivators.

"Hmph!"

"Magical combat is magical combat, mechanisms are mechanisms, and artifact refining is artifact refining."

In the crowd, a young cultivator cast the sharpest gaze at Ning Zhuo.

This person was tall and thin with a slightly reddish complexion. There was a small fire mark between his eyebrows. His hands were broad, with thick and hard knuckles. The palms of his hands, baked by fire poison year-round, showed a deep red color approaching purple.

He wore an artifact refiner's robe with a black base and red patterns. A compact hammer pouch hung at his waist. The mouth of the pouch bulged slightly, faintly showing the flow of fiery qi.

His name was Tie Yanqiu, an artifact refining genius among the younger generation of the Artifact Refining Hall, enjoying quite a reputation.

It was said that at the age of ten, he could distinguish between thirty-six types of fire properties. At fifteen, he independently repaired a Foundation Establishment Magic Artifact. After reaching Foundation Establishment, he even stayed in the earth fire room for seven consecutive days to forge a "Crimson Gall Furnace," making him widely favored by the older generation of the Artifact Refining Hall.

He wasn't very clear about the specific situation of the Naming Fire Furnace, as he had been cultivating in the artifact refining workshop the whole time. The reason he participated in this minor trial was that he was also missing a Dharma Pool.

His Dharma Pool had been destroyed in a recent artifact refining attempt due to his own mistake.

To quickly replenish his Dharma Pool, this Rising Cloud Minor Trial was naturally his first choice.

The first stage of the trial was "Identifying Materials and Determining the Pool."

A deacon of the Artifact Refining Hall waved his sleeve, and an iron desk rose in front of everyone. The desks were filled with materials, easily numbering in the dozens: Black-Patterned Copper, Mica Silver Sand, Crimson Furnace Fire Crystal, Cyan Wood Charcoal Marrow, Profound Water Moistening Copper, Abundant Earth Pottery Mother, Cold Iron Filings, Golden Essence Powder, Flowing Cloud Sand, and so on.

The participants had to choose materials suitable for their own Cultivation Arts within the time it takes an incense stick to burn, determining the broad category of their Dharma Pool.

Some chose a Fire Pool.

Some chose a Lightning Pool.

Some chose a Water Essence Pool.

Tie Yanqiu didn't hesitate in the slightest and acted immediately.

He took the Black-Patterned Copper for the pool's frame, the Crimson Furnace Fire Crystal for the pool's core, and selected a small amount of Golden Essence Powder to stabilize the pool's walls.

He wanted to forge a "Crimson Gold Energy Recovery Pool" that harmonized fire and metal. This pool possessed strong explosive power, and when opened, spiritual energy would surge back extremely fast. It was most suitable for Fire Element and Metal Element cultivators to sustain themselves at crucial moments.

His movements were crisp and neat. He picked out materials like plucking the strings of a qin. Though seemingly casual, every piece of material was selected with absolute precision.

Many cultivators from the Artifact Refining Hall surrounding the area nodded secretly.

Within the Artifact Refining Hall, Tie Yanqiu's artifact refining strength had already been widely acknowledged. His performance matched his reputation.

"Originally, Tie Yanqiu was a shoo-in for first place. Who would have thought that right before this test began, Ning Zhuo would suddenly appear."

"So this minor trial is even more worth watching."

"Although Ning Zhuo is strong, his strength lies elsewhere. He is a mechanical cultivator. When it comes to artifact refining, how could he be our Yanqiu's match?"

"Not necessarily! This person has quite a solid foundation in artifact refining. You must not know the specific details of the minor trial at the Naming Fire Furnace."

Amidst the discussions, everyone inevitably looked toward Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo respectively took five types of materials: Mica Silver Sand, Cyan Wood Charcoal Marrow, Crimson Furnace Fire Crystal, Profound Water Moistening Copper, and Abundant Earth Pottery Mother. He then took a small amount of Flowing Cloud Sand to harmonize them.

He had gathered all Five Elements.

Some cultivators immediately frowned.

"He wants to make a Five Elements Dharma Pool?"

"The biggest taboo in refining a Dharma Pool is biting off more than you can chew. Most of the time, we'd rather have a pool with a single property than let its attributes become complicated."

"The cultivation in Ning Zhuo's qi sea is precisely Five Elements spiritual energy."

"Can't he use his Lifebound Magic Artifact to rapidly convert his spiritual energy? Wouldn't refining a single-element Dharma Pool be fine too?"

Everyone was puzzled.

The first stage ended, and the second stage began - "Melting Materials and Forging the Embryo."

Earth fire rose from beneath the iron desks. The flames were divided into three colors: red, yellow, and white, each possessing different fire properties. The participants needed to control the fire with their own spiritual energy, smelting the materials into a pool embryo.

Tie Yanqiu's artifact refining strength was immediately fully displayed!

As he pressed his hands down, the flames circled slowly around his materials like a tamed beast. The Black-Patterned Copper softened first, the Crimson Furnace Fire Crystal melted next, and the Golden Essence Powder was sprinkled last. Every step was clean and beautiful, the heat control so steady it was as if an invisible scale hung within the furnace.

Very soon, a fist-sized crimson-gold pool embryo took shape in front of him.

The pool embryo resembled a miniature cauldron and also a heart furnace. Red light flowed on its surface, and golden patterns were hidden on its inner walls, faintly emitting a sharp heat.

"Excellent!"

Disciples of the Artifact Refining Hall at the edge of the square cheered softly.

Tie Yanqiu's expression remained unchanged, but he slightly raised his chin.

He looked over at Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo's momentum was far inferior to his, and his flames were extremely calm.

The Crimson Furnace Fire Crystal was enveloped by fire, not rushing to melt.

The damp and cold qi was first forced out of the Profound Water Moistening Copper by a gentle fire.

The Cyan Wood Charcoal Marrow was placed on the outermost edge; the fire did not burn the wood, but instead used the wood property to draw the flame.

The Abundant Earth Pottery Mother sank to the bottom like ballast. The Mica Silver Sand, meanwhile, was drawn by Ning Zhuo's subtle spiritual energy into strands of silver threads, wandering among the five materials.

Fine sweat seeped from Ning Zhuo's forehead.

The Five Elements Qi Rhythm Art circulated slowly within his body. The principles of the generation and destruction of the Five Elements were kneaded bit by bit into the pool embryo by him.

Half an hour later, a small pool embryo with flowing five-colored light hovered above his palm.

The pool embryo wasn't as dazzling as Tie Yanqiu's crimson-gold pool embryo, but it was warmer and smoother.

The five-colored radiance flowed slowly within the pool walls. They were neither mixed nor separated; they were like five streams entering their respective channels, ultimately returning to the same lake.

Seeing this, Tie Yanqiu's expression changed slightly, and his heart filled with shock. "Wood does not suppress Fire, Fire does not intrude on Water, Earth sits in the center, the Metal Element forms the veins, and Water qi moisturizes the edges. He actually managed to balance the Five Elements so perfectly - not a fraction more, not a fraction less. Everything is just right! How did he do it?"

The third stage, "Engraving Patterns and Opening the Pool."

The Artifact Refining Hall provided four Formation diagrams for the Dharma Pool, involving energy storage, energy locking, energy recovery, and pressure release respectively.

Tie Yanqiu threw the distracting thoughts out of his mind, suppressed his emotions, and picked up his carving knife, his expression incomparably focused.

His Crimson Gold Energy Recovery Pool excelled in swift ferocity, so he engraved the energy recovery Formation patterns extremely deep, and used the energy locking Formation patterns to suppress the bottom of the pool, preventing the spiritual energy from leaking prematurely. At the very end of carving, he bit his fingertip and dripped in a drop of vital essence blood.

The vital essence blood fell into the pool.

The crimson-gold pool embryo immediately trembled, emitting a faint buzz.

The pool was complete!

Seeing this, many deacons present nodded slightly.

The quality of Tie Yanqiu's Dharma Pool was already quite good. Once opened, it could spew out its stored spiritual energy in a short period of time. If used at a crucial moment in magical combat, it was enough to momentarily turn the tide.

Tie Yanqiu also recovered some of his confidence in his heart.

He put down his carving knife and looked towards Ning Zhuo.

The latter was still engraving patterns, and carving extremely slowly.

The onlookers transmitted messages with their divine sense, conversing privately.

"Isn't Ning Zhuo being a bit too cautious?"

"A Five Elements pool embryo is too complex. If it were me, I wouldn't know where to start carving either."

"Sigh, having all Five Elements is just asking for trouble after all."

However, the expressions of the several deacons hosting this minor artifact refining trial grew increasingly solemn.
