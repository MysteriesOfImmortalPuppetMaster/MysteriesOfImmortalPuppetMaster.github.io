Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 934: Liu Jinke vs Ning Zhuo - The Second Battle

Herd-Startling Whistle, Thousand-Catty Pendant, Shattering Thunder Beads, Connection-Severing Blade Talisman...

Five Golden Marrow Life-Renewing Pills, three Flame-Evading Blood-Protecting Pills, one bottle of Iron Bone Stiffening Pills...

Liu Jinke looked at the magic treasures, talismans, and Medicinal Pills piled upon his desk. Inside his heart, feelings of both coldness and heat surged, intertwining as they ebbed and flowed against one another.

His experiences over these past few days had given him a completely new understanding of Flowing Cloud Peak, the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and even his entire cultivation career!

He was acutely aware of his situation - he was already deeply mired in this vortex, sinking further by the day.

Only by following the will of the various large and small factions and accepting Ning Zhuo's challenge could he survive!

He still feared Ning Zhuo, but the fear was no longer like before, pressing down on him so hard he couldn't breathe.

The massive amount of sponsorship acted like layer upon layer of golden armor draped over his heart. People were just like this: when their hands were empty, they felt the road ahead was a mountain of blades and a sea of fire. But now, with so many magic treasures, Medicinal Pills, and talismans piled high on his desk, courage began to regrow from the cracks in Liu Jinke's heart.

"Ning Zhuo is merely at the mid-Foundation Establishment Stage," Liu Jinke muttered.

"Last time, I didn't know his background and was specifically countered by him. This time..."

He subconsciously stroked his right chest.

There, the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus had been preliminarily refined. Suspended beneath the flesh and skin of his chest, it was like a newly born golden-red heart. With every beat, blood-gold essence liquid fed back into his entire body, allowing him to rapidly recover from even the slightest deficit of golden blood.

He also thought of his newly mastered and diligently practiced Sunder Gold and Stone Spell, and his confidence surged further: "This time, it is still unknown who will die at whose hands!"

Despite this, he still took the initiative to seek out the Demon Subjugation Hall.

He hoped for more support from the Demon Subjugation Hall.

However, the envoy from the Demon Subjugation Hall had a cold expression this time.

"The Demon Subjugation Hall never asked you to fight Ning Zhuo to the death. Now, your second battle with Ning Zhuo has become a matter of life and death. This goes against the original intentions of our Demon Subjugation Hall!"

Liu Jinke stared at him for a long time before letting out a cold sneer.

"Good. What a fine Demon Subjugation Hall." He flicked his sleeves and left.

He wasn't surprised by the Demon Subjugation Hall's attitude. The intelligence that the current Hall Master of the Demon Subjugation Hall, Zhong Dao, highly valued Ning Zhuo had already spread throughout the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gates.

As he walked out the door, Liu Jinke had completely made up his mind.

The Demon Subjugation Hall was no longer participating.

What did this mean?

It meant that he, Liu Jinke, had gone from being a pawn of the Demon Subjugation Hall to a blade bet upon by the numerous factions of Flowing Cloud Peak.

He only had one path: to accept the battle!

"Then I will fight!"

The battlefield was still set at the martial arena near the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

This time, there were far more spectators than last time.

The successful establishment of the Nanming Stronghold had caused a storm. Ning Zhuo's every move was magnified, with who knew how many people secretly keeping an eye on him.

As soon as the news of Liu Jinke fighting again came out, it immediately drew the attention of numerous factions on Flowing Cloud Peak. The Green Tea Society, Floating Life Association, Thorns Society, Bird and Beast Manor, Water-Severing Blade Pavilion, Thunder Cloud Association, and others all had people present.

It wasn't just Flowing Cloud Peak; Danxia Peak, Myriad Beast Peak, the Demon Subjugation Hall, and others also came to observe.

Ning Zhuo entered first. Clad in the Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment, the white-robed youth was calm and unhurried.

Unlike last time, he carried a massive wooden box on his back. It was the Spirit-Siphoning Box.

Composed of Five Elements talismans and mechanical components, it could continuously siphon ambient spiritual energy from the outside world and convert it into usable spiritual energy for the cultivator.

Around Ning Zhuo's waist, there were five additional small, five-colored pools shaped like flat flasks. Faintly, the brilliance of the Five Elements flowed within them.

Tucked inside his lapel was a Cloud Tide Essence-Returning Talisman, and his Storage Belt contained Five Qis Essence-Returning Pills.

These were all preparations to cover his weaknesses.

When Liu Jinke entered the arena, his aura was vastly different!

His expression was cold and stern, and the golden glint in his eyes was deeper than in the previous battle.

Beneath the flesh and skin of his chest, a ball of golden-red light pulsed slightly, as if he possessed an extra, peculiar heart - the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus!

Ning Zhuo only needed a single glance to confirm that the intelligence was accurate.

Besides that, Liu Jinke had Connection-Severing Blade Talismans hidden in his sleeves, Shattering Thunder Beads in his storage bracelet, the Herd-Startling Whistle hanging at his waist, and a pitch-black heavy accessory - the Thousand-Catty Pendant. Even before it was activated, it made the surrounding earth qi faintly stagnate.

The two stood facing each other, and the sound of the wind in the arena temporarily quieted down significantly.

Liu Jinke stared at Ning Zhuo, the golden light in the depths of his eyes gradually growing intense.

The Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus in his chest throbbed, pushing the blood-gold essence liquid throughout his body. He reached out to stroke the Herd-Startling Whistle at his waist and squeezed the Thousand-Catty Pendant.

The fear in Liu Jinke's heart seemed to be suppressed by the layers of treasure light, finally transforming into a violent surge of confidence.

"Ning Zhuo," he shouted in a low voice. "Last time, I underestimated my enemy. A mere mid-Foundation Establishment Stage cultivator actually plotted against me, forcing me into a corner. But you also chose this path, so the situation today is you seeking your own death!"

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly. "The fact that Senior Brother can come again shows your resolute Dao Heart. Thank you, Senior Brother, for giving me this opportunity to fulfill my promise."

Liu Jinke sneered coldly in anger, said no more, and suddenly stomped his foot!

Boom!

The Thousand-Catty Pendant was activated.

In an instant, the entire martial arena seemed to be pressed down by an invisible giant hand. The black stone bricks on the ground trembled in unison, and a muffled sound rolled along the formation lines.

Even the spectating crowd felt a slight sinking sensation beneath their feet.

Let alone Ning Zhuo, who was in the arena.

In just a split second, earth power surged up from all directions, acting like a thick, massive iron garment that directly covered the entire martial arena.

Ning Zhuo's gaze was deep and dark. If the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot was activated now, its speed would inevitably be greatly affected.

He waved his long sleeves, releasing a massive number of mechanical flying birds, comprising five types including the Joint-Clasping Pheasants, Headwind Kites, Gold-Thread Caged Finches, and Sleeve-Piercing Falcons.

The bird flock surged forward, intending to execute the attrition tactics from last time.

Liu Jinke sneered, grabbed the Herd-Startling Whistle at his waist, and blew it forcefully.

The whistle's sound was extremely sharp and piercing, like a red-hot silver needle suddenly jabbing into one's eardrums.

Many cultivators by the sidelines frowned. Those with slightly weaker cultivation bases even subconsciously circulated their spiritual energy to protect their eardrums.

The whistle generated waves of sound. As the sound waves swept over, they instantly threw Ning Zhuo's newly released mechanical bird flock into chaos.

The Headwind Kites hung high in the cloudy sky, yet they seemed pulled by erratic winds, their wings clattering loudly. The dive trajectories of the Neck-Spear Cranes constantly veered off course, and the Joint-Clasping Pheasants collided with one another mid-leap...

"Bird and Beast Manor's Herd-Startling Whistle!" someone immediately recognized it and felt confused. "That thing was originally meant to disrupt beast herds. It can actually affect mechanical birds too?"

"The mechanical flying birds from the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder] are controlled by musical rhythm and divine sense. The Herd-Startling Whistle might not be able to disrupt the mechanisms, but it can disrupt the rhythm and agitate the divine sense, severely interfering with Ning Zhuo's command tempo," another cultivator explained in a low voice.

"Interesting." Ning Zhuo floated backward, releasing the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot midway.

He sat upright on the back seat, took out a Zither, laid it across his knees, and placed his fingertips on the strings.

Zheng!

Zheng-zheng!

Zheng-zheng, zheng-zheng...

The Zither's notes were like clear springs striking rocks, and like white cranes flapping their wings, instantly pressing into the sharp whistle sounds.

The two sounds clashed in midair. One was chaotic, the other was steady; one was like a herd of frightened beasts, the other like a phoenix crying upon a high ridge.

Dong Nishang watched the battle from within the crowd. Hearing Ning Zhuo's playing, she couldn't help but feel slightly surprised. This was the exact sheet music she had gifted Ning Zhuo. She hadn't expected that after just a few short days, Ning Zhuo would actually be able to execute it so well.

Guided by Ning Zhuo's Zither, the initially scattered formation of the mechanical bird flock rapidly gathered together. With feathers vibrating and bamboo bones ringing clearly, they once again spread out in midair like a formation of dark clouds.

Seeing this, Liu Jinke wasn't the least bit disappointed. Instead, a sinister and cold smile flashed across his eyes.

"Ning Zhuo, watch closely!"

He clapped his palms together. Golden blood surged in his palms, but unlike last time when it turned into violently shot needles, it transformed into a fine, dense golden mist.

Urged by his spiritual energy, the golden mist quickly spread forward, sweeping toward the mechanical bird flock.

Sunder Gold and Stone Spell!

The golden mist was silent and traceless, drilling directly into the crevices.

It drilled into the tiny bearings in the necks of the Neck-Spear Cranes, causing the bearings to instantly stiffen. It drilled into the talismanic veins inside the bellies of the Gold-Thread Caged Finches, causing the talisman light to immediately dim. It drilled into the mortise and tenon joints in the legs of the Joint-Clasping Pheasants; the joints slightly dislocated, emitting a teeth-aching clack-clack sound.

A Neck-Spear Crane was just about to shoot out its crane neck when its neck suddenly drooped. The spear mechanism dislocated on the spot and fell to the ground with a clatter.

Several Gold-Thread Caged Finches were just about to explode into entangling bamboo threads. But before the bamboo threads had spread out even three inches, they came loose section by section, falling apart in midair into tattered wisps as if their bones and sinews had been pulled out.

The Joint-Clasping Pheasants drilled into the ground and should have continuously detonated, but because the internal spring reeds were jammed by the golden mist, they froze in the dirt, unable to move at all.

The sidelines instantly erupted into an uproar.

"What kind of Technique is this?"

"Ning Zhuo's mechanical bird flock is completely countered!"

"Liu Jinke's preparations this time are truly adequate. Ning Zhuo is a mechanism cultivator; he's in danger this time."

Ning Zhuo furrowed his brows slightly.

"The Sunder Gold and Stone Technique is troublesome as expected," he sighed inwardly.

The mechanical structures were directly dismantled. The more exquisite the mechanical flying birds and the more components they had, the easier it was for the mist to drill into their crevices.

But fortunately, Ning Zhuo also came prepared.

He immediately activated his divine sense, and his spiritual energy was fiercely consumed at the same time.

Countless loose mechanical parts scattered across the martial arena gathered together. Key nodes interlocked, mortise and tenon joints realigned, and even the broken formation lines had a somewhat rudimentary reconnection.

Adaptation Tokens!

Ning Zhuo had traded for a massive number of these types of mechanical components from Lu Zeng. At this moment, they caused Ning Zhuo's battle-loss situation to drastically pull up from rock bottom, resulting in only about a ten-percent loss.

"Good!" Lu Zeng, mixed in the crowd, showed visible joy upon seeing this.

Especially hearing the uproar from the surrounding cultivators, it multiplied his sense of accomplishment.

Was it only the cultivators by the sidelines paying attention to this battle?

It could be said that the entire Myriad Manifestations Sect's main mountain gate was paying attention to it to varying degrees.

And in that instant just now, the Adaptation Tokens shone brilliantly, making an extremely beautiful debut. Just from this scene alone, Lu Zeng was certain that his mechanical component would no longer have to worry about sales.

The bird flock rose again, lunging at Liu Jinke.

The Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus beneath Liu Jinke's chest gave a violent thump.

Thump!

The sound was deep, like a muffled drumbeat.

Blood-gold essence liquid spat out from the nine orifices, flowing into the golden blood throughout Liu Jinke's body. The golden mist in his palms immediately flourished by another thirty percent, acting like a tide of dark gold that surged toward the bird flock once more.

The mechanical birds continuously disintegrated.

Bamboo bones, feathers, axle springs, and talisman pieces fell from midair one after another, like a rain of shattered mechanisms.

Even though the Adaptation Tokens took effect again, the results were getting worse and worse.

Because the Adaptation Tokens themselves were also mechanical components, they were also countered by the Sunder Gold and Stone Spell!

Liu Jinke hadn't noticed them in the previous wave, but this time the Adaptation Tokens were already exposed. He would naturally target them.

The mechanical bird flock continually collapsed, scattering over a large area on the ground, before being reassembled and then their bodies decomposing once again, pouring down like heavy rain.

Looking at this scene, Liu Jinke could no longer suppress the pleasure in his heart.

He couldn't help but laugh out loud.

Last time, he had been entangled by these mechanical birds like a trapped beast, charging left and right, his golden blood being ground away bit by bit. But in today's battle, countless mechanical flying birds fell apart under the Sunder Gold and Stone Technique, allowing him to vent the majority of his previous pent-up frustration.

"Ning Zhuo!" he laughed mockingly. "How many mechanical flying birds do you have left?"

As he spoke, his figure moved like lightning, piercing through the thinned-out flock of mechanical birds and charging straight at Ning Zhuo.

Riding the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot, Ning Zhuo continuously retreated.

Liu Jinke used his Innate Talent to cast Techniques, activating golden needles and violently shooting them out while simultaneously closing the distance.

Ning Zhuo then deployed the Golden Light Collar and Cyan-White Jade Ring. Golden halos and cyan-white light curtains spread out layer by layer to protect him.

A fierce glint flashed in Liu Jinke's eyes: "Still trying to rely on these two magic treasures to buy time?"

With a flick of his wrist, he threw out three Shattering Thunder Beads.

Boom! Boom! Boom!

The Thunder Beads exploded consecutively, and green-purple lightning bloomed on the light curtains like three lotuses.

The Golden Light Collar's halo violently twisted, and the Cyan-White Jade Ring's light curtain crackled loudly, with cracks spreading like spiderwebs.

Cultivators from the Thunder Cloud Association on the sidelines nodded slightly upon seeing this scene.

In the crowd, Cao Gui watched with a pale face.

At the critical moment, Ning Zhuo summoned the Hollow-Heart Blade-Breaking Guard, leaving it in place to temporarily block Liu Jinke.

He had no choice but to temporarily put away the Golden Light Collar and Cyan-White Jade Ring.

Liu Jinke dashed left and right. Ning Zhuo mainly relied on the Hollow-Heart Blade-Breaking Guard to block him, while using the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot to constantly create distance.

The two exchanged blows back and forth, falling into a stalemate for a time.

Both of their spiritual energys were rapidly declining.

Ning Zhuo directly swallowed a Five Qis Essence-Returning Pill.

The Medicinal Pill melted upon entering his mouth. Five pure streams of medicinal power merged into his Five Elements - Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth - like five small streams flowing into a dry riverbed.

This was still not enough!

He directly activated a Dharma Pool.

The five-colored Dharma Pool lit up faintly. The earthen yellow light in the center first stabilized his Qi Sea, followed by the generation of wood qi, the continuous burning of fire qi, the sharp formation of metal qi, and the smooth circulation of water qi. His Five Elements spiritual energy flowed back in an orderly manner, rapidly making up for his expenditure.

Someone noticed this detail and softly exclaimed, "Ning Zhuo activated a Dharma Pool."

Liu Jinke also saw it and sneered inwardly: "You have a Dharma Pool, but I have the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus!"

He once again channeled his spiritual energy, pouring it into the Thousand-Catty Pendant.

The pressure on the battlefield skyrocketed once more!

The mechanical chariot's speed visibly slowed down by several fractions. As the profound wheels beneath its belly rotated, they emitted dull friction sounds, as if every maneuver required dragging ten thousand catties of iron and stone.

Liu Jinke attempted to close the distance!

Ning Zhuo controlled the Hollow-Heart Blade-Breaking Guard, tracing a precise arc to intercept Liu Jinke once again.

A talisman inside Liu Jinke's sleeve ignited. The thin, black blade mark on the talisman's surface suddenly lit up, forming a streak of blade light.

Connection-Severing Blade Talisman!

The blade light flashed, and the suspended spiritual energy thread between Ning Zhuo and the Hollow-Heart Blade-Breaking Guard was directly severed.

The Hollow-Heart Blade-Breaking Guard's movements paused.

Liu Jinke transformed into a golden rainbow, bypassing the guard and lunging straight for Ning Zhuo.

Situ Xing's gaze suddenly focused: "Danger!"

The hearts of all the cultivators on the sidelines tightened simultaneously.

Liu Jinke in close combat was the true Liu Jinke!

At this moment, his arms were covered in golden armor, and his fingertips had turned into golden blades. The Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus in his chest beat like a drum. His entire body's golden blood surged beneath his skin, resembling tens of thousands of golden snakes slithering about. His imposing aura was extremely astonishing.

"Ning Zhuo, accept your defeat!"
