Chapter 878: Mother Calculates Fortune

Ning Zhuo watched the entire process from the sidelines. Seeing Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin easily resolve the hidden danger, he was genuinely happy. "Mother, since you woke up this time, your strength has advanced by leaps and bounds. You've become so strong."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin flipped her wrist and gently tapped Ning Zhuo's head with the handle of the scythe. "How many times have I taught you? You must not be arrogant or complacent."

"I was able to handle this easily only because every profession has its specialization, and I already have a bit of a grasp on the Strange Path."

Ning Zhuo covered his head, "Yes, yes, yes, Mother, I was wrong. Your child really should be more humble and cautious."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin sat cross-legged on the ground and patted the stone bricks beside her. "Come, Little Zhuo, sit next to Mother. Tell me everything you've experienced."

Ning Zhuo happily sat cross-legged next to Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin, right up against her cold mechanical body, yet feeling incomparably warm in his heart.

He recounted everything he had experienced after coming out of the Ghostly Black Marshlands.

After listening, Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin nodded in praise, "Little Zhuo, you did very well."

"First, you did not stick strictly to the rules, but infiltrated the Jade Cleansing Pavilion ahead of time, taking whatever you needed and acting with flexibility."

"Second, you did not blindly follow my message. You weren't in a rush to claim the talisman businesses, nor did you meet with my close friends from when I was alive. This was a decision you made after assessing the situation - it was both cautious and wise."

"Third, you carved out your own piece of the world all by yourself. This is what makes Mother the happiest. Sigh, you've grown up! You truly are Mother's good son."

His own mother's praise made Ning Zhuo boundlessly happy. However, he was more concerned about Meng Yaoyin's injuries. "Mother, does your progress in the Strange Path help much with your Dao Injury? Next, should we look for fortunate encounters related to the Strange Path? Can we repair your mechanical body now?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin replied, "I can already suppress the Dao Injury somewhat. There is no need to be greedy for too many fortunate encounters on the Strange Path. Thanks to you, having Wu Hen's Dao-Transformation Bead allows me to walk a little further on the Strange Path."

"However, digesting all of this requires time."

"I will fall into a deep slumber once again."

"Ah?" Ning Zhuo's eyes went wide with surprise as he eagerly asked, "Then Mother, when will you be able to wake up this time?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin reached out to stroke Ning Zhuo's head, gently comforting him: "This slumber is completely without risk compared to before, but the duration will be much longer."

"Do not underestimate Wu Hen; to Mother, his value is extremely high."

"However, there is also a 'price' for this."

"During my slumber and secluded cultivation, you can do your best to repair my mechanical body. But the mechanical body must reach at least the Nascent Soul-level, otherwise it will be hard-pressed to bear my power after I wake up."

Ning Zhuo immediately patted his chest in guarantee: "Mother, don't worry, just leave everything to me."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin laughed three times and stroked Ning Zhuo's head three more times, "My son has grown up and can now share his Mother's burdens."

Her laughter subsided, and Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin changed the topic: "But you must be careful. The Myriad Manifestations Sect's rules forbid private fights, but it is absolutely not a safe place."

"Your cultivation is only at Foundation Establishment, yet you are surrounded by experts at the Golden Core and Nascent Soul levels. Within their main sect, existences at the Soul Formation-level and even Void Refinement-level remain hidden and inactive."

"Compared to them, you are as insignificant as an ant."

"For example, the one who was scheming against you just now possesses extremely thick Demonic Path destiny that is unfathomable."

Ning Zhuo nodded, "Your child has already tried to keep a low profile. But some things must be done! Joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect already concerns my Dao Path."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin, already aware of Ning Zhuo's experiences and plans, agreed, "White Paper Immortal City is indeed a treasured land for you to establish the foundation of your Dao Path. Your desire to join a faction within the Myriad Manifestations Sect to protect your future profits in the White Paper Immortal City is also correct."

Hearing Meng Yaoyin's approval, Ning Zhuo was overjoyed, "Mother, which faction of the Myriad Manifestations Sect do you think I should join?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin lightly tapped the tip of Ning Zhuo's nose, "Little Zhuo, your understanding of the Myriad Manifestations Sect is more up-to-date than Mother's. However... I can do a slight divination for you."

She immediately closed her eyes, holding her one arm and hand vertically in front of her chest. The Great Serpent Scythe lay horizontally across her knees, and a profound aura rose from her entire body.

Very soon, she opened her eyes and looked at Ning Zhuo.

Her eyes were deep and weather-beaten, revealing infinite, mysterious possibilities.

Ning Zhuo looked at her eagerly.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin chuckled softly, "Little Zhuo, Mother has calculated a few things for you."

"In your participation in the Nanming Fire Furnace trial tomorrow, your defeat is undoubtedly guaranteed."

Ning Zhuo was greatly surprised, "Ah?"

"Even though I rely on the White Rainbow Righeous Integrity Bone, is that still the case?"

"I felt the previous two rounds were very easy."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin smiled and nodded.

Ning Zhuo scratched his head, "That's not right. I possess the Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra. By burning the fragments of the furnace, I can strengthen the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit. This method is an absolute trump card!"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin smiled and shook her head.

Ning Zhuo suddenly realized, "I understand. Under tomorrow's circumstances, I won't be able to use the Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra!"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin replied, "The origin of this sutra is no small matter. In this current era, it is best not to expose it."

Ning Zhuo thought for a moment, "If that's the case, then I don't need to go tomorrow. Eh? Could it be the Boss who will obtain the furnace? He is also qualified to participate tomorrow."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin shook her head without speaking.

Ning Zhuo thought again, "No, that's not right. Even if I will lose tomorrow, I still have to go. Just putting on an act will do. As for the Boss, there are too many people paying attention to this trial; it is best if he doesn't show his face."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin let out a gratified laugh, "Little Zhuo, your state of mind is peaceful, undisturbed by victory or defeat. Very good, very good."

"However, if you want the Nanming Fire Furnace, it's not entirely without a chance. Mother will help you! Just follow the method I tell you, and the likelihood of success will be very high."

Ning Zhuo's eyes immediately lit up, "That works, Mother! Even if the furnace is broken, I can rely on it to negotiate with the major factions. This is a very significant bargaining chip."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin said, "Lean your ear over here."

Ning Zhuo leaned his big head close.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin softly gave him some instructions, making Ning Zhuo's eyes widen. He turned to look at her, showing a shocked expression, "Mother, is this feasible?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin replied, "If you acted alone, it would be equivalent to actively exposing your trump cards, which naturally wouldn't work. But your Mother can use methods to conceal it for you. The Strange Path carves out alternative routes; Mother has already calculated that this method can successfully conceal your actions for three days. Within this time, as long as you can approach the main body of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, you can achieve great success. But once the three days pass, know when to stop and quit while you're ahead. Do not be greedy!"

Ning Zhuo blinked his large eyes and only spoke after a long pause, "Alright, Mother, your child will listen to you!"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin laughed heartily, reaching out to stroke Ning Zhuo's large head as she praised, "Good child."

Restraining her smile, she continued her guidance, "Little Zhuo, if you obtain the Nanming Fire Furnace, you must be even more careful with your words and actions. Maintain a low profile, do not act arrogantly, treat others with kindness, and form many good karmic bonds."

Ning Zhuo replied, "Mother, I have always done that."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin nodded, "Mother knows that you've been a good child since you were little, acting upright and sitting straight."

"But what you don't know is that the carrying capacity of a person's destiny compass is limited, and their Qi Luck is fixed. Therefore, there is an upper limit to the treasures and assets they can acquire over their lifetime."

"As the saying goes, too much is as bad as too little, and too much wealth burdens the body. If you blindly force things, it will only bring disaster."

"You now carry many heavy treasures: the Buddha Heart Demon Seal, the Yin-Yang One Qi Kettle, the Great Primordial Dispersal Scale, the Luoshu Page, and even a Monarch's Seal. Sigh, you can't even use this seal; find time in the future to properly dispose of it. It is already a huge burden on you."

"You cultivate the Three Sects' Supreme Methods, practicing all Three Dantian simultaneously - something countless people in this world desire. Furthermore, you have the Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra and the Water Burial Harmonious Merit-Accumulating Sutra, and you can look for a chance to restore Gao Sheng's Posthumous Writings in the future. So many treasured scriptures are truly a heavy load."

"It's as if both of your hands are currently full of items; how will you grasp other things in the future?"

"It's better to give up some things that aren't suited for you, so you have the spare capacity and the opportunity to obtain things that are."

Ning Zhuo also expressed his distress, "Mother, I obtained these due to chance encounters. To tell you the truth, I really do want to return that Monarch's Seal to the Forgetful River Prefecture Lord. I certainly don't want the Forgetful River Prefecture to be wiped out by the Ghostfire Prefecture, who would then combine the military forces of both kingdoms to invade and conquer my White Paper Immortal City."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin sighed, "Little Zhuo, you understand the situation clearly and do not let past grudges blind your sight. With such a broad perspective, Mother can be a little more at ease. Oh, and in the future, pay a little more attention to the White Paper City Lord."

"If you obtain the Nanming Furnace, you must be even more careful and cautious!"

"Speaking from the Dao of Divination: Burdened by heavy treasures, you were originally destined to have no connection with the Nanming Fire Furnace. From a normal perspective, how could a mere Foundation Establishment minor cultivator like you possess a spiritual treasure, especially a furnace?"

"You have understood the principle of 'an innocent man getting into trouble because of his wealth' since you were young."

Ning Zhuo caught on immediately, drawing inferences from one instance: "Then Mother, if my cultivation improves and reaches the late Foundation Establishment stage, reaches the Golden Core, reaches the Nascent Soul, does that mean my destiny compass can expand, my Qi Luck can swell, and I can hold more heavy treasures and precious scriptures?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin replied, "Naturally."

"In addition, running a faction and gathering outstanding talents to utilize their destiny compasses and Qi Luck to bear more treasures and resources is also possible."

"The Myriad Manifestations Sect is exactly a pioneer in managing this Dao."

"Sigh!" Ning Zhuo punched his palm, letting out a sigh of deep helplessness. "Mother, I also want to raise my cultivation, but the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art is too complicated and truly drags down my cultivation progress. Your child is wholeheartedly dedicated to righteousness; cultivating demonic arts is truly my weak point."

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin tapped his head: "How old are you? How long have you been re-cultivating? To have such progress is already astonishing! Hehe, as the Flying Cloud Assembly continues, and you exchange for higher-tier tokens, when facing the sect's treasure bell, you will have to reveal some truths about your cultivation, such as your age and cultivation art. It will absolutely make everyone's jaws drop."

"My son is truly excellent!"

"Mother," Ning Zhuo let out a silly laugh.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin advised: "It is correct that you confirmed the White Paper Immortal City to be your treasured cultivation land. But since you chose to cultivate the Three Sects' Supreme Methods, the true affinity for your Dao Path still lies with the Supremes of the Three Sects."

"The Supremes of the Three Sects must have made further arrangements. If your cultivation progress is too fast, you might instead miss out on huge fortunate encounters."

Ning Zhuo instantly became serious, "Mother, are you referring to the Lava Immortal Palace?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin nodded: "Correct. The Lava Immortal Palace holds at least the Golden Core Stage portion of the Three Sects' Supreme Methods. Before you reach the Golden Core, or at the latest right after forming your Golden Core, you must re-enter the Immortal Palace to read the Golden Core Stage cultivation methods. Do not deduce the cultivation methods on your own and cultivate recklessly."

Ning Zhuo nodded heavily, "Mother, I'll remember this!"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin continued, "Let's bring the topic back and talk about your Qi Luck again."

"Originally, I wouldn't have helped you plot for the Nanming Furnace. If you came to possess the Nanming Furnace on top of everything else, it would be too much of a good thing, highly likely to trigger various human disasters."

"But after calculating your Qi Luck clearly, right now you have a Strange Path destiny star shining, which has dispelled your Demonic Path human disaster. Correspondingly, this was shown through me waking up just in time to help you out of trouble."

"Because of this, this Strange Path destiny star has been exhausted. Once I fall asleep, not a single speck of starlight will remain."

"Qi Luck is like the waves; it ebbs and flows and has its own ups and downs. By then, your strong luck will wane, and your rising luck will fall, inevitably plunging you into a period of low Qi Luck."

"Under such circumstances, obtaining the Nanming Fire Furnace again would only bring you massive trouble and attract numerous disasters."

"Even if you manage to overcome them, you would definitely expose your trump cards and attract even more trouble. And if the Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra is exposed, the trouble it brings will be far greater than the Nanming Fire Furnace!"

"Furthermore, if your identity as the Vice City Lord of the White Paper Immortal City is exposed, how do you think the higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect will treat you? After all, you aren't yet considered a member of the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Even if you were, you would still be in considerable danger."

Hearing these words, Ning Zhuo couldn't help but break out in a cold sweat.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin changed her tone: "However, I calculated an external calamitous luck falling onto you. This calamitous luck is closely related to the Myriad Manifestations Sect, deeply entangled, making it hard to distinguish friend from foe."

"Because of this, if you acquire the Nanming Fire Furnace, it can be considered catering to this calamitous luck, rather than overdrawing your fortune to force the issue."

Ning Zhuo asked, "Ah? What kind of external calamitous luck is it?"

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin shook her head: "How could I know everything? Little Zhuo, you shouldn't think that Mother is omniscient. If I were, how could your Mother have ended up in this situation?"

"Mother... I will definitely find a way to save you!" Ning Zhuo said seriously.

"Good child. As long as Mother overcomes the Dao Injury and accompanies you with my current identity, I am satisfied." Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin's voice trembled slightly, deeply moved.

"Mother's physical body and soul have already perished. If you forcefully insist on reviving me, it will only make Mother worry."

Ning Zhuo replied, "Mother, I naturally understand the principle of 'biding my time to strike'."

"Mhm." Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin continued to caution him, "Once you obtain the furnace, it will be considered formally triggering this calamitous luck. With my methods, there is no way to control it. Therefore, once the calamitous luck begins, it will only end when it is completely exhausted."

"Within calamitous luck, fortune and disaster change correspondingly and are deeply intertwined. Obtaining the Nanming Fire Furnace is a fortune for you, but for a certain person or a certain faction within the Myriad Manifestations Sect, it will be a disaster and calamity."

"Therefore, you will offend many people and various factions. The challenges you'll have to face will arrive one after another, and it may even be fraught with difficulties."

Ning Zhuo frowned: "Mother, then I would rather be more cautious and careful. Wouldn't it be safer to give up the Nanming Fire Furnace and wait until I successfully join the Myriad Manifestations Sect, or even return to the White Paper Immortal City, before triggering this calamitous luck?"
