Chapter 865: Spirit Awareness Stage

In the center of Yilin, beneath the old locust tree.

Dong Chen couldn't help but feel his heart sink.

He had been observing Layman Yilin the entire time.

From the moment Shao Qiannong closed his eyes to perform his divination, his gaze had not left that clean and ascetic face.

When Shao Qiannong opened his eyes, the sigh that escaped his lips was like a basin of ice water poured over Dong Chen's heart.

A sense of foreboding immediately welled up within Dong Chen. He hurriedly asked, "Senior, how was the divination?"

Shao Qiannong fell silent for a moment before speaking. His voice was flat, devoid of any discernible joy or anger: "Missed by a hairsbreadth. Falling short at the final hurdle."

Dong Chen's heart sank even further. His voice was hoarse, and a look of urgency appeared in his eyes. "Even with Senior taking action, it couldn't be done?"

"I entreat Senior to use your Divine Art once more."

Layman Yilin slowly shook his head. He looked up at the old locust tree above him, watching its leaves sway gently in the night breeze.

"There is an old saying: even the cleverest housewife cannot cook a meal without rice. The path of divination is the same. Whenever we divine anything, we need clues."

"The more numerous and important the clues are, the lower the difficulty of the divination, and the greater the gains."

"For this divination, the clue was rather special. It was that Calamity Luck."

Speaking of this, Layman Yilin slightly shifted the topic: "Do you know what 'luck' is?"

Dong Chen cupped his fists. "I humbly ask Senior to explain."

Layman Yilin let out a sigh. "Luck is fundamentally about change. Experiencing good luck means producing good changes; experiencing bad luck means producing bad changes; experiencing peach blossom luck means interpersonal changes involving interactions with the opposite sex."

"Luck as an entity is dynamic and never dwells in one place, flowing through the six voids. When I was calculating just now, I engaged in a battle of attrition against the other party's Qi Luck. Although I borrowed the power of the sect's Qi Luck to form a crushing advantage, the other party also has a background and external assistance."

"My divination just now triggered a clash of Qi Luck. Amidst the chaos, that Calamity Luck completely disappeared."

"Clearly, this Child of Destiny must have received guidance from an expert. They adopted the wisest method and carried out an emergency response."

"If I want to grasp this Calamity Luck again, it is already impossible."

Dong Chen's complexion worsened somewhat, and he let out a long sigh. "What should we do then?"

Unexpectedly, Layman Yilin had more to say: "Though we lost a scale, we gained a claw."

Dong Chen was startled.

Shao Qiannong continued, "During the battle of attrition just now, I caught a lingering resonance. It was a residual trace left by the clash of Qi Luck. From it, I deduced that the ones assisting the Child of Destiny were two treasures of Qi Luck. One of them can burn Qi Luck and was likely completely destroyed. Let's put that aside for now."

"But the other supreme Qi Luck treasure is of no low grade and can be used to suppress luck. Even if it was damaged or broken, it definitely wouldn't be discarded!"

He raised his hand and pointed to the southeast. "Moreover, I have calculated the location of this treasure. Centered on this place, it lies to the southeast."

Dong Chen frowned, immediately unfurling the territorial map of the Flying Cloud Kingdom in his mind.

In his mind, he took the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate as the starting point and extended a line to the southeast.

At the same time, he began to rapidly analyze it mentally.

"A supreme treasure capable of suppressing luck is extremely rare. Generally, only Super Sects can possess them."

"Of course, we can't rule out the possibility of an individual possessing it, but that scenario is even rarer."

"If we are to conduct a standard investigation starting from the southeast, we should prioritize investigating the Super Sects along this line."

The southeastern line he sketched in his mind extended all the way to the border of the Flying Cloud Kingdom.

Meanwhile, he also listed all the Super Sects along this line.

There was the Profound Heaven Sword Sect. Established on the Sword Dao, it had a profound heritage passed down for three thousand years. Their luck-suppressing treasure was the "Profound Heaven Sword Token." With the founding patriarch's personal sword as the core and the sword intent of past sect masters as the trigger, it could suppress the Qi Luck of an entire sect.

There was Biluo Pill Valley. An alchemy sect, they had historically produced three Soul Formation alchemists. Their luck-suppressing treasure was the "Biluo Pill Cauldron." Made of ten-thousand-year cold jade and nourished by pill fire for a millennium, it could gather Qi Luck and nurture spiritual pills.

There was the Automaton Ban Clan. Renowned for their Mechanical Arts, they possessed an automaton luck-suppressing supreme treasure called the "Clan Lineage Pivot Chain." It could manifest a target's Qi Luck, constantly monitor it, and suppress the luck of an entire clan.

Then there was the Myriad Treasure Pavilion. A sect famous for Artifact Refining, it had produced generations of master artifact refiners, and the magic artifacts and magic treasures they produced were bestsellers across various kingdoms. Their luck-suppressing treasure was the "Myriad Treasure Furnace." Using the fire of the earth's core as fuel and a hundred types of spiritual materials as the catalyst, it could gather the luck of artifacts. The most remarkable aspect was its ability to cultivate artifact spirits!

Dong Chen voiced his judgment.

Shao Qiannong nodded slightly and added, "Besides this, there is another characteristic."

"The other party's Qi Luck is cunning and ever-changing, deeply fitting the characteristics of the Demonic Path."

"This might mean that the young cultivator is a Demonic Cultivator, or it might mean that the Super Sect is a Demonic Sect."

A sharp glint instantly flashed through Dong Chen's eyes.

Among the four Super Sects, only Biluo Pill Valley belonged to the Demonic Path. The Profound Heaven Sword Sect and the Automaton Ban Clan were Righteous Path factions. As for the Myriad Treasure Pavilion, it stood between the righteous and demonic paths as a neutral faction, and thus also carried quite a bit of suspicion.

Of course, Dong Chen was also aware: what Shao Qiannong mentioned was just a characteristic, not a clue. This meant that the final trait pointing to the Demonic Path wasn't necessarily true; it could just be a disguise put up by the other party.

Dong Chen made his decision: "Next, we will investigate these four factions first. Priority will be given to Biluo Pill Valley, followed by the Myriad Treasure Pavilion."

Shao Qiannong leaned back against the old locust tree. "If you make any discoveries, you may present the clues to me."

Dong Chen was instantly overjoyed.

Layman Yilin had actually offered of his own accord, expressing a willingness to follow up on this matter and continue his deductions.

Dong Chen hurriedly stood up, cupping his fists and bowing in gratitude.

"Many thanks, Senior." His voice was sincere. "After I return, I will definitely investigate this with all my might. Once I gather enough clues, I will immediately deliver them to Yilin for Senior to perform your miraculous divination."

Layman Yilin nodded slightly. "Go back for now."

As soon as he finished speaking, Dong Chen felt his vision blur for a moment.

When he came to his senses, he found himself already standing outside Yilin. In front of him was the bluestone stele engraved with the two words "Yilin." The moss on the stele remained the same, the writing remained faded, as if he had never entered at all.

Dong Chen gave a deep bow toward the center of Yilin. Then, he slowly backed away. Step by step, after retreating more than ten paces, he finally turned around and strode away.

The divination wind had long since dissipated. The fog slowly converged, completely enveloping the entire Yilin once again.

Shao Qiannong leaned against the trunk of the old locust tree. His eyes were half-closed, but a gleam of eagerness leaked through the slits.

Back then, the reason he settled down in the Myriad Manifestations Sect, besides personal connections, was to calculate the Qi Luck within the Myriad Manifestations, to observe and cross-reference it. He used this to refine his own skill in Qi Luck divination.

"Right now, the Calamity Luck has made its move. It seems to be fulfilling that terrifying prophecy."

"How truly interesting."

Bluestone Cave Mansion.

"So, what is all this about?" Ning Zhuo had finished the cremation and was carefully observing the cluster of spiritual energy before him.

This spiritual energy was perfectly round and whole, full and distinct, completely different from any of the spiritual energy Ning Zhuo possessed.

Divine Ability: Life Hanging by a Thread!

Ning Zhuo directly used the Divine Art, linking up with this cluster of spiritual energy.

The next moment, he felt the emotions radiating from this spiritual energy.

The spiritual energy felt both love and hate toward him, a mix of fear and intimacy. It was Ning Zhuo's first time experiencing such complex emotions from a piece of spiritual energy.

Just as Ning Zhuo was about to interrogate it, the spiritual energy asked first: "Who... who... who are you? And who... who... who am I?"

Ning Zhuo was taken aback. He thought for a moment before answering cautiously, "I created you. You were born because of me."

Upon hearing this, the sphere of spiritual energy floated over to Ning Zhuo on its own initiative. It wanted to touch him but didn't dare, falling into an adorably hesitant state.

I'm afraid the level of this spiritual energy is not low! Ning Zhuo suppressed his astonishment and continued to communicate with it.

Although the spiritual energy had the ability to communicate, it was actually a blank slate, quite similar to Qing Chi when she had lost her memory.

More accurately, it was even more of a blank slate than Qing Chi. Qing Chi at least possessed common knowledge about society and nature, but this cluster of spiritual energy understood absolutely nothing, just like a newborn baby.

After communicating for a while, Ning Zhuo discovered that this spiritual energy knew absolutely nothing; it was truly a blank slate.

Ning Zhuo had his Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Ability constantly linked to it, and he even used a few techniques for lie detection to confirm this fact.

He also confirmed his earlier guess: This cluster of spiritual energy has indeed reached the Wisdom Spirit Stage, and it's even at the Spirit Awareness stage!

The levels of spiritual energy were broadly divided into five major grades.

They were: Non-Spirit, Void Spirit, Living Spirit, Wisdom Spirit, and Divine Spirit.

This cluster of spiritual energy had just condensed into form, yet it had already reached the Wisdom Spirit Stage, specifically the Spirit Awareness stage within it.

The Wisdom Spirit Stage was divided into five minor stages. They were: Spirit Connectivity, Spirit Resonance, Spirit Awareness, Inspiration, and spiritual intuition.

Having reached the Spirit Awareness stage, this cluster of spiritual energy would possess a powerful cognitive ability regarding itself and the world. If it could cross the subsequent two minor stages and enter the Divine Spirit Stage, it would be extraordinary!

That would be a qualitative change, allowing the spiritual energy itself to possess various miraculous properties.

Ning Zhuo mentally tallied up his assets.

Yuan Dasheng has reached the Spirit Connectivity stage, the first stage of the Wisdom Spirit Stage. Meng Yehu is the same.

Snow-Colored Maiden Hui and Snow Pivot Guard Xie are a bit weaker; both are at the Spirit Nurturing stage of the Living Spirit Stage. In this minor stage, when spiritual energy reaches one hundred percent, it can self-repair and regenerate endlessly. After that, they must pass through the Spirit Dynamic and Spirit Agility stages before they can ascend to the Wisdom Spirit Stage.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin was special.

Her spiritual energy used to be very high, but she had been constantly dragged down by the Dao Injury from the Strange Path. Up until now, Ning Zhuo was still waiting for a reaction from the Great Serpent Scythe.

So, calculating it all, this cluster of spiritual energy before me is probably the one with the highest growth level.

Wait, did I forget something?

Just now, a thought flashed through his divine sea, but when he tried to grasp it, it had already slipped away.

His gaze refocused on the spiritual energy in front of him.

Although it had been cremated by him and personally created by his own hands, this cluster of spiritual energy appeared to Ning Zhuo as if it were shrouded in a thick, mysterious fog.

That Nascent Soul's arrival was too bizarre!

Furthermore, the Nascent Soul itself was very strange.

Ning Zhuo couldn't help but look down at his Mechanism Ring.

The Nascent Soul brought a fatal killing intent. If it weren't for the Mechanism Ring, I'm afraid I would have lost my life.

Now that it has been cremated, I can be considered to have eliminated the cause and snuffed out the source of the killing intent.

But I have a premonition that this calamity hasn't been completely eradicated, and it will still come knocking on my door in the future.

This made Ning Zhuo frown slightly as he continued his estimations.

That Nascent Soul was severely injured beforehand, on the verge of collapse. This indicates that whatever harmed it was at least an existence of the same level.

So, if there are any future developments, the high probability is that I'll be facing a Nascent Soul-level expert? Or perhaps even someone at the peak of the Nascent Soul Stage?

As for the Soul Formation-level...

Ning Zhuo shook his head, feeling that this possibility was unlikely.

As for levels above Soul Formation, the likelihood was even more remote.

In the Cultivation World, factions and organizations were basically led by Nascent Soul-level cultivators. The most impressive ones were led by Soul Formation-level cultivators.

Even in a massive Sect like the Myriad Manifestations Sect, the Sect Master, Peak Masters, and Hall Masters were all at the Nascent Soul-level, and there were even Golden Core-level ones.

There was also the Forgetful River Prefecture. He was at the Soul Formation-level, and the civil and military officials under him were mostly Nascent Soul and Golden Core cultivators.

They undoubtedly possessed more Soul Formation-level individuals, but these people rarely held secular power.

Ning Zhuo didn't understand the exact reason why this was the case. From his extensive reading, he found that this was a widespread phenomenon, almost like an unwritten rule.

It was actually quite rare for a Soul Formation-level expert to be in charge.

In reality, these existences were often like divine dragons whose heads could be seen but not their tails - mysterious and elusive.

Void Refinement cultivators, who were above the Soul Formation stage, were even more ethereal, essentially existing only in legends.

Those with even higher cultivation were exceptionally rare!

Ning Zhuo knew that there must be a major reason for this state of affairs. But his current level was too low, and he had no access to secrets of that caliber.

He vastly underestimated the terrifying karma behind the Demonic-Confucian dual-aspect Nascent Soul.

Right now, Layman Yilin of the Myriad Manifestations Sect had already locked his sights on him. Layman Yilin alone was a Soul Formation-level existence.

If they truly found him, the entire Myriad Manifestations Sect, the Huazhang Kingdom, and even the Demonic Path would come looking for him to settle scores and resolve the karma.

I cremated a Nascent Soul. Even if it was in an extremely poor state, it was still a Nascent Soul.

It makes perfect sense to obtain a mysterious cluster of spiritual energy at the Wisdom Spirit Stage's Spirit Awareness stage.

But does it have any Innate Talent?

How should I go about designing a mechanical body for it?

Ning Zhuo didn't fully understand this cluster of spiritual energy, and for a moment, he couldn't come up with a specific direction.

Sun Lingtong and Qing Chi returned.

Qing Chi walked in front with brisk steps. She held a palm-sized jade box in her hands. The box was bluish-green, with spiritual light faintly circulating around it.

Her cheeks were still flushed with excitement, the sweat on her forehead had not yet dried, and the corners of her clothes were dusted with a few scraps of talisman paper left over from the trial grounds. At this moment, all she wanted to do was report her achievements to Ning Zhuo.

Sun Lingtong followed behind her. Wearing an apricot-yellow short jacket, with chubby cheeks and large, round eyes that were starkly black and white, he had his hands clasped behind his back. His short little legs moved rapidly, giving him an air of seasoned maturity beyond his years.

"Young Master, look at the reward from the minor trial," Qing Chi offered the treasure to Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo wasn't very knowledgeable about the reward, but when he had helped Qing Chi earlier, Sun Lingtong had briefly mentioned that the reward was quite good.

If it could catch his boss's eye, it naturally wouldn't be bad.

In an instant, Ning Zhuo felt a surge of interest. He took the jade box and opened it to take a look.

Ning Zhuo had originally thought that the reward in this jade box would be related to talismans. He hadn't expected it to be three seeds.

Of these three seeds, one was grayish-brown, one was brownish-brown, and the last was deep brown. The patterns on their surfaces resembled dust, silk threads, or networks. Beneath the seed shells, a trace of spiritual light could be faintly seen circulating.

Ning Zhuo: "These are?"

Qing Chi hurriedly explained, "Young Master, these are three Talisman Network Seeds."
