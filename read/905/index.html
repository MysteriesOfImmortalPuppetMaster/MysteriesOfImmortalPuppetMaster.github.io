Chapter 905: Combat Power

Situ Xing revealed a look of contemplation and hesitation.

Ning Zhuo decisively raised the stakes: "I will also tell Brother Daoist a secret - currently, Tao Liweng has already joined."

Situ Xing revealed a hint of astonishment: "Oh? Tao Liweng has always cultivated bitterly within his cave, rarely interacting with the outside world. He actually took the initiative to join?"

"Could it be that the Nanming Fire Furnace has a miraculous effect on his cultivation?"

Ning Zhuo nodded in praise: "Brother Situ is quick-witted, guessing it right on the first try. What are your thoughts?"

Situ Xing shook his head and sighed: "I completely understand Brother Shen and Brother Lin's dilemma now. If we are to get involved in the struggle for Flowing Cloud Peak, our competitors will not just be the newcomers of this current batch."

Ultimately, Situ Xing did not agree on the spot, leaving with a mind full of heavy thoughts.

Sun Lingtong arrived at the study and asked for the result.

Naturally, Ning Zhuo didn't hide anything.

Sun Lingtong snorted coldly: "Descendants of aristocratic families have always been shrewd. Fools like Tao Liweng are rarely seen."

Ning Zhuo: "Tao Liweng cultivates bitterly year-round in a simple environment, so his concerns are all focused on himself. But for the faces of aristocratic families like Situ Xing, they have been cultivated since childhood. Which one of them hasn't fought their way out of fierce competition to finally stand out?"

"Under such complex struggles and mind games, none of the figures who emerge to prominence only think simply."

"He is actually already tempted, but there are too many things to consider."

"If Situ Xing doesn't come to join, I might invite Zhu Fenxiang."

Sun Lingtong gave an 'mm', nodding: "Zhu Fenxiang cooperated quite well with us. The Zhu Clan and the Situ Clan are also on the same level."

The cooperation Sun Lingtong spoke of referred to the minor trial of the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Previously, Ning Zhuo had taken the initiative to find Cao Gui, and through the Commerce Hall, he contacted Danxia Peak. Then, through the Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord Statue, he contacted Zhu Fenxiang.

After Zhu Fenxiang discussed it with her mother, Zhu Guizhi, they decided to believe Ning Zhuo and take a gamble.

This led to the phenomenon where Ning Zhuo successfully "pacified" the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, Zhu Fenxiang, through the Spirit Communication Mirror, and subsequently attempted to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace, gaining the "approval" of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

Many Cultivators like Chun Yangzi, Cang Yazi, and others only changed their established strategies and frantically placed bets to repair the furnace after seeing this scene.

The Zhu Clan and the Situ Clan were generational enemies.

Therefore, if Ning Zhuo invited Situ Xing, he would have to give up on Zhu Fenxiang, and vice versa.

"It would be best if Situ Xing joins; if not, we will invite Zhu Fenxiang to bolster our prestige."

Ning Zhuo estimated: Based on their past few cooperations, there was a high probability that Zhu Fenxiang would agree.

"Of course, the best situation is still for Situ Xing to join. My secret alliance with Zhu Fenxiang will continue on."

This was the most advantageous outcome for Ning Zhuo.

To outsiders now, he and Zhu Fenxiang appeared to be leaning towards hostility because Ning Zhuo was closer to Situ Xing.

For example, this time, when confirming and negotiating the debt, Zhu Fenxiang didn't appear in person, only communicating via flying letters. This further validated the public's perception.

But in reality, Ning Zhuo and Zhu Fenxiang had long since secretly reached an agreement by relaying messages through the statue.

Afternoon.

Mu Yuehua was led into the Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor.

She sat in the seat Situ Xing had previously occupied, facing Ning Zhuo across the desk.

She drank the mortal tea, yet she didn't reveal the slightest unnatural expression. Her face was beautiful and clear, her skin like creamy jade, and her elegantly detached, aloof temperament was undiminished.

It seemed as though even the mortal tea had been tainted with a touch of immortal aura.

Since Mu Yuehua was highly valued by Wang Yu, naturally, she was also highly valued by Ning Zhuo.

In fact, this female cultivator's broad future prospects were also widely recognized.

Ning Zhuo was even secretly envious because, during the furnace's minor trial, Mu Yuehua had publicly displayed a Grand Yin True Fire Fire Seed.

A Fire Seed was hard to come by.

Let alone a Grand Yin True Fire Fire Seed.

Ning Zhuo's debt to Mu Yuehua wasn't much, and he didn't hold any expectations of her joining his side.

However, Mu Yuehua took the initiative to express: "Daoist Ning Zhuo, I have a need to utilize the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"I cultivate Grand Yin spiritual energy. Coupled with my Innate Talent, it causes my Yin Qi to flourish while my Yang Qi declines. I have long been on the verge of Yin-Yang imbalance. The slightest carelessness will result in Qi Deviation."

"If I stay in the Yuehua Sect, I will only cultivate according to the Sect's mandate. Once I form my core, there will be no room for regrets."

"I want to use the Nanming Fire Furnace, where the stars and moon intersect, to gradually improve my physique and spiritual energy, achieving a realm of Yin-Yang harmony. Ultimately, when I advance to Golden Core, I can embrace Yang within Yin, rather than reach the extreme of Grand Yin."

"This is a broader Dao Path."

"I wonder if Daoist Ning Zhuo can help me?"

Ning Zhuo was slightly taken aback, thinking to himself: "This is actually quite easy to handle. Don't I have the Yin-Yang One Qi Kettle in my hands? It's perfectly suited for Mu Yuehua's situation."

Mu Yuehua then suggested: "I know this move will occupy Daoist's pill refining time. The debt between us isn't much and will quickly be paid off. Perhaps I could help refine pills in exchange for opportunities to cultivate at the furnace?"

Ning Zhuo didn't mention a single word about the Yin-Yang One Qi Kettle; instead, he first laid out the plan for Flowing Cloud Peak.

Mu Yuehua revealed a look of deep thought. She didn't agree on the spot, only saying she needed to go back and consider it carefully.

After she left, Sun Lingtong said to Ning Zhuo: "Mu Yuehua is a great seed for pill refining! If she could join us, that would be wonderful. She is only at Foundation Establishment right now. Perhaps we could adjust our offer appropriately and secretly promise her higher treatment?"

"Don't forget, we also have the Yin-Yang One Qi Kettle! Let her test this treasure a few times, and I guarantee she won't be able to put it down. This way, the probability of her agreeing to join our alliance will be even higher."

Ning Zhuo, however, shook his head, holding a different viewpoint: "Her needs are actually different from Tao Liweng's."

"Tao Liweng needs a better pill furnace for his cultivation; his range of choices is actually very small."

"Mu Yuehua wants Yin-Yang harmony, but there are many methods that can help her. Although our Yin-Yang One Qi Kettle is good, it is absolutely not irreplaceable."

"I believe that with Wang Yu's nature of cherishing talent, he has definitely already set his sights on this woman."

"Her pill-refining talent, Grand Yin Fire Seed, and Innate Talent can all attract much higher and superior recruitment conditions. To recruit her, we are actually competing with Wang Yu and others. Boss, do you think we can win that competition?"

Sun Lingtong shook his head and sighed.

"Then forget it."

"Let's look at the others."

"But Little Zhuo, you are going to publicly establish the alliance in three days. Is a mere three days enough time?"

"Right now, we only have Tao Liweng who has explicitly expressed his intent to join."

Ning Zhuo smiled: "Three days is neither long nor short; it's just right. Boss, trust me and just watch."

Sun Lingtong: "As long as you have confidence, that's good!"

Sun Lingtong truly relaxed. Ning Zhuo was becoming more and more reliable in his actions; what else was there for Sun Lingtong to worry about regarding his own brother?

Ning Zhuo brought up another matter, wanting to return Sun Lingtong's Formation Dao realm.

Sun Lingtong waved his hands repeatedly: "You keep using it for now. You need it more."

This was indeed a fact.

For example, in the previous battle between Ning Zhuo and Situ Xing, it was by relying on the Master-level Formation Dao realm that he sequentially peered into the many truths and falsehoods of the Big Dipper Seven Luminaries Sword Formation and the Cosmic Starlight Sword Formation, thus easily establishing an advantage. Of course, behind the scenes, there was also the help of Ning Zhuo utilizing the Dao artifact, the Luoshu Page, to enhance his own calculation power.

Seeing that Sun Lingtong wasn't in a hurry, Ning Zhuo naturally wouldn't stand on ceremony with him: "I indeed have a use for it moving forward."

The relationship between the two brothers far exceeded the ordinary, so there was no need to say more.

After Ning Zhuo finished discussing the debt repayment with all the creditors, he first had Chen San go out and release some slight rumors.

"Let these rumors blow around."

"The highest priority is to pay attention to the Demon Subjugation Hall's reaction."

This was how Ning Zhuo instructed Chen San.

After doing all these things, Ning Zhuo was finally able to properly review his battle with Situ Xing.

Situ Xing's combat power was inferior to Ning Zhuo's, but this time Ning Zhuo had used the battle as practice, so his gains were not small either.

"The Black Iron Han Armor - Golden Swallow Model is as easy to use as ever. However, this combat style currently limits my maximum combat capability."

"When I used the combat power of the Guzheng Magic Artifact, the Langgan Hundred-Joint Whip, the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Shaft, and the Green Bamboo Myriad Transformation Armor, it already exceeded the Black Iron Han Armor - Golden Swallow Model."

There was also room to improve the Black Iron Han Armor - Golden Swallow Model. But the issue wasn't that; it was Ning Zhuo's combat style - he needed to use wearing this mechanical battle armor as a foundation to design and develop suitable methods before he could significantly raise his combat performance.

Conversely, pairing the Langgan Hundred-Joint Whip, the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Shaft, and the Green Bamboo Myriad Transformation Armor with musical instruments, as well as mechanical puppets like the Empty-Minded Blade-Folding Personal Guard, was a combat style recorded in the inheritance of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Tube].

For the former, Ning Zhuo would need to go through steps like rough drafting, improving, correcting, and fine-tuning, taking decades or even centuries to mature.

The latter, however, had already gone through verifications and improvements by several generations of Cultivators before finally taking shape. Based on its current foundation, it had almost reached a point where nothing could be changed.

This combat style might not be the most suitable for Ning Zhuo, but its battlefield performance was clearly far superior to the Black Iron Han Armor - Golden Swallow Model.

Ordinarily, Ning Zhuo didn't spend much time practicing with these mechanical constructs. This time, fighting with Situ Xing gave Ning Zhuo deep insights, and his relevant manipulation skills improved greatly.

"But it's still not enough."

"The two major characteristics of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Tube] inheritance are, first, musical skills, and second, mechanical arts."

"I held back too much on my manipulation of the mechanical arts. Just in terms of control methods, I only used Five Elements Silk Condensation and sound control, not even managing to use Void Spider Silk. Let alone Heart Seal assistance and Life Hanging by a Thread."

"For improvements in mechanical arts, I can just practice the accompanying mechanical arts within the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Tube] inheritance."

For example, the Firecracker Whip Ning Zhuo used in the battle was one of its contents.

"The key is musical skills. This can be rapidly improved by a large margin."

"Speaking of which, after the Cloud Prison escapee incident, more demonic souls flowed into the black market, and they were of excellent quality."

"Among them were Demonic Cultivators skilled in musical techniques."

"Perhaps I should increase the price to secure one?"

Sun Lingtong had secretly been in charge of this matter. But because the souls were of premium quality, they attracted many bidding Cultivators.

Sun Lingtong and the gray-robed Cultivator had been negotiating but hadn't yet reached an agreement.

"After my musical skills are improved, I will still need a period of time to refine various mechanisms from the inheritance on a large scale."

"Especially the avian category, which has a very wide range of applications in actual combat."
