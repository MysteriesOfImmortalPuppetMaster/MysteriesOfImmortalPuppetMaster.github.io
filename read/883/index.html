Mysteries Of Immortal Puppet Master

Chapter 883: Falsification

Tying for first place with Ning Zhuo was none other than Zhongli Mei.

This Nascent Soul-level old cultivator was one of the supreme elders of Danxia Peak and the former hall master of the Alchemy Hall. Looking at the entire Myriad Manifestations Sect, he was also one of the most senior veteran alchemists.

"As expected of Senior Zhongli!"

"Once you make a move, it is truly extraordinary."

"With you here, our minds are set at ease."

As soon as Zhongli Mei stepped down, he received congratulations from Jiang Ping, Shen Hongyao, Tai Ke, Qing Baizi, and others.

Some gazes turned toward Ning Zhuo.

As the previous first-place holder, now that someone had tied with him, many people subconsciously wanted to see Ning Zhuo's reaction.

Ning Zhuo remained calm and breezy, cupping his hands from afar to congratulate Zhongli Mei.

Zhongli Mei gave Ning Zhuo a faint nod, nodded to Jiang Ping and the others as well, and then stood next to Old Man Taoli, striking up a conversation with the latter.

As a Nascent Soul-level cultivator, before making his move, he had merely been sitting alone with his eyes closed in meditation.

Old Man Taoli was known as the Furnace Immortal. Although he only had Golden Core cultivation and usually kept strangers at bay, when a great Nascent Soul cultivator like Zhongli Mei initiated a conversation, he had no choice but to stop his meditation and engage with him.

Zhongli Mei not only had unattainable attainments in alchemy and a cultivation level a whole realm higher than his, but he had also just gained the favor of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit. All these factors combined meant that Old Man Taoli felt no annoyance at being disturbed; rather, he felt quite honored.

Especially since during their conversation, Zhongli Mei proactively shared his speculations about the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit and some ideas on how to gain its favor. This made Old Man Taoli prick up his ears, listen carefully, and commit it all to memory, feeling he had reaped a great harvest.

Seeing this scene, a glint of sharp light flashed in the depths of Ning Zhuo's eyes.

He guessed it: "Danxia Peak is secretly cheating, artificially elevating Zhongli Mei's worth. Among all the cultivators participating in this minor trial, Nascent Soul-level cultivation is the highest present."

"Zhongli Mei's presence can suppress the Red-Robed Guest, Pure Yangzi, Cang Yazi, Nine Fires Dragon Lord, and the others. Just from this point alone, Danxia Peak will definitely escort him all the way to the end."

"Zhongli Mei taking the initiative to chat with the Furnace Immortal must be under the instructions of Danxia Peak. The Furnace Immortal is interested in the Nanming Fire Furnace, but Danxia Peak is even more interested in him as a person."

Ning Zhuo's gaze turned to the pink pillar of light. His expression revealed nothing, but his heart was actually quite heavy.

"If Danxia Peak wasn't manipulating things behind the scenes, the winner of this match would absolutely be me!"

Ning Zhuo had already used Life Hanging by a Thread to control the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, and with the concealment of his Strange Path methods, no one else had noticed.

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit had already become Ning Zhuo's inside agent. Under these circumstances, even if subsequent cultivators performed better than him and were truly fated individuals, Ning Zhuo would forcibly control the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, preventing it from showing any reaction.

From this point alone, Ning Zhuo could eliminate everyone else.

The problem was, Danxia Peak had its own vested interests. Relying on their home-ground advantage, they were willfully manipulating the situation.

The crucial point was that none of the cultivators present had seen through this hidden manipulation!

Because detection methods were strictly forbidden.

If a cultivator suddenly started investigating the formation, what would their intentions be?

If a participating cultivator did so, it would obviously be seen as harboring malicious intentions, trying to cheat, right?

If it were an observing cultivator, a pure spectator doing so, what were they trying to do? Did they lack trust in the Myriad Manifestations Sect? Had they been bribed by certain participating cultivators? Were they trying to tamper with things for their own selfish calculations?

Ning Zhuo didn't even have to guess; he could figure it out with his toes: Since Danxia Peak was manipulating things behind the scenes, they would definitely be strictly guarding against any detection. Once discovered, they would surely mark the person, stop them on the spot, or perhaps even deliberately allow cultivators to detect it to feign innocence.

Even if someone discovered that Danxia Peak was pulling the strings, the vast majority of cultivators would never dare to point it out.

Are you kidding?!

This was the main headquarters of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

Did they have a death wish?

Doing so would damage the credibility and image of the Myriad Manifestations Sect; wasn't that essentially digging at the roots of a major Righteous Path sect?

That would breed a life-and-death blood feud.

In this situation, where no one would or dared to testify against them, it became extremely troublesome for Ning Zhuo.

He could now be certain that this minor trial, currently in its third round, would absolutely not be the final round.

His biggest worry was that the minor trial would be forcibly dragged out beyond three days. That would be disastrous.

Because Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin had clearly warned Ning Zhuo: her Strange Path methods could conceal his actions, but she had also divined that after three days, the concealment could no longer be guaranteed. It was highly likely to leak!

The reason Ning Zhuo hadn't wanted to use the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Ability earlier was out of fear of exposure, which would deal a devastating blow to his reputation, prestige, and so on.

From then on, anyone who interacted with him would harbor the deepest wariness.

This would absolutely be the most severe damage and restriction to Ning Zhuo's development on the Righteous Path.

Ning Zhuo couldn't guarantee that there were no capable individuals within the Myriad Manifestations Sect who could detect Life Hanging by a Thread. The Myriad Manifestations Sect was a Super Sect, and one of the largest at that.

Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin had previously tipped him off, saying that Void Refinement-level powerhouses were hidden within the main sect.

Furthermore, right now was the current session of the Flying Cloud Assembly. Talented and capable individuals from all over had rushed here and were everywhere to be seen.

"I will not take any risks after three days."

"If that situation arises, I will have no choice but to... destroy the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit!"

Ning Zhuo had already made up his mind.

With the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit possessing such high intelligence, it would inevitably leak the information that someone possessing the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Ability had secretly forced control over it.

Ning Zhuo didn't dare leave such a critical clue out in the open, especially since there were undoubtedly masters of divination within the Myriad Manifestations Sect!

Therefore, the three-day limit wasn't just a window of opportunity for Ning Zhuo; it was equally the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's deadline for survival.

Of course, Ning Zhuo didn't want this to happen.

"If it does happen, it would be quite ironic."

"I clearly could have saved the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit and truly repaired the Nanming Fire Furnace, yet I'd become the murderer who destroyed it."

"In order to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace, Danxia Peak made such massive moves, hosting this widely influential Rising Cloud Minor Trial. In the end, they would instead become accomplices facilitating its ultimate destruction."

Everyone else was kept in the dark.

Even the Danxia Peak faction was no exception.

Ning Zhuo was currently the only person who knew the full picture.

But he also felt very powerless.

Because the performance of the pink pillar of light was being internally controlled by Danxia Peak.

The publicly recognized first place was determined based on the movements of the pink pillar of light.

Even if Ning Zhuo controlled the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to acknowledge him as its master on the spot, it would be unilaterally covered up by Danxia Peak.

Danxia Peak's arrangements were extremely rigorous. The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit seemed to be right in front of them, but it was actually protected within a dream realm formation. Even if Ning Zhuo used the Spirit Communication Mirror, he had little confidence in being able to extract it.

On one hand, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was extremely weak; forcibly extracting it would be very dangerous and could likely lead to its destruction. On the other hand, for Ning Zhuo to use this method, it would necessitate the coordination of the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Ability. This would leave behind obvious clues.

If someone were to divine the truth that he possessed this divine ability, he would be doomed.

"I cannot let Danxia Peak drag this minor trial out. But I also can't influence the situation. What should I do?"

Ning Zhuo was certain that Danxia Peak already knew he was the best performer and the one most highly regarded by the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

He was also certain that the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's state would definitely allow it to survive beyond three days.

He couldn't hold out for three days.

"How do I break this deadlock?" Ning Zhuo stood amidst the crowd, pondering bitterly for a solution.

While Ning Zhuo racked his brains, cultivators took the stage one after another.

Hundred Herbs Old Man presented a stalk of Purple Sunset Spirit Mushroom, which was the result of his arduous planting and tied to a century of painstaking effort. After being infused with spiritual energy, the spirit mushroom emitted motes of purple light, circling the open-air pavilion like fireflies.

The pink pillar of light rippled with waves.

Dong Nishang played her zither with delicate hands, the notes tinkling like a clear spring. A piece titled "Vermilion Bird's Guidance" spoke not of sorrow or resentment, but only of tranquility. The flowing light within the pink pillar rose and fell in time with the zither's melody, leaving people astonished.

Jiang Ping placed a hand over his chest, using his sincere alchemist heart as a seal. A brilliant red-gold light lit up in his palm, causing even Shen Hongyao to remain silent for a long time. The pink pillar of light similarly rippled with shimmering waves.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord unleashed all nine of his fires at once. The display was spectacular and imposing, causing the pillar of light to once again dye the open-air pavilion a solid shade of pink.

Old Man Taoli fully displayed his alchemy qi, Cloud Wanderer remained silent while executing hand seals, Cang Yazi called out in beast language, and the Red-Robed Guest burned his vital essence blood to make a vow... All sorts of methods caused the pink pillar of light to produce anomalies, proving the artifact spirit's affinity.

Then it was Zhu Fenxiang's turn.

She walked briskly up to the pink pillar of light, a look of absolute confidence on her face.

She spread her arms as if to embrace the pink pillar of light right in front of her. At the same time, an aura of Divine Power unexpectedly surged from her body!

Mid-air, the divine power astonishingly coalesced into the phantom image of the Nanming Vermilion Bird.

This scene left everyone incredibly astonished. It also successfully propelled Zhu Fenxiang into a tie for first place, putting her on equal footing with Ning Zhuo and Zhongli Mei.

Situ Xing's turn was placed further back.

He drew his flying sword and unleashed his lifelong cultivation, triggering the appearance of stars in broad daylight. Starlight cascaded down like water, flowing behind him to form the miniatures of numerous constellations.

Between the stars, a natural resonance existed. The flowing colors within the pillar of light flickered slightly, like starlight echoing each other across the night sky, brightening and dimming, inhaling and exhaling. This particular anomaly was strikingly different from those of the previous cultivators.

Once all the cultivators had taken the stage, the third round of the minor trial officially concluded.

The presiding cultivator announced that any cultivator who had triggered an anomaly in the pink pillar of light could proceed to the next round.

This generally surprised many in the audience - not many cultivators were eliminated in the third round. Astonishingly, the vast majority were qualified to advance to the next round of competition.

Ning Zhuo sneered inwardly; this was the scene he least wanted to see.

Wang Yu, as the person pulling the strings behind the scenes, was also surprised.

Among the cultivators qualified to advance to the next round, all the Golden Core and Nascent Soul cultivators logically should have been eliminated. Yet, almost all of them had been "escorted" forward by Wang Yu.

The only exception was Cloud Wanderer. This person was a Golden Core cultivator but still naturally evoked the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's affinity.

Among the Foundation Establishment cultivators, there were surprisingly three people who had successfully drawn the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's affinity.

They were Ning Zhuo, Zhu Fenxiang, and Situ Xing!

Among them, the degree to which Zhu Fenxiang invoked the artifact spirit's favor faintly exceeded even that of Ning Zhuo.

This caused his perception and evaluation of Ning Zhuo to plummet significantly.

Wang Yu wrung his wrists and sighed, "Why aren't there any cultivators from my Danxia Peak or the Alchemy Hall among this group? Sigh."

He calmed his mood. "The focus moving forward is still to approach these outsider cultivators, communicate with them secretly, and strive to recruit all of them into Danxia Peak or the Alchemy Hall."

"Investigate that Cloud Wanderer thoroughly."

"Set Ning Zhuo, Zhu Fenxiang, and Situ Xing aside for now. The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit can still hold on for another ten days or so."

"Why are there still no results from Ning Zhuo's background check? What the hell is the Foreign Affairs Hall doing?"

Ning Zhuo returned to the bluestone cave dwelling and discussed countermeasures with Sun Lingtong.

Sun Lingtong said, "Danxia Peak plays a slick game. They've treated Pure Yangzi, the Red-Robed Guest, Old Man Taoli, and the rest as prey. Right there on the stage, they used Zhongli Mei to get close to Old Man Taoli. Tonight, they will definitely meet secretly and collude with these talents in private."

"On the surface, they've done a very thorough job; at least their disguise is quite good."

"They even eliminated Gu Lianjie and Qing Baizi from their own ranks. Gu Lianjie is in Foundation Establishment, so set him aside, but even a Nascent Soul-level cultivator like Qing Baizi was eliminated publicly. If someone were to claim Danxia Peak was rigging it, this serves as their best proof of innocence."

"They also supported other Foundation Establishment cultivators. In the end, the number of people tied for first place exceeded six. They know the truth, and they're relying on this to prove that you aren't special, making it easier to pressure you during later negotiations. Tsk tsk tsk."

Sun Lingtong shook his head repeatedly, sighing, "These Righteous Path tactics - they really play them layer by layer."

He then looked at Ning Zhuo. "Little Zhuo, intentionally giving Zhu Fenxiang a boost and supporting Situ Xing was absolutely the right move."

"It helped share a lot of the pressure off us!"

Ning Zhuo sighed. "I only figured some of it out later on. Since there's no way to expose them, and since I can only watch helplessly as Danxia Peak fakes the results, my only option was to play along to the end and help fake the results as well."

If Ning Zhuo had been the only one, he would have been too unique, and the pressure he'd have to bear would be too great!

Danxia Peak's factional power within the Myriad Manifestations Sect was very formidable.

Putting everything else aside, the simple fact that the Alchemy Hall was primarily controlled by Danxia Peak was enough to see a glimpse of their influence.

According to Ning Zhuo's continuous inquiries, Danxia Peak also wielded immense control over the Talisman Crafting Hall and the Commerce Hall.

Ning Zhuo wasn't a fresh arrival anymore; he now had a vague but general estimation of the strength of the internal factions within the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

The Commerce Hall wouldn't sell this kind of intelligence to outsiders. It could only be gathered by Ning Zhuo continuously collecting information from various sources, piecing it together from here and there, and studying it constantly to achieve these small, phased results.

"The true power and resources of the Myriad Manifestations Sect are primarily concentrated within the Eight Peaks and Sixteen Halls."

"The Sixteen Halls are the ones handling practical affairs."

"The Eight Peaks are the largest conglomerations of factions."

"Among the Eight Peaks, Heavy Formation Peak, Danxia Peak, and Myriad Beasts Peak are visibly dominant. They should be the top three."

"These three main peaks all share a common, obvious trait - they specialize in a certain cultivation craft."

"Master-disciple relationships and the inheritance of crafts provide cultivators with a greater sense of identity, more references, and more of others' experiences, guidance, and assistance."

"In contrast, Purple Thunder Peak and Hundred Herbs Peak primarily focus on producing resources."

"Sword Resonance Peak has strong combat power but gives off a feeling of lacking endurance. Soaring Peak has a casual attitude, especially its peak master, who is far too carefree."

"The special one is Flowing Cloud Peak. This main peak has never been stable and is constantly in turmoil. In particular, every session of the Flying Cloud Assembly triggers massive upheaval and even subversion of the power structure."
