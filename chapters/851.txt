Chapter 851: Blood Mist Demonic Seed

Only Qin De remained in the cell.

He slumped weakly on the ground, panting heavily. The soul-searching had brought his soul to the brink of collapse; had it not been for Wang Yu's Medicinal Pill sustaining his life, his soul would have already scattered and perished by now.

"But I am still alive!"

"That means there's still a chance!"

The excitement of surviving a desperate situation greatly offset the pain in Qin De's soul.

Outside the Cloud Prison, Xiao Juxia's eyes burst with a sharp, brilliant light.

"This is the profound and wondrous use of Destiny!" he murmured, his voice carrying a complex mix of emotions.

This was within his expectations, yet at this moment he still felt a touch of terror, with waves of chill rising from the bottom of his heart.

Xiao Juxia pinched his fingers in calculation, his digits flying and flipping like dancing butterflies, like phantoms.

Streams of formless fluctuations spread out from his fingertips and merged into the void.

This was precisely the Divination Art, a hidden discipline among the myriad cultivation arts. It was the method to pry into Heavenly Secrets that the big-headed youth and others had bitterly pursued to no avail.

A moment later, he stopped, a trace of peculiarity flashing in his eyes.

"So that's how it is..." he muttered.

He had calculated a part of the truth - an abnormality had occurred with the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy, and Dong Chen had urgently summoned Wang Yu and the others back to assist the destiny-suppressing heavy treasure.

"Qin De just went through a tribulation, surviving a scare without real danger. As the saying goes, when things reach their worst, they will turn for the better; his destiny is definitely rising!"

He calculated Qin De again. After the deduction, the result was the same as predicted, yet there was also a difference.

Qin De's destiny had indeed turned prosperous, but after this, there would still be a Human Tribulation.

If handled slightly improperly, it would result in his body dying and his soul dissipating!

"Someone wants to assassinate Qin De, extremely viciously, not even leaving Qin De the chance to become a ghost."

"Interesting."

"This assassination is related to Confucian cultivators."

Xiao Juxia's expression suddenly changed slightly, and he felt a sudden, sickening chill.

His mind was stirred again, and he hastily pinched his fingers to calculate: "The Myriad Manifestations Sect has become a dangerous place."

This situation was the same as before. The deduction result indicated that Xiao Juxia had to leave the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate to avoid the heat. Waiting for the right opportunity to return would be more conducive to achieving his goal.

"Then I'll leave." He did not hesitate, getting up and departing.

As for Qin De, it didn't matter.

The destiny of the Demonic Path had already displayed its profound might. With it protecting his body, what was there to fear? Even if Qin De died, it would definitely unfold in the most advantageous direction.

From beginning to end, Xiao Juxia's goal was only to complete the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art. Qin De was merely a chess piece to achieve this goal.

-----

Cloud Prison.

"Wang Yu stopped. What happened to make him immediately abandon the soul-searching?" Qin De didn't understand what had occurred.

"Perhaps that person made a move and lent me a hand?" He did not receive a response from Xiao Juxia.

Qin De couldn't find an answer because Xiao Juxia had already left the main gate. The distance was too great for him to sense or respond.

"No matter the reason, I must save myself as soon as possible, and every second counts!"

If Wang Yu came back and soul-searched him again, that would be disastrous.

Qin De had already walked through the gates of life and death, fully experiencing Wang Yu's style of doing things.

It was hard for him to harbor any illusions of luck anymore.

"The key to breaking this deadlock is only the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art! It's just..."

"Once I completely demonize, I really won't be able to turn back!"

Qin De gritted his teeth in his heart.

Having experienced this life-and-death ordeal, he still had not changed his resolve!

He still had to prove himself, to show those people that he, Qin De, was not a madman, not a traitor, and certainly not a demon. He was just a... Confucian cultivator who walked a different path.

He would not abandon his foundation as a Confucian cultivator.

That was the persistence of nearly his entire life, the meaning of his existence.

"Fundamentally, Wang Xinyue and I are no different!"

The School of Mind brought by Zhao Hansheng made Qin De want to emulate the virtuous, secretly giving rise to a brand-new fighting spirit.

On the surface, Qin De was still slumped on the ground, but in reality, he was silently circulating the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art, continuing to stealthily convert his cultivation base.

The Golden Core in his dantian gradually gained more withered yellow patterns on its surface, dissipating more wisps of demonic qi.

But at its core, a patch of pure golden light was always preserved; that was the foundation of the Confucian Dao he had painstakingly cultivated his entire life.

"It's not enough!" Qin De's heart went cold.

He converted it for a moment, did a slight calculation, and found that just converting the surface of his Golden Core was simply not enough to let him fight his way out of the Cloud Prison and escape with his life.

"That person probably knew this long ago, so he didn't fear me holding back. As long as I yearn for freedom and want to escape this cage, I must completely demonize myself!"

"Damn it."

Qin De gritted his teeth secretly and cursed.

However, right at this moment, a flash of spiritual light exploded in his divine sea.

"Wait, I think I have a way!"

Qin De mobilized his demonic power and executed a Technique recorded in the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art, leaving behind a phantom on the ground.

His true body passed through the cell door and arrived in the corridor.

He walked along the corridor for several dozen paces before coming to a halt.

The outline of the cell in front of him was similar to his, but there were many more talismans on the walls. The Cultivator imprisoned inside was tied up tightly, completely unable to move.

"Zhou Sumo..." Qin De muttered in his heart.

Having been imprisoned here for several decades, Qin De naturally knew who his fellow inmates were.

Among all the inmates, Zhou Sumo was considered rather special. Not just because of his Righteous Path identity, but also because of the... Blood Mist Demonic Seed within his body!

"That is exactly what I need." Qin De immediately struck, casting another Technique that landed on Zhou Sumo's body.

Zhou Sumo was in a deep slumber. How could he have expected that deep within the Cloud Prison, an inmate would suddenly have freedom of movement? Hit by the Technique from the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art, he immediately fell into a dead sleep, completely unresponsive to the outside world.

Qin De stepped through the cell door and walked to Zhou Sumo's side.

He placed his palm flat against Zhou Sumo's abdomen and once again circulated the Technique from the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art.

His demonic power was continuously consumed. A wisp of demonic qi, extremely faint and light, yet incomparably pure, quickly permeated into Zhou Sumo's Lower Dantian.

The Blood Mist Demonic Seed was lodged right inside!

Sensing the demonic qi cultivated from a supreme-grade cultivation art, it immediately began to tremble, becoming extremely agitated.

Qin De sneered, exerting all his strength to execute his methods.

The demonic seed trembled even more violently.

A demonic seed naturally possessed a survival instinct, and its original host, Zhou Sumo, was already approaching the end of his lifespan. Now, a brand-new host appeared before it, one that even possessed supreme-grade demonic qi!

Then what was there to hesitate about?

In the next instant, the demonic seed fiercely rushed into Qin De's body.

A massive force immediately surged out, incredibly violent, rampaging through Qin De's meridians and nearly tearing his Physical Body apart.

Qin De let out a muffled groan, gritting his teeth to endure as he guided the Blood Mist Demonic Seed into his Lower Dantian.

In his Upper Dantian, the Confucian Golden Core trembled as a result, instinctively wanting to release power and flow down into the Lower Dantian to repel the Blood Mist Demonic Seed.

But Qin De was prepared. He immediately manipulated the Golden Core, suppressing its instinctive reaction.

His Confucian Golden Core had already had its outer layer demonized; it was no longer intact. Therefore, without breaking a sweat, Qin De managed to pacify the Confucian Golden Core.

On the contrary, the Blood Mist Demonic Seed was far more unruly and difficult to tame.

After falling into the Lower Dantian, it immediately spewed out blood mist demonic power, wreaking havoc everywhere. After filling the Lower Dantian, it tried to surge upward, eroding the middle dantian and attacking the Upper Dantian.

Qin De had anticipated this as well. He directly mobilized the demonic power of the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art, setting up defenses at the crucial checkpoints in his meridians, greatly delaying the erosion and flooding of the blood mist demonic power.

As long as he bought some time, Qin De would hold the advantage.

He immediately circulated the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art, beginning to convert the overflowing blood mist demonic power.

Time passed in torment.

After about the time it takes to drink a cup of tea, the violent rampage within Qin De's body gradually subsided.

He had initially suppressed the Blood Mist Demonic Seed, but he still needed to continuously run the Myriad Laws Demonic Fall Art to completely convert the core entity of the demonic seed.

This would require even more time!

"This way, as long as I convert the demonic seed, I will have abundant demonic power to mobilize."

"I will have ample strength and a chance to escape the Cloud Prison!"

"Furthermore, I also guarantee the purity of the Golden Core's core, without completely losing my foundation as a Confucian cultivator."

After making a few more arrangements, Qin De buried himself in bitter cultivation, racing against the clock!

Post station.

Song Taosheng's letter had been delivered here.

After reading it, Zhao Hansheng passed it to Gu Qing.

Gu Qing read the letter. In it, Song Taosheng stated that he needed to handle some matters properly before he could safely rendezvous with them and head to the Huazhang Kingdom together.

Gu Qing immediately had some objections: "This disciple means no disrespect, but I feel that Mr. Song Taosheng is putting on airs. To actually make you, Master, continue waiting for him. Following Master to the Huazhang Kingdom and being able to enter Mr. Wang Xinyue's tutelage, what a massive opportunity is this?"

Zhao Hansheng shook his head slightly: "Qing'er."

"Song Taosheng is the person Duanmu Zhang entrusted to us. Duanmu Zhang sent him here not to be a subordinate to us master and disciple, but to become our comrade-in-arms."

"In the future, when I switch to Wang Xinyue's tutelage and fully align with the School of Mind, Song Taosheng will do the same. We will resist strong oppression together."

"Therefore, waiting a few more days is of no harm."

Gu Qing's face still bore a hint of indignation, but since Zhao Hansheng had spoken, he could only obey.

Almost at the same time, Song Taosheng also received a reply.

This Jade Slip did not have any signature. After reading it, Song Taosheng immediately conjured flames and completely incinerated it.

Just as he had anticipated earlier, Kong Zhaoming had agreed to his request, secretly offering his full support.

This time, Kong Zhaoming not only provided several Confucian cultivator treasures and multiple sets of over-spec combat supplies but also mobilized five death-sworn warriors. They would infiltrate the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate in the coming days, constantly awaiting Song Taosheng's orders.

Song Taosheng's letter to Zhao Hansheng and Gu Qing was merely a false show of compliance, an outward disguise. Before the assassination operation, he had to minimize all flaws as much as possible.

"Qin De must die!"

"As long as he lives, we Confucian cultivators will never be able to stand with our backs straight!"

"As long as he lives, he is a disaster, and might even become an enemy of Confucianism!"

Song Taosheng's eyes were full of determination.

Next, he would go all out to quickly master the various reinforcement supplies and wait for an opportunity to execute the assassination plan.

The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy was shaking violently.

Around the Sacrificial Altar, four figures occupied the four directions.

To the east, Dong Chen sat cross-legged, his hands forming seals as Techniques shot from his fingertips and submerged into the Cloud Canopy.

To the south, Wei Ji sat upright like a mountain, his pure black pupils reflecting the anomaly of the Cloud Canopy. He pressed his hands in the air, streams of spiritual energy surging from his body and connecting with the Cloud Canopy.

To the west, Tuoba Huang squatted on the ground, his entire body radiating fiery qi. He clasped his hands together, his spiritual energy erupting from his body, turning into a long rainbow that rushed toward the hall's ceiling before descending and connecting with the Cloud Canopy.

To the north, Wang Yu sat cross-legged, the crimson clouds on his cloud-patterned robe shifting continuously. Holding a fly whisk, he waved it gently, streams of spiritual energy flicking out from the whisk and merging into the Cloud Canopy.

The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy safeguarded the Myriad Manifestations Sect's Destiny. The four people present were all higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, their personal destiny highly bound to the sect's, making them all excellent candidates to assist the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy.

As time passed, the expressions of the four grew increasingly solemn.

They had already exerted their full strength, but the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy still could not stabilize.

The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy specifically targeted the prophetic poem. It was currently intercepting the calamitous destiny represented by the prophetic poem, preventing it from materializing.

Dong Chen and the other three pushed themselves to their limits, laboring extremely hard.

"Even with the four of us taking action, we still haven't seen a delay, showing that this turbulence is no small matter!"

"Sigh, it coincides with the Flying Cloud Assembly. At this crucial juncture, something went wrong with the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy."

"You are unaware. It is precisely because of the Flying Cloud Assembly that our sect's gates are wide open, connecting with the destinies of countless figures, thus triggering this calamity."

"Someone must be playing tricks in the shadows. Quickly finding out who this person is, is the real priority!"

Wang Yu consumed a Medicinal Pill. The skin all over his body turned golden-purple as his strength skyrocketed, acting as the main force.

Dong Chen took the opportunity to hold back slightly, using his spare energy to perform deductions. A sharp light flashed in his eyes: "This destiny fluctuation involves much tighter connections; it's an excellent opportunity for deduction!"

Xiao Juxia had already strayed far from the mountain gate. Dong Chen did not calculate him, but deduced a different result.

His face darkened further as he warned in advance, "Not good. This calamity involves many layers, and a massive force is about to crash down on us. Everyone, bring out whatever methods you have. We must ensure the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy firmly holds its ground!"

The other three were immediately moved and began to be on full alert.

Xiao Juxia, on the other hand, sent another flying letter; he wanted to summon more high-level members, ready to join in at any time, just in case.

The main doors of the Huazhang Kingdom's Imperial Ancestral Temple had not been opened for two full days.

Inside the main hall, it was brightly lit, never ceasing day or night.

The three-tiered white jade Sacrificial Altar, originally used to enshrine the Golden Seal Jade Scroll, was preserved and served as the core of the entire Altar.

Three layers of yellow silk were laid upon the altar. The first layer was embroidered with the sun, moon, and stars; the second with mountains, rivers, grass, and trees; the third with the names of past sages. The three layers of yellow silk overlapped one another, sewn together with golden thread so finely that the stitches were almost invisible.

In front of the Golden Seal Jade Scroll, a purple sandalwood table was placed. On the table rested a three-legged censer. The body of the censer was carved from green jade, with openwork carvings of auspicious clouds. No fire had been lit in the censer yet, but a faint scent of sandalwood could already be smelled - that was the aura of incense that the censer itself had absorbed over hundreds of years.

Around the Sacrificial Altar stood nine three-foot-tall jade pillars, with a fist-sized luminous pearl embedded at the top of each. The nine pearls were of nine different colors - red, orange, yellow, green, cyan, blue, purple, white, and black, symbolizing nine different types of Literary Qi.

Imperial Academy Scholar Zheng Jing was known for his rigorousness. For the Altar he was in charge of setting up, he repeatedly deliberated over every detail and personally inspected every sacrificial vessel. Over the past two days, he had only slept for two shichen, spending the rest of the time repeatedly checking, adjusting, and perfecting it.

At this moment, he stood in front of the Altar, examining every detail for the final time.

After a long while, he nodded slightly: "Flawless."
