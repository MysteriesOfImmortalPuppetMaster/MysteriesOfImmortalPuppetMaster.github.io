Chapter 950: Liu Jinke Fights Ning Zhuo for the Third Time

Qiu Lei and Old Man Youyun, one possessing the heavy majesty of earth veins and the other the ethereal agility of cloud methods, struck at the same time. Yet, they were firmly suppressed by a single palm of smoke from Tan Zhu.

This was the might of a quasi-Divine Art!

For Nascent Soul cultivators to advance, they had to master a Divine Art; only then could their Nascent Soul undergo Soul Formation.

Furthermore, this Divine Art had to be a lifebound technique. After all, the very cultivation of the Nascent Soul was the result of their primary Cultivation Art.

Even if other techniques could be elevated into a Divine Art, they would not be able to influence the Nascent Soul.

Although Qiu Lei and Old Man Youyun were both Nascent Soul cultivators, their lifebound techniques were still a long way from becoming quasi-Divine Arts, let alone true Divine Arts.

It was not that the two of them working together couldn't defeat Tan Zhu.

If it were truly a life-and-death struggle, the two would surge their spiritual energy without regard for consumption, and they would very likely win through sheer volume.

The problem was that during their current clash, they simply couldn't mobilize more spiritual energy—if they actually did, it would turn into a public duel, violating the sect rules of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and the consequences would be extremely severe.

Therefore, in this seemingly "light and breezy" contest, the spiritual energy used by each of them was very limited, so what they were competing in was "quality."

In this aspect, Tan Zhu easily crushed the two of them.

Qiu Lei and Old Man Youyun knew this well in their hearts and felt extremely helpless. Who could they blame but themselves for not mastering a quasi-Divine Art?

The two exchanged a glance, and Qiu Lei said in a deep voice, "Tan Zhu, are you truly going to stand up for the Nanming Stronghold?"

Tan Zhu said indifferently, "I am a person of the Nanming Stronghold to begin with."

Old Man Youyun's expression turned ugly. "By acting like this, are you trying to forge a mortal enmity with my Baiyun Township?"

Tan Zhu glanced at him. "When Baiyun Township funded Liu Jinke previously, did you ever consider forming an enmity with the Nanming Stronghold?"

Old Man Youyun was momentarily at a loss for words.

Tan Zhu voluntarily retracted his quasi-Divine Art. The purple-black smoke automatically gathered like an invisible cape and sank into his robes. "Flowing Cloud Peak has Flowing Cloud Peak's rules. Since you both claim this place belongs to you, yet neither has Sect credentials, let us settle this with a martial contest."

Qiu Lei remained silent.

Old Man Youyun was also silent.

After a moment, Qiu Lei spoke slowly, "How shall we contest?"

Tan Zhu said, "Kuotu Alliance, Baiyun Township, Nanming Stronghold... Our three parties will each send out combatants. We will compete in the Martial Arena, fighting one-on-one, with no limit on the number of participants. Whichever side is the first to achieve three victories, the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar will belong to them. How about it?"

Old Man Youyun's eyes flickered. "Three victories won't do; it must be at least five victories!"

Tan Zhu knew in his heart that the other party was trying to bully the Nanming Stronghold for being newly established and lacking sufficient foundation, deliberately raising the number of required victories.

However, this was also within his expectations.

Tan Zhu nodded slightly. "Acceptable. And the Kuotu Alliance?"

Qiu Lei was already the representative of the Kuotu Alliance for this matter and had the authority to make the decision. He gave Tan Zhu a deep look and finally nodded, "Alright. Five victories will determine the winner!"

"Let us sign the contracts." Tan Zhu waved his sleeve, sending out three blank contracts.

The three parties quickly signed them.

Tan Zhu said no more, turning around and flying away from the area.

The news quickly took flight, spreading throughout the entire Sect.

Countless people were astonished. The Nanming Stronghold had finally taken its next step, and it was exceptionally decisive and swift, pointing their blades straight at the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar.

From this moment on, the dispute over the Cloud Ascension Altar was no longer just a secret tug-of-war between the Kuotu Alliance and Baiyun Township.

With the Nanming Stronghold officially entering the fray, it had become a three-way battle for victory!

The representatives of the major factions on Flowing Cloud Peak gathered together once again.

Nanming Stronghold had acted far too quickly!

They had originally thought that since Nanming Stronghold was newly established, its people were divided, and the five Nascent Souls each had their own calculations. Even if Ning Zhuo had some political skill, he should have needed time to consolidate first, or at least use the third battle with Liu Jinke to continue building momentum.

Who could have expected that the moment Tan Zhu stepped forward, he would directly drag the Kuotu Alliance and Baiyun Township onto the martial arena?

The Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar was no longer simply a piece of treasured feng-shui land.

It had become a flagpole.

If the Nanming Stronghold could plant their flag upon it, it would mean they had truly bitten off a piece of meat from Flowing Cloud Peak.

What the factions of Flowing Cloud Peak feared the most was never that Nanming Stronghold would occupy just one place. What truly kept them awake at night was the realization that once Nanming Stronghold gained a firm foothold, the five Nascent Souls and numerous creditors would have a place to settle. Today it was the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar; tomorrow it could be some cloud nest, some spirit vein, some ferry, or some cluster of cave manors.

Who dared to say that the next one to suffer a loss wouldn't be themselves?

Lei Wangyue of the Leiyun Society stated bluntly, "The Leiyun Society can provide support. Nanming Stronghold cannot be allowed to establish a foothold on Flowing Cloud Peak!"

Zhao Ni of the Bird and Beast Manor spoke in a deep, thick voice, "The Bird and Beast Manor can also contribute. Cloud beasts migrate frequently near the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar. If the Nanming Stronghold occupies this territory, it will definitely affect our manor's routes for hunting and taming spirit beasts."

He wore a grayish-brown beast pelt, and a double-pupiled golden eagle perched on his shoulder. The golden eagle let out low cries, its sharp eyes sweeping across the crowd as if sizing up prey.

Ye Qingming of the Green Tea Society still wore a moon-white long shirt with narrow sleeves, covered by an aqua-green short-sleeved jacket, the plain silver hairpin in her hair gleaming faintly.

Wisps of elegant tea fragrance emanated from her body, and her tone was as gentle as spring water. "With the situation having reached this point, everyone should understand that this isn't about helping the Kuotu Alliance, nor is it about helping Baiyun Township. It's about helping ourselves."

"If the Nanming Stronghold stands firm, the business calculations will have to be redone." Jin Mantang threw his plump hands open. "Just think of the wealth we've invested into Liu Jinke from start to finish. My Fusheng Society has no desire to do a loss-making business."

Wen Suzhen of Xuanhu Residence, Xu Duanchao of the Severing Water Blade Pavilion hugging his saber, and the gold-hairpinned old woman of the Jinshi Alliance all expressed their stances one after another.

Qiu Lei and Old Man Youyun sat closely next to each other, neither looking particularly pleased.

They both knew that while the crowd claimed to offer support, in reality, they had their eyes fixed on the fat piece of meat that was the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar. They had been agonizing over having no excuse to make a move, and now they were seizing the opportunity, acting proactively and forcefully.

Qiu Lei said solemnly, "I understand everyone's intentions. But we have already signed contracts with Tan Zhu."

White clouds slowly flowed within Old Man Youyun's sleeves. "Exactly! It's not just the contracts; the news of our three-way martial contest has already spread throughout the entire main Sect mountain. If we go back on our word, what face will we have left to stay on Flowing Cloud Peak in the future?"

Faced with such a situation, the two decisively joined forces, expressing a firm stance to prevent other factions from interfering.

From the perspective of the Kuotu Alliance and Baiyun Township, they were merely engaging in a fair competition with Nanming Stronghold. If they allowed other peaks to take advantage and intervene, the situation would become far more chaotic, and their control over the circumstances would be weakened to the extreme!

Sparks of lightning flickered in Lei Wangyue's eyes. "Fellow Daoists, please weigh the importance of this. If you two are defeated, the Nanming Stronghold will successfully establish itself! When that time comes, the difficulty of resisting their expansion will be completely different from the difficulty of stopping them from establishing themselves now!"

The moment his words fell, Jin Mantang, Ye Qingming, and the others chimed in in agreement.

With everyone adding their voices one after another, an immense pressure formed on Qiu Lei and Old Man Youyun, exceedingly overbearing.

Qiu Lei's face was ashen, and he remained silent.

Old Man Youyun, however, said, "If everyone truly wants to stop Nanming Stronghold, you can naturally start from other angles. The root of Nanming Stronghold is still attached to Ning Zhuo, isn't it?"

The stone grotto quieted down for a moment.

Qiu Lei, having been reminded, had a flash of brilliant light in his eyes. "That's right! As long as Liu Jinke wins the third battle, he can spare Ning Zhuo's life in public at the Martial Arena in exchange for him publicly disbanding Nanming Stronghold. Then this stronghold will have no foundation to exist!"

"Therefore, you don't need to solely focus on the five victories at the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar."

Qiu Lei slowly said, "So, regarding the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar, we will contest as usual to tie down their energy. On the other side, we must have Liu Jinke immediately challenge Ning Zhuo!"

Previously, the two had argued until they were red in the face and completely at each other's throats at the cloud ferry, but now they coordinated tacitly, joining forces to jointly resist the pressure from the other factions of Flowing Cloud Peak.

The crowd fell into a moment of silence.

The opinions of Qiu Lei and Old Man Youyun were indeed reasonable; they had no grounds to refute them.

In truth, they had already invested too much into the chess piece known as Liu Jinke, and it was time to reap the results.

Lei Wangyue said in a deep voice, "We cannot wait any longer. Liu Jinke's training is just about done. The more we drag this out, the more Ning Zhuo will be prepared."

"Exactly," Xu Duanchao said. "Our side has found out that Qing Huangzi has been working day and night without rest to forge a mechanical flying bird for Ning Zhuo!"

Wen Suzhen added, "The challenge must happen before the results of the five victories are out. In addition to that, there are also the Three Braves of the Xiang Sect!"

At the mention of those final words, everyone's gazes fell toward the corner.

Yin Baiming, a deacon of the Xiangsi Sect, sat there. His grey hemp robe trailed on the ground, his half-white, half-black hair draped over his shoulders, and the bone tiles at his waist hung low one by one. Despite there being no wind, they would occasionally bump together gently, producing clacking sounds.

Lei Wangyue looked at the crowd. "I propose that we all pool our funds to hire them!"

Xu Duanchao said, "I agree."

Zhao Ni said roughly, "Bird and Beast Manor approves."

Ye Qingming said lightly, "Green Tea Society will contribute a share."

Wen Suzhen said, "Xuanhu Residence follows."

The flesh on Jin Mantang's face quivered, and he finally gritted his teeth. "Fusheng Society will follow as well. We've already burned through so many Spirit Stones; we might as well add this last spark."

Qiu Lei stayed silent for a moment before saying, "Kuotu Alliance will contribute a share."

Old Man Youyun let out a sigh. "Baiyun Township will chip in too."

These two parties had previously held each other back, but now that the situation was clear, funding a move against Ning Zhuo was more beneficial to them.

Only then did Yin Baiming slowly raise his eyes.

His eyes were a pale gray, and his voice was low and hoarse. "Since everyone agrees, the Xiangsi Sect will invite the Three Braves out of seclusion. However, the rules remain the same: payment upfront, damages calculated separately. The Three Braves sell their lives, not favors."

The cultivators exchanged looks and nodded simultaneously.

Having struck this deal, everyone felt a mix of heaviness and relief in their hearts.

Heavy because inviting the Three Braves of the Xiang Sect came at a considerable price.

Relieved because the Three Braves of the Xiang Sect were triplets who shared a womb, all of whom were Golden Cores. With their innate talent of sharing life and death, they had already cultivated a Divine Art. Such a trump-card cultivator combination would definitely inflict a crushing defeat on Ning Zhuo. In turn, it would shake the very foundation of Nanming Stronghold!

Nanming Stronghold, the Kuotu Alliance, and Flowing Cloud Peak's three-party dispute over the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar was bound by the five-victory agreement.

Liu Jinke would fight Ning Zhuo for the third time.

Meanwhile, in the shadows, the Three Braves of the Xiangsi Sect were about to be mobilized as a final guarantee.

The formal contest surrounding the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak to prevent Nanming Stronghold from storming the peak had thus fully drawn its curtains open!

The location for the third battle had been changed to the Cloudstone Martial Arena.

This martial arena was incredibly spacious. Formation walls hung high on all four sides, with formation patterns slithering like silver snakes. Layers of light screens expanded outward, neatly separating the grandstands, cloud corridors, flying boats, and viewing platforms.

The number of people who came today was vastly greater than in the previous two battles!

The distant cloud bridges were packed with rogue cultivators, while the nearby high platforms were occupied by the various peaks and halls of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, as well as the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak.

Lei Wangyue of the Leiyun Society sat on the eastern side, rolling two Purple Thunder Iron Baoding Balls back and forth in his palm. With every rotation of the iron balls, tiny sparks of lightning would burst out between his fingers.

Ye Qingming sat behind a screen. Tea steam curled upward, and a faint line of light green stained the rim of her celadon cup.

Zhao Ni of the Bird and Beast Manor wore his old fur coat. Behind him, several beast-taming cultivators walked spirit dogs and carried golden eagles on their shoulders.

Xu Duanchao of the Severing Water Blade Pavilion leaned against a wall, hugging his saber.

The old woman with the gold hairpin leaned on her walking stick, sitting at the Jinshi Alliance's seats with drooping eyelids.

Wen Suzhen, Jin Mantang, Qiu Lei, Old Man Youyun, and the others had also arrived.

Many people from Nanming Stronghold had come as well.

Chunyangzi's red-gold Daoist robes reflected the sunlight, with the three thousand characters of the Pure Yang Pill Scripture faintly visible on the fabric. He sat upright on a high platform, with the disciples of the Pure Yang Palace standing solemnly behind him.

Red Robed Guest half-leaned in a fire-patterned wooden chair. His crimson robes spread out like a blood-colored sunset, the corners of his eyes carrying their habitual arrogant sneer.

Nine Fires Dragon Lord sat cross-legged and motionless, the fire patterns on his forehead surfacing like dragon scales.

Situ Xing hugged his sword, Shen Xi played with a jade seal, Lin Jinglong lowered his head to adjust his cuffs, and Old Man Taoli shrank back into a slightly recessed position, feeling much more comfortable where there were fewer eyes on him.

Tuyuanzi was also there. He sat very earnestly, both hands resting on his knees, his eyes bright.

The formation bell in the arena rang lightly, causing the discussions and clamor outside the ring to die down considerably.

Under the watchful eyes of the crowd, Liu Jinke officially entered the arena!

His dark-gold robes clung to his body, devoid of any extraneous patterns. They solely possessed a faint, dark-golden luster, appearing as if liquid gold was slowly flowing beneath the fabric.

Beneath his exposed skin, azure-gold silk-like veins faintly surfaced, making him look incredibly mysterious.

It was precisely the Golden Network Garment!

And on his chest, hidden beneath his clothes, lay a golden-black divine crest.

Liu Jinke stood firm, raising his eyes to look at the passageway on the opposite side.

Ning Zhuo had not yet entered the arena.

If the two had not yet clashed, everyone would have thought Ning Zhuo was being overly arrogant. But now, with Ning Zhuo having won both previous battles, arriving late for the third battle drew no gossip. Everyone felt that Ning Zhuo deserved this level of prestige!

Liu Jinke stood quietly in place. The noisy discussions outside the arena gradually drifted away; instead, the sound of his own heartbeat grew louder and louder in his ears.

Tension and fear.

These were the first emotions he felt!

Ning Zhuo was only at the mid Foundation Establishment stage, yet he had tangibly severed Liu Jinke's head twice.

That sensation—the blade sweeping across his neck, his vision flipping, and golden blood pouring backward—had chased him down in the night countless times, causing him to jolt awake.

The arrogance of a Golden Core cultivator had long since dissipated. Liu Jinke had long ago come to regard Ning Zhuo as the greatest enemy of his life!

Do not be nervous. Do not fear. I am different now, different, Liu Jinke repeatedly whispered in his heart.

The Golden Blood Core Bead inside his body rotated slowly. With every rotation, his golden blood would warm by a fraction, and his courage would rise accordingly.

Quasi-Divine Art: Golden Liquid Returning Core!

Outside the arena, the smile faded from Jin Mantang's face, involuntarily replaced by a solemn expression.

Ye Qingming placed her teacup back onto the desk. The ceramic base clinked lightly against the surface, the sound as soft as a jade bead dropping into water. "He's here."

"Ning Zhuo!" Liu Jinke gritted his teeth around the name, his tongue tasting a hint of rust-like sweetness.

Under the gazes of countless cultivators, from the other end of the passageway, the white-clad youth also slowly stepped into the martial arena.

Ning Zhuo was still wearing the Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment. Fine sound patterns adorned the hems of his clothes, exuding a distinct, clear, and tranquil charm, like the wind passing through a bamboo valley.

A wooden box hung upon his back, and a few small, flat magic pools were tied to his waist. The aura of mechanical constructs within his sleeves drifted in and out of perception.

The Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot quietly followed behind him. The azure python-shaped mechanical vehicle coiled and lay low on the ground, its scales a deep, dark green. Its mechanical wheel axles were hidden beneath its belly, looking as if it could pierce through forests and shatter mist to depart at any moment.

The white-clad youth's expression was completely calm.

So calm that the shadows of the past surfaced once again within Liu Jinke's heart.
