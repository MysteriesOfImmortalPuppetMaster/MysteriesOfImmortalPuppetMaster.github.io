Chapter 906: Young People Do Not Know the Immensity of Heaven and Earth

In the later stages of his battle with Situ Xing, Ning Zhuo used a massive number of Mechanical Golden Swallows and Thunder Silence Birds. Even though their charges didn't shake the Zhou Cosmic Star Sword Formation, the actual battle results were excellent - Situ Xing had to expend even more of his divine sense to control his star swords, simultaneously attacking and defending. Eventually, he grew exhausted from coping, was pushed to his absolute limits, and could no longer hold on.

The [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Tube] contained many avian mechanical constructs. If Ning Zhuo used them, they would pair perfectly with the Green Bamboo Hundred Transformations Armor, the Langwang Hundred-Section Whip, and the rest.

"In the short term, if I develop my combat style in this direction, I can definitely make my conventional combat power surge by a large margin."

"For close-quarters combat, the concept behind the Embracing Clumsiness Wheel Slash Formation is good, but as a newly created prototype, it is simply inadequate when fighting an opponent like Situ Xing."

There were two clear directions for improvement in this aspect.

"First, find Young Master Qingwu and purchase more, higher-quality Mystic Weapon Armors. Second, search for similar battle formations to study and master."

"The Humble Blade-Snapping Personal Guards are very useful, but they lack spiritual energy, requiring my constant control."

"The Forest-Piercing Azure Python Shaft is indeed agile, but its foundation is lacking; it cannot reach the Golden Core-level. In actual combat, I still have to use other talisman methods to cover and protect it. This point can also be improved."

...

Ning Zhuo's thoughts grew deeper.

He was trying every possible way to enhance his combat power. The faster and higher the increase, the better.

To improve combat power, he primarily considered two major areas.

One was fundamental power, and the other was application technique.

A cultivator with a fundamental power of one hundred would naturally defeat a cultivator with a power of ten.

If their techniques were strong, they could bring out one hundred percent of their power, or even multiply it, acting much like a lever.

By using a sword formation, Situ Xing could allow his combat power to break through to the Golden Core-level. By taking out and wearing the Black Iron Han Armor - Golden Swallow Model, Ning Zhuo immediately possessed Golden Core-level combat power.

The former utilized techniques of Formations and Sword Dao, while the latter relied on mechanical arts.

Beast taming arts were also a prime example.

If a Foundation Establishment cultivator could command a Golden Core-level demonic beast, they would immediately possess Golden Core-level combat power.

However, techniques were not omnipotent. Any technique that leveraged combat power not only required years of bitter cultivation from the cultivator but also had its own inherent weaknesses.

These weaknesses were often difficult to hide. If they were targeted, the cultivator would typically underperform.

For instance, part of the reason Ning Zhuo defeated Situ Xing was because Ning Zhuo possessed a master-level realm in the Dao of Formations, easily allowing him to see through various truths and illusions.

Suppose a cultivator severed the connection between a beast tamer and their pet; they might even cause the demonic pet to turn on its former master.

"Therefore, fundamental power is the cornerstone; it is the king's path."

"Although Old Man Taoli appears simple-minded, his fundamental power is actually incredibly robust. The Embracing the Core to Become an Immortal Art can increase a cultivator's realm in all aspects, leaving no shortcomings whatsoever."

"In a real fight, Old Man Taoli's combat power cannot be underestimated!"

"Sigh, I still haven't comprehended this Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art to the point of a qualitative change."

Ning Zhuo also wanted his fundamental power to rise, but unfortunately, he was being held back by the demonic art.

On this point, he could only continuously comprehend and practice, mastering it step by step.

This was a slow-grinding cultivation method.

It would inevitably take time and could not be accomplished overnight.

Fortunately, Ning Zhuo never gave up. He dedicated a period of time every single day to comprehending the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art, unwavering regardless of the circumstances.

"Releasing the news about Flowing Cloud Peak will definitely cause a stir."

"When the alliance is established in three days, as a core member, I definitely won't have any peace and will face numerous tests."

"Mother guided me, saying that this is the best way to dissolve tribulation luck, and it is also the fastest path for my progression. Naturally, I cannot give it up."

"The struggle for Flowing Cloud Peak is different from the Cloud-Stirring Minor Trial and the Flying Cloud Assembly."

"When the time comes, life-and-death battles will be unavoidable!"

"But that doesn't matter."

"I also need battle achievements to establish my footing. Only by fighting to establish my prestige can I make everyone take me seriously and respect me!"

With the situation developing to this point, Ning Zhuo was determined to bare his fangs and carve out new room for his own development.

-----

Demon Subjugation Hall.

Zhong Dao buried his head in handling administrative affairs while listening to his subordinate's report.

When speaking of Ning Zhuo, the subordinate's speaking speed noticeably hesitated: "There are rumors leaking out saying that in three days, Ning Zhuo wants to gather the cultivators to form an alliance and participate in the struggle for Flowing Cloud Peak."

"This subordinate sent people to investigate and discovered that the source of the news is a cultivator named Chen San. He has secret communications with Ning Zhuo, so we deduce that he is highly likely to be Ning Zhuo's subordinate."

The Demon Subjugation Hall was responsible for striking down demons and heretics within the borders of the Flying Cloud Kingdom. Investigating the relationship between Ning Zhuo and Chen San was merely a trifling matter.

Zhong Dao's movements in handling his affairs paused.

The reporting subordinate held his breath. Another cultivator grew indignant and clamored, "This brat Ning Zhuo really refuses a toast only to be forced to drink a forfeit! Our Lord Hall Master values him so highly and has helped him time and time again. How much trouble has he settled by borrowing our name? Now that there's a profit to be made, he turns around to fight for some Flowing Cloud Peak. Just what position does our Demon Subjugation Hall hold in his heart?"

Zhong Dao continued handling his affairs and said, "What are you making such a racket for?"

The cultivator who had just been clamoring immediately fell silent. The great hall descended into dead silence.

Zhong Dao slowly spoke, "He's a genius, after all. Having such a temperament is quite normal. Furthermore, Ning Zhuo is the most outstanding among this generation's geniuses. Having obtained that furnace this time, it's not surprising that he's holding his head up high."

"I know many of you are resentful, but do you know why I value him?"

"Look at the matter he handled this time. If you were in his shoes, could you have done it?"

Zhong Dao shook his head.

He let out a sigh. "This stinking brat has an excellent eye, and his methods aren't bad either. If we recruited him, he would definitely become a pillar of my Demon Subjugation Hall."

"However, with his consecutive victories time and again, his momentum has grown stronger, and his ambitions have grown higher, to the point where he's actually starting to look down on our Myriad Manifestations Sect."

"Young people not knowing the immensity of heaven and earth is simply a characteristic of the young."

"Letting him charge ahead and temper his character is also a good thing."

The cultivator who had just been shouting hurriedly changed his tune, "Lord Hall Master is magnanimous and has a sharp eye for talent. For that brat Ning Zhuo to meet a talent-spotter like you is simply a blessing he cultivated over several lifetimes."

"Sigh, yet he doesn't realize how blessed he is."

Zhong Dao interrupted, "In three days, he wants to form an alliance in public. Three days... Does our Hall have anyone available?"

The subordinate who was reporting earlier immediately answered, "We do, Lord Hall Master."

Zhong Dao: "Let's hear it."

"Peach Blossom Daoist has just finished planting his peach trees and is waiting in the peach grove at all times for Your Lordship's dispatch."

"Lady Fire-Washing is nearing the end of her closed-door cultivation. We can send a flying letter to summon her to work."

"There is also the Flowing Gold Guest, who has already refined the Heavenly Gold our Hall secretly gifted him. He sent a flying letter long ago asking what he can do for our Hall to repay this favor."

The subordinate recommended three people, and every one of them was a Golden Core cultivator.

It was already widely known that Ning Zhuo possessed Golden Core-level combat power.

Zhong Dao thought for a moment. "Every time Peach Blossom Daoist kills a person, he buries the corpse to plant a tree. His killing intent is too heavy, and he doesn't know how to hold back. Forget about him."

"Let Lady Fire-Washing continue her closed-door cultivation. Finding trouble with Ning Zhuo at this crucial juncture for no reason would waste her current undercover identity."

"Let's go with the Flowing Gold Guest. Have him challenge Ning Zhuo in public three days from now."

The subordinate hurriedly accepted the order.

Zhong Dao's movements in handling his administrative affairs never stopped from beginning to end.

-----

Danxia Peak.

Wang Yu and Zhongli Mei were currently admiring the night scenery.

Wang Yu informed Zhongli Mei about the news regarding Ning Zhuo publicly establishing an alliance in three days.

Zhongli Mei: "Ning Zhuo deliberately spread this? In that case, he is only giving us three days to consider?"

Wang Yu nodded: "Exactly. Three days isn't too long, nor is it too short; it's just right. Elder Zhong, what do you think of this matter?"

Wang Yu was the current Peak Master of Danxia Peak, but Zhongli Mei had long been an elder of Danxia Peak, possessing very deep seniority.

Zhongli Mei let out a long sigh: "My lifespan is nearing its end, and I am willing to use my final days to make a few more contributions to our peak. Why don't I just go and join Ning Zhuo in three days, seizing the position of the alliance leader for our peak?"

Taking the top seat meant becoming the first among his creditors, maximizing their influence over Ning Zhuo. More accurately, it would mean having the greatest influence over the Nanming Fire Furnace among all the creditors.

Wang Yu shook his head: "Elder Zhong has worked hard and achieved great merits for my Danxia Peak; you should be enjoying your twilight years in peace. If you were to join them, the implications would be too vast. The outside world would interpret it as - my Danxia Peak wants to lay its hands on Flowing Cloud Peak! That would not be good."

Zhongli Mei chuckled: "For someone who has lived as long as I have, I also possess a few fake identities of demons and heretics. Sneaking into Ning Zhuo's faction won't attract any idle gossip."

"I could even have my 'lifespan end' ahead of time; that's also an option."

Having reached the final juncture of his life, Zhongli Mei increasingly wanted to leave something behind and make some new contributions.

Wang Yu immediately revealed a tempted yet hesitant expression: "If so, wouldn't that be too much of a grievance for you, Elder?"

Zhongli Mei chuckled darkly: "By then, the Danxia Peak Elder 'Zhongli Mei' will already be dead. How could there be any grievances to speak of?"

Wang Yu clapped his hands and laughed: "Wonderful. Since that's the case, I will make other arrangements to coordinate with Elder Zhong."

Zhongli Mei: "I won't join under my original identity. As a latecomer, it would be best if I could become a new creditor."

Wang Yu: "Understood. Danxia Peak will fund you, Elder, to help you get close to Ning Zhuo."

Zhongli Mei added: "'Zhongli Mei' will not become the leader, nor can he become the leader. Who does the Lord Peak Master have in mind?"

Wang Yu: "As for Chunyangzi's side, I will go communicate with him a bit more."

After finishing their discussion, the two quietly parted ways.

A moment later, Wang Yu summoned Chunyangzi.

Over these past few days, Chunyangzi had been negotiating and playing games of wits with Wang Yu, discussing the conditions for him bringing the entire Pure Yang Sect to join Danxia Peak.

"You want us to join Ning Zhuo's side?" Chunyangzi asked in surprise. "It seems Danxia Peak cares a great deal about the Nanming Fire Furnace. That makes sense; after all, it is a Spirit Treasure-level pill refining furnace!"

He immediately shook his head: "That kid Ning Zhuo has outstanding methods, but I won't play into his hands."

"Peak Master Wang Yu, if you cannot agree to the conditions I proposed earlier, then we have nothing left to discuss."

"I am bringing my entire sect to surrender to you. I refuse to believe that other factions would be as petty and stingy as Danxia Peak!"

Wang Yu flicked his fly whisk: "Daoist Chunyangzi, please do not be impatient. It wouldn't be too late to listen to the conditions I'm offering first."

Chunyangzi coldly snorted but still said: "Speak."

As a result, Wang Yu's very first sentence caused Chunyangzi's expression to change slightly.
