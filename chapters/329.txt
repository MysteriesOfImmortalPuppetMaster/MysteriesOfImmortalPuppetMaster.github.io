Chapter 329: The Righteous Path is Just Like This

Ning Zhuo's small courtyard.

A pair of mechanical arms, like ice crystals, were crystal clear and translucent, with soft and graceful curves. The ten fingers were slender and delicate, smooth and rounded. The talismans on the palms and backs of the hands were like ice flowers, emitting a cold and holy aura.

This was exactly the mechanical component - Floating Ice White Jade Hand.

Innate Talent - Ice Jade Hand!

The freezing qi enveloped Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin, rapidly freezing her.

To boost the power, Ning Zhuo activated a temporary formation. The Formation Core was the Ice Heart Jade Flask! He had specially borrowed this from Ning Jiufan.

With the dual amplification of the Ice Jade Hand and the Ice Heart Jade Flask, Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin was frozen in a giant block of ice, and the dents and pits on her body rapidly repaired.

A moment later, the ice block disintegrated and melted. Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin stood up, motionless.

"How is it?" Sun Lingtong, who was helping to control the formation, walked out with a nervous look on his face.

Ning Zhuo carefully inspected the Buddhist Doctor mechanism once over. He saw that a streak of purple light still remained hidden within Meng Yaoyin's spiritual energy, and the surface of her Golden Core was also blooming with cloud flowers, brimming with purple intent.

He slowly shook his head.

Sun Lingtong couldn't help but sigh.

Ning Zhuo, in turn, comforted him, "Boss, this result is something we anticipated long ago, isn't it?"

"When my mother was alive, she was a Golden Core cultivator. After her death, she transformed into a puppet but could still face and repel Zhu Xuanji head-on; she is definitely extraordinary."

"Even she perished because of these injuries. For us to want to cure the injury that took her life using just one magic treasure and a bit of Innate Talent is practically impossible."

Sun Lingtong nodded: "Yeah, if it were useful, your mother probably would have taken action long ago, snatching Ning Jiufan's Ice Heart Jade Flask to heal herself."

"No." Ning Zhuo shook his head. "My mother is a member of the Righteous Path; how could she plunder others without reason? She definitely traded and borrowed it from our clan's ancestor."

Sun Lingtong: ...

He couldn't help but secretly grumble: "Based on all of your mother's actions in the Lava Immortal Palace, she didn't seem like a good person."

However, he also completely understood Ning Zhuo.

In Ning Zhuo's heart, his mother was naturally different from the rest!

So, Sun Lingtong let out an "Oh" and agreed: "You're right."

Ning Zhuo looked at Sun Lingtong, his eyes full of apology: "Boss, I'm sorry, I hid a lot of things from you. I..."

Sun Lingtong looked at Ning Zhuo in surprise, then waved his hand: "Didn't you already apologize? It doesn't matter!"

He chuckled, resting his hands behind his head: "To be honest, I really never thought that I was injured back then because of your mother. So, that's why you saved me."

"Your mother strikes really ruthlessly!"

"But, there was a reason for it. Noone told me to go snooping around your house."

"No wonder at the final moment of fighting over the Immortal Palace, I was blinded by fate and felt of love and resentment towards her."

On the day Ning Zhuo yielded the position of Master of the Immortal Palace to Meng Kui, he secretly confessed everything to Sun Lingtong.

After Sun Lingtong heard it, he suddenly understood and shouted in anger: "You brat, you actually hid so much from me, making me worry about you for nothing!"

Seeing Ning Zhuo staring at him with a sincere and eager face, what else could Sun Lingtong do?

Naturally, forgive him!

Up to now, hearing Ning Zhuo apologize again, Sun Lingtong instead comforted him: "Trifles, these are just minor matters."

"I was the one who repeatedly asked you to keep your secrets and not let me know."

"You did great, Little Zhuo."

"Hidden matters are easy to execute; open matters are hard to succeed. The reason you were able to succeed, wasn't it precisely because you hid things well? Because you could even keep me in the dark, you were able to use your weak body to eventually catch everyone off guard and snatch the greatest fruit of victory."

"Such an excellent habit must be maintained!"

Ning Zhuo hastily expressed: He would definitely maintain it, and he praised Sun Lingtong for teaching him well.

Sun Lingtong proudly put his hands on his hips and playfully scolded: "You brat, do you think flattering me will make me happy?"

He cultivated Ning Zhuo and could be said to have brought him up single-handedly.

This experience and feeling of an elder brother acting like a father left him without any jealousy. He would genuinely feel proud and gratified for any achievement Ning Zhuo obtained.

At this time, Ning Chen arrived outside the door to report, stating that the formal handover of the Zheng Clan's estate was complete.

Sun Lingtong said seriously: "Alright, it's my turn to do some serious business. Yang Chanyu and I will sweep through the Zheng Clan estate one more time to see if we can find any hidden vaults. If we find any loopholes, we'll come back and tell you."

It turned out that when Ning Zhuo, Meng Kui, and Zhu Xuanji made their agreement, they carved up the Zheng Clan.

According to the agreement's terms, the Zheng Clan's territory would all be transferred to Ning Zhuo, becoming the territory of the Ning Clan's branch family!

Sun Lingtong quietly left.

On the surface, Ning Zhuo had already severed ties with Sun Lingtong and the Void Sect faction. Therefore, he still needed to avoid arousing suspicion where necessary.

This severed state was deeply ingrained in people's hearts thanks to Zhu Xuanji's guarantee.

Even at the very end, it was the "Entry Standard" that seized the Immortal Palace once, letting the Book Lord and others know the information. In their eyes, the "Entry Standard" was Sun Lingtong ; they wouldn't connect it to Ning Zhuo.

Furthermore, many Golden Core cultivators were stuck in the Turning Point Secret Pavilion. Even when Chi Dun, Zhou Nongying, and others rushed over, they were apprehensive of the fierce power of the Great Serpent Scythe and did not teleport into the Main Hall through the grand doors.

This provided a massive prerequisite for Ning Zhuo and the other two to make a private agreement and keep it a secret.

Of course, it wasn't completely without flaws.

For instance, when Meng Kui blew off the roof of the main hall, the situation inside might have been detected by outsiders.

But this actually didn't matter at all!

Even if they knew, so what?

When the three involved parties had already negotiated and mutually recognized the outcome, who would the public believe?

Even if the public didn't believe Ning Zhuo, would they still not believe the City Lord's Mansion? Even if they didn't believe the City Lord's Mansion, would they not believe the Royal Family's Divine Constable, Zhu Xuanji?

Even if they didn't believe any of them, what could they do?

Could they affect the cooperation between the three parties?

When Sun Lingtong and Yang Chanyu met up, hiding their tracks and turning invisible, they arrived at the Zheng Clan territory just in time to see Ning Yong and others replacing the door plaque.

The plaque above the Zheng Clan's lintel was taken down, replaced by one for the Ning Clan.

This scene sparked heated discussions among the passersby in front of the main gate.

"It's changed, it's changed, it really changed to the Ning Clan's plaque!"

The relevant rumors were arranged and actively spread by Ning Zhuo.

"I heard this is the territory of the Ning Clan's branch family. Did you know? The leader of the branch family is actually Ning Zhuo!"

"Ning Zhuo? I think I've heard that name."

"He's a genius of the Ning Clan, very young, only sixteen years old!"

"What's going on? How much cultivation could a sixteen-year-old have?"

"He's only at the middle tier of Qi Refining."

"With such cultivation, he can serve as the head of a clan? What is his background?"

"He's just from an ordinary collateral branch, having suffered bullying for over a decade. You should have heard about the disputes between the main and collateral branches of the Ning Clan, right?"

"Then on what basis can he serve as Clan Leader? Even if it's just the leader of a branch family? I can't figure it out!"

"This is where you're out of the loop. This is a brilliant move played by the Ning Clan. The Zheng Clan was wiped out, leaving a perfectly good piece of territory vacant. The Ning Clan wanted to fight for it, but they already had their own territory, lacking any legitimate justification."

"So, they split into a branch family? Using this excuse to seize this fat piece of meat? Makes sense! So, Ning Zhuo is actually just a puppet pushed to the front?"

"Exactly. Being able to deduce this shows you have some perceptiveness. Wasn't it the same when this Ning Zhuo was pushed out to be the Lord of the Black Market before?"

"Then why didn't the Zhou Clan split off a branch family like this?"

"Sigh, wasn't it because they were a step too late and didn't react in time, letting the Ning Clan strike first? The cultivators of the Zhou Clan are relatively stiff and old-fashioned, it's not like you don't know!"

The passersby immersed themselves in this exchange, their commentary sounding logical and well-reasoned.

Their so-called seeing through the truth was actually just self-righteousness, vastly differing from the facts.

The true division of benefits was far more cruel and cold than low-level cultivators imagined. Many leaked pieces of news were deliberately sugar-coated by high-level cultivators.

Sun Lingtong and Yang Chanyu joined forces and secretly searched several times, but found no hidden vaults. However, they did discover quite a few vulnerabilities in the formations and other areas.

Some were old loopholes, while others were newly added.

Clearly, the City Lord's Mansion wasn't honest before the handover and had played many tricks.

All of these were reported to Ning Zhuo.

The clan territory's formation needed to be redesigned, and the various loopholes needed to be patched. Ning Zhuo knew that he was about to establish his own household and career. Besides, his true combat strength had already been exposed, so acting tough would garner more respect and wariness, rather than inviting people to push their luck due to greed.

The greatest advantage of the Zheng Clan's territory lay in its conditions for smelting and forging, which were the best in Fire Persimmon Immortal City.

In this regard, even the City Lord's Mansion couldn't compare!

Within the Zheng Clan's territory, Ning Zhuo hosted a public sacrifice.

He recited the sacrificial text, so emotional that his voice broke and tears fell: "The heavens shed tears, the winds and clouds lose their color. Today, our generation gathers here to mourn our sacrificed kin."

"... Ning Xiaoren, Ning Ze, Ning Xiangguo..."

"In life, you were both wise and courageous, of noble and pure virtue, your brilliance shining in all directions, bringing prosperity to the family and peace to our neighbors."

"Alas! Today you return to the dust, your heroic spirits falling into the Underworld... May your souls in heaven rest in peace, protecting your descendants and blessing the Ning Clan with eternal tranquility!"

When Ning Zhuo finished reciting, everyone in the hall was deeply moved.

Amidst a sea of white mourning clothes, the sound of crying gradually arose, and sorrowful music played loudly.

"My husband, my son!" Wang Lan was extremely sorrowful and fainted from crying.

Ning Zhuo turned pale with fright and personally attended to her. Extremely anxious, he personally stimulated her acupoints, changed her blood flow, fed her medicinal pills, and revived Wang Lan. Such pure filial piety filled the crowd with deep admiration.

Many unknowing clan members felt a strong sense of identification with him. Having such a branch family leader, even if his cultivation was a bit low right now, was no big deal. How bad could such a benevolent and filial person be!

"Ning Chen, quickly help my aunt to the back courtyard to rest for now."

Ning Zhuo specifically selected a large mansion within the Zheng Clan territory and offered it to Wang Lan to live in.

After handling this appropriately, he continued to host the sacrifice.

Once the main ceremony ended, he actively consoled Ning Xiangguo's family members and publicly gifted them many Spirit Stones.

He reflected on Ning Xiangguo's various contributions in the black market, listing them as if counting family treasures. This deeply moved Ning Xiangqian and the others, making them feel that everything they did was seen and appreciated by Ning Zhuo. This made it all worth it!

Yang Chanyu, who was invisible and leaning against a corner of the wall, wore a face full of mockery: "Rest in peace? Weren't these people killed by him? Can they rest in peace?"

"Hypocrite! This little brother of yours is too hypocritical." Yang Chanyu's tone was disdainful.

Sun Lingtong, sitting on the wall and also invisible, rolled his eyes upon hearing this: "What do you know? The Righteous Path is exactly like this."
