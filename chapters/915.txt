Chapter 915: Who Gives Thorns, Who Gifts Tree Seeds

Ye Qingming had clearly come with ill intentions.

However, Ning Zhuo's words could become extremely sharp when necessary. He had been honing this kind of verbal sparring since he was two years old. His childhood had not been a good one, but conversely, that harsh and oppressive living environment had helped train his glib tongue.

Today was Ning Zhuo’s big day. Since Ye Qingming took the initiative to provoke him, Ning Zhuo would naturally act tough.

The corner of Ye Qingming's mouth twitched slightly. She looked at Ning Zhuo and thought to herself, "This brat hasn't even joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect yet, nor is he an ally of our sect, yet he is already so aggressive and flaunts his edge. He truly doesn't know the immensity of heaven and earth!"

On the surface, Ye Qingming smiled faintly, "If I were Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo, owing such a massive debt, I surely wouldn't have the appetite for food or tea. Yet you, Fellow Daoist, are making a big show of establishing an alliance and stronghold, even making enemies everywhere. Your courage is truly unparalleled."

Ning Zhuo put on a bitter smile. "Fairy Ye misunderstands. Where would I get such courage? I am merely being forced by this massive debt. I am but a lowly Foundation Establishment cultivator; my greatest wish right now is merely to repay my debts."

"Where is this talk of making enemies coming from?"

"The ultimate goal of our Naming Stronghold alliance is to pay off the debt as quickly as possible. I, Ning Zhuo, could never do something like defaulting on a debt. I want to pay it off swiftly so that everyone is happy and well off."

"Fairy Ye represents the Green Tea Society to attend this ceremony. As a junior, I am extremely grateful. Please, take a seat."

How could Ning Zhuo bother to bicker with Ye Qingming about such things? Pure lip service was the most useless endeavor.

Ye Qingming was suddenly left without a retort. If she continued to entangle him in this, it would only make her, a senior, appear to lack grace.

It would have been fine if the two of them were alone, but the key point was that she was under the watchful eyes of the public, representing the Green Tea Society. She could only nod and find a seat.

After sitting down, she still felt somewhat stifled inside. It was truly rare for her to fall into a disadvantage in conversation and fail to grasp the initiative.

Reviewing this minor verbal clash, she discovered Ning Zhuo’s greatest advantage: "This kid is only at the Foundation Establishment cultivation base and is still very young. If I keep pestering him, it would be the strong bullying the weak. If word gets out, my reputation will suffer."

Ye Qingming was at the Golden Core cultivation base, but in this situation, it was Ning Zhuo, who was only at the mid Foundation Establishment stage, who ironically held the "advantage" in terms of cultivation.

Ning Zhuo noticed that Ye Qingming didn't choose to sit at the main table, instead sitting at the closest secondary table. He knew what was going on in his heart: "It seems that 'guests' like this won't be limited to just Ye Qingming."

However, Ning Zhuo had anticipated this scenario. The Green Tea Society was one of the factions most likely to send an envoy to attend; after all, this small faction was famous for its expertise in intelligence gathering and transmission.

Not long after Ye Qingming took her seat, a third Golden Core cultivator stepped into the main hall.

He looked middle-aged, had only one eye, and his sunken eye socket carried a dark bluish tint, as if he chronically lacked sleep. He indifferently scanned the entire hall before walking toward Ning Zhuo.

His walk was very distinctive; his pace was neither fast nor slow, with every step landing on the exact same beat, maintaining a constant speed. The hem of his old indigo robe remained completely still, while the thick hemp rope tied in a dead knot around his waist swayed slightly with his steps.

Underneath the frayed cuffs of his sleeves, a pair of prominent, bony hands were revealed. The knuckles were pale, looking as though they were constantly gripping something heavy, or as if they had been repeatedly crushed by something.

This was also an unfamiliar cultivator, prompting Kong Bailiu, Ah Huo, and the others to guess his identity in their hearts.

Ning Zhuo, however, guessed the person's rough identity; it should be someone similar to Ye Qingming.

Sure enough, the middle-aged, one-eyed cultivator came to a halt in front of Ning Zhuo. He didn't cup his hands in greeting. Standing ramrod straight, he spoke in a condescending tone, "Thorns Society, Li Ku. The President sent me."

His voice was also distinctively husky and low, leaving a lasting impression. It sounded like sandpaper grating against an iron plate, or like withered bamboo knocking together in the wind; every word carried a coarse texture that felt worn down.

There was no "congratulations," no "felicitations," not a single pleasantry.

"Congratulatory gift." Li Ku took out a palm-sized black ore from his sleeve. Its surface was as rough as thorn bark, with a faint metallic luster seeping through the coarse exterior.

Ning Zhuo accepted the ore and found it extremely heavy.

The rough texture ground against his palm, emitting a faint stinging sensation - not a physical sting, but an indescribable, spiritual suppression. It was as if countless tiny thorns were protruding from the ore, gently piercing his skin.

This was merely a Foundation Establishment-level precious material.

"I, Ning Zhuo, accept the goodwill of the Thorns Society," Ning Zhuo said smoothly, putting the Thorn Iron away. "Thank you for your trouble, Fellow Daoist Li. Please, take a seat."

However, Li Ku didn't move.

He stood there, his single eye still fixed on Ning Zhuo, his gaze carrying a heavy weight of scrutiny.

"The President brings a message: Suffering is a mandatory path in Cultivation. Since Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo wishes to come to Flowing Cloud Peak, you must be fully prepared to endure hardship."

Ye Qingming smiled.

Kong Bailiu and the other two had varying expressions.

Jiang Xiaobian was still tasting the delicious tea pastries, while Ah Huo, hearing the lecture hidden in the words, showed a look of indignation.

Zhang Dadan and Niu Cheng didn't understand the underlying meaning.

Patch Sun lowered his head, his gaze fixed on the teacup in his hand.

Gu Huaijiu let out a displeased cold snort.

Cao Gui frowned slightly, thinking to himself, "Young Master Ning Zhuo's momentum in establishing the alliance and charging for the peak is too grand; it really has drawn the attention of the original factions on Flowing Cloud Peak. But to think two factions would show up at once."

Ning Zhuo slightly raised an eyebrow, revealing a hint of a smile. "I've heard that the Thorns Society is famous for 'ascetic cultivation', advocating finding joy in bitterness and taking hardship as a teacher. Meeting Fellow Daoist Li Ku today for the first time has verified these rumors."

"In the future, when my Naming Stronghold settles on Flowing Cloud Peak, we might just seek guidance from your noble society on the profound mysteries of 'ascetic cultivation,' bringing mutual fulfillment."

Ye Qingming revealed a look of surprise.

The displeasure on Gu Huaijiu's face instantly dissipated a bit. He secretly admired Ning Zhuo's eloquence, knowing that with his own clumsy mouth, he could never come up with such an excellent response.

Kong Ran chuckled, also finding Ning Zhuo's response to be quite wonderful: "Mutual fulfillment in ascetic cultivation implies at least two possibilities. One is that the Naming Stronghold and the Thorns Society form a close relationship, mutually discussing their experiences in ascetic cultivation. The other is hostility, where Naming Stronghold inflicts suffering on the Thorns Society, 'helping' the latter carry out their own cultivation ideology and 'helping' them cultivate."

Li Ku stood frozen in place, falling into silence.

A few breaths later, he abruptly turned around and walked to a seat, ultimately sitting at the same table as Ye Qingming.

Ye Qingming glanced at Li Ku but didn't initiate a conversation.

She was more familiar with the Thorns Society's situation than anyone else in the hall. She knew that Li Ku was an authentic ascetic cultivator - taciturn, unsuited for eloquence and socializing, valuing words like gold, with a straightforward personality that often brought an inexplicable sense of oppression to others.

For the Thorns Society to send such a figure was clearly intentional, wanting to put Ning Zhuo in an awkward position.

Yet Ning Zhuo had handled the situation almost perfectly.

At this moment, Ye Qingming realized one thing: in verbal sparring, Ning Zhuo was an expert; the vast majority of cultivators would find it hard to gain an advantage over him.

Liu Fushu swept his gaze over Gu Huaijiu, Ye Qingming, and Li Ku, secretly feeling the oppressive atmosphere: "Although there are three Golden Core cultivators attending the ceremony, there is one friend and two enemies. The single friend is still going to join the Demon Subjugation Hall, so the strength backing Brother Ning up isn't very strong."

"Hahaha." The laughter of the fourth Golden Core cultivator arrived before the person did.

The crowd in the hall cast their gazes over and saw a burly man with a broad waist and a bushy black beard striding in confidently.

"Young Master Ning Zhuo!" he raised his hand, greeting Ning Zhuo from afar.

Ning Zhuo smiled: "Fellow Daoist Che Zhuzi, welcome."

"Young Master is a man who achieves great things." Che Zhuzi quickly walked up to Ning Zhuo, took out his congratulatory gift with both hands, and solemnly presented it to him.

The gift emanated a Golden Core-level aura. Its mechanical design immediately attracted the eyes of Ah Huo, Patch Sun, and Zhang Dadan; even Jiang Xiaobian's bulging little cheeks stopped moving.

It was a nest-shaped object about a foot high. Its entire body was a translucent orange-yellow, with a texture as warm and smooth as amber, radiating a misty yellow light under the candlelight. The light pulsed slowly and rhythmically, as if it were breathing, or perhaps in a deep slumber.

The Yellow Crystal Spider Nest!

Ning Zhuo revealed a hint of surprise. He was not unfamiliar with this item, having entered Che Zhuzi's treasure vault back in the day.

He hadn't expected Che Zhuzi to actually take a treasure from his vault to give him as a celebratory gift.

Ning Zhuo knew that this Yellow Crystal Spider Nest could produce mechanical spiders, which were precise, exquisite, and tiny - capable of even mending a Storage Pouch.

"Brother Che, this congratulatory gift is far too precious." Ning Zhuo tried to decline, but his bright eyes stared unblinkingly at the Yellow Crystal Spider Nest.

Che Zhuzi's heart ached from the expense, but seeing Ning Zhuo's expression, he knew he hadn't chosen the wrong gift. He patted his chest and said heroically, "Young Master Ning Zhuo is my biggest client. I will still need you to look after my business a lot in the future. You must accept this gift!"

"Since Brother Che is so gracious, it would be disrespectful of me to decline." Ning Zhuo smiled genuinely and publicly stored the gift into his Storage Belt.

Although he was in the predicament of being heavily weighed down by debt, the Yellow Crystal Spider Nest was a mechanical construct, which he absolutely loved.

Che Zhuzi was indeed a businessman - extremely shrewd, delivering a gift that struck right at Ning Zhuo's heart.

Ning Zhuo: "Brother Che, please take a seat. You must have a few extra drinks today."

"Absolutely!" Che Zhuzi laughed loudly, turning to temporarily take his leave of Ning Zhuo.

He quickly scanned the room and noticed that Gu Huaijiu was sitting at one secondary table, while Ye Qingming and Li Ku were sitting together at another.

Che Zhuzi naturally wouldn't sit at the main table. Since he himself was at the Golden Core level, he certainly wouldn't sit at the same table as Foundation Establishment cultivators like Kong Bailiu and Ah Huo. After thinking for a moment, he went to sit at the table with Ye Qingming and Li Ku.

With a smile, he struck up a conversation with Ye Qingming and Li Ku. As a businessman, he naturally wanted to expand his clientele.

As a result, Li Ku squeezed out a few words to first reveal his identity, causing Che Zhuzi to freeze for a moment. When Ye Qingming then revealed her identity, Che Zhuzi immediately displayed a bitter smile.

He regretted it immensely. Had he known their identities beforehand, he would have much rather sat next to those Foundation Establishment cultivators.

Kong Ran found this secretly amusing upon seeing it: "If it were any other faction, Che Zhuzi would have definitely recognized their affiliations in advance and wouldn't have made such an error. But it just had to be the Myriad Manifestations Sect, where there are simply too many people. Even among Golden Core cultivators, it's impossible for everyone to know each other."

With Che Zhuzi's arrival, unrestrained laughter filled the hall.

Even if there was still some awkwardness in the laughter, it had ultimately diluted the previously solemn atmosphere.

Amidst the laughter, Situ Xing, Shen Xi, and Lin Jinglong arrived together.

Situ Xing wore a deep blue dharma robe, his silver hair flowing like a waterfall. Shen Xi had a face like cold jade, his hand caressing a jade pendant. Lin Jinglong wore a gold-trimmed azure robe, his gaze profound and secluded.

Although the three of them were only at the Foundation Establishment cultivation base, they all possessed Golden Core battle power, hailing from super-clans with outstanding temperaments. Right now, as they stepped into the hall together, they immediately added a bit of luster to the place.

Ah Huo and his three companions cast envious and slightly reverent glances at them.

Niu Cheng opened his mouth slightly; it was his first time seeing these three, and sensing the obvious gap, he secretly grew a bit more insecure.

Liu Fushu's expression settled into calmness.

Kong Ran and Bai Jiyun felt nothing at all.

Ye Qingming, having already gathered the relevant intelligence, was not surprised.

Che Zhuzi secretly sighed in admiration: "This is Young Master Ning Zhuo's true social circle."

Situ Xing and the other two arrived in front of Ning Zhuo, taking turns to praise his attire and demeanor, showing that their relationship was quite close.

Ning Zhuo returned the greeting. "With the three of you brothers arriving together, I am truly flattered."

Situ Xing first drew out a flying sword and handed it to Ning Zhuo. "Brother Ning, a meager gift. I hope you will accept it."

It was a small sword, only three inches long and entirely deep blue, with dense, silver starlight patterns flowing across the blade. Situ Xing lightly flicked his finger, and the sword trembled slightly, emitting an incredibly soft hum, like the whispering of stars.

"This is a sword embryo. Forged from Meteor Star Refined Iron and tempered with starlight for a thousand days. Brother Ning, if you refine and nourish it with your own spiritual energy, you will be able to cultivate a flying sword entirely your own."

"Brother Situ's generous gift will be remembered," Ning Zhuo stored the sword embryo away, his gaze sincere. However, he was muttering in his heart; although this flying sword was good, it was not suitable for him. He would have to think about how best to deal with it.

Shen Xi took out a scroll of bamboo slips from his sleeve. "These are my clan's insights on Formations. Brother Ning, if you don't mind, you can flip through it."

Ning Zhuo hastily cupped his hands. "The Shen clan's Formations are renowned throughout the entire country. How could a lowly junior like me dare to be arrogant? Thank you, Brother Shen, for this gift!"

Lin Jinglong then presented a bag.

"Inside are the seeds of the Thin-Bark Cloud-Swallowing Birch," Lin Jinglong introduced. "There are many cloud currents on Flowing Cloud Peak. The Cloud-Swallowing Birch can absorb these currents and grow sturdily. Once the tree matures, its bark is as white as snow and can be peeled off layer by layer. Each layer of bark is as thin as a cicada's wing and as transparent as a cloud. It is top-tier material for Talisman Paper."

Ning Zhuo's eyes lit up. This was indeed very suitable for him at the moment.

The others gave gifts with their focus solely on Ning Zhuo himself. But Lin Jinglong was focusing on the Naming Stronghold. By giving this gift, there was another layer of blessing and optimism implied - he believed that Ning Zhuo's attempt to establish an alliance and claim the peak would certainly succeed, and that the Naming Stronghold would definitely secure a place on Flowing Cloud Peak to enjoy its abundant resources.

The items offered by the three of them were all Golden Core-level resources, causing the cultivators in the hall to secretly marvel, "Young masters of noble clans truly are wealthy and generous."

The three found a separate table and sat down together.

This was also a secondary table, even further from the main table, sitting next to Gu Huaijiu's. It offered a wide view while simultaneously distancing themselves from Ye Qingming and Li Ku.

To possess this level of social adroitness at their age, it had to have been cultivated from a very young age.
