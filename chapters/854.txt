Chapter 854: "Zhou Sumo"

Beneath the feet of Song Taosheng and his group was solid ground. The ground was octagonal, with giant trigrams carved in the eight directions - Qian, Kun, Zhen, Xun, Kan, Li, Gen, and Dui. Between the trigrams, countless tiny runes flowed endlessly.

Surrounding them were eight walls of light. Each wall stood a hundred zhang tall, emitting a faint golden light that firmly sealed off this space.

The triple formation within the Cloud Prison had finally been activated. In a mere instant, it swallowed all the assassins into the formation space, trapping them like rats in a barrel!

"Mr. Song!" The remaining three deathsworn gathered around Song Taosheng.

Song Taosheng raised a hand, signaling them to stay calm.

He looked up at the starry sky above, then down at the Eight Trigrams beneath his feet, and finally glanced around at the walls of light. His expression was solemn.

The starry sky above was the Cosmic Starlight Formation.

The Eight Trigrams below was the Eight Trigrams Bewildering Formation.

The surrounding walls of light were the Vajra Mountain-Suppressing Formation.

This formation space was not simple; it was an overlay of three formations!

The next moment, the stars overhead began to move.

Stars of all sizes slowly rotated, spinning faster and faster. With every revolution, a streak of silver starlight descended from the heavens, transforming into a tiny starlight arrow that shot toward Song Taosheng and the others.

The starlight arrows were incredibly fast and numerous, making them very difficult to dodge.

One deathsworn instinctively swung his sword to block, kicking up a shower of sword glints. The starlight struck the blade with a resounding clang, sending sparks flying everywhere.

"Let me." Another deathsworn opened a shield, expelling his spiritual energy to unleash its maximum defensive power. Instantly, a dome of light flared up, covering the group.

The starlight arrows were temporarily intercepted by the shield. The deathsworn who had swung his sword withdrew his hand, looking at his weapon and involuntarily drawing a sharp breath. The surface of his trusted longsword was already heavily pitted and severely damaged.

Song Taosheng's heart sank. "As expected of the Cosmic Starlight Formation!"

According to their intelligence, this formation heavily targeted artifacts. It could break an entire surface by concentrating on a single point, effectively destroying the enemy's treasures.

Song Taosheng swept his divine sense over the shield above them and immediately noticed that it, too, had developed numerous tiny holes. It wouldn't hold out much longer.

Literary treasure - Introspection Mirror.

Mencius states: "If one reflects on oneself and finds oneself to be sincere, there is no greater joy."

Song Taosheng summoned a bronze mirror. The mirror's surface reflected the starlight, perfectly countering the endless barrage of starlight arrows. It protected the group and stabilized their footing.

The deathsworn hastily retracted his shield, his expression grave. "We must find the formation core as soon as possible!"

The literary treasure, the Introspection Mirror, had been specifically prepared to counter the Cosmic Starlight Formation, and it was now serving its purpose. But it wasn't enough - far from it.

The group had only managed to stabilize their position. They had to put their all into breaking the Starlight Formation as quickly as possible to create a flaw, which would allow them to break out of the formation space.

In truth, without needing a reminder, the third deathsworn was already putting all his effort into detecting it. Among the cultivators present, he was the most accomplished in formations. Along the way, most of the artifacts used to break restrictions and formations had come from his hands.

Just as his investigation made some progress, the Eight Trigrams ground beneath their feet began to rotate slowly.

The eight trigrams lit up alternately. Sometimes they spewed scorching flames; sometimes they surged with bone-chilling blazing currents; sometimes fissures crisscrossed the ground, threatening to swallow the group; sometimes the ground transformed into mountains that toppled over to crush them...

Leading the remaining deathsworn, Song Taosheng stumbled along, darting around like headless flies.

This was the might of the Eight Trigrams Bewildering Formation - directions were scrambled, the Five Elements were inverted, and Heaven and Earth shifted positions!

Song Taosheng and the others tried their hardest to discern their orientation, but they couldn't resist the grand formation's power.

"Damn it, the heavens move with the earth. We have to break the Eight Trigrams Formation first!" The deathsworn's initial progress in analyzing the Cosmic Starlight Formation had all amounted to nothing.

Qian for Heaven, Kun for Earth, Zhen for Thunder, Xun for Wind, Kan for Water, Li for Fire, Gen for Mountain, Dui for Lake - eight trigrams, eight variations, appearing alternately with infinite power.

The deathsworn frantically calculated, ignoring the blood flowing from his seven orifices.

Time and time again, his calculations succeeded, pinpointing safe landing spots or correct flight paths, which barely allowed their side to stabilize the situation.

But running themselves ragged like this left him with no spare energy to calculate the formation core.

The guard cultivators controlled the formation, taking in every bit of Song Taosheng and his group's struggles.

"Heh, they're done for!"

"Song Taosheng! To think he actually colluded with Demonic cultivators to break into the Cloud Prison! Traitor!"

"Qin De has already been killed by him... So, is it because of their crushing defeat in the sutra debate that these Confucian cultivators flew into a rage out of humiliation?"

"How should we deal with them?"

"It's already been reported, but Lord Wu An is still sleeping. He drank too much last night..."

The commander responsible for guarding the Cloud Prison was a Nascent Soul-level cultivator, but he was an alcoholic and extremely undisciplined.

"Such a massive incident has occurred, and he's still sleeping?!"

"Lord Wu An is in deep trouble this time. Colleagues have already sacrificed their lives; there's no way to cover this up."

Most of the cultivators worried for Wu An. This immediate superior of theirs was usually very popular.

"Sigh, how many years has the Cloud Prison been peaceful and stable? With such a major incident this time, I'm afraid even Lord Zhong Dao will be held accountable!"

"Stop chatting and hurry up and activate the third formation to capture Song Taosheng's group alive. The more time we waste, the more blame we'll shoulder."

"You're right, you're right!"

Thus, the next moment, the Vajra Mountain-Suppressing Formation was slowly activated.

Within the formation space, the eight massive walls of light began to move toward the center in a slow, ponderous manner.

With every inch they moved, the oppressive pressure within the formation space grew heavier.

Song Taosheng and the others immediately felt an invisible pressure enveloping them from all directions, threatening to suppress them!

The pressure on them grew greater and greater, and their movement speed became increasingly sluggish.

Song Taosheng gritted his teeth and took an item from his robes.

It was a jade bead the size of a fist, entirely jade-green and radiating a warm, gentle light.

Literary treasure - Integrity Bead!

By scrupulously abiding by the Dao of impartiality and righteousness, the upright will not tilt. If it does not tilt, the space secures itself, making it difficult for external pressure to invade.

As Song Taosheng pushed his spiritual energy into it, the Integrity Bead flared brightly.

A pale radiance expanded from the bead, rapidly diffusing into the surrounding heaven and earth. Wherever it passed, the starlight stagnated, the Eight Trigrams solidified, and the walls of light temporarily paused.

This scene left the guard cultivators somewhat astonished.

"To successively bring out two literary treasures to deal with the Cosmic Starlight Formation and the Vajra Mountain-Suppressing Formation respectively shows that they came prepared!"

"Even so, this is all they can do."

"Exactly. Exhausted just trying to defend themselves, they have absolutely no strength left to break out!"

After their brief astonishment, the guard cultivators calmed down.

The situation was quite obvious.

The deathsworn's fighting spirit plummeted.

Song Taosheng, maintaining two literary treasures all by himself, was also running out of steam. He sighed inwardly, "It seems this place will be my grave."

-----

Huazhang Kingdom.

Main Hall of the Imperial Ancestral Temple.

The jade ruyi in Zheng Jing's hands shattered completely, turning into motes of azure light and dissipating.

Wang Shu, meanwhile, collapsed on the ground. With his eyes glaring in anger, he squeezed out the very last trace of his strength, still fighting desperately.

Han Song held nothing in his hands; the white silk bearing the sacrificial text had been completely burned to ashes.

The dragon-shaped azure smoke coiling around the Monarch of Huazhang Kingdom had long since dissipated. He bit his Tongue Sword to draw blood, spat out his vital essence blood, and, regardless of the instability it would cause to the nation's foundation, fiercely unleashed a State Technique - 

Glorious State Prosperity!

It elevated the national destiny for a short period of time, but the aftereffect was the consumption of the nation's foundation, meaning the national destiny would plunge into a prolonged state of weakness afterward.

The next moment, the Golden Seal and Jade Tome suddenly trembled, and its sixty-two jade tablets flipped rapidly. All the characters in the book flew out, transforming into countless golden and jade characters that spiraled and danced within the pillar of light.

Following this, the pillar of light swelled wildly, reaching ten zhang in thickness!

A sudden dragon roar echoed.

This was the destiny of a mighty nation, the accumulation of the Huazhang Kingdom's millennium-long literary lineage, unleashed at the cost of consuming a great nation's foundation.

Having paid a massive price, the Huazhang Kingdom faction once again overturned the situation, escaping the crisis and gaining the upper hand!

-----

Cloud Prison.

Formation Space.

The deathsworn let out a cry as blood sprayed from his seven orifices. He went deaf and blind!

His voice was hoarse but boundlessly excited: "I've calculated it! There's a flaw!!"

The landing points of the starlight were not in harmony with the subsequent transformations of the Eight Trigrams.

Starlight fell on the east, but the ground in the east turned into a fissure, swallowing the starlight.

Song Taosheng and the others' eyes suddenly lit up. They realized that after undergoing a comprehensive overhaul, the Myriad Manifestations Sect indeed still required time for coordination and unification. The timing of their assassination perfectly coincided with this period. Now that the Cloud Prison side had rashly activated the formations at full power, it caused each individual formation to run wild.

Although they could cooperate smoothly at the beginning, as time went on, the three formations each bolted in their own directions. The initial discrepancies widened, drifting further apart and forming a visible flaw!

"Attack there!" the deathsworn, bleeding from his seven orifices, pointed out, having staked his life to calculate the critical coordinate.

Song Taosheng and the others immediately unleashed a torrential bombardment. Just as expected, they pierced through the formation space, forming a temporary gateway.

The deathsworn calculating the formation exhausted his mental energy in the next second and died on the spot.

"Let's go!" Song Taosheng didn't hesitate. Directly discarding the two literary treasures to buy a moment of respite, he took the lead and leaped into the temporary gateway.

The only two remaining deathsworn, both heavily injured, followed closely behind.

The trio's vision went black, then lit up again.

Song Taosheng crashed onto the icy ground, panting heavily.

After regaining his senses, he hurriedly struggled to his feet and looked around.

"Where is this?"

Stone walls, lamplight, a corridor, iron doors - this was the ninth level of the Cloud Prison!

Song Taosheng and the two deathsworn were all dumbfounded.

After fighting tooth and nail and barely managing to break out of the formation space, they had unexpectedly drilled right back in.

"It seems our mission cannot be completed this time."

"Mr. Song, we will face death together right here!"

The two deathsworn spoke in succession.

Song Taosheng nodded, a look of absolute resolve on his face.

The Cloud Prison truly lived up to its reputation as a vital stronghold of the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Earlier, they had only taken advantage of their unpreparedness. Once the prison guards reacted, there was absolutely no possibility of escaping alive.

The best option right now was death, avoiding capture alive.

"Quick, quick, quick, they fell to the ninth level! Hurry up and search for them!"

"There are only three left, and all are in extremely poor condition."

"Try to capture them alive! We need them breathing!"

Chaotic footsteps echoed from the end of the corridor.

The guards had chased after them, but temporarily hadn't discovered Song Taosheng and the others.

This was because one of the deathsworn, who was most skilled in camouflage, had deployed his signature trick, temporarily blocking the surrounding Cloud Prison's detection.

The other deathsworn suddenly made a move against the other cages.

He consecutively shattered cell doors one by one, releasing the prisoners inside.

"Consider yourselves lucky. Now I'm giving you a chance to run for your lives. Go on, run!" he laughed uproariously.

Song Taosheng's expression changed drastically: "You..."

He didn't want to see unrest; he only wanted to assassinate Qin De.

The greater the losses caused to the Myriad Manifestations Sect, the less Duanmu Zhang and the others would be favored.

However, the stances of the other two deathsworn were different from his. Knowing that their deaths were certain, they were eager to see the enemy suffer as much damage as possible.

Those imprisoned on the ninth level of the Cloud Prison were no ordinary inmates.

Some possessed superb strength, some were guilty of heinous crimes, some had sensitive identities... their common trait was that they all held immense value. For example, Qin De, despite only having a Golden Core cultivation, could suppress the development of the Confucian cultivator community.

The first to rush out of his cell was an emaciated middle-aged man.

He was exceptionally tall, standing at a full nine feet, yet he was nothing but skin and bones, like a walking bamboo stick. His hands were abnormal - his ten fingers were unusually long, hanging down to his knees, and his nails were black as ink, three inches long, and curved like hooks.

"I'm finally out!" He laughed wildly at the ceiling. His laughter echoed through the corridor, vibrating the stone walls on both sides and making dust rain down.

He didn't leave; instead, he made a move against the adjacent cell. With his hook-like fingers, he effortlessly destroyed the cell door, releasing more fellow inmates.

A man wearing a tattered azure robe walked out slowly. He mobilized his spiritual energy, which had been suppressed for countless years. His face changed, looking as if a layer of white paint had been applied, and a bizarre smile emerged.

Bang.

A bald monk smashed the surrounding cages into rubble with his head.

His bald head emitted a coppery glow and was a full two sizes larger than a normal person's. It was densely covered with ordination scars.

Eagle Claw Butcher, Ghost-Faced Scholar, Bronze Ascetic!

A fragrant breeze welled up as a woman with an incomparably stunning face stepped out. As her gaze flowed, she oozed charm. Wearing a ragged prison uniform that revealed large swaths of snow-white skin, she walked out with a swaying figure.

Succubus Demon!

"Hahaha - hahaha - " Amidst manic laughter, a Daoist with disheveled hair leaped out, dancing and gesturing wildly.

"Heaven is dark and Earth is yellow; the universe is vast and wild; the sun and moon wane and wax; the stars and constellations are arranged - No, no, that's wrong. It's 'Heaven and Earth are not humane; they treat all things as straw dogs' - "

He occasionally recited scriptures, sometimes spouted gibberish, sometimes danced wildly, and sometimes fell to his knees weeping bitterly.

Mad Wind Daoist!

-----

More and more prisoners regained their freedom.

Although they had been suppressed and imprisoned for countless years, their foundations were profound. After regaining their freedom, they all possessed immense mobility and destructive power.

Many of them were originally sworn enemies, but in this moment, they all helped each other break free. They all knew that the more chaotic their side caused things to be, the greater their chances of escaping.

However, there were also those whose hatred was irreconcilable.

A deathsworn was just about to break open a door to release the prisoner in the cage before him.

A Demonic cultivator next to him shouted loudly, "Don't let him out! He's Zhou Sumo, someone from the Myriad Manifestations Sect!"

The deathsworn immediately stopped.

The next moment, another Demonic cultivator sprinted over. With bloodshot eyes and overwhelming fury, he roared, "Zhou Sumo, I want you dead!"

He smashed through the door and ruthlessly lunged at the tightly bound Zhou Sumo to deliver a killing blow.

"Zhou Sumo" sighed softly. Demonic power surged wildly from him, viciously blasting the incoming Demonic cultivator away.

The Demonic cultivator crashed to the ground, spitting blood, and glared at "Zhou Sumo": "You're not him. Who are you?"

Quite a few gazes shot over.

"Zhou Sumo" thus revealed his true form. With an air of helplessness, he sighed, "Come and die."

Yet he didn't attack the downed Demonic cultivator; instead, he charged toward the deathsworn.

The deathsworn was already heavily injured, an arrow at the end of its flight, barely maintaining his destructive spree. Facing a well-rested "Zhou Sumo" who had been biding his time, he only managed a slight resistance before being killed on the spot.

"Good blood, good blood." "Zhou Sumo" inhaled sharply, instantly sucking the deathsworn dry into a mummified corpse.

After acquiring this blood, he circulated it through his Demonic Seed and then released it, forming a cloud of blood mist that shrouded his body.
