Chapter 926: Discussing the Seating Arrangement

Ning Zhuo withdrew his gaze.

He gently patted the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Carriage, and the mechanical carriage slowly descended.

The python-like carriage glided close to the martial arena's floor, the sound of its mechanisms as soft as spring silkworms eating leaves.

Soon, it arrived at the edge of the martial arena.

Ning Zhuo stored away his zither and stood up from the carriage. His moon-white robe swayed slightly, like a lazy cloud drifting over clear green water.

He stepped down from the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Carriage. Without looking back, he reached out and gave a gentle backward wave. The carriage transformed into a streak of flowing light and drilled into his Storage Belt.

He walked down from the martial arena, his pace neither hurried nor slow.

In the eyes of the crowd, Ning Zhuo's features were placid and his expression warm. He possessed neither the arrogance of a victor nor the fatigue of someone who had just fought.

By now, the golden aura bestowed upon him by Bai Jiyun's poem had dissipated, allowing the Great Thousand Mechanism Melody Garment to reveal its true, elegant flair.

Ning Zhuo was clad in white, with a pair of clear eyes. Behind him lay an arena filled with shattered rocks and broken mechanisms. He looked like a stalk of green bamboo revealing itself from the misty clouds after a mountain rain had ceased.

It was as if the person who had just battled a Golden Core cultivator, severed a head, and forced Liu Jinke to publicly concede defeat had absolutely nothing to do with him.

Such composure and grace momentarily silenced the observing Cultivators.

Jiang Xiaobian's eyes shone brightly, her fingers clutching her sleeves, her heart leaping with absolute joy: "Young Master Ning Zhuo is truly amazing! He is only at the Mid Foundation Establishment Stage, yet he chopped off the head of a Golden Core cultivator. Liu Jinke is so powerful, an independent rogue cultivator surviving on Flowing Cloud Peak, yet he actually surrendered to Young Master Ning Zhuo of his own accord. It's truly unexpected!"

Ning Zhuo was capable of fighting.

He possessed Golden Core-level combat strength!

Previously, the masses could only guess, but now this battle had practically proven it.

The winds and clouds Ning Zhuo had previously stirred up, and the reputation he had built, were no longer just empty, floating illusions.

Ah Huo's chest heaved, his eyes seeming to burn with fire. He gazed at Ning Zhuo, his heart searing hot: "After this battle, Young Master Ning Zhuo rivals Golden Core cultivators. Who would dare say our young master only relied on the momentum of the fire furnace to establish the Nanming Stronghold? He was already a powerhouse to begin with!"

Ah Huo felt even more honored that he had been invited by Ning Zhuo to personally witness this battle.

Such an experience was enough for him to boast to outsiders for a long time.

Gu Huaijiu merely nodded slightly.

His face remained somber, but his eyes were much brighter than before. He already had a deep friendship with Ning Zhuo, even entrusting the matter of his corporeal liberation to him.

Now, watching Ning Zhuo fight, that lingering shred of anxiety at the bottom of his heart completely dissipated.

"I didn't misjudge him."

"In the future, if that day truly comes for me, entrusting it to him means I haven't trusted the wrong person."

Standing at the edge of the crowd, Cao Gui's face first showed pleasant surprise, which then gave way to a bit of a daze.

"Young Master Ning Zhuo's momentum is booming. Through this battle, he has solidified his foundation!"

"Both within my clan and the Commerce Hall, I will benefit from this."

The gaze he cast towards Ning Zhuo contained much more respect and closeness.

Pi Fujie, with deep-set eye sockets and a slightly pale face, cried out in relief in his heart: "Thank goodness I pivoted quickly! Actually, I should have done it long ago - turning from an enemy into a friend with Ning Zhuo!"

Was a Mid Foundation Establishment Cultivator who could behead a Golden Core still just a Mid Foundation Establishment Cultivator?

The key was that Ning Zhuo also possessed formidable methods; he could leverage momentum, lay out schemes, and force Liu Jinke to concede defeat under the watchful eyes of the public. Why continue to offend such a powerhouse?

Pi Fujie secretly gritted his teeth: "What is face? The Pi Clan has always been able to yield when necessary. Ning Zhuo didn't accept my congratulatory gift because I didn't do a good enough job. But I can't force the gift on him this time. Next, I have to think of more ways to get closer to him. I must wash away this old grudge completely; who knows, I might even be able to borrow his strength in the future."

Furnace Immortal Tao Liweng fell into silence.

He originally harbored grievances regarding Ning Zhuo's attitude towards him, feeling that Ning Zhuo was a youth who achieved success too early and was inevitably frivolous. Today's battle dispelled most of that indignation.

Tao Liweng put himself in Ning Zhuo's shoes: "If I were in the Mid Foundation Establishment Stage with his level of strength, I would probably be even more arrogant than him."

The next moment, his brows furrowed slightly again.

"However, Liu Jinke was ultimately unbalanced in his skills. Although the Golden Liquid Returning Core Body is wondrous, it is too severely countered by the Fire Element. I am different. If I were to truly engage in a battle with Ning Zhuo, I wouldn't leave such obvious flaws for him to exploit."

He was not yet entirely convinced.

But he wouldn't proactively challenge Ning Zhuo either.

His temperament was just like that; he lacked the impulse to act out and get overly serious about such things.

Zhu Fenxiang remained expressionless.

But Zhu Guizhi, standing beside her, knew that her daughter's heart must be filled with smiles at this moment.

Zhu Guizhi focused her gaze on Ning Zhuo and sighed inwardly: "For my daughter to be moved by such an outstanding young junior is perfectly normal."

Jiang Ping frowned slightly, still mentally reviewing the entire battle.

"Liu Jinke's combat strength is not weak. To be able to survive on Flowing Cloud Peak as a rogue cultivator, his power is actually commendable. The Midas Touch depletion, Golden Needle strafing, Golden Armor covering the body, Void Teleportation Talismans, Golden Liquid Returning Core Body... When these methods are stacked together, they actually far surpass those of an ordinary Golden Core cultivator."

"But Ning Zhuo first broke the Midas Touch, then dragged out the pacing with a swarm of mechanical birds, borrowed the amplification of a Confucian poem to elevate his offensive, and finally used a Golden Core puppet to restrict Liu Jinke's positioning, sealing the deal with a Fire Element slashing spell."

"Was it truly just good luck that he happened to possess Fire Element Spells that countered Liu Jinke?"

Jiang Ping shook his head inwardly. He believed more strongly that this was due to foundational heritage.

Not just Ning Zhuo's foundational heritage, but the heritage of the clan behind him!

"Where did Ning Zhuo come from? His clan must be far from simple."

"More importantly, Ning Zhuo is a Mechanism Master. In this battle, he didn't even utilize a single mechanism technique. He ended the battle relying solely on Fire Element and Wood Element Techniques."

"He didn't use his full strength!"

The more Jiang Ping thought about it, the more solemn his expression became.

Dong Nishang gently stroked her sleeve, her eyes sparkling, ripples stirring in her heart as well.

She had originally thought Ning Zhuo was a prodigy in the path of mechanisms, yet today she saw that his attainments in both the Wood Element and Fire Element were extraordinary. However, thinking back to Ning Zhuo's stunning performance in several small Confucian cultivator tests, she felt it all made sense.

Hundred Herbs Old Man stroked his beard in silence, a look of inquiry flashing in his eyes.

Like the Poison Annihilation Wanderer, he was quite concerned about the "Spirit Nourishing Pill" that Ning Zhuo had ingested before the battle.

"This Medicinal Pill was disguised. It allowed the power of Foundation Establishment level spells to skyrocket straight to the Golden Core-level. Just what kind of pill is it?"

Hundred Herbs Old Man felt an itch in his heart: "If there's an opportunity, I must ask him for one to examine."

Mu Yuehua looked at Ning Zhuo, a trace of yearning revealing itself amidst her aloof expression.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's impression of Ning Zhuo had changed considerably.

"The fire intent was condensed without dispersing, and the slashing momentum was fierce without being erratic. What's even more brilliant is that it seemed specifically tailored to shatter the metallic nature in Liu Jinke's blood."

"To be able to unleash such a Fire Element killing blow at the Mid Foundation Establishment Stage. Even if he borrowed external power, it sufficiently shows his comprehension in the Fire Element."

"Perhaps, this is also one of the reasons why the artifact spirit of the Nanming Fire Furnace ultimately chose him."

The combat strength Ning Zhuo had displayed belonged to the Golden Core-level, still far from enough to make the Nine Fires Dragon Lord treat him as an equal.

But the fact that Ning Zhuo was not weak was a good thing for the Nine Fires Dragon Lord.

"He has proven that he isn't the weak link of the Nanming Stronghold." The Nine Fires Dragon Lord gave Ning Zhuo a lot more validation.

Ye Qingming, Li Ku, and Jin Mantang had varying expressions, but all were heavy.

Ye Qingming's face was like cold jade, her fingers slowly tightening within her sleeves.

"With the Nanming Stronghold established, it will inevitably cause intense tremors to the old situation on Flowing Cloud Peak. I originally thought Ning Zhuo's greatest reliance was the furnace, his creditors, and the momentum he stirred up. Now it seems he himself is also a nail - a nail capable of piercing through Golden Core cultivators."

Li Ku was as silent as a stone.

He didn't like to speak much, but he knew very well that starting today, any plots directed at the Nanming Stronghold could no longer view Ning Zhuo as a weak point.

Jin Mantang's complexion was awful.

Liu Jinke hadn't been able to suppress Ning Zhuo, and instead propelled his success. The Jade-Shattering Hidden Thunder hadn't worked either.

"What's even more critical is that Ning Zhuo's earlier words have subtly roasted Liu Jinke over an open fire."

He saw through Ning Zhuo's scheme in this matter.

"This kid can not only fight, but he also knows how to use his victorious momentum to set up situations. His methods are even more terrifying than his combat strength!"

In Jin Mantang's eyes, Ning Zhuo's combat strength compensated for his shortcomings; Ning Zhuo's true strength lay in his attainments in the Righteous Path.

Kong Ran's face was full of admiration: "Brother Ning indeed secured the victory."

Liu Fushu smiled and looked at Bai Jiyun: "Brother Bai gifted a poem today. I'm afraid you will also gain fame alongside this battle."

Bai Jiyun shook his head gently, unable to suppress the slight upturn of the corners of his mouth.

His smile was open and clear, and his eyes were much more serious: "The poem is merely the wind. The wind can fan the fire, but it cannot create fire out of thin air. Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo himself is that furnace of new flame."

Speaking up to this point, he couldn't help but feel emotional in his heart.

He originally just wanted to observe a scene of the mortal world, never expecting this scenery to be even more magnificent than anticipated. A Foundation Establishment cultivator beheading a Golden Core cultivator, a youth establishing a stronghold, various factions changing their expressions, and five Nascent Soul cultivators witnessing it with their own eyes!

Such a scenery was more than enough to be written into another poem.

Che Zhuzi narrowed his eyes, yet the familiar smile of a merchant surfaced on his face.

"It was worth it," he said inwardly. "Sending this heavy gift was worth it!"

The stronger Ning Zhuo was, the more likely the Nanming Stronghold was to succeed in the future. Every bit of favor he invested now could yield ten or a hundred times the return in the future.

Shen Xi remained silent from beginning to end.

He looked at Ning Zhuo's back, light and shadows flickering in his eyes.

He was envious.

Because he knew that starting from today, the name Ning Zhuo had already surpassed him in another dimension, surpassing the vast majority of genius newcomers of this generation.

Because Ning Zhuo was already controlling a certain situation.

He already possessed the slight qualifications to be a chess player.

The aftermath of this martial arts demonstration would definitely spread out in all directions.

Ning Zhuo, getting ahead before the impact fed back to them, politely saw off the many guests who had purely come to watch the ceremony. Then, he immediately pulled the Cultivators who had joined the Nanming Stronghold back into the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion for the first internal meeting of the Nanming Stronghold.

After all the cultivators entered the hall, the atmosphere became subtle.

When establishing a stronghold and forming an alliance, the initial and most critical task was to determine titles and arrange seating orders.

Seating order seemed to merely be about where one sat, but it actually concealed the hierarchy of superiority and inferiority, the distribution of benefits, and the allegiance of hearts.

Especially among those present, there were Nascent Souls, Golden Cores, and Foundation Establishments; there were renowned figures of the Righteous Path, Demon Cultivator guest elders, and old demons who had turned over a new leaf; there were also some who were inherently unyielding to one another and harbored deep grudges.

If this matter was not arranged well, the framework of the Nanming Stronghold that had just been set up would immediately loosen by thirty percent.

Ning Zhuo naturally knew this.

His expression calm, he cupped his hands in a bow to everyone.

"Seniors and fellow Daoists, your willingness to join the Nanming Stronghold is my fortune, as well as the Nanming Stronghold's fortune."

"Since today is the first discussion, let us not busy ourselves with other matters, and first finalize the seating order."

Carrying the fruits of his fresh victory, it was enough to change everyone's perspective of him, no longer viewing him as a Foundation Establishment cultivator, but as a Golden Core-level existence. Coupled with his special status, no one questioned his words at this moment.

Ning Zhuo continued: "In arranging the seating order for my Nanming Stronghold, we will not base it on age, reputation, or merely cultivation base."

"This is all because the original intention behind establishing this stronghold was my pioneering act to honor a promise and repay debts."

"Whoever I, Ning Zhuo, owe the most to, will sit at the front."

The Cultivators in the hall were all silent.

Ning Zhuo added: "Of course, cultivation realms cannot be entirely ignored either. Nascent Soul seniors will naturally be listed separately in the front seats. As for the Foundation Establishment and Golden Core members, they will temporarily be grouped in the same tier, and then ranked according to the weight of the debt."

"Arranging the seats in this way is most aligned with my intentions, and most suited to the foundation of the Nanming Stronghold."

"What does everyone think? If there are objections, you may raise them now."

Ning Zhuo waited for a moment, observing everyone's expressions.

Chunyangzi's sleeves were neat, he sat upright and motionless, his eyelids slightly lowered.

The Red-Robed Guest leaned back in his chair, coldly glaring at Chunyangzi, his expression rather unsightly.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord crossed his arms over his chest, his face solemn.

Tu Yuanzi's expression was heavy, like a segment of a mountain's root embedded in the earth.

Poison Annihilation Wanderer Tan Zhu still maintained a sickly appearance, his gaze dark and unfathomable, his thoughts unknown.

None of the five Nascent Soul-level cultivators raised any objections, so naturally, the others had nothing to say.

Ning Zhuo chuckled softly.

This was exactly as he had expected.

Using debts was the most justifiable method. At the same time, it ensured the interests of the vast majority of the Cultivators.

They would also approve of it; after all, it stemmed from contributions, and everyone could measure the gaps between each other.

As for the newly joined Tu Yuanzi, Poison Annihilation Wanderer, and others, they nominally had no debts owed to them. But the key point was that they weren't without opportunities. The Nanming Fire Furnace still had twenty percent of its flaws remaining and required funding to be repaired. If they had any ambitions, then they could just make contributions.

The crucial point was that these contributions were something everyone was happy to see.

After all, only a completely restored and intact Nanming Fire Furnace could truly demonstrate its value.

Ning Zhuo had precisely calculated human nature and interests, walking very steadily on this critical step.

Ning Zhuo thus continued: "Since everyone agrees, then next, I shall first express some of my own views. If there are any shortcomings, I implore the seniors, lords, and fellow Daoists in this hall to point them out and correct me."

Ning Zhuo extended his hand to gesture: "The first seat, Senior Chunyangzi."

Everyone's gazes instantly landed on Chunyangzi.

Chunyangzi slowly opened his eyes. His expression was calm, as if he had expected this all along, or as if he simply didn't mind.

Ning Zhuo said: "To repair the Nanming Fire Furnace, Senior Chunyangzi first took out the Pure Yang True Fire Pearl and threw it into the core of the furnace. This pearl was condensed from Pure Yang True Fire by successive generations of patriarchs of the Pure Yang Palace. It takes hundreds of years to form a single one, and the entire palace possesses merely a few."

"Afterwards, Senior poured forty-nine drops of Pure Yang Treasure Liquid completely into the furnace. This liquid is difficult to accumulate and is a long-held treasure of the Pure Yang Palace. The spiritual liquid transformed into mist, mending the furnace walls, making the furnace body sturdier, and allowing the formation patterns to operate more smoothly."

"Even later, Senior accessed the Pure Yang Palace's treasury, even bringing out their sect-guarding treasure, the Pure Yang Golden Core. When the Golden Core entered the furnace, crimson-gold light pierced the heavens, and the illumination, warmth, vitality, and Dao principles contained within it all merged into the Nanming Fire Furnace."

Speaking up to this point, Ning Zhuo's face revealed a solemn expression.

"The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit chirped incessantly because of this, which is enough to show its approval."

"In terms of the depth of resources, the weight of the treasures, and the merit of repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace, Senior Chunyangzi rightfully takes the first place."

Chunyangzi's expression was indifferent, but a trace of complex emotion surged in his heart.

For the sake of the Nanming Fire Furnace, he had indeed bled profusely.

The Pure Yang Palace's treasury had been heavily depleted because of this, and even a sect-guarding treasure like the Pure Yang Golden Core had been thrown into the furnace by him. If in the end, it was all just drawing water with a bamboo basket in vain, he would inevitably face immense pressure upon his return.

But now, taking the first seat of the Nanming Stronghold, the significance was entirely different.

"The first seat..." Chunyangzi said inwardly, "This kid Ning Zhuo certainly knows the rules and is very tactful."

He recalled what Wang Yu had once said: If he could borrow Ning Zhuo's setup and take control of the Nanming Stronghold, he might just be able to covet the position of the Peak Master of Flowing Cloud Peak.

Chunyangzi's eyelids lowered slightly, a sharp glint flashing through his eyes before vanishing.

"This first seat is just the beginning."
