Chapter 886: Lord of a Thousand Hammers Repairs the Furnace

Seeing the moment Ning Zhuo successfully pacified the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, Wang Yu almost stood up, a brilliant light flashing in his eyes!

"My decision last night was correct!"

"Since Ning Zhuo made such a request through the Commerce Hall, he must be absolutely confident. He has already gained the approval of Zhong Dao from the Demon Subjugation Hall, and is able to erupt with such a surging noble spirit. He definitely wouldn't act without aim, nor is he a selfish person who only looks out for his own interests."

"Therefore, I did not accept the adjustment of the sequence."

"As expected, I used this to force out his true trump card."

Wang Yu let out a long sigh.

The heaven and earth were vast, and capable people emerged generation after generation.

"Ning Zhuo is merely a minor Foundation Establishment cultivator, yet he can actually accomplish something the entire Myriad Manifestations Sect cannot."

"True, I didn't mobilize the Sect's foundational figures above the Nascent Soul level over the matter of the Nanming Fire Furnace."

Such existences wouldn't take action for a mere spiritual treasure either.

"But Ning Zhuo actually did it!"

At this moment, Wang Yu felt the deepest empathy with Zhong Dao's profound approval and high regard for Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo's ability to communicate with and pacify the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was completely a qualitative change!

Tie Kuang, the Hall Master of the Artifact Refining Hall, as well as Danxia Peak and the Alchemy Hall, actually all possessed the ability to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Repairing the physical body of the furnace had never been an obstacle or a difficult problem.

The true difficulty was the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

The artifact spirit had been frightened too severely; the slightest movement would cause it to die from terror.

Tie Kuang's assessment had gained the agreement of Wang Yu and the others.

Whoever could solve this puzzle of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit could save the entire Nanming Fire Furnace.

Now, Ning Zhuo had done it!

It wasn't just Wang Yu; the numerous cultivators behind Ning Zhuo also saw this point, which was why they murmured in unison, filled with astonishment.

How could a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator achieve this?

Countless sharp gazes firmly locked onto the Spirit Communication Mirror in Ning Zhuo's hand.

Ever since the Spirit Communication Mirror appeared in the world, this was the first time it had received such intense attention!

Everyone wanted to see right through the Spirit Communication Mirror!

Were it not for the Rising Cloud Minor Trial, where emitting divine sense carried the suspicion of interfering with Ning Zhuo, countless sweeps of divine sense would likely have thoroughly scanned both Ning Zhuo and the Spirit Communication Mirror by now.

In the outer layers, the many spectating cultivators also erupted into exclamations.

They hadn't expected that the very first cultivator to participate in the minor trial would pull off such a spectacular feat!

"Ning Zhuo can actually pacify the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit? I'm not seeing things, am I?!"

"He truly is a genius!"

"This isn't something a simple genius could pull off. Didn't you see all the Nascent Soul and Golden Core cultivators are dumbfounded?"

"Even the current Hall Master of the Artifact Refining Hall, Tie Kuang, is at his wit's end. Doesn't this mean that in this aspect, Ning Zhuo is better than Tie Kuang?"

"More than just better than Tie Kuang! Didn't you see that Danxia Peak, the Alchemy Hall, and others had to rush to host this Rising Cloud Minor Trial?"

"To think Ning Zhuo possesses such ability in artifact refining? It's simply inconceivable!"

"No, more accurately, his ability to communicate with and pacify the artifact spirit is the most crucial part!"

"How exactly did he do it?"

Countless people discussed fervently, unable to figure it out, all greatly shocked.

Chunyangzi's expression was grave as he looked at Ning Zhuo's back, a heavy look in his eyes.

The red hair of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord fluttered in the wind, but his mood was very heavy. Ning Zhuo's performance made him feel an unprecedented sense of crisis.

Tao Liweng could no longer sit calmly meditating with his eyes closed. He opened his eyes, staring fixedly at Ning Zhuo.

Dong Nishang wore a smile, thinking, "As expected of the junior highly regarded by our Confucian cultivator community." Her mindset was the most relaxed, because she knew she had already gone all out earlier, and this fourth round was just going through the motions.

If Ning Zhuo ultimately won, this was something Dong Nishang would be happy to see, and a result the entire Confucian cultivator group would gladly accept.

In short, no one dared to underestimate Ning Zhuo anymore. At this moment, every cultivator participating in this Rising Cloud Minor Trial placed immense importance on him, viewing him as their most powerful competitor!

Ning Zhuo officially began to repair the pill furnace.

His divine sense surged out like a tide, "pulling" the spirit threads from the refining furnace one by one and carefully "weaving" them into the cracks on the furnace wall.

These spirit threads used Nine Heavens Profound Iron as bone and Violet Palace Spirit Liquid as blood. They had successively refined precious materials like Flowing Cloud Sand, Great White Golden Essence, Celestial Crystal, and Abundant Earth. Every single thread flowed with brilliant colors, harboring extraordinary foundation.

One after another, the spirit threads were precisely embedded into each crack by Ning Zhuo, then rapidly refined to mend the pill furnace. Ning Zhuo's movements were adept and steady, neither rushed nor slow, without the slightest deviation.

His divine sense and spiritual energy were consumed madly, quickly causing Ning Zhuo's face to pale as fine beads of sweat formed on his forehead.

The cracks on the furnace wall gradually shrank under the stitching of the spirit threads.

From a finger's width to a thread's width, from a thread's width to a hair's breadth, and from a hair's breadth to nothing at all.

Some charred edges gradually faded under the nourishment of starlight, revealing a hint of dark red, akin to the hue of a slumbering volcano.

Chunyangzi, Cangyazi, and others who were closer witnessed the entire process and were moved.

"This youth's artifact refining techniques are actually this outstanding; it is hard to imagine he is only a mid-stage Foundation Establishment cultivator."

"Just how old is he? To have such attainment at such a young age?"

"It's not impossible. I have indeed heard of some geniuses capable of reaching this level. However... such figures usually make a name for themselves across the entire kingdom; they are national-level geniuses!"

"Didn't Ning Zhuo defeat Gu Qing? He should be considered a national-level genius."

After the final spirit thread was successfully embedded into a crack, refined, and filled the gap, Ning Zhuo sighed and slowly withdrew his stance.

"Little darling, come be affectionate." On the surface, Ning Zhuo used the Spirit Communication Mirror, giving it a shake, but in reality, he secretly issued a command using the Life Hanging by a Thread.

The shivering Vermilion Bird artifact spirit couldn't control itself and revealed its form.

Thus, everyone saw the fluffy bird head of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit peek out from the main body of the pill furnace, looking at Ning Zhuo and emitting "chirp chirp" sounds just like a baby bird.

"Hiss..." At this moment, Ning Zhuo heard the sound of gasps echoing behind him again.

But the next moment, Ning Zhuo controlled the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, making it retract its head.

"The time limit has been reached. Congratulations to Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo for achieving excellent results. Please step down for now and let the other fellow daoists have a try." The hosting cultivator spoke very politely.

Ning Zhuo sighed once more and said unresignedly, "If I were given ample time, I could..."

His words were interrupted by the hosting cultivator, "Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo, this is the Rising Cloud Minor Trial hosted by Danxia Peak, and the rules have already been set. I hope you can understand."

Ning Zhuo tilted his head back and let out a long sigh, turning around with exceptional reluctance to return to his original spot in the crowd.

Chunyangzi, Cangyazi, and the others all breathed a sigh of relief.

They individually sighed in their hearts; the rules had saved them this time. If Ning Zhuo had truly been allowed to continue, it was foreseeable that once he completely repaired the Nanming Fire Furnace, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit would definitely give its heart to him. Then there would be nothing left for anyone else to do.

Ning Zhuo kept his head down, his lips pressed tightly together. After returning to the crowd, he continued to stare at the Nanming Fire Furnace. His fists clenched subconsciously, his gaze filled with reluctance, nervousness, and anticipation.

A massive amount of gazes remained concentrated on him.

Ning Zhuo's performance had been too outstanding, surpassing almost everyone's imagination.

Right up until the second in line, Zhu Fenxiang, took the stage, these people were still privately discussing Ning Zhuo.

"Judging by the aura, that precious mirror he took out must be his Lifebound Magic Artifact!"

"This is a Lifebound Magic Artifact of the Upper Dantian, which means he actually primary cultivates the Upper Dantian?"

"In the information he reported, didn't it say that he mainly cultivated the Middle Dantian, practicing the... Five Elements Qi Rhythm Art?"

"Nonsense, faking early information to throw a smokescreen at competitors is standard practice, isn't it?"

"But did you watch the recording of Ning Zhuo in the martial arena? He truly is skilled in Five Elements spiritual energy."

"Perhaps that is just a layer of his disguise. Or maybe..."

"He primary cultivates both the Upper and Middle Dantians?!"

"Hiss... If that's the case, just how excellent must the Cultivation Art he practices be?!"

"No wonder he wanted to hide this intelligence. This is very likely his biggest trump card, but for the sake of the Nanming Fire Furnace, he chose to expose it to the public eye."

"Understandable. If it were me, I'd be willing to gamble on it too!"

Dressed in full attire, Zhu Fenxiang walked toward the Nanming Fire Furnace, but her pace grew slower and slower.

She thought of the cooperation plan Ning Zhuo had relayed through the god statue relay last night. She thought of the discussion she had with her mother, and of Ning Zhuo's stunning performance just now.

"He did it!"

"Just as he said he would."

"Since that's the case..."

Zhu Fenxiang had two plans, and which one she followed depended on Ning Zhuo's performance.

Since Ning Zhuo had actually pulled it off, she decisively abandoned her original plan and opted for the new one.

Thus, the crowd watched as Zhu Fenxiang walked slower and slower, eventually stopping at the edge of the pavilion's terrace.

Evident hesitation surfaced on her face.

Then very quickly, her tightly furrowed brows smoothed out, her eyes brightened a bit, and her expression grew resolute.

First, she opened a piece of letter paper and wrote in public view. It transformed into a flying letter and immediately flew away from Danxia Peak.

Next, she removed her black robes and crimson skirts, took off her heavenly crown, and set aside her beaded tassels and jade pendants.

She donned a Hammer Crown. The crown used Refined Iron as a base, covered with black leather on the outside, and was shaped like an iron hammer.

On the front of the crown, a red copper hammer seal was embedded, its engraving depicting a hammer striking an anvil. On either side of the crown hung a nine-inch-long iron chain, its links interlocking as it fell to her ears. At the back of the crown, an iron hairpin was inserted. The head of the pin was carved into a hammer shape, and the characters "Thousand Hammers" were engraved on its body.

She put on a black jacket. The jacket used coarse hemp as its inner layer and black fabric on the outside; it had no embroidery or patterns, bearing neither ornament nor splendor. A red copper protective collar bordered the neckline of the jacket, adorned with seven hammer-shaped studs.

She fastened on a leather skirt. The skirt used cowhide for its outer surface and iron plates for its inner lining, making it as heavy as armor. Iron nails formed hammer patterns on the skirt's surface; nail connected to nail, pattern linked to pattern, like the scars of being tempered a thousand times.

Then, she wrapped an iron apron around herself. Made of three overlapping layers of cowhide, the apron was as thick as an iron board and as heavy as plate armor. She put on iron bracers. Forged from Refined Iron, the bracers stretched from wrist to elbow, measuring one foot and two inches long, lined with cowhide and covered with iron armor on the outside.

Next, she tied on a hammer belt. The belt was made of interlocking iron rings, link upon link, measuring twelve feet long and weighing thirty-six catties. The buckle was an iron hammer; the hammerhead served as the tongue, and the hammer handle acted as the frame.

She stepped into iron boots. The boots had Refined Iron soles and cowhide uppers, each weighing thirty catties. Iron nails formed hammer patterns on the surface of the boots; the toes curled up like hammerheads, while the heels were as square as anvils.

Finally, she applied artisan makeup.

She used soot to draw her eyebrows, making them look like iron rulers; applied iron powder to her face, turning her complexion the color of iron; and dyed her lips with red clay, making them the color of rust. On her forehead, she painted a hammer mark with copper powder.

The expressions of everyone present varied.

They were not unfamiliar with Zhu Fenxiang's previous attire and accessories. Those were used for performing the Divine Summoning Art to summon the [Southern Bright Vermilion Bird]. Zhu Fenxiang had already used it once in the third round and successfully summoned the deity.

But now, Zhu Fenxiang had canceled the originally successful plan and changed into a different set of clothing and makeup for a different Divine Summoning.

Nascent Soul and Golden Core-level cultivators had broad horizons, and they all recognized it.

"Zhu Fenxiang has changed her plan. Does she want to summon the [Lord of a Thousand Hammers]?"

"The Zhu Clan established itself in the world using the Divine Summoning Art. Since Zhu Fenxiang changed her strategy, she should be able to summon the Lord of a Thousand Hammers."

"The Lord of a Thousand Hammers is one of the artisan gods. Does Zhu Fenxiang also want to imitate Ning Zhuo and repair the pill furnace?"

"However, she doesn't have Ning Zhuo's Lifebound Magic Artifact. How will she pacify the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit? This is far too risky!"

The crowd once again grew alarmed and doubtful.

Wang Yu's gaze also sharpened, his heart jumping into his throat once more.

This was something beyond his expectations.

Zhu Fenxiang arrived in front of the pill furnace and slowly stood still.

First, she took out an iron censer and lit sandalwood incense. Then, she retrieved sacrificial vessels - an iron anvil and an iron hammer - and dancing with her hands and feet, she began the ritual of the Divine Summoning Art.

Amidst it all, she chanted:

"Furnace fire as the token, charcoal smoke as the incense."

"Iron anvil as the altar, iron hammer as the word."

"Disciple Zhu Fenxiang humbly uses a heart tempered a thousand times to invite the Lord's descent."

"The Lord's hammer opens heaven and earth."

"The Lord's anvil stabilizes the four directions."

"The Lord's fire melts metal and stone."

"The Lord's charcoal tempers refined steel."

...

The curling incense smoke gradually transformed into a faint phantom, yet it never fully solidified.

Zhu Fenxiang decisively stepped before the iron anvil, exerted a sudden burst of strength, and struck the anvil with her hammer.

First strike. Amidst a muffled, resounding boom, thick smoke billowed.

Second strike. Charcoal sparks erupted, sending embers flying everywhere.

Third strike. The phantom solidified; the Divine Summoning was a success!

The Lord of a Thousand Hammers!

He was an old man with a burly physique like an iron tower, his back slightly hunched as if bearing a crushing weight. His facial features were rugged, with high cheekbones and deep-set eye sockets. His eyes were like two clusters of burning charcoal, shining brightly in the darkness.

His hair was iron-gray and entirely disheveled. His beard was the color of red copper, curled like vines. His hands were as rough as tree bark, with thick knuckles and an indelible rust color embedded under his nails.

He had only condensed his upper half, floating in mid-air.

Right at this moment, Zhu Fenxiang's mother, Zhu Guizhi, having received the flying letter, rushed to the scene.

"Fenxiang, catch!" she said breathlessly, tossing a heavy storage bag to Zhu Fenxiang from afar.

Zhu Fenxiang immediately took out precious materials from it.

The first item was the same as Ning Zhuo's: Nine Heavens Profound Iron. It was pitch black and as heavy as a mountain.

The second item was Dragon Blood Copper. It was scarlet red, its surface covered in fine scale patterns resembling dragon scales.

The third item was Phoenix Feather Gold. It was pure gold in color, its surface covered in fine feather patterns resembling phoenix feathers.

The fourth item was Primordial Cold Iron. It was ice-blue in color, cold to the touch, with fine ice crack patterns on its surface.

...

Zhu Fenxiang bowed in greeting, loudly requesting the Lord of a Thousand Hammers to begin forging.

The Lord of a Thousand Hammers exhaled a mouthful of flames, engulfing all the precious materials in a sea of fire. Then, he took out an iron hammer and struck at the empty air.

The first hammer fell, and the materials deformed.

The second hammer struck, and impurities flew out.

Upon the third hammer, the materials took shape.

Clang, clang, clang...

After several rounds of hammering, the precious materials turned into a stream of molten liquid. It flew onto the shattered body of the furnace, adhered, solidified, and ultimately successfully repaired a small section.

The materials depleted, the Lord of a Thousand Hammers slowly dissipated under Zhu Fenxiang's respectful send-off.

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit poked its head out again and chirped twice, displaying vitality far exceeding what it had before!

Wang Yu took a deep breath, overjoyed, and couldn't help but stand up.

From the moment Zhu Fenxiang started, he had been on edge, strictly monitoring the situation. If the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit showed any abnormal signs, he would instantly activate the Formation to forcefully stop any of Zhu Fenxiang's actions.

But from beginning to end, everything went smoothly for Zhu Fenxiang.

Chunyangzi, Cangyazi, and the others looked at each other in dismay, all seeing the pleasant surprise in each other's eyes.

Only Ning Zhuo clenched his fists tightly. Stripped of his previous composure, his expression looked quite unsightly.
