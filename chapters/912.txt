Chapter 912: Drilling the Flying Birds

"Which factions are close to the Thunder Cloud Society?" Qinghuangzi pondered for a moment before clarifying for Ning Zhuo, "Their external relations have always been quite good."

"This is all because the cultivators of the Thunder Cloud Society primarily practice Thunder path Cultivation Arts. Even if it is not their main focus, they all practice it as a secondary art."

"They help others transcend lightning tribulations."

"Golden Core lightning tribulations, Nascent Soul lightning tribulations, and even the successful refinement of certain important and precious Medicinal Pills can attract lightning tribulations. Refining certain magic treasures will also do the same."

"The Formation they built at the mountain peak, the [Heavenly Thunder Guide], has miraculous uses. It can prematurely trigger a portion of the lightning tribulation for the cultivator transcending it. This way, it helps the cultivator pass through it much more smoothly."

As the number one faction on Flowing Cloud Peak, there was a reason the Thunder Cloud Society had dominated the mountain peak for so long.

Logically speaking, the number one throne was very difficult to sit on, but this group of thunder cultivators could, by helping others transcend tribulations, make many cultivators indebted to them. Therefore, they alleviated a massive amount of external conflict.

The various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak could be said to both love and hate this group of people.

Because of this, Qinghuangzi could not say which faction they were closest to.

"However, the second faction, Bird and Beast Manor, is very obvious. Their relationship with Myriad Beast Peak is extremely close. Once, a formidable expert challenged the manor. Outmatched, Bird and Beast Manor directly used their connections and ultimately invited the Spirit Beasts of Myriad Beast Peak to assist them, defeating the expert and defending their position," Qinghuangzi introduced.

Ning Zhuo nodded slightly, recalling the relevant intelligence in his heart.

"Bird and Beast Manor itself is a group formed by Beast Tamers to help one another. These people revere the ideology that 'all things in Heaven and Earth, birds and beasts belong to the same flock.' They believe that cultivators should coexist with Spirit Beasts and not place themselves above them. Therefore, they don't just help the Beast Tamers themselves; they also help all kinds of Demon Pets and Spirit Beasts."

Qinghuangzi continued: "The third largest faction is the Water-Severing Blade Pavilion. They are located near the Flowing Cloud Waterfall. If you want to join, you must draw your blade and sever the water. The larger the scale of the severed water flow, the higher your status within the organization. These people believe that an ultimate blade strike can sever all bonds. When dealing with problems, they prefer to solve them using the blades in their hands."

"If we're talking about close relationships, I personally think it's the Artifact Refining Hall."

"Many cultivators in the Water-Severing Blade Pavilion purchase Weapons from the Artifact Refining Hall. The main material of these Weapons is called Ink Cloud Iron. This material is deeply processed by the Artifact Refining Hall and is exclusive to them. Generally speaking, the yield is relatively low. However, someone discovered that the Artifact Refining Hall supplies the Water-Severing Blade Pavilion on a massive scale."

"Oh, right. The Water-Severing Blade Pavilion also has conflicts with Jianming Peak. Once, a powerhouse from the Blade Pavilion went alone, challenging swords with his blade. In the Martial Arts Hall, he consecutively defeated nearly ten cultivators of Jianming Peak, causing multiple deaths and injuries. Of course, in the end, he also died in the Martial Arts Hall."

Exchanging words with Qinghuangzi indeed yielded different gains.

Ning Zhuo seized the opportunity to ask further, "Why did this Blade Pavilion powerhouse want to challenge Jianming Peak?"

Qinghuangzi replied, "The public claim was to test his blade. This happened over sixty years ago."

Qinghuangzi conversed with Ning Zhuo for quite a while, telling the latter a lot of information.

Combining this with the intelligence he had purchased himself, Ning Zhuo gained a clearer understanding of the faction distribution on Flowing Cloud Peak.

"On Flowing Cloud Peak, the top three factions are universally acknowledged. They are the Thunder Cloud Society, the Bird and Beast Manor, and the Water-Severing Blade Pavilion."

"After them, the ones with relatively large influence include the Thorns Society, Iron Hand Sect, Setting Sun Society, Facing Death Sect, Territory Expansion Alliance, Green Tea Club, Hanging Gourd Bureau, White Cloud Village, Metal and Stone Alliance, Floating Life Society, and Questioning Heart Studio."

"All the above factions possess Nascent Soul-level combat power."

"Further down are dozens of small factions, led by Golden Core cultivators, with Foundation Establishment cultivators as the main body."

Before leaving, Qinghuangzi said, "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, if you successfully establish your alliance tomorrow, and if any Nascent Soul cultivator joins, it will inevitably draw targeted attacks. The top three factions may not act, but the Nascent Soul-level factions below them are very likely to do so."

Ning Zhuo nodded, indicating that he would be careful.

He personally escorted Qinghuangzi out of his cave dwelling before turning back.

Qinghuangzi and he were quite close, but Ning Zhuo had no intention of persuading him to join.

There was no need.

After all, according to Meng Yaoyin's plans for him, Ning Zhuo was only establishing an alliance to digest this bout of calamity luck. In the future, he would still need to extricate himself from the whirlpool that was Flowing Cloud Peak and find another place.

"On Flowing Cloud Peak alone, there are dozens of Nascent Soul-level cultivators."

"The struggles between factions are too frequent, and the camps are overly chaotic. This is not a place for peaceful cultivation."

"It's not suitable for me."

Ning Zhuo picked up the Storage Bag given as a gift by Qinghuangzi, entered the martial drill Formation inside his cave dwelling, and took out five types of mechanical flying birds to test them one by one.

The Neck-Spear Crane was entirely black and white, its long beak sharp, and its neck featured a series of spiral patterns.

Ning Zhuo infused his spiritual energy into it. The crane's eyes lit up, becoming two points of starlight.

With a flap of its wings, it soared upward, flying into the air.

Ning Zhuo manipulated it with his divine sense. The Neck-Spear Crane's neck sharply contracted, then stretched out, thrusting forward.

During the strike, its beak pierced the air, emitting a sharp whistling sound.

Three feet, six feet, all the way to nine feet. Ning Zhuo tested out the absolute limit of the neck's extension.

But the true strongest strike was not this.

Ning Zhuo first made the Neck-Spear Crane charge to the highest point in the air, then dive downward. During the dive, its wings flapped, continuously accelerating.

As the Neck-Spear Crane was about to hit the ground, it violently pulled up, carving out a massive, valley-like arc.

And during the process of the dive, its entire neck, along with its head and beak, launched outward, forming a mechanical long spear that impaled deeply into the floor tiles.

Without its neck and bird head, the cavity on the Neck-Spear Crane's body was exposed. Wind violently poured into it, causing its speed to plummet abruptly while making it easier to pull the bird's body back into the high sky.

During the pull-up process, a spare bird head drilled out from the crane's body, bringing out a new, slender crane neck covered in spiral threads.

"There are two spare crane necks in total, allowing it to unleash its strongest attack three times."

"But in actual combat, three times is actually too many," Ning Zhuo estimated in his heart.

Because in actual combat, it was highly likely that after a single charge, the entire Neck-Spear Crane would be destroyed. It might even be struck and annihilated by Techniques right in the middle of its first charge.

This was not a flaw in the Neck-Spear Crane's design, but rather that in Ning Zhuo's estimation, his opponents would be at the Golden Core level!

"When I establish the alliance tomorrow, even if someone comes to challenge me, the highest they will be is Golden Core-level."

"A Nascent Soul cultivator actively challenging a Foundation Establishment cultivator would lose all face. Unless there is a blood feud."

"Even if only Foundation Establishment cultivators come to find trouble with me tomorrow, their combat strength should be extremely outstanding."

In fact, Ning Zhuo himself needed Golden Core-level cultivators to act as his enemies, using them to display his own combat strength.

Once his Golden Core-level combat strength was recognized, he would no longer be treated as a soft persimmon in the Naming Stronghold, and he would truly secure this crucial position.

The Neck-Spear Crane's design was not flawed. The problem was that the Neck-Spear Cranes in Ning Zhuo's hands were only at the Foundation Establishment level, and most were even just at the Qi Refining level.

Mechanical cranes of these levels could pose a threat to Foundation Establishment-level opponents, but against Golden Core-level opponents, they would only serve to drain them.

Only upon reaching the Golden Core-level would the Neck-Spear Crane's two spare necks truly show their usefulness.

"Perhaps, I should modify them slightly, adding new power to the mechanical component that is the crane neck? Or take out the two spare crane necks and replace them with other components in the freed-up space?"

The moment this thought arose, Ning Zhuo actively dispelled it.

The mechanical constructs of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Tube] had all undergone rigorous testing and layers of improvement, reaching a point where they were practically unalterable.

If a massive leap in performance were truly possible, it would require completely overturning their foundational architecture. And in that case, it would be equivalent to redesigning entirely new mechanical constructs.

Of course, apart from this reason, there was also a very realistic circumstance - tomorrow was the time to establish the alliance, and Ning Zhuo simply wouldn't have the time.

"I'll familiarize myself with these few mechanical flying birds as much as possible, then rest. Tomorrow is the main event." Ning Zhuo sighed. Time was truly insufficient for him.

Especially after entering the main gate of the Myriad Manifestations Sect to participate in this iteration of the Flying Cloud Assembly, opportunities were everywhere.

Ning Zhuo meticulously counted every second of his days and nights.

After drilling with a single Neck-Spear Crane, Ning Zhuo increased the quantity. The first time, he added ten; the second time, he added fifty. By the fifth time, Ning Zhuo had practically emptied his entire stock of Neck-Spear Cranes.

The flock of cranes circled and danced above the drill grounds, forming a dense, dark cloud.

"Relying purely on divine sense to control them at this scale is noticeably difficult; the Neck-Spear Cranes are no longer agile."

"Using Void Spider Silk on these Neck-Spear Cranes would also be far too wasteful."

"Condensing spiritual energy into threads, however, barely works..."

The reason it barely worked was that condensing spiritual energy into threads would increase the burden on Ning Zhuo's spiritual energy.

Ning Zhuo currently only had the cultivation of the mid Foundation Establishment stage, and the Five Elements Qi Regulation Art offered no advantages in spiritual energy reserves. Ning Zhuo mostly utilized the mutual generation of the Five Elements to convert one portion of Five Elements spiritual energy into multiple portions of single-element spiritual energy, indirectly increasing his total spiritual energy.

However, during the time he controlled the flock of cranes, his reserves of Five Elements spiritual energy in his middle dantian rapidly plummeted. He wouldn't be able to sustain it for long at all.

"I can replenish my spiritual energy by consuming Medicinal Pills and digesting their medicinal efficacy. But doing so would inevitably slow down my rhythm in actual combat."

"The most effective way is still to use the Neck-Spear Cranes in small batches as much as possible."

"By the same logic, it's the same for the Integrity-Clasping Pheasants, Gold-Thread Caged Sparrows, and Sleeve-Piercing Harriers. Only the Headwind Kites don't consume much of my spiritual energy. But the quantity of these paper kites is not very large."

What is learned on paper always feels superficial; Ning Zhuo gained a lot from practical exercises.

After the Neck-Spear Cranes came the Integrity-Clasping Pheasants. After the Integrity-Clasping Pheasants came the Headwind Kites.

In the midst of his practice, Sun Lingtong came over to visit him.

"Be careful tomorrow, don't let it go to your head," Sun Lingtong urged.

Ning Zhuo nodded. "Rest assured, Boss. I know what I'm doing."

Sun Lingtong sighed, "It's a pity I can't act together with you. Tomorrow is definitely going to be very lively!"

The two of them were connected by Life Hanging by a Thread, but unless absolutely necessary, they would not use it lightly.

There would definitely be many people coming tomorrow. Both Sun and Ning worried that if they used this Divine Art, others might notice some clues.

That would be extremely bad.
