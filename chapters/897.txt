Chapter 897: Ning Zhuo, Don't Mess Around

With the conversation having paved the way up to this point, Ning Zhuo naturally informed Tao Liweng of his plan to form a faction and vie for Flowing Cloud Peak.

After hearing this, Tao Liweng's eyes widened immensely. He stared at Ning Zhuo in disbelief, "Ning Zhuo, you, a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator, want to fight for Flowing Cloud Peak? That is one of the eight main peaks of the Myriad Manifestations Sect!"

Ning Zhuo's expression darkened slightly as he forced a smile, revealing the displeased look of a genius youth who was being looked down upon. "Judging solely by cultivation is merely the perspective of the ordinary. Dao Friend Tao, do you take me for an ordinary Foundation Establishment cultivator? To tell you the truth, it’s not as if I haven't defeated a Golden Core cultivator head-on before."

Unlike his previous interactions with Nascent Soul cultivators, Ning Zhuo had adopted an attitude of equality right from the start of his conversation with Tao Liweng, directly addressing him as 'Dao Friend.'

Tao Liweng had already been feeling uncomfortable, and hearing Ning Zhuo’s arrogant declaration now made him frown immediately.

Observing his expression, Ning Zhuo chuckled lightly. "If Dao Friend Tao doesn't believe me, it's quite simple. We can head to the nearest Martial Arts Hall for a direct sparring match. You'll quickly understand that my words are not empty boasts."

Tao Liweng felt even more displeased in his heart.

Being offended to his face by a Foundation Establishment cultivator - and not even one at the peak, but merely at the mid-stage - made his brows furrow even tighter, and anger welled up within him.

Yet, he never opened his mouth to accept the challenge.

I am a dignified late-stage Golden Core cultivator. By steadily following my cultivation routine, I only need ten years to reach the peak of the Golden Core stage.

Spar with a mid-stage Foundation Establishment cultivator?

If I win, it would be expected; I might even be criticized for an 'unfair victory'. But if I lose, I will instantly become a laughingstock, serving as a stepping stone for Ning Zhuo to build his reputation!

This is no good at all.

Thinking of this, Tao Liweng shook his head and coldly sneered, "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, how much could you possibly know about my Core Embracing Immortal Ascension Art?"

"Forget it. I won't stoop to your level."

"It’s a good thing that you have confidence. If you think you are stronger, then just go on thinking so."

Ning Zhuo immediately displayed a look of regret, as if he felt quite disappointed over losing an excellent opportunity to prove himself due to Tao Liweng's refusal.

In reality, he wasn't surprised by Tao Liweng's choice at all.

During the course of the Nanming minor trial, he had gathered intelligence on the other cultivators. Furnace Immortal Tao Liweng was no exception.

Ning Zhuo's assessment was: This person prefers to immerse himself in bitter cultivation, staying holed up in his cave mansion - no, holed up inside his alchemy furnace - year-round. He can be said to be completely aloof from worldly strife, entirely focused on advancing his own cultivation. When dealing with problems, he often chooses to let things go; fighting and killing are absolutely never his first option.

Ning Zhuo's attainments in the Righteous Path were profound.

For every individual cultivator, he had a corresponding negotiation strategy.

Seeing Ning Zhuo's expression, Tao Liweng grew even angrier and blurted out, "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, your ambition to claim Flowing Cloud Peak is tantamount to a fantasy. Let me kindly remind you of something - you seem to have forgotten that although the Nanming Fire Furnace now belongs to you, it still hasn't been successfully repaired."

Tao Liweng held up two fingers. "Twenty percent. There is still a twenty percent gap before the Nanming Fire Furnace is completely repaired. For you personally, that twenty percent is like an uncrossable chasm."

Ning Zhuo burst into laughter. The confidence he exuded was like a glaring light, piercing Tao Liweng's eyes.

"That is but a trivial matter! It might stump the mediocre, but how could it possibly stump me?" Ning Zhuo stated dismissively.

He immediately added, "Not long ago, the current head of the Artifact Refinement Hall, Tie Kuang, proactively paid me a visit. He offered to pay out of his own pocket and mobilize the resources of the Artifact Refinement Hall to help me completely repair the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"I wouldn't lie to you. If Dao Friend Tao doesn't believe me, you can go and personally ask Lord Tie Kuang once you step out of this door."

Tao Liweng was stunned by this bombshell of information.

Then, Ning Zhuo shook his head again, revealing a disdainful expression. "But I refused. Lord Tie Kuang actually demanded a twenty percent ownership stake in the furnace. His asking price was too high, and he vastly underestimated my abilities."

"Under the premise of repairing the furnace, I plan to make a public announcement to attract other cultivators. I will let them advance the funds upfront, making them a new round of creditors."

"This way, I will have enough capital to completely repair the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"If Dao Friend Tao doesn't believe me, do you also doubt the appeal of the Nanming Fire Furnace?"

Tao Liweng's brows furrowed even deeper, and the displeasure in his heart grew.

If possible, he dearly wished he were Ning Zhuo’s sole creditor; that way, he could manipulate the boy as he pleased. But right now, there were far more creditors than just him alone, including five Nascent Soul-level cultivators, none of whom he could possibly contend with.

And now, Ning Zhuo wanted to take on even more new debt. This was simply... preposterous!

Tao Liweng felt deeply irritated.

But there was nothing he could say.

Bringing in more creditors would obviously make the situation even more complicated, yet he was powerless to stop it. He could only watch helplessly as Ning Zhuo carried out his plans.

If these new creditors lend him more funds and hold higher status than me, wouldn't I be pushed even further down the line?

Perhaps... I should lend Ning Zhuo some more resources myself?

As soon as the thought surfaced, he instantly snuffed it out.

Back during the minor trial for the Nanming Fire Furnace, Tao Liweng had been overly protective of his savings. After contributing during the first round, he had received another opportunity to provide funding that very night, but he proactively gave it up.

He had done so because he recognized the situation clearly, feeling that he stood no chance of competing against those Nascent Soul-level cultivators.

He simply lacked the confidence to participate any further.

There's still a massive twenty percent gap left for the Nanming Fire Furnace. Even if I emptied my entire life's savings, it wouldn't even cover half a percent.

Therefore, the new creditors will definitely step right over my head. Damn it...

Actually, even if I did have that much wealth, there's no way I would invest it in Ning Zhuo again. Wouldn't that just make me a massive sucker?

Tao Liweng fell into silence for a moment.

Seeing him go silent, a sharp gleam flashed briefly in the depths of Ning Zhuo's eyes.

Tao Liweng isn't skilled in Artifact Refinement, so he was easily bluffed. He has no idea that the difficulty of repairing the final twenty percent is astronomically high.

Ning Zhuo had deduced this from his intelligence gathering on Tao Liweng.

Tao Liweng spent the vast majority of his time cultivating his Core Embracing Immortal Ascension Art.

His profound foundation of pill qi and outstanding attainments in alchemy were mostly byproducts of this main cultivation art.

As for any other cultivation arts, Tao Liweng hadn't dabbled in them at all.

The main reason for this was that the Core Embracing Immortal Ascension Art was an incredibly superior method. Even though it only cultivated a single dantian, its unique cultivation style ensured that Tao Liweng had no weaknesses in his physical body, soul, and other aspects.

However, it was precisely because it advanced across the board, requiring him to accumulate everything simultaneously, that his cultivation progress had always been exceedingly slow.

Only by pairing it with a high-quality alchemy furnace could he mitigate the negative impact of this incredibly slow cultivation speed.

This was exactly why Tao Liweng coveted the Nanming Fire Furnace so much.

Seeing Tao Liweng remain silent, Ning Zhuo reiterated, "Therefore, Dao Friend Tao, the method I just proposed is actually the best path forward."

"As long as you join us and wait until the furnace is successfully repaired, you will be able to use it to assist your cultivation ahead of the others. Using this method to offset my debt to you - isn't that a win-win for us both?"

"Even those Nascent Soul-level grand cultivators wouldn't be able to object."

"Because you will have joined our ranks. The stronger we become, the more territory we will seize on Flowing Cloud Peak, and the more resources we will obtain."

"And these resources can then be used to pay off my debts. This is beneficial for all the creditors."

Tao Liweng snorted coldly, "Ning Zhuo, you may be young, but your heart is incredibly black. Not only are you plotting for my assets, but you're also plotting to reel me in as well! Do you really think I'd fall for your wicked scheme?"

Ning Zhuo's gaze instantly turned icy. "Tao Liweng! You truly measure the heart of a gentleman with the yardstick of a scoundrel. What assets of yours am I plotting for? If I were truly plotting against you, I could just refuse to acknowledge your debt altogether. What could you possibly do about it?"

"Plotting to reel you in? Hmph, you think far too highly of yourself."

"First of all, you can't even beat me. Second, I am acting out of goodwill, bringing you along so we can strike it rich together. I will admit, I want to use a clever method to clear the debt I owe you. But this is a mutually beneficial arrangement!"

"To tell you the truth, there is already a Nascent Soul-level grand cultivator who has approved my plan and will be joining us. Together, we will establish our foundation on Flowing Cloud Peak!"

Tao Liweng had just opened his mouth to refute Ning Zhuo's claim that 'you can't even beat me' when he was completely dizzy by the heavyweight news that followed.

Tao Liweng's mind was momentarily thrown into chaos before he subconsciously began to speculate.

There are a total of five Nascent Soul cultivators involved. Who could it be?

Zhong Limei? No, it couldn't be him. He is an elder of Danxia Peak and the former head of the Alchemy Hall.

Chunyangzi? How is that possible? He's the current leader of Chunyang Palace. Why would he partner up with a Foundation Establishment cultivator?

Cang Yazi? Even more impossible! This man is an elder of Myriad Beast Peak, another one of the main peaks, but he is notoriously eccentric and prefers the company of beasts. Why would he suddenly change his temperament and fight alongside Ning Zhuo for Flowing Cloud Peak?

The Red-Robed Guest? That Demonic Cultivator kills without blinking an eye. He might seem like he's turned over a new leaf, but that's far from the truth. If Ning Zhuo is collaborating with him, it's like asking a tiger for its skin. Isn't he afraid of getting himself killed?

The final option is the Nine Fire Dragon Lord. This Demon Cultivator possesses a true dragon body and is incredibly arrogant. Would he actually stoop so low as to ally himself with a Foundation Establishment cultivator?

Having gone through the list and finding all of them unlikely, Tao Liweng subconsciously asked, "Who is this Nascent Soul grand cultivator?"

Ning Zhuo shook his head, sporting a smile that made Tao Liweng want to punch him. "I can't say just yet. As you know, Nascent Soul-level cultivators are important figures. Every move they make has far-reaching consequences. This matter is still operating in secret and will only be announced when absolutely necessary."

"With a Nascent Soul cultivator as our leader and my Nanming Fire Furnace as our banner, recruiting followers will be a breeze!"

"When the time comes to enter Flowing Cloud Peak, even if we only manage to occupy a small section, we'll gain incredibly abundant resources. We will use those to fuel our expansion. The more territory we take, the more resources we will reap. As we snowball our power, the massive debt I owe will be paid off in no time!"

As Ning Zhuo spoke, his eyes shone with anticipation for the future.

Hearing his words and seeing the expression on his face, Tao Liweng's heart ran completely cold.

Damn it!

Is this Ning Zhuo really so naive? Could he have been utterly duped by that Nascent Soul cultivator?

Looking at it from another angle, even though that Nascent Soul cultivator failed to win the Nanming Fire Furnace, by tying Ning Zhuo to his war chariot, he's put himself in the perfect position to reap the benefits! How cunning.

I cannot just sit back and watch this happen.

Tao Liweng was highly pessimistic about Ning Zhuo's plan. Even as an independent cultivator, he knew that Flowing Cloud Peak was the most disputed, fiercely contested, and chaotic territory within the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

If Ning Zhuo wanted to plunge headlong into it alone, Tao Liweng wouldn't have cared in the slightest.

But the thought of Ning Zhuo plunging into that vortex with the Nanming Fire Furnace made Tao Liweng incredibly anxious and deeply uneasy.

Please, you're a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator! How can you even think about laying your hands on one of the eight main peaks?

Don't screw around like this!
