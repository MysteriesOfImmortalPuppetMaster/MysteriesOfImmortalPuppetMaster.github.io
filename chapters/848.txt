Chapter 848: Exposed?

Spiritual energy circulated as Wang Yu's divine sense flooded into Qin De's soul like a tidal wave.

Qin De was in agonizing pain, but he gritted his teeth, refusing to let out a scream.

Wang Yu turned a deaf ear to his plight, focusing solely on viewing his memories.

A frail child studied hard under the dim yellow light of an oil lamp. His mother sat nearby, using the faint light to mend clothes, occasionally looking up at him with eyes full of expectation.

"De'er, study hard. When you are useful in the future, you won't have to toil like your mother."

Although the young Qin De was still ignorant of the world, he nodded vigorously, rubbed his sleepy eyes, and lowered his head to continue reciting.

-----

Mother Qin moved for the third time.

Carrying humble luggage on her back and holding young Qin De's little hand, she walked through street after street, alley after alley.

"Mother, why are we moving?"

"Because this place isn't good. Mother wants to find a good place for you to study."

Only after this move did Qin De's life finally settle down. Not far from their dwelling was the academy. Mother Qin eked out a living here by washing and mending clothes for others.

-----

In the academy, he was surrounded by several older students.

"A fatherless bastard like you is fit to read the books of the sages?"

"Get lost, don't dirty our place!"

He gritted his teeth and endured it.

He firmly remembered his mother's words: "De'er, you must learn to endure. Bear it, and it will pass. We have no backing; we can't afford to provoke them."

But endurance only brought insatiable demands and escalated bullying.

One time, the few people making things difficult for him brought up Qin De's mother.

"Your mother is just a washerwoman, what makes her think she can let you study? The money was probably stolen!"

"You are not allowed to slander my mother!" Qin De was beyond furious.

"Oh? You actually dare to talk back?!" The few of them suddenly became interested and surrounded him. "We'll talk about your mother, so what! What are you going to do about it?"

"Ahhhh!" Qin De's anger soared to the heavens. Unable to bear it any longer, his mind went blank.

By the time he came to his senses, he was already straddling that classmate, smashing his fists down one after another. He couldn't hear the screams and shouts around him at all. All he could hear was his own heartbeat - thump, thump, thump.

He was given a major demerit.

His mother traded all their savings for a basket of eggs and secretly delivered it to the teacher's door.

Qin De deeply understood a truth: how important strength was! Whether it was combat power, knowledge, reputation, or wealth, they were all important, far too important!

Scene after scene continuously flashed through Wang Yu's divine sense.

Qin De's childhood poverty, his teenage drive, his youthful diligence, his middle-aged setbacks... every scene was incredibly clear.

He saw how Qin De gradually began to show his brilliance, how his teachers placed high hopes on him, and how he burned the midnight oil studying hard on countless nights.

He saw the moment Qin De finished writing the Sage's Great Thief Scripture - what kind of ecstasy was that? Alone in a quiet room, holding the newly finished manuscript, his hands trembling, tears filling his eyes, believing it to be the truth of Confucian cultivators and the guiding principle for the future.

He saw Qin De's nervousness and excitement during his first practical attempt at thievery. It was a wealthy family's storehouse; he lurked in the shadows, his palms sweating, his heart beating like a drum. When he successfully pulled it off, an unprecedented thrill surged through his entire body.

"I succeeded... I succeeded..."

"My theory is not wrong!"

He also saw the cultivators of the Demon Subjugation Hall break down the door. Qin De instinctively resisted but was ultimately pinned to the ground, his hands bound behind his back, shackled in iron. He struggled, he roared, he begged - but it was all useless.

He then saw the situation on the day of the trial.

"Qin De, for authoring heretical books and committing thievery, your crimes are heinous! You are sentenced to life imprisonment and locked in the Cloud Prison!"

Qin De struggled desperately, wanting to argue back, but helplessly his body and soul were restricted, preventing him from making even the slightest sound.

His anxious gaze swept over the audience below.

Duanmu Zhang's face was full of regret. Among the other Confucian cultivators, some were angry, some were disdainful, some were regretful, and some were indifferent.

But not a single person stood up to speak for him.

-----

Deep in the Cloud Prison, Qin De plunged into endless loneliness.

In the first few years, people still came to debate scriptures with him. Duanmu Zhang had come, Chu Xuangui had come, and Song Taosheng had come. Every time, he went all out.

He wanted to prove that his own way was the correct one.

"I have my reasons."

"If it was unreasonable, why could the Sage's Great Thief Scripture be successfully cultivated!"

And it was precisely because he had successfully cultivated it that the Confucian cultivators hated him.

Every time they debated scriptures, he won.

After every victory, he would review, reflect, deduce, and improve. The questions brought by those Confucian cultivators became his best nourishment.

Gradually, no one came anymore.

He was left alone in the prison cell, day after day, year after year.

The boundless, barren silence was enough to drive the vast majority of people mad.

But he did not go mad.

He used every spare moment to recall, ponder, and deduce. The classics he recited in his youth, the Cultivation Arts he comprehended as a young man, and all the ancient texts he had read and memorized in his middle age (naturally including Demonic Cultivator arts) - all repeatedly circulated in his mind.

One year, two years... five years, ten years... twenty years, thirty years...

Through countless improvements, refinements, and deductions, the Sage's Great Thief Scripture reached entirely new heights.

Then, Zhao Hansheng came.

Wang Yu then saw the entire process of the scripture debate between Qin De and Zhao Hansheng. Qin De clearly had the strength, but he intentionally showed weakness in the early stages to fish out the essence of the Mind School. Treating it like a precious treasure, he immediately used it to perfect his Sage's Great Thief Scripture. Zhao Hansheng had been full of confidence, wanting to seize the final victory, but ended up fleeing in a sorry state, leaving people to sigh.

The first soul search ended.

Wang Yu withdrew his palm and let out a light breath.

Qin De collapsed to the ground, his eyes rolled back, drool trailing from the corner of his mouth, his whole body twitching incessantly. The severe pain of the soul search made him nearly faint, but he held his breath, forcibly maintaining his consciousness.

Wang Yu looked down at him with a somewhat complex gaze.

"He is indeed a talent," he said deep in his heart. "What a pity."

In Wang Yu's view, if a person like Qin De had been born into a major clan, with a background and a backer, how would it ever have come to this?

Qin De authoring the Sage's Great Thief Scripture, although contrary to orthodox Confucian cultivators, was not a crime deserving death. If he were the descendant or junior of higher-ups, they would naturally find ways to maneuver it - serving penal labor, compensating for a settlement, reducing the sentence... there would always be a way.

But Qin De had no background.

His only foundation was the Confucian cultivator group. And the Sage's Great Thief Scripture was exactly what made him mortal enemies with this group.

Duanmu Zhang initially hoped he would "abandon evil and return to the righteous path," but after repeatedly failing in scripture debates, he finally recognized reality. Confucian cultivators could not afford not to develop; Duanmu Zhang leaving the Huazhang Kingdom and coming to the Myriad Manifestations Sect came with a mission. Qin De, this stumbling block, had to be removed.

But just as the Confucian cultivator group planned to eradicate Qin De, the higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect stepped in.

They discovered Qin De's utility.

A "stone" that could suppress the Confucian cultivator group - why remove it?

Thus, Qin De was imprisoned for decades, becoming a boulder that suppressed the development of the Confucian cultivators.

Wang Yu gently shook his head.

"What a pity..." he repeated again in his heart.

He reached into his sleeve, took out a Medicinal Pill, and leaned down to place it into Qin De's mouth.

The Medicinal Pill melted in his mouth, and a gentle, nourishing medicinal power spread out, nurturing Qin De's traumatized soul. Qin De's twitching quickly reduced, and his breathing became steady.

Qin De was a Golden Core-level cultivator; naturally, a pill that could take effect on him had to be at least a Golden Core-level Medicinal Pill.

And to allow him to recover so quickly, this type of Medicinal Pill had to be of premium quality.

During Wang Yu's first soul search, he couldn't help but feel a cherishing intent toward Qin De's talent, so he had taken it out.

Wang Yu waited patiently. When the medicinal effects had mostly taken hold, he raised his hand again and suspended it over Qin De's head.

The second soul search!

Qin De's memories were vast and complex, like a book without an index.

Wang Yu's first soul search had scanned the general outline, noting what contents were where. It was akin to creating a table of contents for this book, but he hadn't had the energy to carefully study the aspects related to the Sage's Great Thief Scripture.

Furthermore, Wang Yu's main Cultivation Art had nothing to do with the soul; he was not adept in this area.

Wang Yu's second soul search was to conduct a selective, in-depth investigation based on that mental table of contents.

The focus, naturally, was the memories related to the Sage's Great Thief Scripture.

Thus, he saw how Qin De dismantled Confucian classics one by one, how he gained experience from the outside world and compiled it himself, how he reinterpreted the words of the sages, and how he constructed an entirely new theoretical system.

The processes of those deductions were logically self-consistent and could be described as absolutely exquisite.

Wang Yu continued probing and saw every single improvement Qin De had made to the Sage's Great Thief Scripture.

He saw how Qin De reviewed, reflected, and deduced after every scripture debate. The questions brought by those Confucian cultivators became his motivation to improve his Cultivation Art. Every victory made the Sage's Great Thief Scripture even more perfect.

Scripture debates had always been a path to increasing strength.

The second soul search lasted for a shorter duration and ended very quickly.

Qin De lay paralyzed on the ground, the sweat from his entire body soaking the nearby floor tiles.

His face was deathly pale, his entire body freezing cold, and he was almost completely motionless - he was in a far more wretched state than after the first soul search.

The injuries that had mostly been healed by the Medicinal Pill earlier were now even worse.

Wang Yu looked down at Qin De on the ground, a much heavier feeling of pity welling up in his heart.

Qin De's talent far exceeded his expectations.

Leaving such a talent to rot in the Cloud Prison... perhaps, it was a waste?

Wang Yu once again took out a premium Golden Core-level Medicinal Pill from his sleeve and fed it to Qin De.

While waiting for the medicinal properties to take effect, thoughts continuously swirled in Wang Yu's divine sea.

"This person... isn't just a talent, he's a great talent," he thought inwardly. "In creating and comprehending Cultivation Arts, he is far stronger than me."

"The Sage's Great Thief Scripture is very likely a mid-grade Cultivation Art, perhaps even already possessing a trace of the grandeur of a high-grade Cultivation Art. In the future, it might not be impossible!"

"It's a pity he can't cultivate it himself... but he could choose a junior to inherit it."

"Whether it's Qin De or the Sage's Great Thief Scripture, neither should be shelved like this. There should be a better plan."

Wang Yu thought deeper and deeper.

The Myriad Manifestations Sect embraced all rivers; who couldn't they absorb? Demonic Cultivators, Demon Cultivators, Ghost Cultivators - as long as they were useful, they could be taken into the sect. Although Qin De was guilty, if he could be secretly subdued and secretly nurtured, it wouldn't be impossible.

"Of course, the implications of this matter are severe."

His gaze as he looked at Qin De became profound.

The Sage's Great Thief Scripture and orthodox Confucian cultivators were already mortal enemies. If the Myriad Manifestations Sect secretly nurtured Qin De's inheritor, once exposed, they would definitely forge a great enmity with the Huazhang Kingdom and all Confucian cultivators in the world.

"The Sect Master might also be hesitating..."

Wang Yu was naturally hesitating as well.

If this were done, the impact would be too huge. But the more dangerous something was, the greater its value usually was.

"If someone successfully cultivates the Sage's Great Thief Scripture, who knows what kind of power it would hold!"

Qin De lay on the ground, unmoving.

His consciousness was sometimes clear, sometimes blurry. Two consecutive soul searches had already brought his soul to the verge of collapse. If not for Wang Yu's Medicinal Pill sustaining him, his soul would have dispersed long ago.

Fear was accumulating in the depths of Qin De's heart.

He had fabricated some false memories. The Literary Palace of Confucian cultivators inherently excelled at this path. The bit of spiritual energy he had gained after switching to cultivate the Myriad Laws Demonic Corruption Art further exceeded Wang Yu's expectations. These half-true, half-false memories had successfully deceived the Peak Master of Danxia Peak twice in a row.

But the third time, he was afraid he would be exposed.

Qin De had such a strong premonition in his heart because he also knew that fake was fake. There would always be some flaws or inconsistencies that he personally couldn't anticipate.

If given ample time, he could mend these flaws one by one. But the problem was exactly that he lacked time!

"No, I still have another hope."

"Since that person imparted the Myriad Laws Demonic Corruption Art to me, and I successfully deceived the higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect twice..."

"My actions this time have already shown my true feelings and my stance. If things go wrong again, he should step in to help me!"

Xiao Juxia had been silently watching.

He knew that at this very moment, Qin De was hovering on the edge of life and death.

"If Wang Yu conducts a third soul search, he won't be able to hide it. Time is limited, and his arrangements are still too crude."

The fact that Wang Yu could be deceived was actually, to a large extent, due to the shackles, chains, and the Cloud Prison on Qin De's body.

Wang Yu had never imagined that Qin De could use spiritual energy.

By switching to cultivate the Myriad Laws Demonic Corruption Art, although Qin De hadn't undergone complete demonic transformation, he had already bypassed the seals on his body, gaining a certain amount of space and ability to cast techniques.

"Then will he conduct a third soul search?" Xiao Juxia continued observing with great interest.

The next moment, the corners of his mouth curled up slightly, revealing a mocking smile.

Because Wang Yu once again stretched out his five fingers, suspended them above Qin De, and began the third soul search!

Wang Yu himself had no suspicions.

But he was a smart man, well-versed in the ways of politics. He knew clearly that his personal soul search this time absolutely could not have any omissions.

At the same time, reporting to the others that he had searched Qin De's soul three consecutive times until he was on the verge of death, and could barely hold on even with the consumption of precious pills - such a statement would eliminate any potential critical opinions from others.

It proved that Wang Yu was serious enough and had never glossed things over. It proved that Wang Yu was responsible - responsible to himself, and responsible to the Myriad Manifestations Sect!

If this matter had been handled by Tuoba Huang, the first time would have been enough.

But Wang Yu's way of doing things was like refining pills; he always had to refine a perfectly round Medicinal Pill. He handled matters smoothly and flawlessly, ensuring not a single drop leaked out. He wouldn't make a mistake on such minor details.

Wang Yu launched the third soul search on Qin De!

"It's over!" Qin De was incredibly anxious in his heart, only hoping that Xiao Juxia would come to save him.

Xiao Juxia chuckled, finding it quite amusing.

Even if he and the Myriad Laws Demonic Corruption Art were discovered, so what?

"I'd actually like to see how the luck of the Demonic Path resolves this tribulation!"

To him, Qin De was nothing more than a chess piece.

"Even I myself am just a chess piece for the destiny of the Demonic Path."
