Chapter 961: Cautious of Contamination, Adept at Transformation

Xu Hanshuang, inner sect disciple of the Tianhe Sect. Quiet in temperament, skilled at cultivating spiritual medicine. Entered the formation and obtained the Rain-Gathering Cloud Nature. Later, she managed the cloud fields at Withered Spring Peak, nourishing the veins with the Rain-Gathering Cloud Nature. In ten years, she made the withered peak spring forth water and the barren ridge turn green again. She also improved the [Three Seasons Nourishing Medicine Cloud], doubling the survival rate of low-grade spiritual herbs.

The Rain-Gathering Cloud Nature is good at nourishing, but also at accumulating emotions. After Xu Hanshuang obtained this nature, her mind became softer, her pity for things deepened. Her cloud field flourished greatly, but she herself was full of sorrow. In her later years, she often sat quietly in the rain, saying that clouds can nourish vegetation, but it is hard to nourish the human heart.

……

Ning Zhuo read the first few names and found that they were all figures from a very long time ago. At the same time, he also found that in the recorded information of each cultivator, apart from their personal life story, the latter paragraph belonged to the author of the Jade Slip, who evaluated them from the perspective of a third-party latecomer.

Ning Zhuo continued reading.

Zhou Beixiu, descendant of a cultivator from Heavy Formation Peak. A man of few words in his youth, with a meticulous mind. Entered the formation and obtained the Hidden Cloud Nature. Later, he created the [Hidden Peak Cloud Formation] himself, using thin clouds to hide the valleys and phantoms to mislead demon soldiers. During the war on the northern front, he relied on three Hidden Cloud Formations to protect sixty thousand migrating mortals.

The Hidden Cloud Nature is good at hiding, concealing, and avoiding disasters. However, those who obtain this nature easily hide their thoughts deep inside. Zhou Beixiu spoke less and less in the latter half of his life, making it difficult for friends to approach him. His Formations grew more refined daily, but his human connections grew thinner daily.

……

Gu Wanzhao, a female cultivator from a branch family of the Gu Clan in the Embroidered Flower Kingdom. Loved bright and beautiful things, unafraid of people's gossip. Entered the formation and obtained the Rosy Cloud Nature. Later, she mastered the 'Evening Glow Rosy Cloud Method', using rosy clouds to warm medicines and reduce the risk of exploding furnaces. Once, after a great fire at Rosy Cloud Peak, she used rosy clouds to protect half a storehouse of medicinal materials, ensuring the spiritual medicine remained unscorched and uncorrupted.

The Rosy Cloud Nature sits between fire and water; it is good at illuminating, warming, and dyeing forms. Those who obtain this nature easily have bright radiance in their hearts, but also easily value reputation. Gu Wanzhao later became famous for a time with her Rosy Cloud Pills, but because she excessively pursued the beauty of the pill's color, she misjudged the medicinal properties three times. Fortunately, she repented in her later years and cultivated Fire Element cultivation methods.

……

He looked at many subsequent names on the list and found that basically all of them were Thunder Cloud Nature, Fire Cloud Nature, Rain Cloud Nature, and so on. The Hidden Cloud Nature was considered relatively rare.

Lu Xuanpu, originally an itinerant cultivator. Solid and heavy in temperament, skilled in fengshui geomancy. Entered the formation and obtained the Heavy Cloud Nature. Later, he used heavy clouds to suppress mountain winds and stabilize the water vapor of spiritual fields. He once helped the Sect open up three new suspended medicine gardens. The Heavy Cloud Nature can bear weight, suppress, store water, and also hinder movement.

The Heavy Cloud Nature is thick; it can bear things, but it can also stagnate the mind. In his later years, Lu Xuanpu disliked flexibility; he was more than sufficient at preserving what he had but lacked the drive to pioneer. A thick and heavy nature, if kept balanced, becomes a mountain peak; if it loses its liveliness, it becomes a stubborn stone.

……

"Heavy Cloud Nature?" Ning Zhuo saw a rare cloud nature.

His divine sense browsed extremely quickly, and Ning Zhuo memorized the rare cloud natures that appeared.

For example, the Gauze Cloud Nature, skilled at weaving cloud qi into layers of gauze patterns. Cultivators who obtained this cloud nature could make clouds no longer just clumped or curtain-like, but turn them into threads, silk, nets, clothes, and tents.

For example, the Mirror Cloud Nature, where the surface of the cloud body is like a water mirror, yet more ethereal than a water mirror. It is not geared toward attacking, but is skilled at reflecting qi mechanisms, bouncing back light and shadows, and revealing the true and false.

The Calamity Cloud Nature carried the aura of calamity. The cloud body is heavy, capable of oppressing the divine soul, drawing out the fears, flaws, and obsessions in a cultivator's heart, absorbing karmic disasters, and transforming into a descending calamity right above one's head.

The Home Cloud Nature could weave homes, allowing cultivators to rest, recuperate, and manage a world, enabling forced teleportation and recall.

"Wandering Cloud Nature? Clouds do not cling to the mountains, water does not cling to the shore. When the wind comes, they move; when the wind stops, they slow. When the Wandering Cloud enters the body, the path of the heart naturally broadens; if there are no attachments, one will eventually fall into drifting. This nature does not value attacking; it values not stagnating. In a trapped situation, being able to take one step often beats forcefully breaking through a hundred moves."

Ning Zhuo's divine sense swept into the depths of the Jade Slip and found another interesting cloud nature.

Lin Qingyuan, outer sect disciple of the Wind Walk Sect. Light and ethereal in temperament, disliking restraint. Entered the formation and obtained the Wandering Cloud Nature. Later, she mastered the "Three-Turn Wandering Cloud Steps", able to change direction three times in a single breath, her figure nimble and agile. She once entered a demon nest three times to scout the way, bringing back topographical maps, and also rescued twelve messenger disciples before the enemy formation closed in.

The Wandering Cloud Nature focuses on flowing, maneuvering, borrowing momentum, and not stagnating. Those who obtain this nature easily master movement techniques, and their mindsets are also broad. Lin Qingyuan was later appointed as a sect deacon but declined three times, spending her life traveling far and wide to various kingdoms. Wandering clouds live when they find a path, but scatter when they lose a place to return. Her movement technique reached grand mastery, but her heart also drifted increasingly far away.

……

Name ■■, descendant of a deacon of the ■■ Hall. The Wandering Cloud Nature entered their pills; the pills were not bound by prescriptions. This person could not bear the restraint of sect rules, disliked the reprimands of elders, disliked comparisons with fellow sect members, and left the Sect to wander the world. The scattered pills they refined were mostly ordinary consumable medicines, but their medicinal properties were flexible, changing according to the person, place, and materials.

Wandering Cloud entering pills means the pills are unbound by prescriptions; the cloud nature entering the heart means the heart is not caged. If this person had remained in the Sect, they might have become a great master of the Pill Hall. However, their ambition was not in the hall. Clouds do not cling to mountains, and people are not meant to be caged. What a pity, what a pity.

……

There were quite a few other interesting cloud natures.

For example, the Slumbering Cloud Nature, gentle and quiet, perfectly suited for healing, hibernation, and sealing.

The Mirage Cloud Nature, able to project false illusions using real qi mechanisms, mixing truth and falsehood, making them hard to distinguish.

The Medicine Cloud Nature, able to absorb medicinal qi yet not easily polluted by medicinal toxins, highly valued by alchemists and medical cultivators.

Ning Zhuo also saw that someone had obtained the White Cloud Nature.

Jingxi, an abandoned disciple of the Xiaohan Temple. Constantly giggled, mentally deficient. This person laughed when seeing people, laughed when seeing flowers bloom, and even laughed when seeing tattered cotton. When he entered the Myriad Phenomena Cloud Attracting Formation, the various cloud natures came and went erratically, none willing to stay for long, except for the White Cloud Nature, which hung above his head, expanding and shrinking with his laughter. Jingxi reached out to touch it, and the White Cloud Nature turned into a ball of white qi and sank into his glabella. Later, he cultivated the "Laughing Cloud Heart-Nourishing Method", able to soothe terrified divine souls with white clouds. He once made dozens of low-grade cultivators, whose heart demons were just beginning to arise, fall into a peaceful sleep.

The White Cloud Nature is empty and pure, close to the innocent heart of the Buddhist Sect. After Jingxi obtained this nature, his foolish laughter did not decrease, but gained an extra layer of benevolence. This person did not understand Buddhist logic, yet was close to the Buddha's heart. White clouds entering laughter can wash away terror. Foolishness, if devoid of the intent to harm others, is also a kind of purity.

……

A'Ci, born a mortal, later a female worker at the Cloud Weaving Workshop. A high fever in her childhood damaged her spirit, leaving her mind permanently like that of a ten-year-old child. She loved washing white cloth and folding cloud velvet the most. Because her hands and feet were clean, she was brought into the Cloud Weaving Workshop to do odd jobs. Entering the formation by chance, the White Cloud Nature felt close to her and lay on her sleeve, refusing to leave. Later, A'Ci could not weave complex cloud silk, but she could wash away turbid clouds and dust clouds, returning them to their white and pure state. Once, after a pollution of turbid fiendish qi, she salvaged over three hundred bolts of cloud velvet.

The White Cloud Nature is easily dyed, and also most needs washing. A'Ci's mind was simple, only recognizing the word "clean," which instead became her specialty. After obtaining this nature, she could not fight, but could use white clouds to cleanse turbidity, washing away superficial fiendish qi, soot, and medicinal residue.

……

Ning Zhuo discovered that very few people obtained the White Cloud Nature, roughly the same number as those with the Wandering Cloud Nature.

He also found that the cultivators who obtained the White Cloud Nature were either foolish or eminent figures who had seen through the mundane world and severed their earthly ties.

"No wonder Senior Yun Sanggu was surprised when she saw that I got the White Cloud Nature."

Ning Zhuo, however, felt a sudden realization in his heart: "I excel at Buddhist Sect cultivation methods and practice the Mirror Platform Spirit Communication Manual, which is the fastest advancing path among the Three Sects' Supreme Methods. Obtaining the White Cloud Nature shows that I have Buddhist Affinity!"

Yun Sanggu's voice drifted over at this moment: "Your information will also be recorded in it, but key details like your name will be obscured."

"Cloud natures are precious and often attract covetous eyes and malicious attacks. Therefore, this special reward has always been carried out in secret."

"I also advise you to keep this matter a secret from now on."

Ning Zhuo nodded. He had just seen that some information in the Jade Slip was obscured by ■■, and he figured his own information would be treated the same. Only dispensable, unimportant information would be revealed.

Ning Zhuo finished reading, withdrew his divine sense, and fell into silence as he looked at the gentle Jade Slip in his hand.

He understood Yun Sanggu's intention in showing him the Jade Slip, and because of this, he gained a deeper understanding of cloud natures.

A cloud nature is not a magic treasure that can directly enhance combat power. It is more like a seed, a mirror, a path that grows along with the cultivator.

The Thunder Cloud Nature made Wen Jinglei, but also amplified his fierce rigidity.

The Rain-Gathering Cloud Nature made Xu Hanshuang, but also deepened her tendency to overthink.

The Hidden Cloud Nature saved mortals, but also made Zhou Beixiu hide his thoughts deep inside.

The Rosy Cloud Nature could protect medicinal materials, but it could also tempt people to chase superficial brilliance.

Yun Sanggu asked, "Finished reading?"

Ning Zhuo respectfully handed the Jade Slip back: "Finished. Thank you, Senior."

Yun Sanggu took the Jade Slip and sighed, "Now you should understand why I said it's hard to teach you."

Ning Zhuo nodded: "The White Cloud Nature has no fixed direction. It seems ordinary, but in reality, it is extremely special. If this junior merely copies past examples, it would instead cause the white cloud to be fixed too early."

A trace of approval appeared in Yun Sanggu's eyes: "Correct. Regarding this, I only have four words of advice for you - be cautious of contamination, excel at transformation."

Ning Zhuo's gaze slightly focused.

Yun Sanggu said slowly: "To be cautious of contamination means not letting it be completely dyed by your momentary mood, a single environment, or one type of spiritual energy. You possess water and fire, you have Mechanisms, as well as pill and Formation paths. In the future, you will also encounter smoke fiends, thunder, earth and wood, Yin-Yang, and all sorts of things. The White Cloud Nature will learn from you, and it will also be contaminated. You must always pay attention to what it has been dyed with."

"To excel at transformation means not being afraid of it changing. If the White Cloud Nature is forever blank, it is merely blank. Its preciousness lies not in never being dyed, but in being able to be washed after being dyed, being able to turn after being set, and being able to return after deviating."

Ning Zhuo was greatly shaken upon hearing this.

Cautious of contamination, excel at transformation.

These words almost completely laid out the fundamental essence of the White Cloud Nature.

Ning Zhuo said earnestly: "This junior has memorized them!"

Yun Sanggu looked at him and suddenly said softly: "Past generations of those who obtained natures were mostly led astray by the cloud nature they acquired. Some achieved greater heights, while others fell deeper into paranoia. You obtaining the White Cloud Nature makes it hard to judge whether it is a blessing or a curse. You need to be even more cautious in your cultivation."

Saying this, she waved her hand: "Go on."

Ning Zhuo nodded, gathered his thoughts, bowed solemnly, and only then turned to leave the small courtyard.

When he walked out of the cloud corridor, the outside twilight was just slanting down.

Ning Zhuo comprehended that burst of cloud nature within his Divine Sea Dantian and couldn't help but silently recite in his heart: "Cautious of contamination, excel at transformation."

He faintly had a feeling that the reminder of these words would be extremely important for his subsequent cultivation!

The second match of the five-win martial contest was set in the Abundant Earth Martial Arena.

For this match, the Earth Expansion Alliance was the first to send someone out. According to the contract stipulations, the Earth Expansion Alliance also had the right to select the venue first.

The Abundant Earth Martial Arena was entirely different from the Clear Sky Martial Arena.

Here, there was no endless azure sky, no floating cloud stairs, nor was there a Formation sun hanging high in the heavens.

Inside the arena was a broad loess plain, surrounded on all four sides by a ring of low mountain walls. The mountain walls were covered with earth-yellow spiritual runic patterns. As the spiritual patterns flickered, the entire martial arena looked like an earthen bowl buried deep within the belly of a mountain, trapping the competing cultivators inside.

The ground was not flat.

There were rift valleys, dirt mounds, stone pillars, and sunken mud pits. From every crack in the ground, heavy Earth Qi was slowly spewing out, like the earth itself breathing in a low murmur.

The moment a cultivator entered this place, they would feel their shoulders sink slightly, as if their internal spiritual energy was being quietly drawn away by the Abundant Earth.

The three parties - Naming Stronghold, the Earth Expansion Alliance, and White Cloud Township - still occupied separate seating areas to watch the battle.

After their defeat in the first match, the atmosphere in Naming Stronghold's seating area was no longer relaxed. The Nine Fires Dragon Lord had not arrived. The Red-Robed Guest sat in the back, his gaze indifferent. Tan Zhu half-closed his eyes, his expression gloomy.

Tu Yuanzi, on the other hand, stared at the yellow soil in the arena with a somewhat complicated look in his eyes, thoughts rising in his heart: "This martial arena is actually quite suitable for me."

Ning Zhuo sat slightly behind Chunyangzi.

It was not surprising that the Earth Expansion Alliance chose this arena.

The representative stepping out to fight was precisely an Earth Element cultivator.

Standing here, he was simply like a fish in water. The Earth Qi, earth veins, mountain walls, and rift valleys could all be leveraged.

Ning Zhuo had obtained basic intelligence and knew this person's background.

This person was named True Monarch Shi Heng. He was a Nascent Soul cultivator within the Earth Expansion Alliance, highly skilled at defensive and attritional battles.

He was burly, with broad shoulders and a thick back. The ravines on his face were deep, looking like yellow stone cracked by mountain winds. He wore an earth-yellow short robe with extremely narrow cuffs, a large axe hanging from his waist, and a yellow triangular flag stuck in his back.

He silently stepped into the martial arena. His slow steps reminded people of the clumsiness and endurance of earth and stone.

After standing still, he looked up at the Naming Stronghold seating area, his voice thick and resonant: "For the Earth Expansion Alliance's second match, Shi Heng seeks guidance from the brilliant techniques of Naming Stronghold."

In the Naming Stronghold seating area, Chunyangzi slowly stood up.

For this battle, Chunyangzi would personally take action!

The defeat of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord caused him to endure tremendous pressure. Therefore, after receiving Ning Zhuo's reply and holding a discussion, he proactively shouldered the responsibility of the second match.

Chunyangzi walked into the martial arena, his pace unhurried.

As soon as the Earth Qi dispersing everywhere in the Abundant Earth Martial Arena approached his feet, it was illuminated and dispersed by a faint layer of golden light.

A jade chime rang out.

The second battle, begin!

True Monarch Shi Heng was the first to act.

He pulled out the large axe at his waist. The axe was grayish-black, its edge broad and thick, looking like a sharpened mountain rock.

Held in his hand, the large axe did not appear sharp, but rather carried a heavy momentum capable of smashing mountains and severing ridges.

He stomped one foot on the ground.

Boom!

The ground cracked open with three long fissures. Three streaks of earth-yellow light erupted from the fissures, transforming into three earthen walls that surged toward Chunyangzi to enclose him.

At the same time, the yellow flag on his back flew up on its own. With a whoosh, the flag unfurled, and the yellow soil in the arena immediately began to churn. Countless grains of mud and sand rolled into giant hands, grabbing at Chunyangzi from all directions.

His methods were quite unpretentious and straightforward.

Chunyangzi flicked his sleeve, and a thread of golden light instantly flew out from it.

The golden light was initially as thin as a hair, but after leaving the sleeve, it grew against the wind, transforming into a three-foot golden gleam.

It did not take a tricky angle or follow a curved path; it simply slashed straight forward.

The first earthen wall was easily cleft open.

When the golden light fell, there was no loud explosive sound, only a clear and melodious chime, like the light strike of a golden bell. The earthen wall split down the middle. The cut surface was illuminated by the pure yang qi, glowing faintly. The Earth Element spiritual energy rapidly dissipated, like snow meeting the sun.

The second earthen wall immediately pressed in, followed closely by the third.

Both were broken open by the three-foot golden gleam!

Chunyangzi formed an incantation seal with his left hand, and a tiny golden sun floated out from his palm.

The golden sun was only the size of a cup's mouth, but its light was blindingly bright.

It hovered in front of Chunyangzi's palm, gently vibrating, and then shot out seven golden threads.

The golden threads pierced through the giant mud and sand hands. All the mud hands froze in mid-air, then immediately began to collapse from the centers of their palms, turning into dry yellow soil that rustled to the ground.

Seeing this, Old Man Youyun sighed: "For this person to be able to lead the Chunyang Palace, he truly is not just for show."
