Chapter 846: The Fourth Imperial Dynasty, Unlike Any Before!

"Song Taosheng?" Gu Qing had a rather deep impression of Song Taosheng. "To think he would be the first person to leave."

Zhao Hansheng said, "As the saying goes, a good bird chooses the right tree to nest in. The suppression of Confucian cultivators within the gates of the Myriad Manifestations Sect is simply too severe. Song Taosheng's choice to leave can certainly be considered a wise move."

Gu Qing sighed deeply. "Now that one person has taken the lead, I'm afraid this situation will occur many more times. Old Mister Duanmu Zhang is truly magnanimous; instead of forcing him to stay, he secretly made arrangements for Song Taosheng to pave the way for him."

"Teacher, can Old Mister Duanmu Zhang hold out for three years?"

Zhao Hansheng looked back in the direction of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, his gaze deep and his tone resolute. "Of course he can!"

"In that case, we are in no rush to return to our country just yet."

"Let's stay in this town for now and talk again after we get in touch with Song Taosheng."

In his communication, Duanmu Zhang had detailed Song Taosheng's abilities and his past contributions to the Confucian community.

He hoped that Zhao Hansheng could take Song Taosheng back to the Huazhang Kingdom to continue his advanced studies.

Deep down, Zhao Hansheng was very welcoming of this.

Because Duanmu Zhang's attitude towards Song Taosheng was not that toward a traitor, Zhao Hansheng immediately understood his underlying intention.

This was not only Duanmu Zhang's way of "parting on good terms," but also a source of strength he was actively gifting to Zhao Hansheng.

For Song Taosheng to stand out within the Myriad Manifestations Sect, his personal strength was certainly sufficient; he was an expert among Golden Core cultivators. If Zhao Hansheng provided him with backing, then his strength would also be used by Zhao Hansheng.

This would be of no small help to Zhao Hansheng in enduring the difficult times ahead after he switched to Wang Xinyue's tutelage.

More importantly, if Song Taosheng developed well, he would become a "bridge" of communication between Duanmu Zhang and Zhao Hansheng!

The cooperation between these two great Confucian scholars had actually become closer after suffering Qin De's blow, gradually showing signs of shifting into an "alliance."

As for Song Taosheng's own willingness...

Neither Zhao Hansheng nor Duanmu Zhang felt this would be an issue.

The Huazhang Kingdom!

This was the holy land that countless Confucian cultivators yearned for.

Song Taosheng would be no exception.

The teacher and student did not rush to depart. Instead, they returned to the posthouse and used the teleportation formation there to send a flying message, striving to contact Song Taosheng as soon as possible.

After the message was sent, Gu Qing caught on, understanding the profound intention behind Duanmu Zhang's recommendation of Song Taosheng and arranging him by Zhao Hansheng's side.

Gu Qing sighed in admiration, "Old Mister Duanmu Zhang is indeed a great Confucian scholar. Such an arrangement carries deep meaning."

"Alas, it's truly a pity."

"With such ability, he nonetheless gave up the chance to enter officialdom in the Flying Cloud Kingdom back then, choosing instead to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect. If he had chosen differently, today's situation would likely be vastly different."

Many years ago, under the leadership of the current monarch, the Huazhang Kingdom took the initiative to dispatch outstanding Confucian cultivators to various countries to spread Confucianism and educate the masses. Some succeeded, such as in the Qingping Kingdom, which had now changed its course to govern the country with Confucianism, even going so far as to revere only Confucian techniques.

Of course, some failed, their bodies dying and their Dao vanishing.

"Old Mister Duanmu Zhang painstakingly operated within the Myriad Manifestations Sect for so many years, yet because of one Qin De, he was suppressed to the point of never being able to raise his head. His luck is truly too terrible."

Zhao Hansheng shook his head slightly. "If I were Duanmu Zhang, I'm afraid I would have made the same choice back then."

"Why is that?" Gu Qing hastily asked for guidance.

Zhao Hansheng patiently explained, "Why does the monarch try every possible means to spread Confucianism with such great effort, persisting from the very beginning until now?"

"It is to strengthen the destiny of our Confucian school!"

"The more people cultivate Confucianism, and the more talented those people are, the more robust the Confucian destiny becomes."

"Conversely, if these outstanding cultivators were to cultivate the Demonic Path, the Demon Path, the Military Strategist path, and so on, it would bolster the destiny of those other paths."

"This is a matter of one rising while the other falls."

This was why Zhao Hansheng wanted to propagate Mind Learning free of charge. He even went so far as to bring out the refinement method for the Dao-Bearing Jade Page.

Aside from helping Gu Qing, producing the Dao-Bearing Jade Page inevitably required the use of Confucian resources. Once a powerhouse or faction wanted to mass-produce it, they would definitely have to seek out Confucian cultivators and cooperate with them. From this perspective, it could elevate the status of Confucian cultivators or attract many people to specialize in Confucianism.

Having accomplished this, Zhao Hansheng would also receive national commendation upon returning to his country.

Zhao Hansheng continued, "Therefore, developing Confucian cultivators and promoting Confucianism in other places is, fundamentally, undermining the foundations of others; it is a direct confrontation against other destinies."

Gu Qing immediately realized, "I understand now, Teacher. If Old Mister Duanmu Zhang had entered officialdom in the Flying Cloud Kingdom back then, he would have faced the national destiny of the entire Flying Cloud Kingdom. That is the destiny of a nation, suppressed by National Artifacts and protected by national arts; the difficulty is extremely high."

"But by joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he only faced the destiny of a Super Sect. Although they have destiny-suppressing spiritual treasures and invisible barriers, it is ultimately easier to deal with than a nation's destiny."

Zhao Hansheng nodded, "That is indeed the case."

"However, he did not anticipate that, even among all the super factions in the world, the Myriad Manifestations Sect is special."

"Its scale is practically the largest, and in terms of destiny-suppressing treasures, even if it isn't the absolute strongest, it is definitely among the top few."

"The fact that Qin De could emerge and create the Sage Great Thief Scripture might very well be the Myriad Manifestations Sect's destiny exerting its influence to suppress the Confucian cultivator group."

Thinking of something else, Zhao Hansheng's gaze flickered slightly. "Perhaps, there's another point... The Myriad Manifestations Sect is very likely the starting point of an era of great contention. Therefore, this sect is quite unique. For Duanmu Zhang to achieve any development, the obstacles he encounters are far greater than they appear on the surface."

Gu Qing's eyes widened. "The start of an era of great contention? Teacher, where does this come from?"

Zhao Hansheng said, "Looking at the world's geography, the Flying Cloud Kingdom itself is located in the central region of the world. This kingdom has almost no natural barriers; the land is fertile and entirely flat. It is a land of endless battles."

"Looking at history, the Flying Cloud Kingdom, as well as the preceding nations in its territory, have always been the first to suffer from the turmoil of the times and undergo regime changes."

"Furthermore, looking at the divinations of destiny, the past mighty sages of the Myriad Manifestations Sect once deduced an extremely important prophetic poem. All along, the Myriad Manifestations Sect has been secretly trying to avoid this prophecy."

Gu Qing asked, "Teacher, exactly what prophetic poem is it?"

Zhao Hansheng took a deep breath, his expression turning solemn as he slowly recited:

Clouds follow Myriad Manifestations, calamity rises from the Eight Peaks,

The living turn to ash, the dead find no end.

The Fourth Imperial Dynasty, unlike past and present,

A new Dao destroys the world, all myriad species vanish without a trace!

Gu Qing stood blankly on the spot, the prophetic poem echoing repeatedly in his mind. A chill rose from his spine and slowly spread throughout his entire body.

"The living turn to ash... the dead find no end..."

The living would be turned to ashes. And the dead would not find a peaceful end.

What kind of scene would that be?

The mortal realm transformed into a sea of fire, plunging all life into suffering. The netherworld reduced to a purgatory, with departing souls wailing. The boundary between life and death completely shattered, and everything ultimately returning to nothingness.

"The Fourth Imperial Dynasty..."

Gu Qing knew what an "Imperial Dynasty" meant.

"Unlike past and present..."

This Imperial Dynasty would be different from the previous three.

Would it be better, or worse?

The end of the prophetic poem provided the answer - A new Dao destroys the world, all myriad species vanish without a trace.

An entirely new path of Dao would destroy the whole world. All living things would become utterly extinct!

"Everything between heaven and earth... all dead and gone?" Gu Qing's heart trembled.

What kind of future was that?

No humans, no beasts, no vegetation, no mountains or rivers. Only endless nothingness and eternal darkness.

"Teacher..." Gu Qing spoke up, his voice somewhat hoarse. "Is this prophetic poem... truly credible?"

Zhao Hansheng turned to look at him, his gaze calm.

Gu Qing continued, "This disciple has heard that prophecies are often inaccurate. Some cultivators pay a massive price to divine the future, only for the result to be pure nonsense; some mighty experts speak with absolute certainty, yet in the end, nothing happens. This prophetic poem..."

He didn't finish his sentence, but his meaning was already obvious.

Zhao Hansheng stayed silent for a moment before slowly saying, "You are right. There have indeed been cases where prophetic poems failed, and not just a few. The future is unpredictable, and no one can truly see it clearly."

He paused, then shifted the topic: "But this one is different."

"This was originally a secret, solemnly disclosed to me by Duanmu Zhang," Zhao Hansheng said. "At first, as your teacher, I too was unwilling to believe it. But Duanmu Zhang's perspective made me agree with him - although there are instances of failed prophecies, based on two points, the likelihood of this prophetic poem coming true is not small."

"First, the mighty expert who deduced this poem died and saw his Dao vanish the very instant the poem was completed. It wasn't an ordinary peaceful passing, nor a standard death, but... a complete erasure. His soul scattered entirely, to the point that even soul-summoning arts couldn't sense him. It was as if everything he was turned into fuel, completely burning away to obtain this prophetic poem."

"Second, the actions of the Myriad Manifestations Sect's higher-ups. Ever since this prophetic poem appeared, the Myriad Manifestations Sect has secretly been trying to avert it. They specifically used their destiny-suppressing heavy treasures to conceal the calamity and delay the arrival of that terrifying future. However, as to what these treasures specifically are or how many there are, Duanmu Zhang was unable to find out; he could only confirm that this operation existed."

Gu Qing lowered his head, falling into silence.

A prophecy exchanged with a mighty expert's life, a calamity that a Super Sect poured all its efforts into suppressing - how could such a prophetic poem not be believed?

Zhao Hansheng looked at him and said gently, "Qing'er, the reason you are unwilling to believe it is because you do not want the future to be so bleak."

Gu Qing looked up.

Zhao Hansheng slowly said, "Alas, this is human nature. Who doesn't want a beautiful and happy life? Who would willingly watch the populace suffer and myriad species go extinct?"

He paused, his voice turning heavy: "But looking at history, after the Three Sovereigns and Five Emperors, which Imperial Dynasty transition did not result in mass suffering and unprecedented tragedies?"

Gu Qing was silent.

He knew his teacher spoke the truth.

Looking back across the history of cultivation, there had already been three great Imperial Dynasties!

The Blood Slaughter Imperial Dynasty, where demons and monsters ran rampant. That was the darkest era for the human race, where the strong fed and the weak were enslaved. Countless cities were massacred, countless living beings devoured. Seas of blood and mountains of corpses piled up into hills.

The Forbidden Imperial Dynasty, where Gods and Buddhas filled the skies. The rules were infinitely strict; every single word and action could potentially violate a taboo. The slightest overstep resulted in arrest, imprisonment, or assassination. The people of the world lived in endless terror.

The Night Rain Imperial Dynasty was even more terrifying. That was a bizarre and uncanny era, where everything exceeded the limits of comprehension. A Nascent Soul cultivator might die from the prick of a seemingly ordinary needle; an Immortal City could turn into a burial ground overnight. You could never predict what would happen in the next second. It was the absolute zenith of fear, an endless nightmare of the unknown.

On the other hand, the Era of the Five Emperors-

An Emperor's Dynasty was one tier lower than an Imperial Dynasty, yet it could revitalize the world and bring about prosperous development. The common people lived in peace and contentment, and all things flourished.

"To be fair," Gu Qing sighed, "if an era of great contention is upon us this time, I would much prefer an Emperor's Dynasty over an Imperial Dynasty."

Zhao Hansheng sighed and said nothing.

Judging purely from the Myriad Manifestations Sect's prophetic poem, the era of great contention would birth the fourth Imperial Dynasty in the history of cultivation.

Moreover, this Imperial Dynasty would be even more terrifying than the previous three!

The living turn to ash, the dead find no end.

A new Dao destroys the world, all myriad species vanish without a trace.

Blood Slaughter was tragic, Forbidden was oppressive, Night Rain was uncanny, while the Fourth Imperial Dynasty was sheer despair.

Complete nothingness, an eternal conclusion.

Between heaven and earth, nothing would remain!

After a long time.

Zhao Hansheng spoke: "Regardless of the prophetic poem, that is ultimately a distant future. The most important thing right now is not to worry about an era of great contention five hundred years from now, but to handle the pressing matters at hand!"

"Three years from now, as your teacher, I will debate scriptures with Qin De again. This time, I will not lose, nor can I afford to lose again!"

Gu Qing nodded heavily: "Teacher, your student will follow closely behind!"

"Moreover, the more people we have, the better."

Zhao Hansheng affirmed with an "mm", "Then let's wait for Song Taosheng to regroup with us."

The flying message had already been sent out through the posthouse's teleportation formation.

Given the current situation, Zhao Hansheng had to fully defect to Wang Xinyue to truly learn the profound mysteries of Mind Learning. Only then would he have the confidence and strength to defeat Qin De in debate and wash away his previous humiliation.

Zhao Hansheng's shift in stance would inevitably cause a rupture in his old relationships, and he would definitely bear immense pressure.

Song Taosheng, this Golden Core-level Confucian cultivator, was no ordinary Golden Core; he possessed powerful Nascent Soul potential. If he was by Zhao Hansheng's side, he could share the pressure to a large extent.

Duanmu Zhang had seen exactly this point, which was why he recommended Song Taosheng - he knew Zhao Hansheng needed him!

Thus, Zhao Hansheng and Gu Qing chose to wait patiently. The teacher and student both felt that after receiving the flying message, Song Taosheng would immediately rush over to join them and head to the holy land in the hearts of Confucian cultivators together.

-----

The flying message arrived in Song Taosheng's hands.

After sweeping it with his divine sense, Song Taosheng handed it to the person sitting across from him.

Sitting across from him was merely a Foundation Establishment cultivator, and a child at that.

His cheeks still held their baby fat, and he wore an apricot-yellow short jacket. His irises were as dark and glossy as lacquer. It was none other than Kong Ran - the son of the Flying Cloud Kingdom's Prime Minister, Kong Zhaoming, a ten-year-old child prodigy.

Kong Ran read the flying message, let out a "Wow," and looked at Song Taosheng with envy.

But Song Taosheng shook his head. "I am not going."

Amidst his calm expression, a resolute firmness was revealed.

Kong Ran said, "Senior, why must you do this? This is such a rare opportunity! The Huazhang Kingdom is the birthplace of Confucian cultivators. There lies the Jixia Academy, the Ancient Seal Stele, and the relics of past sages. Even just travelling there would be better than cultivating behind closed doors for ten years."

"Moreover, with Lord Duanmu Zhang's recommendation this time, Senior can follow Lord Zhao Hansheng to join Lord Wang Xinyue's tutelage together and comprehensively cultivate Mind Learning!"

Song Taosheng looked at Kong Ran and slowly nodded: "You are absolutely correct. However, there are things a gentleman must do, and things he must not."

"My departure from the Myriad Manifestations Sect this time is neither to avoid disaster nor to seek the Dao, but to... eradicate Qin De!"
