Chapter 949: Tan Zhu's Ambition

When they left the stone cavern, the night had grown deeper.

The cloud qi outside the mountainside was illuminated white by the moonlight. In the distance, the layers of the sea of clouds around Flowing Cloud Peak surged, resembling a silent wave of snow.

The two walked shoulder to shoulder, stepping on the clouds. Chunyangzi's pure gold Daoist robe reflected a faint glow, the three thousand characters of the Pure Yang Pill Scripture on its surface flickering in and out of sight. Ning Zhuo, dressed entirely in white, walked half a step behind him, his brows and eyes serene.

They traveled in silence.

This silence was different from before.

On their way there, though both harbored their own wariness, they had been confident. The Red-Robed Guest and the Nine Fire Dragon Lord had been persuaded one after another. More than half of the five Nascent Souls had made their move, and the momentum of the Nanming Stronghold could finally converge into a single force. In the study, they had smiled and clapped their hands together; one wrote "fast," the other wrote "direct," having already grasped the most crucial point of the situation.

However, after meeting Tan Zhu, that composure of strategizing within a command tent had been mostly whittled away.

Not because Tan Zhu refused.

On the contrary, it was because Tan Zhu agreed too quickly.

He acted as if he had been waiting in the game all along. Not only did he know the two would come, but he also calculated that the Nanming Stronghold would inevitably contest this place. He didn't even need the two to lay out the pros and cons before voluntarily taking up the position of the vanguard.

Such cooperation did not resemble a chess piece. It was more like another chess player who had already rested his hand on the edge of the board before they could even place their pieces.

Ning Zhuo finally spoke softly, "The waters of Flowing Cloud Peak are very deep."

His voice wasn't loud. It scattered into the night wind and was quickly swallowed by the cloud qi.

Chunyangzi's flight speed slowed slightly.

He cast a sidelong glance at Ning Zhuo. There was no panic in the youth's eyes, only solemnity, like a clear spring reflecting the night sky - starlight floating on the surface while undercurrents could already be seen beneath the water.

Chunyangzi suddenly laughed.

This laugh wasn't entirely relaxed, but it held a majestic sense of rallying their forces. He looked up toward Flowing Cloud Peak. His pure gold Daoist robe fluttered loudly in the mountain wind, resembling a patch of burning morning glow.

"Only in deep waters can you find big fish," he said slowly.

Ning Zhuo looked at him.

Chunyangzi stood with his hands behind his back, his gaze passing over the layers of the sea of clouds, as if he could already see the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar, obscured by cloud qi and awaiting the rising sun.

"Ning Zhuo, if the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar were just an ordinary territory, why would we go through all this trouble? Flowing Cloud Peak is one of the eight great main peaks, yet its Peak Lord position has been left vacant for many years. Who knows how many hands from top to bottom of the Sect are reaching into it. The Territory Expansion Alliance, White Cloud Village, and the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak are nothing but sails floating on the surface. The true undercurrents are still underwater."

As he spoke, the pure yang light in the depths of his eyes flared slightly.

"But the more it is like this, doesn't it give our actions more challenging significance?"

"Isn't this exactly the joy of Cultivation?"

Ning Zhuo's heart stirred. In this moment, he experienced Chunyangzi's heroism and elegance.

The big-headed youth also looked toward Flowing Cloud Peak, his mood infected by Chunyangzi, becoming invigorated.

"The Nanming Stronghold is originally woven from debts, interests, opportunities, and ambitions. What I need to do has never been to make everyone perfectly united, but to make their respective selfish desires point toward the same place at the critical moment."

Chunyangzi's wild ambitions, Tan Zhu's secrets, the Nine Fire Dragon Lord's debts, the Red-Robed Guest's grudges, Tu Yuanzi's naive straightforwardness, and the counterattacks of the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak - all of them were like hidden threads in the sea of clouds, currently converging toward the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar.

Then, amidst these stormy waves, I will take the newly raised flag of the Nanming Stronghold and truly plant it upon Flowing Cloud Peak! Ning Zhuo thought to himself.

Inside the stone cavern, it became quiet once again.

The bronze lamp burned silently.

That speck of dim yellow firelight illuminated the grayish-white stone walls, as well as Tan Zhu's gaunt profile.

Outside the cave, the moonlight was a cold white. Faint purple smoke drifted slowly against the entrance like a barely-there curtain, isolating the mountain wind, the sound of the clouds, and the distant undercurrents of Flowing Cloud Peak.

Tan Zhu remained sitting cross-legged on the black stone futon.

When Chunyangzi and Ning Zhuo were here just a moment ago, he had been sitting exactly like this.

Tan Zhu let out a low cough.

This cough was suppressed deeply, as if squeezed out from the darkest depths of his chest. A few wisps of purple-black aura escaped from his lips, only to be quickly and forcefully drawn back into his body.

The flame of the bronze lamp flickered slightly because of this, causing his shadow to elongate and twist on the stone wall. For a brief moment, it didn't look like Tan Zhu, but someone else.

Zhongli Mei.

This name was already dead.

At least, in the arrangements made by Wang Yu and himself, Zhongli Mei had to die.

The news of his death hadn't spread yet simply because the timing wasn't right. Danxia Peak needed "Zhongli Mei" to continue existing on the ledgers, to continue suppressing certain debt claims, and to continue making certain people wary. And he himself had already put on the withered skin of Tan Zhu, carrying the lingering disasters of his poison arts as he walked the final stretch of his life.

He would listen to all of Wang Yu's arrangements.

Throughout his life, he had been sheltered by Danxia Peak, highly regarded by Wang Yu, and had run about, schemed, and plotted for Danxia Peak for many years. Now, at this final juncture, even if he had changed his identity, even if his lifespan was nearing its end, even if the backlash of his Cultivation Art clung to him like fire to bone, he still had to finish this last task properly.

At the same time, the Naming Fire Furnace falling into Ning Zhuo's hands was also a thorn in his heart.

Fortunately, the current situation is advantageous to us.

Zhongli Mei's debts were far ahead, surpassing the vast majority of creditors. It was actually better that this identity hadn't joined the Nanming Stronghold. With the debt claims outside and the title outside, not entering the game made it less likely to attract vigilance.

Joining the Nanming Stronghold under the identity of Tan Zhu - exerting effort, bleeding, establishing merit, or even dying at the end of his lifespan - could all secure deeper chips for Danxia Peak.

Thinking of Ning Zhuo, Tan Zhu lowered his eyelids, an extremely faint sneer surfacing on his lips.

Take it slow?

Ning Zhuo wanted to take it slow.

High-level Cultivators could all tell, and Tan Zhu was exactly one of them.

This youth has stunning talent and an exquisite mind. He could use Liu Jinke to build momentum, use the creditors to establish a stronghold, and drag five Nascent Souls, numerous Golden Cores, and genius Foundation Establishment cultivators one by one into this newly woven net. For a mere mid-stage Foundation Establishment cultivator to achieve this level is truly astonishing.

But this was the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

This was Flowing Cloud Peak.

An outsider Foundation Establishment youth suddenly gaining power indeed caught many people off guard.

But being caught off guard didn't mean they would remain slow to react indefinitely.

"How could we let you take it slow?" Tan Zhu muttered to himself, his voice carrying a trace of hoarse mockery. "You want to use Liu Jinke to see Flowing Cloud Peak clearly, so we will use the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar to force you to make a move. You want to integrate the Nanming Stronghold, so we will make the Nanming Stronghold have no choice but to charge the peak ahead of schedule."

The flame of the bronze lamp danced lightly.

Wisps of purple-black smoke emerged from Tan Zhu's body, slowly coalescing into fine threads. The threads swam in the air like tiny ink snakes.

"Chunyangzi's side has long been influenced by Peak Lord Wang Yu."

"The entire Nanming Stronghold has already become a chess piece for my Danxia Peak."

"It's laughable that these people are still unaware."

Tan Zhu suddenly began to cough again.

This time, he coughed for much longer.

His thin shoulders and back trembled slightly. Purple-black aura seeped from between his fingers and fell onto the edge of the black stone futon, corroding several fine lines into the stone surface.

He rested for quite a while before finally calming down again.

He raised his hand and wiped away a trace of dark stain from the corner of his lips.

The bronze lamp illuminated his face.

On that withered, pallid face, there was no fear, only a kind of near-numb exhaustion.

He didn't have much time left.

He knew this clearer than anyone else.

His lifespan was like sand about to run out.

The lingering curse of his poison art was like a fire burning down to its roots.

How long this identity of Tan Zhu could still hold on, even he couldn't say for sure. Perhaps a month, perhaps several months, or perhaps, after the battle at the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar, it would completely reach its end.

"I really wish time would walk a little slower," Tan Zhu said in a low voice.

When these words left his mouth, the cold harshness, calculation, and loyalty in his eyes finally faded for a moment, leaving only a profoundly deep regret.

He looked up toward the outside of the cave, his gaze seemingly able to pierce through.

Beyond the sea of clouds, where the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar was located, a faint pure gold halo lay hidden in the night, shifting like a morning sun yet to leap out from the clouds.

"At least..."

"...let me see the moment the Naming Fire Furnace is restored, right?"

Tan Zhu slowly closed his eyes, the purple-black smoke once again withdrawing beneath his robes.

Inside the stone cavern, the bronze lamp swayed gently.

-----

The next day.

On the outskirts of the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar, cloud qi flowed like the tide. Inside a temporarily modified stone cavern on the mountainside, the atmosphere had reached a breaking point of tension.

Representatives from the Territory Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village sat on opposing sides.

Representing the Territory Expansion Alliance was Qiu Lei. This person had a burly build, a dull yellow complexion, and a square face. Sitting there, he looked like a mountain boulder that hadn't moved in years.

Representing White Cloud Village was Old Man Youyun. His hair and beard were completely white. Wearing wide robes with a loose belt, he had a gentle expression, with cloud qi swirling within his sleeves.

Qiu Lei said in a deep voice, "The three underground cloud roots and two hidden yang fire veins of this place have all been surveyed, organized, and suppressed by our Territory Expansion Alliance for many years. The three vein-suppressing copper wedges in the Yang-Locking Well were forged at great cost by our alliance. Without our painstaking management, this place would have long been scattered by cloud fire. How else would today's phenomenon of sun-supporting ascending clouds exist?"

Old Man Youyun chuckled lightly. "Daoist Friend Qiu, your words are mistaken. This place was originally under the jurisdiction of my White Cloud Village's Flowing Cloud Ferry. The passing cloud paths, cloud boats, and cloud beasts are all tended to by my village. Your Territory Expansion Alliance secretly operated underground for many years, taking without notifying and setting up formations without asking. Now you turn around and say this land is yours? That is far too overbearing."

Behind Qiu Lei, a Golden Core cultivator of the Territory Expansion Alliance sneered. "White Cloud Village has occupied the cloud paths for many years, only knowing how to collect toll Spirit Stones. Have you ever cast a single glance at the changes beneath the earth veins? If not for my Territory Expansion Alliance, this place would still be an ordinary barren cliff on the mountain's waist."

A white-robed cultivator from White Cloud Village immediately flicked his sleeve. "My White Cloud Village has defended this ferry three times, driving back beast hordes and resisting cloud tides. This place has remained peaceful until today relying on the reputation and fists of my White Cloud Village. How could a few of your copper wedges erase that?"

Qiu Lei raised his eyes. "Speaking of fists, my Territory Expansion Alliance isn't necessarily weaker. The Spirit-Locking Formation, Sinking Vein Disc, and Mountain-Moving Earth Puppets - which one of them cannot suppress a section of the mountainside? Back then, when the Thunder Cloud Society tried to use a lightning tribulation to shatter the southeastern cloud root, who was it that ultimately stabilized the earth vein?"

The cloud qi in Old Man Youyun's sleeves churned. His tone remained slow, but his eyes grew much colder. "White Cloud Village also has Cloud-Shearing Sails, Impermanence Cloud Boats, and Form-Dispersing Cloud Formations. If we really come to blows, who sinks first is still unknown."

When words turn sour, even half a sentence is too much. The cultivators behind both sides grabbed their Magic Artifacts, spiritual light faintly appearing within their sleeves.

Right at this moment of drawn swords and bent bows, a wisp of purple-black smoke slowly descended from above the sea of clouds.

At first, no one noticed.

Because there was too much cloud qi and mist on Flowing Cloud Peak. But after that wisp of purple-black smoke fell above the cloud terrace, the surrounding white clouds actually seemed to be dyed by ink, quietly giving way to a circular gap. The sunlight shone down, illuminating a gaunt figure standing quietly in mid-air.

Tan Zhu!

Dressed in a purple-black robe blowing gently in the wind, his figure was as thin and frail as a scattering shadow of smoke. Yet as he stood in mid-air, with no clouds supporting his feet and no attendants following behind him, the mere drifting down of his wisps of poisonous smoke made the entire stone cavern fall silent.

Qiu Lei's face sank.

Old Man Youyun's pupils contracted slightly.

The rest of the cultivators grew faintly restless.

Before they could make a sound, Tan Zhu spoke.

His voice was very light, yet it clearly overpowered the sound of the cloud tides: "This place has an affinity with me, and also has an affinity with the Nanming Stronghold..."

Before Tan Zhu could finish, Qiu Lei stood up abruptly. The rocky ground beneath his feet was heavily jolted, and yellowish-brown cracks spread out like a spiderweb.

"Tan Zhu! This place is a territory our Territory Expansion Alliance has managed for years. How can you covet it just by claiming 'affinity'?" Qiu Lei rebuked angrily.

Old Man Youyun also flicked his sleeves and stood up, his white robes billowing. The cloud qi around him transformed into layers of wave patterns. "Daoist Friend Tan, do not push people too far. This place was originally under the jurisdiction of my White Cloud Village's Flowing Cloud Ferry. You polluted the ferry previously, and we have yet to settle that score with you. Now you dare to publicly seize land?"

After the two finished speaking, they almost simultaneously glared angrily at each other.

For a moment, this scene was actually somewhat comical.

Tan Zhu looked at them, the corners of his lips twitching slightly, and mocked lightly, "You say it's yours."

"He also says it's his."

"Does the Sect have proof?"

The stone cavern instantly fell silent.

Tan Zhu continued, "Has the Myriad Manifestations Sect ever issued an order bestowing the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar upon the Territory Expansion Alliance? Has it ever issued a document assigning this place to White Cloud Village? Is there a clear contract from the halls? Is there an official seal from the main peak?"

Qiu Lei's expression was gloomy.

The corners of Old Man Youyun's eyes twitched.

Naturally, none of these existed.

The reason Flowing Cloud Peak was so chaotic was precisely because the Peak Lord position was vacant, leading to a jagged interlocking of various factions. The way each family occupied territory relied on old precedents, favors, hidden connections, fists, and the facts established through years of operation, rather than any true, upright Sect jurisprudence.

If there truly were legal proof, why would they be so apprehensive about the Nanming Stronghold charging the peak to establish themselves?

Tan Zhu hit the nail on the head. "Since there is no proof, don't speak so high-soundingly."

"On Flowing Cloud Peak, isn't anyone who occupies a piece of land doing so based on their own abilities?"

"Since that is the case, what is wrong with my Nanming Stronghold using its abilities to contest for it?"

Qiu Lei's face darkened completely. "Good, what a good 'based on abilities'."

He lifted his foot and stomped.

Rumble!

Inside the stone cavern, heavy Earth Qi suddenly surged. A patch of yellowish-brown formation patterns spread out from beneath Qiu Lei's feet, transforming into a heavy ring of earth that enveloped Tan Zhu in mid-air.

This formation didn't aim for sharp slaughter, but rather used earth power to suppress clouds and earth qi to lock spiritual energy. Once an ordinary cultivator was caught in it, their flight light would become sluggish, their spiritual energy heavy, and even breathing would feel like swallowing mud and sand.

Seeing Qiu Lei make his move, a light flashed in Old Man Youyun's eyes, unwilling to fall behind.

The white jade cloud token at his waist suddenly lit up. He rolled up the surrounding cloud qi with a sweep of his sleeve, transforming it into a thousand-layered soft white cloud curtain. As the cloud curtain undulated, it actually generated an invisible sweeping force.

One used the earth to lock the air.

The other used clouds to sweep with force.

The two sides had been negotiating, yet at this moment, they tacitly attacked Tan Zhu first.

Hindered by the Myriad Manifestations Sect's rules, the power of their attacks was firmly controlled. They merely wanted to make Tan Zhu fall and lose face.

This was a way of skirting the edge of the sect rules.

If the Sect truly investigated and held them accountable, they would explain that they were merely acting to manage the environment of this area, and Tan Zhu had just accidentally stumbled into it without them noticing.

In mid-air, Tan Zhu's expression lacked any ripples.

He gently raised his hand.

That hand was gaunt and pale, the knuckles slightly protruding, like a chronically ill person reaching out to block the wind.

A wisp of purple-black smoke silently dispersed from his palm.

The smoke was extremely thin at first, like a stroke of light ink falling into clear water. But in the blink of an eye, it transformed into a deep, dark smoke screen that slowly unfurled downward.

The earth ring crashed into the smoke screen first.

The yellowish-brown formation patterns suddenly stagnated.

The originally heavy and stable earth power actually became mottled after the smoke invaded. Sections of the formation patterns turned black, and patches grew dim.

Qiu Lei's expression changed. His hands formed incantation gestures, wanting to forcibly sustain the formation.

But just as his spiritual energy poured in, the purple-black smoke invaded backwards along the formation patterns, as if it had found the crevices where the earth veins breathed. Qiu Lei let out a muffled groan, suffering a minor loss, and hurriedly stopped.

On the other side, the formless cloud power also rushed into the smoke screen.

Cloud power was formless and immaterial, but once tainted by the purple-black smoke, it rapidly turned grayish-purple, completely losing its light and agile essence. It curled and slowed down, eventually crumbling apart powerlessly.

Old Man Youyun's expression changed.

The poisonous smoke eroded its way over, staining his cuffs.

The corner of Old Man Youyun's eye twitched as he decisively severed that section of his sleeve cloud.

A commotion broke out among the cultivators in the stone cavern. The gazes they directed at Tan Zhu were filled with dread and shock.

In this subtle clash between Tan Zhu and the leaders of the two sides, he had actually gained the upper hand with absolute ease!
