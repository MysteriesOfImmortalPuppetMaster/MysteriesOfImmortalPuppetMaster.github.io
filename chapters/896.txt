Chapter 896: Ning Zhuo: The Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit is My Friend

Seeing Ning Zhuo reject him without hesitation, Cang Yazi frowned deeply.

A layer of gloom appeared on his face as he said impatiently, "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, I can compensate you. What kind of compensation do you need? Spirit Stones? Precious materials? Or Cultivation Arts? As long as you name it, we can negotiate."

Ning Zhuo also wore a solemn expression as he shook his head again without hesitation. If he simply went along with the other party's words right now, it would be a massive blunder!

Taking a deep breath, Ning Zhuo said in a deep voice, "Although the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit is not a cultivator, but merely an artifact spirit, it has its own will, emotions, and thoughts."

"It only acknowledged me as its master because it trusts me. How could I force it to undergo Spirit Awakening or transformation just to repay my own debts?"

"In my eyes, it is my friend. Asking me to sell out a friend for glory and wealth is something I cannot do."

Ning Zhuo refused again, but upon hearing these words, Cang Yazi's deeply furrowed brows relaxed significantly. The irritation and anger in his heart also quietly dissipated by more than half.

Why did Cang Yazi live in seclusion?

Because interactions between people were filled with hypocrisy, calculation, and exploitation. He gave his true heart, but he was not treated with sincerity in return. Getting along with spirit pets, however, was different! That made him feel the true beauty of life.

Although Ning Zhuo had rejected him, the young man was considering the artifact spirit's well-being. The gaze Cang Yazi directed at Ning Zhuo softened a bit. This young man was actually somewhat similar to himself.

Ning Zhuo keenly caught the change in Cang Yazi's expression, becoming even more certain in his heart that his response was the correct one.

He was skilled at reading people and figuring out their intentions. From the age of two, he had started observing Meng Kui, the City Lord of the Fire Persimmon Immortal City, from a corner, studying the City Lord's words and actions to ponder the hidden methods behind them. This foundation was not built in a day.

The world only saw Ning Zhuo making rapid progress at a young age and navigating with ease among powerhouses, but they didn't know how much effort he had put in behind the scenes, painstakingly accumulating experience for years. To speak human words to humans and ghost words to ghosts - everyone understood this principle, but truly executing it was not that easy.

Ning Zhuo continued speaking. First, he expressed his understanding of Cang Yazi's desire to help his own spirit pet. Then, he thanked Cang Yazi for taking the initiative to propose this method to help him clear his debt.

Ning Zhuo said, "If the Spirit Awakening Art won't cause damage to the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, then fundamentally, I am extremely willing to help you, Senior Cang Yazi."

"But regarding this point, I will only be able to make an accurate judgment after I join the Myriad Manifestations Sect, borrow some ancient texts, and study them thoroughly."

Cang Yazi directly shook his head. "Ancient texts in this field are of an extremely high classification. A Foundation Establishment cultivator like you won't be able to borrow them. Even if you are such a genius, it remains the same."

"Matters related to spirits easily touch upon a taboo in the Cultivation World. Even if I wanted to obtain them from within the sect, I would have to pay a heavy price."

"But if you truly want it, I... can let you read mine." When he reached the end of his sentence, a hesitant look appeared on Cang Yazi's face.

Such an obvious expression was immediately caught by Ning Zhuo. He hastily waved his hands. "One should not accept a reward without merit, Senior. Now that I have the Nanming Fire Furnace, I want to take better care of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, so I am indeed interested in secret manuals related to spirits. I can buy them at a set price, it's just... I'm quite ashamed to say that I still owe you a debt right now."

Cang Yazi froze for a moment before a smile appeared on his face. Ning Zhuo's reaction had completely dispelled his previous hesitation.

"I'll just give it to you to read," Cang Yazi waved his hand, his tone much more relaxed. "To be honest, using the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to Awaken the Spirit of my pet will indeed be harmful to it. However, as long as it gets ample rest and recovery afterward, the issue will be resolved."

"When Zhong Limei was repairing the pill furnace, he infused it with two hundred years' worth of pill qi. Now that the pill qi has merged into the furnace, it is especially beneficial for the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's recovery!"

"However, your Nanming Fire Furnace still lacks about twenty percent before it is completely repaired. Don't act recklessly. During the minor trial earlier, in order to fight for the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's acknowledgment, we went all out and disregarded the big picture."

"Generally speaking, repairing an artifact like this requires comprehensive planning and a complete restoration blueprint. But back then, we were all just using our own abilities as we saw fit, so the repaired parts do not seamlessly integrate with each other."

"Because of this, the difficulty of the final twenty percent of the repair has spiked, and the current underlying issues need to be resolved. I can help you find people. I have old acquaintances in the Artifact Refining Hall; I can step forward and ask them to assist with the repairs."

After saying this, Cang Yazi added, still somewhat worried, "Once the Nanming Fire Furnace is fully repaired, its foundation will be even more robust than before. This will be of immense help to the nourishment and advancement of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit."

"If you use the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to Awaken the Spirit of my pet, the worst outcome is that it loses some vitality, but it definitely won't perish."

"In fact, the process of depleting the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit and then letting it recover is similar to martial cultivators tempering their physical bodies. It will accelerate the artifact spirit's growth and cause it to rapidly change its form."

Ning Zhuo was puzzled: "Change its form?"

Cang Yazi replied, "This is also just my guess. The main reason the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit has this form is due to the main body of the Nanming Fire Furnace. Of course, it's also related to the types of Medicinal Pills the furnace refined in the past."

"The furnace exploded and had less than ten percent left. Up until now, eighty percent of it has been repaired. During this process, the texture of the furnace's main body has changed significantly. For the final twenty percent of the repair, the precious materials used will definitely be different from the original body as well. There will be a need to perfectly fuse and unify the current furnace materials."

"Since the furnace's texture has undergone drastic changes, the artifact spirit's form will definitely change as well. This process of change is exactly the artifact spirit re-adapting to the furnace. Naturally, the faster it changes and solidifies its new form, the better it will be for both it and the furnace."

This was the first time Ning Zhuo had heard of this, so he immediately cupped his hands. "Thank you for your guidance, Senior. This junior has learned much!"

Cang Yazi waved his hand: "It's a minor matter. After I return, I'll bring the spirit scriptures I've collected and compiled for you to read."

In Cang Yazi's eyes, his and Ning Zhuo's goals were aligned - to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace as quickly as possible. Since that was the case, he just needed to put in some effort. Why haggle over so many details?

Cang Yazi was straightforward and had an eccentric temperament. Yet, Ning Zhuo quite liked dealing with this type of person.

Because they were straightforward, he didn't have to waste energy guessing. The other party's thoughts were clear at a glance, disdaining any kind of disguise. As for people with eccentric temperaments, once you grasped their personality, you could actually build genuine camaraderie much faster and become reliable friends.

Ning Zhuo shook his head. "Thank you for your kindness, Senior, but I cannot accept this heavy gift without a valid reason. This is my family's tradition - no reward without merit!"

Cang Yazi nodded. At this moment, what surfaced in his mind was the image of Ning Zhuo erupting with awe-inspiring righteousness. Ning Zhuo had acknowledged his massive debt, and prior to this, he had successively rejected Zhong Dao and Tuoba Huang. These actions were the best testament to Ning Zhuo's moral character. It was precisely because of this that when Cang Yazi interacted with Ning Zhuo, he largely ignored the fact that the latter only had a Foundation Establishment Stage cultivation base.

Who wouldn't respect such an upright gentleman?

Cang Yazi said, "Such a family tradition is indeed not bad. However, I must remind you that if my spirit scriptures were to be priced, the cost would be incredibly exorbitant."

Ning Zhuo's expression was very serious: "No matter how exorbitant, there will come a day when the debt is fully paid. There are things a man must do, and things he must not do. The reason why my clan's family tradition is like this is naturally because each and every one of us starts with ourselves; only then can it be maintained!"

"Furthermore, if I were greedy for the spirit scripture, it would inevitably fill my heart with guilt. In the future, while accompanying and spending time with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, my heart would not be at peace. I would always feel like a thief, and that would ruin the beauty of it."

Cang Yazi remained silent for a long time before finally nodding. "Very well. I will go back first to fetch the spirit scriptures."

He stood up and left directly.

Watching this elder of the Myriad Beasts Peak leave, Sun Lingtong's divine sense transmitted over via Life Hanging by a Thread: "Little Zhuo, Cang Yazi is in such a hurry to go back and get the spirit scriptures, and he has such a great favor to ask of you. Why didn't you mention your plan for the Flowing Cloud Peak at all?"

Ning Zhuo responded with his divine sense, chuckling with full confidence: "Boss, this kind of thing varies from person to person. When it comes to Cang Yazi, it cannot even be mentioned!"

"Relationships between people are actually quite subtle. Some seem close but are actually distant, while others seem distant but are actually close."

"But rest assured, I am very confident about handling Cang Yazi. Even if he doesn't join us in the future, I can still borrow his strength and connections."

"Since you've said so, Little Zhuo," Sun Lingtong instantly relaxed. "Hehehe. Speaking of which, your luck is quite good. To think that Cang Yazi would actively hand over his own weakness."

Ning Zhuo furrowed his brow slightly and pondered in his heart, "Actually, this isn't surprising. The fact that these Cultivators participated in the minor trial and persevered until the final round already says a lot. People like Cang Yazi, Chun Yangzi, and the others paid a massive price. They must have had stronger motivations or powerful reasons to willingly do so."

"If I can unearth the reasons behind their actions, it will naturally yield twice the result with half the effort!"

At this point, his discussions with the Nascent Soul cultivators had concluded one by one.

The sixth creditor Ning Zhuo was going to meet next was a Golden Core cultivator. The Furnace Immortal, Tao Liweng.

Tao Liweng was an inarticulate man. After Ning Zhuo exchanged a few pleasantries to warm up the atmosphere, he got straight to the point.

He first handed the debt breakdown to Tao Liweng. The latter carefully reviewed it twice and nodded slightly, "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, when will you be able to repay this?"

Ning Zhuo smiled bitterly, "I've already spoken with the few Nascent Soul seniors earlier. To repay the money I owe you, Daoist Tao, I'm afraid it will have to be pushed back quite a bit."

"For one, the debt I owe you is indeed relatively small. Secondly, their status and identities are laid out right there. If I don't prioritize repaying them and instead repay you first, Daoist, I'm afraid it will provoke a lot of trouble."

"Thirdly... sigh, I have no money. By the time I actually earn enough money to finally reach you on the repayment list, Daoist, I'm afraid who knows how long that will take."

Tao Liweng's expression darkened. From his perspective, he had vied for the Nanming Fire Furnace this time in hopes of securing a better future, but the result was that he had emptied out more than half of his life savings instead.

Ning Zhuo's acknowledgment of the debt had calmed him down a bit, but now hearing that the day of repayment was indefinitely postponed, his mood naturally took a turn for the worse.

"But there is a method where I might be able to repay Senior's debt ahead of schedule. Furthermore, the Nascent Soul seniors wouldn't be able to say much about it," Ning Zhuo suddenly said.

Tao Liweng was naturally curious, "Oh? What method?"
