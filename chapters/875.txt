Chapter 875: A Night Visit for Sponsorship

Wang Yu fiercely protected almost all of the Golden Core and Nascent Soul-level cultivators.

Relying on his home-ground advantage, he successfully created the illusion of fierce competition to seize the ultimate benefits that aligned with Danxia Peak.

He had no other choice.

The incident involving the Nanming Fire Furnace and the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit had happened too suddenly, leaving him and Danxia Peak with extremely limited time.

Therefore, even though the Myriad Manifestations Sect fully cooperated and spread the news, the number of external cultivators they could attract in such a short period was limited.

"There are quite a few cultivators who can make the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit feel a sense of closeness. However, a cultivator who can gain its complete trust and actually repair the furnace might not even exist in this minor trial."

"Furthermore, calculating by Qi Luck, even if the Nanming Fire Furnace is repaired, there is a high probability that it won't remain in the hands of my Danxia Peak."

Wang Yu had been making preparations for the worst-case scenario all along.

What he announced to the public was always that they would do everything in their power to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace. In reality, his true goal was to use this golden opportunity to recruit external talents for Danxia Peak and expand the main peak's faction.

As for whether the Nanming Fire Furnace could actually be repaired - it would be great if it could, but if it couldn't, it was still within his acceptable limits.

"The best outcome would be for Chunyangzi to win in the end and join Danxia Peak, but the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit ultimately dies beyond saving."

Who Wang Yu valued the most was Chunyangzi - or more accurately, he valued Chunyangzi bringing his entire sect to join, specifically joining his Danxia Peak.

However, he wasn't in a rush to contact Chunyangzi right now, nor was he certain if this plan was appropriate. At the very least, Wang Yu hadn't yet fully probed just how much Chunyangzi actually valued this furnace in his heart.

"The second round is still about eliminating the majority. Once this round concludes, the situation will become clear and straightforward."

"The third round will be the true crux."

"Hm?!"

Wang Yu's heart suddenly jolted as he felt a sudden abnormal movement from the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

Since the beginning of this Rising Cloud Minor Trial, this was the first time he had felt such an intense reaction.

Wang Yu immediately traced the source and laid eyes on - Qing Chi.

At this moment, Qing Chi was channeling her Innate Talent, Azure Flame. Tense yet filled with anticipation, she stared at the Jade Token in front of her.

Wang Yu's gaze was firmly drawn to this Azure Flame.

"How bizarre! What kind of flame is this?"

As the Peak Master of Danxia Peak, his horizons were broad, yet he actually couldn't discern the origins of this Azure Flame at all.

"I've never seen such a flame. Is this the female cultivator's Innate Talent?"

"But why does it provoke such... disgust and aversion from the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit?"

Wang Yu immediately used the formation to capture wisps of the Azure Flame's fiery aura. After analyzing it, he gradually understood.

"So that's how it is."

A look of sudden realization appeared on his face. "The Vermilion Bird governs growth; it is vigorous and ascending. But this Azure Flame governs decline; it is withering, decaying, and descending."

"This girl doesn't understand. Displaying that Azure Flame was akin to a provocation towards the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, which is why it drew its disgust."

"Interesting."

Wang Yu quietly memorized Qing Chi's appearance in his heart.

He didn't use any methods to secretly assist her; instead, he watched coldly as Qing Chi was eliminated.

Wang Yu continued his observation, and soon his gaze landed on Mu Yuehua.

"What a good seedling."

"To be able to independently cast the Extreme Yin True Fire, and doing so this brilliantly with only Foundation Establishment cultivation."

"According to intelligence, she nearly became the contemporary fairy of the Yuehua Sect."

"Even if she isn't selected this time, I must recruit her into Danxia Peak. Our peak's level of support is far higher than that of the Yuehua Sect."

Wang Yu had a heart that cherished talent. Previously, he had felt appreciation for Qin De in the Cloud Prison, let alone upon seeing the likes of Qing Chi and Mu Yuehua.

The first round of the minor trial regarding the Nanming Fire Furnace had tens of thousands of cultivators, but by the second round, only a little over two thousand participated.

Therefore, from early morning until late at night, the second round concluded.

The cultivators on duty released a notice early on: those who advanced were to rest for the night, as the third round would take place tomorrow morning.

This couldn't help but cause widespread speculation among the public: The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's dangerous situation had reached a point where every second counted. They truly couldn't afford to delay any longer.

-----

Bluestone Cave Dwelling.

Qing Chi, her eyes red, reported to Ning Zhuo, "Young Master, I have failed you. I was eliminated."

Ning Zhuo couldn't help but feel surprised. He had held high expectations for the Azure Flame, and hadn't expected Qing Chi to be eliminated even after utilizing her Innate Talent.

Compared to Qing Chi, Gongsun Yan's failure was well within Ning Zhuo's expectations.

After all, Gongsun Yan only passed the first round by relying on his Innate Talent. The standards for the second round were far stricter, so the probability of him being weeded out was very high.

When it came to Sun Lingtong.

Sun Lingtong shrugged, indicating everything was a breeze. "I walked up to the Jade Token, demonstrated the basic fundamentals of pill refining, and passed."

Ning Zhuo nodded. "As expected of the boss."

Sun Lingtong then asked Ning Zhuo about his situation. Ning Zhuo answered truthfully.

Sun Lingtong chuckled. "As expected of you, Little Zhuo."

The two brothers complimented each other for a bit, then discussed how to act tomorrow.

Suddenly, a cultivator came visiting.

This immediately struck the brothers as strange.

The results of the second round were public knowledge. Everyone knew Ning Zhuo had advanced and definitely needed to prepare for tomorrow's third round. At this crucial juncture, the most people would do is send a flying message. How could someone make such a presumptuous move as a night visit?

Therefore, the visitor definitely came harboring some strong ulterior motive.

Ning Zhuo's divine sense swept out through the cave dwelling's Jade Token. Getting a clear look at the visitor, he discovered it was actually a half-acquaintance.

"Strange, it's actually... Cao Gui."

When Ning Zhuo first arrived at the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate, to deal with the difficulties created by Yu Heye's faction, Ning Zhuo had proactively sought out Cao Gui, resulting in a brief period of cooperation.

Through this cooperation, Ning Zhuo smoothly joined the small clique consisting of Situ Xing, Shen Xi, and Lin Jinglong, gaining a firm foothold.

However, because he unexpectedly reached an understanding with Yu Heye and became friends with him, Ning Zhuo drew Cao Gui's dissatisfaction.

In the end, Ning Zhuo didn't force the issue. He drew a clear line with Cao Gui, and both sides parted amicably.

At this very moment, Cao Gui stood at the entrance of the bluestone cave dwelling, smiling bitterly in his heart: He never expected the tides to turn, and in the end, he would still have to come seeking cooperation with Ning Zhuo.

In fact, as Ning Zhuo's reputation surged and he stood out in various Rising Cloud Minor Trials, the Cao Family had sent people multiple times to persuade Cao Gui. They wanted him to use their past camaraderie to befriend Ning Zhuo, but Cao Gui tactfully refused every time.

In the process, Cao Gui himself gradually saw the truth: Although Ning Zhuo turned his enmity with Yu Heye into friendship, he always maintained a distance. He never turned around to cooperate with Yu Heye to deal with Cao Gui or the Cao Family's forces.

In essence, this was Ning Zhuo using tactics to ensure his own neutral stance - a righteous path strategy to protect his long-term interests.

People in his family had also advised him as such: For Ning Zhuo to be so highly regarded by the Demon Subjugation Hall's Hall Master Zhong Dao and the Myriad Beasts Peak's Peak Master Tuoba Huang, he is bound to be a significant figure in the future. Even a marginalized Golden Core like Yu Heye could befriend him. Why couldn't you, Cao Gui? Why couldn't you continue being friends?

Cao Gui had actually been swayed long ago. However, Ning Zhuo's limelight grew brighter each time, and his outstanding performance plunged Cao Gui into a different kind of hesitation. He worried that Ning Zhuo would hold a grudge over the past and that, in his high spirits, he wouldn't even have Cao Gui in his eyes anymore.

This hesitation led to Cao Gui's indecision.

During this indecision, Danxia Peak's Nanming Fire Furnace minor trial began. After the second round, Ning Zhuo continued to advance. A Vice Hall Master of the Commerce Hall personally found him and ordered him to represent the Commerce Hall to negotiate with Ning Zhuo and seek some sort of cooperation.

With orders from above, Cao Gui had no choice but to come.

Rumble...

The heavy door of the bluestone cave dwelling slowly opened.

"Brother Cao, hahaha! It's been many days. Have you been well?" Ning Zhuo proactively revealed himself, full of enthusiasm, and welcomed Cao Gui.

Seeing Ning Zhuo's passionate demeanor, Cao Gui's previous hesitation and indecision instantly melted away like ice and snow.

"Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo," Cao Gui cupped his fists.

Ning Zhuo quickly walked up to him, looking at him sincerely, and cupped his fists in return. "Brother Cao gracing my humble abode is truly an honor. Please, quickly come inside."

Cao Gui smiled bitterly, bearing a sense of relief mixed with a complex feeling of helplessness. "Sigh, Fellow Daoist still has an important minor trial tomorrow. Visiting tonight is truly a presumptuous move."

Ning Zhuo directly reached out, grabbed Cao Gui's arm, and led him into the cave dwelling. "Brother Cao, between you and me, why fuss over such trivialities? In the past, it was with your help that I was able to gain a firm foothold in the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate. I have always been willing to be friends with Brother Cao!"

A surge of being moved, along with a feeling of admiration, instantly arose in Cao Gui's heart.

He had been keeping an eye on Ning Zhuo and knew this young man's strategies and righteous methods. Even without him, Cao Gui, Ning Zhuo's strength could have absolutely settled Yu Heye with ease and secured his footing.

Yet Ning Zhuo genuinely took that matter to heart, treating him exactly as he did in the beginning. He didn't possess the slightest bit of arrogance typical of a genius youth who was highly successful and proud. What profound self-restraint and magnanimity this was!

Instead, it was him, Cao Gui, who had been measuring the stature of great men by the yardstick of small men.

Ning Zhuo led Cao Gui into the cave dwelling. In the Cultivation Chamber, a desk, tea, and some pastries were already arranged.

"The space in this cave dwelling is limited, so the hospitality is quite simple. I hope Brother Cao won't take offense," Ning Zhuo said politely.

Seeing that the tea and pastries were both at the Spirit Food level, Cao Gui couldn't help but recall the intelligence that stated Ning Zhuo had an Old Chef by his side who prepared Spirit Food for him daily. It truly exuded the demeanor of a great clan.

He sincerely sighed, "Young Master has put a lot of thought into this."

Very naturally, he changed the way he addressed Ning Zhuo.

A glint flashed in Ning Zhuo's eyes, but he didn't correct him. He merely invited Cao Gui to sit.

Cao Gui and Ning Zhuo both sat cross-legged around the desk. The former spoke from the bottom of his heart: "With your current status and reputation, Young Master, still living in a bluestone cave dwelling is indeed 'settling for a humble abode'."

"A relative of mine happens to be selling a cave dwelling. It's slightly larger than this one. Perhaps it would be convenient for you, Young Master, to recruit more capable subordinates in the future."

Cao Gui first threw out an olive branch.

This cave dwelling naturally wasn't his, nor was it truly his relative's. Fundamentally speaking, it was goodwill specifically extended by the Cao Family toward Ning Zhuo.

It was just that earlier, Cao Gui had been hesitant and indecisive, failing to make contact with Ning Zhuo.

"Oh?" Ning Zhuo immediately revealed an interested expression.

This wasn't him putting on an act; he was genuinely interested.

This was the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate. Generally speaking, a cultivator's cave dwelling was rented and fundamentally remained the sect's asset.

It was exceedingly rare for a cave dwelling to be independently sold externally.

Cave dwellings sold in this manner essentially belonged to the individual and were private property.

Such situations weren't completely non-existent. Throughout the Myriad Manifestations Sect's long history, when inner sect cultivators made tremendous contributions, they might be awarded land partitions. Especially if their ancestors included Soul Formation-level existences or higher, private property would be gifted to their bloodline descendants.

The ancestors of the Cao Family were once affluent, so they possessed some private properties.

Their willingness to take out one of these cave dwellings and sell it to Ning Zhuo clearly showed that they were extremely optimistic about his prospects within the sect!

Even True Inheritance Disciples like Mother and Wen Ruanyu are renting their cave dwellings. In the main gate's territory, they do not own any private property.

If Ning Zhuo agreed and "bought" this private property, on this point alone, he would have surpassed Meng Yaoyin, Wen Ruanyu, and numerous other Golden Core True Disciples.

However, such 'hospitality' definitely comes with a price!

Ning Zhuo was very familiar with the underlying implications. He immediately changed the subject: "Let's set aside the matter of the cave dwelling for now. Brother Cao visiting at night must mean there is urgent business. Please speak plainly."

Cao Gui gritted his teeth, first praised Ning Zhuo for being "straightforward and outspoken," and then cut straight to the point regarding his purpose.

He was here representing the Commerce Hall to make a major deal with Ning Zhuo.

Cao Gui leaned forward and lowered his voice. "The Commerce Hall is willing to sponsor you, Young Master, to help you continue competing for the Nanming Fire Furnace. Spiritual Materials, Magic Artifacts, Medicinal Pills, manpower - whatever you need, the Commerce Hall will provide. Full support, no matter the cost!"

Ning Zhuo revealed a look of confusion. "Why should I accept the Commerce Hall's sponsorship? I can manage on my own."

Cao Gui laughed heartily, highly praising Ning Zhuo's confidence and courage. At the same time, he clearly knew this was merely Ning Zhuo testing the waters.

He paused for a moment before slowly speaking: "We have internal information. You'll be able to verify it during the third round, Young Master. Therefore, we would never resort to deception."

"The Nanming Fire Furnace has been far too severely destroyed, and the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit is extremely weak. Even if you are one in ten thousand and become the ultimate victor, if you truly want to utilize the Nanming Fire Furnace, you must repair it."

"Just the labor and material costs to repair a Spiritual Treasure of this level are astronomical. It is not something an individual can bear. In fact, unless it's a super faction, even an organization might suffer grievous harm to their foundations."

"Among this, the cultivator repairing the furnace is incredibly important. Because in order to repair it, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit must be properly cared for. If ordinary artifact refining methods are used, even if the furnace is ultimately repaired, causing the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to perish would absolutely not be worth the loss!"

"Furthermore, just repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace won't allow its wondrous uses to be fully displayed. The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit must be nursed back to health before one can utilize it for refining pills."

"Moreover, during this process, every single time the Nanming Fire Furnace is used for pill refining, extreme caution is required. Because the weakened Vermilion Bird artifact spirit could very well be thoroughly eradicated if the furnace explodes again. At that time, the Spiritual Treasure will drop down to become a mere magic treasure, its value plummeting to rock bottom."

What Cao Gui said was not wrong.

If it was only obtained by an individual, possessing the Nanming Fire Furnace would just be a burden - a bottomless pit that devours massive amounts of resources.

Only by backing onto a major faction with massive amounts of resources poured into its repair could the Nanming Fire Furnace display its true value.

As for nursing the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, I have the 'Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra' in my hands. I can completely burn and refine the furnace fragments to help replenish the artifact spirit's spiritual energy.

But as for repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace, that truly is not something I can afford.
