Chapter 923: Liu Jinke vs Ning Zhuo

This was the martial arena closest to the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

The venue was standard and entirely unbiased, which differed from Ning Zhuo's previous choice when fighting Situ Xing.

There was another difference: the spectators.

Ning Zhuo had opened this battle to many cultivators. According to his disposition, keeping spars hidden was always his first choice. But this time, he chose to fight Liu Jinke with the express purpose of establishing his dominance.

Now that the alliance was formed, Liu Jinke was the perfect target to sacrifice to the flag. Regardless of which faction had sent him, beating him decisively to awe formidable enemies would save a lot of trouble later on.

Chunyangzi, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, and the others all arrived at the edge of the arena.

Ning Zhuo didn't make this spar public to absolutely everyone; doing so would have undoubtedly overcrowded the venue.

Only the guests who had come to Heavenly Treasure Pavilion were granted the right to spectate.

He also did this to prevent accidents during the battle that might force him to use his Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Ability. If that happened, having fewer cultivators present naturally lowered the chances of anyone seeing through this Divine Art.

Situ Xing was full of anticipation. "Liu Jinke has Golden Core cultivation, a rogue cultivator from Liuyun Peak. I wonder how many of Ning Zhuo's trump cards he can force out this time?"

It hadn't been long since his last fight with Ning Zhuo.

Situ Xing deeply felt the massive gap in combat strength between himself and Ning Zhuo. In the end, he had been sealed in ice, only forcing out Ning Zhuo's second Golden Core Mechanical Puppet. This left the Situ Clan's current leading figure of the Foundation Establishment Stage brooding.

His rival, Zhu Fenxiang, along with her mother, Zhu Guizhi, also harbored quiet expectations.

Zhu Fenxiang transmitted a message via divine sense: "This battle is of utmost importance to Ning Zhuo! He needs to defeat Liu Jinke and demonstrate his strength. Otherwise, the backlash from the various factions on Liuyun Peak will give him a hard time."

Zhu Fenxiang had strong confidence in Ning Zhuo: "Don't worry, Ning Zhuo can do it!"

Zhu Guizhi looked at her daughter with profound meaning. "Ning Zhuo is indeed a genius, that's true, but his cultivation level is ultimately lacking. If he were at the peak of the Foundation Establishment Stage like Situ Xing, it would be easier to say. But he is only at the mid Foundation Establishment Stage."

Zhu Fenxiang replied, "Just keep watching. This battle will dispel all your unnecessary doubts."

Zhu Guizhi sighed softly in her heart. She was unaware of the secret that the Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord favored Ning Zhuo; she only felt that her family's proud daughter had absolute faith in him.

This confidence was irrational, so the most likely explanation was - she had secretly given him her heart.

"However, this is also normal."

"Since she grew up, there have been far too few outstanding young talents who genuinely caught her eye. Even if there were, they all had various underlying interests tied to our Zhu Clan."

"Ning Zhuo is an outsider, naturally free from the entanglements of domestic factions. Furthermore, he is undeniably exceptional, surpassing the likes of Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong. My daughter's taste is not wrong."

Zhu Guizhi continuously analyzed in her heart.

"Alas, Fenxiang is entirely devoted to her cultivation and rarely considers matters between men and women. Perhaps right now, she hasn't even realized her true feelings."

"As Fenxiang's mother, I naturally must help her and act as her advisor."

Thinking of this, Zhu Guizhi couldn't help but glance at another female cultivator not far away.

She was as cold and aloof as an epiphyllum, with an ethereal temperament - it was precisely Mu Yuehua.

Mu Yuehua had suddenly joined the Nanming Stronghold, making it truly hard to fathom her intentions.

"Could it be..."

"If that is the case, then she is Fenxiang's rival," Zhu Guizhi's eyes darkened with hidden depth.

Cao Gui wore a face full of worry. "Young Master Ning Zhuo, weapons are dangerous and battles are perilous. You must take care."

All the importance he was currently given came because of Ning Zhuo, so this was genuine concern.

The Immortal in the Furnace, Tao Liweng, stood with his arms crossed, an appraising look in his eyes. He had been repeatedly slighted by Ning Zhuo before, and right now he had only one thought: "Come, come, let me see just how strong you really are, Ning Zhuo!"

Whether it's a mule or a horse, pull it out for a walk.

"Let's see if your strength matches the massive boasting you did earlier!"

Jiang Ping, Han Muma, Pi Fujie, and the others also watched with the intent to examine him.

Everyone's opinion was completely unified - Ning Zhuo had to display combat strength rivaling a Golden Core cultivator. Only then would he pass this test disguised as a martial spar.

Liu Jinke and Ning Zhuo both entered the arena at this moment.

Liu Jinke had the appearance of a young man, his face chiseled as if carved by a knife and axe, with sharp brow ridges and pale gold eyes. Arms crossed over his chest, his gaze flashed like lightning as he coldly swept his eyes over the surroundings before locking onto Ning Zhuo directly in front of him. "Ning Zhuo, though your Nanming Stronghold is strong and boasts five Nascent Soul cultivators, in this battle, not a single one of them can help you."

Liu Jinke used his words to apply pressure.

Ning Zhuo wore a smile, remaining calm and collected.

He was currently wearing the Great Thousand Mechanism Resonance Garment, a silver-threaded magic robe adorned with green cloud-and-thunder patterns, which accentuated his upright posture and handsome features. He possessed both the elegance of a scholar and the heroic spirit of a martial artist.

Even more eye-catching was the extremely fine and thin layer of golden light patterns completely coating his body. This greatly amplified his imposing presence, making his aura radiate an unassailable dignity.

Completely unaffected by the pressure, Ning Zhuo spoke in a joking tone: "Senior Brother Liu Jinke, although you act as a hostile guest, I still treated you to an entire table of fine wine and delicacies. Consider it your last meal. For what comes next, I wish you a safe journey to the other side."

Liu Jinke had long been filled with pent-up anger. Earlier, he was suppressed by the presence of Nascent Soul cultivators and couldn't lash out, but now he finally vented it directly: "Ning Zhuo, once I defeat you, I'll tear your mouth apart first!"

Furious, he directly launched an attack without another word, fiercely lunging at Ning Zhuo.

Liu Jinke had been dispatched by the Demon Subjugation Hall to make things difficult for Ning Zhuo, but the Demon Subjugation Hall had also instructed him not to deal a lethal blow.

Therefore, he had anger and battle intent, but no killing intent.

The successful establishment of the Nanming Stronghold and the addition of the five Nascent Souls also made Liu Jinke feel immense pressure. As such, he didn't want to kill Ning Zhuo either, as doing so would put him in a very troublesome situation. If any one of the Nascent Souls came to settle the score, he wouldn't be able to bear the consequences.

Right now, Liu Jinke only wanted to teach Ning Zhuo a lesson, one that would make the latter feel profound, unbearable pain every single time he recalled it for the rest of his life!

As Liu Jinke lunged, Ning Zhuo exploded backward in retreat.

He softly chanted while his hands formed incantation seals: "Earth veins run deep, profound roots stay hidden, vines entangle, all things imprisoned. Rise!"

Rumble...

The floor of the martial arena trembled violently!

Countless vines, as thick as a child's arm, burst out from the ground!

The vines were entirely emerald green and covered with sharp barbs. Like giant pythons awakening from slumber, they twisted and extended madly, weaving together in front of Ning Zhuo to form an airtight wall of vines.

Liu Jinke broke through the wall and charged straight ahead, imposing and aggressive.

However, the vines grew densely, and another vine wall rose in front of him. At the same time, countless vines lashed out to entangle him.

Liu Jinke slaughtered his way forward, severing vines and smashing through the walls. His momentum grew stronger and stronger, like a golden flood dragon rising from the sea!

He was a body cultivator, highly skilled in close-quarters combat.

While retreating rapidly, Ning Zhuo calculated in his heart, various options surfacing in his divine sea within his Upper Dantian.

The big-headed youth instinctively wanted to choose the "Advent of the Tree World Art," but the Luo Pages allowed him to quickly calculate that doing so would slow down the pace of the battle, making it very easy to fall into his opponent's rhythm.

Thus, Ning Zhuo directly tossed out the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot.

His magic robes fluttered like clouds as he drifted down onto this mechanical vehicle, and his speed soared once again.

The spectators' eyes lit up; the vast majority of them were seeing Ning Zhuo bring out this mechanical construct for the first time.

What a fine mechanism!

A thousand-year crouching bamboo formed its bones, ninety-nine segments made up the python body, its hornless dragon head held a pearl in its mouth, and the profound wheels beneath its belly could scale cliffs as if driving on flat ground.

Wind and thunder runic text floated in the air to clear the path, while he sat steadily on the horned dragon chair, riding the wind. The wheels turned silently, piercing through forests and leaping over ravines like a living python soaring.

Ning Zhuo sat firmly atop the mechanical chariot. As his speed increased, his hands continuously formed incantation seals.

He still quite liked Wood Element Techniques.

At this moment, he directly cast the Advent of the Tree World Art.

Giant trees bulged out from the ground, intervening in the battlefield to form even more obstacles targeted at Liu Jinke.

Liu Jinke felt the resistance against him spike drastically. Watching Ning Zhuo get further and further away, he decisively activated his strongest trump card - Innate Talent: [Golden Liquid Returned Core Body]!

He utilized his Innate Talent to perform a technique, unleashing the Blood-Melting Golden Needles.

The spectating crowd saw him let out an explosive shout and violently throw his arms wide!

In an instant, countless golden needles shot out from the pores all over his body.

Tens of thousands of golden needles, dazzling to the eye, shot toward Ning Zhuo like a torrential downpour, resembling a flowing river of golden light.

Bearing the brunt of the attack was the wall of vines Ning Zhuo had spawned.

Puff, puff, puff, puff, puff...

Amidst piercing sounds as dense as drumbeats, the golden needles passed straight through the wall, like red-hot iron nails piercing through tofu.

The tough vines were as fragile as paper before the golden needles. Upon being pierced, a massive amount of sap sprayed out.

In barely the blink of an eye, the vine wall was riddled with holes.

Then, having been shot into a sieve, it crashed to the ground!

Giant trees blocked the path.

The thick trunks were effortlessly pierced through, their branches and leaves withering before the trees crashed down one after another.

A moment ago, it had been a lush green wall of vines and a forest of giant trees towering into the clouds, but now, all of it was reduced to a pile of ruins!

Within the ruins, waves of golden intent permeated the air, rapidly eroding the surroundings.

Ning Zhuo steered the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot backwards, bringing out two magic treasures and directly infusing them with spiritual energy.

They were precisely the Golden Light Collar and the Cyan-White Jade Ring.

Round as a full moon, with flowing golden rays, the collar cast a layer of golden halo over Ning Zhuo, making him seemingly indestructible.

The jade ring circulated clear qi, generating a cyan curtain of light that covered the golden light, shielding him from all directions.

The golden needles attacked, crashing against the golden and cyan brilliance.

For a time, clinking sounds rang out as sparks flew everywhere.

Countless golden needles fell, melting into drops of golden liquid.

Heavy layers of golden intent appeared and expanded upon the golden and cyan light curtains.

A thin layer of golden "rust" began to coat the Golden Light Collar.

The Cyan-White Jade Ring was also gradually plated with a layer of golden paint.

The vitality of the vines and giant trees withered. Golden light flashed upon the broken ruins as they turned into golden vines and golden wood, completely locking out any Wood Element transformations.

Even the stone slabs of the martial arena were eroded into metal-element floor tiles.

"This is the Golden Transmutation Art."

"An ordinary Transmutation Art couldn't reach this level. The rumors weren't wrong: Liu Jinke using his Innate Talent to cast the Golden Transmutation Art is indeed formidable!"

"Ning Zhuo brought out two defensive magic treasures, but right now they are being eroded by the Golden Transmutation Art, constantly lowering his defense. Let's see how he handles this next."

None of the spectating crowd felt surprised.

Ning Zhuo had deliberately kept Liu Jinke dining in the main hall, naturally to buy enough time to gather intelligence on his opponent.

Many cultivators in the hall had done the same, so even though it was their first time seeing Liu Jinke, he was not unfamiliar to them.

The battle had been extremely brief so far, yet Liu Jinke had already gained the upper hand.

The worry on Cao Gui's face deepened.

Ah Huo gritted his teeth, clenching his fists: "To use Golden Core cultivation to challenge a Foundation Establishment cultivator, this Liu Jinke has completely thrown away his face!"

Jiang Xiaobian clasped his hands together and had already begun desperately praying for Ning Zhuo.

Zhu Guizhi sighed inwardly and sent a message to her daughter via divine sense: "Ning Zhuo is going to be in trouble. Supported by his Innate Talent, Liu Jinke's Golden Transmutation Art has already begun to reshape the battlefield."

"Ning Zhuo previously used Wood Element Techniques, also hoping to reshape the battlefield. But right now, in the contest over the battlefield's environment, he has completely fallen into a disadvantage."

Zhu Fenxiang shook her head, replying secretly: "Don't worry, this is absolutely nothing to Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo."

Zhu Guizhi looked at Zhu Fenxiang but said nothing.

Situ Xing had already furrowed his brow.

Ning Zhuo's Wood Element Techniques were not unfamiliar to him. In their previous battle, Ning Zhuo had also opened with Wood Element Techniques.

"But your tactics are wrong."

"Don't you have plenty of mechanical flying birds? Use them."

"Using defensive magic treasures just gets them corrupted by the Golden Transmutation Art. If you want to dispel this layer of erosion, I'm afraid Liu Jinke won't give you the chance on the battlefield."

As the saying goes, "Timing, Terrain, and Unity." Cultivators have always paid close attention to "Terrain" during clashes. Cultivators with the capability would modify the environment while fighting, making the battlefield more advantageous for themselves.

Situ Xing's own tactics relied heavily on the concept of preparing the battlefield, so he noticed the "mistake" in Ning Zhuo's tactical response.

The Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation (Zhongli Mei) looked on with a profound gaze, anticipating in his heart: "Ning Zhuo possesses a Golden Core-level humanoid puppet; he should bring it out to handle the situation now, right?"

Lu Zeng, however, was thinking: "We mechanists excel in battles of attrition. Bringing out ordinary mechanisms to trade blows and exhaust the golden needles might be a good method."

Having gained the upper hand, Liu Jinke felt smug in his heart: "My Golden Transmutation Art is founded on my Innate Talent. Unless you possess targeted Fire Element Techniques, you cannot melt away my Transmutation Art."

"Hehe."

"Fire Element Techniques are typically used for offense. Those with healing effects are already exceedingly rare, and those with purifying effects are even more obscure."

"How old are you, anyway? What are the odds that you just happen to have mastered a Fire Element Spell specifically meant for purification?"

Under the watchful eyes of the crowd, Ning Zhuo brought out an ancient guqin.

He placed the qin horizontally across his lap. At the same time, mobilizing his divine sense, large swaths of mechanical bird swarms flew out from his Storage Belt.

Ning Zhuo began to play.

Combining the sound of his qin with his divine sense, he controlled these Silent Thunder Birds and Mechanical Golden Swallows.

The mechanical birds swarmed in, but they only served as a slight hindrance to Liu Jinke.

Liu Jinke moved about freely, crisscrossing the field as if charging through an unpopulated wasteland.

Riding the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot, Ning Zhuo was forced to fly around frantically by Liu Jinke, and the situation continued to deteriorate.

The Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot was also eroded by the golden intent. Many of its parts were transmuted into gold, clashing incongruously with its original body and turning into weaknesses and flaws.

The speed of the Forest-Piercing Cyan Python Chariot slowly decreased, and the power of the two great defensive magic treasures also continued to plummet.

Ning Zhuo maintained a calm expression, as if he weren't the one falling into a disadvantage at all.

"Alright then," he suddenly said with a faint smile.
