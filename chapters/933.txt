Chapter 933: Business and Favors

Ning Zhuo carved more than just four types of formation patterns.

He used the Spell-Storing Pattern as the outer pool and the Spell-Locking Pattern as the inner ring. He then split the Essence-Returning Pattern into five paths, corresponding respectively to Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth. The Pressure-Releasing Pattern was carved right in the center, where the Thick Earth Pottery Mother was located, acting like a well stabilizing the pool.

Even more ingeniously, he referenced the layout of the Nine Palaces Formation.

The Five Elements were divided into their respective positions, the center provided suppression, and the four corners were left for circulation.

This way, when the Dharma Pool opened its gates, the Five Elements would not surge out all at once. Instead, the central Earth Palace would first bear the pressure before releasing the Five Elements spiritual energy in sequence. This could sustain the power without disrupting the qi sea.

Relying on his profound mastery of the Dao of Formations, combined with the Luo Shu, Ning Zhuo successfully improved the four basic formations on the spot!

Finally, he bit the tip of his finger.

A drop of blood fell into the center of the five-colored Dharma Pool, like a piece of red jade sinking into a clear pond. The walls of the pool trembled slightly, and the five-colored brilliance converged toward the center before slowly radiating outward.

There were no violent sounds or blinding lights - only a highly stable ring of five-colored ripples slowly spreading across the pool's walls.

The Dharma Pool successfully acknowledged its master.

The third round ended, eliminating the vast majority of the cultivators.

Ning Zhuo, Tie Yanqiu, and fewer than ten others proceeded to the fourth round.

This round was a public test of the pools.

When Tie Yanqiu's Crimson Gold Essence-Returning Pool opened, crimson-gold light surged violently, like a wave of fire from a furnace pouring back into his qi sea. His face flushed slightly, his aura instantly spiked, and with a raise of his hand, he fired a Fire-Gold Awl, shattering three layers of cloud targets.

Cheers immediately erupted across the arena.

The others took turns testing their own Dharma Pools, but none could match Tie Yanqiu.

The cultivators of the Artifact Forging Hall had keen eyes; this minor trial was indeed a contest between Ning Zhuo and Tie Yanqiu.

It was Ning Zhuo's turn to step forward.

He activated his Dharma Pool, but the five-colored pool did not erupt.

It was like a small, deep well. First, an earthen-yellow light lit up in the center, stabilizing his qi sea. Then, Wood Qi sprouted, Fire Qi ignited, Earth Qi bore the weight again, Metal Qi formed an edge, and Water Qi lubricated the cycle.

The Five Elements spiritual energy did not pour in all at once; instead, like five orderly streams of different colors, it flowed sequentially into Ning Zhuo's qi sea.

Tie Yanqiu's Crimson Gold Essence-Returning Pool excelled in ferocity. Like downing a mouthful of strong liquor, it could instantly invigorate a person, but it inevitably came with a harsh afterkick to the head.

Ning Zhuo's Five Elements Essence-Returning Dharma Pool, on the other hand, excelled in stability.

Like a parched field receiving rain, it nourished things silently.

Ning Zhuo raised his Five Elements Creation and Destruction Fan and gave it a gentle wave.

Five Elements Techniques flew out one after another.

Wood vines entangled, fire dragons burned, metal blades severed, water vortexes swallowed, and earth walls crushed.

As the five types of techniques cycled through, there was no stagnation whatsoever. In the end, the Five Elements generated each other. With a rotation of his spiritual energy, it actually condensed into a small five-colored ring that crashed into the final cloud target.

Boom!

The cloud target collapsed layer by layer.

The plaza was dead silent.

After everyone had finished their tests, the deacons transmitted their divine sense to discuss among themselves.

They evaluated from several aspects - stability of spell storage, efficiency of essence return, compatibility of spiritual energy, and finally, the hidden dangers of backlash - before making a comprehensive judgment.

First place - Ning Zhuo!

When Tie Yanqiu first heard the news, his face instantly turned red. "I refuse to accept this."

The deacons had anticipated his reaction. The lead deacon smiled. "That is easily resolved."

He asked Ning Zhuo to temporarily lend his Dharma Pool and handed it over to Tie Yanqiu for a test.

Ning Zhuo naturally had no objections. With a smile, he proposed exchanging Dharma Pools with Tie Yanqiu, expressing a desire to observe and learn.

Tie Yanqiu coldly snorted and swapped Dharma Pools with him.

After testing it, his expression changed. He opened his mouth to say something but found himself at a loss for words.

"I've lost," Tie Yanqiu said as he returned the Dharma Pool to Ning Zhuo.

Faced with the facts, his temperament led him to admit defeat on his own accord.

Ning Zhuo smiled. When returning the Crimson Gold Essence-Returning Pool, he praised Tie Yanqiu's artifact-refining techniques with genuine sincerity.

Tie Yanqiu's expression softened considerably. The gaze he directed at Ning Zhuo no longer carried any sharpness; instead, it held gratitude and a faint trace of shame.

Finally, the Artifact Forging Hall's deacon stood up and announced on the spot, "This is the Dharma Pool Diagram, the reward for the first place in this minor trial. In addition, Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo may take away the Dharma Pool he successfully refined on the spot."

Ning Zhuo accepted it with both hands, a slight joy in his heart.

Ning Zhuo returned to his cave manor. He retrieved the Five Elements Talismans from Old Chef and obtained the newly refined mechanism accessories from Gongsun Yan.

He combined the Five Elements Talismans and the mechanism accessories to form the Spirit-Drawing Box.

This type of mechanism box could convert external spiritual qi into spiritual energy.

"The Cloud Tide Essence-Returning Talisman, the Five Qi Essence-Returning Pill..." These were also obtained through Old Chef's purchases. For Ning Zhuo, who was at the Foundation Establishment Stage, these were the most compatible items available on the market.

"Once I refine a few more Dharma Pools, my weakness in spiritual energy should pretty much be resolved."

Just as he was thinking, Sun Lingtong transmitted secret intelligence through the Life Hanging by a Thread.

"Xuanhu Residence, the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus..."

Liu Jinke's Upper Dantian had been lost, and his divine sense and thoughts were deeply fused with the golden blood of his physical body, making it difficult to wield ordinary magic treasures as easily as one's own arms. However, the Nine-Orifice Blood Gold Fetus was different; it was extremely compatible with Liu Jinke.

"It really is custom-prepared for him."

"The Jinshi Alliance, the Golden Hairpin Crone - it seems she plans to pass on a technique?"

"What could Liu Jinke learn from her?"

Sun Lingtong offered his guess in the intelligence report - the Metal and Stone Opening Art!

Seeing the description of the technique in the intelligence, Ning Zhuo's gaze darkened slightly.

"This is aimed directly at my mechanical constructs."

This point was very lethal.

If he couldn't solve this, Ning Zhuo wouldn't be able to execute his war of attrition tactics, and would instead be worn down by Liu Jinke.

"Green Tea Society, the Soft Cloud Spirit-Dispersing Incense..."

This incense was colorless and had a very faint scent. When mixed with the aroma of tea or clouds and mist, it was almost indistinguishable. It was not a poison, but something that made a cultivator's divine sense become soft and sluggish.

During a tea-drinking gathering, it could create a relaxed and soothing atmosphere.

But if used in battle, for someone like Ning Zhuo who needed to simultaneously coordinate a large number of mechanical constructs, falling victim to it would be absolutely dangerous.

"Bird and Beast Villa, the Flock-Startling Whistle..."

This whistle was originally used by beast tamers to hunt demonic beasts. Once blown, it could disrupt a beast herd's spiritual senses, causing their formation to scatter and driving them to the brink of a rout.

"This is aimed at my ancient qin."

"Earth Expansion Alliance, the Thousand-Jin Drop..."

This magic treasure could shape the battlefield, enhancing the earth's gravity, making every movement require resisting a downward force of at least a thousand jin.

"This is to suppress my speed, targeting the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Carriage and the like."

"Liu Jinke excels in close-quarters combat. If my speed drops drastically, it will be extremely advantageous to him."

"Thunder Cloud Society, the Shattering Thunder Beads..."

These beads were not meant for killing, but specialized in breaking external spiritual energy manifestations and defensive light curtains. If defensive magic treasures like the Golden Light Collar or the Azure-White Jade Ring were continuously bombarded by these imitation thunder beads, their spiritual energy would easily fall into chaos in a short time, and the defenses would be shattered.

In the previous battle, Ning Zhuo had relied on two defensive magic treasures to withstand the golden needles, buying time to deduce the Flame-Washing Golden Taint Art.

Without good defensive measures, his buffer room would be drastically reduced.

"Water-Severing Blade Pavilion, the Fate-Severing Blade Talisman..."

The blade talisman looked unremarkable, with only a thin, black blade mark on its surface. But once activated, it could lock onto enemies on its own, severing spiritual energy connections and threads of divine sense - especially excelling at breaking the strings used for object and puppet control.

Seeing this, Ning Zhuo's expression couldn't help but shift slightly.

"While I am patching up my weaknesses, the assistance Liu Jinke has received is far greater than mine."

"However, it's not like I don't have anything of my own."

"Boss, where did you dig up this intelligence? It's almost too detailed."

Sun Lingtong nodded, "I felt it too. While I was scouting and purchasing intelligence on Liu Jinke, I must have received secret assistance from certain people, or perhaps a faction from one of the peaks."

"But as for which party it actually is, I have no way of knowing."

Ning Zhuo sighed.

After all, he was still a newcomer, and his understanding of the Myriad Manifestations Sect's internal affairs was too limited.

"But the current situation is exactly the direction of development I want to see."

"The more Liu Jinke is sponsored and the stronger he gets, the stronger my Calamity becomes."

"With Tribulation Luck on me, the greater the calamity I resolve, the greater the benefits I will reap!"

Ning Zhuo intended to use Liu Jinke as a pawn to make the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak place their bets. This would not only make it easier for him to discern the enemy's situation and clearly see the political struggles on Flowing Cloud Peak, but also allow him to more quickly digest the Tribulation Luck lingering around him.

"I don't need to worry about the rest for now, but the Metal and Stone Opening Art poses the greatest threat to me. I must find a way to deal with it."

At this thought, a piece of intelligence popped into Ning Zhuo's mind.

"Old Freak Can Lu set up a Rising Cloud Minor Trial. The person who passes it will receive his teachings on the Shattered Mechanism Patching Art."

"This mechanical art is perfect for large-scale mechanical construct combat."

"If I obtain it, I will no longer fear the Metal and Stone Opening Art."

Acting with lightning speed, Ning Zhuo set off immediately. However, halfway there, he received the latest intelligence.

His expression changed slightly, disappointment filling his heart. "What? Old Freak Can Lu's Rising Cloud Minor Trial has already been passed by someone else. And that person is actually Ban Ji?!"

Ban Ji held a grudge against Ning Zhuo. With this, he could forget about getting the Shattered Mechanism Patching Art.

"Could this be a coincidence? Why did it have to be Ban Ji?" Ning Zhuo couldn't help but let his thoughts wander, returning to his own cave manor deep in thought along the way.

"Are there any other remedial methods? How can I counter the Metal and Stone Opening Art?"

Suddenly, Ning Zhuo's heart stirred, and he took an item out from his Storage Belt.

It was a mechanism accessory named the Compatibility Token!

-----

In a certain cave manor.

Ban Jie and Lu Zeng sat facing each other.

On the table lay a row of mechanism boxes, all containing Compatibility Tokens.

The Compatibility Token was Lu Zeng's flagship product. After Ban Jie caught wind of it, he immediately made contact and offered a high price to buy out the entire stock!

With the Compatibility Token freshly created, what it lacked most was a buyer and a reputation. If the Ban Clan was willing to pay, it was equivalent to paving the way. This transaction was extremely beneficial to Lu Zeng.

Just as he was about to nod in agreement, a flying letter landed on the table before him.

The letter was from Ning Zhuo.

The wording in the letter was polite. It simply stated that he urgently needed a batch of Compatibility Tokens. The grades didn't all need to be high, but they had to be stable and reliable, suitable for the improvised connection of mechanism parts on the battlefield. The price was negotiable.

After reading it, Lu Zeng fell silent for a moment.

A sense of foreboding rose in Ban Jie's heart.

Lu Zeng had already put down the flying letter and cupped his hands. "Apologies, but I must supply this batch of Compatibility Tokens to someone else first."

The foreboding feeling in Ban Jie's heart grew heavier, and he couldn't help but ask, "Is it Ning Zhuo?"

Lu Zeng nodded.

Ban Jie's expression instantly darkened. "Doing business is about first come, first served, isn't it?"

Lu Zeng laughed heartily. "We hadn't finalized a deal yet. Naturally, the highest bidder gets them."

Ban Jie sneered coldly. "Are you certain Ning Zhuo will outbid me? I am acting on behalf of the Ban Clan!"

Lu Zeng revealed a serious expression. "Even if Ning Zhuo doesn't pay a single cent, I would still do this business with him. This isn't about money; it's about a massive favor."
