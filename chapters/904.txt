Chapter 904: Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor

Situ Xing suddenly asked, "What are your thoughts, you two? Will you set up a new faction with me, or join forces with Brother Ning?"

Lin Jinglong and Shen Xi exchanged a glance, both looking deeply conflicted.

One of them said, "We are both still hesitating."

The other added, "To tell the truth, this is a momentous decision."

Situ Xing felt surprised. Lin Jinglong appeared slow on the surface, but in reality, he was quite shrewd; he often remained inactive, but when he moved, he astonished everyone. Shen Xi had always been steady, preferring to plan ahead and act only after devising a strategy. Both of them were outstanding talents among men. What kind of situation was so complex that it left both of them wavering?

Situ Xing pondered and said, "Allow me a moment to heal my injuries, and then I will go speak with Ning Zhuo."

Just as he finished speaking, he paused in surprise.

He had originally thought he was injured, but to his surprise, he didn't have a single wound. He was in excellent condition.

Situ Xing looked at Shen Xi, then immediately turned to Lin Jinglong: "Did you guys..."

Shen Xi shook his head, and Lin Jinglong replied, "The ice wall Ning Zhuo used to trap you was intrinsically a healing method."

Situ Xing fell into silence once more.

To think he had actually been held back by a healing method?!

An incense stick's time later.

-----

Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor.

Situ Xing stood before the main gate of the cave manor, looking around. Heavenly light poured down, the distant mountains looked like dark silhouettes, and a sea of clouds churned beneath his feet, giving him the illusion of standing above all living beings.

Ning Zhuo had previously lived in the Bluestone Cave Manor, but to avoid suspicion during the Nanming Fire Furnace Minor Trial, he had ended his lease early, evading the encirclement of numerous hidden currents.

On the second day after the Nanming Fire Furnace Minor Trial concluded, Ning Zhuo rented this cave manor.

The Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor was the sole cave manor at the peak of this mountain. It was Golden Core-level and possessed a unique, wondrous nature.

Gongsun Yan opened the cave manor's doors and received Situ Xing: "The Young Master has not yet finished his daily cultivation exercises, so I ask Fellow Daoist Situ to wait for a short moment. The Young Master left instructions earlier for me to guide you on a tour around the cave manor. I wonder if Fellow Daoist has any interest in this?"

Situ Xing nodded and looked around: "This is my first time entering this Golden Core-level cave manor, so I will take a look around to pass the time."

Gongsun Yan extended a hand: "This way, please."

The most important facility in the Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor was the Three Heavenly Lights Refining Formation.

This was a massive, spacious plaza.

The dome hung high, like an inverted vault of heaven. The majority of it was hollowed out, forming a giant, circular skylight.

The floor tiles were paved with Heavenly Track Crystals, and the formation runes were intricate and precise, as if condensing a realm of Heaven and Earth into a tiny space. Within the formation runes were thirty-six small circles, which were the formation cores. A cultivator sitting cross-legged here could control the refining formation and assist in the forging process.

In the center of the Grand Formation was a massive Sun Pillar. Its body was as transparent as glazed glass, with golden light flowing inside. It constantly absorbed the bright, blazing sun through the skylight.

At the top of the Sun Pillar sat a Moon Sundial.

The dial's face was carved from cold jade, and its gnomon was cast from moonflower silver. When moonlight fell upon it, the gnomon's shadow would slowly shift, condensing the lunar glow.

The dome and four walls were inlaid with star talismans. The star talismans were densely packed and innumerable, arranged according to the numbers of the universal cycle. They remained hidden and invisible during the day, but come nightfall, they would absorb starlight.

The Sun Pillar, the Moon Sundial, the star talismans, and the Grand Formation together constructed the Three Heavenly Lights Refining Formation!

After observing it, Situ Xing praised from the bottom of his heart, "What a fine refining formation. It can produce heavenly fire, which perfectly matches the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit. If this formation is paired with the Nanming Fire Furnace and manned by enough people, producing miraculous pills and wondrous medicines will be of no difficulty."

Having finished touring the refining formation, Gongsun Yan led Situ Xing into the Martial Drill Formation.

Gongsun Yan placed a Jade Slip into position and activated the Martial Drill Formation.

The formation runes lit up one after another. Sunlight, moonlight, and starlight focused and transformed into a ball of light and shadow. The light and shadow shifted and rapidly solidified, forming a clear, humanoid construct of light that looked exactly like Situ Xing.

The corner of Situ Xing's eye twitched slightly.

Gongsun Yan introduced, "This Martial Drill Formation uses the three heavenly lights and relies on the intelligence stored in the Jade Slip to question the heavens and grasp the Dao. It thereby evolves a corresponding illusion of light and shadow to act as a sparring partner."

"Fellow Daoist Situ, sometimes fighting 'oneself' can lead to profound insights."

"Alternatively, I also have an intelligence Jade Slip regarding the Young Master here. You can clash with the 'Young Master' once again right here."

Situ Xing lacked the interest to fight "himself," but upon hearing that he could fight Ning Zhuo again - even if only an illusion - his heart fluttered with eagerness.

Just as he was about to speak, Ning Zhuo's voice suddenly rang out, "Brother Situ, please come into the room for a chat."

It turned out that Ning Zhuo had finished his daily cultivation, sensed Situ Xing's arrival, and immediately sent a sound transmission.

Situ Xing followed the voice, passing through several corridors before stepping into a study.

The study was not large, and its furnishings were simple. A bookcase stood against the wall, holding several rows of Jade Slips and ancient texts. By the window was a desk, upon which sat a cup and teapot, emitting curling wisps of fragrant tea steam.

Ning Zhuo was sitting behind the desk. Seeing him enter, he raised his hand and poured two cups of tea, the hot steam rising softly.

"Brother Situ, please sit."

Situ Xing did not stand on ceremony. He sat opposite him, picked up the teacup, and took a light sip.

"Pah. What kind of garbage tea is this." Situ Xing instantly frowned and almost spat it out. Fearful of breaking his aristocratic demeanor, he forced himself to swallow it.

Ning Zhuo smiled: "It is merely mortal tea. Please do not take offense, Brother Situ. I am burdened with massive debts; I spend where I must and save where I shouldn't. Now that I've rented this cave manor, it's never wrong to be a bit frugal on minor things like tea."

Situ Xing shook his head: "The Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor truly lives up to being a Golden Core-level cave manor; it is genuinely extraordinary. Though the rent is exorbitant, once the Nanming Fire Furnace is fully repaired, it will bring in vast amounts of wealth daily, so there's no fear of failing to repay. Even if it can't be repaired in the short term, just having it as an excuse allows you to owe the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and you can even use it to attract new creditors, can't you?"

As a scion of an aristocratic family, Situ Xing had been groomed since childhood. His political acumen was far higher than others'.

He was much harder to negotiate with than a Golden Core cultivator like Tao Liweng.

If he unleashed his full strength, he could also break through to the Golden Core level.

His peak Foundation Establishment cultivation was merely his facade, not his true essence.

Ning Zhuo had long found his previous Bluestone Cave Manor too small. He took the opportunity during the Nanming Fire Furnace Minor Trial to terminate the lease, feeling absolutely no pity. For his new residence, he naturally wouldn't choose the Demon Subjugation Hall, and he needed to keep a distance from Danxia Peak.

Therefore, buying the Cao Family's cave manor was also the wrong option. He still had to rent a cave manor!

Now that he was renting a cave manor, his most pressing need was alchemy. Secondly, the place had to be large enough to house Old Chef, Qing Chi, and the others, with the possibility of accommodating even more people in the future. Finally, the defensive measures had to be up to standard.

Thus, even though the rent was expensive, Ning Zhuo had swiftly settled on this place.

Ning Zhuo smiled, took a sip of tea, and said nothing.

Situ Xing inwardly gritted his teeth, let out a light scoff, and candidly admitted, "In our previous battle, I lost."

Both his tone and expression were calm, once again revealing the poised demeanor of an aristocratic young master.

Ning Zhuo smiled, set down his teacup, and slowly shook his head. "Brother Situ is too modest. In that battle, I was also an arrow at the end of its flight. Despite the grand momentum I displayed at the time, the truth is I was powerless to control anything more. Had you held on for just a moment longer, the outcome would have remained unknown."

Though Situ Xing was skeptical of these words, they still made him feel a bit better.

He said, "I had also reached my limits. Rather than slowly being ground down in exhaustion, it was better to condense my remaining strength into a single strike to gamble for a chance to turn the tables. If successful, it would have reversed Heaven and Earth; if not, then my skills were simply inferior. There is nothing more to say about it."

"Brother Situ is magnanimous," Ning Zhuo praised, before bringing up the debts between the two of them.

He proposed a solution: in the future, once the Nanming Fire Furnace was fully repaired, he could refine spiritual pills to boost Situ Xing's divine sense.

"Using the value of the medicinal pills to repay the debt. How does that sound?" Ning Zhuo asked Situ Xing.

Situ Xing pondered and said, "The debt between us actually needs to be divided into two parts. The first part is from my personal assets; I can agree to your terms on that right now. But the second part is what my family funded me with. I will need to report this back to the family."

"However, it's highly likely they will agree."

After saying this, his Adam's apple bobbed as he prepared to voice his true request.

But Ning Zhuo spoke first: "Brother Situ, though I luckily won our arranged duel, I cannot sit in the head seat of this alliance."

"Oh?" Situ Xing raised his brow slightly. "Don't tell me it's Shen Xi or Lin Jinglong?"

That would actually be a workable solution.

After all, the Shen, Lin, and Situ families were powers on the same tier. Having one of them act as the leader would not disgrace his family's prestige.

But Ning Zhuo shook his head in the next moment. "It isn't them, either. The one who will truly sit in the head seat will be, at the very least, a Nascent Soul great cultivator."

Situ Xing was stunned. "What do you mean?"

Instead of answering, Ning Zhuo asked, "How does Brother Situ view Flowing Cloud Peak?"

Situ Xing was taken aback once more.

Without waiting for him to reply, Ning Zhuo detailed his plans.

Situ Xing was stunned yet again.

He absolutely never expected that Ning Zhuo's vision would be so grand, his courage so immense, and his methods so radical and risky!

"But wait..."

"Ning Zhuo possesses the Nanming Fire Furnace, which serves as the foundation for establishing a faction. And with so many creditors involved, this is indeed a superb opportunity."

"What a brilliant scheme, what incredible means!"

Situ Xing mulled it over carefully and realized it actually had a high degree of feasibility.

Situ Xing evaluated Ning Zhuo anew, as if truly recognizing the person sitting before him for the very first time.

Ning Zhuo's master plan was vastly grander than his own previous idea of a small faction!

Ning Zhuo's eyes shone brightly: "In this session's Flying Cloud Assembly, Brother Situ is bound to successfully enter the Sect. But after joining, your status will depend on your future efforts."

"If we can rely on this golden opportunity to unite and eventually occupy a corner of Flowing Cloud Peak, wouldn't you gain an extra layer of status out of thin air, along with massive amounts of cultivation resources?"

"This would also have a considerable impact on boosting both your reputation, Brother Situ, and that of the Situ Family."

Situ Xing remained silent for a moment before suddenly asking, "It couldn't be the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, could it?"

"This lord borrowed from the black market and owes a great deal of debt. Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo might not know, but the mastermind behind the black market here is a major figure. His main body has been in closed-door cultivation for a thousand years, while his Divided Spirit manages the black market's businesses."

Ning Zhuo raised an eyebrow. "Soul Formation-level?"

Situ Xing nodded.

Normally, when cultivators reached the Soul Formation level, they might be able to split a clone from their Primordial Spirit - this was known as a Divided Spirit. A Divided Spirit was built upon the foundation of a Divine Art. This meant that every Divided Spirit commanded at least one Divine Art!

Ning Zhuo said, "Thank you, Brother Situ, for sharing this information. The Nine Fires Dragon Lord is indeed a possibility, but I have also lobbied other Nascent Soul great cultivators, and each of them is a potential candidate."

"Three days from now, I will announce it widely and publicly establish this alliance."

"Therefore, the answer will be revealed in three days."

"At that time, if Brother Situ sees that the Nine Fires Dragon Lord is the leader, you are completely free to leave. But if it is any other Nascent Soul cultivator, Brother Situ can announce your decision to join us on the spot. How about it?"
