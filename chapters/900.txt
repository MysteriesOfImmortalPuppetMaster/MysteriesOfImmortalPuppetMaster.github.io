Chapter 900: Situ Xing vs Ning Zhuo - Star-Guiding Sword

Waves of emerald light surged outward from beneath Ning Zhuo's feet, wave after wave, layer overlapping layer.

The emerald waves billowed, turbulent and surging, attacking toward Situ Xing!

The three techniques - Root and Vine Entanglement, Tree World Descent, and Pine Soughing Emerald Waves - could be considered three moves.

The emerald waves were like an ocean tide, like a landslide, yet Situ Xing stood quietly in place, motionless, allowing the emerald waves to strike him.

Boom...

The emerald waves crashed into Situ Xing's body, immediately triggering the defensive measures of his Daoist robe, as if crashing into a massive deep-blue boulder.

The emerald waves split to the left and right, throwing up shattered spray and drowning Situ Xing within.

Situ Xing's body swayed slightly, but his feet were firmly nailed to the spot - this was the Stellar Track Boots silently doing their work.

The emerald waves receded, and the talismans and formation patterns on Situ Xing's magic robe also slowly dimmed.

He was completely unharmed.

"A grand display, but hollow on the inside!" Situ Xing's eyes contained anger. "These are your three moves? Very well, since you are so arrogant, I'll let you know the price!"

The next moment, Situ Xing directly summoned his lifebound flying sword.

The Star-Guiding Sword shot out, tracing a long rainbow in mid-air.

Instantly, the starry rainbow streaked forth, piercing through everything in its path.

Vines were easily sliced apart, and giant trees were directly pierced through.

Ning Zhuo, still maintaining the three techniques, responded at this moment.

Countless vines rushed toward the Star-Guiding Sword, as well as Situ Xing.

Situ Xing leaped into mid-air. The canopies of two giant trees suddenly drooped, acting like massive palms slapping toward Situ Xing from both sides.

Situ Xing activated his Starlight Magic Robe, enveloping himself in a blue stellar boulder to block the siege.

At the same time, he remotely controlled his lifebound flying sword to strike directly at Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo slowly retreated. Around him, emerald waves gushed, vines danced, and giant trees grew in clusters, doing his utmost to delay the Star-Guiding Sword's sharp assault.

Among the Cultivation streams, Sword Cultivators' attacks were often the most incisive.

The Star-Guiding Sword's intrinsic material was outstanding, containing a lifebound talisman within, serving as Situ Xing's lifebound flying sword. Back then, he had mobilized his family's connections, consumed a vast amount of resources, and spent seven months to finally forge this sword.

Although the vines and giant trees around Ning Zhuo were numerous, they were all condensed from spiritual energy and struggled to withstand the sharpness of the Star-Guiding Sword's true body.

Ning Zhuo's use of a war of attrition and stalling tactics - using quantity to wear down quality - also yielded little success. This was because surrounding the Star-Guiding Sword were countless miniature flying swords, continuously grinding away against the vines and giant trees.

Ning Zhuo was not surprised.

He also knew the intelligence on Situ Xing.

The primary Cultivation Art that Situ Xing practiced was named "Divine Sea Stellar Sword Guidance." The spiritual energy Situ Xing cultivated, once released through the Star-Guiding Sword, would automatically dissipate and condense into countless miniature flying swords. Like a cluster of stars orbiting, they were guided and controlled by the Star-Guiding Sword, flying freely hither and thither according to his will.

Therefore, when this Cultivation Art reached great mastery, it was often praised with the saying, "When one sword strikes, ten thousand follow; the shifting of stars and rotation of the dipper are all within my grasp."

The Star-Guiding Sword that Situ Xing shot forth was not merely a single flying sword, but a swarm of swords.

The sword swarm was incomparably sharp. Wherever it went, vines were shredded to pieces, giant trees toppled in succession, and the roots of the flora were all snapped. The emerald canopy that had just managed to blot out the sky was quickly torn to shreds.

The entire forest sea was collapsing.

No matter how boundless your forest sea, I will break it with a single sword!

Shen Xi lightly drew in a breath of cold air: "The Seven Stars Crown on Situ Xing brings a massive amplification to his flying sword. Its power is at least three times greater than when he fought Zhu Fenxiang."

Lin Jinglong looked at Ning Zhuo, his eyes suddenly lighting up: "That's it."

Ning Zhuo took out his lifebound Magic Artifact - the Five Elements Generation-Destruction Fan.

Ning Zhuo gathered materials representing the Five Elements to create twelve fan ribs. Using Cloud Shark Silk, he formed a blank fan surface. At the fan's pivot pin, he engraved the talisman mark of his lifebound technique - the Five Elements Generation-Destruction Spell.

Holding the warm and smooth handle, Ning Zhuo gently pushed with his thumb and flicked it open with a smooth motion.

With a poof, the fan surface unfolded, completely blank.

As Ning Zhuo infused his spiritual energy, five-colored clouds immediately bloomed on the fan surface, their mist flowing and transforming endlessly.

Ning Zhuo practiced the Three Sects' Supreme Methods, cultivating his Three Dantian simultaneously. The Upper Dantian held the Mirror Platform Spirit Communication Manual, and thus he possessed the lifebound Magic Artifact, the Spirit Communication Mirror. His middle dantian practiced the Five Elements Qi Rhythm Manual, holding the lifebound Magic Artifact, the Five Elements Generation-Destruction Fan. His Lower Dantian cultivated the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art, but due to limited time, his progress was slow, and it had not yet produced a talisman mark for a lifebound spell.

Now with the fan in hand, the speed and efficacy at which Ning Zhuo cast Five Elements techniques instantly skyrocketed.

An earthen-yellow wall of light rose in front of him, blocking the path of the Star-Guiding Sword swarm.

The earth wall was about three zhang high and one zhang thick. Golden light flowed across its surface - it was astoundingly a combination of the Metal and Earth elements, possessing terrifying defensive power.

Situ Xing remotely controlled the Star-Guiding Sword to press forward against it, while the countless small swords behind it slightly contracted.

The Star-Guiding Sword still managed to pierce the earth wall, but the entire process emitted an ear-piercing sound of friction, scattering sparks everywhere.

Situ Xing frowned slightly, feeling that his spiritual energy consumption had more than doubled, while the Star-Guiding Sword's speed simultaneously plummeted by a significant margin.

He hurriedly invested more spiritual energy, remotely steering the flying sword swarm to pull up sharply. After smoothly accelerating, it fiercely dove back down toward Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo waved the Five Elements Generation-Destruction Fan.

The Five Elements techniques rotated through generation and destruction, and in the blink of an eye, they were all converted into pure Fire Element spiritual energy, with the amount of energy instantly swelling by more than six times.

The spiritual energy condensed into a spell, transforming into a fire dragon that bared its fangs and brandished its claws as it lunged at Situ Xing.

Seeing the ferocity of the fire dragon - far exceeding the previous emerald waves - Situ Xing decisively chose to retreat, putting distance between them.

For a time, Ning Zhuo and Situ Xing fiercely exchanged blows. Ning Zhuo dodged the Star-Guiding Sword swarm, while Situ Xing twisted and turned amidst an endless barrage of techniques.

The Five Elements techniques took turns taking the stage, and the sharp flying swords crisscrossed the battlefield in a spectacular display of chaos.

Initially, both sides were merely launching probing attacks, but under the fast-paced tempo of their clash, they continuously upped the ante.

The battle grew increasingly intense.

Suddenly, the Star-Guiding Sword swarm shot high into the sky, looking down over the battlefield.

Flying Sword Technique - Meteor Sword Rain!

Situ Xing formed hand seals and silently chanted in his heart.

Hand seals, Heart Seals!

The next moment, his spiritual energy plummeted like a waterfall, causing the miniature flying swords near the Star-Guiding Sword to rapidly expand as their numbers exploded.

The Star-Guiding Sword's tip drooped, pointing toward the ground.

All the small flying swords followed suit, their tips uniformly pointing downward, rapidly accumulating momentum.

The Star-Guiding Sword pressed down lightly.

Thousands of small flying swords fell simultaneously, pouring down like a torrential rain, crashing down like falling meteors.

Each small sword trailed a bright tail flame as it fell. Boom, boom, boom! They struck the ground, setting off a series of explosions and layered shockwaves.

The emerald forest sea collapsed beneath the sword technique.

Vines were shattered into powder, giant trees toppled one after another, and the emerald canopy was torn open with massive gap after massive gap.

Ning Zhuo forcefully waved the Five Elements Generation-Destruction Fan.

Crimson flames and azure water currents intertwined and coiled in mid-air, forming a red and blue vortex.

Five Elements - Blending of Water and Fire!

The blue and red vortex hovered above Ning Zhuo, swallowing and rapidly destroying all the incoming meteor flying swords that shot into it.

Situ Xing's pupils shrank slightly, his heart filled with astonishment: "He can actually execute the Water Element and Fire Element in such harmony and synchronization. Ning Zhuo's realm in these two elements must absolutely not be underestimated!"

Lin Jinglong and Shen Xi also revealed surprised expressions.

To unleash such a technique, not only was an extremely high level of spiritual energy control required, but also a profound understanding of the generation and destruction cycle between the Water and Fire elements.

"Since that is the case..." A fierce glint burst from Situ Xing's eyes, and his hand seals changed.

Flying Sword Technique - Three Stars Align.

All remaining small spiritual energy swords rapidly merged, eventually leaving only two auxiliary swords flanking the left and right of the Star-Guiding Sword's main body.

"Go." With a single thought from Situ Xing, the three swords formed a triangular array and dove downward, piercing through the air and thrusting straight toward Ning Zhuo.

During the dive, a gravitational chain reaction occurred between the three swords. Their flight trajectories dragged and accelerated one another, their positions rapidly rotating. Their speed grew faster and faster, and their path became increasingly erratic.

Ning Zhuo's pupils constricted slightly; he recognized this move.

Back when Situ Xing fought Zhu Fenxiang, he had also used this flying sword technique, forcing Zhu Fenxiang into continuous retreat. Even the invoked God could not withstand it.

Once this move closed the distance, it was extremely difficult for the target to block. The gravitational chain reaction between the three swords would distort the flight trajectories of all the flying swords, allowing them to always attack from unexpected angles.

Ning Zhuo didn't meet it head-on.

As he retreated, he waved the Five Elements Generation-Destruction Fan, continuously firing off various Five Elements techniques to mount a defense and wage a war of attrition.

In a short time, he shifted his attacks away from Situ Xing, focusing his attention on the three swords.

This allowed Situ Xing to seize the initiative. Freeing his hands and gaining plenty of leeway, he sequentially executed two more flying sword techniques.

One was Stardust Sword Scraps. The sword body vibrated, shaking off countless star-light particles as fine as dust. These starlight particles hovered densely over the battlefield.

The other was Stellar Track Pull. Countless starlight particles merged with one another, forming tracks of starlight. Resembling ribbons of light of varying thicknesses, they curved and coiled, positioning themselves across different areas of the battlefield.

Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong had been watching the battle the entire time.

Lin Jinglong sighed with emotion: "Ning Zhuo's Five Elements techniques are indeed astonishing, but relying solely on techniques to fend off flying swords puts him at too much of a disadvantage."

Shen Xi nodded in agreement: "Flying swords often counter techniques because they can pierce a plane with a point. The Star-Guiding Sword is also very special in that it possesses auxiliary spiritual energy swords. This means that even if Ning Zhuo uses single-target techniques, they will be worn down by the auxiliary swords, making it difficult to land a hit on the Star-Guiding Sword's true body."

Lin Jinglong: "From the looks of it, Ning Zhuo does indeed primarily cultivate his middle dantian. However, his Upper Dantian is also a primary focus, as he possesses a lifebound Magic Mirror. The Cultivation Arts he practices are quite excellent."

Shen Xi, meanwhile, looked at the growing number of starlight track ribbons on the battlefield: "If this continues, the situation is clearly leaning in Situ Xing's favor."

"Mm," Lin Jinglong nodded.

Both of them knew Situ Xing's combat style - in the early stages of a battle, he primarily utilized flying swords and flying sword techniques. The residual sword qi, spiritual energy, and so forth, were all simultaneously setting up the battlefield.

His opponents would often be suppressed, lacking the spare capacity to clear the battlefield. Even if they could, it was usually a very bad trade-off. Situ Xing merely used residual spiritual energy to provoke his opponent into intentionally casting techniques. Whether in terms of spiritual energy or timing, this war of attrition would allow Situ Xing to continuously gain the upper hand.

Once the battlefield setup was complete, Situ Xing could establish a sword array. And upon establishing the sword array, his combat power would soar exponentially, breaking past his limits in one fell swoop and rising to the Golden Core level!

Whenever Shen Xi sparred with Situ Xing, he had to set up formations in advance if he wanted to win. Once Situ Xing was allowed to establish his sword array, Shen Xi never won a single match.

Under the current battle conditions, in the eyes of Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong, the scales of victory had already tipped heavily in Situ Xing's favor.
