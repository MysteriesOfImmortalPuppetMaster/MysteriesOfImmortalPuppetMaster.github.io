Chapter 957: Nine Fires Refining the Cloud Material

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord took a step into the void.

He activated the Mystic Flame Dragon Claw Ring, conjuring five crimson-gold dragon claw phantoms that tore through the clear sky, grasping directly at that figure.

This claw was incredibly fast!

Wherever the claw marks passed, five scorched black cracks were left in the air, as if the clear sky had been forcefully scarred by the dragon claws.

Yun Wuji’s Wind-Listening Bell rang softly.

The extremely faint chime was nearly inaudible amidst the lingering echoes of lightning and fire, but Yun Wuji had already leaned his body half an inch away. The crimson-gold dragon claws brushed past his shoulder, tearing off a large piece of his grayish-white Daoist robe’s hem.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's other palm had already pressed down following the momentum, and the Alchemical Fire Soul Lock quietly clung to the protective cloud light around Yun Wuji.

The crimson-gold alchemical fire did not explode; it merely rotated and contracted, much like tightening the heat when refining pills, forcing the spiritual energy surrounding Yun Wuji back into his body little by little.

Yun Wuji frowned slightly.

A fierce light flashed in the Nine Fires Dragon Lord’s eyes. He suddenly closed in mid-air, and the Mystic Flame Dragon Claw Ring lit up once more.

"Got you!"

The Fog Heart Jade Pendant at Yun Wuji's waist emitted a soft white light.

Gathering and Scattering in a Breath.

His figure suddenly scattered, turning into a patch of white fog that slipped through the gaps of the alchemical fire vortex and the dragon claws.

The fog did not flee far, gathering back into human form a hundred zhang away. Only this time, a crimson-gold burn mark appeared on the chest of his grayish-white Daoist robe; clearly, he wasn't completely unharmed.

In the Nanming Stronghold seating area, this round of aggressive attacks by the Nine Fires Dragon Lord slightly invigorated everyone's spirits.

The Red-Robed Guest said coldly, "Good, keep pressing forward, maintain the heavy pressure."

Tan Zhu said indifferently, "Scattering the form isn't difficult; reforming is what drains the spirit."

Ning Zhuo looked at the increasing cloud qi in the clear sky, his expression even more focused. He knew in his heart that Tan Zhu's evaluation was extremely precise. Every attack by the Nine Fires Dragon Lord could only be considered as gaining a slight upper hand if it forced Yun Wuji to scatter and reform.

Every time Yun Wuji retreated, he left behind a cluster of clouds. Every time he scattered, he spilled a patch of fog. Every time he was burned, he used his Cloud-Hiding Sleeves to draw back a portion of the water vapor.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord fought more and more fiercely, yet Yun Wuji was slowly cultivating the clear sky into his own cloud domain amidst the raging fire.

Ning Zhuo had never seen such methods before, but he had reviewed the relevant intelligence: "To think the [Traceless Cloud Dao] of White Cloud Township could actually be used this way. This wasn't mentioned in the reports."

Observing a battle between Nascent Soul cultivators broadened his horizons by a fraction.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord’s offensive did not stop.

Fifth Revolution, Spirit Chaos.

Heart Fire arrived formlessly!

In Yun Wuji's consistently calm, light-colored eyes, a trace of chaotic ripples finally appeared. Beside his ears, the roaring of furnace fires and heaven-shaking dragon roars seemed to sound. Before his eyes, clusters of firelight transformed into pill furnaces, dragon claws, and crimson-gold scales, attempting to drag his state of mind into the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's battle rhythm.

Yun Wuji breathed out lightly.

The white light of the Fog Heart Jade Pendant swirled, protecting his mind and thoughts.

He raised his sleeve and waved it. A small White Cloud Banner suddenly trembled in the distance, spewing out even more cloud qi. The Cloud-Hiding Sleeves gathered the thin mist from all directions, kneading them into a cloud screen that stood horizontally between him and the Nine Fires Dragon Lord.

Impermanent Cloud Screen!

The cloud screen was extremely thin. If it were a cloudy day, it might not be noticeable, but at this moment, under the blazing sun, it refracted tens of thousands of golden fragments. The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's firelight fell onto the cloud screen and was refracted into countless tiny light patterns. As the True Fire shone over it, the figure in the cloud screen split from one to three, three to nine, and nine scattered into dozens of faint shadows.

A heavy look flashed deep in the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's eyes. He simply took out the Demonic Scale Fire Box and opened it directly.

Dark green Demonic Fire drifted out from the box, transforming into tiny scale fires, falling towards the cloud screen, the small White Cloud Banner, and the cloud qi spat out by the Cloud-Hiding Sleeves.

Sixth Revolution, Demonic Refining.

Demonic Fire Corrodes Artifacts!

Mottled scale patterns immediately surfaced on the face of the White Cloud Banner, and the originally pure white cloud qi was tainted with a trace of turbid green. The edges of Yun Wuji's two Cloud-Hiding Sleeves also showed dark green scorch marks, as if burned by some gloomy, cold fire scale.

Demonic Fire was difficult to defend against, making the expressions of many cultivators grow tense.

The white clouds in Old Man Youyun's sleeves also paused slightly.

Yun Wuji did not hesitate; he was extremely decisive, directly severing that patch of cloud qi polluted by the Demonic Fire.

The cloud qi fell, and in just a few breaths, it was burned into turbid ashes by the dark green Demonic Fire.

The White Cloud Banner swayed in mid-air, shaking off the dark green scale patterns on its surface, while a fine crack fractured the banner itself.

Yun Wuji swung both sleeves, surging out spiritual energy to wash away the signs of the flames, but streaks of scorch marks ultimately remained on the cuffs.

Seeing this, Yun Wuji knew deeply that at his current rhythm, he would likely be unable to hold on. How could he dare be careless? He directly crushed the [Wisp of Returning Cloud Bottle]!

Pure white cloud qi gushed out from the bottle like a spring, filling the severed cloud domain in just a few short breaths.

The cloud qi churned, rapidly forming the first true Sea of Clouds in the clear sky battlefield!

It was not thick, but it stretched endlessly.

It was not vast, but it was enough for Yun Wuji to borrow steps, hide his body, and redirect incoming force.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord revealed an angry expression.

He had clearly chosen the Clear Sky Martial Arena, yet Yun Wuji stubbornly laid out a white cloud position directly beneath his raging fire and blazing sun.

Yun Wuji looked at the destroyed [Wisp of Returning Cloud Bottle], also feeling extremely pained.

"Good, good!" The Nine Fires Dragon Lord laughed in extreme anger, the Nine-Orifice Furnace Heart Pendant on his chest vibrating violently.

Seventh Revolution, Dragon Convergence.

All Nine Fires move together!

Earth Fire as the furnace base, Wood Fire as the furnace firewood, Lightning Fire as the furnace roar, Heart Fire as the furnace breath, True Fire as the furnace light, Alchemical Fire as the furnace core, Spirit Fire as the furnace thread, Demonic Fire as the furnace poison, and Dragon Fire as the furnace master.

The nine types of flames suddenly contracted in the clear sky, transforming into a nine-colored fire furnace phantom. The fire furnace overturned downwards, and nine fire dragon reliefs on the furnace walls wandered with their heads held high, their dragon eyes lighting up in unison.

Nine Fires Unified Furnace!

Although this was not a pseudo-Divine Art, it was still a great technique formed from the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's more than forty years of bitter cultivation to unify the nine fires in one furnace.

Once the fire furnace phantom formed, the clear sky felt as heavy as if it were crashing down. The sea of clouds that Yun Wuji had just laid out was entirely encapsulated and trapped inside the furnace. White clouds, thin mist, cloud skins, refracted shadows, along with Yun Wuji himself, all became contents of the furnace in an instant.

In the three seating areas, everyone's expressions changed.

Qiu Lei of the Kuotu Alliance had eyes full of apprehension, saying in a deep voice: "Using the void as a furnace, this is the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's true foundation!"

Old Man Youyun didn't speak, only staring at that constantly churning patch of white cloud inside the furnace.

"Inside the furnace," Yun Wuji fell into a perilous situation.

The entire space was being refined.

As soon as the Cloud Skin Substitute appeared, it was refined thin by the Alchemical Fire. As soon as the Flowing Cloud Refracted Shadows scattered, they were pierced through by the True Fire. Cloud Redirecting Force was also hard-pressed to completely disperse the nine fires inside the furnace, because the nine fires were not a single heavy strike, but nine types of fire intensity pressing down layer upon layer.

Yun Wuji's Cloud-Hiding Sleeves were burned completely crimson.

The cloud steps taken by the Traceless Cloud Shoes became shorter and shorter.

The Fog Heart Jade Pendant's white light flashed violently, and the fog heart inside the jade was illuminated to the point of being nearly transparent by the firelight.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord crossed his arms and floated high in the sky, looking down with eyes full of arrogance.

"Refine!"

The Nine Fires Unified Furnace suddenly shrank.

The white clouds in the clear sky were compressed into a ball, like a cloud pill being forcefully refined.

The sounds of thunder, dragon roars, furnace ringing, and wind howling intertwined into an ear-shattering boom.

The audience's cloud seats outside the protective light barrier were shaken up and down. The cultivators from the three parties—Nanming Stronghold, Kuotu Alliance, and White Cloud Township—all couldn't help but hold their breaths.

Yun Wuji raised his head, the firelight reflecting in his pale eyes.

Cloud-Rain Transformation!

The white clouds in the furnace suddenly turned into rain.

The rain threads were as fine as silver needles, passing through the gaps in the Wood Fire and avoiding the explosion points of the Lightning Fire. The rain fell on the furnace walls and instantly evaporated into white fog. The white fog drilled into the Alchemical Fire vortex, causing the contraction of the Alchemical Fire to experience a half-breath delay. The white fog gathered back into clouds, lifting Yun Wuji and sliding him laterally out from directly beneath the Dragon Fire's pressure.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord sneered, "How many times can you transform?"

He urged the furnace fire once more.

A large area of Yun Wuji's Daoist robe was scorched black. One corner of his Cloud-Hiding Sleeve was burned off, and the cracks on the small White Cloud Banner deepened. But every time the sea of clouds was refined thin, he would take the remnant clouds back into his sleeves; every time they turned into rain, he squeezed out new fog amidst the fire.

Ning Zhuo watched unblinkingly.

Chunyangzi revealed a solemn expression. He noticed that Yun Wuji had constantly been avoiding the enemy's edge, preserving his vital strength!

Every time the Nine Fires Dragon Lord adjusted the fire, Yun Wuji didn't clash head-on, but changed his own form: When the clouds were burned by fire, they turned to rain. When the rain was boiled dry, it turned to fog. When the fog was refined thin, it returned to the sleeve. The cloud qi in the sleeve spread out again, becoming clouds anew.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord controlled the Nine Fires, while Yun Wuji used the transformations of the Cloud Dao to maneuver around them.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord showed a look of impatience. He felt it deeply at this moment: He wanted to use this battlefield as a smelting furnace to refine the enemy, but Yun Wuji was like a shapeless medicinal ingredient, causing the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's targeted methods to fail one after another.

"Too slippery!" The Nine Fires Dragon Lord clearly held the upper hand, but he was always lacking that final bit.

And this missing final bit made the anger in his heart accumulate more and more.

The nine fire dragon patterns on the Nine Dragons Heaven-Incinerating Robe all burst out with dazzling brilliant light.

The Myriad Scale Fire Armor appeared on his chest and arms, and every crimson-gold scale pattern sprayed sparks like a small fire furnace.

The Nine-Orifice Furnace Heart Pendant vibrated violently, and nine threads of fire intersected in front of his chest, forcibly stabilizing the Nine Fires Unified Furnace!

The Nine Fires Unified Furnace technique was extremely formidable, but at the same time, its consumption was also massive.

Yun Wuji suddenly activated the Idle Clouds Returning to the Peak!

The scattered clouds, broken fog, rain traces, and cloud skin remnants inside the Nine Fires Unified Furnace all gathered back towards the Cloud-Hiding Sleeves.

A furnace full of cloud qi was like thousands of white birds throwing themselves into a forest, flowing back through the gaps of the Nine Fires. The fire furnace suddenly became empty, and the Nine Fires Unified Furnace technique abruptly lost its target.

The Nine-Orifice Furnace Heart Pendant on the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's chest suddenly jolted.

The fire furnace phantom showed a slight tilt.

Taking advantage of this instant, Yun Wuji used White Cloud Steps to escape the edge of the furnace.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord shouted angrily and raised his hand to pursue.

An aerial pursuit battle thus unfolded.

Yun Wuji retreated quickly while laying down clouds.

The Impermanent Cloud Screen covered the clear sky.

Flowing Cloud Refracted Shadows drew away the Spirit Fire.

The Cloud Skin Substitute bore the dragon claws.

Cloud Redirecting Force dispersed the Lightning Fire.

The Cloud-Entangling Sleeve held back the Mystic Flame Dragon Claw Ring for half a breath...

White Cloud Steps avoided the Alchemical Fire Soul Lock...

Gathering and Scattering in a Breath spilled out from the fire net...

Yun Wuji became increasingly battered, the injuries on his body constantly accumulating and worsening.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's aura was tyrannical, his offensive magnificent, suppressing Yun Wuji to the point of breathlessness. The fire dragon patterns on the Nine Dragons Heaven-Incinerating Robe were overly bright, and the vibration of the Nine-Orifice Furnace Heart Pendant remained quite frequent. Over time, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's breathing also grew heavy.

"Do you only know how to run?!" The Nine Fires Dragon Lord grew increasingly frustrated.

Yun Wuji solidified his form from behind a patch of remnant cloud. There was already pale blood on his lips. His Cloud-Hiding Sleeve was half-destroyed, and his grayish-white Daoist robe was scorched black in many places: "Dragon Lord, are you out of tricks?"

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord suddenly inhaled deeply, no longer suppressing the dragon nature that had long been restless.

He threw his head back and let out a long howl.

When the howl rose, the clear sky changed color.

His crimson-gold robe suddenly swelled, and the nine fire dragon patterns on the robe detached and wildly circled around him. The Myriad Scale Fire Armor flipped outward piece by piece, turning into genuine dragon scales. His arms elongated, his palms turned into claws, his spine arched, and his fiery red long hair transformed into a flaming mane. Horns grew on his forehead, and vertical pupils ignited in his eyes.

Boom!

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord transformed into his original dragon form.

It was a crimson-gold fire dragon.

The dragon's body was massive, spanning across the clear sky. Its scales looked as if they were cast from crimson-gold, and the edge of each dragon scale danced with a different fire color. Earth Fire bobbed beneath its belly, Wood Fire spawned azure flames along its dragon whiskers, Lightning Fire exploded around its dragon claws, Heart Fire hid deep in its dragon pupils, True Fire illuminated its dragon horns, Alchemical Fire coiled in its dragon belly, Spirit Fire entangled its dragon tail, Demonic Fire crouched in the gaps of its scales, and Dragon Fire rose from its entire dragon body.

The blazing sun of the Clear Sky Martial Arena paled at this moment because of it, as if it had become a dragon pearl held up by the fire dragon's body.

The crimson-gold dragon body circled the sun, its dragon roar shaking the heavens! The protective light barrier fluctuated violently, and the cloud seats of the three spectating parties were all pressed down several feet by the dragon's might.

Chunyangzi's expression was solemn.

Ning Zhuo's heart also received a strong shock. This was his first time so directly seeing a Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator thoroughly transform into its original form. That kind of might where the physical body, spiritual energy, bloodline, and fire mastery erupted as one was completely different from battling in human form!

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord used his dragon body to forcefully converge the nine fires, executing Dragon Fire Crushing Down.

The huge dragon head dived from beneath the blazing sun, and the entire crimson-gold fire dragon, bringing along nine-colored flames, crashed down towards the cloud domain where Yun Wuji was.

The remaining white clouds in the clear sky shattered one after another under the dragon's might, and the sea of clouds was squeezed into thin white lines.

Yun Wuji lost all of his composure.

He poured out all his strength to retreat, explosively retreating.

The White Cloud Banner spun in front of him, and the banner surface fractured with a crack.

The Cloud-Hiding Sleeves desperately spat out cloud qi, but the moment it was expelled, it was crushed by the Dragon Fire.

The Fog Heart Jade Pendant emitted a bright white light, protecting a wisp of his mind when he scattered his form.

The Traceless Cloud Shoes stepped continuously in the air, every step shattering a patch of cloud.

He used Cloud Skin Substitutes.

One layer, two layers, three layers.

Even if three layers of cloud skin were illuminated by the Dragon Fire, they were instantly burned through.

He executed Cloud-Rain Transformation.

Clouds turned to rain, rain became fog, and fog returned to clouds.

But the charging momentum of the crimson-gold dragon body was too strong; the moment the fog took form, it was torn apart by the dragon claws.

He used Cloud Redirecting Force.

Layers of white clouds blocked in front of the dragon head, first caving in, then spinning, attempting to disperse the might of the Dragon Fire crushing down. But this time, the True Form of the fire dragon had arrived in person, all nine fires ignited together. Cloud Redirecting Force could only shave off a portion of the charging momentum, while the remaining oppressive might still blanketed the area.

Boom!

The Dragon Fire descended.

A crimson-gold sun of fire exploded in the clear sky.

The intense light pricked the eyes of everyone in the three seating areas, making them squint. Dense formation patterns lit up on the protective light barrier. The cloud seats beneath the audience's feet churned violently, as if they would be sent flying by the waves of fire in the next moment.

The radiance of the explosion rapidly dissipated, and where Yun Wuji had been, a massive void was blasted out.

All the cloud qi was completely incinerated!

The clear sky became bright and clean once again.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's massive dragon body hovered in the air, his dragon scales shining, dragon whiskers burning with fire, his might astonishing.

Yun Wuji had not gone out of bounds; he re-solidified his form at the very edge of the arena boundaries.

His grayish-white Daoist robe was almost half destroyed, his Cloud-Hiding Sleeves burned scorched black, the White Cloud Banner snapped, and the Fog Heart Jade Pendant covered in fine, dense cracks. The fresh blood on his lips finally dripped down; before the blood beads could fall far, they were evaporated into a faint blood mist by the surrounding residual fire.

He was in extremely sorry straits, yet still within the arena.

Ning Zhuo's pupils constricted slightly: "He dodged it? How did he dodge it?"

With his strike missing, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's dragon body circled in the clear sky, his breathing becoming heavy and rough.
