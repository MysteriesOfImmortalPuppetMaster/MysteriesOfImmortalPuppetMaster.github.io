Chapter 859: Demonic-Confucian Dual-Aspect Nascent Soul!

Qin De suddenly froze.

Like a pitiful man on the verge of drowning, he desperately grabbed onto his last straw of hope.

"I was struck by the bell's toll; I am bound to die."

"My Soul cannot be saved. Whether my Physical Body remains or not no longer matters."

"But perhaps I can rely on my Golden Core to fake my death and escape!"

"No, a Golden Core isn't enough; it must be at least a Nascent Soul!"

If a Cultivator could cultivate a Nascent Soul, it was equivalent to having a second life. Even if their Soul and Physical Body were completely destroyed, the Nascent Soul could seize another body and continue to exist in the world.

"My Golden Core cultivation is not yet at its peak, but Song Taosheng's is!"

Normally, it was absolutely impossible to shatter someone else's Golden Core to form one's own Nascent Soul.

But Qin De's situation was extremely unique - he had the peerless Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art. This demonic art could refine the myriad methods of the world and transform them into his own version.

Similarly, Song Taosheng's Golden Core could be completely refined by Qin De, becoming a Golden Core of the Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art, free for him to utilize.

The trouble right now was that Qin De had neither the time nor the conditions to leisurely refine Song Taosheng's Golden Core.

He didn't even have enough time to refine the Blood Mist Demonic Seed; to this day, he had only refined it to nearly fifty percent.

"I simply don't have time to refine Song Taosheng's Golden Core!"

Realizing this cruel reality, Qin De naturally thought of his own Golden Core.

Naturally, he had complete control over his own Golden Core.

"I'll shatter both Golden Cores simultaneously and strive to forge a Nascent Soul!"

"I'm still missing an 'inkstone'."

The "inkstone" here was merely a metaphor. Qin De couldn't shatter his own Golden Core because his cultivation was still lacking. Nor could he shatter Song Taosheng's Golden Core because he hadn't fully refined it.

Only by combining the two Golden Cores and shattering them together would he have a sliver of a chance to form a Nascent Soul.

The Golden Cores were like two different tubes of pigment; they had to be mixed in the same 'inkstone' to blend together and ultimately form a Nascent Soul.

Qin De had absolutely nothing; where could he produce an "inkstone"?

But in reality, he did have one.

In his hands was a ready-made "inkstone" - the Blood Mist Demonic Seed!

There were no other options.

With no time to hesitate, Qin De threw both Golden Cores directly into the Demonic Seed.

Then, he activated the Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art!

The power of the Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art shattered the two Golden Cores one after another, blending them together. Originally, this power was boundlessly immense, but it was barely suppressed by the Blood Mist Demonic Seed.

The Blood Mist Demonic Seed expanded madly. Gold streaked across its interior, quickly followed by the spread of purplish-black. Then, gold and purple intertwined, dazzling and resplendent!

"It can't hold on much longer!"

"Was it really just wishful thinking after all..."

At this moment, the Blood Mist Demonic Seed had become a bomb, on the verge of failing to contain the clash between the Confucian and Demonic Path powers within.

A Cultivation Art suddenly flashed through Qin De's mind.

This Cultivation Art was none other than the Sage's Great Thief Scripture!

This was the Cultivation Art he had painstakingly created and refined after his sutra debates, the one he was most familiar with and understood most thoroughly.

This Cultivation Art appeared to be a demonic art, but in reality, it was a Confucian method. Its core principle was using Confucianism to walk the Demonic Path, and using the Demonic Path to validate Confucianism.

Qin De had been agonizing over his inability to harmonize the Confucian and Demonic Path powers; they were too strong and turbulent, and the Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art couldn't refine them in time.

"But I can rely on the Sage's Great Thief Scripture to harmonize these two mighty forces!"

Qin De then channeled the Sage's Great Thief Scripture!

Crack, crack, crack.

The cracks on the surface of the two shattered Golden Cores grew more numerous and dense.

Subsequently.

Boom - !

The two Golden Cores exploded simultaneously. The surging, tempestuous power made Qin De's vision go black, and he passed out on the spot.

The Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art, the Sage's Great Thief Scripture, the Blood Mist Demonic Seed, and the two Confucian Golden Cores all melded into one, beginning a natural evolution.

An unknown amount of time passed.

"I... I didn't die?" Qin De slowly opened his eyes.

He was astonished to discover that the Blood Mist Demonic Seed and the two Golden Cores in his body had vanished without a trace, replaced by an infant.

It was only the size of a fist, pure white all over, and radiated a warm, gentle light. It sat cross-legged, hands forming seals, eyes tightly shut, with a serene expression. A faint layer of purplish-black mist wrapped around its body, acting as swaddling clothes, an amniotic sac, and a guardian.

"A Nascent Soul!"

"I actually forged it?!"

For a moment, Qin De was ecstatic.

It felt like a dream!

While he was unconscious, the two boundless streams of spiritual energy within his body continued to circulate following the principles of the Sage's Great Thief Scripture and the Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art, eventually undergoing a natural evolution to become this Nascent Soul.

"Just how heaven-defying must my luck be to perfectly achieve such a monumental result?!"

"No, perhaps it's the Demonic Path's Qi Luck aiding me."

"From another perspective, isn't this Nascent Soul my personal version of the Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art?"

"Isn't this Nascent Soul formation method exactly what that person wanted to obtain?"

Thinking of Xiao Juxia, Qin De shivered in shock, his doubt and fear overriding his joy.

He attempted to activate the Nascent Soul.

The next moment, the Nascent Soul suddenly transformed. The purplish-black demonic mist was inhaled by the infant, which then expelled a layer of pure white Confucian light.

The Confucian infant had turned into a demonic infant, completely dark purple, draped in a layer of warm, bright Confucian light.

Qin De also sensed the secret of this Nascent Soul: "This Nascent Soul has two aspects: a Confucian aspect and a Demonic aspect."

"The Confucian aspect Nascent Soul can utilize Confucian techniques, while the Demonic aspect can activate Demonic Path methods."

Of course, a Nascent Soul could only primarily cultivate one Cultivation Art, and that was the Sage's Great Thief Scripture. More accurately, it was the Sage's Great Thief Scripture transformed by the Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art.

Even Qin De himself hadn't fully mastered this version of the Sage's Great Thief Scripture.

Because it was something that naturally evolved while he was unconscious, relying on monstrous Demonic Path Qi Luck.

"However, I am the founder of the Sage's Great Thief Scripture. In the future, by continuously using this Nascent Soul, I will comprehend a great deal of knowledge and gradually reconstruct a brand-new Sage's Great Thief Scripture!"

Qin De had turned the tables in a desperate situation; his extreme misfortune had turned into a blessing, granting him a completely new prospect and Dao Path.

"Since this Nascent Soul possesses both Confucian and Demonic qualities, I shall call it the Demonic-Confucian Dual-Aspect Nascent Soul. And this method of forming the Nascent Soul shall be called the Demonic Seed Sacred Fetus."

"Not good!"

Clang, clang, clang.

Three bell tolls rang out, and Qin De's soul was completely shattered and scattered, leaving behind only his Physical Body corpse.

The Demonic-Confucian Dual-Aspect Nascent Soul, originally one entity with Qin De, was also affected, suffering severe damage right after birth.

The Nascent Soul's eyelids barely opened, revealing Qin De's complex expression.

With one last glance at his Physical Body, he activated the Nascent Soul and fled into the earth.

Not long after, Wu An and the others led a team to this location.

"Qin De is dead!"

"It's about time he died."

"Relying on his own strength alone, how could he possibly hold out for so long?"

"He was a Confucian cultivator, too. Why wasn't he as smart as the Eagle Claw Butcher and the others, surrendering on his own accord to save his miserable dog life?"

Someone looked at Qin De's soulless corpse and couldn't help but feel a surge of respect, blurting out, "Perhaps, this is what it means to be a Confucian cultivator. Tell me, do you think that guy named Song Taosheng considered that he might die when he prepared to enter the prison for the assassination?"

The crowd fell silent.

-----

At the summit of Cloud Canopy Peak.

Cultivators continuously arrived, masking their figures as they flew in.

Whispers arose among the crowd.

"Zhong Dao letting Wu An atone for his crimes through meritorious service has proven highly effective. All the Demonic Cultivators have either surrendered or died; they've been wiped out in one fell swoop."

"Isn't the Sect Master being too cautious? Calling so many people... is there really a need to be this tense?"

"I think the Sect Master is overworrying. The Cloud Canopy has stabilized, and the target point for the destiny strike has disappeared. Where would the enemy find the strength to counterattack?"

Dong Chen sat cross-legged on the east side of the Sacrificial Altar, resting with his eyes closed. Although his face was pale, a hint of rosiness had gathered on it.

However, his eyebrows twitched faintly.

"As the Sect Master of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, my personal destiny and the sect's Qi Luck are deeply intertwined, granting me a much deeper level of perception."

A twitching brow was not a good sign.

It was exactly because of this that Dong Chen had summoned even more high-ranking sect members.

A streak of purple light quietly landed at the edge of the Sacrificial Altar, materializing into a slender figure.

The newcomer had a face as handsome as flawless jade, eyebrows holding a purple charm, dressed in a long purple robe with a purple jade token hanging at his waist. It was none other than the Vice Peak Master of Purple Thunder Peak - Meng Wuyan.

Meng Wuyan's arrival immediately attracted quite a few gazes.

Although he held the noble status of a Vice Peak Master, these gazes were full of contempt and disdain.

Meng Wuyan turned a blind eye to these looks and stepped forward to greet Dong Chen.

Dong Chen's reaction was indifferent, instructing him to stand by to the side.

Right at the next moment.

Boom-!

An earth-shattering boom exploded in the depths of Dong Chen's heart.

The final strike unleashed by the Monarch of Huazhang Kingdom - at the cost of using the Precious Seal and shaking the very foundation of his entire nation - explosively erupted.

The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy was hit as if by a battering ram.

The nine-layered canopy surface shuddered violently.

The seven-colored clouds instantly dimmed.

The nine main cloud ribbons suddenly pulled taut, and then, with rapid snaps of crack, crack, crack, they snapped one after another.

Out of the nine main pendants, five exploded simultaneously. The fragments scattered everywhere, cutting into the Physical Bodies of nearby Cultivators.

The entire Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy violently rocked, like a sailboat on the verge of capsizing. The whole canopy structure tilted over toward the northwest.

Dong Chen bore the brunt of it. Having the deepest connection with the Cloud Canopy, he spat out a mouthful of blood. Then, blood frantically poured from his seven orifices; his body slumped like mud, and his consciousness blurred in an instant.

Wei Ji's body convulsed violently, his pitch-black eyes instantly losing focus. He collapsed on his side, motionless.

Tuoba Huang, whose heavy injuries had yet to heal, was directly knocked unconscious by the shockwave of this strike.

The fly whisk in Wang Yu's hand spontaneously combusted without fire. He hastily let go of it, then pulled out bottle after bottle of Medicinal Pills. With trembling hands, he poured the pills into his mouth. Even though he swallowed them desperately, his complexion was an ashen gray, still looking like a corpse.

Ling Juejian's worn sword shattered instantly, leaving him severely wounded on the spot.

The other high-ranking members were jolted so hard they stumbled all over the place. Some vomited blood, some fainted, and some collapsed to the ground. Under this single strike, almost all of the dozens of people were felled!

Even Meng Wuyan, who had just joined and lacked a deep connection with the Cloud Canopy, was forced back repeatedly by the massive force of destiny, sustaining minor injuries. His purple eyes were filled with horror.

What was even more terrifying was the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy itself.

A corner of the ninth-layer canopy surface was directly flipped upwards. As a result, the seven-colored clouds churned madly before extinguishing in massive swathes.

"Not good!" Seeing this scene, Dong Chen felt an impending sense of doom; his eyes practically split open with fury and panic.

Pushed to a dead end, he resisted with all his might, recklessly pouring out his spiritual energy to support the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy.

Inspired by his actions, the surrounding Cultivators snapped back to reality and took action one after another.

Supported by a dozen streams of spiritual energy and personal destinies, rays of light injected into the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy from all directions.

The canopy's surface stopped its violent fluttering.

The tilting canopy was pulled back into its original position bit by bit under the collective effort of the crowd.

It was truly pulled back bit by excruciating bit.

One inch.

Two inches.

Three inches...

Every inch felt as heavy as a thousand mountains. Every inch left them utterly exhausted.

Finally, the canopy body was restored to its normal position, and the previously flipped corner of the canopy surface fell back into place.

The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy gave a slight shudder and began to rotate once more. The rotation speed was extremely slow.

Its seven-colored rosy light had almost entirely dissipated, and its aura was weak.

But in the end, it had held out!

Dong Chen collapsed on the ground, gasping for air. His heart was filled with relief - relieved that he hadn't been overconfident and had practically brought along all the sect's higher-ups.

Behind him, many Cultivators either collapsed or sat down, looking utterly miserable.

"Although we survived it, our own destinies have been battered beyond recognition. We must recuperate for a period of time to return to normal."

"That isn't even the crucial part. Just now, a corner of the Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy's surface was flipped open; that is the real major issue!"

As the Cultivators communicated via divine sense and discussed, a layer of sorrow and worry clouded their brows.

Indeed.

The Heaven-Bearing Cloud Canopy was responsible for suppressing calamity luck. A corner of the canopy surface being flipped open meant that a portion of the calamity had already fallen onto the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

"The brewing of the calamity will definitely still take some time, right?"

"During this time, we must take remedial actions."

"It's just that we don't know exactly where the landing point of the calamity is!"

-----

The Demonic-Confucian Dual-Aspect Nascent Soul was quietly escaping.

"I succeeded! I succeeded!" Qin De was overjoyed.

He had endured the most difficult period and had now regained his freedom.

Furthermore, the three soul-urging bell tolls that used to ring out at regular intervals never sounded again.

And without the bell tolls exposing his whereabouts, Qin De was no longer hunted.

"Next, I need to lie in wait near the teleportation formation. I'll find an opportunity to secretly attach myself to a Cultivator or an object, and teleport away!"

"My situation hasn't reached true safety yet."

Qin De had never underestimated the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

He guessed that the Myriad Manifestations Sect would very likely notice something amiss afterwards and conduct a renewed investigation. Besides that, what he worried about most was Xiao Juxia.

The latter had appeared mysteriously and imparted the Myriad Laws Demonic Degeneration Art to him, bringing him an immense amount of psychological pressure all along.

Now, Qin De, aided by the Demonic Path's Qi Luck, had birthed a special method for forming a Nascent Soul. Wasn't this exactly the result Xiao Juxia wanted to achieve?

If Xiao Juxia discovered that Qin De was still alive and had obtained such a peculiar Nascent Soul...

What he would do went without saying.

What Qin De didn't know was that Xiao Juxia was currently hiding in the Cloud Layer.

"Come out quickly, come out quickly," Xiao Juxia murmured softly, his gaze deep and brimming with anticipation.

Several hours ago, Xiao Juxia had just started counting on his fingers to divine the future.

He had successfully avoided the destiny shockwave from the clash of the two powerhouses, escaping unscathed, and had even divined a certain possibility!
