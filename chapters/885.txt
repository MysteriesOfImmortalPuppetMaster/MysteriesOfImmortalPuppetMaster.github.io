Chapter 885: Ning Zhuo Pacified the Artifact Spirit?!

The hosting cultivator announced: "The rules for the fourth round are as follows: You Fellow Daoists may come forward one by one to directly touch the main body and fragments of the Nanming Fire Furnace and face the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit directly. Each person has a time limit of one incense stick. The final results will be determined by your respective performances. The specific order will be decided by the results of the third round."

"The first one - Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo!"

The order for the fourth round was based on the results of the third round.

Among the cultivators who tied for first place, Ning Zhuo was the first to show outstanding performance, so he was placed first in the fourth round. The subsequent order was arranged accordingly.

Ning Zhuo had previously proposed modifying the fourth round's order through Cao Gui of the Commerce Hall, but his request had been rejected.

This was actually not unexpected for Ning Zhuo - this Rising Cloud Minor Trial concerning the Nanming Fire Furnace had an immense impact. It had caused massive waves even before it began, and up to this moment, it was the most highly anticipated competition in this Flying Cloud Assembly. It could even be said to stand out prominently compared to past assemblies.

Under such circumstances, Wang Yu of Danxia Peak, as one of the leaders deeply versed in the Righteous Path, would absolutely not do something as foolish as constantly changing his issued commands.

Under the watchful eyes of the crowd, Ning Zhuo slowly stepped forward, unhurriedly took out a meditation cushion, and sat down cross-legged.

The dilapidated main body of the Nanming Fire Furnace in front of him was simply too horrible to look at. Countless scorched black cracks meandered down from the furnace mouth, densely covering it like a spiderweb. Less than twenty percent of the entire furnace body remained.

As for the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, it hid deep within the furnace body, curled up into a ball, shivering.

"Don't be afraid, don't be afraid, little cutie, I'm here." Ning Zhuo secretly communicated first through Life Hanging by a Thread, his voice very gentle.

Upon hearing this voice suddenly, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit stiffened and was about to scream in terror.

However, Ning Zhuo had already anticipated this and directly used Life Hanging by a Thread to forcefully control it. In an instant, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was rendered completely immobile.

Having completed the "pacification" work for the artifact spirit, Ning Zhuo was not in a hurry to make his move. Instead, he first used his divine sense to manipulate objects, drawing over many furnace fragments and broken pieces that had detached from the main body.

He extended his hand, gently stroking these fragments with his palm and fingers, feeling their delicate textures and rough edges.

He was carefully selecting them.

Just picking out these fragments took Ning Zhuo more than half an hour. Most of the competing cultivators behind him revealed impatient expressions, especially the Nascent Soul-level existences among them.

But there was nothing they could do.

This Rising Cloud Minor Trial did not limit the time for cultivators to make their moves. The original design of this Rising Cloud Minor Trial was to maximize the chances of saving the Nanming Fire Furnace and the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit. Therefore, from the perspective of Wang Yu of Danxia Peak, no matter how long a cultivator took, as long as it was beneficial to saving the furnace, he could accept it!

Therefore, Ning Zhuo was not anxious at all at this moment.

He leisurely and unhurriedly inspected all the fragments and broken pieces, carefully selected a few from among them, and placed them around his meditation cushion within easy reach.

Only then did he stand up, take a few more steps forward, and walk up to the furnace.

He reached out and gently touched the furnace body, the scorched black scars, the dilapidated edges, and so on.

His divine sense spread out like ripples, incorporating every fragment, every crack, and every distortion into his perception.

His fingers moved slowly along the furnace wall, from the mouth to the belly, from the belly to the feet, stroking meticulously inch by inch.

He felt the thickness of the furnace wall, sensed the depth of the cracks, and ascertained the degree of distortion in various places.

The Nanming Fire Furnace was truly in terrible shape. The formation runes were shattered beyond recognition, the furnace fire had long been extinguished, and not a trace of residual warmth remained. More than half of the furnace body was wide open, as if it had been disemboweled.

But fortunately, the core of the Formation, located at the bottom of the furnace, had miraculously survived and not been destroyed.

"Why isn't he making a move yet?"

"What exactly is this boy doing?"

"He's so young, what could he possibly figure out? Heh, even Tie Kuang was helpless, but looking at his posture, does he actually want to forge an artifact?"

"A newborn calf is truly unafraid of tigers."

"Hmph, you could also say he lacks self-awareness, wanting to treat a dead horse as a living one?"

The crowd could already see some clues, especially the Nascent Soul-level existences among them. Their years of cultivation were far longer than Ning Zhuo's entire life so far.

With their incredibly rich life experiences, they had already discerned some signs of what Ning Zhuo's performance was leading to.

Of course, they were unwilling to let Ning Zhuo take such a risk. As the current Hall Master of the Artifact Forging Hall of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, Tie Kuang's artifact forging abilities definitely ranked at the top of the entire Myriad Manifestations Sect and the entire Flying Cloud Kingdom. It wouldn't even be an exaggeration to say he was among the top ten.

Since Tie Kuang had given his diagnosis of the Nanming Fire Furnace and even he didn't dare to make a move for fear of scaring the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to death, now you, Ning Zhuo, actually want to perform artifact forging and take this path?

How dare you?

You're far too audacious!

Several Nascent Soul-level cultivators frowned, wanting to step in and stop him. Their biggest fear was - what if Ning Zhuo insisted on taking the risk and ended up scaring the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to death? Then the value of this rare Spirit Treasure-level alchemy furnace would plummet by more than half!

They believed in themselves more, believing they could save the artifact spirit and win the furnace.

However, when Ning Zhuo took out the first precious material, their attention was instantly drawn away.

"Nine Heavens Profound Iron! That's a Spirit Treasure-level material!"

This piece of Nine Heavens Profound Iron was profoundly black and as heavy as thousands of pounds; it was a core material for refining Spirit Treasure-level alchemy furnaces. This iron was produced above the Nine Heavens, serving as the core left behind when meteors fell. Ordinary cultivators would find it hard to seek even a single piece in their entire lives. The piece in Ning Zhuo's hand was the size of a fist. It was a treasure that the Commerce Hall had transferred overnight from its vault.

"He's merely at Foundation Establishment, yet he can take out such a precious material?"

"Speaking of which, we all have intelligence on Ning Zhuo in the Myriad Manifestations Sect. But his background and origins have never been investigated. What's going on?"

"From the first time he showed his talent, the Myriad Manifestations Sect should have dispatched a team to investigate him. Such a genius seed is almost certain to become a pillar of the sect in the future and take up an important position. But even now, the Myriad Manifestations Sect hasn't yielded any results."

"Is it that there are no results, or are they deliberately hiding it?"

"Hiss... For the Myriad Manifestations Sect to hide his identity information, it must be of great significance. Just what exactly is Ning Zhuo's background?"

The Myriad Manifestations Sect had never leaked any relevant intelligence, which caused others to make even more mental connections.

At this moment, Ning Zhuo took out the second precious material.

This was a bottle of liquid, purplish-gold in color, with a texture like flowing dawn clouds, light as smoke yet heavy as thousands of pounds. Right now, contained within the jade bottle, it flowed quietly, emitting a faint fluorescence like liquid stars.

"Purple Mansion Spirit Liquid?!" Even Nascent Soul-level cultivators turned their eyes in shock.

The Purple Mansion was an alternative name for a cultivator's Upper Dantian, the place where spiritual sense resided and divine consciousness was stored. The reason this liquid was named as such was because it could best nourish the spiritual sense, warm the divine consciousness, and unlock wisdom.

The Purple Mansion Spirit Liquid was exceptionally precious because it was not produced in the mortal realm.

It came from the Purple Mansion Secret Realm. This secret realm was located deep within the starry sky. Inside, there was no sun or moon, only a sky full of stars.

In the secret realm, the richness of star essence was over a hundred times that of the outside world. Every drop of Purple Mansion Spirit Liquid condensed over a long period was the essence of the stars, the distillation of astral power.

When used on people, it could greatly increase the foundation of the Upper Dantian. When used on objects, it could add star attributes and cause a surge in spiritual energy. As for the Nanming Fire Furnace, its star attribute aligned with the Nanming Vermilion Bird, and the growth in spiritual energy would benefit the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit. It was an extremely suitable precious material for repairing the furnace right now.

Next, Ning Zhuo successively took out Flowing Cloud Sand, Great White Golden Essence, Celestial Crystal, Abundant Earth... piece after piece of precious materials. If they weren't incomparably rare, they were supremely expensive. Ordinary cultivators would find it hard to see even one of them in their entire lives, yet right now, they were placed on the ground like ordinary iron and stone, radiating various colored spiritual lights in the morning light.

The cultivators participating in the minor trial could hardly conceal their shock.

"This boy Ning Zhuo is actually this wealthy?!"

"Just two or three pieces would be equal to my entire net worth." A Foundation Establishment cultivator's face was full of astonishment, experiencing the difference between heaven and earth from looking at Ning Zhuo.

The Nascent Soul-level cultivators, however, came to a realization one after another.

"Even if Ning Zhuo has a profound background and is the seed of some super sect, he shouldn't be traveling outside at the Foundation Establishment Stage with so many precious materials."

"There's only one possibility. He must have received funding from some faction or peak within the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

"Demon Subjugation Hall? It's possible. Even though this hall is relatively poor, they can still bring these out."

"Not necessarily. Last night, someone saw a layman surnamed Cao from the Commerce Hall head to the Bluestone Cave Dwelling, where they plotted in secret for a long time..."

Ning Zhuo taking out so many precious materials sparked heated discussions.

Soon, this wave of heated discussion spread outward from the core area into the large crowd of onlookers.

A noisy wave of sound rose from the crowd, everyone fervently discussing Ning Zhuo's net worth and what he was trying to do this time.

Ning Zhuo had long anticipated the outside world's reaction to this, and at this moment, he simply turned a deaf ear to it.

He took a deep breath, set up a half-open refining furnace, brought out a Fire Seed, and stoked the flames.

This refining furnace and the Fire Seed were also provided by the Commerce Hall. Of course, they were only temporarily provided to Ning Zhuo on a rental basis.

Ning Zhuo first threw the Nine Heavens Profound Iron into the refining furnace.

The Profound Iron slowly turned red in the flames, transitioning from profound black to dark red, from dark red to crimson, and from crimson to bright white.

Ning Zhuo sent in his divine sense and "pulled" the melted Profound Iron into fine spiritual threads, thin as strands of hair.

Just this scene alone caused many cultivators behind him to let out gasps of surprise.

Even Wang Yu, who was hiding behind the scenes controlling the formation and maintaining constant observation, felt surprised: "To think that Ning Zhuo actually possesses such profound attainments in the art of artifact forging!"

Ning Zhuo's artifact forging realm had reached the Renowned Artisan level. At the same time, he also harbored a massive amount of "Dao and memories" of artifact forging on his person, which gave him a profound foundation in the craft that far surpassed ordinary cultivators.

When the spiritual threads were circling within the refining furnace and gradually took form, Ning Zhuo decisively poured the Purple Mansion Spirit Liquid into the furnace. The spirit liquid transformed into a purple mist that permeated the inside of the furnace body.

Ning Zhuo formed hand seals, causing the spiritual threads to generate a suction force that rapidly absorbed the surrounding purple mist onto the threads.

Flowing Cloud Sand, Great White Golden Essence, Celestial Crystal, Abundant Earth... these precious materials were also successively processed properly with exceptionally high efficiency. One after another, they integrated into the spiritual threads, making the threads over ten times thicker and shining with a bizarrely brilliant, flowing radiance.

Shen Yaohong, Jiang Ping, and others could hardly conceal their complicated expressions.

"Ning Zhuo is somewhat lacking in alchemy, but he's quite skilled in artifact forging."

"This didn't show up in the intelligence reports. How does this kid cultivate? To have such accumulation in artifact forging!"

"No wonder his cultivation is only at the mid-Foundation Establishment stage. It turns out he invested all his energy and time into formation arrangement and artifact forging. Therefore, he is relatively weak in alchemy."

"But... even if he's weak at it, he still absolutely surpasses ordinary cultivators in alchemy. Almost everyone in his generation has been left far behind by him."

"How did a young man achieve this? To be so outstanding in all these cultivation arts?"

"Say, do you think he concealed his true age? And in reality, he's already seventy or eighty years old?"

Chunyangzi, Cangyazi, and the others wore gloomy expressions. The Red-Robed Guest and the Nine Fires Dragon Lord frequently looked over at the cultivator from Danxia Peak who was hosting the event.

They had already guessed that Ning Zhuo refining these precious materials was an attempt to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace, thereby exchanging it for more intimacy and goodwill from the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

However, this sort of thing was extremely risky. Tie Kuang of the Artifact Forging Hall had previously made the judgment that the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was on the verge of its limits and any minor action would scare it to death.

And repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace - how could that just be a minor action?

The injured party was already terrified beyond belief; even blowing a breath of air would make it feel like someone was trying to kill it. Now, repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace was no less than directly holding down the injured party and performing major surgery.

They feared that as soon as he made a move, the injured party would roll its eyes back and die of fright on the spot, dissipating its last breath.

The state of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit clearly showed it only had one last breath remaining.

These Nascent Soul cultivators had abundant experience and broad horizons. They had already seen through Ning Zhuo's intentions. The only reason they didn't step in to stop him and merely grew restless was because they had first guessed that the one funding Ning Zhuo was the Commerce Hall, and one of the backers behind the Commerce Hall was Danxia Peak.

At the same time, they also saw that both the guarding cultivators and the hosting cultivator were quietly watching Ning Zhuo's actions, and none of them stepped in to stop him.

This was obviously highly peculiar.

"It's about time." Ning Zhuo silently estimated in his heart.

His divine sense was spread out, so of course he had long noticed the expressions and hidden concerns of Cangyazi, Chunyangzi, and the others.

He knew even better that beyond his divine sense's probing, the higher-ups of Danxia Peak, and even Wang Yu, were all on tenterhooks, staring intently at his every move.

If he directly proceeded to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace next, he would definitely be called to a halt instantly and have his actions forcefully restrained. No one would just watch helplessly as Ning Zhuo recklessly took risks regardless of the consequences. Even with only its last breath remaining, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit still possessed massive utilization value!

Therefore, with a thought, Ning Zhuo extracted his Lifebound Magic Artifact from his Upper Dantian - the Spirit Communication Mirror.

Ordinary Magic Artifacts could not enter the Dantian, but a Lifebound Magic Artifact could. High-grade treasures similar to the Buddha Heart Demon Seal could as well.

The Spirit Communication Mirror was not large. Its entire body was silver-white, the mirror surface as smooth as if freshly wiped, and ancient Runes were engraved on the mirror frame, glowing with faint spiritual light.

Ning Zhuo directly activated the Spirit Communication Mirror, carefully initiating communication with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

At this instant, Wang Yu and the others almost had their hearts in their throats.

Previously, Ning Zhuo was separated by the Formation, and the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was immersed in a dreamscape; it was impossible to communicate with the artifact spirit using just a single Magic Artifact.

But now, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was right in front of him, giving the Spirit Communication Mirror a place to be used.

The most crucial part was that Ning Zhuo had long ago planted Life Hanging by a Thread, so there was absolutely zero room for failure.

"Come, little baby, quickly respond to my mirror," Ning Zhuo gently issued the command in secret.

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit shivered, but being forcefully controlled by Life Hanging by a Thread, it thus displayed a series of reactions indicating it was being perceived, touched, and communicated with by the Spirit Communication Mirror.

"What's the situation?!"

"The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit is being communicated with by Ning Zhuo? It's being pacified?!"

For a time, everyone present was shocked!

Even Nascent Soul-level existences were visibly moved.

Ning Zhuo had his back to them, but at this moment, he heard the crowd's irrepressible, collective low gasps.
