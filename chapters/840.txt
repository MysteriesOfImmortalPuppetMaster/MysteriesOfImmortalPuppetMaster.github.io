Chapter 840: Demonic Desire

Qin De continued, "Innate knowledge recognizes filial piety when seeing a father, brotherly respect when seeing a brother, and compassion when seeing a child fall into a well. However, if one sees wealth and greed arises, or sees beauty and lust stirs, innate knowledge also knows this is wrong. Knowing it is wrong means knowing that it can be stolen but choosing not to do so. Yet, knowing it can be stolen - is that not 'knowing theft'? What I mean by 'Innate Knowledge is the Thieving Heart' is not that innate knowledge teaches people to steal, but that innate knowledge knows the theft that can be committed, yet refrains. Does this not implicitly align with my theory that 'To know is to know what can be stolen'?"

In his heart, Zhao Hansheng thought, Hmm?

His brows furrowed deeply.

Qin De’s words seemed like sophistry, but they touched upon a subtle issue - innate knowledge knows right from wrong, and knowing wrong means knowing evil. Knowing evil means knowing that evil can be done. But is "knowing evil can be done" one and the same as "knowing evil can be done but not doing it," or are they two different things?

If innate knowledge knows that evil can be committed, then it can be said to "know theft." If innate knowledge does not know that evil can be committed, then how does it know it is evil?

For a moment, Zhao Hansheng actually couldn't find a rebuttal.

Xiao Juxia’s eyes suddenly burst with a brilliant light; he almost clapped his hands in praise.

"This Qin De is interesting. Very interesting."

No one knew that Xiao Juxia had witnessed the entire debate. He was well aware that throughout the argument, Zhao Hansheng had been on the offensive, while Qin De had been passively taking a beating. The defensive line Qin De had forged with the Sage Great Thief Sutra had been breached time and again by Zhao Hansheng using the principles of Heart Science, like a knife slicing through bamboo.

But Qin De truly had talent!

Amidst the fierce debate, he had absorbed the principles of Heart Science and used them to substantiate the theory of his own cultivation method. This clever counterattack, using the opponent's spear to attack the opponent's shield, had momentarily silenced Zhao Hansheng.

"However, this is ultimately just sophistry, a deathbed struggle. I fear Zhao Hansheng is already close to figuring out the crux of the matter."

Watching from the side, Xiao Juxia had a very clear insight into the entire situation.

"For such a debate to end like this would be far too boring. Let me give you a helping hand."

He suddenly performed a hand seal, executing an extraordinarily secretive technique.

On the other side, Zhao Hansheng was racking his brains, the feeling that he was about to break through the deadlock growing stronger in his heart.

Suddenly, he became impetuous and irritable. His thoughts became chaotic, like sand obscuring the wind. "Why can't I think of it? Why can't I figure it out? I can feel that there is a flaw here."

"This is just a trick!"

"Damn it."

"Think fast! Think it through!"

However, the more anxious Zhao Hansheng became, the less he could grasp the concept. The anxiety in his heart spawned anger.

In the end, he flicked his sleeves and left. Before departing, he left Qin De with a message to wait; within three days, he would return to deliver the fatal blow.

The entire process was recorded on a jade slip and was quickly delivered by the Demon Subjugation Hall into Duanmu Zhang’s hands.

Duanmu Zhang then summoned Chu Xuangu, Song Taosheng, Situ Gu, and others, distributing the jade slip to let everyone know the details.

Chu Xuangu remained silent.

Song Taosheng sighed with regret, "The power of Heart Science is indeed invincible. The Sage Great Thief Sutra was almost completely broken! In the end, it was truly just Qin De’s sophistry. Zhao Hansheng simply failed to see through it in the moment and didn't complete the victory."

Situ Gu defended Zhao Hansheng, "The spectator sees clearly while the player is lost in the game. We are viewing this with hindsight, which is why we have such clear insight. If we were really in Great Scholar Zhao Hansheng’s position, I fear we might have done worse than him."

Duanmu Zhang stroked his beard. "Zhao Hansheng did not disappoint our expectations. Once he calms down and thinks, he will see the essence. When he goes to the Cloud Prison again, he will harvest the fruits of victory in this debate."

Only then did Chu Xuangu speak up. "The victory is apparent, but I fear that long nights bring many dreams."

Upon hearing this, the expressions of the gathered cultivators changed slightly. Some subconsciously looked in the direction of the Eight Peaks.

Duanmu Zhang, however, maintained a calm expression. "It matters not."

What Chu Xuangu was worried about was obvious: the high command of the Myriad Manifestations Sect!

After the Myriad Manifestations Sect changed its Sect Master, the entire sect's strategy toward the Confucian cultivator community had also changed.

Qin De had been deliberately kept alive. Over the past years, he had become the perfect tool for the sect's higher-ups, firmly suppressing the development and expansion of the Confucian cultivators.

Now that this "tool" was clearly about to be broken, how would the Myriad Manifestations Sect's high command react?

-----

Deep within the Cloud Prison.

Qin De sat withered, gazing at the darkness beyond the iron bars, his expression dazed.

He had been imprisoned in this cell for so many years, stumping Duanmu Zhang and countless other Confucian scholars. But today, he had finally met his "nemesis." His self-created Sage Great Thief Sutra, the philosophy he was so proud of, had been refuted to the point of complete ruin, nearly suffering a total collapse.

If not for a sudden flash of wit at the end - a lucky struggle using sophistry - he would have been completely debated down today!

Zhao Hansheng’s parting words echoed in his ears. The man would definitely return within three days.

"But why would he need three days? I fear once he calms down, he'll be able to break through this joint." Qin De smiled bitterly. "What use was that struggle of mine?"

"Heart Science..."

"Truly profound, beyond words!"

"Just from this brief debate, I have already benefited greatly. Is the Sage Great Thief Sutra ultimately just a delusional dream?"

Just as Qin De was filled with doubt and confusion, a voice suddenly sounded in his ear: "There is no need to belittle yourself, Little Friend Qin De. For you to persist this far in the face of Heart Science is already rare."

Qin De’s body shook, and he turned his head sharply.

In the shadows of the prison cell's corner, a person had appeared at some unknown time.

The man wore grey robes, had white hair and a white beard, and a lean face. It was none other than Xiao Juxia.

"Who are you?" Qin De asked warily.

Xiao Juxia smiled slightly. "Here to fix the cell."

He looked Qin De up and down, his gaze filled with keen interest. "I didn't expect to watch such a show."

A brilliant light burst from Qin De's eyes. In his urgency, he wanted to stand up, but he quickly suppressed the impulse.

He hurriedly glanced back outside the iron bars, seeing no reaction, then looked back at Xiao Juxia. "Why has Your Excellency come?"

In Qin De’s eyes, Xiao Juxia was unfathomable!

Because he knew very well that this was the Cloud Prison, a heavy prison controlled by the Demon Subjugation Hall. To be able to sneak in so unknowingly and reveal himself right before his eyes - such a bold and composed demeanor was definitely not something an ordinary cultivator could achieve.

Xiao Juxia did not answer Qin De’s question. Instead, he walked out of the shadows and came to Qin De’s side.

Seeing him act so boldly, Qin De’s heart hung in his throat, but he soon discovered with surprise that Xiao Juxia’s actions did not attract any reaction from the Demon Subjugation Hall.

Xiao Juxia said, "Your Sage Great Thief Sutra is indeed unique and has great character. Very good. To be able to interpret Confucian classics to this extent is rare in this world."

Qin De smiled bitterly. "Since you saw the 'show,' you know I actually lost miserably. Why mock me with such words?"

Xiao Juxia replied, "I appreciate you. The reason you suffered a crushing defeat is not that you lack talent, but because you haven't truly drawn from others' strengths. The Sage Great Thief Sutra is a draft; it still lacks much. There is one thing in particular you are severely lacking. If you possessed it, you would surely defeat Zhao Hansheng!"

Qin De was stunned, then asked urgently, "What is it?"

Xiao Juxia smiled. "The Demonic Path."

Qin De was stunned again. "The Demonic Path?"

He spread his hands to show his confusion. "When I first created the Sage Great Thief Sutra, I also referenced many demonic art classics within the Myriad Manifestations Sect..."

Xiao Juxia shook his head, interrupting, "You are proficient in Confucian classics, so you interpret the Sage through Confucianism and use the Sage to explain the Thief, reaching a state of perfection. Perhaps you have borrowed from demonic arts, but in reality, your understanding of the Demonic Path is too superficial."

"Zhao Hansheng’s 'Heart is Principle' made it hard for you to parry. But if you supplement it with the Demonic Path, it will be completely different. Heart is Principle; Demon is Desire. Only when Principle and Desire contend can the truth be seen. Zhao Hansheng’s Heart Science talks only of the Heart, not of Desire. If you can introduce Desire into the Heart and verify the Heart through Desire, why worry about not defeating Zhao Hansheng?"

Hearing this, Qin De’s heart shook violently.

He recalled the debate from earlier, and a wild light shot from his eyes.

"That's right!"

"Heart Science speaks of 'removing desire to preserve principle,' but without desire, where does principle come from? The struggle between desire and principle is inherently two sides of the same coin. If I can introduce desire into principle and verify principle through desire, then the foundation of Heart Science will surely be shaken."

The more Qin De spoke, the more excited he became.

Receiving Xiao Juxia’s pointer was like a drowning man clutching a life-saving straw.

He stood up, his shackles and chains clanking loudly, and bowed deeply. "Your Excellency... no, Senior, please instruct me."

Xiao Juxia laughed heartily, pointed at the ground, and sat down cross-legged first. "Since you want to learn, I will teach you a demonic art. After hearing it, you can decide whether or not to switch your cultivation to it."

Qin De frowned upon hearing this. He was already at the Golden Core stage; how could he change his cultivation method? But the person before him was unfathomable; perhaps he had powerful means to break conventional rules.

Qin De followed Xiao Juxia and sat on the ground.

Xiao Juxia’s face became solemn. "Listen well. This technique is called - Ten Thousand Laws Fallen Demon Art!"

The night grew deeper.

Xiao Juxia taught with care, and Qin De listened with increasing absorption. A look of shock and wild ecstasy gradually filled his face.

Meanwhile, in the dead of night, Wang Yu paid a secret visit to Zhong Dao.

Demon Subjugation Hall, Main Hall.

Most of the lights in the great hall had been extinguished. Only a solitary lamp remained lit behind the massive profound iron desk at the head of the hall.

Under the lamp, Zhong Dao sat as steady as a mountain, his broad shoulders and back perfectly straight, like an iron tower that never bends.

He held a scroll in his hands. The deep vertical lines between his brows looked even more like they were carved by a knife or axe under the lamplight. The edges of the desk were sharp, just as distinct as the man himself.

Suddenly, he looked up.

A figure slowly emerged.

This person wore a cloud-patterned Daoist robe, upon which crimson rosy clouds flowed, remaining conspicuous even in the night. He nodded with a smile, appearing confident and composed, as if this late-night visit was by invitation.

It was none other than the current Peak Master of Danxia Peak - Wang Yu.

"Hall Master Zhong is still reviewing scrolls late at night. Truly diligent," Wang Yu said as he walked to the desk, not sparing his praise.

Zhong Dao put down the scroll, his sharp gaze falling on Wang Yu’s face. "Peak Master Wang, visiting so late at night - what is the matter?"

Wang Yu smiled slightly, flicked his whisk gently, and slowly walked up the dais to stand before Zhong Dao. The crimson clouds on his robe brightened and dimmed with his breathing.

"There is an old case I wish to discuss with Hall Master Zhong," Wang Yu said.

Zhong Dao’s brows twitched. "An old case? Related to my Demon Subjugation Hall?"

"Whether it is related to the Demon Subjugation Hall is yet unknown," Wang Yu said with deep meaning, "but it is certainly related to the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

Zhong Dao snorted coldly. "My Demon Subjugation Hall is a division of the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Speak. What old case is it that requires the labor of a dignified Peak Master like you?"

Wang Yu then recounted a story: "Years ago, my Danxia Peak accepted a batch of orders placed by the Royal Family of the Qingping Kingdom. However, after my peak delivered all the refined medicinal pills according to the order, the Qingping Royal Family did not fulfill their contract. Does Hall Master Zhong know why?"

Zhong Dao frowned deeply, his expression impatient. "Wang Yu, this is the Demon Subjugation Hall. I won't collect debt payments for you. You know my temperament - speak plainly!"

Wang Yu smiled slightly, not annoyed in the least. He simply placed a jade slip gently on the desk and pushed it toward Zhong Dao.

"Hall Master Zhong might as well take a look first."

Zhong Dao fell silent for a few breaths before finally reaching out to take the jade slip.

As his divine sense delved into it, a fierce light flashed in Zhong Dao’s eyes.

It turned out that back then, the Qingping Royal Family hadn't intentionally delayed Danxia Peak's payment; rather, they had been wiped out.

The reason for their destruction was internal political strife.

The previous ruler of the Qingping Kingdom had actively introduced Confucian cultivators, appointing a large number of them as officials. Under the guise of "education" and the great cause of "enlightening the people," the Confucian cultivators infiltrated the higher levels of state power step by step.

First, they established academies to teach Confucian classics; then, they advised the monarch to transform the court with Confucian etiquette and law; finally, they fully implemented the Confucian system, even declaring the original Hundred Schools of Thought as "heresy" and strictly forbidding their practice.

In just twenty short years, the Qingping Kingdom was unrecognizable.

The cultivation world, once blooming with a hundred flowers, had turned into a domain solely for Confucian cultivators. Those cultivators who refused to change their ways were either expelled, imprisoned, or simply disappeared.

The new ruler of the Qingping Kingdom, unwilling to see power slip away, gathered forces in an attempt to restore order, but failed completely.

The current Qingping Kingdom could be seen entirely as a miniature version of the Huazhang Kingdom!

Zhong Dao looked up at Wang Yu, expressionless. "I fail to see how this matter is related to my Demon Subjugation Hall."

Wang Yu smiled, gently flicking his whisk. "If we speak of relations, the mastermind leading the reform in the Qingping Kingdom was precisely a Great Scholar from the Huazhang Kingdom. He is as famous as Duanmu Zhang and Zhao Hansheng."

Zhong Dao pressed, "What does this have to do with my Demon Subjugation Hall?"

Wang Yu’s smile did not diminish, ignoring the increasingly heavy atmosphere. "The change in the Qingping Kingdom was not the work of a single day. The ambition of the Huazhang Kingdom is not the scheme of a single person. Tracing it to the source, the fact that my peak’s payment was not collected is also the responsibility of the Confucian cultivators."

"Qin De, in the Cloud Prison, is a Confucian cultivator and is very likely involved in this matter."

"I request that Hall Master Zhong sign the order allowing me to interrogate Qin De and strictly investigate this matter."

"Heh heh heh." Zhong Dao laughed coldly in anger. "Where is the evidence?"

Wang Yu looked straight at Zhong Dao. "After I interrogate him, there will be evidence!"

Zhong Dao’s gaze was as sharp as a blade. "I fear the only 'proof' will be a corpse."

He had already seen through Wang Yu’s intent.

The talk of medicinal pill payments was just an excuse.

Wang Yu brought it up deliberately to reveal a fact to Zhong Dao - wherever Confucian cultivators go, the state will cease to be a state!

Years ago, it was precisely because of the coup in the Qingping Kingdom that the Myriad Manifestations Sect Master, Dong Chen, Danxia Peak Master Wang Yu, and others changed their attitude toward the Confucian community.

Afterward, the high command of the Myriad Manifestations Sect used Qin De to suppress the Confucian community, stifling their development until now.

Today, Zhao Hansheng had nearly completely debated Qin De into submission.

Once he was defeated openly, the shackles on the development of the Confucian community would be swept away. This was a situation the Myriad Manifestations Sect's high command did not wish to see.

Therefore, Wang Yu had come late at night to take away the prisoner Qin De. Nominally, it was for interrogation, but in reality, it was to dispose of him!
