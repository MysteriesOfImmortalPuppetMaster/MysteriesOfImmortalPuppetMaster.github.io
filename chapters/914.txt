Chapter 914: Assorted Congratulatory Gifts

Ning Zhuo looked surprised.

This was the first time someone had written a poem in public to congratulate him.

"This is truly a special congratulatory gift, thank you Brother Bai..." Ning Zhuo's expression of gratitude was interrupted.

His heart stirred as he suddenly realized a certain connection had formed between himself and the poem.

Immediately after, the poem emitted a profound light that reflected onto Ning Zhuo's body.

Everyone present witnessed this scene and was visibly moved.

In that instant, Ning Zhuo heard a clear, echoing dragon roar.

Promptly, a wisp of golden light radiated from within his body. At first, the light was very faint, seemingly there yet not there, like the distant, indistinct fish-belly white of the sky just before dawn.

But quickly, the golden light surged, bursting forth from every inch of Ning Zhuo's skin and every pore, enveloping his entire being in a warm, dazzling brilliance.

The whole room was brilliantly illuminated.

After reaching its peak, the golden light retracted, collapsing from the edges toward the center, ring by ring, layer by layer, until it fully concentrated on the surface of Ning Zhuo's body.

The previously scattered golden light solidified and compressed, turning into strips of golden lining.

The edges of Ning Zhuo's robes, his collar, cuffs, the seams of his belt, and even the outline of his face - every boundary was plated with an extremely fine, thin layer of golden light patterns. The golden lining didn't flicker or dance; it was as steady as fine brushwork meticulously outlined stroke by stroke using the finest brush dipped in the purest gold dust.

Ning Zhuo slowly raised his hands and looked down at his palms.

The edges of his palms also had a faint ring of gold.

At the same time, he could still hear the dragon's roar. This bizarre sound was actually erupting from the deepest part of his body, constantly rippling outward and bringing him an amplification of power.

"This is..." Ning Zhuo was endlessly surprised.

Liu Fushu, however, looked at Bai Jiyun with an expression full of admiration, blurting out in praise, "As expected of Senior Bai. Good poem, truly a good poem!"

Kong Ran slightly parted his lips. "This is profound imagery triggering a resonance of Literary Qi. The Confucian cultivator writing the poem and the subject being described must be highly compatible and fitting to form a resonance, thus achieving the buffing effect of Literary Qi."

"What is the specific buff?" Ning Zhuo asked curiously.

Kong Ran shook his head.

Ning Zhuo then looked toward Bai Jiyun, only for the latter to reply with a bitter smile, "This situation is a serendipitous occurrence, and literature has no fixed form. As for the specific effect, Ning Zhuo, you will have to test it out personally to figure it out."

"Understood." Ning Zhuo laughed, took a step back, and cupped his hands in a salute to Bai Jiyun, his expression highly sincere. "I, Ning Zhuo, will remember this poem and this gift."

Bai Jiyun also burst into laughter, then added, "If not for the congratulatory gifts of my two worthy younger brothers, Kong Ran and Liu Fushu, I wouldn't have been able to compose this poem either."

Kong Ran held his head high. "That is only natural."

Liu Fushu smiled.

Ning Zhuo took the opportunity to express his thanks to Kong Ran and Liu Fushu once more.

Kong Ran secretly sent a telepathic message using his divine sense: "Brother Ning, if you have free time tonight, we need to chat in private regarding the restoration of the Nanming Fire Furnace."

Ning Zhuo's gaze flickered slightly as he thought of something, and he promptly replied, "Alright."

"Three worthy brothers, please take a seat. There are no seating restrictions." Ning Zhuo extended his arm.

Kong, Bai, and Liu temporarily took their leave. They looked around the room and unanimously chose to sit in a corner.

They certainly wouldn't sit at the main table in the center of the hall, knowing they didn't have the qualifications for it. They didn't even want to sit at the several side tables adjacent to the main table.

Confucian cultivators liked to observe from the sidelines and rarely involved themselves directly in situations. The three of them sitting in the corner deeply aligned with the demeanor of a Confucian cultivator.

Bu Dingsun, Jiang Xiaobian, Zhang Dadan, and Huo Nian'er A'Huo walked into the hall together.

It was obviously their first time at the Tianzhen Pavilion, and their eyes were drawn to the restaurant's decor. But soon, they spotted Ning Zhuo and hurried forward.

"Young Master... Ning Zhuo." They had never seen Ning Zhuo look like this before.

Ning Zhuo had previously eaten at the same table with them, but his current appearance was like an unadorned piece of rough jade that had suddenly been polished open to reveal the jade within - warm, translucent, and precious.

Ning Zhuo revealed a warm smile, spread his arms, and took the initiative to walk toward the four. "I am very happy that you could all come to witness the ceremony."

"It is an honor for us to receive an invitation!" Bu Dingsun said.

"Th-this is my congratulatory gift," Zhang Dadan said.

Jiang Xiaobian clenched her fists, unable to speak for a moment.

A'Huo's Adam's apple bobbed. "L-Lord Ning Zhuo."

Ning Zhuo quickly waved his hand. "I'm younger than you, so just calling me by my name is fine. I'm very grateful you could come and support me."

Ning Zhuo's response made the four nervous individuals realize that he was just as approachable as ever, causing them to relax a little.

The four of them successively took out their congratulatory gifts, all of which were small mechanisms.

Ning Zhuo expressed his thanks. "Please take your seats, the four of you. There is no need to be formal, just make yourselves at home."

The four chose another secluded corner to sit down.

"It's all Spirit Food!" Jiang Xiaobian took a bite of the pastry in front of her, and her eyes immediately lit up. "Delicious, delicious. You guys try some too."

"Really?" Zhang Dadan also took a bite, and his expression instantly changed.

Bu Dingsun took a sip of tea and immediately felt his body relax and his mind refresh.

A'Huo stared at Ning Zhuo, his face full of reverence.

Liu Fushu shifted his gaze back from the four of them. He sent a message using his divine sense, filled with emotion, "Although Ning Zhuo is a noble young master of a prominent clan, he is different from the others in one regard. He always treats lower-level cultivators as equals."

Kong Ran said, "I know of them. When Ning Zhuo first arrived and participated in Qing Huangzi's Cloud-Rising Minor Trial, he befriended them. At the time, they interacted as peers. Now that Ning Zhuo has revealed his true colors, they finally realize the gap between them."

Bai Jiyun sighed, "Ning Zhuo is a true gentleman. He is not like those young masters and misses from noble families who fish for fame by putting on a show of deliberately getting close to the lower class. I can feel that he genuinely considers these people his friends from the bottom of his heart."

They did not know that Ning Zhuo could also be considered to be from the lower echelons. Since childhood, he hadn't been favored by his clan. He mixed in the streets with Sun Lingtong and experienced the harsh realities of the world. Therefore, when treating these people, Ning Zhuo never had a superiority complex.

While they were discussing, a young cultivator stood at the edge of the privacy screen. He carefully leaned his upper body over to peek inside. Seeing many eyes looking back at him, he immediately shrank back.

After taking a deep breath, he finally stepped forward holding an invitation, formally entering the hall.

He had a square jaw and wide mouth, broad shoulders and a thick back. His skin was bronze-colored, his clothes were ordinary, and he wore a pair of cloth shoes on his feet.

Cao Gui, Kong, Liu, Bai, A'Huo, and the others didn't recognize this person.

"It's him." Ning Zhuo's heart stirred. He had an impression of this person and immediately understood where he came from.

When the young cultivator saw Ning Zhuo, he felt dazzled and trotted forward. "T-this lowly one is Niu Cheng, entrusted by Senior Yu to come and observe t-the ceremony."

"Young Master Ning Zhuo." Niu Cheng raised a treasure box to the height of his eyebrows. "Th-this is Senior Yu Heye's congratulatory gift. The senior has urgent matters and cannot come in person, so he specifically ordered me to deliver this on his behalf."

His phrasing was a bit more fluent than before; he had likely rehearsed these lines in his mind many times.

Ning Zhuo took the treasure box and opened it to take a look. To his surprise, it was a Golden Core-level magic treasure. Besides that, there was also a letter.

Ning Zhuo didn't need to read the letter to know that Yu Heye must have sincerely apologized in it.

"This congratulatory gift is precious; Fellow Daoist Yu is very thoughtful," Ning Zhuo smiled. "Fellow Daoist Niu Cheng, go ahead and sit wherever you please."

Niu Cheng awkwardly retreated.

Ning Zhuo had also sent an invitation to Yu Heye, having even saved him once in the past. If this person didn't show up, Ning Zhuo could immediately guess the reason. It was one of the scenarios he had anticipated, so he wasn't surprised.

As for Niu Cheng, it was originally Ning Zhuo's scheme that allowed Yu Heye to recruit him.

By using this person as a messenger, Yu Heye was also showing goodwill and apologizing to Ning Zhuo in a subtle way that outsiders couldn't discern.

Jiang Xiaobian looked at Niu Cheng and discussed with the people around her with curiosity and unease, "Who does this person represent? They actually gifted a magic treasure. Did we gift something too light?"

Bu Dingsun directly sent a telepathic message: "Of course not. There is an expert behind this person who was clearly invited but couldn't come. Therefore, they sent a heavy gift to express their apologies."

A'Huo said unhappily, "Young Master Ning Zhuo has a boundless future. To send an invitation and only get a representative in return is too arrogant."

Speaking of which, this Fire-Twister had once been at odds with Ning Zhuo, but he was quickly won over by him. In recent days, having repeatedly heard of and even witnessed Ning Zhuo taking first place in the minor tests and being in the unmatched limelight, he harbored a deep admiration and worship for Ning Zhuo.

A trace of worry appeared on Kong Ran's face.

He said to his two friends beside him, "Someone who can produce a magic treasure as a congratulatory gift and dispatch Niu Cheng must have a cultivation level of at least the Golden Core-level. Yet, he didn't show up in person."

"This isn't very good." Liu Fushu nodded, communicating secretly via divine sense. "Look around the hall; there are only us, and our cultivation levels are all at Foundation Establishment."

Kong Ran smiled bitterly. "Don't forget, I am merely a cultivator in the Qi Refining Stage."

If the only ones who showed up were their group, then Ning Zhuo's alliance-establishing ceremony this time would become a classic case of "all thunder and no rain," and it would likely become a laughingstock.

Although cultivation level was only one aspect of strength, the public still valued it highly in most situations.

Every level up in cultivation basically represented a different social status and treatment.

Ning Zhuo's ceremony this time absolutely needed heavyweights to participate; otherwise, he would lose face.

Cao Gui shared similar concerns, thinking to himself, "Doesn't Young Master Ning Zhuo still have his good friends Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong? Situ Xing might not attend, but if those two young masters from major clans arrived, it would immediately alleviate the current awkwardness."

Ning Zhuo remained calm and collected.

He wasn't worried at all; right now, what he was pondering over was the magic treasure Yu Heye had gifted.

"I'm temporarily not lacking in magic treasures."

"Mother said that too much wealth is a burden, and going too far is as bad as not going far enough. But I feel like the people invited to observe the ceremony this time will probably all bring quite a few treasures."

"Sigh, I'll still have to figure out ways to resolve this hidden danger in the future."

Just as Ning Zhuo was having a happy dilemma, a cultivator strode proudly into the hall.

His aura had impressively reached the Golden Core-level!

This was the first Golden Core to arrive to watch the ceremony.

This Golden Core cultivator was tall and thin with greying temples. His facial features were deep as if carved by a knife, and his eye sockets were slightly sunken, yet his eyes were startlingly bright. His gaze was sharp as an eagle's, revealing the steadiness of someone who had weathered many storms and a lingering sense of gloomy isolation.

What was especially eye-catching were his long, snow-white eyebrows, the tips of which drooped down to his cheekbones.

He wore a washed-out navy blue martial suit, and a sheathless black iron sword hung at his waist. Pinpointing Ning Zhuo with a single glance, he strode boldly toward him.

A'Huo and the other three didn't recognize him.

But Kong Ran and Cao Gui both revealed expressions of surprise.

"It's actually the White-Browed Hero, Gu Huaijiu?"

"Ning Zhuo invited him? And he actually came?"

Gu Huaijiu cupped his fists. "Brother Ning Zhuo."

Ning Zhuo laughed heartily. "Brother Gu, you're here."

Gu Huaijiu looked Ning Zhuo up and down. "You have great bearing, brother. This is my congratulatory gift."

Gu Huaijiu's gift was a jade pendant.

"This is the 'Harbored Jade'. Channeling it allows you to sense surrounding treasures." Gu Huaijiu's introduction was very simple.

This was impressively a Golden Core-level magic treasure, but Gu Huaijiu downplayed it as if it were merely an ordinary item.

Bai Jiyun didn't know Gu Huaijiu, but he had heard of the Harbored Jade: "An innocent man is guilty only of harboring jade. The refining method for this Harbored Jade has already been lost. Every piece remaining in the world is a priceless treasure that cannot be bought on the market. Possessing a Harbored Jade represents an endless stream of treasures."

Liu Fushu also expressed surprise. "So this person is the White-Browed Hero, Gu Huaijiu. I've long heard of his reputation, and today I've finally seen him. How is his relationship with Ning Zhuo so close that he would actually use a Harbored Jade as a congratulatory gift?"

Kong Ran added, "According to the intelligence, Gu Huaijiu and Ning Zhuo participated together in the Demon Subjugation Hall's Cloud-Rising Second Test. It's just unexpected that their relationship is this intimate. You have to realize, Gu Huaijiu is about to join the Demon Subjugation Hall."

Ning Zhuo's establishing an alliance and rushing the peak this time was tantamount to rejecting Zhong Dao's recruitment. It was precisely out of apprehension about this that Lu Xuan didn't come to observe the ceremony.

Gu Huaijiu had no such misgivings. Not only did he come, but he also presented a heavy gift.

Just as Ning Zhuo was about to chat with him for a bit, another cultivator stepped into the hall.

This was an unfamiliar female Golden Core cultivator.

She appeared to be a young woman with a beautiful, refined face and skin like congealed fat. Her features exuded a delicate, gentle beauty like a fragile willow swaying in the wind.

Wearing a moon-white long shirt with narrow sleeves, a water-green half-sleeve jacket on the outside, and a light jade-green silk ribbon tied around her waist, she walked gracefully up to Ning Zhuo.

"Ye Qingming of the Green Tea Society, under the orders of the Society Head, has come to congratulate Young Master Ning Zhuo on the joy of establishing an alliance at the Tianzhen Pavilion."

Her eyes were cool and carried a hint of scrutiny.

Green Tea Society!

Ning Zhuo's heart trembled slightly. This was one of the Nascent Soul-level factions on Flowing Cloud Peak. He didn't expect them to be the first to dispatch an envoy.

To be able to enter here, they must have obtained an invitation from somewhere else.

This also demonstrated the methods of the Green Tea Society.

"Brother, go ahead and busy yourself first." Gu Huaijiu patted Ning Zhuo's shoulder and, before leaving, glanced at Ye Qingming.

Ye Qingming also glanced at Gu Huaijiu, her pupils shrinking slightly. She understood in her heart: Gu Huaijiu purposely calling Ning Zhuo "brother" was to display their intimate relationship and show his support for Ning Zhuo.

This weakened Ye Qingming's imposing aura somewhat.

She smiled faintly, took out a celadon tea box from her sleeve, and handed it to Ning Zhuo with one hand.

The tea box was no larger than a palm. Its glaze was warm and smooth like pale green jade, and a minimalist character for "Tea" was carved into the lid.

"This is Snow Sprout Spirit Tea, produced by the thousand-year tea tree within the Green Tea Society. Only twelve taels are harvested every year." Ye Qingming's voice remained soft and gentle. "The tea's aroma can clear the mind, brighten the eyes, and stabilize one's divine sense. A meager gift to show our respects."

Ning Zhuo expressed his thanks.

Ye Qingming continued, "Brewing this Snow Sprout Spirit Tea requires patience and mastery of the heat. If some people use an intense and hasty fire, the tea will turn bitter. Does the Young Master usually drink tea?"

A smile hung on Ning Zhuo's face, but a trace of coldness seeped through his gaze. "I like to build things up from scratch. I am currently in massive debt, so elegant matters like tea tasting have nothing to do with me."

"To be honest, I'm going to take this box of Snow Sprout Spirit Tea to sell. I wonder how much it can sell for - could it cover even half a percent of my debt?"

Ye Qingming's smile instantly stiffened.
