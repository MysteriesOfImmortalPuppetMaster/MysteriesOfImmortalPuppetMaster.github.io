Chapter 919: Please, Take the Seat of Honor

In the main hall of the Heaven's Treasure Pavilion, the atmosphere had undergone an earth-shattering change!

Even an ascetic cultivator like Li Ku had a change in expression at this moment.

He stared fixedly at the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, his heart greatly shaken.

As soon as this Nascent Soul-level great cultivator appeared, he immediately announced his intention to join the Nanming Stronghold.

Thus, the Nanming Stronghold had become a Nascent Soul-level major faction. The impact such strength brought to Flowing Cloud Peak was completely different from before.

Even the top three factions - the Thundercloud Society, Bird and Beast Villa, and Water-Severing Saber Pavilion - would not hold any contempt for the Nanming Stronghold.

In fact, if they knew this information, they definitely would have sent envoys over to observe the ceremony, scout things out in advance, and establish relations.

This was an absolute qualitative change.

Jin Mantang and Ye Qingming also wore highly solemn expressions.

The two exchanged a glance, and Jin Mantang spoke up deliberately, "Daoist Ye, there is a matter I do not understand. The Nine Fires Dragon Lord... Senior is a Guest Elder of Danxia Peak. If he joins the Nanming Stronghold to challenge Flowing Cloud Peak, then what of this status?"

Ye Qingming knew what Jin Mantang meant. After a thought, she decided to play along with his little act.

Thus, she replied, "According to our sect's rules, sect cultivators cannot hold multiple concurrent statuses across the Eight Peaks and Sixteen Halls. If Lord Nine Fires Dragon Lord wishes to join the Nanming Stronghold, he must first have his status as a Danxia Peak Guest Elder removed."

Although the two conversed in low voices, their words were just loud enough to carry into the ears of the others in the hall.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord also heard them and immediately shot a furious glare at the pair.

Ye Qingming and Jin Mantang instantly fell as silent as winter cicadas, sitting rigidly and properly, not daring to speak another word.

The further one progressed in Cultivation, the wider and deeper the corresponding chasm between cultivation levels and strength became.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was still extremely irritated.

In his view, his status as a Guest Elder of Danxia Peak was far more renowned and valuable than the position of Grand Chieftain of whatever Nanming Stronghold.

However, he was heavily in debt and had been forced to do this!

Ning Zhuo extended his palm. "Lord Nine Fires Dragon Lord, please take a seat first."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord cast a sideways glance at Ning Zhuo, then turned and strode towards the main table with his head held high.

As the only Nascent Soul cultivator in the hall, everyone felt it was only natural for him to put on such airs.

Kong Ran was filled with excitement, just short of clapping his hands together as he thought to himself, "Brother Ning's alliance formation is a success!"

Bai Jiyun let out a sigh of marvel.

Liu Fushu cast a gaze of envy and longing toward Ning Zhuo.

The three young masters from major clans, temporarily sharing a table with them, all looked at each other in dismay.

Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong both looked toward Situ Xing.

Situ Xing's expression was complex, showing hints of temptation, hesitation, and conflict.

"Although a Nascent Soul cultivator has joined, it's... a Demon Cultivator."

Even if the Nine Fires Dragon Lord belonged to the Dragon Clan, he was still a Demon Cultivator.

The world today was ruled by Humans. Even the Dragon Clan was considered a step below.

If Situ Xing were a rogue cultivator, his misgivings wouldn't be so great. But his every move represented the face of the Situ Clan. As a Super Sect and a prominent clan famous throughout the country, it would be bad for their reputation if their outstanding younger generation were seen taking orders under a Demon Cultivator.

Meanwhile, Dong Nishang, who sat alone at a table, did not have Situ Xing's concerns, nor had she ever considered joining the Nanming Stronghold.

But at this moment, she also felt a trace of hidden worry for Ning Zhuo's future.

"Ning Zhuo has a bright, limitless future. If he eventually becomes a Nascent Soul cultivator and rules a region, then his experience of following a Demon Cultivator's orders in his youth might become a not-insignificant stain on his record."

In comparison, Gu Huaijiu did not think that deeply.

He glanced at the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, raised his teacup, and offered a toast from afar to Ning Zhuo.

He was genuinely happy for Ning Zhuo.

At another table, Ao Pan also made the same gesture.

In his eyes, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's participation meant that Ning Zhuo's alliance formation was already a success. Ning Zhuo would now have a Nascent Soul-level great cultivator backing him!

This instantly raised Ao Pan's evaluation of Ning Zhuo by several levels, and he constantly pondered how to draw closer and establish a deeper relationship with Ning Zhuo.

Che Zhuzi and Lu Zeng also had faces full of joy.

The two were essentially merchants; they felt that the better their friends fared, the more profit it could bring them.

Cao Gui was very excited.

The reason he was valued by his clan and the Commerce Hall was precisely because of his personal friendship with Ning Zhuo. Now that Ning Zhuo's value was soaring, Cao Gui's value naturally rose with it.

Niu Cheng, however, couldn't understand these convoluted twists and turns. He secretly cast a reverent glance at the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, but after stealing just one peek, he averted his eyes, not daring to look further.

His heart pounded rapidly, filling him with excitement. "Good heavens, I'm actually dining with a Nascent Soul Lord."

The one having the hardest time was undoubtedly Liu Jinke.

At this moment, his face was as sunken as deep water.

With the Nine Fires Dragon Lord joining, if Liu Jinke wanted to deal with Ning Zhuo, it meant standing in opposition to the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, offending a Nascent Soul great cultivator!

"Damn it," Liu Jinke gritted his teeth.

The Demon Subjugation Hall had actually informed him long ago that he might face pressure at the Nascent Soul-level.

Liu Jinke had thought to beat Ning Zhuo up in advance, avoiding the subsequent joining of any Nascent Soul-level cultivators. This way, he could avoid offending a Nascent Soul cultivator while still repaying his favor to the Demon Subjugation Hall.

But this opportunistic move had now completely failed.

Previously, Liu Jinke had held onto a sliver of luck, thinking that no Nascent Soul cultivator would actually join the Nanming Stronghold. Now, his heart was entirely cold, and he was deeply perplexed.

"How did this demon dragon decide to join so quickly?"

Little did he know, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's mindset was actually quite similar to his - they both just wanted to get it over with quickly!

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord thought: Since he had already received orders from the figure in the black market, he absolutely had to join the Nanming Stronghold. In that case, short pain was better than long pain; he might as well get it done quickly and be over with it.

That was what he thought, and that was what he did.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord sat at the main table, pinched some tea pastries, and tossed them into his mouth. After eating a few bites, he revealed a look of dissatisfaction. "These petty snacks aren't even enough to fill the gaps between my teeth. Bring out the good wine and good dishes!"

"Why does his table have wine and food?"

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord looked in dissatisfaction at Liu Jinke.

Liu Jinke felt as though he was sitting on pins and needles. He was arrogant toward Ning Zhuo, but facing the irritation of a Nascent Soul great cultivator, he didn't dare put on airs.

He was just about to answer, but when the words reached his lips, he couldn't spit them out.

How should he explain it?

Was he supposed to tell the truth and inform the Nine Fires Dragon Lord that this was the "last meal before execution" Ning Zhuo had arranged for him?

How could he possibly say that out loud?!

In the moment Liu Jinke hesitated, Gu Huaijiu on the side explained it for him, succinctly telling the Nine Fires Dragon Lord the truth in just a few words.

Lu Ban, Che Zhuzi, and the rest were merchants; they wouldn't do something that offended people like this.

Kong Ran and the others were merely Foundation Establishment cultivators, as well as Confucian cultivators, so they wouldn't easily offend someone openly.

As for Situ Xing and the others, they had the courage to offend a Golden Core cultivator, but they had to consider their own factions and their clan's stance.

Gu Huaijiu, on the other hand, was a lone wolf, and he had a reincarnation pact with Ning Zhuo. Although their time together was brief, they had already entrusted their lives to each other. This relationship gave Gu Huaijiu the reason to speak up for Ning Zhuo.

This saved Ning Zhuo a bit of trouble.

After all, if Ning Zhuo were to explain it himself, it would damage his image on the Righteous Path and make him appear aggressively overbearing.

"A last meal before execution? Hahaha!" The Nine Fires Dragon Lord laughed heartily, feeling much more refreshed.

Liu Jinke's face grew even gloomier.

But facing the mockery of a Nascent Soul great cultivator, he had no choice but to endure it. For a time, he could only sit frozen in his seat, practically "unable to move," being publicly humiliated!

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord laughed for a bit, then suddenly knitted his brows tightly.

He stared at Ning Zhuo: "You signed a life-and-death duel with him. If you lose and forfeit your life, what happens to the Nanming Stronghold?"

Ning Zhuo spread his hands. "Lord Dragon Lord, I formed this alliance and am challenging the peak in order to better repay my debts. If I really lose my life, then even if the debt reaches the heavens, there is nothing I can do to help."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's brows twisted into knots. He looked at Ning Zhuo, gritted his teeth, and then turned his head to stare fixedly at Liu Jinke.

At that instant, sweat dripped from Liu Jinke's forehead!

The gaze of a Nascent Soul cultivator, moreover one brimming with hostility, caused the pressure on him to spike drastically.

Seeing this scene, Ning Zhuo laughed out loud. "Lord Dragon Lord, set your mind at ease. Since I took the initiative to sign the life-and-death duel contract, I naturally have enough confidence in my victory."

"Furthermore, once my Nanming Stronghold prepares to challenge the peak, we will naturally suffer all sorts of challenges."

"When the time comes, a core figure like myself will inevitably face all kinds of 'weighing.' Such probing will definitely not be scarce, so I ought to bravely face them, accept these various challenges, and display my caliber to the world."

"Only in this way can I be worthy of the status as the founder of the 'Nanming Stronghold,' no?"

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord snorted coldly and said no more, considering it a tacit agreement.

Because of Ning Zhuo's words, the hall fell into a profound silence.

-----

Meanwhile.

On a nearby mountain peak, Ban Jie and her entourage had already set up a simple pavilion. From here, they could receive information concerning the Heaven's Treasure Pavilion at the first possible moment.

"The Nine Fires Dragon Lord has already announced his participation?!" Ban Jie's face was clouded with gloom, and the fingers gripping the intelligence Jade Slip turned slightly white from the force.

She was extremely clear that there was a qualitative gap between Nascent Soul and Golden Core.

In the current golden age of Cultivation, Nascent Soul cultivators could often dominate a region. The strong among Nascent Soul experts were the mainstays within cultivation nations.

This was because the further along in cultivation, the greater the disparity between individuals.

Examples of successful Foundation Establishment challenges against Golden Cores could often be seen among genius cultivators. But when it came to a Golden Core challenging a Nascent Soul, the number of successes was not even ten percent of those of Foundation Establishment challenging Golden Core!

Ban Jie knitted her brows tightly, a look of worry on her face. "With the Nine Fires Dragon Lord joining, Ning Zhuo's alliance formation is already a success! Will there be other Nascent Souls joining next?"

She was thinking of Ban Ji; the greater the enemy's momentum, the more disadvantageous it would be for Ban Ji.

Ban Jie desperately wished that the Nine Fires Dragon Lord was the one and only Nascent Soul cultivator in the Nanming Stronghold.

"What happens next is the key."

"If there really are Nascent Soul cultivators with the intention of joining, they will surely hurry to show themselves."

"The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's move has already brought this ceremony to its climax!"

As the saying goes, great minds think alike; Han Muma held the exact same view as Ban Ji.

"Not good. It seems I must hurry and make my appearance!" Han Muma felt a great sense of tension.

He had been dispatched by Tuoba Huang to serve as an envoy for the ceremony, primarily to strictly prevent Cang Yazi from doing something stupid - leaving Myriad Beast Peak to join the Nanming Stronghold.

Han Muma made his appearance.

He did not conceal his own Nascent Soul aura.

Seeing an unfamiliar Nascent Soul approaching, Gongsun Yan grew extremely nervous and braced himself to step in front of Han Muma to block him.

Just as he was about to speak, Han Muma handed him an invitation.

Gongsun Yan breathed a sigh of relief. He accepted the invitation, verified its authenticity, and then yielded the way: "Senior, please."

Thus, Han Muma slowly stepped into the main hall of the Heaven's Treasure Pavilion.

He wore a grayish-brown leather robe with white fur trimming the edges. A broad leather belt was tied around his waist, and hanging from the belt were a curved saber, a fire striker, a leather pouch, and a palm-sized bronze bell.

He possessed a thin, gaunt figure with slightly hunched shoulders. His face was long and narrow, his eyes slender with the corners slightly upturned. He had high cheekbones and a sharp, pointed chin.

His skin was almost a deep bronze color, saturated with the marks left by year-round exposure to the wind and sun, as rough as the bark of an old tree.

His hair, a mix of gray and white, was woven into dozens of thin braids draped over his back. Tied to the ends of the braids were various beast fangs and copper rings, which made soft clinking sounds as he moved.

Lin Jinglong's pupils constricted slightly as he became the first to recognize Han Muma's identity.

Following that, more people recognized him.

Han Muma was also famous on Myriad Beast Peak, particularly renowned for his horse-breeding skills. He had spent his entire life in the company of horses, taming countless wild steeds and breeding countless fine stallions.

Han Muma arrived in front of Ning Zhuo, offered his congratulations, and then took the bronze bell from his waist belt and handed it to Ning Zhuo.

"This is a congratulatory gift."

"The magic treasure is named the Heart Snake Horse-Tethering Bell."

"To activate this bell, apart from spiritual energy and divine sense, one must also possess spirit communication arts. The stronger the communication method, the more the bell's sound can pierce directly into the horse's heart, allowing for communication, and even taming."

The bronze bell was the size of a palm and tinted completely in blue-yellow. Its surface was covered in dense, fine lines, resembling green and yellow grass intertwining. Engraved on the inner side of the bell were intricate Runes, ancient and profound. The clapper of the bell was a curled-up little snake, pitch-black all over with its eyes shut tight, as if in deep slumber.

Ning Zhuo accepted the bronze bell with both hands, finding it icy cold to the touch.

When he probed slightly with his divine sense, he immediately felt waves of an ancient and boundless power, like the howling gales sweeping across a great prairie.

Han Muma continued, "Before I came, the Peak Master personally instructed that if Little Friend Ning Zhuo has the intention to join Myriad Beast Peak in the future, you may take this bell to any horse farm belonging to Myriad Beast Peak and choose any steed. As long as you successfully establish spirit communication, you may lead it away and claim it as your personal property."

Hearing these words, Cao Gui couldn't help but gasp in shock.

Even Situ Xing, Shen Xi, and Lin Jinglong couldn't help but feel somewhat envious.

The same went for Zhu Fenxiang.

Even if Ning Zhuo didn't know the exact value of the gift, just seeing the changes in the expressions of these major clan disciples told him a thing or two.

He immediately put away the bronze bell, solemnly thanked Han Muma, and invited him to take any seat.

Confirming that Han Muma was only there to observe the ceremony and not to join the alliance, Liu Jinke breathed a sigh of relief.

Ye Qingming and Jin Mantang also relaxed slightly.

"I... I want to join the Nanming Stronghold." A voice suddenly rang out.

Everyone in the hall was startled. None of them had even noticed someone stepping into the hall.

Numerous gazes darted toward the owner of the voice, only to see a youth in yellow clothes, with ordinary features and ordinary attire.

The youth in yellow held an invitation in his hand, still standing near the screen by the doorway, as he announced once more, "I want to join. Where do I sit?"

He asked Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo was momentarily stunned. It was his first time seeing this youth in yellow, and he was completely unfamiliar. Thus, Ning Zhuo cupped his fists and asked, "May I ask who Your Excellency is?"

But the yellow-clothed youth shook his head. "I don't want to say much. I want to join the Nanming Stronghold."

Ning Zhuo frowned slightly, just as he was about to change his tact and continue questioning.

The yellow-clothed youth said, "I am a Nascent Soul-level cultivator. Where should I sit?"

Saying this, he stopped concealing himself and leaked out his aura.

Another Nascent Soul-level Demon Cultivator?!!

Everyone's hearts jolted simultaneously in shock.

Ning Zhuo, on the other hand, straightforwardly declared, "For Your Lordship to join is the fortune of our stronghold. Please, quickly take the seat of honor! Quickly, please take the seat of honor!"
