Chapter 916: Jin Mantang

Kong Ran looked at Situ Xing and the other two, suddenly sending a message via divine sense: "Brother Bai, Brother Liu, let's go chat over there."

Bai Jiyun thought for a moment and nodded. He had previously traveled through the Flying Cloud Kingdom and had been hosted by the Lin and Situ clans. Chatting with Situ Xing and his companions would be quite interesting.

Seeing that both of them had this intention, Liu Fushu naturally didn't want to remain in his original seat alone, which would make him appear aloof and unsociable.

The three immediately took action, walking over to Situ Xing's group. Received warmly, they sat down to chat. For a time, the hall became much livelier.

Che Zhuzi also no longer had to occasionally force an awkward laugh.

Jin Mantang, as the fifth Golden Core to arrive, stepped into the main hall.

The moment he walked in, the candlelight in the hall seemed to brighten a few degrees - not because of the broad smile on his face, but because of the light reflecting off his golden brocade robe.

The robe was embroidered with copper coin patterns that sparkled dazzlingly in the candlelight, enough to make one dizzy. Around his waist was tied a golden belt adorned with Spirit Stones. The Spirit Stones, large and small, were densely packed together, clinking against each other and emitting faint, crisp sounds as he walked.

"Greetings, fellow Daoists!" He cupped his hands in greeting as he walked in.

Then, he stopped in front of Ning Zhuo, clasping his fists in a formal salute: "Young Master Ning Zhuo! I am Jin Mantang of the Floating Life Society. By the orders of our Society President, I have come specifically to offer congratulations!"

Resplendent with jewels and wealth!

This was Ning Zhuo's first, very profound impression of Jin Mantang.

The youth carefully observed him and saw that Jin Mantang was portly, with a round face, large ears, and a double chin.

His skin was fair and delicate, extremely well-maintained, looking like someone who had never suffered hardships or endured exhaustion in his life.

His eyes were squeezed into two narrow slits by the flesh on his face, but his eyeballs darted around rapidly within those slits, revealing an undisguised shrewdness.

Ning Zhuo also cupped his hands: "Fellow Daoist Jin Mantang."

Jin Mantang's smile widened: "Congratulations, congratulations! Establishing an alliance at the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion is a major event! For the Young Master to achieve so much at such a young age, your future is limitless! Everyone in the Floating Life Society thinks very highly of you."

"Therefore, our President specifically asked me to bring a generous gift!"

He took out a square box wrapped in brocade from his sleeve. Opening the lid, he revealed a stack of jade tablets inside.

These jade tablets were an emerald green throughout, edged in gold. The words "Floating Life Society" were carved on the front, while the back was covered in dense, fine lines, likely the external manifestation of an anti-counterfeiting array.

"Our Floating Life Society wishes to be on good terms with Young Master Ning Zhuo!"

"With these jade tablets, a cultivator can become an esteemed guest of our Floating Life Society and participate in almost all our regular activities."

"Internally, our Floating Life Society also hosts trade and exchange events. Using the contribution points stored in the jade tablets, one can receive corresponding discounts."

"There are twelve jade tablets here. The Young Master may distribute them to cultivators you favor, and they will enjoy the same treatment."

"At the same time..."

At this point, Jin Mantang deliberately lowered his voice slightly, yet ensured it was just loud enough for everyone present to hear.

"Should things not go as planned, the Young Master can use these jade tablets to settle down in our Floating Life Society! Hahaha."

Hearing these words, Niu Cheng, Zhang Dadan, A'Huo, and the others felt quite a bit of goodwill toward Jin Mantang.

"This person is actually quite nice."

"It seems the Floating Life Society is friendly toward Young Master Ning Zhuo and the Nanming Stronghold."

However, the expressions of Gu Huaijiu, Kong Ran, and others turned cold.

A wave of coldness also filled Ning Zhuo's heart.

To some extent, this Jin Mantang was even more detestable than Ye Qingming!

He deliberately used friendly words to package his true intentions. If Ning Zhuo really distributed these jade tablets on a large scale, what would that make him? What kind of faction hands out the benefits of an external power to its own members?

By then, would Nanming Stronghold still be independent? It would likely be viewed by many as merely a branch of the Floating Life Society.

Especially that last sentence - the Floating Life Society was hoping for the Nanming Stronghold to fall apart so they could take the opportunity to annex them and strengthen themselves.

Despite Jin Mantang's smiling face and somewhat endearingly clumsy demeanor, he actually had zero faith in the future of the Nanming Stronghold and was hoping for this alliance to fail!

Situ Xing narrowed his eyes, carefully observing Ning Zhuo's reaction.

Lin Jinglong suppressed his displeasure. He knew Jin Mantang was a malicious guest, but as the host, Ning Zhuo couldn't pull a long face and lose his composure.

Shen Xi, however, wore a smile. Among the three, he was the first to discover Ning Zhuo and understood him the deepest, so he had the most confidence in him.

Ning Zhuo wore a calm, composed smile, as if he hadn't caught the underlying meaning at all.

The youth cupped his hands, his expression sincere: "I, Ning Zhuo, am a newcomer here. Forced by circumstances to pay off debts, I established this alliance. To receive such support from the Floating Life Society on the very day of our founding ceremony - it truly relieves much of my anxiety and tension, filling me with newfound confidence. As expected, there are still many good people in this world!"

A flash of doubt crossed Jin Mantang's heart, but he verbally played along: "It's nothing, it's nothing."

Although he knew a bit of intelligence regarding Ning Zhuo, this was ultimately his first time dealing with him, and he hadn't quite grasped Ning Zhuo's style yet.

But right after, he fully realized that Ning Zhuo was not to be trifled with.

Ning Zhuo continued: "Although we established our alliance at the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion today, founding the Nanming Stronghold, there is still a crucial treasure that has not been completely repaired."

"Fellow Daoist Jin Mantang surely knows of it; it is the Naming Fire Furnace."

"The Naming Fire Furnace is currently eighty percent repaired, leaving twenty percent damaged. The Floating Life Society boasts abundant wealth and vast connections. If you could fund the repair of this remaining twenty percent, you would become my biggest creditor!"

"If this matter is accomplished, I would be willing to nominate the current President of the Floating Life Society to concurrently serve as the Grand Chieftain of our Nanming Stronghold, taking the highest seat of authority."

In an instant, Jin Mantang's pupils contracted.

He heard the trap in Ning Zhuo's words.

Ning Zhuo only said "he would be willing to nominate," fully expressing his sincerity, but in reality, he was intentionally digging a pit. Even if he agreed, what about the other creditors?

Even if the Floating Life Society took on the debt related to the final twenty percent of the repairs, the other creditors combined held eighty percent. With a slight alliance among them, the Floating Life Society's President wouldn't be able to sit in the top seat at all.

Yet Jin Mantang couldn't even refute it, because Ning Zhuo was someone capable of keeping his word on the nomination.

"Excellent," Lin Jinglong praised inwardly.

Situ Xing also felt that Ning Zhuo's response was brilliant.

Kong Ran evaluated in his heart: "As expected of Brother Ning. The Floating Life Society underestimated Brother Ning and the Nanming Stronghold, bringing out jade tablets as bait to reveal an intent to assimilate them. Brother Ning, however, paid them back in their own coin. He countered using the very system of the Nanming Stronghold, inviting the Floating Life Society to become a new creditor, aiming to drag them into the Stronghold's sphere of influence."

In short, it was a battle over whose rules they were going to play by.

Playing by someone's rules usually meant that person was in charge.

Jin Mantang also saw through this, causing the smile on his face to turn stiff.

Ning Zhuo, however, didn't let him off, pressing his advantage: "What does Fellow Daoist Jin think? Isn't it wonderful?"

"Wonderful my ass! This kid is wicked." Jin Mantang cursed in his heart, only able to temporarily stall: "Repairing a twenty percent deficiency is no small sum. I'll have to return and seek instructions from the President."

"Of course, of course." Ning Zhuo nodded repeatedly, his expression earnest. "Once Fellow Daoist Jin receives your instructions, you can contact me directly. In matters concerning the Nanming Stronghold, I call the shots."

When he said the words "I call the shots," he even placed extra emphasis on his tone.

Kong Ran almost couldn't hold back his laughter.

Situ Xing, Lin Jinglong, and Shen Xi looked at each other, all seeing the amusement in one another's eyes.

They all heard the underlying meaning behind Ning Zhuo's words - You are a Golden Core, but you can't make decisions. I am Foundation Establishment, but I call the shots.

Between the two of us, who is stronger and who is weaker?

"The Young Master is truly... straightforward." Jin Mantang laughed dryly and took a half step back.

Ning Zhuo cupped his hands: "Fellow Daoist Jin, please take a seat."

Jin Mantang turned and walked toward the secondary tables. He had a smile on his face, but felt stifled inside.

When he first stepped into the hall, he had spotted Ye Qingming's table and decided to sit there.

As he sat down, he saw Ye Qingming looking at him with a smile.

This smile was gentle and serene, like a spring breeze brushing the face, yet Jin Mantang tasted a sense of "schadenfreude" within it.

The muscles at the corners of Jin Mantang's mouth twitched slightly.

Che Zhuzi let out a bold laugh and struck up a conversation with Jin Mantang.

He hadn't picked up on the recent verbal sparring. Seeing Jin Mantang and the Floating Life Society bring out such an excellent congratulatory gift for Ning Zhuo and the Nanming Stronghold, he assumed their attitude was genuinely friendly.

This gave Che Zhuzi a feeling of cordiality toward Jin Mantang, whom he was meeting for the first time.

He was much more willing to chat with Jin Mantang than to engage in awkward conversation with Ye Qingming and Li Ku.

However, just as Che Zhuzi finished introducing himself and looked at Jin Mantang full of expectation, Jin Mantang cast a sidelong glance at him, the smile on his lips unable to hide his mocking tone: "A Demon Cultivator?"

Che Zhuzi's expression instantly froze. A flash of anger crossed his eyes, his smile faded away, and he straightened his back: "That's right."

Jin Mantang merely nodded at him, then turned his head and began chatting with Ye Qingming.

Che Zhuzi, having faced discrimination and being left hanging, felt anger, grievance, and pain in succession, before developing a deep sense of confusion - Wasn't Jin Mantang on Ning Zhuo's side? Didn't the Floating Life Society want to help the Nanming Stronghold? Wasn't Ning Zhuo about to invite them to become a creditor?

They had communicated so well.

Then, did he simply look down on my origins as a Demon Cultivator?

"This damn discrimination! Truly, not everyone is like Young Master Ning Zhuo." Che Zhuzi felt indignant and resentful, once again deeply regretting choosing to sit there.

But now, if asked to leave, he was unwilling: "If I did that, wouldn't I be running away like a deserter?"

Jin Mantang's own heart was full of indignation. On the surface, he exchanged pleasantries with Ye Qingming, but secretly, he transmitted a message via divine sense: "The young people these days really have a lot of drive. I just fear they'll bash their heads in."

Ye Qingming replied, "At the very least, this kid Ning Zhuo can hold his ground in today's situation."

She had tested him and also witnessed Li Ku and Jin Mantang's defeats. She understood that Ning Zhuo's attainments in the Righteous Path meant he, at the very least, wouldn't be at a disadvantage in a battle of words.

Jin Mantang snorted coldly: "Not necessarily. Looking at the cultivators in this hall, the numbers are sparse, and we're the only Golden Cores. Aside from the three of us, out of the other two, one isn't sitting at the main table and the other is just a Demon Cultivator. Neither wants to join the Nanming Stronghold. Which other faction on our Flowing Cloud Peak would come to observe the ceremony?"

Ye Qingming: "I only received orders from our Society President this morning to come and observe the ceremony. As for the other factions, I know nothing."

Jin Mantang stared at her intently, a look of suspicion revealing itself in his eyes.

If one had to name which small faction on Flowing Cloud Peak was the most capable at gathering intelligence, it was absolutely the Green Tea Society.

Ye Qingming had anticipated Jin Mantang's reaction and didn't explain. She merely speculated, "If there are envoys from other factions coming next, they should have arrived right after Li Ku and myself."

None of the factions on Flowing Cloud Peak wanted to see a new, powerful faction intervene. If they were to apply pressure, they would definitely try to act collectively to maximize their deterrence.

Therefore, after Ye Qingming appeared first, Li Ku immediately followed, and Jin Mantang should have been next. But that hadn't been the case.

Jin Mantang transmitted via divine sense: "I was just slightly careless and was beaten to the punch by this Demon Cultivator, that's all."

In reality, he had wanted to wait and observe to see if other factions were also keeping an eye on this place, which resulted in him being a step slow.

Ye Qingming let out a soft laugh, not lingering on this point: "Even if there are envoys from other factions, not many will be able to come. When Ning Zhuo distributed the invitations, he was already guarding against this."

Only by holding an invitation could one be allowed entry by Gongsun Yan into the true venue.

The invitations Ning Zhuo distributed were limited, and simultaneously targeted only at creditors, friends, and the like. This resulted in any cultivators who intentionally wished to participate having to employ whatever means necessary to procure an invitation within a mere three days.

The difficulty involved in this was no small matter.

This was because this place was the Myriad Manifestations Sect, which maintained highly strict oversight and punishments for internal conflicts. During every iteration of the Flying Cloud Assembly, the entire Myriad Manifestations Sect's forces would consolidate, making the defense and surveillance of the main sect at its absolute peak!

If someone harbored the thought of "barging right in even without an invitation," that meant they were utterly unpresentable and couldn't be taken seriously.

Gongsun Yan's strength was not high, yet the cultivators still had to hold their invitations and wait for him to verify their entry.

This was a rule set by the hosts. Should anyone refuse to abide by it, it would be a slap to Ning Zhuo's face. And slapping Ning Zhuo's face was very likely slapping the face of the Nanming Stronghold. If a Nascent Soul-level cultivator joined the Nanming Stronghold, that would mean offending a mighty Nascent Soul cultivator!

Even without a Nascent Soul cultivator, the addition of several Golden Core cultivators still provided significant strength.

Who would be foolish enough to make enemies for no reason?

Furthermore, rumors regarding Ning Zhuo establishing an alliance to charge the peak had been circulating for three days, and many pairs of eyes were fixed on this place. This definitely included the Demon Subjugation Hall, Ten Thousand Beasts Peak, and Danxia Peak.

If someone truly broke the rules and barged in, it would undoubtedly be telling the public and the true higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect: they do not follow the rules. Without a convincing excuse like a blood feud, such a person would be alienating themselves from the Righteous Path and cutting themselves off from the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

In the future, who would trust them? Who would cooperate with them?

Therefore, while this situation might seem minor, it was actually very serious.

Jin Mantang grunted in acknowledgment, directing his attention to the screen at the entrance of the hall. He had already sensed that another cultivator exuding a Golden Core aura had arrived.

This was a female cultivator.

She wore a moon-white traditional robe, her dark hair cascading down to her waist. She had an oval face with soft yet distinct features. Her cheekbones were neither high nor low, and her jawline was smooth and rounded - lacking sharp angles, yet not appearing weak. The most captivating part about her, however, was her hands. Her fair hands were like jade, her ten fingers slender and perfectly tapered like scallion roots, her nails shimmering with a lustrous glow.
