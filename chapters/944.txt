Chapter 944: Battle for the Treasured Land

Sun Lingtong did not answer immediately.

He narrowed his eyes, his gaze circling the half-silver mask of Mr. Shadow Ledger before falling back onto the jade slip registry in the gray-robed cultivator's hands.

Information regarding the Seven Fiends Blade Demon surfaced in his mind.

This Nascent Soul demonic cultivator had infused the Seven Fiends into his blade and once slaughtered three cultivation market towns in a row in the North. Despite being tortured by the Cloud Prison Fiend Chains for many years, his ferocity remained undiminished, and his blade intent was incredibly tenacious.

This demonic soul was not important to Ning Zhuo.

Sun Lingtong understood very well that the demonic soul Ning Zhuo needed was one that could yield an immediate and visible improvement to the latter's combat power.

Thanks to their previous interactions, Sun Lingtong held a position of strength in his negotiations with the gray-robed cultivator, which indirectly gave him bargaining power against Mr. Shadow Ledger.

Sun Lingtong waved his hand. "The Blade Demon is good, but I am more willing to help others achieve their goals. I'll yield him to Mr. Shadow Ledger. I am, however, quite interested in a new treasured feng shui land that recently appeared on Flowing Cloud Peak."

Mr. Shadow Ledger smiled. "Daoist friend is very straightforward; therefore, I cannot be stingy."

He took out a grayish-white jade slip from his sleeve and handed it directly to Sun Lingtong.

"All the intelligence regarding the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar is right here."

Sun Lingtong took the jade slip with a smile.

He hadn't expected to obtain such precious intelligence for free.

However, when his divine sense probed the jade slip and casually scanned it, the smile on his face immediately faded.

"To think there's such an inside story."

"It's best to let Little Zhuo handle this matter..."

Mr. Shadow Ledger immediately completed a transaction with the gray-robed cultivator, successfully obtaining the remnant soul of the Blade Demon. Before leaving, he looked at Sun Lingtong and indicated that if he wanted any intelligence in the future, he could always come find him in the black market.

After Mr. Shadow Ledger left, the gray-robed cultivator asked Sun Lingtong if he had made his decision.

Sun Lingtong mentally went over the list.

Dark Nightmare Scholar, Madame Blood Lantern, Wailing Child, Palace-Rending Ghost Seer, Three Thoughts Daoist, Crimson Vein Butcher, Thick Soul Slave...

"Currently, all sorts of rumors are spreading, saying that Liu Jinke is receiving massive support from the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak. This is absolutely not without basis!"

"Little Zhuo still needs to increase his combat power as soon as possible."

Ning Zhuo had obtained the inheritance of the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Cylinder], and along with the Phoenix Singing to the Morning Sun zither score gifted by Dong Nishang, his mechanical bird swarm also had a profound connection to musical rhythm and modulation.

However, he was ultimately not someone who had immersed himself in this for years. He didn't have the time to slowly learn the methods of proper tones, elegant melodies, and tuning.

In the second battle against Liu Jinke, the moment the Flock-Startling Whistle sounded, the mechanical bird swarm was disrupted and thrown into disarray.

"But if I can gain enough experience from a demonic soul, I will definitely be able to make up for this flaw and truly execute the mechanical bird swarm's war of attrition tactics." These were Ning Zhuo's exact words to Sun Lingtong.

"There are other demonic souls suitable for Little Zhuo. But this one can elevate his combat power the most! The main point is that we have a faction in White Paper Immortal City, so we won't lack demonic souls in the future."

Thinking up to this point, Sun Lingtong made his decision and said to the gray-robed cultivator, "I'll choose the Palace-Rending Ghost Seer."

Not long after, Sun Lingtong secretly returned to the cave manor with his harvest and smoothly handed it over to Ning Zhuo.

Sun Lingtong reminded him that the matter concerning the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar seemed a bit complicated.

Ning Zhuo thus inspected the intelligence jade slip first.

"The Territory Expansion Alliance?" Ning Zhuo frowned slightly.

It turned out that the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar was not an unowned treasured land that had naturally surfaced, but rather something the Territory Expansion Alliance had secretly cultivated and built up over many years.

The Territory Expansion Alliance had three layers of arrangements within it.

The first was the Cloud Ring Sun-Shielding Formation. Using the cloud qi constantly exhaled by the Cloud Crossing as a curtain, it hid the sunlit cave entrance from outside eyes.

The second was the Yang-Locking Well. On the well's walls were three vein-suppressing copper wedges, nailed respectively into three law nodes: sunlight, fire vein, and cloud root. With a forward rotation of the copper wedges, it could gather the sun's radiance, earth fire, and cloud qi, trapping the three in an infinite cycle within the well.

The third was the Sun-Supporting Cloud Ascension Altar. The top of the altar caught the sun, the waist swallowed clouds, and the base suppressed fire. The true value of this altar lay not in its dense spiritual energy, but in its ability to let cultivators observe the profound worldly principles of "the sun's radiance entering the clouds, the fire vein giving birth to Yang, and the cloud qi supporting the sun."

"Looking at it this way, it seems even more suited for Chunyangzi's Pure Yang Pill Scripture." Seeing this, the worry in Ning Zhuo's heart deepened.

The following content added yet another layer of worry for him.

"The Territory Expansion Alliance has a deep background? It's suspected to be an arrangement specifically laid down on Flowing Cloud Peak by one of the main peaks?"

Ning Zhuo slowly put down the jade slip, his eyes as calm and deep as a profound well.

On the surface, the Territory Expansion Alliance was an association of Earth Element cultivators, specializing in the geomancy of earth veins, expanding mountain spaces, and extending spiritual veins. Their slogan sounded majestic: The earth has its limits, but our ambitions are boundless.

"More than ten years ago, the Territory Expansion Alliance had already begun secretly laying out arrangements and managing this treasured feng shui land, the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar."

"Is such a grand scale and plan something a group of rogue cultivators could achieve?"

"This intelligence makes a lot of sense."

Saying this, Ning Zhuo looked at Sun Lingtong. "Boss, which main peak do you think is supporting them from behind?"

Sun Lingtong took a sip of tea. "Heavy Formation Peak?"

Ning Zhuo's expression was heavy, and he sighed.

"Very likely," he said. "Heavy Formation Peak controls the mountain-protecting grand formation and is the most sensitive to the changes in Flowing Cloud Peak's earth veins. If the Territory Expansion Alliance could lay out earth veins on Flowing Cloud Peak for a long period of time, it would be almost impossible without the tacit consent and support of Heavy Formation Peak."

"Besides Heavy Formation Peak, the Formation Arrangement Hall is also a possibility. Even the Artifact Refining Hall needs fire veins."

As Ning Zhuo spoke, he took out another jade slip and tossed it to Sun Lingtong.

This was intelligence regarding Tan Zhu.

Ever since Nanming Stronghold successfully formed an alliance, Ning Zhuo had been gathering information on its relevant members, and one of the focal points was Tan Zhu.

Poison Annihilation Wanderer, a Nascent Soul-level demonic cultivator. His lifespan was nearing its end, he was a loner with no family or disciples, and his cultivation art left behind deep, disastrous aftereffects... all of this could be easily found.

But digging any deeper revealed only a blur.

Why did he come here?

Why did he suddenly join Nanming Stronghold?

Who exactly influenced him?

Was he simply using Nanming Stronghold to evade a tribulation, or was he testing the waters for some faction?

Ning Zhuo had no answers.

Tan Zhu's appearance was simply too abrupt.

And this time, Tan Zhu's pollution of the White Cloud Village's Flowing Cloud Crossing seemed to be him standing up for Nanming Stronghold, retaliating against White Cloud Village for supporting Liu Jinke.

The development of the event itself was extremely reasonable: Nanming Stronghold was besieged by the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak, so Tan Zhu's counterattack was fully justified and highly intimidating.

But as it happened, his very move broke the cloud ring and exposed the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar.

It was too coincidental.

It was truly far too much of a coincidence.

Looking at the contents of the jade slip, Sun Lingtong subconsciously raised an eyebrow. "A Quasi-Divine Art?"

He was referring to Tan Zhu's poison smoke methods.

Ning Zhuo nodded. "Several Nascent Souls from White Cloud Village joined forces but were still unable to resolve it. Shifting the clouds failed, the wind couldn't blow it away, the rain couldn't wash it clean, and attempting to cut the clouds only allowed the poison smoke to seep deeper through the gaps. This isn't a simple poison art; it indeed possesses the nature of a Divine Art."

Sun Lingtong shrugged and said, "No wonder the Flowing Cloud Peak side couldn't solve it. It's too early to make guesses about Tan Zhu now; we lack crucial information."

"What we can confirm right now is this: Tan Zhu's move has made our Nanming Stronghold's prestige even greater. With the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar exposed, the situation has also become more chaotic."

Ning Zhuo fell silent.

Originally, he had wanted to slowly use Liu Jinke as a conduit to make the various factions on Flowing Cloud Peak continually place their bets. Meanwhile, he would get a clear view of his enemies, digest his tribulation luck, and consolidate Nanming Stronghold.

But now, the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar had suddenly appeared out of nowhere.

This disrupted Ning Zhuo's plans, catching him somewhat off guard.

"If Tan Zhu did this unintentionally, then it is the surging of tribulation luck, where disaster and fortune give birth to one another."

"If Tan Zhu did this on purpose..."

A trace of coldness surfaced in Ning Zhuo's eyes.

"Then whatever this person is plotting is absolutely not simple."

"We still need to continue investigating intelligence on Tan Zhu," Ning Zhuo sighed.

Sun Lingtong nodded and said, "I will keep looking into it."

Sun and Ning parted ways.

Using the Burning Boat Soul Ferrying Art, Ning Zhuo delivered a righteous judgment upon the Palace-Rending Ghost Seer's demonic soul. He used his Innate Talent True Self to understand her life, and finally mercifully transcended her soul.

The Palace-Rending Ghost Seer was skilled in evasion techniques, with musical skills as a secondary focus.

Her musical skills were rough, frantic, noisy, and completely unsystematic.

This was not the orthodox musical Dao of Dong Nishang, which sounded like pearls falling onto a jade plate, nor was it the grand and upright phoenix melody of Phoenix Singing to the Morning Sun. The Palace-Rending Demonic Ghost Seer's musical skills were more like suddenly smashing a thousand copper gongs in the dark of night - abrupt, manic, and utterly chaotic.

"Violence hidden within the chaos, sound concealed within the noise..."

Ning Zhuo arrived at the martial arena, brought out a zither, and placed his fingertips on the strings.

The first sound was raspy and unpleasant to the ear.

The second sound was like tearing silk, threatening to rupture a listener's eardrums.

The third sound unexpectedly clashed with the lingering echoes of the previous ones, forming a bizarre syncopation.

Ning Zhuo closed his eyes and continued.

Twang, twang, twang...

The zither notes grew increasingly chaotic, but it was not a chaos without rules. It was like a cluster of dead leaves swept up by the wind; scattering on the outside, but possessing a swirling eye of the storm on the inside.

Order within the chaos!

Ning Zhuo released several mechanical flying birds.

At first, the flock of birds also flew wildly in shock from the noise, but soon, they found a new attacking rhythm amidst the chaos.

A Sleeve-Piercing Sparrowhawk swooped low.

Two Bamboo-Joint Pheasants dashed across the ground in a crisscross pattern.

The long-necked mechanism of a Spear-Necked Crane shot out with a sharp click amid the ear-piercing noise, the timing even trickier than usual.

After practicing like this for the time it takes to drink half a cup of tea, Ning Zhuo slowly opened his eyes, a flash of joy passing through them.

The increase in combat power was extremely obvious!

The mechanical bird swarm's offense was clearly more than ten times stronger than before! Furthermore, it made up for their greatest weakness. If he encountered a sound-disrupting magic treasure like the Flock-Startling Whistle again, Ning Zhuo would absolutely not be as passive as last time.

"Not bad," Ning Zhuo praised lightly.

The Palace-Rending Demonic Ghost Seer's experience had already produced such an effect after only preliminary practice and application. If he continued to comprehend it and cross-referenced it with Phoenix Singing to the Morning Sun - one orthodox and one heretical, one elegant and one chaotic - his musical skills would definitely be elevated to another level entirely.

Just as Ning Zhuo was about to continue practicing, Chunyangzi suddenly paid a secret visit, disrupting his plans.

"To think he came over personally?" Ning Zhuo's expression grew solemn, immediately having a bad premonition.

Chunyangzi was still wearing his scarlet-gold Daoist robe, the three thousand words of the Pure Yang Pill Scripture on the fabric faintly glowing.

Ning Zhuo bowed respectfully. "Great Stronghold Leader."

Chunyangzi looked at Ning Zhuo and got straight to the point: "Ning Zhuo, I am determined to obtain the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar."

Ning Zhuo had anticipated this, but at this moment he looked up, revealing a look of confusion.

Chunyangzi waved his hand. "Alright, don't I know you well enough by now? I'm sure you had long anticipated the possibility of me coming to meet you in secret."

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, thought for a moment, and said, "Senior, do you know that this place might involve the Territory Expansion Alliance, and even deeper, a main peak? And that the appearance of the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar is very likely a conspiracy?"

As he spoke, Ning Zhuo handed the two intelligence jade slips to Chunyangzi.

After reviewing them, Chunyangzi nodded slightly. "Even so, we must occupy the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar."

"Since Nanming Stronghold wishes to charge the peak and establish a permanent settlement, we need a foundation. This place is perfect!"

Ning Zhuo felt very helpless in his heart.

Although Chunyangzi's tone was calm, Ning Zhuo acutely sensed his determination!

Thoughts flashed like lightning within the youth's Divine Sea: Tan Zhu exposed this place and established his prestige; his intentions are hard to fathom. But if Nanming Stronghold pivots towards the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar, Chunyangzi will become the firmest driving force. Nine Fires Dragon Lord might also be tempted by the fire vein.

The various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak already view Nanming Stronghold as a major enemy.

What reaction will Tie Kuang from the Artifact Refining Hall have?

Furthermore, will the people behind the Territory Expansion Alliance sit back and watch the treasured land fall into the hands of outsiders?

Originally, Ning Zhuo had wanted to stall, intending to use Liu Jinke to continue observing and digesting his tribulation luck.

But Chunyangzi was sitting right in front of him at this moment, and the fiery light in his eyes explained everything.

The situation had slipped out of Ning Zhuo's control!

Chunyangzi stared unblinking at Ning Zhuo. His expression was calm, yet it brought an immense oppressive force upon Ning Zhuo.

He possessed profound political acumen and was very clear that the core key to Nanming Stronghold had always been Ning Zhuo. Therefore, if he wanted to act through Nanming Stronghold and borrow the strength of others to help him fight for the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascension Altar, he could not bypass Ning Zhuo.

Thus, the very first thing he did upon taking action was to secretly find Ning Zhuo to rally his support.

Ning Zhuo figured it out in this moment.

It was impossible for him to stop Chunyangzi.

If they were not united in purpose, Nanming Stronghold's reputation would be greatly discounted, and outsiders would widely believe that they were internally divided and lacking cohesion.

Even though he was being strong-armed into it, after pondering for a long time, Ning Zhuo still judged that it would be better to act together with Chunyangzi on this matter.

Ning Zhuo looked at Chunyangzi and slowly spoke, "Since Senior is resolute in this, I naturally cannot treat it lightly."

"It's just that this matter requires the utmost caution."

Chunyangzi laughed heartily. "It's good as long as you agree."

Ning Zhuo: "And the specific price?"

To get Ning Zhuo to cooperate proactively, Chunyangzi naturally had to pay a price.

Chunyangzi patted Ning Zhuo on the shoulder and said gallantly, "The price is highly negotiable. Everything between us can be discussed!"

Ning Zhuo didn't hesitate, having already thought it through, and said directly, "I am fully supporting Senior this time. So, please, in the future, Senior must support me with the same vigor once. How about it?"

Chunyangzi gave Ning Zhuo a deep look.

Ning Zhuo's asking price seemed low, but in truth, it was exactly right. The precision in grasping the appropriate limit couldn't help but make Chunyangzi secretly praise him in his heart.

Chunyangzi nodded. "Deal."

Right then, the two signed a contract.

Immediately following that, they discussed how to persuade the others to act in unison.

"I can vouch for Senior Tu Yuanzi," Ning Zhuo was the first to state his stance.

Chunyangzi gave Ning Zhuo a deep look once again, thinking to himself: That Tu Yuanzi is likely genuinely sent by the family behind Ning Zhuo to assist him.

Chunyangzi said, "Since Tan Zhu is the cause of the exposure, it is highly likely that he will continue to follow up."

Of the five great Nascent Souls of Nanming Stronghold, that was almost three who agreed.

Only the Red-Robed Guest and Nine Fires Dragon Lord remained.

Chunyangzi continued, "I will convince the Nine Fires Dragon Lord. You take care of the Red-Robed Guest?"

Ning Zhuo thought for a moment and nodded. "That's the only way."
