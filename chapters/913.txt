Chapter 913: The Rising Dragon's Roar

The last time he could use Life Hanging by a Thread, it was because Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin had cast Strange Path methods in advance to conceal it. Not only that, but Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin had also divined a time limit. If it exceeded three days, even with the disguise of the Strange Path methods, it would be recognized by others.

Therefore, Ning Zhuo became increasingly hesitant to use the Divine Art of Life Hanging by a Thread in public.

Seeing Sun Lingtong's anxiety, Ning Zhuo comforted him, "Boss, don't worry."

"I am doing things according to my mother's guidance. The direction we are taking right now is correct. I have destiny on my side, and the benefits of acting this way are far greater than participating in various Rising Cloud Minor Trials."

"Furthermore, I am currently riding high in the spotlight; the Rising Cloud Minor Trials could very well become traps for me."

"After my conflict with the Ban Clan, I haven't dared to casually participate in any mechanism-type Rising Cloud Minor Trials."

"Although the competition to establish an alliance is fierce, there are no hidden dangers that I cannot detect."

Comforted by Ning Zhuo's words, Sun Lingtong's expression slightly eased. "Little Zhuo, you will have to face tomorrow's matters on your own."

"I'll head out in a bit and go back to the black market to negotiate."

"I'll try to buy a suitable Demonic Soul for you as soon as possible!"

"Mm. I'll leave it to you then, Boss," Ning Zhuo laughed.

Because of the incident at the Cloud Prison, both the quality and quantity of Demonic Souls had surged significantly. Sun Lingtong had already negotiated with that cultivator in the black market three times, but the other party was asking for too much. They were clearly waiting to see how the market would shift, so the two sides had yet to reach an agreement.

After bidding farewell to Sun Lingtong, Ning Zhuo stayed in the martial arts arena and intensified his practice for a while.

With three sichen remaining until dawn, Ning Zhuo decisively ended his extra training, dragged his exhausted body to bed, and quickly fell fast asleep.

Ning Zhuo slept for a little over two sichen before waking up on his own.

Although he had confidence and certainty, he also deeply knew that establishing this alliance was of utmost significance to him.

In the future, even if he were to leave this faction and join elsewhere, this foundation could serve as his route of retreat. Even if the peak-charging failed and the alliance disbanded, this experience would still serve as his credentials.

"So, I'm still nervous. I am still too young," Ning Zhuo smiled bitterly.

After all, with only a Foundation Establishment cultivation - and not even at its peak stage - he had to navigate around and utilize a large group of Golden Core and Nascent Soul cultivators. In the course of Ning Zhuo's life, this was a first.

Through meditation, Ning Zhuo slightly calmed his state of mind. After carefully washing and dressing, he took out a magic robe.

The magic robe was impressively Golden Core-level, originating from the [Empty Valley Syllable Azure Mechanism Tube] inheritance, and was named the Grand Myriad Mechanism Melody Garment.

Its body was entirely moon-white - not a piercing white, but a white carrying a faint undertone of bamboo-green, like a sea of bamboo in the morning mist, hazy and profound. Under the light, silver threads faintly swam across the robe's surface, like flowing moonlight or dancing bamboo shadows.

Looking closely, the silver threads were not mere decorations, but the array lines of a Sound Path Formation.

The collar of the robe adopted a "right-overlapping crossed collar" style. The collar edges were embroidered with continuous cloud-and-thunder patterns using deep azure [Twining Silk Bamboo] threads, the lines as fine as hair. The inside of the collar was lined with a ring of bamboo membrane, as thin as a cicada's wing. Right in the center of the collar hung a jade button the size of a fingernail.

The cuffs were wide, presenting a "broad sleeve" style, but the edges were tapered so they wouldn't drag too much. The edges of the cuffs were embroidered with gold thread in the pattern of [Startled Swan Bamboo]. Its bamboo leaves were slender and seemed to sway in the wind, looking incredibly lifelike.

This was a major event. As the initiator of the alliance, Ning Zhuo naturally had to dress up to display his prestige.

Ning Zhuo put on the magic robe, ate the breakfast prepared by Old Chef, instructed Old Chef and Qing Chi to stay in the Cave Dwelling, and left, bringing only Gongsun Yan with him.

Qing Chi stared blankly at Ning Zhuo until his back completely disappeared. This was the first time the Ghost Clan Girl had seen Ning Zhuo dressed so grandly, and her heart was filled with a sense of awe and admiration.

The Heavenly Treasure Pavilion sat at the summit of Delicacy Peak. It was a seven-story pavilion with flying eaves, upturned corners, and magnificently carved beams and painted pillars.

On either side of the pavilion's doors stood a stone lion. Their eyes were inlaid with spirit pearls whose light circulated, making them look almost alive.

A plaque hung above the lintel, with the three characters "Heavenly Treasure Pavilion" written in gold powder, shining brightly in the morning light.

Today, Ning Zhuo had spent a heavy sum to reserve the entire Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

As soon as he and Gongsun Yan arrived, a cultivator immediately came over to guide them.

Ning Zhuo led Gongsun Yan inside the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion, inspected the main venue, and nodded in satisfaction.

He arranged for Gongsun Yan to go to the door to welcome the guests, while he himself oversaw things inside.

Following Ning Zhuo's orders, the cultivators of Heavenly Treasure Pavilion began setting out tea and pastries, then quietly awaited the guests.

The first to arrive was Cao Gui.

He had specifically changed into a new outfit today: a navy-blue brocade robe with a white jade belt around his waist, and his hair combed meticulously. He held a brocade box in his hands, walking with steady steps and a smile on his face.

"Fellow Cultivator Cao," Gongsun Yan cupped his hands.

"Mr. Gongsun," Cao Gui returned the greeting and handed over his invitation.

Gongsun Yan took the invitation and stepped aside to make way. "Please come in."

Cao Gui stepped into the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion and was greeted by a massive purple sandalwood folding screen. Carved onto the screen was a depiction of a hundred birds paying homage to the phoenix. Every bird was inlaid with gold wire, looking incredibly lifelike.

Bypassing the screen led into the main hall.

The hall was spacious enough to accommodate over a hundred people. The floor was paved with green jade bricks, smooth as a mirror, faintly reflecting silhouettes. Thirty-six glazed lamps hung from the domed ceiling. The light scattered through the lampshades, illuminating the entire hall brightly yet softly.

The tables and chairs were all made of mahogany. The tabletops were covered with brocade tablecloths, and a celadon vase was placed on each table, holding a few fresh flowers that gave off a faint fragrance. Exquisite teacups and pastries were also laid out on the tables.

The seats by the windows offered the best view, overlooking distant mountains that looked like dark strokes of ink and a churning sea of clouds.

Having always made his living within the main sect gates of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, Cao Gui naturally knew of the Heavenly Treasure Pavilion. He had dined here before and was not unfamiliar with its furnishings.

What made him feel "unfamiliar" was Ning Zhuo.

The Ning Zhuo of today was vastly different from the one in his memories.

The moon-white robe fit his body perfectly, accentuating Ning Zhuo's posture to be as straight and tall as bamboo. Under the light, the cloud-and-thunder patterns on the collar shimmered with a deep azure gloss, complementing his slender neck and handsome, elegant features.

The belt cinched his waist, outlining a silhouette of a narrow waist and broad shoulders. He possessed both the refinement of a scholar and the heroic spirit of a martial artist.

The cuffs were wide, covering the backs of his hands when his arms hung down, and revealing the bamboo reeds sewn into the inner lining when he raised them, flashing with the cold gleam of metal in the light.

This was a young master from a prestigious, noble family - gentle as jade, with an extraordinary bearing, his every movement exuding a natural grace.

At the same time, there was an air of a worldly hermit about him, unstained by worldly dust, dressed in white, playing a clear melody.

Cao Gui quickened his pace, bowed deeply to Ning Zhuo, and then marveled aloud at his elegant demeanor.

Ning Zhuo deflected the praise, attributing it to the exquisiteness of the magic robe.

Cao Gui shook his head and said earnestly, "No matter how good the magic robe is, it still needs the right person to wear it. Wearing this Grand Myriad Mechanism Melody Garment, Young Master is like a gentleman amidst the bamboo, an immortal beneath the moon. Others couldn't imitate it even if they tried."

Ning Zhuo smiled, no longer deflecting, and extended a hand to gesture. "Brother Cao, please take a seat."

Cao Gui was not in a hurry to sit down. Instead, he took the brocade box from his sleeve and presented it with both hands. "A small congratulatory gift, merely a token of my respect. With Young Master about to establish the alliance, I thought long and hard about what to give. Then I remembered that Young Master is a grandmaster of mechanisms and also well-versed in music, so I specially sought out this little trinket. Please accept it, Young Master."

Ning Zhuo opened the box lid and found nine circular rings placed inside.

The nine rings were interlocked, forming an exquisite spherical shape. Each ring was similar in size, cast from silver essence, and their surfaces were carved with dense musical Runes. The lines of the Runes flowed smoothly like a musical score, reflecting a cold silver light.

Ning Zhuo took the nine-linked rings out of the box and held them in his palm to examine closely.

The rings felt smooth and slightly cool to the touch. He gently plucked one of the rings; the musical reed within it vibrated, emitting a crisp "Ding," like a silver bead dropping onto a jade plate, the lingering sound curling beautifully.

He plucked another, and the pitch was different - this "Dang" was a half-step lower than the previous one.

Ning Zhuo plucked them continuously. The nine rings produced nine musical notes: Ding, Dang, Dong, Weng, Ling, Qing, Zheng, Zheng, Long.

Ning Zhuo looked at Cao Gui.

Cao Gui smiled slightly and explained, "This item is called the 'Nine-Linked Ring Sound Lock.' It was crafted in the later years of a reclusive mechanism artisan. It looks like an ordinary set of nine-linked rings, but it is actually not. The method to unlock it is not by physically dismantling the rings, but by plucking them in a specific sequence so that the sounds of the nine rings connect into a musical score. Only when the musical score is correct does it act as the key to unlock the nine connected rings."

"After Young Master refines it, if you have any important information, you can seal it inside and set a new musical score. And this musical score will be known only to you."

"If an outsider acquires it and tries to break it open with brute force, it will simultaneously destroy the information stored within the sound rings."

Ning Zhuo revealed a look of interest. "A good item. Nine rings interlocked, each restricting the other. This is not just a congratulatory gift, but also a Mechanism paradigm worth studying. You put a lot of thought into this, Brother Cao!"

Cao Gui's face immediately lit up with joy; this was indeed something he had carefully selected. But at this moment, he waved his hand and modestly said, "Young Master flatters me. I simply thought this item was exquisite, so I presented it as a gift. To be valued by Young Master and invited to attend this ceremony is truly my honor."

Seeing Ning Zhuo stir up the winds and clouds with a mere Foundation Establishment cultivation, daring to challenge Flowing Cloud Peak, daring to think what he wouldn't dare to think, and capable of what he was incapable of doing, Cao Gui admired Ning Zhuo even more from the bottom of his heart.

Ning Zhuo reached out his hand to gesture. "Brother Cao, please take a seat. There will be more guests arriving shortly, so make yourself at home."

Cao Gui nodded. Just as he picked a seat by the window and sat down, three people stepped into the hall.

It was none other than Kong Ran, Liu Fushu, and Bai Jiyun.

Ning Zhuo walked up to welcome them. "Worthy Brother Kong, Brother Liu, Brother Bai, the three of you have arrived at just the right time."

Seeing Ning Zhuo's brand-new image, the three were all momentarily stunned.

Bai Jiyun sighed in admiration, "This must be what Brother Ning looked like when he was back in his clan."

To this day, everyone who knew of Ning Zhuo believed he had a profound background and most likely came from a super clan.

"My poetic inspiration has struck, it's here."

"Quick, quick, quick."

"Bring out the paper and brush."

Bai Jiyun's eyes sparkled, his expression radiant.

Liu Fushu and Kong Ran exchanged a glance, not expecting Bai Jiyun's poetic inspiration to strike so quickly.

The two immediately took out their respective congratulatory gifts
, offering up official paper and a bamboo brush.
Bai Jiyun immediately began writing a poem with effortless strokes.

The poem read:

Moon-white robe with bamboo for bones,

A new kiln's warm fire reflects flowing clouds.

Do not say Foundation Establishment lacks grand ambition,

Just watch as the dragon's roar rises from this ground!

He finished writing the poem first, then added the inscription: Thoughts on Observing Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo Establishing an Alliance at Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.
