Chapter 872: A Drop in the Ocean of Humanity

Qing Baizi had successfully cultivated a technique that could observe "Pill Qi."

Alchemists spent years accompanied by pill furnaces, unceasingly refining pills day after day, year after year. As a result, a special aura would accumulate within their bodies - Pill Qi.

Pill Qi was formless and immaterial, imperceptible through ordinary means. It was like an Alchemist's second face, recording their lifelong journey in alchemy.

Those with rich and dense Pill Qi were undoubtedly hardworking individuals who refined pills year-round. Those with faint Pill Qi, even if they had profound cultivation, might not have achieved much in the path of alchemy.

Not only that, but the type, color, temperature, and fluctuation of the Pill Qi could reflect the field in which the Alchemist specialized.

For example, an Alchemist who perennially refined healing Medicinal Pills possessed gentle and enduring Pill Qi, like a spring breeze brushing against the face. An Alchemist who refined offensive Medicinal Pills had sharp and fierce Pill Qi, like an unsheathed sword. An Alchemist who refined poison pills had bone-chillingly cold Pill Qi, like a hibernating viper or scorpion. And an Alchemist who refined Divine Soul-type Medicinal Pills had an ethereal and elusive Pill Qi, like passing clouds and smoke.

Qing Baizi had mastered this art for over a hundred years and had long since reached the pinnacle of proficiency.

After entering the Formation Space, he had silently activated this Spell. Scanning the area, he saw the Cultivators wreathed in their respective Pill Qi, densely packed like dots of starlight. Some were as weak as a bean-sized flame, some as bright as a candle, some as blazing as a torch, and some as gloomy as ink.

The majority of people's Pill Qi was mixed and impure; either they lacked sufficient accumulation in alchemy, or their Innate Talent was limited.

There was only one person whose Pill Qi was as majestic as a hill, carrying a kind of perfect harmony within its heaviness, and revealing an untainted purity within its simplicity. It was quite extraordinary.

This was an old man.

He looked to be in his seventies, with a thin frame and a slightly hunched back. His grayish-white hair draped loosely over his shoulders, with a few strands falling over his forehead, fluttering slightly in the mountain breeze. His facial features were rigid and square, with high cheekbones and deeply sunken eye sockets. His eyes were half-closed, as if he was resting his mind or lost in deep thought.

On the surface, he only emitted an aura at the Foundation Establishment Stage, blending unobtrusively into the surrounding Cultivators.

Qing Baizi's pupils shrank slightly as he pointed in the direction of the old man: "Everyone, look at this person. He has hidden his cultivation base, but his attainments in the Dao of Alchemy are extremely profound."

Prompted by his reminder, Jiang Ping and the others immediately cast their gazes over.

Qing Baizi was a guest elder with a rogue cultivator background, so he didn't possess much intelligence. Jiang Ping, however, was different. He was the disciple of Wang Yu, and his goal in joining the Alchemy Hall was the position of Hall Master. Therefore, he was very familiar with the outstanding alchemy talents of the Flying Cloud Kingdom.

Jiang Ping was the first to recognize him and expose the old man's identity: "I know him. He is Tao Liweng, known as the [Immortal in the Furnace]."

Qing Baizi suddenly realized, "So it's him. No wonder his Pill Qi is so unique."

There was a period when the reputation of the Immortal in the Furnace, Tao Liweng, spread across the entire country, and Qing Baizi had heard of him.

Now, after several decades, Tao Liweng had reappeared, and his target was clearly the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Shen Hongyao's gaze remained fixed on Tao Liweng. Protected by a Formation, even a Late Golden Core stage cultivator like Tao Liweng didn't detect her scrutiny in the slightest.

Shen Hongyao said, "I've heard of his exploits. This person cultivates the Embracing the Core to Become an Immortal Art and possesses the magic treasure, the Baopu Furnace. Using himself as the material, he only refines the single Golden Core within his own body. He uses various excellent medicinal ingredients to augment his own cultivation."

"Depending on the medicinal ingredients chosen, the aspects of his cultivation that increase differ. Therefore, despite him always being in secluded cultivation, his comprehensive strength is very high, and his combat power far exceeds those of the same level."

Tao Liweng could use the pill furnace to refine himself, tempering his meridians, bones, flesh, skin, divine sense, soul, and more.

Thus, even though the Embracing the Core to Become an Immortal Art only cultivated a single dantian, it was a high-grade Cultivation Art. Cultivating it allowed a Cultivator to improve all aspects of their constitution, making them extremely well-rounded without any shortcomings.

Gu Lianjie murmured softly, "If that's the case, then Tao Liweng can also be considered a medicinal ingredient, a Medicinal Pill. To the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit, wouldn't that make him feel endearing?"

Hearing this, everyone's expressions changed slightly, and they all felt a surge of pressure.

Right at this moment, the air in front of the mountain gate suddenly turned blisteringly hot, like noon during the dog days of summer, with a scorching sun high in the sky and rolling waves of heat.

A streak of fiery light shot over, trailing a blazing tail of fire in midair.

The fiery light stopped in front of Danxia Peak and abruptly dissipated, revealing a Nascent Soul-level Cultivator.

He had long, fiery-red hair that draped over his shoulders, fluttering slightly with his steps like a burning flag.

He wore a crimson-gold robe embroidered with nine Fire Dragons; their heads were held high towards the sky, their claws spread out like hooks, and their tails intertwined like vines.

His surging aura attracted the gazes of countless people.

"A Nascent Soul Cultivator! As expected, even an existence like this would be attracted by the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"Is he... a Demon Cultivator?" Although the newcomer didn't possess any physical traits of a Demon Cultivator, there was still a demonic aura mixed in his presence, making his identity clear at a glance.

"It's him, the Nine Fire Dragon Lord." Qing Baizi recognized this person.

This was because both he and the Nine Fire Dragon Lord were guest elders of the Myriad Manifestations Sect's Danxia Peak. However, the Nine Fire Dragon Lord's status was slightly higher than his; he was a Guest Elder.

Jiang Ping and the others were naturally familiar with him as well.

Shen Hongyao said enviously, "If I had the Fire Dragon bloodline and a natural affinity with fire, my alchemy skills would definitely reach a higher level, and my achievements wouldn't be limited to what they are today."

Qin Ke snorted coldly, "Even if you had to degrade into a Demon Cultivator?"

Shen Hongyao sneered, "So what if I become a Demon Cultivator? As long as my strength is sufficient and I can refine top-tier Medicinal Pills, that's all that matters."

Qing Baizi watched from the sidelines, his face expressionless.

He shook his head inwardly, feeling that Shen Hongyao was too naive. After all, she was someone who had fought her way up from the bottom and didn't understand the landscape at the top.

Qin Ke's teacher was an elder of Danxia Peak, so he understood very well that with the Human race currently reigning supreme, any Demon Cultivator within a Human cultivation faction faced a rigid ceiling.

Despite the Myriad Manifestations Sect's appearance of teaching without discrimination and being all-encompassing, it was the exact same; they just kept it hidden and unstated.

Gu Lianjie said, "I heard that Lord Nine Fire Dragon can simultaneously control nine types of flames, keeping them coexisting in a single furnace for alchemy. Such supreme fire-controlling mastery is simply breathtaking."

"The Nanming Fire Furnace can give birth to the Nanming Vermilion Bird Fire. If it gets controlled by the Nine Fire Dragon Lord, wouldn't he easily become the winner?"

Everyone's expressions sank.

After making a high-profile appearance and announcing his arrival, the Nine Fire Dragon Lord immediately drew the duty Cultivators to step forward and guide him.

He was directly led into the main peak, disappearing from everyone's line of sight.

Ning Zhuo withdrew his gaze.

The participation of Nascent Soul-level figures was completely within his expectations. The intelligence he had gathered beforehand had also given Ning Zhuo relevant information about the Nine Fire Dragon Lord.

He pondered to himself, "Compared to the Nine Fire Dragon Lord, I wonder whose Fire Element realm is superior?"

There were too many people.

Even the sky appeared crowded.

The Cultivators on duty did their utmost to maintain order, continuously guiding Cultivators to land at the foot of the mountain and climb the mountain path.

As Ning Zhuo gradually approached Danxia Peak, a clear and resonant crane's cry echoed from the distant horizon.

In an instant, the cry grew loud and bright, prompting countless Cultivators to look up in awe.

Deep within the Cloud Layer, a speck of ink broke through the air.

In a flash, it arrived in front of Danxia Peak.

"Such incredible speed!"

"Is that a Red-Crowned Ink-Feathered Crane?"

"Another Nascent Soul-level great Cultivator!"

Upon the crane's back sat a person.

It was none other than Cang Yazi.

He still wore a grayish-brown Daoist robe, washed until it was faded. There were several mending marks at the cuffs and collar, with coarse stitches that looked like he had sewn them himself, stitch by stitch. His grayish-white hair was casually draped over his shoulders, tied up with a beast bone hairpin, with a few loose strands falling over his forehead, whipping wildly in the high-altitude winds.

The duty Cultivators hastily flew over, bowed in respect, and then led the way.

Even though Cang Yazi was not from Danxia Peak or the Alchemy Hall, he was a high-ranking fellow sect member from the Myriad Beasts Peak, so he naturally received such treatment.

Shen Hongyao found it strange: "Why would a Cultivator whose main focus is beast-taming arts also come?"

Sensing Cang Yazi's Pill Qi, Qing Baizi shook his head slightly, "From what I can see, Senior Cang Yazi's attainments in the Dao of Alchemy are also no small matter."

Jiang Ping nodded in agreement, "Setting everything else aside, just in terms of refining Form Transformation Pills, even within our Danxia Peak, no one can compare to Cang Yazi. My master once mentioned this."

Everyone's expressions immediately turned a fraction more solemn.

Gu Lianjie guessed, "Lord Cang Yazi excels at taming spirit beasts. Although the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit is not a beast, it is still a spirit. Could it be that he has some special method that can make spirits feel close to him, or even command them?"

Everyone's expressions grew yet another fraction heavier.

"The people from Hundred Herbs Peak have arrived." Jiang Ping pointed to the foot of Danxia Peak, at the starting point of one of the mountain paths, to alert the others.

Everyone cast their gazes over.

They saw a group of Cultivators dressed as herb farmers. They didn't have magnificent Daoist robes, didn't display dazzling Magic Artifacts or magic treasures, and lacked any overbearing aura.

Most of them wore coarse, short brown clothes with sleeves rolled up to their forearms, revealing tanned, sun-darkened skin. Some had dirt on their hands, and others had irremovable grass stains under their fingernails. Most of them had sickles, herb hoes, or bamboo baskets hanging from their waists.

"Basically all Foundation Establishment Cultivators," Shen Hongyao observed. "Except for the two leaders."

Qin Ke shook his head, "One of them goes by the Daoist name 'Baicao Weng'. He is a newcomer this term and has participated in multiple Rising Cloud Minor Trials. His alchemy skills are also outstanding."

Shen Hongyao realized, though with a hint of disdain, "So, Hundred Herbs Peak only sent one Golden Core Cultivator this time? If they are giving up on this opportunity, they should just be straightforward about it. Why send such a team just to put on a show?"

Jiang Ping, however, stared at Baicao Weng for a short while.

As an Alchemist, Baicao Weng had performed exceptionally well in the recent Rising Cloud Minor Trials and was originally a recruitment target for Danxia Peak and the Alchemy Hall. But looking at it now, with Baicao Weng walking so closely with the Cultivators of Hundred Herbs Peak, it was highly likely that both sides had already reached some sort of agreement - once the Flying Cloud Assembly ended, Baicao Weng would be joining Hundred Herbs Peak.

Jiang Ping silently memorized this situation and continued to sweep his gaze around.

The next moment, his gaze paused halfway up one of the mountain paths. "If we're talking about 'giving up', Purple Thunder Peak seems to have done so even more thoroughly than Hundred Herbs Peak."

"That fox should be the close attendant of Lord Meng Wuyan."

Everyone's gazes shifted over and saw a young Demon Cultivator sporting fox ears, well-behavedly waiting in line to go up the mountain.

They soon withdrew their gazes.

Qing Baizi's eyes flickered slightly as he muttered to himself, "Speaking of which, Purple Thunder Peak has been keeping a very low profile these past few years. It's rare to see this faction take any large-scale actions; it's almost no different from sealing off their mountain for secluded cultivation."

Whether it was the herb farmers from Hundred Herbs Peak or the fox youth, none of them had the qualifications to be ushered in, so they obediently waited in line.

Just then, a large group of Cultivators wearing identical attire walked to the starting point of a mountain path. The scale of their numbers attracted many onlookers' gazes.

Jiang Ping said, "Looks like these are the people from the Skill Transmission Hall."

Gu Lianjie clicked his tongue. "Over fifty people? The Skill Transmission Hall has a very strong ambition regarding the Nanming Fire Furnace this time."

Shen Hongyao evaluated, "These people are surrounded by fiery energy; it seems they all practice Fire Element Cultivation Arts and have an affinity with the Nanming Fire Furnace and the Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit. This should be the Skill Transmission Hall's strategy."

Qing Baizi remained silent, not taking these people to heart. In his eyes, their Pill Qi was very low, so they weren't a cause for concern.

He mentioned a few other halls: "I heard that the Internal Affairs Hall, External Affairs Hall, and Commerce Hall have all offered high prices, recruiting capable individuals and strange talents across the entire Flying Cloud Kingdom to compete for the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"Who knows, they might actually manage to dig up one or two powerhouses."

Gu Lianjie exclaimed, "Oh! I see Ning Zhuo."

Shen Hongyao gave a passing glance and withdrew her gaze: "Just a mere Foundation Establishment Cultivator."

Qin Ke didn't even look. With so many rounds of the Rising Cloud Minor Trials having taken place, the talents that Danxia Peak and the Alchemy Hall wanted to recruit had long since emerged.

Even though Ning Zhuo had participated in a few alchemy minor trials recently, his performance was far less brilliant than in other areas, so he was not yet on Danxia Peak's talent list.

Jiang Ping transmitted his divine sense, asking Qing Baizi how Ning Zhuo's Pill Qi was.

Qing Baizi was momentarily stunned before replying truthfully: "This child's Pill Qi is thin and weak; it is not his main focus."

Jiang Ping thanked him and continued to observe other Cultivators.

He paid a bit more attention to Ning Zhuo than the others because his master, Wang Yu, had mentioned that if he had the chance, he should befriend Ning Zhuo.

Actually, he didn't need Wang Yu's instructions to know this.

"Just from the fact that Ning Zhuo could utilize the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone to pull off such astonishing actions shows that he will definitely secure a place for himself in the Myriad Manifestations Sect in the future."

Ning Zhuo landed at the foot of the mountain and joined the line to climb.

His cultivation base was only at Foundation Establishment, not Nascent Soul, so he received no preferential treatment. Even Golden Core-level experts like the Immortal in the Furnace and Baicao Weng had to obediently line up to ascend the mountain.

Some people around Ning Zhuo recognized him, but the vast majority were immersed in their own views and conversations.

Ultimately, even though Ning Zhuo had been in the limelight quite a bit, in the eyes of others, he was just a standout newcomer, merely a Foundation Establishment Cultivator. Even with a powerful background and a bright future, he still needed time to grow.

Moreover, aside from Ning Zhuo, many other genius Cultivators had also emerged. For example, Ban Ji, Pi Fujie, Situ Xing, Zhu Guizhi, and others.

The progress of this term's Flying Cloud Assembly was only at thirty percent; it was still far from the time to draw final conclusions. The geniuses emerging at this moment might not necessarily be the ones having the last laugh.

Ning Zhuo didn't feel the slightest bit of disappointment over this.

This was a golden age of Cultivation!

"Compared to the countless powerhouse Alchemists and renowned Nascent Soul and Golden Core seniors, what does my meager reputation and strength amount to?"

Instead, Ning Zhuo preferred this atmosphere of blending into the crowd.

This had been his experience for more than half his life. The life of endurance and forbearance in the Fire Persimmon Immortal City made this familiar to him, bringing a sense of security.

Although the line was long, the pace of advancement wasn't slow at all.

In less than half an hour, Ning Zhuo had reached the peak of the mountain.

He stepped forward, crossing over into the Formation Space.
