Chapter 856: Lord of the Interstitial Crevice

The guarding Cultivators suffered heavy casualties. Even though the Demonic Cultivators had deep foundations and backgrounds, they still had no worldly possessions, were in poor condition, and their combat strength had declined severely.

The two sides fought to a standstill. The guards' morale grew increasingly high, while the Demonic Cultivators became anxious and restless. Some had twisted expressions, on the verge of slipping into madness.

Qin De mingled among them, his heart sinking to the very bottom, feeling increasingly despairing. "If this continues, I won't be able to escape at all! Once caught, all hope will vanish like a bubble!"

"Should I detonate the Golden Core?" A portion of Qin De's divine sense constantly remained divided, enveloping Song Taosheng's Golden Core.

Catching his opponent off guard with premeditated intent, he had fully utilized the peerless-grade Myriad Methods Demonic Fall Art, catching Song Taosheng by surprise and luckily seizing his Golden Core.

Of course, this was also built upon the prerequisite that Qin De was proficient in Confucian classics and quite familiar with Song Taosheng.

The situation of having one's Golden Core snatched away was relatively rare.

Methods to easily detonate another person's Golden Core were even rarer.

A Golden Core was a Cultivator's foundation, base, and essence. It required a corresponding Cultivation Art to fully unleash the Golden Core's power and subsequently detonate it.

Qin De's situation was very special. First, he himself was still a Confucian cultivator and had a deep understanding of Song Taosheng's Cultivation Art. Second, the Myriad Methods Demonic Fall Art could transform any Cultivation Art.

This meant that Qin De understood Song Taosheng's Golden Core very well. At the same time, he could use the Myriad Methods Demonic Fall Art to transform a portion of it, similar to a gunpowder fuse, which would then affect the entire Golden Core and ultimately detonate it!

"Battle formation, battle formation!" Someone among the guarding Cultivators began to organize.

They formed a battle formation and pressed forward step by step.

This was another clear advantage they had over the Demonic Cultivators - they knew how to cooperate and had practiced battle formations during normal times.

Moreover, reinforcements continuously arrived from the upper levels, causing the guards' morale to surge even higher. They all knew that as long as they stalled these Demonic Cultivators until the higher-ups made a move, not a single one of them would be able to run away!

The Demonic Cultivators cursed incessantly, only able to fight while retreating, their expressions entirely twisted and crazed.

They had been imprisoned here for countless years and had no possessions. Their magic treasures, Medicinal Pills, and talismans had long been confiscated clean. At this moment, all they could use was their own Physical Bodies and their demonic arts.

Even so, the state of their Physical Bodies and demonic arts was very poor, a far cry from their complete, peak condition.

The first beat of the drum rouses the spirit, the second exhausts it, and the third drains it completely!

If the Demonic Cultivators could have broken through the defense line on their first charge and reached the eighth level, it would have been fine. Once at the eighth level, they could have released more Cultivators, allowing their forces to snowball and grow.

But now, the guards stubbornly held the vital choke points, and the two sides seesawed back and forth between the eighth and ninth levels. Every moment, Cultivators fell and lost their lives. The battle was extremely tragic!

Eagle Claw Butcher roared hoarsely, the fine threads from his ten fingers dancing madly as he barely blocked a wave of attacks.

The mask on Ghost-Faced Scholar's face had long shattered. His featureless face constantly squirmed as he pulled another Soul from the battlefield, fashioned it into a ghost face, and wore it.

Bronze Toutuo's head was his hardest body part, yet it had now been beaten bloody.

Charm Demon's fragrant breeze was already faint and imperceptible. Her face was deathly pale, and her footsteps were unsteady as she stumbled.

Only Mad Wind Daoist was still dancing and gesticulating wildly.

"Rather than suffering alive, we might as well die together!" In the increasingly oppressive and despairing atmosphere, a Golden Core Demonic Cultivator threw caution to the wind.

He was short and stout, his face covered in purulent sores. His skin displayed a bizarre bruised-purple color, and his entire body exuded a pungent, fishy stench.

His eyes were a murky yellow as he locked onto the guarding Cultivators in front of him, bypassing his own camp and charging viciously forward.

"Stop him!" the guard commander yelled sharply in alarm.

But it was already too late.

The pustule poison Cultivator lunged into the guards' formation, the purple-blue light around his body suddenly expanding-

Boom!

With an earth-shattering explosion, the entire corridor trembled violently. The purple-blue poisonous light erupted like a sun, spreading madly in all directions. Wherever the toxic light passed, the air corroded and the stone walls melted. Guards died and were injured in droves, while the survivors screamed continuously, retreating frantically.

This pustule poison Cultivator was clearly a ruthless figure, preferring to self-detonate his Golden Core and drag a few down with him before dying.

Seeing this, Eagle Claw Butcher and the others were overjoyed.

Because of the pustule poison Cultivator's self-detonation, a massive gap was torn in the guarding Cultivators' frontline, throwing them into chaos. This was the best opportunity to break out!

But just as the group of Demonic Cultivators wanted to charge collectively, the surrounding walls suddenly lit up with a white light.

The white light was soft and gentle, like spilling moonlight.

Wherever it reached, the purple-blue pustule poison and toxic light were absorbed. In just three breaths, the residual power of the pustule poison Cultivator's self-detonation had completely dissipated.

"No, it's not just that. The Demonic Cultivator who just self-detonated had an astonishing foundation. Normally speaking, it should have collapsed more than half of this prison level. But in the end, it only affected a tiny area!"

Qin De witnessed the entire process, his heart turning cold over and over again.

"This is the Nine Heavens Cloud Astral Formation!"

He recognized it.

What absorbed the power of the Golden Core's self-detonation was the omnipresent Cloud Astral Rock. What subsequently absorbed the toxic light and pustule poison was also the Cloud Astral Rock!

It turned out that this defensive Grand Formation could not only defend against external enemies but also absorb internal explosive impacts, dissolving them invisibly. This was intelligence that Song Taosheng and the others had failed to scout out.

Although the power of a Golden Core self-detonation was strong, in front of the Nine Heavens Cloud Astral Formation, it was nothing more than a mayfly trying to shake a tree.

Song Taosheng's Golden Core was clutched in Qin De's hand, but the scene before him made Qin De feel waves of despair.

This trump card he had relied upon now seemed completely ineffective and unable to change anything.

"Could it be that I have no choice but to completely transform my Golden Core into a Demonic Core?"

Thinking of this possibility, a sharp pain pierced his heart.

Once completely transformed, there would be no turning back.

His Confucian cultivator foundation, his Golden Core core, his final pure land - all would be thoroughly devoured by the Myriad Methods Demonic Fall Art.

He would become a true Demonic Cultivator.

A traitor who had fallen to the Demonic Path.

A sinner forever spat upon by the Confucian Sect.

"No, I am not resigned to this."

His eyes bulged as he glared at those frantically struggling Demonic Cultivators, glared at those guards pressing closer step by step, and glared at that Cloud Prison which could never be destroyed.

His heart was filled with a surge of resentment.

This was resentment towards fate.

He was furious, hating that fate was forcing him down a path he absolutely did not want to take!

"Don't panic!" A hoarse voice came from the rear of the Demonic Cultivators' ranks. The voice sounded like a broken bellows being pumped, grating and unpleasant to the ear, yet carrying an unquestionable majesty.

Everyone looked toward the source of the sound.

The speaker was a scrawny old man, his back hunched, wrapped in a tattered gray robe that barely resembled a human shape. His face was covered in wrinkles, each line so deep it could pinch a pebble. His eyes were a bizarre grayish-white, like two dead fish eyes without any luster.

"Old Demon Withered Bone!" Eagle Claw Butcher exclaimed in a low voice, a trace of apprehension flashing in his eyes.

Old Demon Withered Bone was a Demonic Path Cultivator who had run rampant across a region three hundred years ago. His cultivation had reached the Nascent Soul stage, and he was proficient in various bizarre demonic forbidden arts. However, just when he was terrorizing the region and at the height of his prominence, he suddenly mysteriously disappeared. This disappearance lasted for over two hundred years.

Who would have thought that he had been captured by the Myriad Manifestations Sect and imprisoned in the deepest depths of the Cloud Prison!

The old demon, with his hunched back, walked out of the crowd step by step. "This old man has a forbidden art that can open a teleportation array and send everyone out of the Cloud Prison."

As soon as these words came out, the expressions of both friend and foe changed.

Bronze Toutuo hurriedly shouted, "Then why aren't you casting it right now?!"

Old Demon Withered Bone's grayish-white eyes shifted slightly, and he said unhurriedly, "But there is a catch."

A Demonic Cultivator yelled out, "Old thing, just spit out whatever conditions you have! Hurry up!"

Bang.

In the next moment, the shouting Demonic Cultivator was crushed to pieces by Old Demon Withered Bone's grasp.

Flesh, blood, bone fragments, and pieces of internal organs exploded outward in all directions.

A skeletal arm, like a withered and rotten branch, slowly retracted. What drew everyone's attention was that pinched between the bony fingers was a dim Golden Core!

All the Demonic Cultivators were shocked and intimidated.

Old Demon Withered Bone chuckled maliciously, his voice still unhurried. "Everyone, just watch me cast."

In the next moment, the scattered flesh and blood quickly melted, transforming into pieces of blood-colored talismans that seeped into the floor tiles, the ceiling, and the surrounding walls.

"What is this?"

"It's not a Formation! The Cloud Prison has one layer on the outside and three layers of Formations on the inside. To set up a new array in here in such haste is nearly impossible."

"This is... a Sacrificial Text!"

Naturally, the Demonic Cultivators capable of being imprisoned in the deepest part of the ninth level of the Cloud Prison were full of talent.

Countless blood-colored Runes flickered in and out, like breathing.

When the majority of people's gazes were focused on these Runes, Eagle Claw Butcher suddenly made his move!

His ten fingers shot out like venomous snakes, but they weren't aimed at the guards; rather, they shot towards an injured Demonic Cultivator beside him.

The Demonic Cultivator was dressing his wounds and, caught completely off guard, had his neck hooked by the steel claws.

"You!" His eyes widened instantly. Before he could utter a second word, the hooked claws viciously tightened.

In the next moment, his head hit the floor, blood spurting wildly.

Eagle Claw Butcher tossed the headless corpse toward Old Demon Withered Bone.

The old demon chuckled maliciously, using his divine sense to manipulate the object, letting the corpse fall straight to the ground.

Where the corpse touched the floor tiles, bizarre blood-colored Runes spread out once more. As the Runes multiplied, the corpse melted like remaining snow under the sun.

The Demonic Cultivators' side instantly plunged into chaos!

Ghost-Faced Scholar's ghostly visage lunged out suddenly, causing the closest Demonic Cultivator's eyes to lose focus, freezing him on the spot. Ghost-Faced Scholar smashed the man's head with a single palm strike and threw the corpse to Old Demon Withered Bone.

Bronze Toutuo bent over and lowered his head, moving ferociously, ramming into a Demonic Cultivator he had been fighting shoulder-to-shoulder with just a moment ago. He shattered the man's head like a watermelon, killing him on the spot.

"Wanna kill me? I'll kill you first!"

"Even if I die, I'm taking you down with me!"

The Demonic Cultivators who were injured or chosen as prey began to fight back one after another.

The guarding Cultivators felt a chill in their hearts watching this and also attacked to intervene. Naturally, they wouldn't just sit by and watch the Blood Sacrifice succeed.

For a time, a massive free-for-all unfolded, turning the ninth level of the Cloud Prison into a river of blood.

The chaotic battle lasted for dozens of breaths.

"The Sacrificial Offerings are sufficient." A single sentence from Old Demon Withered Bone put an end to the tragic mutual slaughter among the Demonic Cultivators.

The Demonic Cultivators guarded against each other, yet simultaneously turned their heads to deal with the endlessly pouncing guarding Cultivators.

The previous chaos had forced the Demonic Cultivators' frontline back several dozen paces, making their situation even more critical.

Old Demon Withered Bone unhurriedly sat down cross-legged, his hands forming hand seals.

The blood-colored Runes began to flash madly, emitting a blinding light. That light grew brighter and more intense, gradually transforming into a massive blood-red pillar of light that shot into the sky!

This naturally provoked a reaction from the Cloud Prison Grand Formation.

But soon, the blood pillar directly smashed through space, its very peak sinking into the void!

Old Demon Withered Bone chanted incantations, his voice growing louder and louder - 

"Deep within the void, among the myriad realms. Amidst the continuous space, a crevice exists."

"Heaven and earth have seams, the sun and moon leave marks, mountains and rivers possess crevices, plants and trees bear grains."

"Though boundary walls are solid, their gaps remain; though barriers are firm, their seams persist."

"With blood as guidance, with souls as sacrifice, requesting the Lord's descent, to open this crevice door."

"Lord of the Interstitial Crevice - please listen."

"Fracture the wall before my eyes, pave the path beneath my feet, ferry my broken body, and transcend this cage."

"The blood has flowed, the souls are offered, the crevice has appeared - open!"

As his words fell, the entire Cloud Prison began to tremble violently.

The blood-red pillar of light abruptly expanded, transforming into a massive rift.

Within the rift lay a swath of nothingness. No light, no darkness. No up, and no down. That was pure void, the chaos between myriad realms, the abyss that laws could not touch.

Many of those in the midst of fierce battle involuntarily slowed their movements, looking in astonishment at this rift.

Fear, awe, ecstasy... a myriad of emotions ran the gamut.

"It worked! It worked!"

"I can finally get out!"

"Why aren't you running?!"

Several Demonic Cultivators closest to it leaped directly into the rift's void.

Behind the guarding side's battlefront.

Zhong Dao stood with his hands behind his back, casting a technique to hide his figure, silently observing everything on the ninth level.

His face was stern and cold, the vertical creases between his brows deep as if carved by a knife. His sharp, falcon-like eyes were currently staring coldly at the slaughter taking place on the front line ahead.

Behind him stood a team of elite Cultivators. They were messengers specially dispatched by Dong Chen to notify Zhong Dao, ready to join the battle at any moment.

This was the elite force of the Heavy Array Peak, each one at the late Golden Core stage, and every single one had experienced a hundred battles.

Right now, at Zhong Dao's request, this team of elite Cultivators had also concealed their figures, remaining unknown to outsiders.

Zhong Dao and the others had already been here for a little while.

"Hall Master Zhong Dao!" Among the elite Cultivators, a young Cultivator finally couldn't hold back. "If we don't act soon, those Demonic Cultivators will escape!"

Zhong Dao did not answer.

Another impatient Cultivator directly knelt down. "Hall Master Zhong Dao, I request to fight!"

Zhong Dao finally spoke. "No rush."

He was evaluating.

The guarding Cultivators displayed various performances in this sudden test.

Some desperately resisted, some subtly retreated, some fiercely self-detonated, and some died fighting in fear. Some died heroically, some died in a cowardly manner, and some died utterly meaningless deaths.

In Zhong Dao's eyes, there was no sympathy, no anger, only scrutiny.

"Only through bloodshed and sacrifice, only through pain, can a change from the inside out be catalyzed - a true change." He said this deep in his heart.

Their own side's sacrifices were, to him, just a trivial matter of no importance.

He harbored a cold fury in his heart - the Cloud Prison's guarding Cultivators were far too lax! There were deficiencies in the Formations, loopholes in the patrols, and a complete lack of vigilance. For someone to infiltrate, for prisoners to be released, for them to be slaughtered until rivers of blood flowed - this was the lesson they deserved.
