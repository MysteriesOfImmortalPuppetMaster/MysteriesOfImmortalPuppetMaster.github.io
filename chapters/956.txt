Chapter 956: Nine Fire Dragon Lord vs Yun Wuji

Clear Sky Martial Arena.

A scorching sun hung at the highest point of the arena's sky. This was not a true sun, but one formed by the sect's Grand Formation channeling heavenly light, fire spiritual energy, and Yang Qi.

As the martial contest progressed, it would slowly emerge from the depths of the void - initially like a copper basin, then a golden disk, and finally radiating boundless light.

The first match of the Five-Victory Martial Contest was chosen to be held here.

According to the three parties' contract, the Naming Stronghold would send out a person first and choose their arena, while the Earth Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village would consult with each other to send out the corresponding challenged combatant.

The three parties soon arrived at the edge of the arena and took their seats to watch.

Because the arena was entirely in the sky with no ground, the audience sat on clusters of clouds. These clouds intertwined to form cloud steps suspended in mid-air, each tier higher than the last, resembling layers of white jade floating bridges.

In the Naming Stronghold's seating area, the Nine Fire Dragon Lord slowly stood up and flew into the arena.

The cultivator dispatched by the Naming Stronghold for the first match was not Tan Zhu, but him.

The Nine Fire Dragon Lord's fiery red long hair cascaded over his shoulders, fluttering slightly in the wind like a burning battle flag. He was currently wearing a crimson-gold long robe, upon which nine fire dragon patterns coiled and intertwined, their heads raised toward the sky and claws bared.

He had a tall and sturdy build, his features exuding the arrogance of the Dragon Clan. As his eyes swept over the seating areas of the Earth Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village, the corners of his lips pressed down slightly in what seemed to be a cold sneer or disdain.

Chunyangzi sat in the main seat of the Naming Stronghold, his expression composed, while a point of golden light remained motionless within his sleeve.

Ning Zhuo sat a bit further back. Dressed in white with a large head, he appeared quiet among the various Nascent Soul cultivators.

Looking at the Nine Fire Dragon Lord's back, he knew in his heart that Chunyangzi letting him fight the first match actually violated their previous decision to have Tan Zhu serve as the vanguard.

"Tan Zhu possesses Quasi-Divine Art level methods and has already made White Cloud Village suffer losses twice."

"Now, Chunyangzi has made the Nine Fire Dragon Lord the first to take the field, replacing Tan Zhu's vanguard position... He wants to consolidate his own authority."

Ning Zhuo understood clearly in his heart.

Although he was relatively young, his political acumen was not low, and he had already tasted the hidden implications of this arrangement.

When Chunyangzi persuaded the Nine Fire Dragon Lord earlier, he had taken a step further and drawn the latter into his own camp.

The Naming Stronghold was a newly established organization where everything was just beginning. Chunyangzi had managed the Pure Yang Palace for many years and possessed profound attainments in the Righteous Path. By making this move now, he was taking the opportunity to gather people's hearts and seize power while riding the momentum.

The struggle for power is incredibly cruel. As the saying goes, one mountain cannot accommodate two tigers. The final result of a power struggle will only yield one victor.

Chunyangzi's actions to seize power would undoubtedly reduce Ning Zhuo's influence within the Naming Stronghold.

But right now, both sides were united against the external enemy.

Ning Zhuo acutely perceived this point, but it was not appropriate to cause a scene. Not only could he not stop it, but he also had to fully support it.

Chunyangzi was a Nascent Soul powerhouse, while Ning Zhuo was the weaker party.

The youth thought to himself, "Tan Zhu acting as the vanguard is undoubtedly the safest choice. But since Chunyangzi insisted on letting the Nine Fire Dragon Lord attack first, he must have some assurance. I just hope he can win."

The first battle was extremely important!

From ancient times to the present, in any war, the vanguard's responsibility is massive.

Floating halfway, the Nine Fire Dragon Lord looked toward the seating areas of the Earth Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village, shouting a question: "Which of you will step up to accept defeat in this first match?"

In the Earth Expansion Alliance, Qiu Lei's gaze was deep and profound.

Honestly speaking, they had also thought that the Naming Stronghold would dispatch Tan Zhu as the vanguard. Therefore, when they learned it was the Nine Fire Dragon Lord, they were quite surprised.

This Five-Victory Martial Contest agreement concerned the ownership of a treasured land like the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar. Although the three parties did not explicitly state it, they all tacitly agreed to bring out their strongest combat power to settle this matter.

Therefore, this was almost entirely a stage for Nascent Soul cultivators.

The Earth Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village had a rough ranking of the Naming Stronghold's five Nascent Soul combatants, specifically from strongest to weakest: Tan Zhu, Chunyangzi, Nine Fire Dragon Lord, Red-Robed Guest, and Tu Yuanzi.

Tan Zhu was an expert among Nascent Souls. Those who could make a name for themselves in the Demonic Path always had impressive combat power. He possessed a Quasi-Divine Art, and his two previous moves had caused White Cloud Village to suffer losses. Even more terrifying was that this person's lifespan was nearing its end; anyone facing such a desperate, ferocious man would feel dread.

Chunyangzi ranking second was also universally recognized. If this person didn't have solid combat power as a foundation, how could he have led the Pure Yang Palace for so many years? The reason he brought the Pure Yang Palace to defect to the Myriad Manifestations Sect was not because of weak combat power, but a lack of management skills. Of course, if he possessed Soul Formation-level combat power, poor management wouldn't matter much. Therefore, he was ranked behind Tan Zhu.

The Nine Fire Dragon Lord being third was also agreed upon by most. This individual was not only a Nascent Soul but also of the Dragon Clan. The talents of the Dragon Clan were fundamentally top-tier among Demon Cultivators, with physical aptitudes that were overly formidable. Moreover, the Nine Fire Dragon Lord was an Alchemy Cultivator; his foundation and logistical supply were fully sufficient.

Although the Red-Robed Guest was a Demonic Cultivator, he had no combat achievements to speak of in recent years. It was widely believed that his threat level was relatively low.

Lastly was Tu Yuanzi, placed at the bottom because he was overly mysterious, with no past battle records to investigate and a lack of basis for judgment.

On the Naming Stronghold's side, if they sought stability, Tan Zhu would definitely serve as the vanguard. But now, letting the Nine Fire Dragon Lord - estimated to be third in combat power - take action was an arrangement that also made sense.

Facing the Nine Fire Dragon Lord's challenge, the many cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village felt no unease or panic.

Before coming here, they had already discussed and finalized their plans.

Soon, from the White Cloud Village's seating area, Yun Wuji slowly stood up and flew into the arena.

He wore a grayish-white Daoist robe. His eyebrows and eyes were extremely faint, as if they had been washed by clouds and mist.

Ning Zhuo cast his gaze over, his brows slightly furrowing.

The feeling Yun Wuji gave him was quite strange. This was the youth's first time seeing this type of Nascent Soul cultivator.

Yun Wuji's lip color was light, and his irises were equally pale. His entire being showed no sharp edges, displayed no majesty, and even lacked the usual oppressive aura of a Nascent Soul cultivator. He stood there as if a wisp of a cloud shadow had coalesced into human form. If one didn't look carefully, they would feel he could fade away at any moment.

His sleeves were broad, hanging down below his wrists, and within the sleeves, extremely faint white qi seemed to slowly drift about.

At his waist hung a semi-transparent jade pendant, within which a wisp of white mist floated back and forth.

The grayish-white cloud shoes on his feet stepped upon the air as if walking on flat ground, without making the slightest sound.

The two entered the arena and stood in confrontation. The protective light barriers in front of the three parties' seating areas lit up simultaneously.

Suddenly, a jade chime rang out.

The first battle, begin!

Before the sound of the jade chime had completely dissipated, the heavenly light in the Clear Sky Martial Arena suddenly brightened by several degrees.

The formation's scorching sun slowly rose, scattering tens of thousands of golden rays. The Astral Wind was formless, yet it scraped out fine, fragmented trails of light along the edges of the formation wall.

A hundred zhang separated the Nine Fire Dragon Lord and Yun Wuji. There were no obstacles between them, no ground, no cloud qi - only a vast, empty stretch of clear sky.

Ning Zhuo's eyes narrowed slightly as he thought, "A clear sky without clouds. The Nine Fire Dragon Lord's choice of arena this time was indeed not simply so his dragon body could soar freely, but primarily to weaken the methods of cultivators like Yun Wuji."

The Nine Fire Dragon Lord was the first to act.

Hovering in mid-air, his crimson-gold long robe billowed fiercely. The scorching sun's golden light fell upon the nine fire dragon patterns on the robe, illuminating the dragon heads, claws, and scales one by one. Those nine fire dragon patterns were not mere decorations; spurred by his spiritual energy, they actually seemed to slowly swim across the surface of the robe, scales rubbing together as fiery veins flowed.

He raised his right palm, and a wisp of crimson-gold pill fire first emerged in the center of his palm.

That pill fire was not violent, but rather extremely steady, much like the initial furnace fire used to gently nurture medicinal ingredients in an alchemy furnace.

Subsequently, the Nine-Aperture Furnace Heart Pendant on his chest vibrated slightly. From the nine apertures on the crimson-gold jade pendant, a line of firelight sprayed out from each.

The Nine Fire Dragon Lord shouted in a low voice: "Open the furnace."

Boom!

The first streak of firelight sank downward.

The clear sky originally had no ground, yet when that streak of crimson-brown earth fire sank into the void, it was as if a fiery nail had been driven into the depths of the firmament. The empty martial arena violently shook, as if an invisible furnace bottom had been forcefully erected.

The second streak, an emerald-green wood fire, spread along the crimson-brown earth fire, its branch-and-leaf-shaped fire patterns unfurling in all directions. Rooted in the Nine Fire Dragon Lord's spiritual energy, it bloomed into a patch of azure flame branches out of thin air.

The third streak, a purple-white lightning fire, exploded with a bang!

Above the clear sky, thunder rumbled. The Astral Wind was ignited by the lightning fire, transforming into streaks of purple-white fire serpents that coiled and slithered around the Nine Fire Dragon Lord.

The fourth streak, heart fire, sank into the depths of his eyes. It was dark red and gloomy, making anyone who merely looked at it from afar feel a restless heat rising in their heart.

The fifth streak, true fire, was as clear as white gold, illuminating the clear sky until it became increasingly transparent, even revealing the faint outlines of the weak patterns on the formation wall.

The sixth streak, pill fire, coiled around his palm. It was a smooth crimson-gold, like a condensing pill revolving within a furnace.

The seventh streak, spirit fire, scattered into fine threads that cruised through the air, seemingly searching for a Qi signature to entangle.

The eighth streak, demon fire, was a ghostly emerald that danced closely along the edges of his robe's sleeves and the dragon patterns. Its fire color was coldly sinister, completely out of place with the other eight fires, yet it was suppressed by the dragon fire, not daring to overstep its bounds.

Finally, the ninth streak was dragon fire.

The Nine Fire Dragon Lord's back trembled violently as a crimson-gold dragon shadow rose from behind him, raising its head toward the scorching sun at the zenith. Before the dragon shadow had fully solidified, a muffled dragon roar was already rolling through the clear sky.

Nine Fires Open the Furnace!

In the Naming Stronghold's seating area, a bloody light flashed in the Red-Robed Guest's eyes as he sneered, "This dragon isn't stupid. First refining the clear sky into a furnace chamber to further suppress Yun Wuji's cloud-type methods. The moment any cloud appears, it will be refined away."

Tan Zhu's grayish-black deep robes hung down, his face pale. Hearing this, he said indifferently, "A clear sky without clouds is indeed a good stage. However, if Yun Wuji couldn't even manage to lay down clouds, he wouldn't be worthy of White Cloud Village sending him out."

In the White Cloud Village's seating area, white clouds slowly flowed within Old Man Youyun's sleeves; his expression was unpanicked.

In the Earth Expansion Alliance, Qiu Lei silently watched the arena.

Yun Wuji stood beneath the scorching sun, his grayish-white Daoist robe illuminated until it was nearly transparent.

He was not in a hurry to evade.

Those pale eyes looked up, glancing at the high scorching sun, and then out at the empty, clear sky around him. The brighter the sky, the fainter his figure became, like a white streak that could be scattered by the sunlight at any moment.

He gently flicked his sleeve.

From his broad Cloud-Hiding Sleeve flew a small banner.

White Cloud Banner!

The small banner was only about a foot long. The banner face was grayish-white, and the pole was as thin as a bamboo twig. As soon as it exited the sleeve, it unfurled in the wind and suspended in front of Yun Wuji.

In the clear sky, the first wisp of cloud qi was spat out from the edge of the small banner.

That cloud qi was very thin and light, like the water vapor rising from the edge of a teacup. If it weren't for the fact that the three parties' seating areas were filled with experts, it would be almost impossible to see clearly under the intense heavenly light.

The Nine Fire Dragon Lord scoffed.

He raised his hand and pressed down.

His alchemy had a seven-revolution process and methodology.

First Revolution, Warming the Furnace!

The crimson-brown earth fire and crimson-gold pill fire advanced simultaneously. The invisible furnace bottom violently expanded, and a low hum of a furnace rang out in the clear sky. The moment that tiny bit of thin cloud spread out, it was forced to curl inward by the pill fire, its edges taking on a scorched crimson-red hue.

Beneath Yun Wuji's feet, his Traceless Cloud Shoes tapped gently.

His entire person retreated backward, not moving quickly, but with an unhurried, composed posture. The small White Cloud Banner continued to spit out clouds in front of him, like an old farmer facing the scorching sun, still methodically and unhurriedly sowing seeds.

The second wisp of cloud qi appeared.

The third wisp of cloud qi appeared.

The Nine Fire Dragon Lord's gaze darkened slightly.

Every wisp of cloud qi spat out by the small White Cloud Banner underwent a process of being gathered, kneaded open, and then spread out again. Seemingly weak, they overlapped layer by layer, much like a sheet of white paper being folded repeatedly, becoming thicker with every fold.

"Don't even think about it," the Nine Fire Dragon Lord flicked his sleeve.

Second Revolution, Spurring the Medicine!

The emerald-green wood fire surged wildly, its fire branches growing madly out of thin air and extending in Yun Wuji's direction. Fine threads of spirit fire mixed among the fire branches, chasing the source of that tiny bit of cloud qi.

Yun Wuji raised his left sleeve.

His long robe was a magic treasure, but the grade of his two broad cloud sleeves was impressively even higher - they were Cloud-Hiding Sleeves!

A cluster of white clouds was spat out from the Cloud-Hiding Sleeve, directly meeting the emerald-green fire branches.

The fire branches drilled into the white clouds, instantly turning the white clouds a burning red.

Yun Wuji did not protect it. Instead, with a twirl of his cuff, he caused that cluster of white clouds to transform into over a dozen thin cloud flakes. Every thin cloud was burned a scorched red, but they also used the fire's momentum to evaporate, generating even more water vapor.

Ning Zhuo's eyes lit up. "He's using the burning clouds to generate mist?"

The clear sky originally had no clouds.

But after the white clouds were burned by the fire, they scattered into boundless water vapor. The water vapor was gathered by the Cloud-Hiding Sleeves and then fed back into the cloud domain.

The fiercer the Nine Fire Dragon Lord's fire, the greater Yun Wuji's consumption. However, as long as it couldn't burn it all away, he could accumulate a Cloud Dao environment of his own amidst the attrition.

This method was not at all overbearing, yet it was extremely shrewd, mature, and steady.

The Nine Fire Dragon Lord clearly discovered this as well.

He coldly snorted. Third Revolution, Breaking the Shell!

The lightning fire explosively detonated!

The purple-white lightning fire sprinted wildly along the azure flame branches, instantly shattering three clusters of newly formed white clouds in front of Yun Wuji. The sound of thunder rolled through the clear sky, like the Nine Heavens Thunder Drum being struck by the claws of a fire dragon. The protective light barriers in front of the three parties' seating areas rippled slightly, and the spectator cloud seats were shaken back and forth.

Yun Wuji's figure was forced out by the lightning fire.

The grayish-white figure was pierced by the lightning fire. Purple-white flames ignited on its shoulders and back before immediately scattering with a snap, turning into scorched white cloud wisps.

The true Yun Wuji had already used the white clouds to mask his steps, landing behind another patch of thin clouds.

By this time, there were already seven or eight cloud clusters in the arena.

None of the clusters were large.

Within the furnace-like clear sky spread by the Nine Fire Dragon Lord, they looked like torn pieces of white silk, floating erratically, liable to be swallowed by the waves of fire at any moment. Yet it was precisely these fragmented cloud clusters that ultimately provided Yun Wuji with paths to shift and dodge.

The fiery intent in the Nine Fire Dragon Lord's heart grew even stronger.

He loathed this kind of evasive combat tactic.

Thus, Fourth Revolution, Illuminating Impurities!

The true fire, like white-gold heavenly light, shot out from the space between the Nine Fire Dragon Lord's eyebrows and swept across the clear sky. When those cloud clusters were illuminated by the true fire, their internal structures were clearly revealed. The flowing clouds' refracting shadows were also exposed, revealing more than a dozen figures of Yun Wuji scattered about - some with cuffs slightly raised, some with heads bowed and eyes lowered, some turning sideways to dodge.

As the true fire swept past, the false shadows rapidly thinned.

The only figure with a slightly heavier Qi signature stood behind the cloud cluster on the far left.
