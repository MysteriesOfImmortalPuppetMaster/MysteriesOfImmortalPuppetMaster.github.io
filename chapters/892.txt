Chapter 564: Starting From a Humble Abode is the Same Since Ancient Times; Why Seek a Tall Tree for Shade? (Bonus Chapter)

Chunyangzi snorted coldly and glared at Ning Zhuo. "Is this your attitude toward repaying debts? Doesn't this mean you can just drag it out forever?"

Ning Zhuo chuckled, his expression calm and assured, brimming with confidence.

He extended his hand, palm facing up, gesturing first to Chunyangzi, then bent his arm and placed his palm flat against his own chest. "Senior, since I, Ning Zhuo, have spoken publicly and acknowledged this debt, naturally, I will repay it."

"I am betting my reputation, my future in the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and most importantly, my family name."

"I, Ning Zhuo, intend to revitalize my family's estate. How could I possibly disgrace our family traditions?"

Chunyangzi almost rolled his eyes, thinking to himself, "I don't even know which family is yours."

He fell silent.

He was secretly alarmed by Ning Zhuo's Righteous Path maneuvers.

Ning Zhuo taking the initiative to acknowledge the debt was a brilliant move. If he hadn't done this, he would have become a public target - the common enemy of Chunyangzi, Cangyazi, the Red-Robed Guest, the Nine-Fire Dragon Lord, Zhongli Mei, and the other Golden Core and Foundation Establishment cultivators.

Among them, most had emptied their coffers to participate in the competition.

By seizing control of the Nanming Fire Furnace, Ning Zhuo had essentially wiped out their life savings in one fell swoop.

What was even more terrifying didn't stop there.

Behind these individuals were financial backers - Danxia Peak, the Commerce Hall, Baicao Peak, the Artifact Refining Hall, and so on. These were all powerful factions and domains within the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

As a result, Ning Zhuo had offended these people. Even if he joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect in the future, his room to maneuver would be extremely narrow.

His future development within the Myriad Manifestations Sect, even if not an arduous struggle, would hit walls at every turn.

Now, by taking the initiative to acknowledge the debt, Ning Zhuo seemingly made a massive sacrifice, but in reality, he gained far more!

On one hand, he earned a tremendous reputation. From the public's perspective, Ning Zhuo absolutely didn't need to do this, yet he went ahead and accepted these massive debts. This was truly honorable; some might even think Ning Zhuo was adorably foolish.

On the other hand, by having these creditors, Ning Zhuo effectively transformed countless enemies into friends, or rather, allies bound by mutual interests. Since the creditors wanted Ning Zhuo to repay them, they would inevitably have to support him, protect him, and even promote him. Moreover, the creditors would guard against one another, competing to ensure they were Ning Zhuo's priority for repayment.

With this one step back, Ning Zhuo opened up a sky full of boundless possibilities. He instantly cleared his gloomy future, dispersing the dark clouds to reveal a brilliant, bright path ahead.

What made Chunyangzi curse him as shameless while simultaneously marveling at him was Ning Zhuo's attitude toward repayment.

The latter's stance was very clear - I promise to repay, but I cannot guarantee when.

Had it been anyone else, Chunyangzi would have slapped them to their senses long ago.

But Ning Zhuo?

Chunyangzi absolutely could not do that!

Because even though Ning Zhuo was a newcomer who had yet to officially join the Myriad Manifestations Sect, the Demon Subjugation Hall stood behind him. Furthermore, if he wished, he could also rely on Danxia Peak or Wanshou Peak.

Given the Cultivation arts Ning Zhuo had displayed, if he wanted to join the Artifact Refining Hall, they would gleefully accept him.

Not to mention his attainments in Formation setup. At the very least, Chunyangzi knew for a fact that high-level cultivators from Zhongzhen Peak had already contacted Ning Zhuo in private.

Zhongzhen Peak was the premier primary peak of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and its Peak Master was the current Sect Master of the Myriad Manifestations Sect!

It could be said that it wasn't these main peaks and factions choosing Ning Zhuo, but Ning Zhuo choosing them.

Not to mention, Ning Zhuo also possessed a mysterious background. All of these factors combined meant that even a Great Cultivator of the Nascent Soul-level like Chunyangzi wouldn't dare offend Ning Zhuo easily.

Aside from this, there was another scene etched deep within Chunyangzi's heart that left a profound impression.

It was the moment Ning Zhuo erupted with Noble Qi, the pillar of light shooting straight beyond the highest heavens, intensely roaring a message to everyone around him - Look! I belong to the Righteous Path! I am an honorable gentleman!!

And now, having contacted Ning Zhuo privately, a brief exchange was all it took for Chunyangzi to recognize Ning Zhuo's political acumen.

Coupling this with Ning Zhuo's relaxed, peer-to-peer negotiating attitude, all these factors stacked together, causing Chunyangzi to subconsciously treat Ning Zhuo as an equal.

"Little Friend Ning Zhuo's methods are truly outstanding," Chunyangzi sighed, revealing a bitter smile. "My Pure Yang Palace truly suffered a devastating blow this time for the sake of the Nanming Fire Furnace."

His words were a genuine display of emotion, also serving to close the distance between himself and Ning Zhuo.

Who could have known that Ning Zhuo would steer the conversation elsewhere: "This junior understands Senior's intentions and the current predicament of the Pure Yang Palace. Therefore, the reason I invited Senior here today - aside from settling the debt between us - was for another matter entirely."

Chunyangzi was curious. "Oh?"

In the next moment, Ning Zhuo gazed intently at Chunyangzi. His expression was incredibly calm, even carrying a certain sense of inevitability. "This junior wishes to invite Senior to join us. Of course, it would be even better if Senior brought the entirety of Pure Yang Palace along to join as well."

Chunyangzi froze.

He almost thought he had misheard.

However, there was not the slightest hint of a joke in Ning Zhuo's expression.

The big-headed youth solemnly took out a contract and handed it to Chunyangzi. "This is a contract that Senior Zhongli Mei and I secretly signed just moments ago. Please look it over, Senior."

Chunyangzi didn't take it immediately. "Are you sure you want me to read this?"

He already regarded Ning Zhuo as an equal, so he was somewhat worried that Ning Zhuo was digging a pit for him. Yet, at the same time, he possessed an even greater curiosity - Zhongli Mei undoubtedly represented Wang Yu and Danxia Peak, while Ning Zhuo had the Demon Subjugation Hall standing behind him. Did these two parties actually sign a secret pact?

Chunyangzi didn't suspect the contract was fake or that Ning Zhuo was lying.

Because that would be far too easy to verify.

The mere fact that the two parties had signed a secret pact allowed Chunyangzi to sense the surging undercurrents at play. He understood that the superficial antagonistic relationship had already undergone some profound transformation.

Were it someone else - such as the Furnace Immortal Tao Liweng, who devoted himself solely to ascetic cultivation - they might not have been interested.

But Chunyangzi was the supreme authority of Pure Yang Palace; he had a razor-sharp sensitivity to power, political landscapes, and strategy.

Therefore, when Ning Zhuo saw him hesitate to take it, he wasn't anxious in the least and merely waited quietly.

Sure enough, after just a few breaths, Chunyangzi couldn't bear the itch in his heart and proactively picked up the freshly signed, still-warm contract.

The content of the contract was very simple: It was an agreement between Ning Zhuo and Danxia Peak. If he did not join the Demon Subjugation Hall in the future, Danxia Peak would forgive half of Ning Zhuo's debt!

However, falling into Chunyangzi's eyes, it held a far deeper interpretation. Almost instantly, he connected the dots to a much broader picture.

Chunyangzi stared at Ning Zhuo. "Interesting. If it were anyone else, they would be beside themselves with joy. But you actually... don't want to join the Demon Subjugation Hall?"

Ning Zhuo nodded, then shook his head. "To be valued by Lord Zhong Dao of the Demon Subjugation Hall is truly the fortune of three lifetimes. I have sought his help twice now and owe him favors. These are also debts, and I will repay them!"

"It's not a certainty that I won't join the Demon Subjugation Hall, but it is merely one possibility for me."

"The reason the future is so beautiful and captivating is precisely because it holds infinite possibilities, isn't it?"

Chunyangzi remained silent for a moment. "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, you are ambitious. But do you know what terms Wang Yu offered me?"

"A subordinate peak, an independent establishment, the position of Vice Peak Master for myself, and priority resources from Danxia Peak."

"And now you're asking me to join you? This is simply preposterous!"

Ning Zhuo laughed aloud. "Starting from a humble abode has been the same since ancient times; why seek a tall tree for shade? When I stand atop the peak in the years to come, I will smile and ask who among them granted me my success."

Chunyangzi's body and mind jolted. In that moment, he felt an intense youthful spirit, confidence, and heroic ambition radiating from the young man before him.

Following that, Ning Zhuo spoke with fervor and assurance to Chunyangzi.

A short while later, Chunyangzi left Danxia Peak, his face filled with deep contemplation.

Ning Zhuo's plan hadn't fully convinced him, yet his estimation of Ning Zhuo had risen to an entirely new level. "Although this child is young, he already possesses the demeanor of a chess master moving the pieces. Based on this alone, his future is limitless! Whose family does this child belong to? To actually be willing to cast him into another sect!"

Although Chunyangzi had refused Ning Zhuo, Ning Zhuo wasn't disheartened. Instead, he seized the time to have a face-to-face meeting with a third cultivator.

This third person was none other than the Nine-Fire Dragon Lord.

If ranked according to contribution, this cultivator should have been second. But who could blame the fact that he was a Demon Cultivator?

By placing him third, Ning Zhuo had already demonstrated a high degree of respect.

The Nine-Fire Dragon Lord practically barged through the door.

His situation was far from good.

He had borrowed a massive amount of wealth from the black market, an amount equivalent to more than ten times his net worth.

The moment the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit acknowledged Ning Zhuo as its master, the Nine-Fire Dragon Lord's vision went black. He was immediately consumed by shock and fury, and a surging, torrential killing intent directed at Ning Zhuo erupted from the bottom of his heart.

He was unwilling to accept this!

The price he paid was simply too immense.

Aside from his staggering debt, he had also consumed his Dragon Pearl. This was the only memento left by his father, and he had merged it into the Nanming Fire Furnace to increase his chances of winning.

Ultimately, the victor was not him, but Ning Zhuo.

It could be said that Ning Zhuo now possessed his father's relic.

"Ning Zhuo." The Nine-Fire Dragon Lord's voice was hoarse and low. "You are a smart person to proactively acknowledge the debt. Since that's the case, I won't beat around the bush with you. Repay me right now and wipe my debt clean."

The Nine-Fire Dragon Lord's attitude was overbearing, and his Nascent Soul aura made him seem even more aggressive.

Ning Zhuo, however, paid it no mind. "Senior Nine-Fire Dragon Lord, this junior greatly admires your courage to go as far as borrowing from the black market for the sake of the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"However, it is impossible for me to repay you right now."

"Because I cannot afford it at the moment."

The Nine-Fire Dragon Lord was just about to flare up when Ning Zhuo raised a hand to stop him. "I haven't said this solely to you. I gave the exact same response to Lord Zhongli Mei and Senior Chunyangzi before you. Both of them have already agreed to my terms."

The Nine-Fire Dragon Lord snorted coldly. "Then offer me the same terms, and prioritize my repayment. Exactly when will you be able to pay me back?"

He appeared extremely impatient.

He had no choice.

He had borrowed too much wealth from the black market, resulting in monthly interest rates so high they gave him a massive headache.

Ning Zhuo smiled. "Senior, even if I wanted to prioritize repaying your debt... would you dare to collect it before Zhongli Mei, Cangyazi, and the others?"

The Nine-Fire Dragon Lord's body stiffened.

He wasn't the sole creditor! Ning Zhuo's words made him realize that he wasn't just dealing with a lowly Foundation Establishment cultivator standing before him, but rather an entire consortium of creditors centered around Ning Zhuo!

Ning Zhuo maintained his smile. "This junior also knows a thing or two about Senior's current predicament. Perhaps if Senior joins us, it might slightly alleviate the burden of your loans."

The Nine-Fire Dragon Lord frowned. "What do you mean?"
