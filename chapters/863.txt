Chapter 863: Qi Luck's Self-Salvation

The Qi Luck arm representing Ning Zhuo existed somewhere between ethereal and solid, between illusion and reality. This arm was noticeably thicker and more solid than the last time it was observed.

Previously, layers of formation lines were still obvious upon it, but now they had been internalized into the lines of the arm and palm. This indicated that Ning Zhuo had become more adept and profound in his mastery of a certain skill.

The nebula that the Qi Luck arm previously grasped had already vanished without a trace. However, from the blue starlight occasionally flashing across the lines of Ning Zhuo's arm, one could tell - the item represented by the nebula's Qi Luck had also fallen into Ning Zhuo's hands and had been deeply mastered by him.

"What boundless fortuitous encounters this child has."

"But what a pity, he is about to die..."

The expressions of the three Supreme Clan Elders were quite complex.

Regardless of how solid the Qi Luck arm was, how many fortuitous encounters Ning Zhuo had absorbed, or how much he had grown, the external crisis he was currently facing was simply too massive.

The surrounding clouds shifted, taking the form of a heavy, pale coffin that had thoroughly and tightly enclosed Ning Zhuo's entire Qi Luck arm.

The coffin lid had also taken shape, and it was only a hair's breadth away from completely shutting.

As for the previous paper and ink rain, or the thorny vines, although they were still there, compared to the calamity of the coffin, they paled in comparison and were not even worth mentioning.

"Just what kind of entity has this brat offended to bring about such a desperate situation?"

"In comparison, the other calamities are entirely insignificant."

"He really knows how to stir up trouble."

The three Supreme Clan Elders sighed endlessly.

-----

If Ning Zhuo were to see this scene and hear the inner thoughts of the three Supreme Clan Elders, he would definitely feel wronged.

He was truly innocent.

This time, he was caught in the crossfire while doing absolutely nothing. It perfectly echoed the old saying - sitting at home, disaster falls from the sky.

Ning Zhuo also sensed the fatal killing intent from the unseen world.

He was completely drenched in sweat. He had continuously asked over ten questions, and every single time, it provoked a contraction from his Mechanism Ring.

He was well aware that he had fallen into a situation where he was besieged on all sides. No matter what he chose, it would be a dead end.

"Then, what if I cremate this Nascent Soul?"

Finally, Ning Zhuo asked the right question.

The Mechanism Ring did not react any further.

Ning Zhuo's eyes suddenly lit up. He hurriedly asked a wrong question on purpose to verify.

As expected, the Mechanism Ring slightly contracted again.

"Understood! I'm going to cremate it!"

Cremation Prajna Spirit Release Sutra!

Ning Zhuo didn't hesitate and immediately activated this marvelous method.

Accompanied by the consumption of Ning Zhuo's spiritual energy, a cluster of flames fell onto the Nascent Soul's body.

The Nascent Soul shuddered violently, the cracks all over its body lighting up simultaneously as it let out an extremely faint, shrill wail. The sound was like a crying infant or a mourning baby bird, sounding intensely sorrowful.

Ning Zhuo remained expressionless, his hands forming hand seals at an unchanged speed.

The flames burned more and more vigorously, gradually shifting from crimson to golden-red, from golden-red to pure gold, and from pure gold to a glaze-like transparency.

The flames burned brightly yet silently. Every tongue of fire swayed in the air, sometimes leaping up rapidly, sometimes swaying gently, as if an invisible hand was directing them.

The dance of the flames was light and elegant, rippling with a Buddhist Sect halo. That halo took on a faint golden color, warm and gentle, enveloping the Nascent Soul like a layer of thin gauze.

The Nascent Soul's body began to melt, like ice and snow thawing, like a candle dripping, like morning mist dispersing.

Its pure white body gradually softened, deformed, and turned transparent in the flames. That layer of purple-black mist surged madly, attempting to resist the burning flames. But the flames permeated everything, stripping it away layer by layer, devouring it layer by layer.

Suddenly, the Nascent Soul changed drastically. Its entire body turned purple-black, while a layer of pure white halo rippled on its surface.

A thick wave of purple-black Demonic Qi gushed out, violent and fierce, struggling madly in the flames like a wounded beast.

Ning Zhuo furrowed his brow, secretly shocked. "What's going on with this Nascent Soul? On the surface, it's a Righteous Path Nascent Soul, but in reality, it's a Demonic Cultivator? Impressive, I was almost fooled myself."

The Nascent Soul's body began to crack, and the gushing Demonic Qi manifested into all kinds of human faces.

These faces were wailing, cursing, and scolding. They were all marks of resentment left behind after being devoured by the demon seed.

Seeing this, Ning Zhuo's resolve firmed even more. "This person has committed profound slaughter, even engaging in torture and sadistic killing. He is definitely not a righteous figure."

The Nascent Soul's will to survive was stronger than Ning Zhuo had imagined.

This was all because the blood mist demon seed, acting as the main resource to enlighten and form the Nascent Soul, occupied a much larger portion than two Confucian Dao Golden Cores combined.

Demon seeds throughout generations all possessed an intense will to live.

Naturally, the Demonic Confucian Nascent Soul, transformed with the seed as the core, was no different.

Therefore, at this moment, it was unwilling to be incinerated, unwilling to dissipate just like this, struggling and resisting desperately.

Ning Zhuo formed hand seals with both hands, activating the spell with all his might, his will unwavering.

The flames continued to burn, eventually incinerating the purple-black Demonic Qi until nothing was left.

The Nascent Soul transformed again, taking on a Confucian appearance.

It looked at Ning Zhuo, its gaze like that of seeing an old acquaintance. Then, sweeping a glance around, it suddenly revealed a smile of relief.

It lowered its head and softly murmured, "A scholar with high ideals and a benevolent man will not seek to live at the expense of injuring their benevolence. They will even sacrifice their lives to complete their benevolence."

The moment the words fell, its body abruptly disintegrated, no longer retaining a human shape. It rapidly melted, turning into a cluster of semi-transparent liquid flowing slowly within the flames.

With the secondary consciousness already annihilated, Qin De's primary consciousness was violently awakened by the intense pain of the burning flames.

He observed his surroundings and immediately felt as if the sky had collapsed!

Just a moment ago, he had barely escaped the void-shattering turbulent currents, and now he was being buried in a sea of fire.

The golden-red flames, as gorgeous as glazed glass, were completely silent yet incomparably scorching, burning away everything he had left.

Qin De struggled desperately, only to find that he didn't have a single ounce of strength left.

He had already been sealed layer by layer by Ning Zhuo previously, completely locked down.

"No - !" A massive wave of terror surged in his heart. He didn't want to die. He had gone through so much trouble to escape the cloud prison, to cultivate to the Nascent Soul stage, to finally see hope.

His own power was tantamount to nothing; all he had left was to beg for mercy.

He decisively pleaded, "Stop! Stop! Don't burn me, stop burning me!"

His voice drifted out from the flames, hoarse and weak, like a flickering candle in the wind.

"What kind of mess is this? It's as if they keep changing people." Ning Zhuo frowned, feeling doubtful, but he didn't stop his hands.

The flames continued to burn.

"You can't burn me! You can't burn me! I am... I am from the Myriad Manifestations Sect! I am a Golden Core cultivator! I am... I'm also a Confucian cultivator! You can't burn me!"

Ning Zhuo raised an eyebrow, thinking to himself, "A Confucian cultivator? Hmph, he finally remembered?"

He was just about to pause slightly when the Mechanism Ring contracted again, reminding him not to slack off in the slightest!

Thus, Ning Zhuo was instantly alerted and remained unmoved.

-----

The Buddhist sutra flames seemed to blaze even fiercer.

The trees of the Yi Forest rustled in the wind.

Shao Qiannong closed his eyes in divination, having already reached a critical point.

He knew the progress in his heart. "Just a little bit more."

-----

Ban Clan, Ancestral Shrine.

"Just a little bit more." The three Supreme Clan Elders had been closely monitoring the changes in Ning Zhuo's Qi Luck.

"If Ning Zhuo dies, what should we do about our clan's Royal Mandate Human Tribulation?"

"How would I know? This is the first time in history that a Royal Mandate has appeared in our clan. And this Royal Mandate is so special!"

"I only know that this tribulation destiny is too terrifying. We just need to quietly observe from the sidelines."

"Perhaps Ban Ji doesn't even need to make a move, and his Human Tribulation will collapse on its own? Or perhaps, a new Human Tribulation will spawn?"

"Looking at it from another angle, is this where the Royal Mandate leads? Even if one doesn't take action, the calamity will eliminate itself?"

At this moment, within the miraculous Qi Luck scene manifested by the Clan Fortune Pivot Chain, the lid of the pale coffin had basically closed. With just a tiny bit more, it would completely bury Ning Zhuo's Qi Luck arm!

Suddenly, a trace of Buddhist Light rippled out from Ning Zhuo's Qi Luck arm.

The three Supreme Clan Elders immediately noticed this change, their expressions varying.

"It seems he has already reacted and is currently using a certain Buddhist Sect method."

"This kid's main cultivation isn't a Buddhist Sect Cultivation Art. It seems this is due to a treasure in his hands."

"As expected of the Royal Mandate's Human Tribulation, to have so many treasures! It can actually influence such a perilous death tribulation!"

The Supreme Clan Elders observed carefully, finding that the Buddhist Light was struggling to pierce through the coffin, but the effect was subpar.

It was obvious that the speed at which the Buddhist Light pierced through couldn't match the speed of the coffin lid closing.

-----

Bluestone Cave Mansion.

In the Cultivation Chamber, Qin De's primary consciousness spoke at lightning speed to beg for his life. "I have many secrets, much intelligence! I can tell you everything and make you a man above men."

"I am a Confucian cultivator. I know countless Confucian cultivator classics. You can take them out and sell them for money!"

"I'm also, also a Demonic Cultivator. I also know Demonic Sect Cultivation Arts. Peerless Cultivation Arts, yes! I have a peerless Cultivation Art! If you get it, you will definitely become the head of ten thousand immortals in the future."

Ning Zhuo turned a deaf ear!

He sensed that the Buddha Heart Demon Seal within his divine sea had been triggered, blooming with brilliant Buddhist Light.

This was a destiny-suppressing treasure. Every time it was triggered, it was no small matter.

Ning Zhuo dared not be the slightest bit careless, focusing solely on refining the bizarre Nascent Soul before him.

-----

Outside the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

"Not good, not good, not good!" Xiao Juxia pinched his fingers in secret calculation, shaking his head repeatedly, a trace of rare panic in his expression.

He was willing to risk everything this time to sneak into the main gate of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, solely because he had calculated a fortuitous encounter, where his karma would bear fruit in this place.

The fated person was none other than Qin De.

But at this moment, Xiao Juxia calculated that the "great fruit" borne by Qin De would very likely end in total ruin, leaving him with absolutely nothing in the end!

"The Myriad Laws Demonizing Art is a peerless Cultivation Art. Every time it is perfected, it consumes a massive amount of Qi Luck. This Flying Cloud Assembly of the Myriad Manifestations Sect is exactly a gathering of wind and clouds, reaching the extent where cause and effect will bear fruit."

"Such an opportunity is rare to begin with. Being able to reach the final result is even more precious!"

"The crucial point is that it's only a single step away. Even if I have to sacrifice this body, I must push it to succeed."

Thinking of this, Xiao Juxia immediately formed a resolute intent, promptly taking out a Rope Dragon Fortune-Burning Lamp.

The lamp was three point three inches tall, cast from crimson copper. Its base was shaped like lotus petals, with twelve petals layered upon one another, each carved with intricate fire patterns. The lamp stand was like a spear, straight and tall, wrapped with a yellow rope.

The rope was woven incredibly well, vividly resembling a long dragon.

Xiao Juxia gripped the lamp body and activated it with his divine sense.

In the next moment, lamp oil spawned from within the Rope Dragon Fortune-Burning Lamp, rising rapidly.

As a price, Xiao Juxia's physical body's vital essence blood, spiritual energy, and even his lifespan were drastically depleted.

By the time the lamp oil reached a height of one inch, Xiao Juxia had already become skin and bones, with a head full of white hair and deep wrinkles.

He stopped immediately and blew softly on it.

The rope dragon, acting as the wick, had long been soaked in lamp oil. At this moment, it spontaneously combusted without a flame, a spark of fire shooting up from the dragon's head.

Snap.

With a crisp sound, the fire suddenly popped, immediately leaping up to an inch in height.

The lamp oil accumulated within the lamp's body rapidly decreased at a rate visible to the naked eye.

Xiao Juxia's expression changed slightly. "Boiling oil over a raging fire, Qi Luck's self-salvation! This is a completely spontaneous act. Qin De's situation is likely extremely bad."

"Sigh, I only hope my action hasn't been in vain..."

At the same time.

-----

Ban Clan Ancestral Shrine.

The Supreme Clan Elders were surprised to find that Ning Zhuo's Qi Luck manifestation had changed again.

Withered-yellow Demonic Qi gushed out from the Qi Luck arm. Within the Demonic Qi, a demonic star faintly flickered, but had already begun to reveal itself.

Influenced by the Demonic Qi, the Buddhist Light radiating from the Qi Luck arm quickly dimmed, struggling to hold on. Consequently, the speed at which the outer cloud-white coffin was shutting noticeably accelerated.

-----

Bluestone Cave Mansion.

"Hm?!" Ning Zhuo cried out in astonishment and doubt.

He discovered that the Buddha Heart Demon Seal in his divine sea was trembling. It had previously emitted Buddhist Light, but now it was producing a demonic aura.

The demonic aura grew increasingly dense, clearly interfering with the Buddhist Light. The clash between Buddha and Demon caused the power of the Buddha Heart Demon Seal to plummet.

This was the first time Ning Zhuo had seen the Buddha Heart Demon Seal simultaneously unleash the powers of both the Buddhist and Demonic Paths.

A sense of foreboding suddenly welled up in the big-headed youth's heart. "Strange! This magic treasure accommodates both the Buddhist and Demonic Paths. They should be one entity, so why are they infighting at this moment?"

Meanwhile, the Demonic Nascent Soul Qin De, currently being cremated, was still pleading for mercy, saying, "Let me go. I will give you all my Cultivation Arts and secret stashes. Besides the peerless Cultivation Art, I, I also created the Sage..."

-----

At this moment, a star abruptly lit up on Ning Zhuo's Qi Luck arm.

The Ban Clan's Supreme Clan Elders' hearts jolted, and someone softly exclaimed, "Ning Zhuo's Demonic Path destiny star has completely lit up!"

If Ning Zhuo had heard the rest and recognized the Sage's Grand Thief Scripture, he would likely guess Qin De's identity immediately, and might very well choose to pause and take a risk for Sun Lingtong.

Because he knew that after cremation, only spiritual energy would remain, and any Sage's Grand Thief Scripture would be completely burned away!

"Young Master, Young Master!"

Ning Zhuo's Divine Art was suddenly triggered, and at this critical moment, he couldn't help but be distracted.

This was because Qing Chi was transmitting a distress message to Ning Zhuo through Life Hanging by a Thread - "Young Master, we are about to be unable to hold on! Can you help me?"

This immediately caught Ning Zhuo's attention!

Ning Zhuo felt quite a bit of guilt towards Qing Chi, and he genuinely wanted to take good care of her.

Previously, Qing Chi had been dispatched by Ning Zhuo, which was why she accepted Sun Lingtong's invitation to join a certain Cloud-Raising Minor Trial.

This minor trial mainly involved both sides taking the stage to craft talismans on the spot and engage in a talisman battle right then and there.

Right now, Qing Chi and Sun Lingtong had run into a tough opponent.

"How do you need my help?" Ning Zhuo knew that even if he diverted his attention, it wouldn't slow down the speed of cremating the Nascent Soul, so he decisively transmitted his thought using his divine sense.
