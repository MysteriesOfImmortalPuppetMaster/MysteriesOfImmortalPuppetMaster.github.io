Chapter 899: Situ Xing vs Ning Zhuo - Night Battle

The night was like a curtain, dotted with stars.

This was not out in the open air, but inside a martial arts arena of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

At the outermost perimeter stood twelve Sunken Star Golden Pillars, their bodies carved with complex formation patterns. The ground was paved with Starshine stone slabs, black with a hint of blue. At this moment, they emitted faint, silvery-white specks of light. Walking upon them felt as if one were treading on the night sky itself.

Two cultivators arrived here side by side.

The one on the left had a lean figure, skin like cold jade, and above-average looks. His hair was tied up with an ebony hairpin, and he fiddled with a circular jade pendant in his hand, exuding the bearing of a Young Master from a prominent family.

The one on the right wore a deep azure long robe trimmed with golden threads. His facial features were sharply defined, and his eyes were calm and profound, as if hiding a deep valley of dense ancient trees.

They were none other than Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong.

The two had been invited to watch the battle and were also its most important witnesses.

As they teleported in, their first glance landed on Situ Xing, who was waiting at the side of the arena.

Situ Xing was sitting cross-legged in meditation, his eyes slightly closed.

He had silver hair and wore a blue robe, with a flying sword floating in front of him.

The sword blade was a profound starry blue and semi-transparent, containing countless tiny specks of starlight that flowed slowly like a galaxy. The silvery-white spine of the sword flickered in time with Situ Xing's breathing. The sword guard was shaped like an astrolabe and rotated slowly, bearing a formation diagram of the cosmic stars on its surface.

This was Situ Xing's Lifebound Flying Sword - the Star-Guiding Sword!

Situ Xing's breathing was not only connected to his Lifebound Flying Sword, but at the same time, it resonated continuously with the stellar phenomenon overhead.

Countless streams of starlight were drawn in, ultimately converging onto the man and the sword. This caused Situ Xing's entire body to overflow with starlight, his aura growing increasingly surging and powerful.

Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong were inwardly astonished and conversed in low voices.

"Fellow Daoist Situ's aura is a few degrees stronger than when he fought Zhu Fenxiang."

"It seems that battle gave him profound insights."

"This martial arts arena he chose is also famous for night battles. It's spacious and unobstructed, making it highly suitable for him to display his abilities. This could practically be called Fellow Daoist Situ's home ground."

At this moment, Situ Xing slowly opened his eyes and looked at the two. "I wasn't the one who chose this battlefield."

Lin Jinglong and Shen Xi quickly exchanged glances, both feeling surprised.

"It was actually Ning Zhuo who chose it?"

"He clearly knows Fellow Daoist Situ's intelligence; how could he make such a choice?!"

The impact of this scheduled battle between Situ Xing and Ning Zhuo would likely be immense.

Among the first batch of geniuses to emerge in this session of the Flying Cloud Assembly, these two were outstanding figures.

Ning Zhuo had been in the limelight recently, especially when he used the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone to release a pillar of vast righteous qi, which shook the entire Myriad Manifestations Sect. Subsequently, he became the owner of the Nanming Fire Furnace - a result that exceeded everyone's expectations.

Although Situ Xing wasn't as prominent as him, he had meticulously chosen his battles, and his win rate in the various Rising Cloud Minor Trials was exceptionally high, securing first place in many of them. Such a combat record was stunning, allowing him to exchange for an identity token that was even a grade superior to Ning Zhuo's.

In terms of reputation, Situ Xing had become famous at a young age, and he was renowned even across the entire Flying Cloud Kingdom. In this regard, his foundation was much deeper than Ning Zhuo's.

In this battle between the two, the victor would inevitably use the loser as a stepping stone to elevate their own reputation to the next level. The winner goes up, the loser goes down. It could possibly have even more impact on what was to follow.

Such situations rarely occurred unless the two parties had deep-seated conflicts. For example, Situ Xing and Zhu Fenxiang.

Yet, a battle still broke out between Ning Zhuo and Situ Xing!

It was difficult for outsiders to understand why this battle was taking place, but Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong were insiders. Having jointly experienced the causes and consequences, both knew this battle was inevitable.

"For Ning Zhuo to deliberately choose this martial arts hall, there seems to be only one reason!"

"Yes. He wants to let Situ Xing utilize his full potential, so that when he loses, he will have absolutely no excuses to shirk and must concede defeat."

"This shows that Ning Zhuo has exceedingly abundant confidence."

"Or rather, is he being overly arrogant?"

Shen Xi sighed and asked Lin Jinglong, "You've fought him before, do you still not know his true strength?"

Lin Jinglong had indeed clashed with Ning Zhuo; at the time, it was Ning Zhuo who had taken the initiative to deliver a letter of challenge.

The cause of that battle was Ning Zhuo poaching Gongsun Yan and undermining his foundation. Furious, Lin Jinglong accepted the challenge. Ultimately, under Ning Zhuo's proactive proposal, both sides stopped in time.

Lin Jinglong coughed. "Our battle was merely a sparring match, stopped early and counted as a draw. I didn't use my trump cards, and neither did Ning Zhuo."

"In today's battle, I'm afraid both of them will have to use their full strength."

Shen Xi nodded.

He knew that Situ Xing had a reason he must win. This small faction was built up by Situ Xing. If the latter lost, he could only step down and yield his position to someone more capable. At that time, Situ Xing's reputation would take a massive hit, and he would inevitably break away to establish another faction elsewhere.

Situ Xing carried the expectations of the Situ Clan; he was the family's figurehead among Foundation Establishment stage cultivators. Therefore, it was impossible for Situ Xing to subordinate himself to Ning Zhuo after a defeat.

Because Ning Zhuo was just an outsider cultivator, and his background had always been unclear.

Submitting to Ning Zhuo would cause a long-term erosion of Situ Xing's personal reputation, as well as that of the Situ Clan.

Originally, Shen Xi felt that Ning Zhuo might not have such great battle intent. But now, just hearing that Ning Zhuo had proactively chosen this arena immediately changed his mind.

"Compared to before, Ning Zhuo's actions are much more sharp and imposing," Shen Xi sighed with emotion in his heart.

When Ning Zhuo first arrived at the main gate of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he came on the same teleportation array as him. It could be said that he was one of the earliest cultivators to establish a connection with Ning Zhuo.

In these two or three months, Ning Zhuo went from being an unknown to rising in fame.

Shen Xi had witnessed this process with his own eyes.

If Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong could see through Ning Zhuo's intentions, Situ Xing naturally could as well.

Ning Zhuo setting the competition venue here made Situ Xing feel slighted; the battle intent in his heart was already burning like a raging fire.

The space suddenly rippled.

Immediately after, the formation's light flashed, and a youth was teleported in from the outside.

Wearing white clothes and sporting a large head, it was none other than Ning Zhuo!

"Brother Shen, Brother Lin." Ning Zhuo walked up and cupped his hands in greeting, his voice mild. "Thank you both for coming to watch and bearing witness to today's battle."

The two returned the greeting.

Situ Xing took a light breath, and his flying sword entered his body. He stood up and turned to face Ning Zhuo. "You've arrived."

Ning Zhuo smiled lightly and looked at Situ Xing. "Brother Situ, I've kept you waiting. Are you ready? I'm in no rush; I can let you prepare a bit more thoroughly."

The corners of Situ Xing's eyes twitched slightly.

He took a deep breath, suppressing the surge of anger welling up from the bottom of his heart.

"Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo, if you want to goad me with this, you'll be disappointed," Situ Xing's voice grew cold. "It's a futile effort."

His gaze was icy. "If you truly underestimate me, I will use my flying sword to make you fully realize what a grave mistake you've made!"

Ning Zhuo, however, smiled delightedly. "With such fighting spirit, you will inevitably go all out. How could it be considered a futile effort?"

"Please." He turned sideways and raised his hand, pointing toward the center of the martial arts arena.

Situ Xing glanced at him but didn't speak further.

He turned and walked toward the center of the arena.

Ning Zhuo walked toward the other side, his pace neither hurried nor slow.

The two took their positions in the center of the arena, separated by a distance of thirty zhang.

Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong retreated to the edge of the arena, standing under one of the starry pillars.

Both of their gazes fell upon the two people in the center of the field, both harboring intense anticipation for this battle.

Ning Zhuo and Situ Xing stood facing each other; neither made the first move.

Situ Xing wore a Seven-Star Crown on his head, a Starlight Dharma Robe, a Stardust Cape on his back, Star-Whirl Bracers on his wrists, and a pair of Star-Track Boots on his feet. He could be said to be fully armed. Shrouded in starlight, he looked like a god of war walking out from the Milky Way.

Ning Zhuo, on the other hand, wore a long robe with wide sleeves, pure white and unadorned. With no extraneous items on his person, he was like a clear mountain breeze.

Situ Xing's brow furrowed even deeper, almost laughing out of anger.

It had been many years. He hadn't been underestimated like this in many years.

Situ Xing took a deep breath. "Fellow Daoist Ning Zhuo, since you specifically chose this martial arts hall and proactively yielded the advantage to me, then I shall yield three moves to you. You attack first."

As a genius cultivator who achieved fame at a young age, Situ Xing inherently possessed arrogance. From the moment he agreed to fight Ning Zhuo, he had never thought of taking any unfair advantage.

Ning Zhuo looked at Situ Xing, stayed silent for three breaths of time, and then chuckled softly. "Situ Xing is highly righteous. Then I shall respectfully obey rather than politely decline."

The large-headed youth could sense Situ Xing's firm resolve. In the next moment, he raised his hands and formed hand seals.

His ten fingers flew rapidly, like butterflies weaving through flowers. Every hand seal was incomparably precise, carrying a fluid and graceful beauty. At the same time, he chanted an incantation, his voice clear and rapid.

Utilizing hand seals and verbal incantations simultaneously - this scene alone completely revealed Ning Zhuo's profound attainment in Spells.

"Earth veins dormant, profound roots secluded, vines entwining, all things imprisoned. Arise!"

The stone slabs paved with Starshine stones emitted a muffled boom. Cracks spread in all directions from beneath Ning Zhuo's feet, expanding like a spiderweb.

Countless vines drilled out from the cracks.

They squirmed like pythons, spreading out toward all corners of the arena.

Ultimately, they climbed up the Sunken Star Golden Pillars at the very edge of the battlefield, coiling tightly around the bodies of the pillars.

The ground was completely covered by them, the silvery-white specks of the Starshine stone slabs entirely obscured beneath the emerald green.

"Ancient trees verdant, arboreal boundary boundless, heaven and earth change colors, spirit roots descend. Arrive!"

From amidst the thicket of vines, small saplings shot up from the ground and immediately expanded, transforming into giant trees. Every single one had deep roots and lush leaves, bearing the momentum of holding up the sky.

Seeing this familiar scene, the corner of Lin Jinglong's eyes twitched slightly. Back then, when he fought Ning Zhuo in the martial arts hall, he had suffered quite a bit under this very technique.

Vines the thickness of arms rapidly climbed the giant trees, meeting in mid-air along the branches. They intertwined, coiled, and stacked, forming a green dome in the air. The dome was about twenty zhang high and almost completely covered the entire arena.

The starlight was almost completely blocked out, and the ground was directly covered in layers of vines.

The air was filled with the scent of vegetation, and the Wood Element spiritual energy was dense to the extreme.

The chaining of two Wood Element Spells directly allowed Ning Zhuo to alter the battlefield.

"Pine winds howling, emerald waves surging, forest sea churning, overturning the earth. Sweep!" He cast another technique, using verbal incantations combined with hand seals.
