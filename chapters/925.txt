Chapter 925: A Single Battle Shocks the Golden Cores

"Ning Zhuo won?!"

"With a mid Foundation Establishment cultivation base, he defied the odds and beheaded a Golden Core cultivator!"

"He truly is a genius cultivator whose name shakes the Main Mountain Gate."

"Although it was somewhat expected, the combat power Ning Zhuo displayed throughout the entire process was no small matter. Aside from being suppressed at the very beginning, he quickly regained the upper hand."

The faces of all the cultivators watching the battle showed varying degrees of astonishment.

Jiang Xiaobian, Ah Huo, and the others had their eyes shining. When they looked at Ning Zhuo, their admiration was palpable.

Gu Huaijiu nodded secretly.

Pi Fujie stared at Liu Jinke's decapitated corpse and rejoiced in his heart: "Turning from an enemy into a friend with Ning Zhuo was the right choice!"

The Immortal in the Furnace, Tao Liweng, fell into silence, waves rippling in the lake of his heart. "Ning Zhuo really does have some skill to successfully kill a Golden Core cultivator."

"His attitude toward me is also understandable."

"However, my strength is far more comprehensive than Liu Jinke's. Ning Zhuo has ultimately underestimated me."

Tao Liweng's feelings of injustice were greatly reduced due to Ning Zhuo's performance.

Zhu Fenxiang transmitted a message with her divine sense to Zhu Guizhi, "Mother, you saw that, right? Don't worry about Ning Zhuo."

Zhu Guizhi carefully savored the joy hidden beneath these words and remained silent.

Jiang Ping frowned slightly, rapidly reviewing the entire battle in his mind. "Liu Jinke possesses the Innate Talent: Golden Liquid Reverted Core Body, giving him remarkable combat strength. If it were an ordinary cultivator, Liu Jinke would have steadily established an advantage."

"But Ning Zhuo sequentially executed two Fire Element Techniques, completely deciding the outcome of this battle."

"The first technique purified the [Gold-Turning Technique], stripping Liu Jinke of his advantage in a war of attrition. The second technique was even sharper, unexpectedly beheading him directly!"

"Is Ning Zhuo... really a mechanical cultivator?"

This doubt inevitably popped up from the bottom of Jiang Ping's heart.

Having similar doubts as him were Dong Nishang, Baicao Weng, and Mu Yuehua.

"Although Ning Zhuo deployed the Mechanical Flying Birds with great proficiency, he never used a single mechanical art from start to finish."

"On the contrary, his Five Elements Techniques are extremely formidable. Both the Wood Element and the Fire Element ones are commendable."

"Especially the Fire Element, Ning Zhuo is truly outstanding in it."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was highly sensitive to the Fire Element. Gazing at Ning Zhuo from afar, he thought, "What was that flaming blade technique he used at the end? Liu Jinke's personal defense should have been comparable to at least a heavy defensive magic treasure. Yet he was slain instantly; it was far too clean and decisive. Could this technique also subtly counter Liu Jinke's Innate Talent?"

"Both of Ning Zhuo's consecutive Fire Element Techniques perfectly countered Liu Jinke. His luck is truly quite good."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord couldn't help but appreciate Ning Zhuo even more.

The combat power Ning Zhuo displayed had earned his recognition to a certain degree.

After all, Ning Zhuo's safety was closely tied to the Nanming Stronghold. Seeing that Ning Zhuo, a critical focal point, wasn't a weak link was exactly what the Nine Fires Dragon Lord wanted to see.

Zhong Limei, disguised as Lone Cultivator Poison Annihilation, was fixated on the Medicinal Pill Ning Zhuo had deliberately taken.

He analyzed in his heart: "Ning Zhuo's two Fire Element Techniques laid the foundation for his victory in this battle. The reason his Fire Element Techniques had such explosively increased power - far surpassing his previous Wood Element techniques and reaching the Golden Core-level - was because of the Medicinal Pill he consumed."

"On the surface, this Medicinal Pill looked like a common Spirit Nourishing Pill, but it actually amplified the Fire Element. What exactly is its true identity?"

Zhong Limei had spent his entire life refining pills and prided himself on his vast horizons and sharp eye, yet he failed to see through the true nature of that Medicinal Pill. This caused curiosity to ripple in his heart.

His evaluation of Ning Zhuo subsequently rose another level.

Ye Qingming, Li Ku, and Jin Mantang wore grave expressions.

The successful establishment of the Nanming Stronghold would severely impact the old power structure of Flowing Cloud Peak. With this battle, Ning Zhuo had washed away the public's doubts regarding his combat power.

This youth with a mid Foundation Establishment cultivation base genuinely possessed Golden Core-level combat power!

He was no soft persimmon.

Among the three, Jin Mantang's thoughts were the most complex.

On the surface he appeared somber, but his eyes were fixed on Liu Jinke's corpse. "I had already gifted the [Jade-Shattering Hidden Thunder] to Liu Jinke, yet he didn't use it."

"But it doesn't matter. This [Jade-Shattering Hidden Thunder] can also sense its surroundings, especially after its owner dies in battle; it will constantly remain in a triggered state."

"So, Ning Zhuo, aren't you going to clean up the battlefield yet?"

On the martial arts arena, Ning Zhuo exhaled a breath of turbid qi.

"It's over."

He could clearly sense that Liu Jinke's life aura had already dissipated.

With a thought, he was just about to collect the enemy's corpse when the Mechanism Ring in his hand slightly contracted.

"Hm?!" Ning Zhuo instantly became alert.

"Is there some lurking danger that I haven't discovered?"

He had already used his divine sense several times in a row, sweeping it over Liu Jinke's corpse.

Ning Zhuo sat upright on the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot and immediately drove this mechanical vehicle, rapidly distancing himself and retreating to the edge of the battlefield.

At the same time, he left behind a few Mechanical Puppets, commanding them to pounce onto Liu Jinke's corpse.

This unusual scene immediately gave the spectating crowd a sense that something was off.

Jin Mantang's pupils shrank abruptly as astonishment filled his heart: "What's going on? My [Jade-Shattering Hidden Thunder] is a one-time use magic treasure that excels at concealment. Ning Zhuo didn't even use a detection technique; he was able to identify it just using his divine sense?"

Ning Zhuo's Mechanical Puppets were only the size of an adult's palm.

They were originally created by him for daily mechanism training.

Ning Zhuo controlled them with great proficiency, having once even used them to torture Han Ming. Using them now for a body search was just perfect.

"Damn it."

"At this distance, and with him being so vigilant, even if the hidden thunder is triggered, it won't be able to deliver a fatal blow to Ning Zhuo!"

Jin Mantang felt a deep sense of regret.

However, in the next moment, Jin Mantang's eyes widened, revealing a look of pure astonishment.

The spectating cultivators experienced almost the exact same feeling.

A barrage of golden needles suddenly exploded outward from Liu Jinke's corpse, instantly destroying the Mechanical Puppets one by one.

Immediately after, Liu Jinke's head transformed into a puddle of golden liquid, quickly flowing toward his body and merging into the cross-section of his neck, rapidly fusing back into one entity.

The golden liquid shifted for a moment before finally solidifying back into his head.

"Hah, hah, hah..."

Liu Jinke violently opened his eyes, panting rapidly like a patient just waking up from a nightmare. His face was covered in weakness and horror.

"I'm alive!" Liu Jinke hurriedly struggled to stand up.

He reached out his hand, touching his cheeks and then his neck, still in a state of shock.

Clearly, this was a resurrection he had experienced very few times.

The spectators were all amazed.

In fact, so was Ning Zhuo.

Just a moment ago, his divine sense had swept over him several times, confirming that Liu Jinke's life aura had completely dissipated. He never expected him to actually rise from the dead!

Among the cultivators present watching the battle, there were no less than five Nascent Soul cultivators, yet none of them had noticed anything fishy.

Liu Jinke's resurrection method was indeed quite something, extremely well-concealed.

"This should be related to his Innate Talent: [Golden Liquid Reverted Core Body]!"

"This kind of Innate Talent has long been recorded in the annals, but each cultivator's development of it differs greatly."

"Liu Jinke must have developed a resurrection method based on this Innate Talent."

The cultivators quickly guessed the truth.

Liu Jinke's expression was incredibly ugly. This was one of his most important trump cards. Having it completely exposed after this single battle was a massive loss for him, filling his heart with immense pain.

Expressionless, Ning Zhuo drove the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot, slowly flying back over.

"Did the Mechanism Ring give a warning just now because Liu Jinke possessed a resurrection method?"

"If I had been careless and approached him, would I have been suddenly ambushed by him?"

"But right after his resurrection, his counterattack was nothing spectacular. Did he hold something back?"

Ning Zhuo took out the zither he had previously stored away once more, placing it on his lap.

Seeing Ning Zhuo's posture indicating he was ready to fight again, Liu Jinke hurriedly waved his hands while flying backward. "Hold on, hold on. Ning Zhuo, I concede, I concede! No more fighting!"

Ning Zhuo sneered coldly. "Senior Brother, we have already signed a Life and Death Duel contract. Do you think you can just stop fighting whenever you want?"

Liu Jinke replied, "Although we signed a Life and Death Duel, the contract doesn't stipulate that neither of us can actively concede on the spot!"

Ning Zhuo fell silent for a moment before continuing his slow advance. "Didn't Senior Brother want to 'weigh' me?"

Liu Jinke's eyes bulged as he retreated even further. "My head was chopped off by you, what's left to weigh?! You're strong, you're amazing, I can't beat you, alright?!"

Seeing Ning Zhuo continuing to press closer, he hurriedly emphasized again, "Anyway, I concede!"

As far as Liu Jinke was concerned, he and Ning Zhuo held absolutely no grudge against each other. He only wanted to use this opportunity to act in order to repay the debt of gratitude for the covert funding from the Demon Subjugation Hall.

Essentially, he was just like Ning Zhuo; both were trying to pay off debts.

Therefore, for his battle against Ning Zhuo, Liu Jinke only possessed battle intent from beginning to end, with absolutely no killing intent.

Of course, he also absolutely never expected that when he closed in on Ning Zhuo, he would actually be slain by one of Ning Zhuo's Fire Element Techniques!

The Golden Core-level combat power Ning Zhuo displayed had made Liu Jinke take him completely seriously, leaving not a shred of underestimation behind. Thus, he had no psychological barrier to proactively conceding.

The only reason he signed the Life and Death Duel contract earlier was to save face, unable to back down.

Now that Liu Jinke himself had already been defeated in battle, it was tantamount to being kicked off the stage by Ning Zhuo, so he decisively conceded!

His previous surging battle intent had also completely vanished after walking the line between life and death once.

"Can I continue to fight him?" Ning Zhuo pondered, shaking his head in his heart.

He was a cultivator of the Righteous Path, and the opponent had already surrendered. If he continued to ruthlessly pursue and beat him, it would be a huge blow to Ning Zhuo's Righteous Path reputation.

"When it comes down to it, I haven't truly entered the Sect yet. Killing Liu Jinke at this moment would essentially be an outsider killing a member of the Myriad Manifestations Sect."

"This detail will definitely be dug up by others and used to attack me. At that time, I will be very politically passive!"

During previous Flying Cloud Assemblies, the newcomers all actively participated in the cloud-raising minor tests arranged by Sect members, making friends everywhere, and then attempting to cooperate to complete the various tasks of the Flying Cloud Assembly.

When would it ever escalate to the level of someone like Ning Zhuo beating and killing a Sect member?

If it were like earlier, where Liu Jinke was closing in and Ning Zhuo, facing a threat to his life, violently resisted and killed Liu Jinke, everyone could accept it.

But now, with Ning Zhuo being so aggressively overbearing, relentlessly pursuing and attacking a weaker person despite possessing a massive advantage... if this was spoken of, how would everyone view Ning Zhuo?

How would all the people at the Myriad Manifestations Sect's Main Gate view Ning Zhuo? You're an outsider, you clearly don't need to fight, and a member of our Sect has already conceded, yet you still kill him?!

Ning Zhuo thought for a short while and fully understood that with Liu Jinke's active surrender, this martial exhibition could no longer continue.

"However, perhaps I can take advantage of the situation and use Liu Jinke..."

A flash of inspiration crossed Ning Zhuo's divine sea, and he immediately sneered coldly. "Senior Brother, what you say makes sense."

"The contract for the Life and Death Duel indeed does not constrain either of us from conceding."

"In today's battle, I will allow you to escape with your life this one time."

"But you and I had no grudges in the past and no enmities recently. It was supposed to be our first time meeting, yet you came looking for trouble with me."

"With my Establishing an Alliance to Charge the Peak, those who should be finding trouble with my Nanming Stronghold should be factions like the Green Tea Society, Floating Life Society, and Thorns Society. Yet you, a rogue cultivator, suddenly showed up to 'weigh' me. You must have been instructed by someone."

"I won't ask you who instructed you either."

"But the grievance between us must be settled."

Liu Jinke's eyes widened, screaming in his heart: "The one who instructed me is the Demon Subjugation Hall! Lord Zhong Dao values you so highly, yet you want to Establish an Alliance to Charge the Peak. I came to teach you a lesson."

It was precisely because of this point that Liu Jinke simply conceded.

He only wanted to come and teach Ning Zhuo a lesson, never thinking of killing the other party. Before the battle started, although Liu Jinke was angered by being humiliated, he always kept a bottom line in his heart, reminding himself to spare Ning Zhuo's life.

As a result, he was severely taught a lesson by Ning Zhuo and even lost his life once!

Of course, Liu Jinke absolutely didn't dare to speak of the Demon Subjugation Hall's secret to outsiders.

Liu Jinke stood his ground and argued reasonably in the face of Ning Zhuo: "Wait a minute, Ning Zhuo!"

"What grievance is still left between us?"

"I admit, coming to find trouble with you was my mistake. But I was just killed by you a moment ago! Isn't one of my lives enough to settle it?"

Ning Zhuo sighed. "You forgot, I also treated you to a delicious meal."

Liu Jinke was so angry his eyebrows stood straight up. "That was a last meal before execution! My neck was already chopped off by you. Furthermore, I didn't even eat a single grain of rice!"

Ning Zhuo nodded, speaking seriously. "Senior Brother, you also know it was a last meal. Your head was chopped off, and then you reattached it. This makes me unable to fulfill my promise to you, going against my reputation. Once news of this battle spreads, the world will know that I 'do not keep my word.' As a dignified gentleman of the Righteous Path, how can I conduct myself like this?"

"I... you..." Liu Jinke was so furious he almost cursed out loud.

I managed to resurrect with great difficulty, reattaching my head in a terribly thrilling fashion, and it's still my fault?!

There is something seriously wrong with your concept of "keeping your word."

What kind of good person keeps their promises like this?!

Besides, since when did it become a promise you made to me?!

I absolutely refuse to admit it!

Where are there good people who make a promise to someone to chop off their head?

Are you really of the Righteous Path, or the Demonic Path?!

Liu Jinke was flushed bright red with anger, yet he managed to suppress it and didn't curse out loud.

Through this battle, Ning Zhuo had already firmly established a deterrence against Golden Core cultivators!

The other spectators also wore strange expressions upon hearing Ning Zhuo say this.

Ning Zhuo looked around and announced, "Today's martial exhibition will be left at this, we'll consider it a draw."

"Next, I will continue to challenge you, Senior Brother, in order to fulfill my promise of beheading."

Liu Jinke hurriedly waved his hands. "How is it a draw? You won, you definitely won, you achieved a complete victory!"

"You are of the Righteous Path, don't speak nonsense with your eyes wide open!"

He then blurted out, "I do not accept your challenge, I will not accept it! Farewell, farewell!"

Wanting to get away from Ning Zhuo immediately, he turned around and left.

After taking a few steps, he remained highly vigilant. Discovering that Ning Zhuo stayed in place and didn't intercept him, he walked even faster.

Ning Zhuo looked at his departing back. "Senior Brother, I don't believe that you can constantly cower and refuse to accept my successive challenges, considering I am merely a Foundation Establishment cultivator! At that time, how will the world look at you? Laugh at you?"

Stepping out of the martial arts arena, Liu Jinke thoroughly breathed a sigh of relief, turning a deaf ear to Ning Zhuo's words.

He grumbled inwardly, "I'd be a massive idiot to fight you again!"

"Compared to life and death, what does reputation count for?"

Making up his mind, he quickly disappeared from everyone's line of sight.

A gleam of cold light flashed in the depths of Ning Zhuo's eyes as he thought to himself, "Senior Brother, when the situation changes later, I'm afraid it won't be up to you whether you accept or not."

Liu Jinke was only a rogue cultivator and lacked political vision.

Ning Zhuo's simple sentence was actually a trap that he was almost guaranteed to fall into.

Cao Gui worried, "Young Master Ning Zhuo clearly already won, yet he still insists on clinging to Liu Jinke without letting go. Alas, perhaps this is exactly the temperament of a favored child of heaven!"

Witnessing all this, Shen Xi appeared lost in thought.
