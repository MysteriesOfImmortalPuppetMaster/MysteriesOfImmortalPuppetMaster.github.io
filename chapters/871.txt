Chapter 871: The Minor Trial Begins

After discussing things with Ning Zhuo, Sun Lingtong immediately and quietly left the Bluestone Cave Dwelling.

"Although the Nanming Fire Furnace was bombed and is on the verge of destruction, if it is successfully restored, its value will be extraordinary!"

Sun Lingtong had broad horizons, and coming from the Void Sect, his ability to judge the value of treasures was extremely strong.

"A Spirit Treasure alone is already rare and invaluable."

"Production-type treasures are like inexhaustible Spirit Stone veins; the cost to forge every single one far exceeds others of the same grade."

"Let alone the Spirit Treasure-grade Nanming Fire Furnace, even magic treasure-grade alchemy furnaces command extremely high prices on the market. The high-quality ones among them usually only appear at auctions."

"And the Nanming Fire Furnace is not just a Spirit Treasure-grade alchemy furnace; it is an outstanding existence even among its peers. It is one of the foundational reserves of Danxia Peak!"

Danxia Peak was one of the eight main peaks of the Myriad Manifestations Sect. The Nanming Fire Furnace was its foundational reserve, which was equivalent to being a foundational reserve of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

This was the foundational reserve of a super faction!

To make a comparison, the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone that Ning Zhuo wanted was one of the foundational reserves of the Demon Subjugation Hall.

But the Demon Subjugation Hall was merely one of the sixteen halls, while Danxia Peak was one of the eight main peaks. The White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone had a narrow range of applications, and the Noble Righteous Qi it produced was difficult to use elsewhere. The Nanming Fire Furnace was entirely different; its value far exceeded the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone by tens, if not a hundred times!

"With the Nanming Fire Furnace destroyed, Danxia Peak is out of options and can only host this Rising Cloud Minor Trial."

"As long as there is a glimmer of hope, the Nanming Fire Furnace will possess immense appeal."

"Therefore, the scale and impact of this Rising Cloud Minor Trial will absolutely far exceed an ordinary minor trial! Looking throughout the history of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, it will likely rank highly."

"Little Zhuo's idea is correct. If we could have an alchemy furnace like this, it would bring immense, long-term benefits to us, as well as to the Ning Branch Family and the Qing-Jiao Army."

"Since he wants it, I'll help him!"

After getting far enough away from the Bluestone Cave Dwelling, Sun Lingtong decisively used his Theft Path Methods, disguised himself as an old man, and began gathering intelligence.

He first arrived at Medicine Dregs Mountain.

This was an artificial mountain piled high with the medicinal dregs produced by Danxia Peak, the Alchemy Hall, and other peaks, cultivators, and internal organizations.

The odor of Medicine Dregs Mountain was incredibly pungent. Even Golden Core-level cultivators would feel dizzy and lightheaded if they stayed for too long.

Many cultivators trudged up the mountain, rummaging through the medicine dregs to pick out usable medicinal herbs from the waste. After a series of treatments like washing and sun-drying...

...these herbs would flow into the black market and other secondary markets.

This was one of the foundational ecosystems of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

Acting as Old Sun, Sun Lingtong blended into the crowd as naturally as a drop of water merging into a stream.

"You're looking for Danxia Peak's medicine dregs? Look, they're right over there. But I advise you not to go; that area has been monopolized by a group for a long time," a cultivator provided Sun Lingtong with valuable information.

Sun Lingtong thanked him, asked a few more people, felt out a network of connections, and smoothly made contact with the group monopolizing Danxia Peak's medicine dreg resources.

"You want intelligence on Danxia Peak's medicine dregs? Oh, I get it. You're here to snoop for information about the Nanming Fire Furnace!"

The cultivator rubbed his fingers together in front of Sun Lingtong. The meaning was obvious.

This information was valuable.

It's going to cost extra!

Extra money it was. Sun Lingtong didn't care about such a small amount.

A moment later, fully rewarded, he quietly descended Medicine Dregs Mountain.

"The Nanming Fire Furnace's value is extremely high, so it's not just me; others have long been frantically digging for any related information."

"Since that's the case..."

Upon realizing this, Sun Lingtong decisively modified his espionage plan.

His second stop shifted from the handyman disciples of Danxia Peak to their relatives and friends.

"You came to the right person to ask about this!"

"If you actually looked for the handyman disciples who were originally responsible for cleaning the alchemy rooms, they would definitely keep their mouths shut. The higher-ups have already issued a gag order."

"Of course, by coming to us for the inside scoop, we aren't bound by that restriction. It's just that the price..."

Sun Lingtong nodded. "Understood."

He took out a bag of Spirit Stones and handed it over.

What followed was a barrage of eager chatter from various relatives and friends.

Sun Lingtong's third stop was the Artifact Refinement Hall.

He had already found out that it was Tie Kuang, the Hall Master of the Artifact Refinement Hall, who had personally inspected the Nanming Fire Furnace and made the most accurate assessment of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's condition.

Tie Kuang's status was too high; Sun Lingtong naturally couldn't reach him.

In fact, even Ning Zhuo, who was currently in the limelight with his reputation spreading throughout the main sect, wasn't qualified to request a private audience with Tie Kuang.

Sun Lingtong approached the internal personnel of the Artifact Refinement Hall, paving his way with Spirit Stones.

It wasn't that he didn't have other methods for gathering intelligence, but time was currently tight. Only by using brute force wealth could he pull off a miracle and gather enough valuable information.

A day passed, and Sun Lingtong returned fully loaded.

"Little Zhuo, I've found out everything."

"Right now, the number of registered cultivators has already reached the tens of thousands."

"The scale of this minor trial will certainly be massive. It could very well rank in the top ten in history."

"The format Danxia Peak is hosting is like this..."

Ning Zhuo listened carefully.

The Nanming Fire Furnace was severely destroyed; it was quite tragic, with barely a tenth of the furnace body surviving. The most troublesome part was the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

On the day the Rising Cloud Minor Trial is held, a spell will be cast on the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, submerging it into a dreamscape.

The Formation Hall has already dispatched hundreds of people, working around the clock, day and night without rest, to set up a grand sensory formation.

When the time comes, the Formation Space will be divided into countless individual spaces, allowing the cultivators to perform freely within them. Anyone who can manage to elicit even the slightest reaction from the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit will advance to the next round.

Every cultivator has three chances to try. Three consecutive failures result in complete elimination.

Sun Lingtong continued, "I managed to inquire about Tie Kuang's opinion from the Artifact Refinement Hall. He once said that gaining the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's favor, or even its trust, actually has a traceable path."

"The fundamental nature of the Nanming Fire Furnace lies there. Therefore, those with alchemy skills, Fire Element cultivators, and those with low cultivation bases will all have an advantage."

Hearing this, a hint of joy appeared on Ning Zhuo's face. "Other than my weak alchemy skills, my cultivation is only at the Foundation Establishment Stage, and my Fire Element realm is robust. My advantage isn't small."

But this time, Sun Lingtong shook his head.

"Little Zhuo. The situation isn't as optimistic as we previously estimated."

"From what I've gathered, an extremely large number of cultivators have been attracted this time, and among them are many experts!"

"We won't even mention Danxia Peak; they will definitely go all out to keep the Nanming Fire Furnace in their own hands. In that aspect alone, the alchemists stepping forward will absolutely be among the top-ranked in the Flying Cloud Kingdom."

"Aside from that, Old Man Baicao has explicitly stated he will participate."

"The Nine-Fire Dragon Lord has also mobilized."

"Old Man Taoli, known as the 'Immortal in the Furnace', has also emerged from seclusion, coming specifically for the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"Baicao Peak has dispatched a large group of personnel."

"The Internal Affairs Hall and Commerce Hall have directly offered high prices to hire capable individuals."

"The Skill Transmission Hall has organized about fifty people, mostly practitioners of Fire Element cultivation arts or some profound alchemy techniques."

"As for the Artifact Refinement Hall, Tie Kuang is personally selecting his people."

"The Medical Hall hasn't been sitting idle either."

"You know about the Confucian cultivator groups. Dong Nishang will also make a move."

Ning Zhuo caught his meaning: "Dong Nishang is not skilled at alchemy at all, yet she is a pillar of the Confucian cultivator community and the current highest representative of the zither arts. Does she intend to use her zither music to move the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit?"

Sun Lingtong nodded. "Little Zhuo, you hit the nail on the head."

Ning Zhuo narrowed his eyes. "Since ancient times, food and medicine have been inseparable. Judging by this logic, Spirit Chefs will also step in?"

Sun Lingtong nodded again. "Exactly."

He spread his small hands. "Who wouldn't want to try their luck after hearing this news?"

Ning Zhuo's expression also grew somewhat heavy, feeling a formless, intense pressure.

He sighed. "Once the Nanming Fire Furnace is restored, it will instantly become a faction's foundational reserve. If my Ning Branch Family obtains it, it can instantly transform the clan from top to bottom, from the inside out, allowing the family to soar from then on, benefiting endlessly!"

"Looking at it from this perspective, it's no surprise that countless people are swarming towards it like ducks to water."

The value of the Nanming Fire Furnace was simply too massive; even if a super faction obtained it, it would increase their foundational reserves.

Everyone knew that the Nanming Fire Furnace was severely shattered, and the chances of saving the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit were extremely slim.

But even a sliver of hope was enough to tug at the hearts of countless people.

Ning Zhuo's eyes flickered. "Since that's the case, we might as well let Gongsun Yan and Qing Chi participate as well to increase our slight chances of winning."

-----

Three days later.

This Rising Cloud Minor Trial revolving around the Nanming Fire Furnace and the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit officially began.

Danxia Peak.

The sea of clouds surged like boiling water.

From the sect gates down, along the mountain path stretching for tens of miles, there was already a sea of people standing shoulder to shoulder.

Various colors of escape lights chased the moon like shooting stars, converging from all directions, dyeing half the sky in a vibrant, multicolored array, as magnificent as an unfurling brocade. Then, they all landed at the foot of the mountain, merging into the massive tide of humanity on the various mountain paths.

Stepping on a white cloud, Ning Zhuo was also approaching Danxia Peak. Sweeping a glance over, he saw a sky full of human figures like a swarm of locusts, densely packed. Flying swords, spirit boats, cloud chariots, treasure fans, gourds, feathers, lotus thrones... all kinds of Magic Artifacts and magic treasures bloomed with wondrous light, dazzlingly radiant, outshining even the sun.

Sun Lingtong was not with him. At this moment, he transmitted a message from afar via divine sense: "This scene is huge! The Myriad Manifestations Sect truly deserves its reputation as the number one super faction in the Flying Cloud Kingdom; what majestic phenomena."

"Yeah." Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, suppressing the faint throbbing in his heart.

He had always been steady, but at this moment, the hot blood in his chest couldn't help but surge. Not out of nervousness, but out of excitement - the thrill of being amidst a grand cultivation gathering, witnessing and personally participating in such a monumental event.

"Counting all the Flying Cloud Assemblies, a trial like this is rare. Regardless of the outcome, the Myriad Manifestations Sect will talk about it with great relish for decades to come."

Jiang Ping and the others were currently situated within a Formation Space.

This space wasn't large, only about three zhang in radius. The four walls were condensed from transparent spiritual light. Looking out from the inside, the scenes on the mountain paths, in the sky, and before the mountain gates could be seen entirely, as clear as observing the lines on one's palm.

This was the viewing platform Danxia Peak had prepared for its main team.

This wasn't cheating, but a home-field advantage.

Jiang Ping stood before the spiritual light wall with his hands behind his back, continuously scanning the cultivators on the mountain paths and in the sky.

Today he had changed into a brand-new Danxia-colored Daoist robe. The alchemy furnace patterns embroidered on the hem flowed with a faint luster under the reflection of the spiritual light.

Normally, he wouldn't wear such high-profile attire, but today he had to step up as the "Captain" of the main team.

Since he was Wang Yu's disciple, he naturally had to take on this duty; he was honor-bound to do so.

"Vice Hall Master Jiang, have you spotted anything noteworthy?" The speaker emphasized the word "Vice," slowly walking to Jiang Ping's side and standing shoulder-to-shoulder with him.

Without turning his head, Jiang Ping knew the speaker was Shen Hongyao.

As always, Shen Hongyao wore a red dress, looking like a ball of burning flame. The hem of her dress was embroidered with large red peony-like medicinal flowers, their petals layered and incredibly lifelike, as if they would drift down with the wind.

Her hair was tied high with a red string, exposing her smooth forehead and fair neck. Her almond eyes shot out an imposing glint, as high-profile as ever.

Jiang Ping only then cast a sidelong glance. "Sigh, there are so many cultivators, it's a dazzling blur. I can't see clearly."

Shen Hongyao locked eyes with him, but as their gazes clashed, Jiang Ping's aura remained entirely peaceful, making Shen Hongyao feel like she had punched cotton.

Unreconciled, Shen Hongyao emphasized, "The Nanming Fire Furnace is the foundational reserve of our Danxia Peak. We must join forces and are bound to keep it here; there is no room for failure. As the Captain this time, Vice Hall Master Jiang, what countermeasures do you have? Speak quickly."

Her temperament was aggressive and her actions bold, so much so that she had been repeatedly punished for exploding furnaces. However, her magnanimous attitude of openly competing with others, coupled with her extraordinary talent, gave her considerable influence within Danxia Peak.

Looking somewhat weak-willed, Jiang Ping sighed again. "Sigh, it's just a matter of countering water with earth, and stopping soldiers with generals. We just do our human best and leave the rest to Heaven's Will."

Shen Hongyao immediately frowned. She looked down on Jiang Ping's weak posture and was just about to say something.

Someone behind them spoke, "This time, Danxia Peak and the Alchemy Hall have summoned us to bear this heavy responsibility. The pressure is immense. Only by working together with one heart and casting aside past grievances can we maximize our chances of success."

Jiang Ping and Shen Hongyao turned to look, only to see Qing Baizi sitting cross-legged on a bluestone, his eyes slightly closed, his expression as calm as water.

Qing Baizi was a guest elder of Danxia Peak and possessed a Nascent Soul cultivation. Naturally, his words carried weight.

Jiang Ping and Shen Hongyao nodded immediately, expressing their agreement.

Qin Ke leaned against the side, arms crossed, not saying a word. His gaze pierced through the spiritual light wall, falling upon the sea of people at the bottom of the mountain, his eyes as calm as water, revealing no emotion.

Gu Lianjie, on the other hand, took out some pastries and, with a passionate smile, distributed them to everyone in the room.

The Spirit Food gave off a fragrant aroma, but Qin Ke shook his head, showing no interest.

Jiang Ping and Shen Hongyao each took a portion, greatly easing the atmosphere in the room.

As Qing Baizi graciously accepted, his mind was already completely clear on the dynamics of the main team.

"Shen Hongyao is one of the deacons of the Alchemy Hall. She dares to fight and risk it all, thirsting for competition. The more people she defeats, the more capital she has for promotion."

"Jiang Ping, as Wang Yu's disciple - this identity alone places him in an invincible position. In his view, there's no need to take risks, so he prioritizes stability above all. Therefore, his repeated tolerance and concessions towards the sharp-edged Shen Hongyao is simply his nature, though not lacking in brilliance. Unfortunately, this time, because of that very same identity, he has no choice but to be dragged into this."

"As for Gu Lianjie, he originated as an outer sect disciple. His foundation and background are the thinnest, which is why he spoke last, trying his best to maintain good relations."

"Meanwhile, Qin Ke is an alchemist cultivated by Danxia Peak itself, and his master is Elder Sun of Danxia Peak. He's constantly immersed in his own world, possessing a withdrawn and solitary personality."

"As for me..."

Qing Baizi let out a soft chuckle.

He was a guest elder, with a Nascent Soul cultivation base, considered half an outsider. His status was subtly transcendent. Because of this, people like Jiang Ping and Shen Hongyao would not offend him.

As for Zhongli Mei, who held the highest status within the main team, he had not shown up yet.

That man was the former Hall Master of the Alchemy Hall and currently one of the Supreme Elders of Danxia Peak. To not come here beforehand to observe the enemy's situation naturally stemmed from his seniority and confidence.

"Hm? Who is that person?" Qing Baizi suddenly focused his expression, sensing a majestic wave of alchemical qi.
