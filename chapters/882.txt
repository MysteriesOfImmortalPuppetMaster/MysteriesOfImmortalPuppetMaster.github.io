Chapter 882: Little Darling, Don't Be Afraid!

Actually, Ning Zhuo had previously considered using the Divine Art, Life Hanging by a Thread. But the very next moment, he suppressed the thought.

Exposing his trump card for the Nanming Fire Furnace was simply not worth it.

This was because the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Art was highly taboo.

Putting oneself in another's shoes made it easy to understand - if someone around you mastered this Divine Art, wouldn't you constantly worry that one day they might make a move and control you?

Ning Zhuo had also considered that if he wanted to use this Divine Art, he had to resolve at least two issues.

The first issue was being able to lock onto the target.

The casting range of Life Hanging by a Thread was fixed. However, once planted, it could detach from Ning Zhuo and stretch out over a very long distance.

The second issue was avoiding exposure.

Currently, beneath the Illusory Eaves Open Courtyard, the outside world and the Formation Space inside were connected. Ning Zhuo could also visually see the artifact spirit, the Vermilion Bird, within the pink pillar of light.

This allowed him to lock onto his target.

A Divine Art was by no means an inflexible tool.

Previously, in White Paper Immortal City, Ning Zhuo had been able to combine the Spirit Communication Mirror with the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Art to pull back Sun Lingtong, who was far away and trapped within a Formation Space.

This successful tactic had already proven that the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Art could cross the boundary walls of a Formation and be planted on a target.

The first issue was resolved.

As for the second issue, Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin provided immense help.

Just last night, with her strength greatly increased, she used Strange Path methods to help Ning Zhuo conceal it.

She told Ning Zhuo, "The Strange Path carves out a new route. The vast majority of Cultivators find it difficult to understand and cultivate. Because of this, Strange Path methods can often yield miraculous effects."

Under the concealment of the Strange Path methods, even if Life Hanging by a Thread was cast, it would not be recognized.

This was a conclusion Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin had reached through her own Divination Method.

She even calculated a "safe period" - in her own words - it could be maintained for three days. Beyond this time, it would be exposed.

Naturally, Ning Zhuo completely trusted Buddhist Doctor - Meng Yaoyin.

Without his mother's sacrifice and her constant care, Ning Zhuo would not have reached this point today, nor achieved his past glories and accomplishments.

"Mother's divination abilities are very strong. She definitely wouldn't harm me."

"Mother also mentioned something about destiny and tribulations."

"If I fight for the Nanming Fire Furnace, I can dispel a portion of the tribulation. This is a wonderful thing with multiple benefits."

"At the same time, this tribulation destiny also signifies that I naturally have an affinity with the Nanming Fire Furnace."

"Let's do it!"

Thus, Ning Zhuo brazenly cast the Divine Art: Life Hanging by a Thread.

Of course, being naturally cautious, he superficially used his pillar of Noble Qi as a cover.

Otherwise, not even putting on an act would look a bit suspicious.

The power of a Divine Art was, naturally, no small matter.

Generally speaking, a Cultivator's Innate Talent was cultivated during the Foundation Establishment Stage. By the Golden Core Stage, the Innate Talent would reach great completion. Only at the Nascent Soul Stage would corresponding Divine Arts be generated.

Of course, this applied to the vast majority of Cultivators.

For Gods, it was different.

Even if a God was very weak, they were still likely to grasp certain Divine Arts.

For example, Land Gods basically all knew the Earth Element Technique Divine Art. Mountain Gods often had mountain-moving Divine Arts, especially capable of moving the peaks where their Dao Fields were located.

The current era was a golden age of Cultivation, so rare instances of special geniuses mastering Divine Arts ahead of time did exist.

Ning Zhuo's Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Art was obtained from the Master of the Three Sects.

He had another Divine Art called Stone-Cast Earth Treasure, which he had secretly learned. This Divine Art was meant for management and operations, and Ning Zhuo had not yet truly developed its potential.

Divine Arts also had their respective limitations, and Life Hanging by a Thread was naturally no exception.

But when Ning Zhuo used it on Yuan Dasheng, Meng Yehu, and the others, it had been invincible. Now that it fell on the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, it was the same; he directly brought the latter under his complete control.

Danxia Peak and the Alchemy Hall were still thinking about things like closeness, approval, and trust.

Ning Zhuo skipped all those tedious steps and took it directly!

"From now on, you are mine." The big-headed youth was full of joy. By using his Divine Art, he had completely conquered the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

Everything happened so fast, so fiercely, and so tyrannically that right when the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit realized what was going on, it couldn't even let out a cry before being forcefully controlled by Ning Zhuo. It was as if it had been grabbed by the throat, instantly covering up any abnormal signs.

Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit: ???!!!

"What just happened to me?"

"What exactly is going on?!"

"I can't control myself anymore."

"Ah, who can save me?!"

Through the life-hanging thread, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's fear and shock were continuously transmitted.

It was too terrifying.

Too frightening!

Ning Zhuo, as the main culprit, instead felt a ripple of joy in his heart. "This artifact spirit has such vivid and abundant feelings. It seems its level of spiritual energy is quite high."

Ning Zhuo immediately used Life Hanging by a Thread to communicate with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

In this aspect, Ning Zhuo had abundant experience. After all, the examples of Yuan Dasheng and Meng Yehu were right in front of him.

Out of fear, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit was almost shivering.

Ning Zhuo transmitted a message through Life Hanging by a Thread: "Don't be afraid, don't be afraid. I am your good friend."

To conceal the truth and prevent Danxia Peak from noticing, Ning Zhuo comforted it with a gentle tone while forcefully controlling the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's body.

What kind of "good friend" forcefully takes over someone else's body the moment they meet?!

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit screamed internally, desperately wanting to call for help.

Ning Zhuo sensed this urge. "Don't shout, don't scream. Shh! Shh! Shh!"

"You're too tense, little Vermilion Bird artifact spirit."

"I'm very gentle."

Vermilion Bird Artifact Spirit: Who would believe that?! I can't even make a sound!

This makes it even scarier, okay?!

Ning Zhuo sighed inwardly, knowing there was no way to gain its trust or active cooperation in the short term.

"I'll just have to clear up this misunderstanding properly in the future."

"Even if you don't want to cooperate, you must. Little Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, this is all for your own good."

"Believe me, it's all for your own good."

Ning Zhuo transmitted the message along with an attitude of utmost sincerity, and then forcefully commanded the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to excavate more inside information from it.

"Hm?" Ning Zhuo became surprised.

This digging was quite something; it directly allowed Ning Zhuo to uncover the secret of Wang Yu pulling the strings from behind the scenes and secretly cheating.

The truth was clear at a glance!

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit felt nothing for the previous four participants. It naturally held an immense, innate wariness toward Cultivators with a Golden Core cultivation base or higher, making it fundamentally impossible for it to generate any favorable impression or trust.

Wang Yu's figure automatically surfaced in Ning Zhuo's divine sea.

Although the information he dug from the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit couldn't definitively prove that Wang Yu was the mastermind, the first person Ning Zhuo thought of was this current Peak Master of Danxia Peak.

"Whether it's him or not, Danxia Peak is manipulating all this behind the scenes."

"What deep calculations."

"Following this trend, even if the Nanming Fire Furnace cannot be saved, a large number of foreign talents and resources will still pledge allegiance to Danxia Peak. They might even profit more from it."

Ning Zhuo exclaimed "good heavens" in his heart. As it turned out, their entire group had actually become the prey.

"I've learned something new!" He put himself in their shoes and gained a deeper understanding of Danxia Peak.

Looking back at the system of the Flying Cloud Assembly, he couldn't help but feel even more admiration.

"Truly formidable."

"Every time this system is implemented, it acts like a giant net, absorbing and trapping the talents and resources of the entire Flying Cloud Kingdom, as well as the surrounding Cultivation countries."

"Just like this minor trial before my eyes. Talents like Mu Yuehua and Chun Yangzi are like moths to a flame, willingly throwing themselves into the net. They bring people, Fire Seeds, and sometimes even their entire Sects."

While Ning Zhuo was deep in thought, the atmosphere in the venue quietly shifted.

Even though Ning Zhuo had formed a pillar of Noble Qi, the pink pillar of light remained unresponsive, not showing a single ripple.

Qin Ke's brows furrowed slightly, revealing a look of impatience.

Chun Yangzi stood with his hands behind his back, secretly breathing a sigh of relief.

A hint of disappointment flashed in Zhu Fenxiang's eyes, but she simultaneously became energized. "Daoist Ning Zhuo, it seems you are going to be eliminated. What a pity. If you had agreed to cooperate with me earlier, things might not have turned out this way. Now, you'll have to watch my performance."

Nascent Soul Cultivators like Cang Yazi, Nine Fire Dragon Lord, the Red-Robed Guest, and Zhongli Mei all shifted their attention, believing that Ning Zhuo was no longer worth watching.

A buzzing sound rose from the observing crowd.

"Ning Zhuo doesn't stand a chance this time."

"Sigh, to think he would be eliminated so terribly."

"He isn't very strong in alchemy. I don't understand why he's participating in all these recent minor alchemy tests."

"If you ask me, trying to win a Spirit Treasure-level alchemy furnace like the Nanming Fire Furnace is purely delusional on his part."

"He thinks too highly of himself. Although I admit he is a genius in certain aspects, a true genius should be more pragmatic. They should know which fields they excel in and which they don't."

"Exactly. Cultivation has developed for so many years, producing countless Cultivation techniques. How could there possibly be an all-rounder?"

Just as everyone was waiting for the hosting Cultivator to announce Ning Zhuo's elimination, the pink pillar of light underwent a change.

First came a fluctuation of radiance, producing a series of ripples.

Soon after, the ripples expanded continuously, dyeing the entire Illusory Eaves Open Courtyard in a warm, gentle shade of pink.

The ripples quickly dissipated, and the pink light reflected onto the faces of the many Cultivators participating in the minor trial, clearly illuminating their expressions of astonishment and shock.

"Ning Zhuo?!"

"The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's reaction is actually this massive?"

"To me, it feels like an anxious soul has found peace and gained an overwhelming sense of security?! So, what exactly is going on?"

"Doesn't this mean Ning Zhuo has already won?" Gu Lianjie's eyes widened as she blurted out.

"It shouldn't go that far, right?" Qin Ke frowned deeply. He admitted that he had underestimated Ning Zhuo just now. "There are still so many people who haven't made a move yet. At most, Ning Zhuo has gained a massive advantage and is temporarily ranked first... that's all."

Mu Yuehua's cold, frost-like face revealed a look of attentiveness for the very first time. She stared at Ning Zhuo, examining him with profound seriousness and curiosity.

Shen Hongyao's fingers fiercely clenched inside her sleeves, her knuckles turning white. Looking at the pink pillar of light, it was as if she were watching the Alchemy Hall's prospects for promotion fading right before her eyes.

Jiang Ping barely managed to maintain a calm expression.

Nascent Soul Cultivators like Cang Yazi, Nine Fire Dragon Lord, and the Red-Robed Guest, however, all cast concerned glances his way once more.

Even Tao Liweng, who had been meditating with his eyes closed the entire time, opened his eyes for the first time. He looked at the pink pillar of light, and then at Ning Zhuo.

The crowd felt shock, curiosity, and wariness, but the person who was the most astounded was hidden behind the scenes - Wang Yu.

"The previous few people were all facades I created through covert manipulation."

"But Ning Zhuo is the one who has truly drawn out the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's approval!"

"C-could it be that he is the fated person we've been desperately searching for - the one capable of gaining the artifact spirit's trust and subsequently assisting in repairing the furnace?!"

Nobody knew what Ning Zhuo was thinking.

At this very moment, he was wondering, "How should I control the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit to display an appropriate level of reaction?"

He hadn't designed this Grand Formation, and his understanding of it was only rudimentary. The vast majority of Formation-detecting methods couldn't possibly be used!

Therefore, unsure of the precise limits within, he could only make slight attempts.

He secretly increased his force and ordered the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, "Come, little darling, show a bit more closeness toward me."

Thus, in the next moment, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit within the pink pillar of light spun around in a circle on the spot.

Hiss...

The sound of people sharply drawing in cold breaths rang out behind Ning Zhuo.

Even the Nascent Soul-level Cultivators were visibly moved, their pupils contracting. Their hearts involuntarily leapt into their throats, worried that Ning Zhuo would become the sole victor.

But in the next moment, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit returned to its original state.

It had merely spun around in a circle on the spot.

In his heart, Wang Yu was crying out, "If no one else matches up after this, it has to be Ning Zhuo!"

Ning Zhuo opportunely revealed a look of pleasant surprise. He maintained the pillar of Noble Qi for a short while longer, but never saw the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit make any new movements.

With a face full of "disappointment and loss," he dispelled the platinum pillar of Qi. His body stopped its slight levitation, and his feet landed back on the ground.

The hosting Cultivator announced, "Currently in first place is Daoist Ning Zhuo. Please return for now, Daoist, and allow the others to test."

Consequently, Ning Zhuo sighed, turned around regretfully, and walked back into the crowd.

Many people cast attentive glances at him, with the looks from Shen Hongyao and Chun Yangzi being the most complex of all.

Hidden behind the scenes, the way Wang Yu looked at Ning Zhuo had changed entirely.

It became fervent; it became resolute.

"Ning Zhuo!"

"What an excellent Ning Zhuo."

"He is a true talent. I must recruit him; I absolutely must bring him into my Danxia Peak."

"Good, good, good. To think that this minor trial would actually unearth the fated person."

Originally, Wang Yu hadn't held out much hope, but reality turned out far more optimistic than he had imagined.

"The Nanming Fire Furnace might be repaired, and as for Chun Yangzi, Mu Yuehua, and their lot, I want them too!"

In Wang Yu's plan, the third round was not the endpoint; the fourth round was.

The sixth, seventh, and eighth... Cultivators continuously took the stage one after another.

Some were eliminated, but there were also those who caused anomalies and stirred up ripples in the pink pillar of light.

Witnessing this with his own eyes, Ning Zhuo repeatedly exclaimed in his heart, "Good, good, good. It seems fair and just, but in reality, Danxia Peak is repeatedly interfering, manipulating the situation, and constantly putting up a facade!"

Ning Zhuo was constantly communicating with the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, seeing the truth as clearly as a burning fire.

Many people were secretly "guaranteed entry" into the next round.

And when Wang Yu manipulated the pink pillar of light to create the illusion of pink light dazzling the courtyard, and even the artifact spirit spinning around, a cold glint finally flashed across Ning Zhuo's eyes.

Someone had tied with him for first place.
