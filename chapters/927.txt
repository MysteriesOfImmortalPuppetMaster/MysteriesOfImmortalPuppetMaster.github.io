Chapter 927: Night Talk

The Red-Robed Guest gave a cold snort. He looked highly displeased, but ultimately did not lash out.

Ning Zhuo’s rules were public and transparent, thoroughly convincing everyone. Therefore, even though the Red-Robed Guest had a deep-seated hatred for Chunyangzi and was extremely unwilling to let the latter take the first seat, there was nothing he could do.

Ning Zhuo then said, "The second seat, Senior Nine Fires Dragon Lord."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord slightly raised his eyes, firelight faintly flickering in his crimson pupils.

The Red-Robed Guest’s expression darkened by another fraction.

To think he was ranked beneath a Demon Cultivator!

The Red-Robed Guest secretly resolved that after this, he would definitely find a way to acquire more debt claims, rise to the second position, and challenge for the first seat.

Ning Zhuo explained in detail, "Senior Nine Fires Dragon Lord possesses the Fire Dragon bloodline and excels in the Artifact Refining technique of the Nine Fires Co-furnace. Earth fire, wood fire, lightning fire, heart fire, true fire, pill fire, spirit fire, demon fire, and dragon fire - the nine fires dance together, each performing its own duty without interfering with the others. Such fire-controlling ability is of immense help in restoring the Naming Fire Furnace."

"Senior once took his own dragon scales and placed them into the furnace. Within the dragon scales lies the most primal power of the Fire Dragon bloodline in Senior's body. This has greatly enhanced the furnace's foundation."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord remained expressionless, but his gaze became complicated.

Ning Zhuo continued, "After that, Senior also provided White Essence Dragon Saliva, Sub-Prime Dragon Bone, Dragon Peony Bloodstone, Ten-Thousand-Year Dragon Crystal, and many other precious materials, all of which are rare treasures."

"The most important of all is that Fire Dragon Pearl."

As soon as these words came out, the hall went quiet.

Everyone was very clear about the matter of the Dragon Pearl. When the Nine Fires Dragon Lord brought it out back then, no one wasn't shocked - he was truly bringing out his life savings; the gamble was simply too huge.

Ning Zhuo's tone softened, "When the Dragon Pearl entered the furnace, the nine fires swam like dragons, swallowing the pearl and dancing, with dragon roars shaking the clouds. The power of the Fire Dragon contained within this pearl has already fused with the Naming Fire Furnace. Where the furnace body was repaired, a Fire Dragon insignia can still be faintly seen to this day."

The corner of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's eye twitched.

That was no ordinary Dragon Pearl!

That was an item left behind by his father.

On the day the Dragon Pearl entered the furnace, he had felt his father's aura within the flames. Majestic yet gentle, like the giant claws his father used to protect him during his childhood.

Such a price could absolutely not be measured by a few boxes of precious materials!

Ning Zhuo cupped his hands in a bow toward the Nine Fires Dragon Lord. "What Senior has paid is not just precious materials, but also the weight of your bloodline and the feelings of your fond memories. Since the Naming Fire Furnace has received this grace, I, Ning Zhuo, also bear this debt. The second seat belongs to none other than Senior!"

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord took a deep breath. "As long as you remember."

He had lost the bet. His debt to the black market was too heavy; the monthly interest was like a fire hook, tugging at his heart every single day.

Therefore, he had to join the Naming Stronghold.

Hearing Ning Zhuo say this just now, the unwillingness he felt at being forced to join faded considerably.

Being able to get close to the Naming Fire Furnace was also a desire deep within his heart. After all, the furnace had absorbed his father's Dragon Pearl!

Ning Zhuo turned to look at the Red-Robed Guest.

"The third seat, Senior Red-Robed Guest."

The Red-Robed Guest could no longer bring himself to smile. He could only pull a long face and give a slight nod.

Ning Zhuo said, "To repair the Naming Fire Furnace, Senior Red-Robed Guest brought out the Blood Soul Essence he had accumulated over a lifetime. This item was originally a supreme treasure meant to be used when Senior breaks through to the Nascent Soul stage, and is extremely precious."

"Senior Red-Robed Guest also took his trump card, the Blood Soul Bead, and threw it into the furnace. When the bead entered the furnace, ten thousand souls wailed together, and the stench of blood shot into the sky. Although its origin does not align with the elegance of the Righteous Path, after being smelted by the Naming Fire Furnace, the stench of blood transformed into fragrance, and the filth was purified into essence, becoming the resources to repair the furnace body."

"To fight for the Naming Fire Furnace, Senior almost emptied out his own precious materials. Later, he obtained a large amount of funding from the Commerce Hall, shouldering a heavy debt just to continue investing in this matter."

Ning Zhuo said, "Senior's merits are sufficient to occupy the third seat."

The Red-Robed Guest raised his head, leaning back against his chair without speaking.

Ning Zhuo then turned to Tu Yuanzi.

"The fourth seat, Senior Tu Yuanzi."

Tu Yuanzi nodded slowly.

Ning Zhuo: "Senior has no debt ties with me, but Senior proactively joined the Naming Stronghold earlier, hence ranking fourth."

Finally, Ning Zhuo looked at Wanderer Poison Annihilation Tan Zhu.

"The fifth seat shall go to Senior Poison Annihilation."

Wanderer Poison Annihilation coughed lightly twice, a hint of a smile appearing on his pale face. "I too have no major debts. Sitting in the fifth place, I accept it for now."

His true identity was Zhong Limei; if they were to really calculate the debts, he far exceeded Chunyangzi and was unequivocally the true number one.

"Sitting too far forward would attract unnecessary attention instead. This seat is just right."

Ning Zhuo acted with sweeping momentum, rapidly finalizing the five seats, leaving the hall momentarily silent.

Chunyangzi first.

Nine Fires Dragon Lord second.

Red-Robed Guest third.

Tu Yuanzi fourth.

Wanderer Poison Annihilation fifth.

These five were all at the Nascent Soul level. Occupying the front seats, they were like five great mountains, suppressing the entirety of the Naming Stronghold.

Following them were Old Man Taoli, Old Man Baicao, Mu Yuehua, Situ Xing, Lin Jinglong, Shen Xi, and others. Ning Zhuo arranged them properly one by one according to the rules, and no one raised any objections.

Although Yunyouzi did not come, a middle-aged cultivator stepped forward on his behalf to fight for his interests, so he also took one seat.

Everyone had their own seat; only Ning Zhuo did not.

The Red-Robed Guest looked at Ning Zhuo. "We all have ours. What about yours?"

Ning Zhuo smiled. "Naturally, this junior cannot stay out of it. Having luckily become the new master of the Naming Fire Furnace, I owe everyone too much, so I can only sit in the very last seat."

No one objected.

Ning Zhuo looked around at everyone, cupped his fists, and said slowly, "Seniors and fellow Daoists, please rest assured. I, Ning Zhuo, will clearly record every debt I owe, and I will repay them one by one."

"Today, the alliance has been established. I invite all fellow Daoists to return and rest for now. As for when we should charge the peak, and which location on Flowing Cloud Peak to choose? This is a matter of great importance, so we will pick another auspicious time to discuss it in secret together."

Ning Zhuo bowed with cupped hands in all four directions, taking the initiative to conclude this alliance ceremony.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was the first to leave.

The Red-Robed Guest glared at Chunyangzi, and with an indignant expression, flicked his sleeves and left.

He prepared to raise funds from others, taking in more resources to strive to make his debt higher than Chunyangzi's, thereby taking the first seat.

Chunyangzi ostensibly turned a blind eye, but in reality, he was smiling bitterly in his heart.

He had already guessed what the Red-Robed Guest was thinking: "This way, in order to counter his actions of raising his debt and secure my position in the first seat, I'm afraid I'll have to continue putting effort into repairing the furnace later on."

Thinking of this, he couldn't help but look at Ning Zhuo. "This must also be within your expectations, right?"

"What a cunning kid."

"He appears to be merely a debtor, seemingly oppressed by all the creditors, but in reality, he is using debt as a rope to tie everyone into his hands."

"The fiercer we compete, the more we have to gravitate towards him."

"If we want to sit higher, we have to continue providing funds, continue repairing the Naming Fire Furnace, and continue expanding the Naming Stronghold."

"In the end, when the Naming Fire Furnace is repaired and the Naming Stronghold has grown, Ning Zhuo's foundation will become deeply rooted and unshakable!"

Chunyangzi had taken the first seat, but he acutely realized that the true situation this time was being manipulated by Ning Zhuo. He himself felt more like a chess piece!

"All of us Nascent Souls have fallen into Ning Zhuo's calculations," Chunyangzi sighed endlessly in his heart.

-----

That night.

Kong Ran walked into the study.

The baby fat on his cheeks had yet to fade. He wore an apricot-yellow short jacket, and his pupils were as dark and bright as black lacquer. He greeted Ning Zhuo, "Brother Ning."

Ning Zhuo stood up to return the greeting and invited Kong Ran to sit down. The fragrance of tea curled upwards; Ning Zhuo had prepared it in advance.

During the alliance formation today, Kong Ran had secretly transmitted his divine sense, arranging to meet privately tonight.

As soon as Kong Ran sat down, his little mouth was as sweet as honey, "Brother Ning, today you slew a Golden Core as a Foundation Establishment cultivator, established the Naming Stronghold, and set the five seats. With everything succeeding, you truly have my utmost admiration."

With his appearance as a child, his praises felt all the more sincere.

Ning Zhuo smiled. "Brother Kong overpraises me. The success of today's events relied largely on the seniors giving me face, as well as Brother Bai gifting a poem, Brother Liu's assistance, and Brother Kong's support."

Ning Zhuo had already taken off his mechanical garments, but even in ordinary clothes, his bearing was quite extraordinary at this moment.

Although this battle ultimately allowed the Flowing Gold Guest to escape with his life, Ning Zhuo had proven himself, so the impression he gave to the world was subtly different from before.

After exchanging a few pleasantries, Kong Ran took a thin letter from his sleeve. Instead of handing it over immediately, he first said, "My father was quite approving when he heard of Brother Ning's deeds today. He said that for a young man, having fortuitous encounters is not difficult, and having courage is not the hardest thing either. The truly difficult part is that, after gaining power, one still knows how to establish rules, knows propriety, and knows how to borrow the strength of the masses rather than just blindly acting tough."

He spoke methodically, clearly reciting Kong Zhaoming's words from memory, revealing a serious and solemn expression rarely seen on his face.

Ning Zhuo did not immediately respond, but listened quietly.

Kong Ran added, "My father also said that previously, because I was young and impatient, I actually wanted to privately trade an Innate Talent method for a single tacit understanding brush, which was truly absurd. His refusal back then was to teach me a lesson, not to look down on Brother Ning."

Ning Zhuo said gently, "Prime Minister Kong is prudent, which is only natural. An Innate Talent method concerns the foundation of a Cultivator; how could it be lightly given away? If it were me, I would be just as cautious."

Kong Ran laughed heartily, his expression becoming cheerful. "But today is different from the past."

He offered the letter with both hands.

"My father says that Brother Ning now has the Naming Stronghold, with many Nascent Soul and Golden Core cultivators tied to you behind the scenes, and in the future, you will establish your foothold on Flowing Cloud Peak. Such a person is no longer an ordinary Foundation Establishment cultivator. The Kong family is willing to renegotiate a deal with Brother Ning."

Ning Zhuo's eyes lit up.

This was something he had wanted to scheme for a long time ago.

Today, he was not what he used to be. Not only was he valued by various factions, but he had also successfully established an alliance, with a large group of creditors standing behind him.

What Kong Ran just said was naturally just Kong Zhaoming's excuse and rhetoric.

Application methods revolving around Innate Talent had always been extremely precious.

But now, Ning Zhuo had proven himself absolutely worthy of this "preciousness," which also prompted Kong Zhaoming to take the initiative to show goodwill and bring up this trade again.

Ning Zhuo's face remained calm. Instead of taking the letter, he first asked, "What does Prime Minister Kong want?"

The previous trade was because Kong Ran wanted the tacit understanding brush. But now the situation was different. Ning Zhuo knew clearly that since Kong Zhaoming had brought up the trade again on his own initiative, he likely had other designs.

Kong Ran shook his head and said, "My father's requests are all in the letter. I am only passing it on and don't know the details."

Ning Zhuo then unfolded the letter and glanced through it, his expression shifting slightly.

Within the letter, shockingly, were the full contents of the Innate Talent method, Myriad Phenomena Cradle. Only at the very end were a few sentences attached by Kong Zhaoming.

He stated that this was a transaction, but also an act of goodwill. If Ning Zhuo was willing, he could lend the tacit understanding brush for Kong Ran to use. He asked if Kong Ran could become a creditor of the Naming Fire Furnace. If not, there was no rush; they could make other deals in the future.

Ning Zhuo did not hesitate and directly handed the letter over to Kong Ran.

Kong Ran initially declined, but Ning Zhuo convinced him with a single sentence.

After Kong Ran finished reading, an odd look appeared on his face. He slapped his thigh. "Oh! So you're saying I can also join the Naming Stronghold?"

Ning Zhuo, however, looked at him seriously. "Your esteemed father is the Prime Minister of a nation. Is he truly willing to let you join the Myriad Manifestations Sect?"

Kong Ran: "This is indeed my personal wish! I have many brothers and sisters at home; they won't miss just one of me."

A look of sudden realization dawned on his small face. "I get it now."

"I originally wanted to join the Confucian cultivator group in the Myriad Manifestations Sect. But with the current situation, I'm afraid the Confucian cultivators are going to have a hard time."

"So, the reason he's doing this is because he wants me to defect to you. Brother Ning."

Ning Zhuo chuckled. "If you can come, I sincerely welcome you! If you can contribute effort and funds to repair the Naming Fire Furnace, your seat could even be ranked above mine."

"But the Myriad Phenomena Cradle will not do."

"It only benefits me; it does not contribute to the repair of the Naming Fire Furnace."

"If word of this gets out, it would violate the rules of the Naming Stronghold and be untenable."

Kong Ran nodded repeatedly. "I understand, I understand. Brother Ning, am I someone who doesn't know the rules?"

He then revealed a hesitant expression. "I've also figured out what my dad meant. But..."

"I'm still more willing to stay with the Confucian cultivators."

"Brother Ning, I deeply admire that you are going to brave Flowing Cloud Peak. But look at me..."

"With my little arms and thin legs, I can't handle all that tossing about."

Ning Zhuo couldn't help but laugh aloud. "Hahaha."

Kong Ran truly possessed the temperament of a Confucian cultivator; a gentleman does not stand under a collapsing wall.

Not long after Ning Zhuo personally saw him off, he welcomed Mu Yuehua.

Mu Yuehua wore a moon-white long dress. The hem of the skirt swayed gently, like a night-blooming cereus unfurling in the dark. Her black hair was loosely pinned up with a jade hairpin, and a few strands hung by her ears, further accentuating her elegant features and her ethereal, refined temperament.

"Young Master... Ning."

Ning Zhuo invited her to sit down and got straight to the point. "It is presumptuous of me to invite you so late at night. But there is one thing I really cannot figure out."

Mu Yuehua slightly raised her eyelashes. "Please speak, Young Master."

Ning Zhuo asked, "Why do you want to join the Naming Stronghold?"

Mu Yuehua's expression remained unchanged.

Ning Zhuo continued, "Danxia Peak thinks highly of you. Peak Lord Wang Yu's vision, resources, and status are all out of the ordinary. With your pill-refining aptitude, Taiyin True Fire, and past reputation, if you joined Danxia Peak, the support you'd receive would likely far exceed that of the Naming Stronghold."

He paused.

"Although the Naming Stronghold has been established today, its foundation is newly set, and people's hearts are complicated. Creditors, guest elders, old grievances, and new alliances - there are numerous entanglements. Staying here will definitely not be as stable as Danxia Peak."

Mu Yuehua listened quietly.

She did not answer immediately, but looked out the window at the moonlight.

After a long while, she finally spoke, "It is exactly because Danxia Peak is too strong that I am worried."

Ning Zhuo's gaze slightly narrowed.

Mu Yuehua's voice was very soft, but very clear. "A powerful faction gives a person many things. Resources, titles, inheritance, protection - everything is excellent. But once you accept them, you must walk the path they wish for you."

She turned her head and looked at Ning Zhuo.

"I have already walked such a path once."

Ning Zhuo thought of Mu Yuehua's intelligence report and let out a sigh of understanding.

Yuehua Sect.

Mu Yuehua cultivated Taiyin spiritual energy and possessed outstanding Innate Talent, narrowly missing becoming the contemporary Fairy Yuehua.

Mu Yuehua said, "It's not that the Sect didn't treat me well. It's just that the better they treated me, the more they hoped I would become the person they wanted me to be."

Her tone remained cold and clear, but there was a subtle ripple of emotion.

"They cut off many branching paths for me and filtered out many possibilities, leaving only the most orthodox, the most stable, and the one most aligned with the Sect's interests. But reaching the end of that path was not the Dao Path I desired."

"The Naming Fire Furnace benefits my Dao Path. Stars and moon shining together, Yin and Yang generating each other. If I can use it to harmonize, my Dao Path might be able to change."

"And... I like the Naming Stronghold more."

She looked at Ning Zhuo and said seriously, "Young Master ranked the seats today based on debts, seemingly binding everyone to the Naming Stronghold. But debts, ultimately, are something that can be measured, repaid, and negotiated. The grace Danxia Peak offers is the grace of a Peak Lord, the grace of a master's teachings, and the grace of nurturing. That kind of favor is often impossible to quantify, and impossible to ever fully repay."

Ning Zhuo was enlightened and was just about to sigh in admiration, when at this moment, Gongsun Yan came to report, saying that Tie Kuang had dispatched an envoy who had already arrived outside the Cave Manor.
