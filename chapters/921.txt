Chapter 921: Three Nascent Souls

Artifact Refining Hall.

"Report - ! Two Nascent Soul cultivators have already joined the Naming Stronghold, and both are Demon Cultivators," a subordinate reported.

Tie Kuang frowned deeply, a sharp light glinting in his eyes. "Oh?"

"Two Demon Cultivators? So fast?"

Tie Kuang analyzed the situation in his mind.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord being the first to join was within his expectations. But where did this mysterious Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator, Tu Yuanzi, spring from?

"Whether he comes from the family backing Ning Zhuo or not, the fact that Naming Stronghold now has two Nascent Souls means I have very little leverage left over him."

"Sigh..."

Tie Kuang let out a deep sigh in his heart.

He had once eagerly hoped to use the final twenty percent defect of the Naming Fire Furnace to get his hands on this spirit-treasure-level alchemy furnace. But his bluff had been seen through by Ning Zhuo - who also possessed outstanding Artifact Refinement skills.

Tie Kuang had pinned his hopes on Ning Zhuo's Naming Stronghold being all bark and no bite, but the addition of two Nascent Souls was already a torrential downpour.

Ning Zhuo's influence had grown too large; he was no longer someone Tie Kuang could easily manipulate.

Tie Kuang was very pragmatic. "Since that's the case, I must contact Ning Zhuo immediately and offer my help to repair the furnace on my own initiative."

"This way, I can still make him owe me a moderately-sized favor."

"If I drag this out instead, with his current power and the indirect connections brought by the Nascent Soul cultivators, repairing the Naming Fire Furnace will only be a matter of time."

Tie Kuang had previously issued an order requiring the relevant artifact refining experts of the Artifact Refining Hall to stay away from the Naming Fire Furnace and not help Ning Zhuo repair it.

Now, before Ning Zhuo could react, he decided to revoke this measure.

The opponent had already gained momentum; trying to suppress him was impossible.

On the contrary, cooperation was in the best interest of both Tie Kuang and the Artifact Refining Hall.

-----

Heavenly Treasure Pavilion.

At this moment, a new guest arrived in the main hall.

His figure was thin and slightly hunched, with grizzled hair casually tied up and a few strands falling across his forehead. His hands were as rough as tree bark with thick knuckles, but his eyes were incredibly bright.

The aura of a peak Golden Core cultivator was unmistakably revealed!

It was Old Man Baicao.

He had also participated in the minor trial of the Naming Fire Furnace and had paid a price to repair it.

In other words, he was also Ning Zhuo's creditor.

Old Man Baicao walked up to Ning Zhuo. "Daoist Friend Ning Zhuo, I also wish to join the Naming Stronghold."

Ning Zhuo was surprised.

Many cultivators in the hall also felt surprised.

Because previously, Old Man Baicao had been very close with Baicao Peak, and it was almost universally believed that the two sides had reached a tacit understanding - that Old Man Baicao was already going to join Baicao Peak.

No one expected that Old Man Baicao would actually volunteer to join Naming Stronghold at this moment.

What was his goal?

Abandoning the chance to join Baicao Peak, one of the eight main peaks? And instead joining a nascent faction that was still preparing to challenge Flowing Cloud Peak?

After recovering from their surprise, Situ Xing, Dong Nichang, and the others looked thoughtful.

A sharp glint flashed in Ning Zhuo's eyes as he realized the most likely possibility - Old Man Baicao didn't voluntarily want to join, but was dispatched by the Baicao Peak backing him!

Baicao Peak had sponsored Old Man Baicao, meaning they were Ning Zhuo's true creditors.

Old Man Baicao being dispatched to join Naming Stronghold was fundamentally Baicao Peak's infiltration into the stronghold.

No matter! You do your infiltrating, and I'll do my alliance-building. Ning Zhuo was already prepared in this regard. A smile immediately appeared on his face. "Daoist Friend Old Man Baicao, it's truly wonderful that you can join. Please, take a seat first."

Ning Zhuo treated such a peak Golden Core cultivator equally and impartially, without discrimination.

He watched Old Man Baicao choose a seat and sit down, then glanced at Old Man Taoli, finding the latter looking at Old Man Baicao with a delighted expression.

Ning Zhuo: "..."

He had hoped Old Man Taoli would voluntarily join to dilute the overwhelmingly dense Demon Cultivator atmosphere. But in the end, it was the joining of a different 'Old Man' that achieved this goal for Ning Zhuo.

Old Man Taoli was currently filled with joy: Naming Stronghold is getting stronger and stronger. I, Old Man Taoli, have also chosen this place!

With the contract in hand, I'm not anxious at all. I'm definitely joining the stronghold. I'll wait and see if anyone else joins; if another Nascent Soul cultivator joins, that would be even better.

Sigh, I know it's difficult. Honestly, having two is already very good.

Old Man Taoli inevitably developed higher expectations, but having expectations that were too high made him start worrying about gains and losses.

Inside the hall, a speck of dark red suddenly began to diffuse in the air.

In an instant, it rapidly expanded, like a drop of fresh blood falling into clear water, spreading and diffusing rapidly into a scarlet mass.

A figure stepped out from the scarlet hue-

In the blink of an eye, the Red-Robed Guest was already standing in front of Ning Zhuo!

His face was devilishly handsome, and his lips were a deep red, as if smeared with a layer of fresh blood. He was still wearing a scarlet robe embroidered with dark blood patterns that twisted like snakes.

Accompanied by his appearance, a dense stench of blood assaulted the senses.

Many cultivators in the hall felt immense survival pressure, making them instinctively anxious.

"The... The Red-Robed Guest..." Cao Gui clicked his tongue.

Jiang Xiaobian held a pastry in his mouth, forgetting to swallow.

Cold sweat quickly seeped out of Niu Cheng's back.

Situ Xing and the others wore grave expressions.

Ning Zhuo maintained his smile and gave a slight bow to the Red-Robed Guest. "Senior, you've arrived!"

The Red-Robed Guest gave Ning Zhuo a deep look before sweeping his knife-like gaze over the surroundings, pausing on the three Nascent Soul cultivators at the main table.

Then, he spoke, "Naming Stronghold rightfully deserves my leadership."

His tone was light, and his voice was hoarse, calmly delivering words that blew up the entire atmosphere in the hall.

Everyone: ??!!

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's pupils shrank violently. He let out a cold snort and glared at the Red-Robed Guest.

But the Red-Robed Guest ignored him.

Just a mere Demon Cultivator.

Discrimination is everywhere. This damn Human superiority complex! Che Zhuzi and the Nine Fires Dragon Lord gnashed their teeth simultaneously.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord quickly looked at Tu Yuanzi, seeking the latter's support.

But Tu Yuanzi kept his eyes lowered and his mind focused inward; he only cared about Ning Zhuo and paid no attention to anyone else's gaze.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord narrowed his eyes, staring intently at Tu Yuanzi. His gaze was full of questioning - We Demon Cultivators are facing discrimination and being challenged. Hurry up and unite with me!

How could Tu Yuanzi possibly understand that?

He was actually quite confused internally: What's wrong with this Dragon Lord? A Human Nascent Soul appears, and he... he just stares at me. Why?

After receiving no response for a long time, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord couldn't help but feel discouraged. He was truly winking at a blind man.

While the others were still in shock, Ning Zhuo was the first to react, laughing heartily. "With Senior joining, my great endeavor is assured! Senior, please take a seat of honor and rest for a moment."

The Red-Robed Guest nodded and walked to the main table. He cast a downward glance at the Nine Fires Dragon Lord and Tu Yuanzi, gave a nod to Han Muma, and then sat down with an expression full of arrogance.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord ground his teeth audibly, glaring furiously at the Red-Robed Guest.

Then, he looked at Tu Yuanzi again.

Tu Yuanzi's heart tightened: Why is this demonic dragon looking at me again? Could it be that my disguise has been seen through?

Stop looking at me, stop looking at me.

On the surface, Tu Yuanzi was utterly deadpan, but inside, he was panicking.

Th-Three Nascent Souls! Cao Gui was incredibly excited internally.

To think so many Nascent Souls are supporting Lord Ning Zhuo. As expected of the Lord, Ah Huo worshiped Ning Zhuo even more.

The faces of Buding Sun, Zhang Dadan, and Jiang Xiaobian, who sat at the same table as him, were filled with shock and awe.

Ye Qingming and Jin Mantang both had expressions as deep and gloomy as still water.

A faction with three Nascent Souls!

This was a rare sight even across the entirety of Flowing Cloud Peak.

A newly established stronghold actually had three great Nascent Souls right from the start?

How did it turn out like this?!

Ning Zhuo was clearly just a mere Foundation Establishment cultivator, and only in the mid-stage at that. How could his rallying power be this absurd?

Or perhaps, did the Naming Fire Furnace truly have such a powerful attraction?

It was too unbelievable.

Everyone seemed to have forgotten that this spirit-treasure-level alchemy furnace still had defects and had not yet been repaired to a usable state.

Even Li Ku was filled with a bitterness that was plainly evident in his expression.

However, when he caught a glimpse of Liu Jinke at another table, his bitterness immediately alleviated significantly.

Sure enough, happiness is found through comparison.

Facing a table full of delicacies, Liu Jinke was currently in a state of utter panic.

Three Nascent Souls.

That was three Nascent Souls!

He had previously taken a job from the Demon Subjugation Hall and had already been warned. But when truly facing the reality of three Nascent Souls, he still felt pressure as heavy as a mountain.

Earlier, facing the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's questioning, his heart had already been in a panic.

When Tu Yuanzi joined in, even though he didn't glare at him, Liu Jinke still didn't dare to breathe too loudly.

Now, the Red-Robed Guest had joined too!!

This guy was no saint.

He was a genuine Demonic Cultivator. Even after joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect, he remained a semi-exiled existence whose demonic nature remained unchanged.

If Liu Jinke caused trouble for Ning Zhuo now, it would be equivalent to turning the Red-Robed Guest into an enemy.

Troublesome. Too troublesome!

"A Nascent Soul, and a Human Nascent Soul at that! Brother Ning's momentum is unstoppable now." Seeing this, Situ Xing hesitated no longer. He immediately stood up and cupped his hands toward Ning Zhuo from afar.

"Brother Ning, I request to join the Naming Stronghold," Situ Xing declared aloud.

He no longer hesitated.

Ning Zhuo didn't rush to respond; instead, he looked at Shen Xi and Lin Jinglong.

The latter two scions of prominent clans both revealed smiles, stood up simultaneously, and voiced their willingness to join in unison.

Ning Zhuo cupped his hands. "With three good friends joining, my heart is greatly comforted."

At another table, Zhu Fenxiang took in this scene and couldn't help but curl her lip.

The successive joining of several Nascent Soul cultivators had gradually stirred up the thought of joining the Naming Stronghold in her own mind as well. But seeing Situ Xing join ahead of her instantly caused most of her thoughts and impulses to dissipate.

Her mother, Zhu Guizhi, secretly sent a divine sense transmission: "Xiang'er, why do I feel like you should make your relationship with Ning Zhuo public? Don't be fooled by his mere mid-stage Foundation Establishment cultivation. He commands the winds and clouds with a flip of his hand; his methods are simply too formidable, truly formidable."

"Three Nascent Souls have joined Naming Stronghold under his influence. This situation has already surpassed the outside world's most imaginative estimates."

"Once the situation here spreads, no one will ever gossip about Ning Zhuo again. They will all give him a thumbs-up and regard him even higher!"

Zhu Fenxiang replied via divine sense transmission, sighing inwardly, "No. If we directly expose our relationship and disrupt Brother Ning's plans and arrangements, that would be making an enemy out of him, not extending goodwill."

"We have a secret alliance. This kind of relationship is perfectly fine."

Zhu Guizhi glanced at her daughter, wanting to say something but stopping herself.

She really wanted to say: Ning Zhuo is so outstanding. If you don't make your relationship public, I'm afraid you will regret it in the future.

But then she remembered that Zhu Fenxiang was a proud daughter of heaven with her own pride. Persuading her with such a tone might actually backfire.
