Chapter 970: Ning Zhuo is About to Stumble

Inside the cave, the soul search was still ongoing.

The cultivators saw that within Golden Cloud Valley, the midday sun was at its peak. The sword light of the Pure Yang Palace blanketed the valley, weaving a net of golden clouds that radiated an overwhelming surge of righteous energy!

The Red-Robed Guest was surrounded at the bottom of the valley. He had sword wounds on his shoulders, back, and waist. His blood had spilled all along the path, dyeing the river stones crimson.

Someone shouted loudly, "Surrender without a fight, and we'll leave you an intact corpse."

Leaning against the mountain wall, the Red-Robed Guest’s long hair was disheveled, and his face was crisscrossed with bloody streaks. He raised his eyes to look at the sky full of golden clouds and laughed mockingly. "An intact corpse? Does this seat need you to leave me one?"

The next moment, he burned half of his vital essence blood.

A sea of blood surged backward from the bottom of the valley and crashed fiercely into the Pure Yang golden clouds. The roar was deafening, the mountain walls collapsed, the stream evaporated, and crushed stones flew like rain. The Red-Robed Guest charged and slaughtered within the sea of blood. His blood-red robes were shredded, but his eyes shone with a terrifying, piercing light.

The cultivators once again smelled the thick scent of demon blood - coppery, searing, and violent!

Inside the cave manor, a pill furnace exploded, spewing a mist of blood that dyed the stone walls scarlet.

The Red-Robed Guest was bare-chested. Beneath his skin, twisted blood-red veins bulged, darting around frantically as if worms and snakes were trying to bore out of his flesh.

The Red-Robed Guest, cultivating the Blood Pill Scripture, had failed his pill refining and suffered a severe backlash.

Under these circumstances, severing his bloodlines and crippling his Cultivation Art could still save his wretched life. But to continue suppressing the demon blood within his body and wage a life-or-death struggle meant near-certain death, offering only a sliver of hope to fight for a higher future.

Sitting before the ruined furnace, the Red-Robed Guest pressed both hands against his chest. His teeth were shattered, his mouth was full of bloody froth, and his face was a mask of ruthlessness. "Come!"

For him, there was only moving forward; dragging out a miserable existence was never a choice.

Just like that, he suppressed the thirty-six types of demon blood inch by inch. The pain caused him to smash into the stone walls and roll around on the ground. After struggling to catch his breath, he crawled back to the ruined furnace to continue refining.

For three full days and nights, he eventually emerged victorious.

Inside the cave manor, the ground was littered with broken stones. Covered in blood, the Red-Robed Guest passed out, but the corners of his mouth were turned up in a smile full of untamable wildness.

The final segment was his memory of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

After joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect, the Red-Robed Guest was never truly accepted. Demonic cultivator, Blood Dao, outsider - these labels were like branding irons seared into his flesh. He was dispatched on perilous missions but received meager rewards. The sect's higher-ups were unreachable, and their cold glares and disciplines pressed down on him day after day.

The Green Blood Sect's mountain gates were cold and damp, the hall doors tightly shut, and a bloody mist hung low over the mountain steps.

The demonic cultivators who had once run rampant were now locked away in the obscure corners of the sect.

Thus, everyone understood why the Red-Robed Guest wanted to vie for the Naming Fire Furnace, why he joined the Naming Stronghold, and why he participated in the Five-Victory Martial Contest.

The suppression of the past built up in his chest and, on the Cloud Marsh Martial Arena, was poured out along with the sea of blood at Daoist Yunhu.

And then, facing the Dao artifact, he exhausted everything until he dissipated.

A long time later.

Daoist Jingxin put away his bronze mirror.

The black and white soulfire beneath the soul banner had been suppressed very low. The Red-Robed Guest's soul was so transparent that its outline could barely be seen, and a spiderweb of ghostly blue cracks spread from the hook mark on his chest.

If they searched any further, this wisp of the Red-Robed Guest's remnant soul would shatter on the spot.

The cultivators inside the cave manor fell into complete silence.

Their gazes toward the demonic soul no longer held any contempt. The impact of the memories brought by the soul search allowed them to empathize, bringing their understanding of the Red-Robed Guest to its peak.

Throughout the extreme agony of the entire soul search, the Red-Robed Guest had never let out a single cry of pain!

Old Man Youyun remained silent as he manipulated the soul banner, sweeping the demonic soul into the banner to recover.

The Red-Robed Guest's soul plummeted into the depths of the banner, falling completely unconscious.

The cultivators generally had very ugly expressions. Some still had wet cold sweat on their temples; some had hands that were still slightly trembling; others lowered their heads to swallow medicinal pills, needing the pill's fragrance to suppress the bloody, sweet horror of the memories.

Daoist Yunhu leaned against the couch, his chest heaving for a long time. His expression was complicated. "This demon... indeed has tough bones."

Qiu Lei's voice was deep. "To reach the Nascent Soul stage as a rogue cultivator, there was absolutely no luck involved."

Daoist Jingxin said, "His various Cultivation Arts, as well as his cultivation experience and lifelong secrets, have all been obtained by us. The harvest is not small."

Old Man Youyun added, "There is a recent memory we can utilize. The Red-Robed Guest once lent a Blood Ember Fire Seed to Ning Zhuo. We should spread this matter out first to strike a blow to that brat's reputation."

Everyone nodded in agreement.

The third battle between Ning Zhuo and Liu Jinke had left an incredibly deep impression on them.

"This boy possesses remarkable Righteous Path tactics and is riding a high wave of momentum. He deserves to take this hit!"

"I still remember, during the third battle, Ning Zhuo used Liu Jinke to disparage the various factions of our Flowing Cloud Peak. His tongue was like a poisoned sharp sword."

"He's rotten to the core; his true colors should be exposed. A Righteous Path cultivator, heh, isn't that easy to be."

Even though the Five-Victory Martial Contest was about to enter its final round, everyone still held a grudge against Ning Zhuo.

The Five-Victory Martial Contest at least allowed the many factions of Flowing Cloud Peak to mobilize their full strength; even if they lost, they wouldn't feel stifled. But the dispute with Ning Zhuo forced the cultivators to pinch their noses and invest in Liu Jinke, unable to use their strength directly, making it exceptionally frustrating.

Especially in the end, Liu Jinke actually surrendered publicly and turned around to defect to Ning Zhuo!

This was simply trampling the faces of Flowing Cloud Peak's major factions into the mud.

Especially the rumors that flooded out afterward, which viciously demeaned the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak and packaged Liu Jinke's actions as forsaking darkness for the light. It was truly disgusting.

The reputations of the major peaks of Flowing Cloud Peak had all suffered severely, and they had already launched a full-scale investigation targeting the rumors.

The Red-Robed Guest's life memories were incredibly heavy, but all the cultivators latched onto the matter of the fire seed, wanting to make things difficult for Ning Zhuo, watch him suffer a setback, and collect a bit of interest for the time being.

Opening the cave doors, the cultivators walked out together.

The dawn's morning light shone on their faces, and the fresh mountain air broadened their chests.

Old Man Youyun summoned a disciple of the White Cloud Village, instructing him to go and spread the rumors to damage Ning Zhuo's reputation.

However, the disciple's expression turned strange.

Old Man Youyun felt a stir in his heart. "Speak, what is the situation?"

The disciple cupped his hands. "Just last night, Ning Zhuo invited Shen Xi, Lin Jinglong, Situ Xing, Mu Yuehua, as well as several Confucian cultivators from the Myriad Manifestations Sect to bear witness as he publicly sealed the Red-Robed Guest's corpse and belongings."

"He also publicly confessed that the Red-Robed Guest had lent him the Blood Ember Fire Seed before he died. Ning Zhuo solemnly declared that he would find a way to rescue the Red-Robed Guest's soul. If his soul returns, the corpse, personal treasures, and that Blood Ember Fire Seed will all be returned to the Red-Robed Guest!"

Old Man Youyun and the others looked at each other in dismay.

That little bit of excitement in their hearts was blown away by the mountain's morning breeze.

Daoist Yunhu's mouth twitched, and he let out a muffled cough.

Qiu Lei's face sank. "This kid moves fast! However..."

"Since he has made such a declaration, we can take this opportunity to set our terms and facilitate a battle between him and the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect!"

As soon as these words were spoken, everyone's expressions lifted.

"What an excellent way to push the boat along with the current! Brilliant, truly brilliant!"

"Doesn't Ning Zhuo want to rescue the Red-Robed Guest's demonic soul? Then he'll have to agree to this battle."

"Hahaha, he outsmarted himself. He's practically delivered himself to our doorstep."

Old Man Youyun nodded. "To think that the Five-Victory Martial Contest would ultimately come back to this boy. This approach is correct - Ning Zhuo is the new master of the Naming Fire Furnace. Even though he sat at the lowest seat to keep company, he has fundamentally always been the most core figure in the Naming Stronghold. Take him down, and the Naming Stronghold will naturally fall apart. Let's do it!"

Heavy Formation Peak.

Dong Chen learned of this matter during a break from handling his daily affairs.

"Oh?" A sharp light flashed in his eyes.

Yue Qingshan's death still weighed on his chest like a boulder. Now that the Red-Robed Guest was also dead, the outside world said that both sides had paid with a life, making it very fair.

But the hatred in Dong Chen's heart hadn't dissipated in the slightest; instead, it sank even deeper.

This was all because he knew that Tan Zhu was merely a blade on the outside; the true murderer was the one holding the blade!

But as for who it actually was, he was still investigating.

"This boy Ning Zhuo is trying to win people's hearts. He's a young man after all, acting so recklessly that he delivered himself right to the edge of the blade."

"He will not be a match for the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect."

Dong Chen made his judgment but did not intervene.

Ning Zhuo was indeed outstanding, but with the Myriad Manifestations Sect's sheer scale, how many young geniuses emerged during every Flying Cloud Assembly?

Dong Chen chose to watch from the sidelines. In his mind, Ning Zhuo was just a chess piece that wanted to be a player, completely unaware of how deep the waters of Flowing Cloud Peak really were. He wanted to see how the person behind Tan Zhu would react.

Danxia Peak.

Wang Yu stood on the outer corridor of the Pill Pavilion.

The morning wind, carrying the heat of the fire veins, blew across the mountainside and fluttered the corners of his sleeves. In the distance, the pillars of smoke from the pill furnaces rose slowly, the red glow reflecting on his face.

A subordinate reported the matter regarding Ning Zhuo and the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect, adding a brief commentary: "The Red-Robed Guest's physical body is dead, and his Nascent Soul has dissipated, leaving only a wisp of a soul. For Ning Zhuo to act for the sake of the Righteous Path's reputation just for such a demonic soul is truly unwise."

Wang Yu looked at the fire clouds in the distance, his voice slow and deep. "Unwise?"

The Peak Master of Danxia slowly shook his head. "To dare to accept this debt, he already possesses enough courage."

He gently rubbed the railing with his palm, entirely calm and composed.

He was the mastermind behind the scenes that Dong Chen was desperately trying to uncover, and the current situation had been orchestrated single-handedly by him.

"This boy Ning Zhuo is indeed excellent, but he is absolutely no match for the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect. How should I preserve his little life and ultimately guide him to join my Danxia Peak?"

Wang Yu's feelings of cherishing talent began to swell.

Artifact Refining Hall.

Clang! Clang! Clang!

After a shower of sparks flew, Tie Kuang placed his forging hammer on the anvil, took out a gourd, and poured water over the glowing red weapon.

Hiss...

Amidst the steaming water vapor, his brows were tightly furrowed, but he was thinking about the situation of the Naming Stronghold. "Ning Zhuo handled this matter quite toughly, and at the same time, very wisely."

"He seized the time and acted that very night. Otherwise, after that bunch from Flowing Cloud Peak searched the soul and spread rumors, this kid Ning Zhuo's situation would have become passive."

"However, Flowing Cloud Peak bringing out the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect to force a battle between them and Ning Zhuo is a well-placed move."

"I'm afraid Ning Zhuo is acting too tough this time and is about to stumble."

Tie Kuang wasn't optimistic at all about Ning Zhuo's battle with the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect. Yet, as it happens, Ning Zhuo was the new master of the Naming Fire Furnace.

"Right now is a golden opportunity."

"This time, I will personally go and talk to Ning Zhuo to facilitate cooperation between both sides!"

Several Confucian cultivators held an emergency meeting regarding this matter.

Duanmu Zhang sat upright in the main seat. "I didn't expect the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak to mobilize the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect to make things difficult for Ning Zhuo."

Chu Xuangui said, "The Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect possess Divine Abilities. Through multiple operations, they have beheaded Nascent Soul cultivators. Ning Zhuo has been exploited by the enemy and is currently in an extremely passive position. How should we assist him?"

Back then, Wen Ruanyu had entrusted Ning Zhuo to this group of Confucian cultivators at the main gate of the Myriad Manifestations Sect. The Confucian cultivators naturally had to take responsibility to the end, and now they were all getting headaches over this matter.

Nangong Zhi suggested, "The current situation is complicated, and it's hard for us to intervene. Why don't we first write a letter and send it to Daoist Wen Ruanyu?"

After the incident with Qin De, the prestige of the Confucian cultivator faction had plummeted. It was very difficult for them to provide even a little help to Ning Zhuo.

But at the mention of sending a letter to Wen Ruanyu, Chu Xuangui, Duanmu Zhang, and the others all showed troubled expressions. Having failed to take good care of Ning Zhuo, they felt somewhat ashamed to face Wen Ruanyu.

Li Guanyu suddenly said, "Ning Zhuo's identity is special; it involves the White Paper Immortal City. Perhaps this layer of his identity could lend him a hand and help him break free from this current predicament?"

Everyone's expressions froze for a moment.

This new perspective from Li Guanyu made their eyes light up.

All along, Ning Zhuo had been hiding this identity. After all, his cultivation base was low, and the White Paper Immortal City was of grave importance, involving massive amounts of interests. With his weak little arms and legs, recklessly getting involved in this vortex would easily cause him to lose control of his own fate, even being ground into fine powder.

But who could have predicted that this kid Ning Zhuo was so capable of making waves?!

Previously, when the Confucian cultivators agreed to Wen Ruanyu's request, they hadn't hesitated, feeling deep down that as a junior at a mere Foundation Establishment mid-stage, what kind of trouble could Ning Zhuo possibly attract?

Even if it involved the White Paper Immortal City, Ning Zhuo's identity was solid enough to definitely secure a foothold within the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

But who would have thought that Ning Zhuo would stir things up to this extent?!

This was something none of the Confucian cultivators present could have anticipated.

Even the most gifted geniuses would be racing against time right now, striving desperately to pass more of the Cloud-Rousing Minor Exams. But what about Ning Zhuo?

He directly rolled up his sleeves, seized an opportunity, and actually gathered a gang of people to seize territory right in the main headquarters of the Myriad Manifestations Sect!

"This boy really doesn't let anyone worry less. This time, Flowing Cloud Peak actually played the trump card of the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect. This kid Ning Zhuo has played himself into a corner," Chu Xuangui sighed.

He continued, "But the secret of the White Paper Immortal City still cannot be carelessly leaked."

Duanmu Zhang nodded. "Once released, the situation will inevitably become more complicated. Moreover, has the Foreign Affairs Hall truly not found out anything? I suspect they already have results, but because a victor hasn't been decided from above, they are merely holding it back."

"The interests in the White Paper Immortal City are far greater than the dispute over the Naming Stronghold's first base."

The crowd fell into silence once again.

Foreign Affairs Hall.

"Achoo!" Xiang Ji, the Hall Master of the Foreign Affairs Hall, rubbed his nose.

He opened the latest jade slip. Seeing a portion of its contents, he was so angry that he directly stood up.

"What is going on!"

"The seven teams of cultivators dispatched consecutively have all failed midway due to various circumstances?"

"Is investigating Ning Zhuo's background truly this difficult?!"

"Fishy. Truly fishy."

"Should I report this upwards?"

At first, Xiang Ji had suspected whether a faction behind the scenes was interfering to obstruct his investigation. As a result, he sent additional personnel to investigate the various delays. Yet, they genuinely didn't discover any mastermind behind the scenes.

Everything was natural and normal.

The only thing to blame was the genuinely poor luck of these investigation teams.

Xiang Ji scratched his head. "Now in the Naming Stronghold dispute, Ning Zhuo has to fight the Three Valiants of the Death-Facing Sect? This kid is going to die! I absolutely cannot fail to investigate his background before something happens to him!"
