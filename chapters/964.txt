Chapter 964: The Poison Art That Saves Lives

In the Miasma Poison Martial Arena, the nine mountain-stone long snakes had already charged right in front of him.

The hint of reminiscence in Tan Zhu's eyes quietly dissipated.

He raised his hand for the second time.

This time, he brought two fingers together and gently tapped forward.

A second patch of purple-black poison smoke spread from his fingertips.

The color was even deeper than the first time!

As the poison smoke unfurled, there was no overwhelming aura, nor was there any dazzling brilliance. It was just a silent purple-black smokescreen, like the soundless night, blocking the path of the nine mountain-stone long snakes.

The first long snake crashed into the poison smoke, and the stone scales on its head immediately turned black. The poison-cleansing runic patterns on its belly lit up, attempting to force the poison smoke back. However, the purple-black poison smoke drilled in through the gaps in the stone scales, directly annihilating all the spiritual energy inside that supported the snake's body.

The snake's head stiffened.

The second long snake thrust forward. The moment its stone tongue entered the poison smoke, it rapidly softened. The green-black tip of the tongue rotted into sludge, dripping onto the ground and burning out black pits one after another.

The third, the fourth, the fifth...

The nine long snakes charged into the poison smoke one after another. Unexpectedly, they were like a pack of stone beasts blundering into a deep bog - the more they struggled, the deeper they sank. The poison-cleansing runic patterns on the snakes' bodies flashed continuously but were gradually eroded and extinguished by the purple-black poison smoke. The stone scales cracked, the mud bones loosened, and the snake bellies collapsed.

Bang, bang, bang!

The nine mountain-stone long snakes crashed to the bottom of the valley in succession.

They did not rise again, but instead turned into nine piles of blackened mountain mud. The residual earth-cloud nature in the mud tried to flow back, but it was suppressed by the poison smoke, letting out a faint wail before being completely annihilated.

Yue Qingshan's heart sank to the bottom of the valley.

The situation before him was far worse than he had anticipated.

Not just him, but the move that many major factions on Flowing Cloud Peak had placed their hopes in - when actually put into combat, it hadn't even managed to budge Tan Zhu from his standing position.

The earth cloud rolled slowly beneath his feet, its edges visibly dimmer.

In the air, Yue Qingshan remained silent, pondering countermeasures without launching another attack.

He already knew that forcibly attacking with the earth cloud again would be useless.

"Tan Zhu's poison smoke actually counters living clouds, earth and stone, and Earth Qi to such an extent!"

Yue Qingshan sighed. He decided to defend first, replacing offense with defense, continuously uncovering new intelligence during the battle and waiting for a flaw to appear.

He took out a round cyan jade mirror from his sleeve.

As soon as the round mirror appeared, pure light flowed across its surface, shining onto the earth cloud beneath his feet. Illuminated by the mirror's light, the dim edges of the earth cloud gradually recovered a little.

Subsequently, he took out three short white incense sticks and planted them into the earth cloud.

The short incense ignited on its own, emitting a cold, clear fragrance. The fragrance drifted into the cloud, causing the toxic intent inside the earth cloud to slowly surface, turning into wisps of black smoke that were sucked away by the round cyan jade mirror.

Yue Qingshan then took a handful of purifying sand from a black leather pouch and scattered it all around.

The purifying sand hung in midair, transforming into a ring of silvery-white sand curtain that enveloped both him and the earth cloud. Outside the sand curtain, a water-blue light shield rose. The light shield flowed like a clear spring circling his body; whenever poison smoke approached, it was diluted by the watery light.

Finally, he took out a platinum talisman.

The talisman stuck to his chest and immediately emitted rings of pure medicinal light. The medicinal light did not attack the enemy; it only protected him. A few wisps of black energy that had surfaced on Yue Qingshan's skin were forced out bit by bit by the medicinal light.

He had consecutively laid down four layers of defense.

The round cyan jade mirror illuminated the poison, the short white incense drew out the poison, the silvery-white sand curtain isolated the poison, the water-blue light shield washed the poison, and the platinum talisman protected his life!

Once the five-layered poison-cleansing methods were complete, the earth cloud beneath Yue Qingshan's feet finally stabilized. The yellow mud within the cloud churned once more, the outlines of rolling stones reappeared, and the earth-cloud nature stopped decaying.

In the seating area of the Earth Expansion Alliance, the cultivators who had been suppressing their anxiety for a long time breathed a sigh of relief.

Someone whispered, "It's still better to advance steadily and strike surely."

Another person said, "Tan Zhu has acted twice in a row, and both times he used a quasi-Divine Art. The consumption must be enormous. As long as Yue Qingshan drags this out, there will definitely still be a chance."

In the seating area of White Cloud Village, someone was extremely envious: "These few treasures are all outstanding. Especially that round cyan jade mirror, which can illuminate the paths of the poison; and the platinum talisman, which can protect the meridians. Tan Zhu's quasi-Divine Art will also be heavily discounted."

Yue Qingshan looked at the five-layered poison-cleansing barrier around him and exhaled a long breath, feeling as if he had taken a half-step back from the edge of a cliff.

He looked down at Tan Zhu and said in a deep voice, "Senior's poison method is truly world-shocking. However, since this junior dared to enter the arena, I did not come unprepared. Please continue to instruct me!"

Tan Zhu did not answer.

Yue Qingshan thought he was unwilling to speak, and his heart actually settled even more.

He continued to channel the round cyan jade mirror. The mirror light swept over the earth cloud layer by layer, confirming that the toxic intent was retreating. The short white incense burned more vigorously, the cold, clear fragrance growing thicker. The silvery-white sand curtain and the water-blue light shield merged together, forming a layer of pure protective boundary.

Tan Zhu still stood on the black rock at the bottom of the valley. His sleeves drooped, and there was no joy on his pale face. He slowly raised his head and looked at his opponent in the air. Deep within his pitch-black eyes settled a much deeper, older shadow.

"You want instruction?"

Past events resurfaced in his heart once again.

Danxia Peak was no pure land.

Within the peak, there were also factional disputes, struggles over furnace positions, medicine field conflicts, pill recipe rivalries, and so on.

The lineage of Zhong Limei's master was not considered powerful. The master treated his disciples generously, but he was not adept at managing affairs, nor did he like to compete.

That year, the Alchemy Hall reassigned several fire vein furnace positions, triggering a dispute over them.

The fire vein furnace position affected the pill-refining future of a lineage's disciples for the next ten years. If they lost the fire vein, the senior and junior brothers' pill-refining assignments would decrease, their medicinal material quotas would decrease, and their chances of promotion would also lessen.

The opposing side proposed an alchemy competition.

His master had no choice but to accept.

However, Zhong Limei's master was deeply afflicted with pill poison. He refined pills year-round, and the medicine smoke from the pill furnace, the residue of precious materials, and the backlash of the medicinal pills had accumulated bit by bit into his meridians and internal organs. He could suppress it under normal circumstances, but once he engaged in high-intensity alchemy, it would backlash against the cultivator's body and mind.

During a practice session before the competition, his master was refining a batch of Azure Yang Meridian Recovery Pills.

The furnace fire was very steady at first, the pill fragrance was mild, and the azure light was soft. Zhong Limei was assisting on the side, silently learning.

But during the third transition of the fire's heat, his master suddenly turned pale, and his fingers trembled.

The furnace fire deviated by a hair's breath.

It was exactly this tiny deviation that caused the pill liquid to abruptly change color, and grey-black pill spots surfaced from within the furnace.

His master tried to forcibly stabilize the furnace fire, but the pill poison in his body had already surged back out along with the pill Qi.

The next moment, the medicinal properties inside the furnace were contaminated, pill smoke spewed out, and his master was struck in the chest, falling unconscious on the spot.

"Master!" Zhong Limei hurriedly supported his master. Feeling his pulse, Zhong Limei's heart instantly sank into the depths of an icy pool.

His master's injuries weren't severe, benefiting from the alchemy room's defensive formation, but his pill poison was far, far too deep.

Fire poison, medicine poison, metal-and-stone pill poison, furnace ash baleful poison, and the backlash poison of spiritual materials - entangled layer upon layer, blocking the depths of his dantian and meridians.

Let alone competing, his master's very foundation of the alchemy Dao was being damaged!

The senior and junior brothers, and senior and junior sisters came to visit one after another, none of them willing to wake their master.

"Just let the old man get some good rest."

"We're only losing a few furnace positions, there's no need to trouble the old man."

"But what about the futures of the junior brothers and sisters..."

"What's there to fear? We senior brothers will all pitch in and lend a hand."

"Where there is an ebb, there is a flow. We don't need to force things. Taking a step back will also open up a vast sky and boundless sea!"

"We still have to fight for it, even if there's only a sliver of a chance. Let me do it!"

"No, let me."

"Neither of you are up to it, let me do it instead!"

The fellow disciples argued among themselves, while Zhong Limei stayed behind to care for their master.

In the dead of night, he closed the door.

The lamplight by the bed was dim and yellow.

His master remained unconscious, his breathing as light as a flickering candle in the wind.

Zhong Limei sat by the bed, looking at his master's hand, and remembered the night of the landslide.

It was this very hand that had saved his life.

He then thought of the alchemy duel tomorrow. He knew clearly in his heart that his senior brothers just didn't want to show weakness; on their side, only their master had a possibility of winning.

Zhong Limei had long made his resolve.

He sliced open his palm.

Master and disciple pressed their palms together.

He executed that poison art from the fragmented text in the forbidden repository!

Using his own body as a furnace and opening his meridians as the furnace's pathways, he drew the pill poison from his master's body into his own.

When the first wisp of grey-black pill poison entered his palm, he was in so much pain that he almost fainted.

It felt like a venomous snake biting his body, and then as if a raging fire was incinerating him. He felt as though an entire rotting pill furnace was forcibly stuffed into his meridians. Furnace ash, ruined fire, medicine grime, and the dregs of metals and stones surged within his body.

He bit the tip of his tongue and continued to absorb the pill poison from his master's body!

Grey-black pill poison, dark purple pill poison, reddish-brown pill poison, turbid gold pill poison...

Wisp by wisp, layer by layer.

Through ordinary means, it was extremely difficult to extract pill poison. The reason Zhong Limei's method worked was that it was fundamentally not an extraction of the pill poison, but a transfer!

As dawn approached, his master's complexion finally looked a bit better.

Meanwhile, the first grey-black poison mark quietly appeared on Zhong Limei's palm.

By noon, his master slowly awoke, surprised to feel that his own pill poison had been reduced by ten percent.

He quickly uncovered the truth and questioned Zhong Limei angrily, "Your master is already old, but you are still young! You cannot ruin your own future! What method is this? Tell your master immediately, and I will think of a solution."

Zhong Limei knelt before the bed and merely said, "My life was saved by you, Master. Master, my aptitude is dull; this is all I can do to help you. I beg you, I beg you, let me help you this one time!!"

His master was both heartbroken and gratified. He insisted on acquiring the method, and after reading it, his brows furrowed deeply - there was absolutely no countermeasure.

He had no choice but to first deal with the imminent alchemy duel, and then think of a way for Zhong Limei.

What left the deepest impression on Zhong Limei was that during the alchemy duel, his master's opponent had learned of the inside story. Brimming with confidence, the opponent made harsh remarks on the spot, threatening to "instruct" Zhong Limei's master.

As a result, his master won by a large margin, ruthlessly tearing down the opponent's pride.

Their faction was also able to preserve its furnace position and grew increasingly stronger from then on, eventually becoming an extremely important internal power within Danxia Peak.

Regrettably, his master, along with his many senior and junior brothers and sisters, exhausted all their efforts searching for a way to extract the pill poison for Zhong Limei, but to no avail.

The cultivation art Zhong Limei circulated was already fragmented; it was like plunging headlong into a dead end, or an arrow that had left the bow - there was simply no way to turn back.

And since then, Zhong Limei began to help his master and his fellow disciples in his own way.

Zhong Limei's quasi-Divine Art was, in essence, pill poison!

Along with his cultivation, and as the pill poison accumulated to the point of overflowing, he also developed a way to utilize this pill poison.

In the martial arena, the quasi-Divine Art that caused headaches for the cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance and White Cloud Village, making them feel as if they were facing a formidable enemy, was essentially an art for saving lives!

It was the action of an orphan saved by Danxia Peak, wanting to help his sect in his own way.

It was self-sacrifice!

In the Miasma Poison Martial Arena, Tan Zhu coughed lowly.

With this cough, he coughed up a trickle of dark blood.

The blood did not hit the ground; instead, it turned into purple-black smoke right at his lips, drilling back into his robes.

His pill poison was far, far too heavy, so much so that his blood had long become a part of the pill poison.

Seeing this scene, Yue Qingshan's heart lifted.

"This person used his quasi-Divine Art twice in a row, and sure enough, it is not without a price. His lifespan is nearing its end, and he is tied down by the five layers of poison-cleansing methods. As long as I continue to drag this out, I might just be able to hold out until the end."

In the seats of the Earth Expansion Alliance, Qiu Lei's tense expression relaxed slightly.

In the seats of White Cloud Village, the white clouds in Old Man Wandering Cloud's sleeves subtly rolled; he also seemed to feel that a turning point in the situation remained.

Tan Zhu raised his head and looked at Yue Qingshan suspended in midair: "You seek my instruction? Fine, I will satisfy you."

He raised his hand for the third time.

This time, he spread his five fingers wide, palm facing down.

The purple-black poison smoke did not surge from in front of him; rather, it rose from the valley floor of the entire Miasma Poison Martial Arena.

It rose from the grey-green puddles, from the dark brown muck, from the green-black mountain walls, and poison smoke rose from the severed roots of dead trees.

Even from the residual Qi of the earth cloud that Yue Qingshan had dispersed, poison smoke rose.

Yue Qingshan's expression changed drastically.

He suddenly realized that Tan Zhu's poison smoke had already eroded the entire martial arena!

Tan Zhu had been setting the field the entire time.

It was just that he had set it too quietly, too faintly, making it look so much like defense that everyone believed he was merely neutralizing the offense earlier.

Yue Qingshan hurriedly activated his methods!

The round cyan jade mirror emitted a brilliant light, attempting to illuminate the source of the poison smoke. The short white incense burned to its peak, its cold, clear fragrance almost condensing into a white mist. The silvery-white sand curtain swirled like snow, the water-blue light shield rippled layer by layer, and the platinum talisman stuck to his chest, its pure medicinal light protecting his five organs and meridians.

As the poison smoke struck, it hit the outside of the sand curtain and a layer was shaved off.

When it touched the water shield, it was washed a fraction paler.

As it neared the platinum talisman, it was forced back by the medicinal light.

The poison smoke attempted to bypass the cyan jade mirror light, but it was drawn out by the short incense.

A hint of joy surfaced in Yue Qingshan's eyes once again.

But in the very next instant, Tan Zhu's extended hand gently clenched.

There was no tremor of mud and stone, no collapse of mountain walls, no overturning of smoke screens.

There was only an extremely quiet death that spread out from Yue Qingshan's five-layered defense!

The round cyan jade mirror suddenly dimmed.

The poison paths illuminated in the mirror suddenly turned into countless black lines. Those black lines surfaced from within the mirror. Immediately after, the mirror's light trembled, as if someone had wiped away its luster, and a massive web of cracks spiderwebbed across the cyan jade surface on the spot.

Following closely, the short white incense turned black.

The tip of the incense was still burning, but what it gave off was no longer a pure fragrance, but wisps of grey, withered smoke.

The silvery-white sand curtain was the third to collapse.

Every grain of purifying sand seemed to have its spiritual energy drained, falling from midair in droves, turning into ordinary sand and dust. The water-blue light shield subsequently grew murky; black-green spots surfaced within its clear spring-like luster, like a pool of water thoroughly dyed by toxic ink.

Finally, it was the platinum talisman.

The talisman was stuck to Yue Qingshan's chest and was supposed to protect his five organs and meridians. But at this moment, the platinum runic patterns on the talisman suddenly lit up, illuminating not the incoming poison smoke, but the residual poison inside Yue Qingshan's body that had just been suppressed by the purification methods.

That residual poison had originally faded, but it was drawn out by the Poison Annihilation quasi-Divine Art.

As if hearing a summons, it surfaced simultaneously from the gaps in his meridians, the depths of his flesh and bones, and the flow of his spiritual energy.

Yue Qingshan was horrified!

He hurriedly opened his mouth, wanting to concede.

But he could no longer make a sound.

Purple-black poison patterns rapidly appeared in his throat. His pupils dilated, and the whites of his eyes were covered in black lines. The earth cloud beneath his feet let out a wail, trying to carry him backward, but it was invaded by the poison annihilation along its cloud roots, causing the entire earth cloud to turn from yellow to grey.

Yue Qingshan tried to raise his hand to signal his concession.

But the moment his fingers moved, they turned black inch by inch.

His flesh did not immediately rot; rather, it was as if all vitality had been drained away, becoming withered, brittle, and ashen.

In the next breath, the platinum talisman on his chest shattered into dead ashes, the round cyan jade mirror plummeted, the short incense extinguished, and the water shield dissipated.

His body fell from the earth cloud.

Before even hitting the ground, his life breath had already been cut off.

Bang.

Yue Qingshan smashed into the muck at the bottom of the valley, splashing up a ring of grey-black muddy water.

He was dead.

The jade chime did not sound immediately.

The duty cultivator judging the victory or defeat seemed to have frozen as well.

In the entire Miasma Poison Martial Arena, even the sound of the wind seemed to stagnate for a moment.

A short while later, the judging cultivator finally announced in a tight voice: "Third match, Naming Stronghold's Tan Zhu wins!"

As the word "win" fell, there were no cheers from outside the arena.

In the seats of Naming Stronghold, many cultivators turned pale.

Tan Zhu had won, but his victory was too absolute, too tyrannical, and too ruthless.

Although the Five-Win Martial Contest did not prohibit life-and-death outcomes, the first two matches had ultimately just ended in severe injuries and clear victories or defeats. Coming to this match, Yue Qingshan had died in action on the spot, and the flavor of the entire situation completely changed.

In the seats of the Earth Expansion Alliance, Qiu Lei abruptly stood up, his sallow face tinged with livid iron-grey. The eyes of several Earth Expansion Alliance cultivators nearly split open in rage, but they were pinned down tightly by their companions beside them.

In the seats of White Cloud Village, Old Man Wandering Cloud's wide sleeves paused in midair, the white clouds within freezing motionless. He looked at Tan Zhu at the bottom of the valley, unhidden dread appearing in the depths of his eyes for the first time.

Chunyangzi's expression also changed.

His sitting posture remained upright, but the golden light in his eyes had already sunken. The fierce might of Tan Zhu's battle was far more awe-inspiring and terrifying than the previous two matches. That was not the victory of an ordinary Nascent Soul cultivator, but the terror of an old monster of the Demonic Path still capable of dragging someone into the abyss right before death.

As the Grand Camp Leader, he was the person standing at the very front.

Therefore, he had to bear the fallout of this battle.

Other than him, who else could they count on?

Count on Ning Zhuo, a mere cultivator at the mid-Foundation Establishment Stage?

Ning Zhuo's heart shook.

"So poisonous!"

The poison smoke turned mud and stone.

The poison smoke broke mountain snakes.

The poison smoke destroyed purifying treasures.

The poison smoke killed a Nascent Soul.

Tan Zhu stood beside the black rock at the bottom of the valley, his deep greyish-black robes hanging silently.

From beginning to end, he hadn't shifted his footing even once.

Three moves, and he dominated the entire process!

Tan Zhu did not look at Yue Qingshan's corpse, nor did he look at the seats of the Earth Expansion Alliance. He merely raised his hand and slowly retracted the purple-black poison smoke that filled the arena.

The poison smoke returned to his sleeves.

The miasma at the valley floor consequently lightened.

When he walked out of the martial arena, his footsteps were slow, and his entire body presented a sickly posture. Who would have thought he was merely an old man with his lifespan nearing its end, with one foot already in the grave!

The valley of the Miasma Poison Martial Arena sank into a deathly silence.

The cultivators of the Earth Expansion Alliance descended to clean up the battlefield.

Outside the protective light curtain, the cultivators of the three parties were all slowly exiting the venue, no one speaking loudly.

The Five-Win Martial Contest, starting from this very moment, had truly drawn blood.

Tan Zhu's face was pale, devoid of any expression.

No one knew that beneath the withered skin of Daoist Poison Annihilation hid the entire life of Zhong Limei.

His life was saved in a landslide, and Danxia Peak became his home.

Indebted after an exploding furnace, his senior brothers protected him.

Swallowing poison to save his master, his poisonous bones forged his method.

"I once exploded a pill furnace on Danxia Peak, and my senior brother paid the compensation for me. A drop of kindness should be repaid with a gushing spring! In the remaining days of this life, I shall do my utmost to win back the Naming Fire Furnace and repay this kindness."

Today, he used this body full of pill poison to win a match for Naming Stronghold, and also to place another piece on the chessboard for Danxia Peak.

However, as this piece fell, fresh blood had already dyed the chessboard red.

Everyone understood that the next two matches would be entirely different from the ones before!
