Chapter 907: Ning Zhuo Gains Momentum

Wang Yu's first sentence was, "I have received exact news that the Red-Robed Guest will definitely join. Fellow Daoist Chunyangzi, if you do not participate in Ning Zhuo's alliance, I'm afraid the Red-Robed Guest will become the Alliance Leader."

Ning Zhuo only possessed a mid-Foundation Establishment cultivation base. Although he was the owner of the Nanming Fire Furnace, he was also a debtor; it was absolutely impossible for him to take the position of Alliance Leader.

If the five Nascent Soul creditors participated, the leader would inevitably be one of them.

Chunyangzi's expression suddenly changed.

He had a grudge against the Red-Robed Guest!

Once, he had hunted down the Red-Robed Guest, pursuing him until he had no choice but to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect and turn over a new leaf. Intimidated by the prestige of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, Chunyangzi could only let him go.

Now, Chunyangzi had brought his entire sect to join, becoming fellow sect members with the Red-Robed Guest. The distance was too close; they would have to face each other.

Previously, Chunyangzi hadn't paid much attention to the Red-Robed Guest. In his eyes, the Red-Robed Guest's political acumen was terribly lacking. He had already supposedly forsaken evil for good, yet he still thought about killing people to refine pills all day. He was simply foolish to the core.

Such a great opportunity for "Heart Cleansing and Reform" had been stubbornly squandered by the Red-Robed Guest, ultimately leading to him being marginalized to other mountain gates.

"But if the Red-Robed Guest and Ning Zhuo join forces to form an alliance..." Thinking of this, Chunyangzi couldn't help but narrow his eyes.

"Ning Zhuo..."

Before and after the minor trial for the Nanming Fire Furnace, the impression Ning Zhuo left on this Nascent Soul leader was truly too profound.

He recalled Ning Zhuo's calm demeanor in front of him - neither servile nor overbearing, unhurried and unagitated.

He recalled Ning Zhuo's youthful ambition: "If I stand atop the peak in future years, I will laugh and ask who granted me my success."

He recalled Ning Zhuo publicly acknowledging his debts and utilizing them to build an alliance - a method befitting the Righteous Path.

A Red-Robed Guest was nothing to worry about, and a Ning Zhuo didn't need to be taken too seriously right now. But if the two were combined - brawn paired with brains - the threat level would more than double!

In the past, under such circumstances, Chunyangzi would have definitely schemed to suppress them.

But right now, Chunyangzi truly found it hard to make a move against Ning Zhuo!

Like Ning Zhuo, he was a newcomer joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect. The sect rules strictly forbade infighting; any conflicts had to be resolved through the Combat Hall.

Furthermore, Ning Zhuo was simultaneously favored by several higher-ups of the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

And if Chunyangzi wanted to deal with Ning Zhuo, would creditors like the Red-Robed Guest and Cang Yazi just sit back and watch?

Thorny!

Chunyangzi found the combination of Ning Zhuo and the Red-Robed Guest quite troublesome.

And an even more troublesome problem was currently sitting right across from him - Wang Yu.

The current Peak Master of Danxia Peak had his own motives. By merely bringing up the external circumstances, he had successfully applied pressure on Chunyangzi.

Wang Yu continued, "Fellow Daoist Chunyangzi, if I were you, I would join this alliance and take the top seat."

"At that time, no matter how many schemes Ning Zhuo has, he will be right under your nose and won't be able to make any waves."

"Even the Red-Robed Guest will be your subordinate, serving your purposes."

Chunyangzi shook his head and sneered. "If I join this alliance and become its leader, how could the Red-Robed Guest possibly join? How could he willingly be beneath me?"

A faint smile surfaced on Wang Yu's face, his gaze deep and profound.

Seeing this expression, Chunyangzi froze for a moment before realizing, "The Red-Robed Guest received funding from the Commerce Hall; he owes the Commerce Hall a massive sum of money!"

"Danxia Peak... Commerce Hall..."

Chunyangzi, being a sect leader himself, immediately understood the crucial connection here.

Wang Yu chuckled. "Our Sect has eight peaks and sixteen halls. The eight major peaks are the largest factions within the sect. Even Flowing Cloud Peak can be seen as a chaotic faction."

"The sixteen halls each have their own functions. Among them, the Alchemy Hall and the Commerce Hall can both be utilized by Danxia Peak."

The Alchemy Hall was in charge of all the sect's pill-refining affairs, and Danxia Peak was the gathering place for the most numerous and strongest alchemists in the Sect.

The Commerce Hall managed the Sect's internal and external trade. The reason it could be controlled by Danxia Peak was still the same - alchemy.

Alchemy was the foremost of the four great arts of Cultivation. It was the most lucrative Cultivation skill, bar none!

This was because Medicinal Pills were consumables.

The Magic Artifacts and magic treasures created through artifact-refining could be used by a Cultivator for many years, for most of their life, or even passed down as family heirlooms.

The threshold for Formation skills was high and very difficult to learn. The Formations laid down required years of testing, emphasizing stability and longevity.

The talismans created through talisman-crafting skills were also consumables, but they were mostly used for combat. Although they were also used for cultivation and other aspects, compared to Medicinal Pills, the return on investment was noticeably lower.

Therefore, Danxia Peak was rich.

Extremely rich!

This faction could be said to be the wealthiest among the eight major peaks.

Thus, controlling the Commerce Hall was only a natural outcome.

A sharp light flashed in Chunyangzi's eyes.

This was true intelligence regarding the Myriad Manifestations Sect, revealing the reality of its power structure.

Chunyangzi said, "So, you are simultaneously arranging for me and the Red-Robed Guest to join this alliance, with the goal still being to reclaim the Nanming Fire Furnace?"

Wang Yu's face turned solemn. "Since my Peak publicly hosted the minor trial for the furnace and the results are out, we naturally acknowledge them. This Nanming Fire Furnace is Ning Zhuo's personal treasure!"

"It's just that the Nanming Fire Furnace has not been completely repaired. I'm worried Ning Zhuo will act too hastily and stumble at the final hurdle."

"Therefore, I am making some arrangements to protect the furnace."

"Of course, if Ning Zhuo comes to understand my painstaking efforts in the future and is moved to join my Peak, then my good intentions won't have been in vain."

Chunyangzi chuckled.

Wang Yu continued, "Fellow Daoist Chunyangzi, my personal suggestion is that you join this alliance alone."

"Your Chunyang Palace disciples can all be settled on another subordinate peak, establishing a Chunyang Peak."

"But since you are joining Ning Zhuo's alliance, this peak will not be a subordinate peak of my Danxia Peak; it will hold an independent status. You also cannot serve as a Guest Elder of my Peak."

Chunyangzi naturally understood.

Flowing Cloud Peak and Danxia Peak were both among the eight major peaks, with a clear dividing line between them. If a Guest Elder of Danxia Peak were to occupy Flowing Cloud Peak, how could that be explained? Was an infighting about to break out between Danxia Peak and Flowing Cloud Peak?

This was a very serious political error, one that Wang Yu would absolutely never commit.

Seeing Chunyangzi fall silent, Wang Yu knew he was tempted and continued his persuasion, "Flowing Cloud Peak is one of the eight major peaks, with extremely abundant resources. At that time, as the Alliance Leader, you will inevitably reap massive benefits. This can also make up for the price your side paid during the furnace minor trial."

To compete for the Nanming Fire Furnace, Chunyangzi had utilized many of Chunyang Palace's storehouses. It was a true hemorrhaging of wealth; his own foundation had been almost entirely hollowed out, causing Chunyang Palace to suffer heavy losses as well.

Wang Yu flicked his whisk. "According to my deductions, the probability of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord joining is also extremely high. When that happens, your side will have at least three Nascent Soul cultivators, and even more Golden Core cultivators. As for Foundation Establishment cultivators, leaving aside the rest, Ning Zhuo alone is more valuable than most Golden Core cultivators."

"Ning Zhuo played this hand wonderfully, but Chunyangzi, once you become the Alliance Leader, won't the fruits of Ning Zhuo's schemes belong to you?"

"With this foundation, perhaps you truly might be able to compete for the position of Flowing Cloud Peak's Master in the future. Who knows?"

The final sentence moved Chunyangzi once again.

The sharp light in Chunyangzi's eyes shone brilliantly. He leaned his upper body slightly toward Wang Yu, transmitting his voice via divine sense: "Peak Master Wang, if I truly go and compete for the position of Flowing Cloud Peak Master, will you... support me?"

Wang Yu chuckled softly, also replying via divine sense: "That will be a different price."

Chunyangzi took a deep breath, straightened his posture, and subconsciously narrowed his eyes, doing his best to conceal his gaze.

Almost at the exact same time.

The Vice Hall Master of the Commerce Hall took the initiative to visit the Red-Robed Guest.

He directly stated his purpose.

The Red-Robed Guest looked at the contract before him, still in disbelief. "The Commerce Hall's intention is that no matter what happens, I must join Ning Zhuo's alliance? And for this, you are proactively gifting me this many resources?"

The Vice Hall Master nodded. "Yes, Fellow Daoist Red Robe. Although the Nanming Fire Furnace minor trial has concluded and Ning Zhuo has become its new master, since Ning Zhuo is audacious enough to want to participate in the conflict over Flowing Cloud Peak, he has revealed an opening. Thus, our Commerce Hall must inevitably follow up on it."

"No way." The Red-Robed Guest directly shook his head.

The Vice Hall Master's heart skipped a beat.

The Red-Robed Guest scoffed, "Asking me, a dignified Nascent Soul, to voluntarily join an alliance pulled together by a mere Foundation Establishment junior - where is my face? You have to pay me more!"

The Vice Hall Master immediately set his mind at ease. The Red-Robed Guest's political acumen was just as abysmal as the intelligence had stated.

The Vice Hall Master was delighted inwardly, but outwardly he pretended to look troubled. "This..."

The Red-Robed Guest's eyes shone brightly as he excitedly began to haggle.

A similar scene played out with the Nine Fires Dragon Lord.

An envoy brought the demands of a black market boss.

"You want me to join Ning Zhuo's alliance? But only as a higher-up, not the Alliance Leader. This..." The Nine Fires Dragon Lord frowned deeply.

"Are you telling me that I, a dignified Nascent Soul, must be beneath this Foundation Establishment Ning Zhuo?!"

"How is that possible?"

The envoy remained expressionless. "You're overthinking it. You are but a mere Demon Cultivator; how could you possibly sit in the position of Alliance Leader? My master deduces that there will naturally be other Nascent Soul cultivators joining the alliance."

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's expression shifted again, opening his mouth to argue.

The envoy held up a palm. "My master has already waived three portions of interest for you. If you are insatiably greedy, you will find yourself in trouble. Do you understand?"

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was a Nascent Soul cultivator, yet upon hearing this entirely unceremonious warning, he immediately backed down, nodding repeatedly. "I understand. Having three portions of interest reduced is already excellent news. I thank your master for his mercy; I will definitely participate."

Myriad Manifestations Sect, North Frost Mountain Gate.

Baimang Valley.

Cang Yazi returned to his residence. He was in his cave dwelling organizing his spiritual texts when he received a letter from an old friend.

The letter explicitly mentioned the news that Ning Zhuo had deliberately released, advising Cang Yazi in advance not to let the blood rush to his head and make the foolish decision of leaving Myriad Beasts Peak to join Ning Zhuo's alliance.

"The current Peak Master, Tuoba Huang, is not easy to get along with!" Fearing that Cang Yazi wouldn't understand, the old friend explicitly stated this in the flying message.

"Understood." Cang Yazi put away the message and chuckled lightly.

He was now a debtor as well, owing debts to many old friends. These people were terrified that something might happen to him.

Cang Yazi hadn't intended to join in the first place, but upon careful thought, the smile on his face quickly faded.

He thought of Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo's attitude towards the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit perfectly suited his tastes.

He also thought of the spirit pets by his side that had yet to take human form.

"I need the Nanming Fire Furnace to awaken their spirits."

With this in mind, he decisively called out, "Huang Yue."

Not long after, Huang Yue walked in and knelt before Cang Yazi. "Master, what are your orders?"

"You have successfully taken human form, and no one is aware of your current appearance. This is exactly where you can be of use."

"Make some preparations and head to the main mountain gate to join Ning Zhuo's alliance."

"I will prepare some supplies and a letter of introduction for you."

-----

Danxia Peak.

Zhongli Mei sat cross-legged in a secret chamber.

After finishing his breathing exercises, he slowly opened his eyes.

With a shift of his divine sense, he took out a purple sandalwood box from his storage bag.

The wooden box opened, revealing nine Medicinal Pills arrayed in rows of three. Each emitted a different luster. Some were crimson as blood, some pitch-black as ink, some pale as bone, and others were semi-transparent, seemingly containing living creatures swimming inside.

Zhongli Mei looked at this set of pills with a complex expression, sighing as he muttered to himself, "To think that I would eventually pass away under such a guise. Ah well, ah well, being able to make one last contribution makes it all worthwhile."

His lifespan was nearing its end. He had exhausted every possible method, but could not delay it any further.

Living Flesh Pill.

He first took out a pill. It looked like a beef meatball, tender yellow in color, highly elastic on the surface, and gave off a strong, meaty aroma.

Zhongli Mei placed it into his mouth, bit down directly, and swallowed it.

As the medicinal efficacy took effect, his entire body bulged. His skin and flesh turned to soft mud, temporarily detaching from his bones and deforming at will.

Bone Altering Pill.

Zhongli Mei took another pill.

This pill seemed to be made of white bone, entirely emitting an eerie white light and a cold, sinister aura.

Under the effect of the medicine, Zhongli Mei's bones trembled continuously. First the vertebrae, then the ribs, and finally all the bones in his limbs - every single bone emitted a faint cracking sound.

Intense pain struck!

His bones began to disintegrate, reassemble, and reshape.

Tendon Altering Pill!

Soul Turning Pill.

Demonic Qi Pill.

...

After sequentially consuming the nine pills, Zhongli Mei was completely transformed from the inside out.

His bones, flesh, facial features, spiritual energy, divine sense, aura, and so on - all were remolded. Furthermore, they were perfectly unified and harmonious, forming a seamless whole without the slightest discord.

Zhongli Mei had disappeared. In his place stood a middle-aged Demonic Path cultivator!

Artifact Refining Hall.

Tie Kuang stared fixedly at the intelligence report in his hands.

The contents of the report were exactly the news that Ning Zhuo had instructed Chen San to spread.

"This kid is testing the waters, and he's also telling us that we only have three days to make a quick decision!"

"He wasn't fooled..."

Tie Kuang let out a long sigh.

He frowned, pondering a question - would Wang Yu match this move?

He understood Wang Yu quite well. "There is a high probability that Wang Yu will follow up. As for the black market, they will likely deploy the Nine Fires Dragon Lord."

"In other words, there will be at least one Nascent Soul cultivator joining this alliance."

"Just one is already enough."

"Because Ning Zhuo still holds the Nanming Fire Furnace in his hands!"

Thinking of the Nanming Fire Furnace, a look of regret crossed Tie Kuang's face once again.

When he had previously discussed conditions with Ning Zhuo, he had exaggerated and hyped up the difficulty of the repairs, but Ning Zhuo had not fallen for it.

"True, that kid's artifact refining attainments are quite impressive as well."

"This being the case, my sealing off the Artifact Refining Hall to him has completely lost its meaning."

Tie Kuang originally wanted to use this to make things difficult for Ning Zhuo, but now that Ning Zhuo had gained momentum, this alliance alone was enough to leverage more resources and help the latter repair the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Tie Kuang and the Artifact Refining Hall were just a part of the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and the Myriad Manifestations Sect was just a Super Sect in the Flying Cloud Kingdom.

Once the alliance was formed, Ning Zhuo could borrow the power of others to help repair the Nanming Fire Furnace.

He wouldn't need to beg Tie Kuang at all!

Tie Kuang forcefully suppressed the irritation in his heart and gritted his teeth, saying, "In that case, the best strategy is to take the initiative to concede and provide the funding for the repairs, thereby making us his biggest creditor."
