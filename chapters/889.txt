Chapter 889: Ning Zhuo, Join My Danxia Peak

Inside the main hall of the Demon Subjugation Hall, the candlelight shone brightly.

As Wang Yu stepped into the hall, he saw Ning Zhuo asking Zhong Dao for advice on how to use the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone.

Zhong Dao's voice was deep and resonant, echoing like an ancient bell in the night wind.

Ning Zhuo nodded, his expression respectful and serious. "This junior will remember. Match it with righteousness and the Dao, nourish it with uprightness, and it will endure without harm."

Seeing this, Wang Yu couldn't help but scoff inwardly, Putting on quite the show.

His sole purpose in visiting the Demon Subjugation Hall late at night was to recruit Ning Zhuo! Looking at this scene now, he knew his actions had been anticipated; the other party had been waiting for him all along.

"Hall Master Zhong, please forgive this late-night intrusion." Wang Yu wore a Danxia-colored Daoist robe and held a white jade fly whisk. Although he felt anxious inwardly, his face showed none of it. His pace was neither fast nor slow, displaying a rather unhurried demeanor.

Zhong Dao smiled. "For what reason has Peak Lord Wang visited so late?"

Wang Yu spoke directly, "Hall Master Zhong, I wish to speak privately with Little Friend Ning Zhuo for a moment. Could you accommodate us?"

Zhong Dao maintained his smile but cast a glance at Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo met Zhong Dao's gaze for a moment, then turned to Wang Yu and slightly cupped his hands. "Peak Lord Wang, please forgive me. Tomorrow is the final round of the minor trial. This junior is currently going all out, seeking guidance from Hall Master Zhong on how to use the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone, hoping for a better performance tomorrow. I am truly overwhelmed and have no time for other matters."

His tone was respectful and earnest, directly rejecting the request without any ambiguity.

The smile on Wang Yu's face remained unchanged, but in his heart, he sighed, This boy is indeed not to be underestimated.

He was well aware of Ning Zhuo's intelligence. He knew the boy harbored a righteous heart but was by no means pedantic. He had strategies and methods, knew how to leverage the influence of others, knew how to bide his time, and most importantly, knew when to advance and when to retreat.

Now, by retreating to Zhong Dao's side, he was actually aiming to take a step forward in other aspects!

Wang Yu smiled faintly and gently flicked his fly whisk, his tone gentle. "Little Friend Ning Zhuo is fully focused on preparing for the trial; it seems I am being somewhat tactless. But I am truly curious - exactly which faction cultivated such an outstanding younger generation talent like you?"

This was the first time since Ning Zhuo arrived at the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate that a higher-up of this Super Sect had publicly probed into his background!

Zhong Dao's gaze also landed on Ning Zhuo, curiosity in his eyes. This was the same question he wanted to ask.

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly, his expression calm and his gaze candid. "Although this junior has only been at the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate for a short time, I have witnessed the Sect's magnanimity, broad-mindedness, and profound foundation with my own eyes, and I have long since been won over. No matter the past, from now on, this junior only wishes to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect and become a part of it."

Hearing this answer, Zhong Dao couldn't help but burst into loud laughter.

Wang Yu also smiled, though inwardly he cursed, Little rascal.

He had tried to open up the situation from another angle, but Ning Zhuo had smoothly shifted the topic right back to the frontal battlefield he didn't want to face.

Wang Yu knew he was in a passive position, but left with no other choice, he could only spit out his intentions: "Ning Zhuo, are you willing to join my Danxia Peak?"

Ning Zhuo didn't speak immediately; instead, he shifted his gaze to look at Zhong Dao.

Just this small gesture alone made Zhong Dao extremely satisfied.

Zhong Dao looked at Wang Yu with a smile, a trace of teasing in his tone. "Peak Lord Wang. This old man vaguely remembers you previously mentioning that your Danxia Peak was only interested in Cultivators with profound attainments in alchemy. What's this, changing your tune now?"

Wang Yu flicked his fly whisk and slightly raised his chin. "First of all, Ning Zhuo's alchemy skills are already far from ordinary."

Zhong Dao pressed, "In what way are they not ordinary?"

Wang Yu's face darkened.

He knew very well that Zhong Dao was mocking him. But he could only swallow this grievance and slowly speak.

"Firstly, Little Friend Ning Zhuo's foundational skills in alchemy are solid and flawless, leaving no room for criticism. Secondly, Little Friend Ning Zhuo is proficient in the Fire Element and possesses formidable strength; every Fire Element Spell he casts holds unique and profound intricacies. Thirdly, with such a foundation and at such a young age, as long as Little Friend Ning Zhuo pursues further studies in the future, his alchemy attainments will absolutely be extraordinary, and he will undoubtedly stand out."

"Makes sense, makes sense." Zhong Dao stroked his beard and nodded before changing the subject. "But in this old man's view, the Artifact Forging Hall is far more suitable for Ning Zhuo. The artifact forging skills he has mastered completely surpass his alchemy. He was actually able to repair the Nanming Fire Furnace! This point is the best proof."

"In my opinion, he is best suited to join the Demon Subjugation Hall. Second to that is the Artifact Forging Hall. Only after that would it be Danxia Peak."

Wang Yu's face darkened once more.

He had been mocked again.

Wang Yu took a deep breath, his gaze landing on Ning Zhuo with a serious expression. "Actually, given Little Friend Ning Zhuo's spirit communication methods, joining Myriad Beast Peak would also be an excellent choice. Our entire Myriad Manifestations Sect was at a loss, yet Little Friend's spirit communication arts successfully managed to soothe the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit. I imagine that if Little Friend used this spirit communication art to communicate with pet beasts, it would definitely be unfailingly successful."

"So that's how it is." Reaching this point, he pretended to have a sudden realization. "No wonder Tuoba Huang, the Peak Lord of Myriad Beast Peak, valued Little Friend so highly back then, not hesitating to offer heavy gifts to recruit you."

Zhong Dao's smile faded slightly.

Wang Yu bringing up Tuoba Huang of Myriad Beast Peak made Zhong Dao feel a certain amount of pressure.

Under Zhong Dao's leadership, the Demon Subjugation Hall was very dominant. But it was precisely because of this dominance that they faced ostracism. This exclusion was subtle, but it truly existed.

Zhong Dao could handle Wang Yu with ease. But if Tuoba Huang was added to the mix, the pressure would be somewhat immense.

Wang Yu's recent words seemed to be praising Ning Zhuo's spirit communication arts, but in truth, they hid a sharp edge. As long as one pondered over it carefully, they could feel the underlying threat - if Danxia Peak and Myriad Beast Peak joined forces, the Demon Subjugation Hall might not be able to handle it.

The atmosphere in the main hall underwent a subtle change, becoming a bit more oppressive.

Ning Zhuo, of course, caught the hidden edge in Wang Yu's words. He immediately spoke up, "Peak Lord Wang praises me too highly. This junior is shallow in talent and limited in comprehension. Just trying to grasp the Four Major Cultivation Arts - Alchemy, Artifact Forging, Formation Setup, and Talisman Forging - is already extremely taxing. If I were to also attempt to focus on beast taming arts, that would simply be too greedy, ignorant, and arrogant."

He went on to express that aside from the Four Major Arts, he currently had no excessive interest in anything else. At the same time, he expressed his gratitude for Tuoba Huang's past high regard, but stated that he could not accept rewards without having earned them; his family's traditions dictated this, and he dared not violate them.

Hearing this, both Wang Yu and Zhong Dao had flashes of sharp light in their eyes. Similar thoughts couldn't help but surface in both their minds: This kid says he is focusing on the four arts of Alchemy, Artifact Forging, Formation Setup, and Talisman Forging. Does that mean he also has attainments in Talisman Forging?

Their gazes landed on Ning Zhuo, a look of astonishment flashing through the depths of their eyes. They were both aware of Ning Zhuo's various performances within the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

Among Ning Zhuo's Cultivation arts, his formation setup is undoubtedly number one!

Although his artifact forging was only displayed publicly during the Nanming Fire Furnace minor trial, his attainments are absolutely profound, enough to make many Golden Core cultivators blush with shame.

His alchemy, compared to the previous two, is much worse. But in reality, it far surpasses his peers, and his future prospects are boundlessly broad.

And Talisman Forging?

No wonder this kid is only at the mid Foundation Establishment stage. It turns out he spent all his energy and time on these Cultivation arts?

Zhong Dao stroked his beard, thinking to himself: For Ning Zhuo to have such attainments proves he is willing to study deeply and can calm his mind. He completely lacks the impetuousness of young geniuses. He deeply possesses the disposition of the Righteous Path, has great self-control, and is extremely steady. With a little cultivation, he will be a future pillar of my Demon Subjugation Hall.

Wang Yu was someone who cherished talent, and his gaze toward Ning Zhuo also softened a bit. Back in the Cloud Prison, even when facing a Demonic Cultivator like Qin De, he could feel a sense of pity for wasting talent, let alone a rising star of the Righteous Path like Ning Zhuo.

The only concern is his background. Sigh, how are the people in the External Affairs Hall doing their investigations? There's still no result even now. But even if Ning Zhuo comes from the Demonic Path, so what? My Myriad Manifestations Sect is like the ocean embracing all rivers; greatness comes from inclusivity! As long as his heart leans toward the Righteous Path, his origins don't matter.

Wang Yu took a deep breath and no longer hid anything, speaking bluntly: "Little Friend Ning Zhuo, I came tonight specifically for you. As long as you join Danxia Peak, you may make any request you want. Danxia Peak's wealth is known throughout the entire Sect. The Demon Subjugation Hall has a foundation like the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone, and Danxia Peak has similar things as well."

"Three portions! As long as you join Danxia Peak, I can directly give you three portions of foundational treasures of the same grade as the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone."

To directly give away three portions of foundational treasures like the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone - if this news spread, it would definitely shock countless people. The dignified Peak Lord of Danxia Peak truly favored Ning Zhuo a bit too much.

Wang Yu continued, "Among these three foundational treasures, there will absolutely be crucial items that can help you secure the Nanming Fire Furnace in the upcoming rounds of the minor trial. In that case, you would possess four foundational treasures."

The hall instantly fell silent.

Ning Zhuo's pupils constricted slightly; his mind seemed to be struck by the offer.

At this moment, Zhong Dao spoke up: "Three Sect foundational treasures! Peak Lord Wang's generosity is astonishing. A fine bird chooses the best tree to nest in. Little Friend Ning Zhuo, this old man can bear witness here. If Wang Yu goes back on his word in the future, this old man will definitely help you get them back."

He didn't stop it; instead, he persuaded Ning Zhuo: "In all fairness, this condition is indeed extremely generous. No wonder Peak Lord Wang had the confidence to come here and make this offer. Countless True Inheritance Disciples desire foundation-level treasures but have no way to obtain them. It is only because the current situation is special that you have such an opportunity."

"Ning Zhuo, if you join Danxia Peak, we will still be our own people of the same Sect. You shouldn't have any misgivings. We can all contribute together to the future development of the Righteous Path."

But Ning Zhuo seemed to snap out of it and directly shook his head.

He stood up, bowed to Wang Yu, and then straightened up, saying, "Thank you, Lord Wang Yu, for your good intentions, and thank you, Lord Zhong Dao, for your support, but this junior cannot accept!"

"Firstly, it is my family's tradition. No reward without merit."

One would have to get their head slammed in a door to accept this.

Don't be fooled by Zhong Dao's pleasant words; they were all just polite formalities!

Ning Zhuo's mind was perfectly clear.

If he actually agreed, he would offend Zhong Dao. Ning Zhuo had come late at night seeking shelter, and as soon as an outsider offered a heavy price, he was supposed to jump ship? He would be nothing more than a despicable person.

He would also offend Tuoba Huang. If Tuoba Huang saw Ning Zhuo switch to Wang Yu, he would definitely have complaints: You previously rejected my heavy gifts, citing your family's traditions as the reason. Now Wang Yu also offers heavy gifts - just heavier than mine - and you agree. Where are your family traditions? Were you just playing me before?

Important figures wouldn't lash out immediately, but they would secretly remember it. There would be plenty of opportunities to make life difficult for him in the future.

There was absolutely no way Ning Zhuo would make such a stupid move.

The big-headed youth looked at Wang Yu, his expression earnest and serious. "Lord Wang Yu's concern for the Nanming Fire Furnace is something this junior completely understands! This junior also believes that possessing the Spirit Communication Mirror indeed grants me a certain advantage, and I will go all out tomorrow. But in this kind of competition, one must win fairly and squarely! If I were to join Danxia Peak early and rely on three foundational treasures to secure victory? That is not my style, and it would give others a handle against me."

Pausing slightly, Ning Zhuo continued, "If someone else obtains foundational treasures and wins, this junior will accept it. If I lose, I lose. This junior would rather bear that bitter fruit for the sake of staying true to my own nature."

Before Wang Yu could respond, Zhong Dao clapped his hands and praised, "Well said."

Ning Zhuo's words, especially the last sentence, suited his taste perfectly.

Wang Yu smiled bitterly and was about to speak, but Zhong Dao waved his hand, cutting him off.

"Ah, Wang Yu, we already know why you came, and you've asked everything you needed to ask. You may leave now; don't interrupt my conversation with Little Friend Ning Zhuo. Little Friend Ning Zhuo still needs to go all out to prepare for tomorrow's unfair competition!"

Saying that, he swept his large sleeve, and the Formation within the hall suddenly lit up.

The Formation burst with a powerful force, directly teleporting Wang Yu out and making him vanish from his spot.

Ning Zhuo looked speechlessly at Zhong Dao, knowing in his heart that Zhong Dao was deliberately damaging Wang Yu's face in this way to sow discord between the youth and Peak Lord Wang.

Zhong Dao smiled at Ning Zhuo.

Wang Yu was teleported to an uninhabited peak, his sleeves billowing with anger. "What a Zhong Dao."

Shaking the fly whisk in his hand, he added in his heart, And what a Ning Zhuo!

Ning Zhuo had expertly leveraged the situation, leaving Wang Yu with no way to press him. The sense of defeat he reaped tonight was even greater than when he negotiated with Chun Yangzi.

In truth, after the most recent round of the minor trial ended, Wang Yu had noticed that the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's state had clearly regressed, showing a strong tendency to return to a frightened state.

He had secretly dispatched and invited many experts, using similar spirit communication methods to continue soothing the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit, but all of them had failed.

From this, he deeply understood that Ning Zhuo's spirit communication methods were truly indispensable at this stage.

"Forget it, forget it." Wang Yu sighed at the sky and returned in low spirits. "It's not guaranteed that Ning Zhuo will win tomorrow anyway!"

-----

The next day.

The new round of the minor trial officially began.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was scheduled in an early position and was the first to take the stage.

He walked up to the Nanming Fire Furnace and came to a halt.

Today, I must win! And I definitely will win! he roared inwardly.

Under the watchful eyes of the crowd, he took out precious materials, one piece after another.

Crimson Flame Mystic Light Iron! Not just one piece, but an entire chest. The moment the lid was opened, a crimson-black light shot into the sky. The flame light on the mystic iron danced in the morning light like a living creature.

Nine Hells Cold Clear Spring! Not a single bottle, but a whole jar. As the spring water poured out, water and fire aided each other, and Yin and Yang mutually generated each other. Only when a precious material was of extremely high quality, capable of Yin Extremity Generating Yang and possessing a touch of extreme Yang, could such a scene be achieved.

Phoenix Parasol Wood. Not one section, but a full three segments, each ten feet long. The wood contained the lingering vitality and spirituality left behind by a phoenix's nirvana rebirth. As the wood burned in the flames, it transformed into a crimson-gold light that seeped into the furnace walls, forming the foundation of the pill furnace. Over time, it would nourish the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

"I didn't expect the Nine Fires Dragon Lord to still be able to bring out such materials?"

"How could he do it with the power of just one dragon? He must have received funding!"

"Who would be so bold as to fund a Demon Cultivator under such circumstances?"

"It's that person from the sect's black market."

"Oh? He actually took out a loan? Then this demon dragon is truly ignorant and insanely audacious!"
