Chapter 962: Chunyangzi vs. True Monarch Shi Heng

Ning Zhuo did not speak; he merely focused on watching the battle.

Chunyangzi's Yang-attribute spiritual energy was extremely pure, completely different from the Nine Fires of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord.

If the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's fire techniques were variable, complex, and blazing like a myriad-fire pill furnace, then Chunyangzi's spiritual energy was unified, majestic, clear, and pure, like an impartial midday sun.

True Monarch Shi Heng was unmoved by his initial disadvantage. Gripping his large axe with both hands, he chopped heavily into the ground.

Rumble!

The ground of the Abundant Earth Martial Arena trembled violently. Dozens of stone pillars thrust up from beneath the earth, rising like a forest to form a formation that surrounded Chunyangzi in the center.

Spirit runes flashed on the surface of the stone pillars, and each one spewed out earthen-yellow halos. The halos overlapped, acting like heavy chains that bound toward Chunyangzi's shoulders, wrists, and waist.

The golden light surrounding Chunyangzi sank slightly, and the ground beneath his feet faintly caved in.

True Monarch Shi Heng shouted sternly, "Suppress!"

A yellow flag waved violently in midair, dust flying from its surface.

The earthen-yellow halos abruptly tightened. A creaking friction sound echoed in the air, like invisible rocks grinding against metal.

Chunyangzi had no choice but to stay in place. With his sleeves hanging down, his golden light was compressed to within three feet of his body. His pure Yang aura remained bright, but it was no longer as unconstrained and outwardly expansive as before.

In the Earth Expansion Alliance's seating area, someone clenched their fist and whispered, "It worked!"

In the White Cloud Village's seating area, Old Man Youyun narrowed his eyes.

Ning Zhuo furrowed his brow slightly.

True Monarch Shi Heng's method wasn't exactly exquisite, but it was incredibly suited for this battlefield. The Earth Qi of the Abundant Earth Martial Arena was already heavy. With the stone pillars forming a forest, the halos suppressing, and the yellow flag drawing upon the earth's power, if Chunyangzi's Yang Qi could not ascend and project outward, he would be like a sun trapped within the belly of a mountain. The situation would become disadvantageous for him.

Chunyangzi suddenly chuckled. "A good method. Plain and unadorned, quite fitting for you."

True Monarch Shi Heng didn't reply. He merely gritted his teeth and clamped his hands fiercely onto his large axe.

The earthen-yellow halos descended one after another, bearing down so heavily that the ground beneath Chunyangzi's feet cracked open. Splintered stones bounced up, only to be burned into fine dust by the golden light.

Chunyangzi raised his right hand.

His fingers were long and slender, and a single point of golden light condensed at his fingertip.

That point of light was very small, yet blindingly bright, making it hard for anyone to look at directly.

Chunyangzi said softly, "First Yang Genesis."

As his voice fell, that point of golden light resembled the first wisp of Yang Qi rising in the dead of the winter solstice night.

It slowly floated into the sky.

It wasn't fierce in the slightest, yet it was unstoppable!

When the earthen-yellow halos met this point of golden light, they actually began to melt away layer by layer.

The golden light was like a new sprout emerging from winter soil; the muddy husk remained, but the intense vitality had already broken through the surface.

The first layer of halos dispersed.

The second and third layers dispersed.

True Monarch Shi Heng's expression drastically changed as he pushed the yellow flag to its limits.

More earth qi surged out, thickening the halos once again, and the stone pillars emitted muffled hums, as if the entire Abundant Earth Martial Arena was helping him suppress Chunyangzi.

However, that point of golden light had already been born and would not be easily extinguished.

Chunyangzi took a step forward.

Crack!

The earth and stones beneath his feet split open, and endless golden light sprayed out from the fissures, as if a golden vein of light was hidden beneath the ground.

A second step.

Fine cracks began to appear on the surface of the stone pillars, and golden rays shot outward from within the cracks!

A third step.

A faint sun wheel manifested behind Chunyangzi. The sun wheel wasn't large, but it was perfectly round and upright. Its golden radiance illuminated his hair crown, shoulders, and sleeves, giving his entire person an almost sacred and majestic aura.

True Monarch Shi Heng's eyes reddened as he felt a boundless oppressive force.

"I can't suppress him!" He was decisive as well; he violently yanked up his large axe, shattered the ground, and charged toward Chunyangzi.

His speed wasn't fast, but every step made the martial arena tremble!

The large axe dragged across the ground, plowing open a deep trench. Earthen light surged within the trench like a yellow earth dragon tailing behind him.

Chunyangzi neither dodged nor retreated, remaining perfectly still.

With a flip of his right palm, he produced a golden short sword.

The short sword lacked a scabbard, and its blade was translucent, as if forged from sunlight. The blade wasn't wide, but it was extremely straight and proper. The moment it appeared, the heavy Earth Qi within the Abundant Earth Martial Arena was forced back by half a fathom.

Earth Element Technique  -  Earthshrinking!

True Monarch Shi Heng had already closed in, sweeping his large axe horizontally.

Whoosh!

The axe's wind was heavy and thick, rolling up a large swath of earth and stones. Countless rocks were swept up by the axe wind, whistling like rain. Some rocks smashed against the formation light barrier, causing it to flicker violently.

Chunyangzi swung his short sword down vertically.

The golden light collided with the large axe.

Clang!

The sound was incredibly loud, like a bronze bell crashing into a mountain.

Golden sparks and yellow earth light splattered in all directions, splashing several fathoms high, resembling a phenomenally grand display of iron flowers.

Shockwaves swept in all directions.

The nearby stone pillars were caught in the aftermath, every one of them snapping into several pieces on the spot.

The broken pillars collapsed, smashing into the ground and kicking up billowing waves of dust.

True Monarch Shi Heng's arms shook violently, the webs of his fingers splitting open as blood flowed down the axe handle.

He took a slight step back, marveling at Chunyangzi's physical strength, and then, using the rebounding force of his large axe, spun around to chop again.

A second axe strike.

A third.

A fourth!

Each strike was heavier than the last.

His tenacity was astonishing, like a mountain rock continuously baked by the scorching sun, yet refusing to fully crack apart.

Chunyangzi swung his short sword repeatedly, crisscrossing golden light.

Every falling sword beam was bright and majestic, completely devoid of any insidious or tricky aura.

The sword rays struck from the front like the sun hanging high in the sky, illuminating everything so that no shadows had anywhere to hide.

True Monarch Shi Heng exchanged over a dozen blows with Chunyangzi. His earthen-yellow short robe was sliced open in multiple places by the sword light, and charred sword marks appeared on his shoulders, chest, and left arm.

Unable to hold on any longer, he voluntarily retreated, pulling away to create distance.

Chunyangzi stood in place, surprisingly not giving chase.

This gave Shi Heng the time to reverse his large axe and plunge it into the ground.

"Rise!" he roared through gritted teeth.

The ground churned with a deafening boom.

A hill-like mound of earth thrust upward, blocking the path between him and Chunyangzi. Immediately after, more earth mounds, stone walls, and massive hands of mud and sand continuously emerged. They layered over each other, surrounding Chunyangzi, as if the entire martial arena had flipped over to bury him alive!

Chunyangzi's expression remained unchanged.

He glanced around, slid the short sword back into his sleeve, and brought his hands together in front of his chest.

Golden light gathered in his palms.

"Three Yangs Bring Prosperity!"

Three golden suns rose behind him.

The first sun illuminated his body, dispelling all the earth qi around Chunyangzi.

The second sun illuminated the ground; the yellow earth beneath his feet dried and cracked, forcing the hidden Earth Element spiritual energy to reveal its true form, completely calculated and analyzed.

The third sun illuminated his front; the encroaching earth mounds, stone walls, and giant mud hands simultaneously emitted ear-piercing sounds.

Crack, crack, crack!

Cracks spread across the surface of the earth mounds, light pierced through from within the stone walls, and the giant mud hands directly crumbled into dry powder.

Under the radiance of the three golden suns, dust billowed in the Abundant Earth Martial Arena. Countless rocks and boulders collapsed and crumbled, unable to approach within three fathoms of Chunyangzi.

At this moment, Chunyangzi's formidable power was fully displayed!

True Monarch Shi Heng gritted his teeth, deeply feeling the disparity between him and his opponent.

"He is actually several times stronger than the intelligence stated! Relying on myself alone, I have no hope of winning." He reached his left hand into his robes and took out a talisman.

The talisman was as thin as a cicada's wings, but it wasn't made of paper; rather, it looked as if it had been ground from some kind of subterranean yellow jade. There were no Cinnabar runes on its surface, only fine, dense black lines. The black lines crisscrossed like mountain ranges, earth veins, and underground rivers. The edges of the talisman were embedded with three grains of black-gold sand the size of rice grains. Each grain was abnormally heavy, as if it could bend the very air.

True Monarch Shi Heng slapped the talisman onto his chest.

The moment the talisman was attached, his entire body abruptly sank, submerging all the way underground.

Qiu Lei couldn't help but sigh. "He ended up using this talisman after all!"

This was the foundation of the Earth Expansion Alliance, and it was a one-of-a-kind talisman.

The talisman's function was very simple, yet extremely terrifying - it allowed the user to temporarily borrow the martial arena's earth veins.

Not only borrowing power, but also borrowing life. As long as the earth veins were not severed, a portion of the injuries the user sustained would be transferred to the thick earth beneath their feet.

Of course, the price was also heavy. Afterward, earth attributes would accumulate in the meridians, and the Divine Soul would be oppressed by the Earth Qi. At best, it would require several years of recuperation; at worst, their cultivation progress would stagnate.

Countless earth mounds surged upward, stone walls closed in, and massive boulders rolled forward, attacking Chunyangzi from all directions.

Chunyangzi stood in place, unmoving, his body radiating endless golden light to resist the terrifying offensive whipped up by Shi Heng.

Rumble, rumble...

Hills collapsed, the earth trembled, and smoke and dust shot into the sky, yet they could never conceal the golden radiance!

This kind of clash lasted for the time it takes to drink half a cup of tea before it slowly came to a halt.

As the dust settled, the Abundant Earth Martial Arena was damaged beyond recognition, having almost become a valley.

The spot where Chunyangzi stood was the deepest, lowest point of the valley.

A fine tear had appeared on his cuff. A thin line of blood also seeped from the webbing of his right hand.

Behind him, the three golden suns shone brightly. Within the highest sun wheel, a Nascent Soul could vaguely be seen sitting cross-legged. That Nascent Soul appeared solemn and dignified, its facial features resembling Chunyangzi's. Its entire body was brightly lit with golden light, its hands forming hand seals, and its aura was so pure that it was nearly immaculate.

What about True Monarch Shi Heng?

Just as everyone felt confused, a golden ray suddenly shot out from the ground, bringing a cultivator with it.

With the golden ray pierced into his chest, True Monarch Shi Heng had already fallen unconscious.

The crowd saw it more clearly: Shi Heng was covered in blood, the talisman on his chest was charred black, his large axe was missing a chunk, and the yellow flag on his back had several holes burned into it, large and small, looking utterly devastated.

In the Earth Expansion Alliance's seating area, Qiu Lei's expression was extremely grave.

In the White Cloud Village's seating area, Old Man Youyun sighed, "Shi Heng did his best."

However, no one in the Nanming Stronghold's seating area mocked him.

Putting themselves in his shoes, it would be very difficult for them to handle Shi Heng in a one-on-one fight. It wasn't that Shi Heng wasn't strong; it was just that Chunyangzi's combat power completely overwhelmed his!

The jade chime rang again.

The adjudicator's voice echoed throughout the Abundant Earth Martial Arena: "Second match, Nanming Stronghold's Chunyangzi wins."

In the Nanming Stronghold's seating area, the long-suppressed atmosphere finally relaxed a bit.

Some let out heavy breaths, while others showed joy. The shadow brought by the defeat in the first match was torn apart by Chunyangzi's victory at this moment.

Chunyangzi put away the golden short sword.

The martial arena didn't forbid life and death, but he only took one look at the unconscious Shi Heng and didn't deal a killing blow.

He turned and flew back to the Nanming Stronghold's seating area.

First match, Nanming Stronghold lost.

Second match, Nanming Stronghold won.

One to one.

The true decider was yet to come.

After the battle, in the Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor.

Ning Zhuo closed his eyes, and Luo Shu slowly unrolled within his divine sea.

The terrain of the Abundant Earth Martial Arena, True Monarch Shi Heng's footwork, the shrinking rhythm of the earthen-yellow halos, the flow of Earth Qi from the vein-borrowing talisman - these scenes replayed in Ning Zhuo's mind one after another.

"The Earth Element isn't just about thickness and weight."

"It can bear burdens, it can seal and suppress, it can transfer, and it can accumulate momentum."

"True Monarch Shi Heng's greatest strength doesn't lie in killing power, but in connecting himself with the environment. When the person is injured, the earth shares the burden. When human strength is exhausted, the earth veins replenish it. In the truest sense, he obtained - geographical advantage!"

Borrowing the terrain's momentum, accumulating the power of the thick earth, using defense to drain offense, using weight to crush lightness.

Spiritual light gradually dawned in Ning Zhuo's heart.

The first minor Earth Element technique took shape in his mind.

Its name was: Earth-Borrowing Damage Distribution.

This technique was not a complete replica of True Monarch Shi Heng's talisman. That talisman was too high-grade; it could temporarily borrow the martial arena's earth veins and even transfer injuries into the thick earth. What Ning Zhuo comprehended at this moment only grasped its superficial principles.

The Earth-Borrowing Damage Distribution technique used Earth Element spiritual energy to engrave technique seals onto the ground. When the cultivator was struck, they wouldn't transfer the injuries out of thin air, but rather use the technique seals to utilize the earth to share the burden.

The strength of this technique was that it could mitigate blunt force impacts, concussions, and heavy pressure-type attacks. Its weakness was that it couldn't share the burden of poison, Five Elements, curses, and other specific types of damage.

The second minor Earth Element technique originated from those stone pillars and earthen-yellow halos.

Its name was: Ringing Earth Body-Binding Method.

True Monarch Shi Heng used a forest of stone pillars to spew out earthen-yellow halos, looping them layer by layer onto Chunyangzi's shoulders, wrists, and waist. This method was not fancy, but it was extremely suited for restricting an enemy's movement.

Ning Zhuo imitated it, simplifying and improving it.

After a cultivator cast the technique, it would instantly form low earth stakes. Earthen-yellow rings of qi would spew out between the stakes. The rings of qi wouldn't forcibly bind the target, but rather increase the feeling of heaviness around the enemy's body. If the enemy's movement techniques were elusive, the rings of qi would follow their footsteps' landing points, slowing their evasive rhythm.

The strength of this technique was its low consumption and rapid deployment, making it especially effective against opponents relying on lightweight, dodging, and short-distance evasive maneuvers. The flaw was that the earth stakes had weak defenses and were easily broken.

Ning Zhuo's feelings toward the Pure Yang cultivation art weren't deep.

Chunyangzi's path was upright, pure, and singular. Ning Zhuo, on the other hand, had Mechanisms, alchemy, the Five Elements, the Blood Ember Fire Seed, the Luo Shu's deductions, and the newly acquired White Cloud Nature.

Ning Zhuo majored in Mechanism arts; his Cultivation path was destined to be mixed and ever-changing. He was not suited for a singular cultivation method like Chunyangzi's.

However, Ning Zhuo could distinguish strength and weakness.

Chunyangzi's strength obviously exceeded True Monarch Shi Heng's. In this second battle, he used an imposing momentum, neither dodging nor evading, and crushed Shi Heng head-on. It could be said that he displayed the prestige of the Nanming Stronghold, sweeping away the negative impact of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's defeat, and firmly securing his position as the Grand Stronghold Chief.

"He was clearly holding back, hiding some of his methods. He preferred to take minor injuries himself rather than use them."

"Without a doubt, he is a powerhouse among Nascent Souls, at least of the first-rate tier. As for whether he is top-tier, that has yet to be verified."

"One win, one loss - it's a draw. The upcoming matches will be crucial."

Thinking to this point, Ning Zhuo directly wrote a flying letter and sent it to Chunyangzi, straightforwardly stating his view: Tan Zhu should be dispatched for the third match!

Chunyangzi received and read the letter, immediately sending a reply.

Ning Zhuo soon received the reply, which contained only a few words: "I was just thinking the same."

The youth smiled faintly, putting the matter out of his mind and focusing on his daily cultivation routine.

After cultivating the Three Sects' Supreme Methods once, his focus remained on pondering the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art. Following a round of practicing his Cultivation skills, he went to use the [Fire Refining Spell] to deal with the Blood Ember Fire Seed.

The fire seed was divided into three layers. As Ning Zhuo slowly refined the outer layer of blood flames, he felt his mind continuously stirred, with every realization related to the Blood Dao.

Joy swelled in his heart: "Excellent! Refining this fire seed can also help me deepen my understanding of the Blood Dao and accelerate my cultivation of the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art!"
