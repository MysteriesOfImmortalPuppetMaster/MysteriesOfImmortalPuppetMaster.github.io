Chapter 775: Sudden Sense of Crisis

The battle scene was incredibly intense.

Situ Xing controlled sword lights that crisscrossed like meteors, displaying a posture as if he could charge seven times in and seven times out of an army of thousands.

Zhu Fenxiang, on the other hand, had a different fighting style. She occupied a corner of the martial arts arena, defending with rock-solid stability. At least two divine spirits orbited her; she attacked little and defended much, yet she still held the upper hand.

Ning Zhuo discerned the reason: "Situ Xing's combat method uses swords to lay down formations. This way, the formation is powerful, capable of modifying the battlefield and elevating his own combat power, while the sharp sword lights can inflict heavy damage or even slay enemy cultivators."

"Compared to him, Zhu Fenxiang's fighting style is much simpler and cruder."

"She only needs to summon divine spirit clones to assist in the battle."

"Zhu Fenxiang has been preventing Situ Xing from laying down a larger sword formation. Therefore, relying on just one Golden Core-level divine spirit clone for offense is enough to cause severe interference."

"At the start of the battle, Situ Xing cast a sword formation that surrounded his body, elevating him to Golden Core combat power, but that wasn't enough to defeat Zhu Fenxiang. Only by laying out a stronger sword formation would that be possible."

"Both sides are grappling over this point."

Thinking of this, Ning Zhuo paused slightly: "My grasp of the battle situation is clearer and more thorough. This must be the amplification from the true intent and principles of the Military Strategist. Very good, very good."

He suddenly thought of the point that "Situ Xing and Shen Xi do not get along."

He gained more understanding of this intelligence: "No wonder Situ Xing wants to suppress Shen Xi; it might be because Shen Xi is also skilled in formations, and specifically the Nine Palaces Formation, which requires the most calculation among the nine great static formations."

"Previously, when the two clashed, Shen Xi had actually defeated Situ Xing. Could it be because Shen Xi is stronger than Situ Xing in formations, making it difficult for him to set them up?"

The three true intents brought an elevation to Ning Zhuo's realm, and this elevation would affect every aspect of Ning Zhuo's future cultivation and life.

The improvement in his ability to spectate battles was just one small manifestation of this.

Situ Xing and Zhu Fenxiang had fought several times before and knew each other inside out.

The two sides went back and forth; one wanted to execute his sword formation tactics, while the other had few tactical requirements and was consistently trying her best to obstruct Situ Xing's calculations.

During the battle, the specks of sword light Situ Xing left on the battlefield were like stones placed in a game of Go. The more of these stones, the more advantageous it was for him.

Finally, by throwing out a new trump card, he managed to buy the critical time needed to successfully erect another powerful sword formation.

"Zhu Fenxiang, accept defeat within my Universal Star Dipper Sword Formation!" Situ Xing shouted, his hands flying rapidly to form complex sword seals.

Every seal his fingers precisely formed drew upon the power of the stars within the sword formation.

Consequently, the star swords multiplied.

One became two, two became four, four became eight... in the blink of an eye, three hundred and sixty-five star swords hovered densely around Situ Xing.

The next moment, sword lights shot out, falling like a meteor shower.

Zhu Fenxiang hurriedly mobilized her three divine spirits to fully guard her.

For a moment, the star swords showed their might, firmly suppressing Zhu Fenxiang and the three divine spirits into a disadvantage.

She half-knelt on the ground, cupping her hands together, holding the miniature statue in her palms against her heart.

"Nine Heavens and Ten Earths, Abundant Earth is supreme! Eight Desolations and Six Harmonies, suppressing hell and bearing the heavens! Believer Fenxiang, using my blood, my bone, and my soul as a guide, kowtows to the Mysterious Dome! Respectfully inviting the Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord - may your Dharma body descend to the mortal realm!"

She called out in a high voice.

In the next moment, Zhu Fenxiang's aura plummeted like a waterfall, and her entire being became incredibly weak.

Using her own foundation as the sacrificial material, she successfully drew the gaze of the Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord and brought down divine power, descending as a clone!

The statue in Zhu Fenxiang's hands burst forth with blinding divine light.

The divine light shot into the sky, transforming into an earth-yellow pillar of light.

When the pillar dissipated, Zhu Fenxiang had vanished from her spot, replaced by a divine body standing three zhang tall.

The god was draped in Black-Yellow Rock Armor, and dark golden leyline magma flowed slowly through the gaps in the armor.

Inside the heavy helmet, the revealed eyes were incomparably deep, steady as ancient mountains, capable of bearing all things and suppressing all evils!

A majestic divine might, like the awakening of a primordial divine mountain, descended with a boom!

Great God - Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord!

Zhu Fenxiang was already protected within the body, safe and sound.

Situ Xing's expression changed drastically. The Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord merely stood inside the formation, yet he felt the operation of the Universal Star Dipper Sword Formation stagnate, as if a giant python had swallowed a small hill and was struggling to digest it.

The Prison-Suppressing True Lord launched an attack.

Boom, boom, boom...

Every strike erupted with a massive sound, whipping up earth-yellow shockwaves of dust.

The Universal Star Dipper Sword Formation operated with difficulty; the star power crisscrossing within the array shattered inch by inch like fragile spiderwebs.

Sword gleams falling like meteors struck the Prison-Suppressing True Lord's body, only kicking up clouds of dust before disintegrating.

Zhu Fenxiang's voice came from within the divine body: "Situ Xing, I specifically let you complete this grand formation so that I could crush you head-on! With this defeat, you will have no excuses!"

Big! Big! Big!

The Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord's divine body expanded, almost propping up the sky and pressing down on the earth.

The Universal Star Dipper Sword Formation became increasingly difficult to operate, like a belly about to burst.

The True Lord extended a giant palm of black-yellow divine rock, grabbed the empty air, and pulled fiercely toward himself.

The next moment, the Universal Star Dipper Sword Formation seemed to have its spine removed; the grand formation collapsed, and star swords flew wildly in all directions, completely out of control.

The backlash of the formation slammed into Situ Xing's divine soul and physical body, causing him to feel as if he had been struck by lightning, and he wildly sprayed a large mouthful of fresh blood.

Specks of starlight were in the blood, mixed with many fragments of internal organs.

Situ Xing's aura decayed to its lowest point in this instant, then suddenly erupted with an unwilling fighting spirit.

"Zhu Fenxiang, even if you can invite a Great God, don't think you can win easily!" he shouted. His entire person exploded into starlight, transforming into a human-shaped comet dragging a long tail of flames, charging toward the Great God's body.

Man and sword as one, pressing forward with indomitable will!

Even if the jade and stone burn together, he would not hesitate!!

Under this intense and decisive battle intent, a conflict arose between the wills of Zhu Fenxiang and the Prison-Suppressing True Lord.

Zhu Fenxiang wanted to dodge, but the Prison-Suppressing True Lord felt it was an affront; being merely a clone, a loss was just a loss.

If Zhu Fenxiang and the Prison-Suppressing True Lord were separate, it would have been fine, but the key was that they were united as one, fighting with combined strength. The contradiction in their wills immediately created a flaw.

This flaw was quite minute, but Situ Xing happened to be attacking with full force and caught it perfectly.

At the critical moment, the Prison-Suppressing True Lord still raised a palm, but just as it emitted a phantom image of a black-yellow giant hand, it was pierced by Situ Xing's human-shaped comet.

Boom!

A deafening explosion rang out! Blinding yellow light and star radiance mixed together and erupted violently.

The light dissipated.

The phantom of the Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord dissipated unwillingly, leaving behind only specks of black-yellow light dust.

Zhu Fenxiang was bathed in blood; her right arm suffered a comminuted fracture and hung down limply. The wound pierced all the way through to her right chest, nearly endangering her heart, with blood gushing out.

Zhu Fenxiang's face was full of lingering fear. She glared at Situ Xing in shock and anger, only to find helplessly that although Situ Xing's aura was even weaker - on the verge of death, his life hanging by a thread - his life-saving jade token had already lit up, encasing him in an incredibly hard sphere of light.

Having dealt with Situ Xing before, Zhu Fenxiang naturally knew how sturdy such a defense was. The killing intent that had surfaced in an instant could only be dispersed.

Beside her, a miniature statue still floated.

It was the very treasure she had just used to summon the Wutu Prison-Suppressing True Lord. But now, the state of this treasure was quite strange; the earth-yellow radiance it emitted flowed constantly, its aura fluctuating between strong and weak, affecting the surrounding environment and forming a twisted black-yellow light shadow.

The light shadow was still expanding continuously.

Zhu Fenxiang's expression changed drastically. Not caring to declare victory over Situ Xing, she called directly to the Martial Arts Hall for help: "This is bad, the statue is undergoing Dao assimilation, send someone to handle it quickly!"

The Martial Arts Hall immediately mobilized formations to suppress it with full force.

Zhu Fenxiang hurriedly moved away, her face full of apprehension.

Fortunately, the Martial Arts Hall's formations were powerful enough. After a stalemate of several breaths, they successfully suppressed the Dao-assimilating statue.

However, this martial arts arena could not be used for a short period.

The Dao assimilation was still ongoing; although it was suppressed and the situation was controlled, it needed time to cool down.

Zhu Fenxiang had won, but the price was heavy - she had lost a precious statue.

It was a pyrrhic victory!
