Chapter 958: Watching the Battle Yields Five Arts

After combining the nine fires into a furnace and then transforming into his original dragon body to channel the dragon fire from above, his expenditure had reached an extremely high level. The red-gold dragon scales were still dazzling, but Ning Zhuo could see that the nine-colored firelight at the edges of those scales was no longer as continuous as it had been at the beginning.

Yun Wuji silently raised his tattered Cloud-Hiding Sleeve.

Within the sleeve, only a final wisp of cloud remained.

That wisp of cloud was extremely faint, almost scattered by the blazing sun.

He did not attack the Nine Fires Dragon Lord.

He merely released this wisp of cloud gently.

Cloud Entwining Sleeve.

The remnant cloud, like a white ribbon, entwined towards the tail end of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's massive dragon body.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord swung his dragon tail, easily shattering the white ribbon.

However, this swing strained his already heavy dragon body. Having just missed his attack, his spiritual energy had not yet recovered, and now, forcibly shattering the cloud ribbon caused his dragon body to sink slightly in the clear sky.

Beneath Yun Wuji's feet, his Traceless Cloud Shoes stepped out once more.

He did not take the opportunity to forcefully attack, but instead continued to retreat, continuing to widen the distance, and continuing to force the Nine Fires Dragon Lord to chase.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord chased for three breaths of time.

On the fourth breath, the nine fires on his body suddenly fell into chaos.

The Nine Orifices Furnace Heart Pendant hung beneath his dragon belly, trembling violently. The rotation of the nine fires finally showed obvious sluggishness due to consecutive failed strong attacks, the expenditure of transforming into a dragon, and the missed dragon fire suppression.

The red-gold dragon body halted in mid-air.

He wasn't injured; rather, his energy expenditure was too immense.

Yun Wuji threw out the last section of the banner face from his tattered Small White Cloud Banner. The banner face turned into a thin white cloud in the air, blocking the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's path. If the Nine Fires Dragon Lord were at his peak, a single breath would be enough to burn it to ashes. But at this moment, just as he was about to spew fire, his dragon breath was half a beat too late.

Half a beat later, the flames spewed out, and the white cloud dispersed.

But Yun Wuji had already reformed on the other side.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord chased again, but just as his dragon claw reached out, the claw phantom formed by the Mystic Flame Dragon Claw Ring also dimmed for a moment.

His exhaustion could no longer be completely concealed.

"The tactic worked!" Seeing this scene, Old Man Youyun finally breathed a sigh of relief.

Conversely, in the seating area of the Nanming Stronghold, Chunyangzi and the others had very ugly expressions.

Not long after, the sound of a jade chime rang out.

The voice of the deacon of this martial arena spread throughout the three seating areas.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord had already retracted the nine fires. His massive dragon body was forced to shrink back into human form, and his aura plummeted to rock bottom. Although monstrous rage burned in his eyes, his combat prowess could no longer sustain an offensive.

On the other hand, Yun Wuji stood in the distance, his figure swaying.

His Cloud-Hiding Sleeve was destroyed, the Small White Cloud Banner was broken, the Fog Heart Jade Pendant was cracked, and there were scorch marks on his Traceless Cloud Shoes. His grayish-white Daoist robe was almost burned into rags, and the blood on his lips had not yet dried. But in the end, he was the one left standing there, possessing more remaining strength.

The outcome of this martial contest was already very obvious.

Among the seats of the Nanming Stronghold, there was a bout of silence.

The Red-Robed Guest looked displeased and let out a cold snort.

Tu Yuanzi looked at the Nine Fires Dragon Lord, then at Yun Wuji, feeling astonished by this result: The Nine Fires Dragon Lord was clearly so strong, so why was he the one who lost?!

The youth Ning Zhuo lowered his eyelashes. Looking at the remnant flames and cloud traces that had yet to completely disperse from the arena, he replayed this battle in his mind from beginning to end.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord had a Fire Dragon bloodline. As a Nascent Soul Demon Cultivator, his Physical Body's foundation was extremely robust. Especially at the end, when he manifested his true dragon form, that massive dragon body spanning the clear sky, that tyrannical might of his scales bearing fire and his dragon claws rending the air, even Ning Zhuo felt his mind tremble as he watched from behind the protective light screen.

Ning Zhuo also astutely noticed: In this battle, the Nine Fires Dragon Lord actually failed to fully utilize the physical advantages of a Demon Cultivator.

"His primary cultivation is alchemy," Ning Zhuo thought to himself. "His combat style is not the pouncing slaughter of a demonic dragon, but rather an alchemist controlling fire and opening a furnace."

The true nature of the Nine Fires Dragon Lord was completely laid bare in Ning Zhuo's mind!

"Yun Wuji's victory lies in his tactical choice, and even more so in his thorough execution."

From the moment he exhaled the first wisp of cloud qi, Yun Wuji never got greedy for a single winning strike. He didn't seek a counter-kill, he didn't seek to inflict heavy damage, and he didn't seek to make the scene look pretty. He solely focused on spreading clouds, nurturing clouds, hiding clouds, scattering clouds, and recalling clouds, continuously causing the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's forceful attacks to miss. Even at the end, when the Nine Fires Dragon Lord manifested his true dragon form and his descending dragon fire almost incinerated the entire cloud domain, Yun Wuji still used his last bit of remnant cloud to force the Nine Fires Dragon Lord to chase for a few more breaths.

Ultimately, he exhausted the Nine Fires Dragon Lord.

"Yun Wuji definitely wasn't holding on purely by his own skills. He must have received a massive amount of funding and resources."

"During this battle, a large number of his magic treasures were completely ruined, and the precious materials consumed in the process were quite valuable. For this victory, Yun Wuji - no, White Cloud Village - paid an enormous price."

"In fact, what Yun Wuji burned through in this match was probably not just his and White Cloud Village's foundation, but also many hidden trading chips."

Behind White Cloud Village and the Expanding Earth Alliance, there were other factions from Flowing Cloud Peak. They would definitely exert their power to stop the Nanming Stronghold's peak ascension as much as possible.

But their aid was definitely not given for free.

As this battle concluded, all three sides wore solemn expressions. Although the Nanming Stronghold lost the first match, they still had four chances remaining.

Filled with insights, Ning Zhuo returned to his own Cave Manor.

Inside the Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor, the three-colored spiritual lights of starlight, moonlight, and sun halos flowed and intertwined.

In the Cultivation Chamber, Ning Zhuo sat cross-legged on a futon, his expression calm, yet fiery spiritual light continuously flickered in the depths of his eyes.

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord used battle as refining and enemies as materials. This kind of "personal demonstration," which Ning Zhuo had watched from close range the entire time, gave him the most concrete object to imitate.

Don't forget, his Five Elements realm was incomparably profound!

Besides this, the lifelong alchemy experience of the Demonic Soul Jia Luanzhen was like a black library sunk deep within his sea of consciousness, which he flipped open scroll by scroll.

The Luo Shu's calculations quietly unfolded, and streaks of spiritual light intersected in his mind.

A moment later, Ning Zhuo's face was filled with exhaustion. He had consumed eighty percent of the divine sense and thoughts in his divine sea, but he had obtained three Techniques.

The first one, Ning Zhuo named the [Seven-Revolution Fire Control Art].

The Nine Fires Dragon Lord's seven revolutions of alchemy had taught Ning Zhuo a great deal. The Nine Fires Dragon Lord could simultaneously manipulate nine fires; Ning Zhuo couldn't reach that level yet, nor was that Ning Zhuo's direction.

The [Seven-Revolution Fire Control Art] allowed a single flame to be clearly divided into seven different fire intensities. It could protect the medicinal properties, stimulate the medicinal power, break the medicinal shell, discern impurities, guide the spiritual intuition, merge the pill qi, and set the pill shape.

The second Technique, Ning Zhuo named the [Furnace-Returning Flame-Recalling Art], which could gather back scattered flames.

The third Technique was the [Fire Refining Art]. As the name implied, it allowed for deeper refinement and mastery over a certain type of flame.

This was created by Ning Zhuo after watching the Nine Fires Dragon Lord wield his nine fires from beginning to end, seeing through many of the profound mysteries of fire control within.

Ning Zhuo took out some Medicinal Pills, tilted his neck back, and swallowed a few.

He mobilized his spiritual energy to quickly dissolve their medicinal power, causing his divine sea to fill up once again and his personal energy to recover.

Ning Zhuo tested out the three new methods.

First was the [Seven-Revolution Fire Control Art].

He took out multiple portions of ordinary medicinal ingredients and placed them into a small, ordinary alchemy furnace to test it.

At the bottom of the furnace, the flame first turned into a gentle heat, clinging to the furnace as it moved, warm and stable. Then, its size expanded, climbing along the texture of the medicinal ingredients, slowly forcing out their medicinal properties. After that, the tongue of flame took on a blue hue and lightly tapped the hardest part of the ingredients, causing the shell to crack open. Following that, the firelight flared up, illuminating three dark spots of impurities within the medicinal liquid.

The flame became ethereal, drawing the spiritual intuition into thin threads and twisting them into a single strand. The flame's volume expanded, tightly wrapping around the medicinal liquid. Finally, the flame suddenly retracted, and the prototype of the pill was formed by the fire.

Soon, a faint, clear medicinal fragrance wafted out from the small alchemy furnace.

The pill was successfully refined.

"With the success of this art, my control over the heat during future alchemy sessions will be at least three times more precise. At the same time, this means a massive increase in my alchemy efficiency."

Ning Zhuo then tested the [Furnace-Returning Flame-Recalling Art].

He formed hand seals with both hands, intentionally allowing the flame in the furnace to leak out by three inches first. Medicinal smoke escaped from the gaps in the furnace lid; under normal circumstances, this would be a loss. Unhurried, Ning Zhuo drew a looping fire rune with his fingertips. The fire rune acted like an inverted vortex, pulling the escaped medicinal smoke, residual heat, and floating flames all back into the belly of the furnace.

The small alchemy furnace trembled slightly.

The originally scattered fire qi returned to its place. Not only did it not ruin the medicinal properties, but it actually made the pill qi inside the furnace slightly more perfect.

"This art can compensate for lost fire and gather residual flames. During combat, it can also retract externally released firepower, reducing consumption."

Finally came the [Fire Refining Art].

Ning Zhuo suspended the Blood Ember Fire Seed in the palm of his hand and channeled the [Fire Refining Art] to perform a deeper level of refinement!

The Blood Ember Fire Seed jumped abruptly.

Inside the Cave Manor, the firelight flared brilliantly, casting a dark red glow onto the surrounding walls.

Ning Zhuo's face paled slightly. The palm and skin surfaces illuminated by the firelight transmitted a dense, stinging sensation, as if countless tiny fire needles were piercing his flesh and blood.

He didn't pull his hand back; instead, he exhaled slowly, continuously channeling the Technique towards the Blood Ember Fire Seed.

The Blood Ember Fire Seed was originally a unified whole, but under the influence of the Technique, it unexpectedly began to divide into three layers.

The outermost layer was a layer of floating flame. Its fire nature was the most active, igniting upon touching blood and carrying the bright red hue of blood.

The middle layer was a layer of gray flame. Its fire nature was inconspicuous, looking like dark ash after burning out, yet it was the most stubborn.

At the very center was a layer of blue flame. It was as still as a gemstone, fixed in place, and difficult to communicate with.

"It's viable." A look of joy surfaced in Ning Zhuo's eyes.

After refining it for a moment, he found that his degree of control over the outer floating flame was continuously and noticeably deepening.

"It just needs time. This is a matter of slow, persistent effort."

Ning Zhuo felt satisfied in his heart. He hadn't expected that observing the Nine Fires Dragon Lord's battle would yield such an unexpected harvest.

But on second thought, he felt it was well within reason.

Outside the Cave Manor, the night was growing darker.

However, Ning Zhuo didn't feel the slightest bit of drowsiness.

Aside from fire methods, cloud methods were also continuously surging in his mind.

His own Water Element realm was profound, giving him quite an understanding of water vapor, mist, circulation, and the gathering and dispersal of elements. Clouds were formed from rising water vapor; they drifted with the wind, gathered when meeting cold, and dispersed when meeting heat. Yun Wuji's cloud dao seemed erratic, but in reality, it implicitly aligned with the transformations of the Water Element everywhere.

Ning Zhuo closed his eyes, and the cloud methods his Mother had once used suddenly surfaced in his mind.

Cloud Net Demonic Garment.

That Cloud Net Demonic Garment still left a deep impression on him to this day. Cloud qi formed the garment, while baleful intent served as the bones. It appeared soft and gentle, but it could actually protect the body, conceal one's form, transform, and counterattack. His Mother had once instructed him to learn the Cloud Net Grand Canon.

The Cloud Net Grand Canon was left behind by the Myriad Manifestations Sect's past sage, Cloud Demonic Daoist.

Ning Zhuo silently recited that sentence in his heart: "The Cloud Net is no mere net, but an image of the Heavenly Dao's compassion; the Demonic Garment is no mere garment, but truly formed from the reverse scales of Heaven and Earth!"

"It's a pity I haven't truly joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect." The more Ning Zhuo thought about it, the more he felt an itch in his heart.

Regrettably, for a core inheritance like the Cloud Net Grand Canon, he currently had no qualifications to acquire it.

Ultimately, using his own Water Element realm as a foundation and Yun Wuji's combat methods as a reference, he deduced two minor cloud Techniques.

The first one was named [Cloud Gap Borrowed Step].

This art was used for evasion. As long as there was water vapor, mist, smoke, medicinal smoke, or even the humid heat escaping from an alchemy furnace nearby, one could use Water Element spiritual energy to temporarily condense a cloud gap. If a cultivator took a step into the cloud gap, their figure could use the cloud qi to slide anywhere from a few to dozens of zhang away.

It wasn't instantaneous movement; it required water vapor to rely on, and the distance was limited, but its consumption of spiritual energy and physical stamina was low.

Ning Zhuo tested it inside the Cave Manor.

He first used the medicinal smoke from the alchemy furnace to condense a faint cloud gap. With a light tap of his toe, his figure actually shifted laterally from the front of the futon to beside the stone wall three zhang away. The movement wasn't considered perfect, and the hem of his clothes was still messed up by the airflow, but the effect was guaranteed.

The second one was named [Cloud Skin Shadow Swap].

This art was used for disguise and avoiding surprise attacks.

Drawing reference from Yun Wuji's cloud skin substitute, and combining it with the concept of the Cloud Net Demonic Garment, Ning Zhuo designed an extremely thin layer of cloud skin. The cloud skin clung to the surface of his body. It didn't aim to be a complete Transformation Body, but only sought to create a slightly offset outline of a fake body when scanned by an enemy's divine sense or initially touched by a Technique.

If an enemy's attack fell, the cloud skin could first create a fake body to receive the attack, while Ning Zhuo's true body could take the opportunity to escape.

Ning Zhuo raised his hand, condensing a faint layer of cloud skin.

Draped over his white clothes, the cloud skin looked like a layer of transparent thin gauze. He took a step forward, but the cloud skin lagged behind by half an inch. It then swayed gently, leaving an extremely shallow afterimage in its original place.

"Wait a minute, I remember there's a Rising Cloud Minor Trial related to cloud methods."

"I didn't have confidence or interest before, but now, I feel the probability of taking first place is very high."

"Perhaps I can give it a try."

Before the morning colors faded, Ning Zhuo left the Cave Manor.

The morning breeze blew from the halfway up the mountain, carrying a faint hint of moisture. It brushed past his sleeves and condensed into several tiny dewdrops on the edges.

Ning Zhuo raised his hand, the pads of his fingers gently wiping across the dewdrops.

The dewdrops were round and smooth, their watery essence condensed and unscattering.

"Clouds arise from water," Ning Zhuo silently recited in his heart, a light shining in his eyes. "Today I'll go participate in that Rising Cloud Minor Trial and test out my cloud methods."

In the five-win martial contest, the Nanming Stronghold had lost the first match, which felt like a heavy blow to the head. After the first night passed, Chunyangzi and the others would definitely have a reaction.

But this was no longer something Ning Zhuo wanted to participate in.

Regarding the matter of the martial contest, his abilities were not yet sufficient.

The layout he had painstakingly set up previously had already begun to take effect, helping him digest his Calamitous luck.

He took the opportunity to look for breakthroughs in other aspects.

The Cloud Nursery Main Courtyard was established within a mountain col where floating clouds lingered year-round.

There were no ordinary spirit fields in the mountain col, only suspended cloud nurseries one after another. The cloud nurseries varied in size; some were only as big as a desk, while others spread out for over a dozen zhang, nurturing various types of cloud materials inside.

Pure white infant clouds, pale green moist clouds, light red rosy clouds, heavy gray turbid clouds, and thunderclouds carrying tiny arcs of lightning were all enclosed by dense cloud fences. They slowly inhaled and exhaled water vapor, sometimes expanding and sometimes shrinking, looking like breathing white jade medicinal fields.

Outside the Cloud Nursery Courtyard, quite a few cultivators had already gathered.

The Rising Cloud Minor Trial here was an old tradition and had already been running for several months.

The minor trial did not test killing or cultivation realms, but rather foundational cloud arts such as generating clouds, nurturing clouds, transforming clouds, and herding clouds. According to the rules of past years, it was mostly low-tier cultivators, rogue cultivators, and cultivators with weak Innate Talent who came to sign up and participate.

As soon as Ning Zhuo appeared, the sidelines immediately quieted down quite a bit.

"It's actually Ning Zhuo? Why is he here?"

"The Nanming Stronghold just lost the first match. Instead of going to discuss the martial contest with Chunyangzi, he ran over here to participate in the Rising Cloud Minor Trial?"
