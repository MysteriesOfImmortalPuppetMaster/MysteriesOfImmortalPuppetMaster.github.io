Chapter 942: Divine Ability Reaching the Divine

"It's just that this place is extremely, extremely suitable for Chunyangzi." A trace of worry appeared in Ning Zhuo's expression.

Chunyangzi's status was very special; he currently held the number one seat.

Ning Zhuo had learned from the intelligence: the Cultivation Art Chunyangzi practiced was called the Pure Yang Pill Scripture. He maintained a pure yang body, used yang to generate pills, and used pills to nourish his Dao.

"I do not know the specific contents of the Pure Yang Pill Scripture, so I cannot deduce just how attractive this place is to Chunyangzi."

"This point needs to be paid close attention to!"

-----

Pure Yang Peak.

Pure Yang Palace.

Red-gold lamp light shone on the four walls, brightly illuminating the study.

Chunyangzi had originally been sitting upright at his desk, his posture calm and composed. But when he infused his spiritual energy into the Jade Slip and manifested the related scenes of the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud-Ascending Altar, he suddenly froze.

Beneath his golden crown, his eyes suddenly shot out a brilliant light!

A massive pleasant surprise!!

"This, this is?!" His heart palpitated. Before he even read the detailed intelligence, a strong realization washed over him - this place held unparalleled, extraordinary significance for him!

"Supporting the sun... locking the yang... ascending the clouds..." After reading the intelligence, Chunyangzi's expression was immensely shocked.

He was a peak Nascent Soul cultivator. He had been stuck at this step for many years.

He had fallen into a bottleneck in his Cultivation.

But at this moment, at the end of his long Dao Path, he suddenly saw a heavenly gate open a tiny crack, with light pouring out from within.

This was the light of hope!

Having cultivated for nearly a thousand years, what did Chunyangzi seek?

Naturally, it was a higher realm - Soul Formation!

The difference of a single realm between Nascent Soul and Soul Formation was like a baby and an adult looking at each other across a river. Even if Nascent Soul cultivators possessed the ability to move mountains and drain seas, they were ultimately still "infants." Their spiritual energy, divine sense, and Lifebound Techniques were all rooted in themselves.

But Soul Formation was different.

For those at the Soul Formation stage, their divine intent manifested externally, and their comprehension of laws reached the heavens. At that stage, a cultivator didn't just use techniques; they used their own divine intent to pull upon the principles of Heaven and Earth.

Every Soul Formation cultivator definitively possessed a Divine Ability. A so-called Divine Ability was not simply a technique with greater power. It was the result of cultivating a Lifebound Technique to the absolute limit, where the law merged with the Dao, and the technique blended with the divine. From then on, a single thought would stir the heavens, and Heaven and Earth would respond.

When a Nascent Soul cast a technique, they still had to circulate and consume spiritual energy.

A Soul Formation's Divine Ability, however, already carried a bit of the implication of "the natural laws acting on their own."

Chunyangzi primarily cultivated the Pure Yang Pill Scripture. His Lifebound Technique had long been tempered thousands of times; it was only a hair's breadth away from becoming a Divine Ability, separated by an incredibly profound threshold. Yet, this very threshold had trapped countless peak Nascent Souls.

To elevate one's Lifebound Technique to the level of a Divine Ability required abundant comprehension and talent to even be possible.

And only by mastering a Divine Ability could a Nascent Soul cross that final step, achieve a qualitative change, and advance to a Primordial Spirit!

It wasn't that Chunyangzi hadn't tried, but despite expending vast amounts of time and energy, there wasn't a single sign of success.

Therefore, he had shifted the focus of his cultivation, taking on more of the responsibility for the Pure Yang Palace's inheritance.

But now, he saw the hope to break through his bottleneck!

The Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud-Ascending Altar!

"The sun's radiance enters the cave, the fire veins lie below, and the cloud qi resides in the middle. The Yang-Locking Well gathers its essence, while the Cloud-Ascending Altar props up its momentum. Pure yang does not leak; there is life within the fire, a sun within the clouds, and yang within the earth."

"Such a terrain is so compatible with and complementary to my Pure Yang Pill Scripture!"

"As long as I cultivate in this place, allowing me to contemplate Heaven and Earth and learn from nature, there is a much higher possibility of allowing my Lifebound Technique to truly sublimate and reach the realm of a Divine Ability!"

Use fire to nourish the pill, use the pill to nourish the Dao.

Use yang to refine the spirit, use the Divine Ability to reach the heavens!

Chunyangzi was endlessly excited and slowly stood up.

The three thousand characters of the Pure Yang Pill Scripture on his red-gold Daoist robe seemed to be triggered by his mood, actually glowing faintly one by one.

The lamp light in the study flared up without any wind. The flames did not burn upwards but slightly bowed toward his body, like courtiers paying respects to their monarch.

"This place is my Soul Formation serendipity!" Chunyangzi muttered to himself.

At this moment, he no longer had a shred of hesitation.

The recruitment of Danxia Peak, the debt claims over the Naming Fire Furnace, the covert struggles between the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak - in the face of this treasured land, everything had to take a backseat.

He had to obtain the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud-Ascending Altar!

"No, I don't need to act alone; that would be inappropriate."

"Nanming Stronghold... Nanming Stronghold can occupy this place, and it must occupy this place!!"

A raging inferno seemed to ignite within Chunyangzi's heart, blazing so intensely that he felt his entire body's blood boil from it.

"I am the Grand Stronghold Chief! I can perfectly take advantage of the situation and use this opportunity to conceal my true objective."

"Others do not know the contents of the Pure Yang Pill Scripture, so they will vastly underestimate the value this place holds for me."

"After I lead Nanming Stronghold to occupy this place, I will immediately enter secluded bitter cultivation. Once I successfully cultivate my Primordial Spirit, that will be an entirely different world."

"Hahaha, hahaha..."

Thinking of the wondrous prospects, Chunyangzi couldn't help but let out a series of hearty laughs.

-----

Flowing Cloud Peak.

A hidden Cloud Cavern.

Suspended in the center of the Cloud Cavern was a low golden platform. Beneath the platform, cloud qi surged, displaying a layer of dark, somber red-gold, as if tens of thousands of fine golden scales were submerged at the bottom of the clouds.

Liu Jinke sat on the edge of the golden platform, his arms crossed and his expression gloomy.

In front of him lay a contract.

This contract was not made of paper, but rather a thin golden page. Blood veins wriggled across the page like earthworms, faintly condensing into the imprints of several factions. The lightning crest of the Thunder Cloud Society, the golden hairpin seal of the Metal and Stone Alliance, the medicine gourd pattern of the Hanging Pot Clinic, the mountain range stamp of the Earth Expansion Alliance, the jade disc emblem of the Floating Life Society, the beast claw mark of the Bird and Beast Villa...

Seal after seal pressed down on the corners of the golden page, actually making this tiny piece of contract heavy as a mountain.

Lightning faintly flickered on Lei Wangyue's purple robes, and impatience showed between his brows: "Liu Jinke, the serendipity is laid out right in front of your eyes. How long are you going to hesitate?"

The Golden Hairpin Old Woman sat in the shadows, her withered fingers leaning on a walking cane, her voice hoarse: "This is no ordinary sponsorship. We are going to help push your Golden Liquid Pill-Returning Physique to the realm of a quasi-Divine Ability! Such good fortune is something countless cultivators couldn't even dream of."

Wen Suzhen said softly: "Your physique is special; your Upper Dantian is lost, and your divine sense has merged completely with your golden blood and physical body. Ordinary magic treasures do not suit you, but this path is perfect for you. If successful, you will elevate to an entirely new state, able to condense your golden blood into pills. Once a pill disperses, it will be like a supreme nourishing spirit pill - repairing the physical body, replenishing spiritual energy, nourishing soul thoughts, and stabilizing qi mechanics. It will be practically omnipotent."

Qiu Lei was more direct: "With us investing so massively, we must have a guarantee. Once you sign the contract, you will serve the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak for thirty years. It's only thirty years; to a Golden Core cultivator, it's nothing."

Hearing this, Liu Jinke repeatedly shook his head.

"Of course I know the wondrous uses of a Divine Ability. But I need to be alive to use it!"

"You are sponsoring me because you want me to fight Ning Zhuo. But I've already had my head chopped off twice."

"A third time?"

"A third time, will Ning Zhuo still spare me?"

The Cloud Cavern fell silent.

Liu Jinke's chest heaved, his face turning pale green, and his voice became hoarse: "You all are at most just bystanders. You have no idea what it feels like to be decapitated!"

No one replied.

Liu Jinke felt as if an old wound in his heart had been ripped open, and the fear suppressed deep within surged up once again.

His fingers involuntarily reached to touch his neck.

It had long since healed perfectly, but in his sensation, it seemed as if an icy blade scar still lay horizontally across it. A light touch was enough to send a chill through his body and mind.

Liu Jinke stared at the crowd, emphasizing every word: "You want me to go risk my life, I'm absolutely not doing that. I am also a member of the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Unless you go through the Martial Arena, you can't do anything to me at all!"

Lei Wangyue's face darkened, tiny arcs of lightning jumping around his body.

The Golden Hairpin Old Woman took a deep breath, her gaze icy.

Ye Qingming was also present. Currently sitting beside a green tea lamp, her features were gentle, and her tone flowed smoothly like spring water: "Fellow Daoist Liu Jinke, you are afraid of Ning Zhuo, we can all see that. That you are afraid is not shameful. How could a youth like Ning Zhuo be an ordinary genius? If I were in your shoes, I fear that after being decapitated the first time, I would never go out to fight again."

"Based on that point alone, your courage, Fellow Daoist Liu Jinke, is above mine."

Liu Jinke's expression softened slightly.

Ye Qingming continued: "But you should also understand that you can no longer retreat. You lost to Ning Zhuo twice, and the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak were also defeated by Ning Zhuo twice. How do the people outside view you? How do they view us?"

Jin Mantang chuckled and patted his belly: "To take a business deal to this stage means we've already taken a bottomless loss. If we don't win back our capital, all of us will truly become a laughingstock on Flowing Cloud Peak in the future."

Liu Jinke said coldly: "That is your joke, what does it have to do with me?"

Xu Duanchao had originally been silent the whole time, but he suddenly spoke up: "Do you think you still have any other path?"

His voice was as cold as the back of a knife scraping against bone.

"If you don't fight Ning Zhuo, Ning Zhuo will continue to make an issue out of you. If you don't accept our fortuitous encounter, you will still just be that Liu Jinke who had his head chopped off twice. If you accept it, you still have a chance to turn things around."

Liu Jinke's facial skin twitched slightly.

He hated those words.

Yet he knew those words made sense.

Nowadays, when he walked on the streets, there were still people secretly calling him "Headless Guest." In the visual transmission Jade Slips, the scene of him being decapitated by Ning Zhuo and conceding defeat circulated over and over again. Every time it was shared, it was a slap to his face, and also a slap to his Dao Heart.

Lei Wangyue added the final strike: "The Sect rules of the Myriad Manifestations Sect are indeed like that; we truly cannot do anything directly to you. But you are a veteran as well, how could you not know that to deal with a person, why must we go through the Martial Arena? There are all sorts of methods, I couldn't even finish telling you about them in three days and three nights, right?"

Liu Jinke's eyes twitched.

He gritted his teeth and said: "Thirty years of servitude, that is absolutely impossible!"

Qiu Lei frowned: "Then what do you want?"

Liu Jinke stayed silent for a moment before saying slowly: "I can accept your sponsorship, and I can also fight Ning Zhuo again. But the contract must be changed."

Lei Wangyue sneered: "You're still negotiating terms?"

Liu Jinke raised his eyes, a trace of ruthlessness finally appearing in his gaze: "If you could find someone else to go fight Ning Zhuo, why would you be here wasting your breath on me?"

The aura within the Cloud Cavern stagnated.

These words accurately poked at everyone's weak spot.

Liu Jinke was a pawn, but he was also the most suitable pawn at present. If they swapped him for anyone else, they might not be able to force Ning Zhuo to accept the challenge. Only Liu Jinke already had the karma of two battles with Ning Zhuo; a third battle would be perfectly logical and justifiable, leaving outsiders no room for gossip.

On another aspect, if the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak didn't achieve victory through Liu Jinke, even if they used someone else to reach that battle result, it wouldn't convince the public.

Ning Zhuo's declaration after the second battle was essentially treating Liu Jinke as a special letter of challenge!

Wen Suzhen sighed: "Never mind. The thirty years of servitude can be changed. You do not need to pledge loyalty to the various factions; you only need to promise one thing."

Liu Jinke looked at him.

Wen Suzhen said: "Each of our families will help you achieve a quasi-Divine Ability. In the future, you must do one thing for each of the participating parties. The matters must not violate the foundation of your Dao Heart, must not intentionally send you to your death, and we must not use the contract to control your Divine Soul. How about this?"

Jin Mantang immediately chimed in: "This is good. The transaction is clear: one favor per family, with clearly marked terms. Everyone is comfortable with it."

Although Lei Wangyue snorted coldly in dissatisfaction, he did not force the issue any further.

Ye Qingming nodded lightly: "An agreement of one task sounds better than servitude, and it's also easier for outsiders to accept."

The Golden Hairpin Old Woman looked at Liu Jinke: "If you still do not agree, then it is no longer caution, but simply useless mud that cannot be plastered onto a wall."

Qiu Lei's gaze was gloomy: "Then don't blame us."

Liu Jinke took a deep breath, staring at the golden page contract.

After a long time, he finally reached out, touched the contract, and infused his spiritual energy.

The golden page buzzed and vibrated. The originally dense blood veins scattered one after another, condensing anew into a different text of agreement. The thirty years of servitude was erased, replaced by the stipulation of "one task for each party."

Liu Jinke watched this scene, unease still lingering in his heart. But aside from the unease, a wisp of the flame of greed quietly ignited.

A quasi-Divine Ability!

"My Golden Liquid Pill-Returning Physique, if it can truly be pushed to the realm of a quasi-Divine Ability..."

Ning Zhuo's face seemed to surface before his eyes once more.

The white-robed youth, his calm expression, the flaming blade light falling from his hand... In front of everyone's eyes, severing his head and simultaneously shattering his prestige.

Liu Jinke's five fingers slowly clenched tight.

"Ning Zhuo," he roared lowly in his heart, "if I truly achieve a quasi-Divine Ability, in the third battle, it will be your turn to fear me!"

He hesitated no longer and signed the contract.

The moment the contract was signed, the atmosphere in the Cloud Cavern instantly shifted.

All the previous oppression, coldness, and probing seemed to be blown away by a gust of cloud wind. The crowd spoke no more nonsense, each with a solemn expression, bringing out their true trump cards.

The first to be opened was the treasure box of the Metal and Stone Alliance.

The Golden Hairpin Old Woman personally lifted the lid. Inside, there was no radiant glow of pearls or jade, only a pale-golden stone the size of a fist. This stone's surface was covered in natural pill patterns, the lines contracting inward circle by circle, resembling a Golden Core embedded within stone.

"Great White Pill Origin Gold."

The Golden Hairpin Old Woman's voice also carried a hint of heartache: "This item was buried in the Metal and Stone Earth Lungs for three hundred years, bathed in the starlight of the Great White Star, before it formed pill patterns. Once refined into your golden blood, it will make your golden blood not just sharp, but also grant it the property of condensing into a pill."

What the Thunder Cloud Society presented was a bottle of Purple Gold Lightning Marrow.

Uncorking the bottle revealed lightning light that flowed like liquid inside. The electric arcs were not azure-purple, but gold with a hint of purple.

Lei Wangyue said: "This is the tribulation lightning essence accumulated beneath the Tribulation Transcendence Terrace. Your golden blood flows too smoothly; it lacks a sense of tribulation tempering. Once this marrow enters your body, it will hurt so much you'll wish to tear your flesh out, but if you survive it, your golden blood will be able to endure lightning without scattering."

The Hanging Pot Clinic brought out a set of golden needles as fine as cow hair.

Wen Suzhen personally held the needles, his expression solemn: "These are Life-Continuing Golden Needles. Sealed within the needles are the residual medicinal properties of the Nine-Aperture Blood Gold Embryo. In a moment, I will use these needles to seal the three hundred and sixty-five blood apertures around your body, ensuring your golden blood cannot leak outward and can only condense inward into a pill."

Qiu Lei of the Earth Expansion Alliance did not bring out a treasure box, but instead poured out a handful of dark golden sandy soil from his sleeve.

As the soil hit the ground, it actually transformed into a small patch of miniature mountain range.

"Earth Lung Hidden Gold Sand," Qiu Lei introduced. "Gathered from the depths of the earth veins of Heavy Formation Peak, mixed with the residual warmth of a fire vein. Laying out a formation with this sand will allow your feet to step upon what feels like a golden vein. The metal qi will flow endlessly, merging into your blood."
