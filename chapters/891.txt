Chapter 891: Settling Debts

With the Nanming Fire Furnace acquiring a new master, this Rising Cloud Minor trial was soon announced to be over.

Ning Zhuo was in no rush to leave. Instead, he simply stayed at Danxia Peak, borrowing the venue here to hold private meetings with key figures one by one to settle his accounts.

This time, it was Ning Zhuo's turn to decide the order.

The first candidate was already set in stone; it couldn't be anyone else - Zhongli Mei!

Seeing Ning Zhuo, Zhongli Mei wore a smile: "Little friend's performance was truly stunning. It greatly surprised me and, at the same time, opened my eyes."

"Let's set aside other matters for now."

"Is little friend willing to join Danxia Peak?"

Ning Zhuo shook his head and was about to answer.

Zhongli Mei reached out a hand to add, "Little friend Ning Zhuo, I am asking once again on behalf of Peak Master Wang Yu. Naturally, Peak Master Wang Yu has already considered your relationship with Lord Zhong Dao."

"Therefore, Peak Master Wang Yu is willing to use the name of Danxia Peak to guarantee to you - as long as little friend joins our peak, he will handle things with Zhong Dao!"

"Previously, Peak Master Wang Yu offered three main peak heritage items, and now he is adding one more. Combined with the Nanming Fire Furnace in your hands, you would possess five great heritage treasures all at once."

"Not only that, from now on, little friend will definitely receive our peak's top-tier resource support and full cultivation. As long as you form a Golden Core, you will automatically be promoted to a deacon of our peak. Upon advancing to Nascent Soul, you will become an Elder of our peak!"

"As for the faction behind you, whether righteous or demonic, they can have long-term trade relations with our peak. The only difference is whether it's out in the open or in the shadows."

Ning Zhuo didn't hesitate at all, decisively shaking his head to politely decline.

Wang Yu was indeed full of sincerity, and he would certainly be able to ensure Zhong Dao didn't make trouble for Ning Zhuo.

But Ning Zhuo also had accomplishments in the Righteous Path and deeply understood that if he agreed and switched to Danxia Peak, his invisible losses would be immeasurably huge!

"Very well." Zhongli Mei sighed. "That was just the first question. Peak Master Wang Yu also asked me to bring a second question."

Ning Zhuo gestured with his hand: "Senior Zhong, please speak plainly."

Zhongli Mei's gaze sharpened as he stared into Ning Zhuo's eyes: "Peak Master Wang Yu asks: did little friend Ning Zhuo already possess the ability to make the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit acknowledge you as its master?"

Ning Zhuo's heart skipped a beat slightly, but his expression remained unchanged. He shook his head again, denying it without hesitation: "How could that be?"

"I was merely trying."

"Otherwise, during the final round, I wouldn't have borrowed so many precious materials from the Demon Subjugation Hall."

"I just chose to take a risk right before the artifact repair process."

"I didn't end up using any of those materials, and I'll have to return them to the Demon Subjugation Hall later."

Ning Zhuo paused, then continued, "The reason I was able to obtain the Nanming Fire Furnace is simply because I saw through the deep thoughts of the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit. I admit that possessing such a spirit communication art gives me an advantage that others find hard to match."

"But this advantage isn't enough to give me the confidence to set up such a scheme. Peak Master Wang Yu thinks too highly of me."

Zhongli Mei quietly listened to Ning Zhuo's explanation, but his gaze revealed a certain implication - since you won, and you are the only person who successfully pacified the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit and communicated with it, then whatever you say is right!

Ning Zhuo then praised Wang Yu, saying he had piercing insight and might have seen Ning Zhuo's unique advantage even earlier than Ning Zhuo himself did, which was why he visited late at night to recruit him personally.

Zhongli Mei said, "In fact, all the precious materials you borrowed from the Commerce Hall were secretly authorized by our peak."

Ning Zhuo nodded: "I had already guessed as much. Thank you for Danxia Peak's high regard. Lord Zhong, let us settle the accounts between you and me."

Zhongli Mei sighed again. Facing the impervious Ning Zhuo, he felt helpless.

Ning Zhuo showed a troubled expression: "The rest of the precious materials are easy to appraise. But the hundred years of Pill Qi that you, Senior Zhong, consumed - how should that be converted?"

The corner of Zhongli Mei's eye twitched.

In order to fight for the Nanming Fire Furnace, he had directly extracted a full three hundred years of his accumulated Pill Qi and poured it all into the furnace.

Nourishing the furnace's artifact spirit with such abundant Pill Qi wouldn't show immediate results, but in the long run, the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit would benefit from it, becoming more agile and clear-minded.

The main reason Zhongli Mei was so selfless and actively made such a sacrifice was that his lifespan was nearing its end.

As an Elder of Danxia Peak, he naturally did not want to see the Nanming Fire Furnace fall into the hands of an outsider.

Therefore, he had placed such a heavy bet.

However, despite his utmost efforts to retain it, it was ultimately an outsider like Ning Zhuo, who wasn't from Danxia Peak, that profited.

At this moment, Zhongli Mei couldn't help but recall the divine sense message Wang Yu had sent him before he came here.

Wang Yu had told Zhongli Mei directly: Do not underestimate Ning Zhuo. Although he is young, his methods are experienced, and he is well-versed in the rules of the Righteous Path.

Given Ning Zhuo's standing, the possibility of him accepting the recruitment is very slim, almost impossible.

As for the proposal of Danxia Peak helping Ning Zhuo completely repair the Nanming Fire Furnace, there is even less need to mention it.

Wang Yu had finally told Zhongli Mei the main goal of this trip - it wasn't to recruit Ning Zhuo, but to lower the possibility of Ning Zhuo joining the Demon Subjugation Hall.

The more Wang Yu understood Ning Zhuo, the more optimistic he was about the boy's future. He didn't want to see such a young junior join Zhong Dao's side, making the Demon Subjugation Hall even more powerful!

Zhongli Mei gathered the distracting thoughts within his divine sea and said to Ning Zhuo, "Let's put the Pill Qi aside for now. I have another deal - if little friend swears an oath never to join the Demon Subjugation Hall in the future, then half of the debt little friend owes our peak will be immediately forgiven!"

Ning Zhuo blanked slightly.

In the final round of the minor trial, Zhongli Mei had consumed the most precious materials of anyone. He had even earned five chirps!

For Ning Zhuo to pay off this debt in a short period of time was basically a fantasy.

And in reality, the debt Ning Zhuo owed Danxia Peak also had to include the sponsorship he received from the Commerce Hall. For example, the Nine Heavens Profound Iron, Purple Prefecture Spirit Liquid, Flowing Cloud Sand, Great White Golden Essence, Celestial Crystal, Abundant Earth, and so on.

The refinement furnace and Fire Seed he brought out were also rented from the Commerce Hall.

Aside from these, there were debts from others as well.

Such as Jiang Ping, Shen Hongyao, Qin Ke, and the red-robed guest from the final round.

The debts Ning Zhuo owed these people were ultimately owed to Danxia Peak, the actual creditor behind them.

Therefore, the condition Zhongli Mei offered to forgive half of the debt was truly extremely generous!

From this, one could see how much effort and what price Danxia Peak had paid in their attempt to retain the Nanming Fire Furnace.

Seeing Ning Zhuo pondering in silence, Zhongli Mei also expressed that if Ning Zhuo was unsatisfied with the offer of halving the debt, the specific ratio could be discussed in detail.

Ning Zhuo shook his head, then nodded: "I accept."

This time, it was Zhongli Mei's turn to be stunned. He couldn't quite believe his ears and couldn't help asking again: "Little friend Ning Zhuo, what did you just say?"

Ning Zhuo reached out a hand: "We can establish the contract right now."

A moment later, Zhongli Mei looked at the signed contract in his hand with a rather complex and indescribable expression.

The contract explicitly stipulated: If Ning Zhuo activated this condition to waive half of the debt owed to Danxia Peak, then he could not join the Demon Subjugation Hall.

"Although the initiative remains in Ning Zhuo's hands, just this contract alone is enough to create a rift between Zhong Dao and Ning Zhuo."

Zhongli Mei felt quite satisfied, thinking to himself: "And this is enough for me to report back to Wang Yu."

"Danxia Peak went all out, wanting to retain the Nanming Fire Furnace; there's nothing wrong with that. But... who asked Ning Zhuo to possess such an outstanding spirit communication art?"

Zhongli Mei departed full of emotion.

If calculated by contribution, the second place would undoubtedly belong to the Nine-Fire Dragon Lord. But Ning Zhuo was deeply aware of the status of Demon Cultivators, so the second cultivator he chose to settle accounts with was - Chunyangzi.

"Senior Chunyangzi, please take a seat so we can discuss in detail." Ning Zhuo stood up and bowed with cupped hands.

Chunyangzi naturally wouldn't stand on ceremony with him. After sitting straight down, he saw Ning Zhuo directly take out a Jade Slip and hand it to him.

Chunyangzi took it, scanned it with his divine sense, and found a list detailing the precious materials he had successively consumed in the matter of repairing the Nanming Fire Furnace.

The first two items were the Pure Yang Golden Core and the Pure Yang True Fire Pearl. After that was a long list of precious materials, including no shortage of spirit treasures.

Chunyangzi's expression grew somewhat heavy.

This wasn't just his personal wealth, but also the wealth of the Pure Yang Palace. After this incident, the foundation of the Pure Yang Palace was greatly damaged, essentially suffering a crippling blow.

Chunyangzi put down the Jade Slip, looked at Ning Zhuo, and praised him for his outstanding methods.

Ning Zhuo replied, "Senior Chunyangzi praises me too much. This junior merely won by a fluke and does not deserve such compliments."

Chunyangzi: "Little friend Ning Zhuo, I'll be blunt. I still want to purchase the Nanming Fire Furnace. Name your price."

Ning Zhuo shook his head: "Senior, I have already publicly stated my stance on this matter. This is my family's tradition. No reward without merit; no wealth without destiny."

Chunyangzi's brow twitched slightly: "Family tradition? Little friend, I am very curious - which family faction is exactly behind you?"

Ning Zhuo chuckled lightly: "Senior, Peak Master Wang Yu personally asked this question late last night as well. This junior did not answer. When it is time for this question to be revealed, it will naturally be revealed."

Chunyangzi looked at Ning Zhuo, and a strange feeling welled up in the depths of his heart.

The young man sitting opposite him was only in the Mid Foundation Establishment Stage, yet facing a Nascent Soul Great Cultivator like him, he showed not the slightest bit of cowardice.

Ning Zhuo was neither haughty nor humble, unhurried and unflustered. This wasn't feigned calmness, nor was it forced composure; instead, it felt like... a habit.

It was as if Ning Zhuo was used to seeing Nascent Soul cultivators, as if he had casually chatted and laughed with Soul Formation cultivators before.

This boy's background is likely extremely profound. It's probably a super-faction, and he's a favored genius within that faction, Chunyangzi thought to himself. His gaze deepened, and Ning Zhuo's weight in his mind continuously increased.

Little did he know that Ning Zhuo, since childhood, had been scheming against a Nascent Soul-level Immortal City Lord. Recently, he had even defeated a Soul Formation-level Monarch.

Naturally, he was completely unfazed by a Nascent Soul Great Cultivator like him.

Chunyangzi calculated carefully, approved the debt list Ning Zhuo submitted, and was about to determine a deadline for repaying the debt.

Ning Zhuo shook his head, directly refusing: "Senior Chunyangzi, clarifying this debt to you is already the limit of what this junior can do at present."

"It is impossible for this junior to set a fixed deadline. Because this junior also does not know when I'll be able to pay off this debt."

Chunyangzi couldn't help but be left speechless.

When this junior was being shameless, he was actually quite candid!
