Chapter 864: A Hair's Breadth Between Life and Death!

The Nascent Soul Qin De blurted out the words Saint's Great Thief Sutra, but Ning Zhuo had already missed it.

Qing Chi was overjoyed. "We need to refine an ice and snow talisman to counter the opponent. Young Master, please guide me at all times. If necessary, you can control my physical body. Together, we will cooperate to create the talisman."

On the other side, Sun Lingtong also used the Life Hanging by a Thread to transmit his thoughts via divine sense, privately messaging Ning Zhuo: "Little Zhuo, this was my idea. Hurry up and lend a hand!"

He still didn't know that Ning Zhuo had just recently been in an almost desperate situation.

Despite being connected to each other through the Life Hanging by a Thread Divine Ability, Ning Zhuo had not informed Sun Lingtong of this major crisis.

This was because Ning Zhuo could not gauge where this peril originated or its magnitude. What if it was an existence that even he and Sun Lingtong combining their strength couldn't resist? Wouldn't asking Sun Lingtong for help just be harming him?

"Boss and Qing Chi rarely show their faces. If something happens to me, it won't be easy for the two of them to be implicated."

"Even if I unfortunately perish, they will be able to avenge me!"

On one hand, Ning Zhuo was considering the safety of Sun Lingtong and Qing Chi; on the other hand, it was a precaution to preserve the possibility of future revenge.

On the Talisman Battle Platform, beads of sweat had already seeped out of Qing Chi's forehead.

On the desk in front of her lay a half-finished piece of talisman paper.

Ning Zhuo's divine sense looked through the Life Hanging by a Thread and saw Qing Chi drawing the talisman: "This talisman paper uses century-old cold bamboo as its base and ice silkworm silk as its tendons, coated with a thin layer of cold pool quicksilver on the surface. The talisman water is cold pool water mixed with some Yin attributes... What Qing Chi is making should be the [Profound Ice Fiend-Breaking Talisman], which is extremely effective at countering baleful energy and the like."

Ning Zhuo possessed many principles of artifact refinement, and his artifact refinement realm had reached the Renowned Artisan level. His attainment in talismans was far inferior to his artifact refinement and formation arrangement, but his advantage lay in his extremely solid foundation. Therefore, with just a glance, he could see the underlying details.

"This [Profound Ice Fiend-Breaking Talisman] consists of seventy-six strokes in total. It must be completed in one breath without interruption."

Qing Chi had already drawn more than half of it, leaving only the final dozen strokes or so, but she was finding it hard to continue.

Ning Zhuo immediately understood: "These last dozen strokes are for drawing the talisman core. A slight deviation in the stroke path will waste all previous efforts. A slight lack of force will drastically reduce the talisman's power. If the spiritual energy fluctuates even slightly, the talisman paper might spontaneously combust and trigger a backlash on the talisman maker."

"Although Qing Chi has been bitterly practicing talisman making recently and making rapid progress, her skill level is ultimately insufficient to complete this final part."

At the same time, Sun Lingtong was explaining the current situation to Ning Zhuo.

This Rising Cloud Minor Trial had reached the final deciding moment. The opponent played tricks while making their talisman, disguising it in the early stages, only to suddenly change their general direction mid-way to craft a [Fire Fiend Dragon Claw Talisman].

Sun Lingtong and Qing Chi were deceived in the early stages. The talisman they had planned to make was not a match for the [Fire Fiend Dragon Claw Talisman] at all. They were forced to change direction and chose the [Profound Ice Fiend-Breaking Talisman], which they had little confidence in making, hence why they had been at a disadvantage all along.

On the opponent's talisman platform, the assistant was making talismans and launching an endless barrage of attacks, which Sun Lingtong parried one by one, leaving him with no spare energy. Qing Chi was responsible for creating the decisive talisman, but her crafting speed was getting slower and slower. Seeing defeat imminent, the two could only seek outside help.

"Relax, don't resist. I will help you." Ning Zhuo immediately activated the Life Hanging by a Thread to seize control of Qing Chi's limbs and bones.

Ning Zhuo's divine sense transmission drifted into Qing Chi's divine sea, making her feel as if bathed in a spring breeze. Her anxiety dissipated significantly at once.

While controlling Qing Chi's arm and fingers, Ning Zhuo instructed her: "Watch, start the stroke here at the Xun position."

Qing Chi's wrist turned lightly, and the brush tip landed on the lower left of the talisman paper. As the stroke fell, the talisman paper trembled slightly. A pale blue runic light spread along the tip of the brush, winding and flowing across the paper like a stream.

"Now shift to the Zhen position. A horizontal stroke, reduce the force by three parts."

Qing Chi's wrist turned lightly, and the brush tip swept horizontally. The brush was as light as a feather, yet as steady as a boulder, and as fast as a shooting star. The runic light on the talisman paper steadily extended forward like a tamed spirit snake.

-----

Within the Yi Forest, Shao Qiannong couldn't help but frown slightly.

Fluctuations had appeared in the divination.

The hexagram images were changing, and the Yao positions were shifting. It was as if he was netting a fish - the germinating seed of tribulation destiny was like a large fish struggling violently.

Moreover, the force of this struggle was growing stronger and stronger!

"This boy's destiny is rising rapidly; he has external assistance."

The destiny of this external aid was fierce and cunning, like a snake or a fox, darting left and right. It caused Shao Qiannong's divination to be temporarily hindered, slowing down its progress.

"However, this has already exposed a flaw. Being so cunning and volatile is clearly the style of the Demonic Path."

Shao Qiannong slowly opened his eyes and looked at Dong Chen, indicating that to continue following the vine to get the melon, he needed the support of the Myriad Manifestations Sect's destiny.

Cultivators skilled in divination all cherished their own destiny. Right now, to continue deducing the pawn of tribulation destiny, it was inevitable to enter a war of attrition with the Demonic Path's destiny. This was something Shao Qiannong did not want to see.

This matter was the Myriad Manifestations Sect's business; he was merely a guest elder. Naturally, the cost of destiny needed to be borne by the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

Dong Chen did not hesitate. He immediately nodded and asked how he should cooperate.

Shao Qiannong smiled faintly: "You are the current Sect Master of the Myriad Manifestations Sect. Now that you are within the Yi Forest, you simply need to sit quietly and not move."

Dong Chen immediately agreed.

Shao Qiannong closed his eyes once again and formed hand seals with both hands.

Qian is three unbroken lines; Kun is six broken lines; Zhen is an upward-facing bowl; Gen is a downward-facing bowl...

The wind began to blow.

This wind was no ordinary wind; it was a Hexagram Wind.

It originated from beneath the old locust tree in the center of the Yi Forest and spread in all directions.

The Heavenly Dipper Pine, the tree of the Qian position, had its canopy tremble slightly, emitting a low rumble. It was like the roar of thunder, deep and distant.

The Earth Mother Locust, the tree of the Kun position, had its canopy hanging low like a lid and roots coiling like a dragon. It emitted a muffled drumming sound, heavy and steady.

The nodes of the Startling Thunder Bamboo at the Zhen position exploded with sound. Section by section, from bottom to top, lightning leaped among the bamboo like countless silver snakes, illuminating the entire Yi Forest.

The Wind-Dancing Willow of the Xun position, the Profound Water Maple of the Kan position, the Li Fire Paulownia of the Li position, the Gen Mountain Cypress of the Gen position, the Dui Marsh Elm of the Dui position...

The Hexagram Wind flowed through the branches and leaves, the Yao positions changed within the roots, and heavenly secrets permeated the air. The entire Yi Forest was breathing, trembling, and divining!

-----

Xiao Juxia's expression changed drastically.

He could barely hold onto the Rope Dragon Fate-Burning Lantern. The flame inside the lantern flickered wildly as if placed in a gale. The entire lantern body was scalding hot, making his palms sizzle. In the blink of an eye, the flesh on his hands had been burned to a crisp.

He hastily bit the tip of his tongue and spat a mouthful of vital essence blood onto the lantern. He then frantically poured his spiritual energy into it regardless of the cost, barely managing to maintain it.

But the power coming from the Yi Forest was too tremendous. It crushed down on him - endless like the tide, and unshakeable like a towering mountain.

This caused Xiao Juxia's face to grow paler and his aura to grow weaker.

-----

The Ban Clan Estate.

The secret chamber of the ancestral shrine.

The Clan Destiny Pivot Chain jolted violently.

The sliding blocks slid up and down at a speed almost invisible to the naked eye. The chains pulled left and right, pulled tautly straight, emitting a piercing screech of friction. The cogwheels rotated back and forth, spinning increasingly fast until they formed afterimages.

The entire mechanism was trembling, roaring, and howling.

The expressions of several of the Ban Clan's supreme clan elders immediately changed drastically. Subconsciously, they exerted their full strength to maintain the Clan Destiny Pivot Chain.

Suddenly, someone exclaimed in a low voice: "Look, Ning Zhuo's Demonic Path star destiny has erupted!"

The Demonic Path Destiny Star had already been shining brilliantly, but at this moment, it radiated a dark red brilliance. The light shone into the withered yellow demonic fog, echoing and blending with it. At times it transformed into a sea of blood, at times it churned with demonic shadows, and at times it revealed the might of fierce malevolence.

Xiao Juxia, the Ban Clan, and Ning Zhuo joined forces to resist Shao Qiannong.

Sensing the surging, turbulent force of resistance, Shao Qiannong couldn't help but chuckle.

He truly mobilized a small half of the Yi Forest.

Witness: The light of the Qian Hexagram was like a blazing sun in the sky, shooting myriad rays of golden light. The light of the Kun Hexagram was as heavy as the earth, a deep yellowish-brown. The light of the Zhen Hexagram carried the force of a thunderbolt, flashing with purple light. The light of the Xun Hexagram was like a long wind riding the waves, surging with vast azure light...

The eight-colored lights intertwined, transforming into a Bagua light diagram.

Carrying peerless divine might, the light diagram allowed Shao Qiannong's divination to press forward indomitably, crushing everything in its path!

Xiao Juxia's Rope Dragon Fate-Burning Lantern trembled violently. The flame on the wick flickered erratically. Crack, crack... Fine cracks appeared on the lantern and rapidly spread across its entire body.

The Ban Clan's Clan Destiny Pivot Chain operated frantically. Suddenly, a sliding block derailed and flew out, smashing into the wall and creating a deep crater. Then, several chains snapped and scattered, the fragments dancing in the air like countless tiny blades.

More cogwheels exploded into fragments that shot out in all directions, riddling the secret chamber's walls with holes.

"How could this happen?"

"What in the world is going on?!"

The several supreme clan elders were dumbfounded, trying with all their might to salvage and maintain the Clan Destiny Pivot Chain.

Ning Zhuo's demonic star was faring no better. It was dull and lusterless, on the verge of being extinguished.

Shao Qiannong was a Soul Formation-level cultivator. Once he truly got serious, and was consuming the destiny of the Myriad Manifestations Sect itself, he immediately presented an overwhelmingly crushing momentum.

Even the joint efforts of the three parties struggled to withstand his sharp edge.

And once they were defeated, Shao Qiannong would undoubtedly meet no resistance and accurately deduce Ning Zhuo's location and identity.

When that happened, one could easily imagine Ning Zhuo's fate - it would clearly be certain death!

Yet Ning Zhuo remained completely unaware of this, totally oblivious in his ignorance.

While cremating the Confucian Demonic Nascent Soul, he was simultaneously secretly assisting Qing Chi in drawing the talisman.

Having reached the final stroke, Ning Zhuo said, "An upward vertical stroke at the Dui position, ride the momentum to lift the brush, finish it all in one breath."

Qing Chi abruptly lifted her wrist. The tip of the brush swept from bottom to top in an upward vertical stroke, swift and accurate.

The runic lines on the talisman paper abruptly burst with light. A cold blue glow seeped out of the talisman like a chill moon piercing through the clouds, brightly illuminating the entire Talisman Battle Platform.

This commotion, however, drew out a loud laugh from the opposing talisman platform: "Ultimately, I was still one step faster. Come, please appraise my [Fire Fiend Dragon Claw Talisman], fellow Daoist across the way."

The opponent activated the talisman, immediately unleashing the sound of a dragon's roar. Soon after, a burst of flame rapidly expanded, flying higher and higher.

The flame reached the space directly above Qing Chi and Sun Lingtong's heads, condensing into a dragon claw. It was the size of a millstone and quite solid; its dragon scales and digits were all incredibly lifelike.

"Quick!" Sun Lingtong used all his strength to throw out the talismans in his hands.

Unfortunately, the talismans he had hastily crafted had limited power. The moment they collided with the Fire Fiend Dragon Claw, they dissipated into smoke one after another, achieving absolutely no effect.

Instead, it allowed the opposing assistant to find an opening and launch a series of fierce attacks, almost breaking through the talisman defense line of their Talisman Battle Platform.

"Crap, there's no time." Even Ning Zhuo was out of options.

Although the talisman crafting was successful, it still needed to be refined before it could be utilized.

Qing Chi's talisman was personally made by her own hands, so the refining speed would be extremely swift. But the problem was that no matter how fast she was, it still required time.

And what they lacked right now was exactly that tiny bit of time!

"Are we going to fail?"

"No, Young Master went through untold hardships to help me. If I fail, wouldn't I be disappointing him?!"

"No, absolutely not!"

Qing Chi screamed in her heart. In her anxiety, she subconsciously summoned a burst of azure flame.

The flame surged out from her palm and sprayed onto the [Profound Ice Fiend-Breaking Talisman], unexpectedly refining this talisman in an instant.

The talisman activated, transforming into a cold beam of blue light that struck the Fire Fiend Dragon Claw in mid-air.

The Fire Fiend Dragon Claw abruptly stopped in mid-air. Immediately after, layers of frost surfaced upon it.

The dragon claw was naturally unwilling to accept defeat just like that, so it also spewed out all sorts of baleful energy and flames. But this was merely a futile struggle.

The baleful energy was weakened and the flames suppressed. Over ten breaths later, the dragon claw completely vanished without a trace, not leaving a single spark behind.

"In this minor trial, the winner has been decided..." the cultivator hosting the minor trial publicly announced.

"We won, we won! Is this the innate talent the Young Master mentioned that originally belonged to me - the Azure Flame?" Qing Chi's face was filled with a look of pleasant surprise.

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly, also quite pleasantly surprised: "I don't care much for the rewards of this Rising Cloud Minor Trial. Qing Chi being able to once again use her own innate talent is the greatest gain!"

-----

Xiao Juxia let out a bleugh, vomiting up fragments of his internal organs while blood spurted from all seven of his facial orifices.

The Rope Dragon Fate-Burning Lantern in his hand burst with a bang, shattering the lantern body. Fragments scattered in all directions, cutting Xiao Juxia's body in many places.

The flame went out, and the wick's yellow rope snapped and turned to ash. The lantern oil was completely exhausted. This heavy treasure had been almost entirely destroyed.

Xiao Juxia knelt on the ground with one knee. His face was pale as a corpse, and his aura was so weak it was almost undetectable. For a time, he couldn't even manage to crawl up.

In the Ban Clan's secret chamber, the Clan Destiny Pivot Chain also entirely collapsed.

Countless mechanism fragments smashed around, leaving the secret chamber in total disarray.

Several of the Ban Clan's supreme clan elders were in a wretched state. Some had even passed out on the spot. But others managed to catch the final scene of Ning Zhuo's destiny manifestation.

Just as Ning Zhuo's cloud canopy was about to completely seal shut without a single gap left, an azure flame suddenly ignited, refining the shining demonic star away on the spot.

Ning Zhuo's destiny arm suddenly rose up. Splaying its five fingers, it abruptly grasped the page of the book above.

At once, the page shattered between the fingers, and the ink rain evaporated powerlessly within the palm.

The coffin-sealing of the cloud-white sarcophagus was ultimately incomplete, leaving a sliver of a gap behind.

Immediately after, the gap suddenly widened, and the entire cloud-white sarcophagus dispersed with a resounding boom!

-----

Bluestone Cave Dwelling.

Ning Zhuo withdrew the majority of his mental focus from Qing Chi's side, arriving just in time to witness the final scene of Qin De's Nascent Soul completely melting away.

At this very moment, the Demonic and Confucian dual-aspect Nascent Soul had entirely vanished, leaving behind only a spiritual orb of light!

-----

The central clearing of the Yi Forest.

Shao Qiannong had no choice but to terminate the divination. He opened his eyes and let out a faint, quiet sigh.

Missing only by a hair's breadth, he ultimately fell short of success at the final stage.

If Ning Zhuo had heard the words Saint's Great Thief Sutra earlier, hesitated even a bit, and delayed for just a moment, he would have been accurately divined.

A miss by a fraction of a hair leads to an error of a thousand miles.

A hair's breadth of difference was the distinction between life and death!
