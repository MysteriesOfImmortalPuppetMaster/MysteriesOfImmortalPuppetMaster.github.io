Chapter 866: Talisman Network Seed Scripture

Ning Zhuo: ?

It was his first time hearing about Talisman Network Seeds.

Sun Lingtong chuckled and reminded him, "This jade box is a specially made Jade Slip that contains a cultivation method. Young Master, you'll know once you take a look."

Ning Zhuo probed inside with his divine sense and indeed found a scripture within the jade box, titled Talisman Network Seed Scripture.

Ning Zhuo quickly skimmed through it, his eyes lighting up as he praised, "Brilliant, this is truly interesting!"

These Talisman Network Seeds were actually real seeds, the kind that could be planted.

They typically looked like lotus seeds, about the size of a pigeon egg, and were entirely brown. Their surfaces were covered in fine, dense patterns, like the remnants of Runes - faintly discernible yet blurry.

To plant this kind of Talisman Network Seed, one had to use a complete talisman. The talisman would be used to wrap the seed, forming a layer of embryonic membrane, which was then buried in the soil.

Afterward, the Cultivator needed to water it, fertilize it, infuse it with spiritual energy, and expend their divine sense, eventually causing the seed to take root, sprout, grow leaves, bloom, and even bear fruit.

Roots, leaves, petals, fruits... Each stage reached yielded a different outcome. Exactly how far it could grow depended on the Cultivator's own attainment in spiritual planting.

When the seed cracked open, it would extend roots. These roots were often as fine as hair, white as jade, and would slowly spread through the soil.

Every fork in the roots corresponded to a turning point in the talisman. The length of the roots corresponded to the length of the talisman's lines. The thickness of the roots corresponded to the weight of the brushstrokes. The density of the roots corresponded to the strength of the talisman's power.

As long as complete roots were grown, the Cultivator would be able to clearly see the complete creation steps of the corresponding talisman!

By tracing the roots and following their patterns with their divine sense, the planter could observe all the various details of the talisman master's creation process. The higher their attainment in spiritual planting, the clearer the meridians would be, and the more details they could read.

If the cultivating planter could make the seed sprout and unfurl leaves, then these leaves - as long as they weren't incomplete - would be replicas of the planted talisman. By activating the leaf with spiritual energy, the Cultivator could trigger the talisman's effects.

The attainment in spiritual planting determined the quantity and quality of the leaves. The higher the quality, the higher the grade of the talisman leaves, and the closer their power and effects would be to the original talisman.

Taking it a step further would be blooming petals.

The petals were also talismans, but their grade and quality often surpassed the original planted talisman itself!

Sometimes, mutated versions could even be grown, which signified a certain direction for improvement.

Ultimately, if it could bear fruit, that fruit would contain the information, creation steps, and so on of the talisman. By swallowing the fruit, a Cultivator could directly acquire the relevant information and experience, saving a vast amount of hard practice.

As for the Talisman Network Seed Scripture contained in the jade box, it taught Cultivators how to plant these Talisman Network Seeds.

From beginning to end, it consisted of the General Introduction, Seed Selection Chapter, Soil Cultivation Chapter, Irrigation Chapter, Fertilization Chapter, Sprouting Chapter, Meridian Observation Chapter, Leaf Harvesting Chapter, Flower Appreciation Chapter, Fruit Consumption Chapter, and finally, the Miscellaneous Chapter.

Ning Zhuo wasn't surprised in the least that such a convenient method for learning talisman crafting existed.

"With my Innate Talent and the use of the Boat-Burning Soul-Crossing Art to absorb the memories of demonic souls, the effects I can achieve are much stronger and more comprehensive than the Talisman Network Seed Scripture."

"I am simply lucky to have unexpectedly gathered both and can use them in tandem. In this golden age of the Cultivation World, there are definitely many similar methods."

"Besides the Talisman Network Seed Scripture, many Confucian techniques can also boost learning speed and learning effectiveness."

"Not to mention anything else, the Unity of Knowledge and Action Technique is something I greatly desire but cannot obtain."

Ning Zhuo's interest in the Talisman Network Seed Scripture wasn't actually that great. If he wanted talisman crafting experience, he could simply purchase and absorb the souls of talisman cultivators.

However, he did admit that the Talisman Network Seed Scripture was an effective supplement to his studies in talisman crafting.

"Furthermore, the learning combination of my Innate Talent and the Boat-Burning Soul-Crossing Art isn't applicable to others. The Talisman Network Seed Scripture, on the other hand, can bless others, allowing my entire faction to benefit."

"This is very good."

Finally, Ning Zhuo quickly scanned the entire Talisman Network Seed Scripture again, his gaze pausing on the General Introduction at the very beginning.

It read: "Talismans are the insignias of Heaven and Earth. In their creation, every stroke and line follows a set method; in their application, every talisman contains profound mysteries. However, the difficulty of the Talisman Dao lies in observing its methods. Beginners only see the completed form, not the method of its creation; they only see its application, not the reason behind its use. Thus, even after studying talismans for ten years, seven or eight out of ten still cannot enter its doors.

"Observing the principles of Heaven and Earth's flourishing and the Dao of the growth of vegetation, I comprehended a method: using the talisman as a womb, the seed as the core, the soil as the medium, the water as the guide, spiritual energy as the sun, and divine sense as the moon. Plant it, and the method of the talisman's creation will be fully revealed among the roots; observe it, and the profound mysteries of the talisman will be fully displayed within its meridians.

"This method is named: Talisman Network Seed. 'Talisman' stands for talismans; 'Network' stands for meridians and veins; 'Seed' stands for seeds. Using a talisman as a seed and meridians as roots, planting it gives birth to the talisman network, and observing it illuminates the Talisman Dao."

...

Ning Zhuo secretly praised in his heart, "The person who created this method definitely had profound attainments in both talisman crafting and spiritual planting! Out of these two Cultivation arts, at least one of them reached the Grandmaster-level."

The Master-level realm means breaking free from mere artisanry, possessing a kind of intuitive connection.

Only at the Grandmaster-level can one establish a sect, create entirely new content, and often leave their name in the annals of history.

Without a doubt, the Talisman Network Seed Scripture was a brand-new creation!

Seeing Ning Zhuo captivated, Sun Lingtong sent a divine sense transmission, communicating secretly, "Little Zhuo, didn't I tell you this reward was incredibly unique and interesting?"

"If I hadn't suggested we participate in that trial, how could we have gotten our hands on such good stuff?"

Ning Zhuo smiled at him and sent a divine sense transmission back in secret, "Yes, yes, yes, Boss. Your eye for these things is naturally top-notch."

Sun Lingtong rolled his eyes thoughtfully, "If these Talisman Network Seeds are planted well, they could form an entire field, mass-producing large quantities of talismans."

"However, the organization behind this only imparts an incomplete Talisman Network Seed Scripture, keeping the actual creation method of the Talisman Network Seeds tightly guarded in their own hands."

"If I could steal it out..."

Hearing Sun Lingtong say this, Ning Zhuo's scalp immediately tightened. He quickly advised in secret, "Stop right there. Boss, now is not a good time."

"There's no need for us to get this creation method."

"This method should have very high requirements for a Cultivator's planting skills."

"The purpose of the faction organizing this Rising Cloud Minor Trial is also very clear. First, to sell Talisman Network Seeds to the outside world. Second, to attract outstanding newcomers to join them, nurturing them into excellent spiritual planters or talisman masters."

"Even if we stole this method, we would still need an excellent spiritual planter to make use of it."

Sun Lingtong smacked his lips, "That's true..."

Ning Zhuo revealed a look of anticipation, "I'm also quite interested in this art of spiritual planting. If I have time in the future, I will definitely study it properly."

Sun Lingtong, however, suddenly thought of another layer, "Oh right, Little Zhuo. Didn't your mother leave you a talisman crafting workshop? That's your property. When do you plan to take it back?"

Ning Zhuo chuckled lightly and replied in secret, "No rush, no rush."

He currently lacked no resources for cultivation.

Ning Zhuo looked at Qing Chi.

"Qing Chi, you did well this time," Ning Zhuo said with a smile, encouraging her. "Not just with the Talisman Network Seeds and the scripture. The most important thing is - you have regained control of your Innate Talent. I must congratulate you!"

Qing Chi hurriedly said, "I, I... All of this is thanks to your help, Young Master."

Ning Zhuo gently shook his head, "It's primarily because of you. You did very well."

Qing Chi's eyes sparkled as she stared at Ning Zhuo. Receiving praise from the big-headed youth made her feel extremely thrilled.

Ning Zhuo took out a precious seal and handed it to her.

It was none other than the Lord of Forgetful River Seal.

"Try using the Azure Flame to refine it," Ning Zhuo assigned her a new task.

Qing Chi took the seal, "Understood, Young Master. Even if I have to risk my life, I will refine it."

Ning Zhuo shook his head slightly, "Just do your best, there's no need to force it."

"This seal is no small matter, and the difficulty of refining it is immense. I just felt we could give it a try. Whether it succeeds or not isn't important."

During the battle with the Forgetful River Prefecture Lord, the scene of the Azure Flame directly refining the Nether Dragon Scissors had left an incredibly deep impression on Ning Zhuo.

This made Ning Zhuo feel like it was worth a try, but in truth, he was also aware that the possibility was exceedingly slim.

Watching Qing Chi back away while respectfully holding the Lord of Forgetful River Seal, a subtle feeling arose in Ning Zhuo's heart.

"Previously, the Mechanism Ring warned me, guiding me to connect my Life Hanging by a Thread to Qing Chi. I followed that instruction."

"This time, while I was cremating the mysterious Nascent Soul, Qing Chi suddenly asked for help halfway through... I fear there is some other profound mystery hidden within this."

"There should be some mysterious connection between the two."

"Sigh... My only regret is that I haven't obtained any methods related to destiny and divination."

"With so many Rising Cloud Minor Trials in the Myriad Manifestations Sect, why isn't there a single one related to this art?"

Although Ning Zhuo hadn't heard Qin De's Nascent Soul cry out "Sage's Great Thief Scripture," he vaguely had some inkling of it.

Only Sun Lingtong and Ning Zhuo were left in the cultivation room.

With no outsiders present, Sun Lingtong sat directly on Ning Zhuo's futon.

Ning Zhuo had no choice but to take out another futon and sit down across from him.

Sun Lingtong said, "The Myriad Manifestations Sect has suddenly fallen into turmoil."

He brought up the latest intelligence.

"Something happened at the Cloud Prison. Demonic Cultivators broke out, and more than twenty of them escaped at once. Fortunately, Wu An and the others captured them in time, preventing any loose ends."

"An alchemy room exploded on Danxia Peak. The blast was massive, but the specific losses are unknown."

"A riot broke out in the spirit beast garden on Myriad Beast Peak. The spirit beasts went mad, killing many people and many beasts as well."

"Seven renowned swords flew out of the Hidden Sword Pavilion on Jianming Peak all at once. Now they're searching all over the world for them."

"And on Baicao Peak, a ten-thousand-year-old Vermilion Fruit tree failed its Tribulation Transcendence, and the entire tree is nearly destroyed. Two Vice Peak Lords went to save it personally, but word is that the situation isn't optimistic. The key is, the current Peak Lord didn't go."

"And now, Little Zhuo, a Nascent Soul suddenly appears to attack your door..."

"With so many things happening all at once, I'm afraid there's some strong connection between them. I get the feeling someone is intentionally stirring up trouble!"

Ning Zhuo furrowed his brows, a layer of fine sweat seeping out on his forehead.

Although he had no proof, he had a strong feeling in his heart and largely agreed with Sun Lingtong's words.

"Fortunately, the Mechanism Ring warned me, and I didn't hesitate."

"I raced against time and directly cremated the mysterious Nascent Soul. Otherwise, if the Myriad Manifestations Sect launched a full-scale investigation and implicated me, my fate probably wouldn't have been very good."

Sun Lingtong nodded, "Little Zhuo, you did the right thing! Even if they only locked you up and investigated you for a period of time, it would have made you miss many Rising Cloud Minor Trials. That loss alone would have been huge!"

Ning Zhuo thought for a moment, gritting his teeth, "To be safe, I need to hide that cluster of spiritual energy first."

"It won't be too late to take it out and study it once I know the inside story and determine there are no risks."

Sun Lingtong nodded, "Or you could wait until this Flying Cloud Assembly is over, after you've successfully joined the Myriad Manifestations Sect, or even wait until we return to the Ghostly Black Marshlands to slowly study it."

Neither of the brothers lacked this bit of patience.

Ban Clan Estate, Ancestral Shrine Secret Chamber.

Several Ban Clan Grand Elders moved like lightning, their faces etched with anxiety. Upon hearing the news, they rushed over and pushed the doors open.

The secret chamber was in total disarray.

Shattered copper littered the floor, broken chains lay strewn about, and a cracked gear wheel embedded in the wall was still trembling faintly.

Wisps of spiritual light seeped out from the fragments like dying fireflies, flickering on and off, struggling a few times before extinguishing entirely.

The air was thick with a scorched scent - the pungent and acrid smell of the Mechanism's internal Runes burning out after being overloaded. Innumerable fine copper shavings were scattered across the floor, glinting with a dim radiance under the surviving lamplight, resembling shattered stars.

Of the three Grand Elders who had been here previously, some were leaning against the walls, while others were slumped on the floor.

Their faces were cut with bloody streaks. One of them had a fragment of a gear wheel embedded deep into his shoulder, cutting down to the bone. Blood gushed out from between his fingers, dyeing half his body red.

Another's chest had been struck by a flying slider, caving in to form a fist-sized depression. With every breath, bloody froth bubbled from the corners of his mouth.

The third had his left leg entangled by a snapped chain. The links dug deep into his flesh, leaving his lower leg twisted and deformed, with sharp shards of white bone piercing through the skin.

None of the three moved.

Beyond their physical injuries, their minds had also suffered a devastating shock, the aftershocks of which still lingered.

The pupils of the several people who entered shrank violently, and they froze in the doorway.

After a long time, the Ban Clan's Head Grand Elder finally spoke, "How did it come to this?!"

One of the Grand Elders leaning against the corner spoke with a trembling voice, "It's just... we were only observing destiny... that's all!"

Towards the end, his voice carried a faint trace of a sob.

The Head Grand Elder exchanged glances with those beside him, all of them seeing the mutual bewilderment in each other's eyes - just observing destiny caused all this?!

The Grand Elder leaning against the corner continued, "Bizarre! That bastard Ning Zhuo is truly too bizarre..."

He meticulously recounted the entire process of their destiny observation this time.

He heavily emphasized Ning Zhuo's final manifestation of destiny.

"At the final juncture, a cluster of Azure Flame appeared out of nowhere and actually directly refined the Demonic Path destiny star."

"If I hadn't seen it with my own eyes, I absolutely would never have believed it!"

"That was an intact destiny star, after all."

"Who the hell knows what that Azure Flame is, and who the hell knows what else will pop out of Ning Zhuo's destiny arm!"

The Ban Clan's Head Grand Elder remained silent for a long time before finally speaking in a hoarse voice, "In that case, at the final juncture, Ning Zhuo escaped the death tribulation. He currently still has the Mechanism and Strange Path destiny stars remaining?"

"And our clan merely observed, yet it caused our Clan Fortunes Pivot Chain to completely collapse?!"

Even though he didn't doubt these few Grand Elders, at this moment, he still found it somewhat hard to accept.

This price was simply too great!
