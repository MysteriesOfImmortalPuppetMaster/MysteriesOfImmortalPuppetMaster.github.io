Chapter 955: The Five-Win Martial Contest Begins

Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor.

Ning Zhuo unfolded Chunyangzi's flying letter.

The rows of upright and vigorous handwriting naturally possessed the bearing of the Pure Yang Palace Master - majestic, yet without lacking meticulousness.

"Liu Jinke has newly surrendered, affecting the face of the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak. Since this person has already entered Nanming Stronghold, he is a person of our stronghold. You must take comprehensive care of him; do not let him be plotted against, coerced, or even incited to rebel again by others."

Ning Zhuo replied: "Rest assured, Chief Stronghold Lord. Since Liu Jinke has surrendered, I will naturally settle him according to stronghold rules. The various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak will definitely be dissatisfied, but the upcoming arranged battle surrounding the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar should draw their energy. Of course, if the opponent wants to cause trouble in the dark, Nanming Stronghold from top to bottom has never been afraid."

Chunyangzi's second flying letter had only a few words.

"Take care of yourself."

Ning Zhuo looked at these words, was slightly startled, and then smiled.

This sentence was not as majestic as Chunyangzi's usual style; it was just a clean and straightforward admonition. But exactly because of this, it instead felt somewhat sincere.

Ning Zhuo knew this was Chunyangzi's heartfelt concern.

The latter was worried that someone in Flowing Cloud Peak might not be able to let it go. After all, this time, Ning Zhuo had greatly damaged the face of the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak.

Ning Zhuo, of course, had also considered this aspect; he had estimated the extent of it.

"The various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak seem to advance and retreat together, but in reality, each has its own calculations. Now that Liu Jinke has surrendered, if someone takes the risk to plot against him or me, regardless of success or failure, they will have to face the risk of severe punishment under Sect rules. If they fail, they will face a counterattack from Nanming Stronghold. Others could then sit back and reap the benefits, or even take the opportunity to carve up their forces."

A faint smile appeared on Ning Zhuo's lips.

"In a pile of loose sand, who would be willing to jump out and take a risk themselves, letting others pick up the benefits?"

Ning Zhuo did not reply. He turned and walked into the Cultivation Chamber.

He released Snow-Colored Maiden Hui.

Her snow-porcelain faceplate remained coldly elegant, but it bore many cracks. Her cyan ice-silkworm hair cascading over her shoulders and back was already mostly missing.

The three-colored enamel armor was perfectly intact, but the rest of her parts all had scars and cracks.

Aside from this, the interior of Snow-Colored Maiden Hui's body also had much damage.

It was true that Snow-Colored Maiden Hui's main body was made of ice, but it actually still contained metal components. The suppression of the Gold-Swallowing Divine Domain on her was far less fierce than on the Profound Weapon Armor or the Empty-Heart Blade-Snapping Guards, but it ultimately still had an impact.

It was just that the quantity of metal was small, its positions were hidden deep, and its critical operations could be concealed by the Ice Jade Hands forming ice on the spot.

"Fortunately, this creation was single-handedly constructed by me. I could secretly form ice during the battle and use my divine sense to sculpt the ice, temporarily replacing the damaged parts."

Ning Zhuo set about starting the repairs.

A jade needle pierced into the cracks.

The ice crystals softened, and starlight-like glimmers gradually spread across the inner walls of the fissures.

Gongsun Yan, meanwhile, was in another room, having accepted Ning Zhuo's task to refine precious materials for him.

Snow-Colored Maiden Hui's six arms remained motionless, but her spiritual energy conveyed threads of eagerness.

She wanted to become stronger.

Very much so!

Previously, when she was at a disadvantage during her clashes with the Lightning Mother and Earth God, she had always been unconvinced. Today, while beating Liu Jinke soundly and shining brightly, the cracks on her body also reminded her: she was still not strong enough.

Through Life Hanging by a Thread, Ning Zhuo felt this mood and couldn't help but smile: "Rest assured, you established military merits this time. Whatever needs to be replenished, I will replenish it. Having personally experienced this battle, you have already gained more experience."

"Remember the feeling of me controlling your body to fight. Remember those critical choices, and how I used your body to engage in close-quarters combat."

Ning Zhuo instructed Snow-Colored Maiden Hui as he performed the repairs.

This repair work, for Ning Zhuo, was naturally something effortlessly achieved.

While repairing, he could also divide his attention to review his three battles with Liu Jinke.

In terms of mechanism arts, the problems were obvious.

The Empty Valley Echoing Sound Technique was usable, but it was difficult to grasp in specific control. The models of the mechanical birds were different; their sound holes, reeds, and bamboo bone responses all had variations. If one simply copied the inheritance verbatim, problems like being half a beat slow, half a beat fast, or accidental triggers would inevitably appear on the battlefield.

The Bamboo Orifice Hidden Sound Technique was another problem.

The pre-hidden sound commands seemed exquisite, but in reality, they relied heavily on combat experience. When to trigger them, how to connect them after triggering, and how to interrupt them if the enemy intentionally induced them - these were not things that could be perfected just by a few days of hard practice.

"I can ask Qing Huangzi for advice."

But to do this, it would inevitably consume time and energy.

What Ning Zhuo lacked was precisely these.

"In this battle, the mechanical bird swarm did well enough."

A large number of mechanical birds were destroyed, a massive loss, but they completed their mission.

Their opening probes had drawn out precious intelligence on Liu Jinke's quasi-Divine Art's endurance, the sound disruption of his Disordered String Sound-Splitting Fork, the suppression range of his Gold-Swallowing Divine Domain, and the protective characteristics of his Golden Mesh Garment, paving a perfect path for Snow-Colored Maiden Hui's entrance.

Without this attrition, there was no way Ning Zhuo could have made an accurate judgment and then let Snow-Colored Maiden Hui enter the fray so swiftly.

"The trump card of the Blood Ember Fire Seed was ultimately not used."

This was very normal.

In life, preparations do not always come into play.

A true battle was not about throwing out all your trump cards in order, but seeing which card was most suitable for the present moment.

Since Snow-Colored Maiden Hui's Ice Jade Hands were already enough to freeze Liu Jinke's outer layer of golden blood, there was no need to expose this trump card.

Because he had to wait for Gongsun Yan to finish processing the precious materials, Ning Zhuo temporarily concluded his repairs on Snow-Colored Maiden Hui.

Ning Zhuo sent out a flying letter to the Red-Robed Guest.

The content of the letter was very simple and straightforward: "Senior, the Blood Ember Fire Seed you lent was not used in this battle. Liu Jinke has surrendered, yet the situation remains unsettled. With the Five-Victory arranged battle for the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar imminent, the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak will surely have follow-up moves. This fire might soon have a place to be used, so I earnestly request Senior to extend the borrowing period."

Ning Zhuo had a certain level of confidence regarding this matter.

The Red-Robed Guest was a Demonic Cultivator. Though unruly in temperament, he was not the type to haggle over every detail. Lending out the Blood Ember Fire Seed was originally him returning a favor to Ning Zhuo.

Now that Ning Zhuo had won a great victory in three battles, Nanming Stronghold's momentum skyrocketed, and the Red-Robed Guest also benefited as a result.

Therefore, there was a high probability that the Red-Robed Guest would agree to the extension.

Liu Jinke was temporarily residing in the Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor.

He was very tactful. After surrendering and exiting the stage, he handed over all the treasures on his body to Snow-Colored Maiden Hui, serving as Ning Zhuo's spoils of war for this battle.

Regarding this procedure, he was already very familiar with it; after all, he had done it during their second battle.

At the same time, he also revealed a massive amount of intelligence related to Flowing Cloud Peak, which included exactly how he was funded by the various factions.

This intelligence made Ning Zhuo's political vision become much more detailed and clear.

After sending the flying letter, Ning Zhuo went to look for Liu Jinke.

Liu Jinke proactively saluted: "Young Master."

Ning Zhuo wore a smile and spoke to comfort him: "Senior Brother, there's no need to be so overly polite. We can be considered to have formed a bond through fighting."

Liu Jinke smiled bitterly: "Basically, it was me taking a beating."

He had also reviewed and recalled the events, discovering that in all three battles, he basically hadn't endangered Ning Zhuo's main body. Therefore, he recognized even more the gap between himself and Ning Zhuo.

After clearly understanding their respective strengths and weaknesses, he was more calm and even-tempered than ever before.

Ning Zhuo conversed with Liu Jinke for a few more sentences, doing his best to comfort the latter.

Ning Zhuo made his arrangements: "This is my cave manor; its defenses are not weak. As long as you are here, even if those people from Flowing Cloud Peak are unwilling to let it go, they won't dare to come capture you openly. For this recent period, just stay here to avoid the heat."

Liu Jinke's political acumen was not high, but he also knew that defecting on the battlefield must have infuriated the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak. He first nodded, then thought about it and asked, "How long do I need to hide?"

He was an itinerant cultivator and loved freedom.

Ning Zhuo replied: "If things go smoothly, I'll be able to let you out very soon."

Liu Jinke raised his head: "The Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar?"

Ning Zhuo smiled: "Correct."

"Once the Five-Victory agreement for the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar begins, the attention of the various factions of Flowing Cloud Peak will be diverted. Seniors like Chunyangzi, Tan Zhu, the Nine-Fire Dragon Lord, and the Red-Robed Guest will all truly enter the field. By the time we successfully seize the territory and you show your face again, the pressure you face will be far less than it is now."

Liu Jinke fell silent.

This arrangement was very safe and secure.

Right now, he was a thorn in the eyes of all the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak, but after the Five-Victory agreement concluded, the greatest pressure would be shared away by the various experts of Nanming Stronghold.

Liu Jinke approved of this arrangement. At their final parting, he warned Ning Zhuo: "You must be careful of the Three Xiang Valiants."

Naturally, Liu Jinke had passed on this intelligence to Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo nodded and smiled freely: "Why would I challenge three Golden Cores for no reason? I am merely at the mid Foundation Establishment Stage."

Liu Jinke blanked for a moment: "By defeating me in three battles, your combat power is already publicly acknowledged to be at the peak Golden Core level. It does not go against common sense for the world to place you on par with the Three Xiang Valiants."

Ning Zhuo smiled and shook his head: "Senior Brother, through our three battles, you should be clearer than anyone else. My victory over you did not rely on realm suppression, but on intelligence, preparation, mechanisms, formations, magic artifacts, on-the-spot judgment, as well as your own limitations. If I really had to fight one against three, facing the Three Xiang Valiants head-on, I would have no chance of winning."

These words were spoken very truthfully.

Liu Jinke was speechless.

Ning Zhuo: "Rest well, Senior Brother. I will take my leave."

Liu Jinke immediately stood up to see his guest off.

Looking at Ning Zhuo's departing back, Liu Jinke revealed a bizarre expression, thinking to himself: "You say you have no chance of winning? Why do I find that hard to believe! Heh, if I really believed that, it would mean I haven't learned my biggest lesson from these three battles."

"The Three Xiang Valiants..." Ning Zhuo had long ago asked Sun Lingtong to purchase intelligence on them.

In his heart, he attached far more importance to these three cultivators than he showed on the outside.

"Although I won't go out of my way to do a thankless task like fighting one against three, there are too many excuses on the Righteous Path. It's hard to guarantee that I won't be forced by the circumstances to make a move and agree to the martial demonstration."

"I still need to continue getting stronger; only then is it possible to resolve all troubles."

Ning Zhuo was clear: since the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak had spent heavy sums of money, crowdfunding to hire the Three Xiang Valiants, they definitely had the means to make Ning Zhuo accept the battle.

Therefore, his most likely upcoming enemies were these three people.

"Liu Jinke was my fortuitous encounter; the limitations of the Golden Liquid Reverting Core Body restricted Flowing Cloud Peak's investment. After three battles, they lost quite a lot."

"But the divine sense of the Three Xiang Valiants is completely normal, so it is not a chessboard of my choosing."

"However, this is not an immediate trouble for me. Next, I should be able to get a moment of peace."

He had already completely broken through Liu Jinke's line. In the upcoming struggle for the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar, it could no longer be just him, a mid Foundation Establishment Stage cultivator, charging at the very front.

Nanming Stronghold had five Nascent Souls.

Especially Chunyangzi!

Since he wanted the Sun-Supporting Yang-Locking Cloud Ascending Altar so much, he should be the one putting in great effort.

Ning Zhuo wouldn't fight every battle personally; that would go against his original intention of establishing Nanming Stronghold.

He still had his own cultivation.

Ning Zhuo closed his eyes and looked inward. Although his divine sea was empty, it had a polished, transparent feeling. His qi sea was dried up, but the newly generated spiritual energy exuded a sense of intimacy.

Although he hadn't gone all out, such a fierce battle could also allow Ning Zhuo to squeeze out his potential and receive tremendous tempering.

He now had a deeper understanding of the Five Elements Techniques, especially the Fire Element, as well as the mechanical birds he used, the zither melodies he played, the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Shaft, and the Great Thousand Mechanism Sound Garment.

This time, after finishing his daily lessons, he felt something again: "My cultivation in the essence sea might break through in the near future."

The Foundation Establishment Stage was divided into six minor levels, which were Unhindered Field Opening, Precious Medicine Generation, Dantian Radiance, Anomaly Talisman Planting, Golden Light Inner Illumination, and Turning Void into Reality.

Ning Zhuo's divine sea and qi sea had both already completed the Anomaly Talisman Planting, but the Three Sects' Supreme Methods progressed in unison. His essence sea's main Cultivation Art, the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art, was vast and profound, requiring continuous comprehension.

Dragged down by this, Ning Zhuo's overall cultivation was stuck at the Anomaly Talisman Planting stage, which was the mid Foundation Establishment Stage.

Once the Demonic Contamination Blood Meridian Art achieved a breakthrough and completed the Anomaly Talisman Planting, Ning Zhuo's cultivation would immediately break through, rising to the level of Golden Light Inner Illumination.

And reaching this step meant entering the late Foundation Establishment Stage.

The progress in his essence sea cultivation was not a harvest brought about by actual combat. Instead, it was Ning Zhuo's day-after-day grinding effort. His daily cultivation tasks were unshakeable, accumulating to this moment until the bottleneck naturally loosened.

Despite sensing this, Ning Zhuo still did not increase his cultivation time.

He was not in a hurry.

Just as Sun Lingtong had consoled him, cultivation was not the most important thing at present. He was merely at the mid Foundation Establishment Stage, yet he was still able to defeat Liu Jinke, nearly playing with the latter in the palm of his hand.

Ning Zhuo chose to proceed step by step.

"Aside from cultivation, I need to do mass maintenance on the mechanical birds and such."

His Profound Weapon Armor was severely damaged, and his Empty-Heart Blade-Snapping Guards and Spirit-Siphoning Box both needed major repairs.

"Senior Qing Huangzi working day and night to rush production for me has helped me a lot."

"But the production capacity is still insufficient, far from enough!"

"In addition to this, my funds are also a bit stretched."

Mechanism cultivators consumed a very, very large amount of resources. After Ning Zhuo's several battles, the maintenance costs alone were incredibly high. Not to mention, he also needed more mechanical birds and other items.

Ghost Path Golden Cores were indeed valuable, but they couldn't be sold in large quantities.

Once too many were sold, their origin would inevitably arouse suspicion, inviting even greater trouble.

"Speaking of which, where have Zhu Xuanji and the others traveled to?"

These people were close to Ning Zhuo, so Ning Zhuo could sell a large amount of Ghost Path Golden Cores to them. Ning Zhuo had contacted them before, and counting the days, he felt that they should be arriving in the Flying Cloud Kingdom soon.

-----

Flowing Cloud Peak.

Here, thousands upon thousands of clouds and mist churned, sometimes transforming into dragons and snakes, sometimes condensing into pavilions, gathering and dispersing unpredictably.

In a stone grotto halfway up the mountain, a dozen or so cultivators were gathered.

"Liu Jinke deserves to die!"

"He should be killed!"

"How many resources did we expend to push him in front of Ning Zhuo? And the result? Losing all three battles is one thing, but to actually switch sides and join Nanming Stronghold under the watchful eyes of the masses!"

"With that kneel of his, did he shatter his own bones? No, it was the face of the various factions of our Flowing Cloud Peak!"

"It's right that we should eliminate him, but how? He is currently hiding in Ning Zhuo's Three Lights Dao Heaven Cave Manor."

"Ning Zhuo is currently the center of attention, a genius cultivator who has emerged in this Flying Cloud Assembly, faintly showing the posture of the strongest newcomer. Ning Zhuo will definitely protect Liu Jinke to the death. For us to make a move on the latter... is difficult."

The stone grotto quieted down.

The cultivators all furrowed their brows tightly, feeling a massive headache.

Two flying letters shot in from outside the grotto, landing in the hands of two cultivators present.

It was precisely Qiu Lei and Wandering Cloud Old Man.

The two of them probed with their divine sense, and their expressions changed drastically.

"Nanming Stronghold has officially issued a challenge."

"That came so fast!"

"Everyone, you can continue discussing; the two of us must immediately rush back to our camp."

After stating the reason, the two Nascent Soul cultivators immediately flew out of the stone grotto, leaving behind the many cultivators whose expressions grew increasingly gloomy.
