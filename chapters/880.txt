Chapter 880: Taiyin True Fire

The cold-faced cultivator revealed his Innate Talent at this moment and displayed it, wanting to obtain the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's acknowledgment through this kind of harmony and balance.

The Vermilion Bird artifact spirit itself was closer to the Fire Element, but it was highly likely to develop a favorable impression of an Innate Talent like the Cold Marrow Dawn, and thereby acknowledge the cold-faced cultivator.

However, he stood in place for quite a while, yet there was no movement whatsoever on the pink pillar of light.

His greatest trump card, his strongest reliance in cultivation, could not provoke the slightest reaction from the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

According to the rules, in the third round, every cultivator only had one chance.

This cruel rule forced the cultivators to go all out the moment they made a move.

No one raised any objections to this, because everyone guessed that the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit didn't have much time left. Therefore, the Danxia Peak faction was in such a rush.

Just like that, the first cultivator was eliminated.

Ning Zhuo witnessed the entire process from a close distance and had a strong feeling about the upcoming minor trial: "Mother performed divination for me; even if I go all out and use the White Rainbow Righteous Integrity Bone, I will not be able to win."

"Now, even the first cultivator mobilized his Innate Talent. After all, there is only one chance. Therefore, the upcoming cultivators will go all out and reveal all sorts of trump cards. The scale of these reveals will definitely not be small!"

Ning Zhuo subconsciously looked around.

Cang Yazi, Chunyangzi, Nine Fires Dragon Lord, Red-Robed Guest, Old Man Taoli, Qingbaizi, Hundred Herbs Old Man... They were all either Nascent Soul or Golden Core cultivators.

The big-headed youth couldn't help but sigh with emotion: "How numerous the heroes of this world are."

"Mother criticized me before and taught me to be humble. She was definitely right.

"Recently, after arriving at the Myriad Manifestations Sect and showing off a bit, I've indeed let it get to my head.

"I need to stay alert."

At this moment, the hosting cultivator called out: "Second person."

Everyone's gazes shifted onto Mu Yuehua.

Mu Yuehua stood at the edge of the crowd, wearing a moon-white dress, as cold and clear as frost.

Her expression was as calm as water, betraying no emotion, but in reality, she was extremely nervous inside.

Her fingers curled slightly, tightly gripping the inner lining of her cuffs, as she slowly walked toward the pink pillar of light in the center.

I cannot lose! She felt an immense amount of pressure in her heart.

Countless people at the scene were looking at her. Without needing to guess, Mu Yuehua knew that there were definitely members of the Yuehua Sect among the audience.

She had suddenly left the Yuehua Sect, traveled in secret, continuously used teleportation formations, and arrived at the Myriad Manifestations Sect's main gate to participate in the Flying Cloud Assembly. This was undoubtedly a major event for the Yuehua Sect and had surely caused a sensation.

She had received the Yuehua Sect's cultivation and high regard, and was the person with the most expectations placed upon her to become the next Yuehua Fairy.

But now, she had voluntarily separated from the Yuehua Sect and come to the Myriad Manifestations Sect, wanting to join them. This was undoubtedly an act of betraying her sect.

However, the Myriad Manifestations Sect was simply too massive, and the Flying Cloud Assembly had been held many times. In past assemblies, there was no shortage of examples like Mu Yuehua, where sect elites defected to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect.

Many factions dared to be angry but did not dare to speak out against the Myriad Manifestations Sect, and the Yuehua Sect was the same.

Mu Yuehua sighed in her heart: If I had a choice, how could I have done such a thing?

Regrettably, the sect's cultivation of her was merely intended to mold her into the most suitable tool for pill refining.

It wasn't that Mu Yuehua hadn't tried to communicate, but the sect had secretly employed methods to eliminate all other possibilities in her life, severing her other Dao Paths to force her down the road of becoming the Yuehua Fairy.

Such a conflict of interest was irreconcilable.

After Mu Yuehua discovered this truth, she simply pretended not to know.

She hadn't completely dismissed the idea of actively bringing this matter to light and communicating with the sect.

But after thinking it over again and again, she didn't dare to take the gamble.

Compared to the Sect, she as an individual, possessing only Foundation Establishment cultivation, was truly too insignificant.

If the sect decided to act against her and take forceful measures, she would truly have no way to counter them and would lose all possibilities.

Instead, it was better to pretend to comply, numb those around her, and then make a sudden escape to quickly participate in the Flying Cloud Assembly.

This was a thought Mu Yuehua had harbored for a long time.

Her actions seemed risky, blind, and reckless, but in reality, they were a move to protect her Dao Path out of absolute necessity. Given her circumstances, it was the safest and most secure option.

"My joining the Myriad Manifestations Sect is practically guaranteed.

"The difficulty lies in the fact that I have no roots in the Myriad Manifestations Sect. This super faction is too massive, with countless factions. Being all alone, I will inevitably face ostracism, suppression, and the like."

"But if I am lucky enough to gain the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit's acknowledgment and win the Nanming Fire Furnace, that will be an entirely different situation."

"When that time comes, even if I lack the power to repair this Spirit Treasure-level pill furnace on my own, I can use it to bargain with other factions and powers, securing massive resources for myself."

"Once it is successfully repaired in the future, with the help of the South Bright Vermilion Bird Fire, I will be able to alter my Dao Path. I won't be forced to condense the Moon Yin Golden Core and fall into the predicament of going too far."

Mu Yuehua stopped in front of the pink pillar of light.

At this moment, she forcibly suppressed the various thoughts and stray ideas in her divine sea.

The pillar of light was hazy and dreamlike, its flowing pink hues dancing across her body, illuminating her face like a fairy in the moon. She raised her head, looking at the faintly discernible Vermilion Bird artifact spirit within the pillar, and couldn't help but take a deep breath.

"Since I have already left the Sect and come this far, why not take another gamble?

"If I can truly win, then my subsequent cultivation will be a smooth road for a long time."

"It's worth the bet!"

Mu Yuehua made her decision.

She raised her right hand, palm facing upwards.

Silver-white light rose from her palm, like moonlight spilling from the clouds. The light was cold and pure, without a trace of impurities or worldly fire. As soon as it appeared, it radiated a dense Taiyin power.

The silver-white radiance quickly condensed, transforming into a ball of flame.

Silver-white like frost, cold like ice, burning without a single sound - it was exactly the Taiyin True Fire!

"Is this the Taiyin True Fire Technique?"

"Although this female cultivator is young, looking at such fire-controlling skills, she must be a seed heavily cultivated by the Yuehua Sect."

"For her to participate in the Flying Cloud Assembly this time, what is the Yuehua Sect thinking?"

"Hehe, what else could they be thinking? That they can topple the Myriad Manifestations Sect? Don't make me laugh."

The spectators were far away, conversing continuously.

However, Ning Zhuo and his group were very close. Seeing the flame in Mu Yuehua's palm, they were first surprised and doubtful, then immediately shaken.

"Isn't this a temporary flame created by expending special spiritual energy to cast a specific Technique?"

"It indeed isn't. This is the Taiyin True Fire."

"Mu Yuehua actually holds a Fire Seed in her hand?!"

The crowd was exceptionally shocked.

The difference between the two was far too great.

The Yuehua Sect's Cultivation Art could cultivate Taiyin spiritual energy. By expending spiritual energy and pairing it with the Techniques from the Cultivation Art, a cultivator could temporarily manifest a Technique flame possessing the properties of Taiyin True Fire. Thus, it could be used for Artifact Refining, refining pills, and other aspects.

But a Fire Seed was different.

Any cultivator who obtained a Fire Seed could use it. There was no need to personally cultivate Taiyin spiritual energy or related fire arts.

And with this Fire Seed falling into Mu Yuehua's hands, it was like adding wings to a tiger!

Ning Zhuo was astonished. He deeply understood the preciousness of a Fire Seed. He had previously gone through great lengths to obtain a wisp of Fire Within Wood, only to consume it in a major battle. Since then, every time he thought of this, he felt a faint ache and regret.

But the Foundation Establishment female cultivator before him possessed a Fire Seed, and it was even the Taiyin True Fire.

The value of this Fire Seed was vastly higher than the Fire Within Wood Ning Zhuo had once obtained.

Even Wang Yu was surprised.

This Peak Lord of Danxia Peak had been observing this minor trial from behind the scenes all along. He had previously seen the Taiyin True Fire Mu Yuehua displayed in the first two rounds through a Formation.

But that was a Technique flame manifested through spiritual energy and incantations; it was merely an imitation of a Fire Seed.

But now, Mu Yuehua had directly drawn out the genuine Taiyin True Fire!

The cultivator from the Yuehua Sect, hidden among the spectating crowd, also changed his expression at this moment.

The Cultivation Art he practiced was also one of the Yuehua Sect's main paths. He was truly familiar with the Taiyin True Fire, so he spotted the difference with a single glance.

"Taiyin True Fire!"

"To think Mu Yuehua actually holds such a Fire Seed in her hand."

"Damn it, is this the Sect's asset? She actually smuggled it out?!"

Within the Yuehua Sect, there were indeed three Fire Seeds. However, only one was the Taiyin True Fire, while the other two were merely similar objects. Normally, if one needed it, they would definitely have to go through strict approvals and a lengthy process, and even then, they might not necessarily be able to borrow the real Taiyin True Fire.

But now, Mu Yuehua held one in her hand!

After confirming this, the Yuehua Sect cultivator stared intently. A few breaths later, his eyes became slightly bloodshot.

"This shouldn't be the Sect's wisp."

"There are slight differences between the appearance of that Fire Seed and this one.

"Mu Yuehua's seed is obviously much smaller than the one in the Sect."

Fire Seeds also required cultivation. With so many cultivators in the Yuehua Sect, backed by the Sect's resources, they took great care of and cherished the Taiyin True Fire, sparing no effort to cultivate it. Naturally, it had grown.

In comparison, Mu Yuehua's seed was much weaker and smaller.

"But even if it's very juvenile, it is still the Taiyin True Fire."

"This must be this woman's own serendipity. No wonder her progress in cultivating the Taiyin True Fire Technique was thousands of miles a day; it turns out she had the genuine article for constant reference. She hid it so deeply!"

"Damn it. If she were still in the Yuehua Sect, wouldn't this Fire Seed potentially become a Sect asset in the future?"

Thinking of this point, the Yuehua Sect cultivator couldn't help but grow even more envious.

Mu Yuehua's divine sense penetrated into the Fire Seed. The silver-white flame bloomed like an epiphyllum flower. Although it was a flame, it was completely cold and clear.

Mu Yuehua stared at the pink pillar of light before her, growing increasingly nervous inside.

Because the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit still remained motionless.

She couldn't help but increase her spiritual energy infusion.

Her Taiyin spiritual energy and the Taiyin True Fire were extremely compatible. The silver-white flame grew brighter and more intense. As it burned quietly, from the faintly distorted air above the flame, the phantom of a moon gradually formed.

The moon phantom emitted moonlight that sprinkled across the surroundings, dyeing more than half of the courtyard silver-white.

The pink flowing light on the surface of the pillar undulated slightly, like a breeze blowing across a lake, stirring up an almost invisible ripple.

Afterward, the ripples multiplied and gradually began to undulate.

Such a bizarre sight immediately elicited a wave of exclamations.

Many spectators widened their eyes, unable to help but think: Could it be that the winner of this minor trial has already been decided?

Qin Ke, Gu Lianjie, and the other cultivators of the main team were especially nervous, praying frantically in their hearts.

In the end, the pink ripples stopped undulating, and the pillar of light rapidly dimmed, returning to its original state.

The hosting cultivator announced at the right moment: "The second person is finished, please step down. If no one can reach this level subsequently, the winner will be Fellow Daoist Mu Yuehua."

"Is this the best I can do?" Mu Yuehua pursed her lips tightly and stepped down helplessly.

However, she didn't know that she had actually failed to meet the standard entirely. Although the Taiyin True Fire shared stellar aspects with the South Bright Vermilion Bird Fire, it hadn't drawn the slightest bit of attention from the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit.

The reason the pink pillar of light had such a reaction was merely the result of the Danxia Peak's Peak Lord, Wang Yu, manipulating it in secret.

Wang Yu's attention remained on Mu Yuehua: "This girl is very good, very good. If I recruit her into my Danxia Peak, I will gain both the person and the fire."

You are the Vermilion Bird artifact spirit!

Wang Yu cherished talent greatly, and at this moment, related emotions surged within him.

The rules of the third round and the semi-open Formation Space were all deliberately arranged by him, merely a tactic to display fairness.

He continued to pull the strings from behind the scenes, keeping a firm grip on this Rising Cloud Minor Trial. Even if he ultimately couldn't keep the Nanming Fire Furnace, he was determined to make up for the loss - and even profit more - from this group of people!

He could even secretly influence the drawing of lots made up of the purple sandalwood box and jade tallies.

"But in this round, I didn't make a move."

"Therefore, this order, to some extent, reveals the strength and weakness of each cultivator's Qi Luck."

"Those ranked in the top ten can be given focused and long-term attention."

Mu Yuehua walked back into the crowd, still standing at the edge, maintaining an expression of complete calm.

The hosting cultivator then called out: "Senior Chunyangzi, please step forward."

Chunyangzi was a Nascent Soul cultivator and the master of a sect, so it was only natural to address him respectfully.

"Easily done." Chunyangzi smiled faintly and walked forward calmly.

The Red-Robed Guest, who didn't get along with him, coldly snorted to show his disdain.

Chunyangzi didn't reveal any Innate Talent, nor did he take out any flame. Instead, he took out a futon and sat directly cross-legged on it.

Then, he began to chant scriptures.

He didn't chant with his mouth, but recited with his divine sense.

The Chunyang Pill Scripture!

This was the foundational scripture he had cultivated for nearly a thousand years. It was the foundation of his Dao Path and encompassed his complete understanding of fire, pill refining, and the myriad phenomena of Heaven and Earth. The scripture consisted of three thousand words, each and every one the crystallization of the blood and sweat of the Pure Yang Palace's successive generations of patriarchs.

His divine sense transformed into countless golden light spots, scattering like stars as they drifted into the pink pillar of light.

The central light pillar was thus triggered, rippling with flowing pink ripples, like a breeze blowing across a lake.

Mu Yuehua's expression remained calm, but her heart tightened.

The others whispered in exclamation: "There's a reaction!"

However, as Chunyangzi continued to recite the scripture, it only caused the pink light pillar to ripple. The intensity was identical to Mu Yuehua's, perhaps exceeding it only slightly at best.

The hosting cultivator announced that he and Mu Yuehua were tied.

Chunyangzi stood up. Although he was unwilling in his heart, he still maintained the demeanor of a sect master and returned to the crowd.

The hosting cultivator then called out the name of the fourth person, Shen Hongyao.

Shen Hongyao had been itching to go for a while now. Hearing her name, she immediately stepped forward and walked out of the crowd.

The cultivators behind her transmitted messages to her via divine sense: "Hongyao Shen, strive for success!"

A sharp glint flashed in the depths of Shen Hongyao's eyes: "If I win this time, will I be able to squeeze Jiang Ping out and become the Vice Hall Master of the Pill Refining Hall myself?"
