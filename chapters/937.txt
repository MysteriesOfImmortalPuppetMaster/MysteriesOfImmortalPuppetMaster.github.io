Chapter 937: Post-War Good Strategies

Ning Zhuo returned to his Cave Manor in a poor state.

The meridians throughout his body still ached with a swollen pain from the scouring of the Five Qi Return to Origin Pill's medicinal power. His qi sea was dry, and his spiritual energy pool was completely depleted. Within the walls of the pool, the five-colored spiritual light was as thin as fading twilight clouds. The Spirit-Drawing Box was even cracked, its lid scorched black and its formation runes half-destroyed.

After resting and recovering, Ning Zhuo began to inspect his spoils of war.

On the desk, he laid out his spoils one by one.

The Flock-Startling Whistle, Thousand-Catties Pendant, Shattering Thunder Pearl, Fate-Severing Blade Talisman, Golden Marrow Life-Sustaining Pill, Flame-Evading Blood-Protecting Pill, Ironbone Stiffening Pill, and a transcribed Jade Slip of the Metal and Stone Opening Art. At the innermost side lay a sealed cold jade box containing the Nine Orifices Blood Gold Embryo.

Ning Zhuo first picked up the Flock-Startling Whistle, gently rubbing his thumb over its body.

The item was not large, but it had the warm, smooth feel of animal bone. Deep within the whistle's opening, faint and intricate beast patterns could be seen, as if the spiritual senses of countless startled birds and beasts had been compressed inside. Ning Zhuo did not blow it; instead, he simply probed it with his divine sense. He immediately felt a sharp, piercing sensation, as though thousands of wings were simultaneously flapping against the lake of his mind.

"Good stuff," Ning Zhuo muttered softly.

This treasure could disrupt rhythms and divine sense. In the hands of the Bird and Beast Manor, it was a hunting tool. In Liu Jinke's hands, it was capable of suppressing a mechanical bird swarm.

Ning Zhuo set the Flock-Startling Whistle down, already forming a plan in his mind.

This item might not only be used against enemies in the future. If he reverse-engineered its principles of disrupting swarms and applied them to the modification of his mechanical bird swarm, it could actually enhance their resistance to such interference.

In the Path of Mechanisms, one did not fear being countered; one feared not understanding why they were countered.

Similarly, the Flock-Startling Whistle could serve as a training prop to hone his musical skills.

Ning Zhuo then picked up the Thousand-Catties Pendant.

It was a heavy, dark pendant that looked unremarkable, but after he injected a wisp of spiritual energy into it, the surface of the desk immediately sank slightly, and the table legs let out a faint, barely audible groan.

Ning Zhuo retracted his power just in time, a look of appreciation rising in his eyes.

The Thousand-Catties Pendant did not kill with sharpness, but it could alter the battlefield. Once activated, the entire martial arena seemed to be suppressed by earth-elemental force, greatly affecting the speed of the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot.

If Ning Zhuo had not prepared a spiritual energy pool, Medicinal Pills, and talismans for sustained combat in advance, simply being bogged down by that heavy earth force would have been enough for Liu Jinke to close the distance bit by bit.

In terms of holding ground, trapping enemies, and suppressing charges, this treasure could be of great use and was worth looking forward to.

Especially when the Naming Stronghold assaulted Flowing Cloud Peak in the future, if they needed to occupy a certain mountainside, cloud nest, or fire vein, the Thousand-Catties Pendant could temporarily reinforce the terrain and slow down the enemy's offensive.

There were not many Shattering Thunder Pearls left; Ning Zhuo picked up just one to observe.

The thunder pearl was a mix of azure and purple, with lightning flashing on and off inside, resembling a tiny eye of thunder. In the previous battle, when they exploded in succession, the defenses of both the golden light collar and the azure-white jade ring were shattered to the point of being covered in cracks. It did not seek to kill in a single blow, but specialized in breaking through external spiritual energy and protective light barriers, making it extremely practical.

"Thunder Cloud Society," Ning Zhuo murmured this name in his heart.

These Flowing Cloud Peak factions all claimed to have different philosophies, but in reality, their strikes were extremely precise - the Flock-Startling Whistle disrupted his bird swarm, the Thousand-Catties Pendant suppressed his speed, the Shattering Thunder Pearls broke his defenses, and the Fate-Severing Blade Talisman cut his suspension threads. If Liu Jinke had not lacked divine sense, rendering him unable to control many conventional magic treasures as easily as his own arm, this battle would have been even more difficult.

"To deal with me, they must have communicated secretly beforehand, reached an agreement, and jointly provided aid to Liu Jinke."

Ning Zhuo picked up the Fate-Severing Blade Talismans.

Only one unused talisman remained; its surface bore a pitch-black streak, like a knife carved into the night. Ning Zhuo scrutinized it for a long time, his expression more serious than before. What this talisman severed was not ordinary rope, but spiritual energy connections, threads of divine sense, and the control lines of puppets. If used well at a critical moment, it was enough to make an mechanism master slip up in an instant.

"This thing is very helpful to me."

Ning Zhuo did not shy away from acknowledging the brilliance of the enemy's methods.

The more he looked at it, the more he felt the talisman was worth studying. The Life Hanging by a Thread technique, mechanism control, and the deployment of Profound Weapon Armors were all inseparable from the word "connection." The Fate-Severing Blade Talisman could sever these connections; conversely, it could also make him think about how to reinforce them and how to set up phantom threads, fake threads, and substitute threads.

"Speaking of which, despite my best efforts to hide my capabilities during my two consecutive public battles, this fight will inevitably attract more targeted countermeasures."

"Especially since the public saw my spiritual energy suspension threads being easily countered. Right now, the various factions on Flowing Cloud Peak must be racking their brains in this area."

"Perhaps I should master a mechanical art specifically aimed at suspension threads, to strengthen them?"

Ning Zhuo suppressed this sudden flash of inspiration for now.

Finally, he held the Jade Slip of the Metal and Stone Opening Art in his palms.

After inspecting it for a moment, Ning Zhuo gained some comprehension in his heart.

The so-called "Metal and Stone Opening" was not about using metal to overcome wood or sharpness to break artifacts. Rather, it utilized the Metal Element's property of "judgment" to strike the "closed" nature of stone, causing all things to transition from a state of "combined" to "open."

The true profoundness of this technique lay entirely in the word "open."

The reason mechanical constructs could move was not just because their parts were pieced together. It was because their mortises and tenons interlocked, their axles and springs supported one another, their formation runes connected, their talismanic pathways communicated, and their spiritual mechanisms aligned. There was a sort of "unifying property" among them. When an mechanism master refined an artifact, they were constantly strengthening this unifying property, turning dead objects into tools and scattered parts into whole forms.

Mortises and tenons no longer interlocked.

Bearings were no longer concentric.

Formation runes no longer connected...

Therefore, to an outsider, it looked as if the mechanism was dismantled by an invisible hand, with bamboo ribs, iron plates, talisman pages, and spirit threads falling in rapid succession. But fundamentally, it wasn't smashed; it was "opened."

"Therefore, the Metal and Stone Opening Art does indeed require 'finding the seams,' but finding the seams isn't the main point."

Finding the seams was merely to allow the Metal Element's opening command to reach the joining points inside the mechanism. The more exquisite the mechanism, the more gaps, joints, axles, hidden clasps, and talisman pathway nodes it possessed, making it ironically easier for this technique to find a "door."

"How strange and wonderful..." Ning Zhuo sighed in admiration.

In Liu Jinke's hands, this technique was used to dismantle Ning Zhuo's mechanisms. But in Ning Zhuo's hands, it became a reference for preventing his mechanisms from being dismantled, patching flaws on the battlefield, and countering enemy mechanisms.

The Nine Orifices Blood Gold Embryo was the most unique of all.

Ning Zhuo did not hastily refine it; he merely observed it through the sealed cold jade box. This item resembled a golden-red heart, with thick blood-gold essence slowly circulating within all nine of its orifices. The assistance it provided Liu Jinke far exceeded that of ordinary magic treasures and Medicinal Pills. It could even be said that the main reason Liu Jinke was able to hold on until the end of the second battle was largely thanks to this embryo.

"What a pity that it doesn't suit me."

Ning Zhuo knew this very clearly in his heart.

The Nine Orifices Blood Gold Embryo was too compatible with the Golden Liquid Reverting Core Physique; it was practically tailor-made for Liu Jinke. Since Liu Jinke's Upper Dantian had disappeared, his divine sense and spiritual thoughts had fused with the golden blood of his physical body, making it difficult for him to wield ordinary magic treasures. However, this item did not take the path of a normal magic treasure. Instead, it acted like a second blood-gold heart, directly nourishing his golden blood.

To Liu Jinke, this was a supreme treasure for sustained combat.

To Ning Zhuo, it was mostly just research material.

He gently closed the cold jade box, beginning to mentally review the events of this second battle.

"As expected, Liu Jinke's greatest flaw still lies in his divine sense."

Liu Jinke possessed Golden Core cultivation, the Golden Liquid Reverting Core Physique, the Nine Orifices Blood Gold Embryo, and the backing of numerous factions. However, he lacked a normal Cultivator's Upper Dantian, leaving him unable to finely control the vast majority of magic treasures. Consequently, his support methods had to rely primarily on talismans, Medicinal Pills, semi-automatic magic treasures, and close-combat exotic treasures.

This doomed Liu Jinke to have limited adaptability.

He could become tougher, fiercer, and more capable in a war of attrition, but it was very difficult for his techniques to become truly intricate and undergo a qualitative transformation.

"In this battle, I also won because I 'anticipated the enemy's moves in advance'."

Ning Zhuo had prepared the spiritual energy pool, Spirit-Drawing Box, Cloud Tide Energy Recovery Talisman, and Five Qi Return to Origin Pill ahead of time, and sure enough, they all came in handy. Without these means of sustaining his energy, he would not have lasted until his Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation gained momentum.

But Ning Zhuo also had to admit: in terms of sheer endurance, he was still no match for Liu Jinke, who was empowered by the Nine Orifices Blood Gold Embryo.

His endurance was pieced together section by section, relying on continuous external supplementation. Liu Jinke's endurance, on the other hand, felt as if it grew naturally from the depths of his flesh and blood. With every beat of the Nine Orifices Blood Gold Embryo, blood-gold essence would nourish his entire body. That kind of recovery was too direct and too perfectly suited, requiring almost no additional mental effort to deploy.

"In the previous battle, I used a war of attrition to win. In this battle, I relied on the gap in our strength."

"And what allowed me to widen that gap in strength between me and Liu Jinke was - the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation!"

Ning Zhuo swept his divine sense through his Storage Belt. The four Golden Core-level Profound Weapon Armors - the saber, sword, axe, and hook - still emanated residual might, their sharp edges gleaming coldly. Meanwhile, the older Profound Weapon Armors, like Old Cold Leg, were riddled with battle scars. Some were covered in cracks, and the spiritual light of others had dimmed, yet they remained silently suspended, much like a group of veteran soldiers who had just fought a grueling battle.

With this battle alone, the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation had evolved, elevating into a viable trump card.

Offense and defense rotated seamlessly, the core remained stable, the suspended weapons could adapt, and they attacked in alternating turns. It first protected itself before taking turns slashing at the enemy. Best of all, the formation could adapt on the fly, borrowing the enemy's strength to pivot into the next round of attacks. The more fiercely Liu Jinke assaulted at close range, the more his attacks were absorbed, redirected, and countered by the battle formation.

"Boss's realm in the Dao of Formations, combined with the Luo Shu, is incredibly useful!"

"As for Profound Weapon Armors, it would be best to buy more."

Ning Zhuo then began to sigh in awe at his "Calamitous Destiny."

The Calamitous Destiny upon him had allowed him to harvest far more than he ever would have by participating in the Rising Cloud Minor Trials!

Especially now that he had drawn so much attention to himself, it would be extremely easy for others to set traps for him in the Rising Cloud Minor Trials. Therefore, forging a different path was the better choice.

Ning Zhuo's original intention was to join the Myriad Manifestations Sect and find the position most suitable for himself.

Now, establishing a stronghold and charging the peaks was a far better way to achieve this goal than participating in the Rising Cloud Minor Trials!

"But I can't give up on the Rising Cloud Minor Trials either."

"I need to pick a few suitable ones, participate swiftly, seize the first place, and keep upgrading the token in my hand to maintain my position within the first tier of genius cultivators."

Sun Lingtong returned to the Cave Manor.

With a gentle flicker in the shadows at the corner of the study, he slipped out silently. His movements were agile, and his landing made no sound.

As soon as he appeared, he saw the numerous treasures on the desk, and his eyes instantly lit up.

"Yo!" Sun Lingtong whispered. "Little Zhuo, your plan was a success. You fleeced Liu Jinke from head to toe, didn't you?"

Ning Zhuo gave a soft chuckle. In front of Sun Lingtong, he could completely relax.

He then took out an item and handed it to Sun Lingtong.

"The Cherished Jade?" Sun Lingtong lowered his voice. Reaching out to take it, he gently stroked it with his fingertips, almost unable to hide the smile on his face. "The one Gu Huaijiu gave you?"

Ning Zhuo nodded. "This item can sense surrounding treasures. If it stays with me, it'll only draw unwanted attention. Boss, since you walk in the shadows, it's far more suited for your use."

Sun Lingtong turned the Cherished Jade over and over in his hands, the corners of his mouth rising higher and higher.

There was truly no need for modesty or polite refusals between him and Ning Zhuo. If an ordinary person had received such a heavy treasure, they would have exchanged a few hypocritical words of polite decline. But Sun Lingtong just chuckled, tucked it directly into his robes, and patted his chest.

"Alright, I'll take it," he said with utter self-assurance.

"You're weighed down by heavy wealth right now, so it really isn't suitable for you to hold onto this. You're absolutely right; with the Cherished Jade, finding treasures and scouting paths will be a lot more convenient for me."

A sharp glint flashed in Sun Lingtong's eyes, and his voice dropped even lower. "I'm keeping an eye on that Demonic Cultivator Soul matter for you. There should be results soon."

Ning Zhuo then took out the sword embryo gifted by Situ Xing.

It was a small, three-inch sword. Silver star patterns flowed across its deep blue blade, resembling a frozen segment of the starry river in the night sky. Ning Zhuo handed it over. "Boss, this item is pretty good too. If you want to use it..."

But Sun Lingtong immediately waved his hand.

"No, no, no."

"I don't want this thing."

"I already have my small blade; it's much more suitable for me than a flying sword."

"Besides, this is a sword embryo. It requires spiritual energy to nourish and emphasizes the harmonization between the person and the sword. It takes way too long, and I don't want to bother. With that kind of time, I might as well use the Cherished Jade to scope out a few more treasure hoards and get us some practical goods."

Ning Zhuo couldn't help but let out a burst of laughter.

He had no choice but to put the sword embryo away.

Sun Lingtong asked, "Little Zhuo, you've cut down Liu Jinke once again, and you've publicly provoked all the major factions on Flowing Cloud Peak. What should we do next?"

The lamplight flickered slightly, casting shifting shadows over the Flock-Startling Whistle, Thousand-Catties Pendant, Fate-Severing Blade Talismans, and other items on the desk. They resembled chess pieces, or perhaps hands reaching out from the darkness.

After a long while, Ning Zhuo finally spoke. "This line with Liu Jinke has already forced out quite a lot of information. The factions on Flowing Cloud Peak really can't sit still anymore, and it's not just one group making a move; it's a combined effort from multiple sides."

Sun Lingtong added, "The Bird and Beast Manor, Earth Expansion Alliance, Thunder Cloud Society, Water-Severing Blade Pavilion, Gold and Stone Alliance... and the Nine Orifices Blood Gold Embryo from the Suspended Pot Clinic. Tsk, none of them want you to have a smooth trip up the mountain."

Ning Zhuo replied, "Because of this exactly, we cannot rush."

Sun Lingtong raised an eyebrow. "You just declared publicly that you were definitely charging Flowing Cloud Peak."

Ning Zhuo smiled faintly. "I said that for them to hear."

Sun Lingtong laughed.

Ning Zhuo continued, "Our momentum must be strong, but our actions must be steady. The Naming Stronghold has just been established; it looks majestic, but internally, we haven't truly synchronized yet. If we rashly charge the peak now, just one defeat will cause all the Cultivators in the stronghold to harbor their own ulterior motives."

"For every day we hold off our assault on Flowing Cloud Peak, we retain the initiative."

Sun Lingtong frowned slightly. "Flowing Cloud Peak won't just sit back and wait. They will definitely make their move through public opinion. How should we handle that?"

Ning Zhuo shrugged. "It's very simple. Our Naming Fire Furnace hasn't even been fully repaired yet. Without completely winning over everyone's hearts, how could we possibly charge the peak?"

Sun Lingtong froze for a moment before bursting into loud laughter.

He gave a thumbs up. "I'd expect nothing less from you, Little Zhuo."

Ning Zhuo also smiled. "Boss, I need you to leak a rumor for me as well."

Sun Lingtong rolled his eyes in thought, his grin growing wider. "Tell me the details."

Ning Zhuo instructed, "Spread the word that even though the Naming Stronghold has yet to charge the peak, the factions of Flowing Cloud Peak are already sitting on pins and needles. The Thunder Cloud Society, Bird and Beast Manor, Earth Expansion Alliance, Water-Severing Blade Pavilion, Gold and Stone Alliance, Suspended Pot Clinic, and others are all scrambling over each other to fund Liu Jinke. Say that they are doing this in hopes that he will challenge me a third time to make things difficult for me - which, by extension, means making things difficult for the entire Naming Stronghold."

"After all, I am the master of the Naming Fire Furnace and everyone's debtor. I am the true core."
