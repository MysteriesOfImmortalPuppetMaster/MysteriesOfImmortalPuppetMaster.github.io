Chapter 953: Female Mechanism Brutally Beats Up Liu Jinke

Listening to Liu Jinke's clamoring, Ning Zhuo's eyes flickered slightly. With a calm expression, he allocated more than half of his divine sense to coordinate the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation.

The sabers, swords, axes, and hooks no longer forced their way toward vital points. Instead, they shifted from slashing to suppressing, from attacking to entangling. The Saber Armors suppressed Liu Jinke's shoulders, the Sword Armors forced him to turn around, the Axe Armors intercepted his charges, and the Hook Armors dragged at his footsteps.

Off to the side, the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guards were fully occupied entangling Liu Jinke's blood talisman clones, leaving them powerless to offer support.

Chunyangzi's brows furrowed slightly, and the scriptures on his crimson-gold robe seemed to dim a fraction.

The Red-Robed Guest tapped his armrest, unable to stop himself from grimacing. "What is the point of this kid dragging things out like this? Why hasn't he pulled out more of his trump cards?"

Previously, Ning Zhuo had operated the Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation to inflict lethal damage. Now that he had shifted his tactical objective to entangling the enemy, Liu Jinke's relentless offensive immediately slowed down.

Liu Jinke suddenly felt as if his fierce, head-on assaults had sunken into a quagmire. Much of the time, his strikes failed to land solidly, leaving a smoldering frustration in his heart.

Jin Mantang’s smile carried a trace of indignant fury at being looked down upon. He had figured it out: "This brat Ning Zhuo is actually using this critical moment to temper his battle formation control over his Mystic Weapon Armors! He is merely at the mid Foundation Establishment Stage, yet he dares to disregard Liu Jinke’s peak Golden Core-level combat power!"

At this very moment, in terms of pure combat strength, Liu Jinke had well and truly reached the absolute peak of the Golden Core-level.

Tan Zhu’s gaze was profound. "Even now, Ning Zhuo hasn't revealed his final cards. Liu Jinke’s combat prowess has indeed been elevated tremendously, but his mindset lacks composure. He is too fixated on defeating Ning Zhuo, consumed by an overwhelming thirst for victory, and thus has failed to see the true flow of the battle."

Many discerning Cultivators looked past the superficial illusion of Liu Jinke’s "overwhelming advantage" and saw the unruffled composure Ning Zhuo maintained beneath the surface.

Crack, crack...

After such a prolonged battle, the Mystic Weapon Armor - Old Cold Legs was the first to give out. Numerous cracks suddenly webbed across its surface. However, the brittle sounds of its fracturing were instantly drowned out by the thunderous din of the battlefield, along with Liu Jinke's roars and maniacal laughter.

Soon after, the other Mystic Weapon Armors below the Golden Core-level began to falter one by one.

Even though their primary role was merely to entangle him, the relentless ferocity of Liu Jinke’s assaults eventually forced the various Mystic Weapon Armors to show signs of structural fatigue.

Cao Gui’s lips moved, but he made no sound.

Huo Nian'er subconsciously clenched her jaw, fraught with worry for Ning Zhuo.

Ning Zhuo opened his third Dharma Pool!

At the same time, his Spirit-Siphoning Box began to operate.

As the wooden box strapped to his back opened and closed, Five Elements Spirit Talismans lit up one by one. The surrounding ambient spiritual energy was forcefully sucked into the box, converted into usable spiritual energy, and channeled along the mechanical conduits directly into Ning Zhuo's body.

Compared to the state of his Mystic Weapon Armors, Ning Zhuo’s spiritual energy reserves were in far worse condition.

Naturally, the Gold-Swallowing Divine Domain did not spare the Spirit-Siphoning Box.

Inside the operating Spirit-Siphoning Box, several metal clasps, rotary shafts, and metal conducting plates for spiritual energy had their metal qi rapidly extracted by the black-gold divine light.

The originally smooth flow of the talisman arrays inside the box soon began to experience subtle micro-stutters.

Ning Zhuo quickly sensed this. His expression remained unchanged, though he was forced to allocate additional divine sense and spiritual energy just to maintain the stability of the Spirit-Siphoning Box.

Immediately after, a Cloud Tide Origin Returning Talisman ignited!

Tucked inside his robes, the talisman dissolved into a tidal wave of cloud-like spiritual energy that surged directly into his body.

Ning Zhuo also popped a Five Qi Return to Origin Pill into his mouth. The pill's medicinal power separated into five streams of pure qi - representing Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, and Earth - which seamlessly merged into the spiritual energy circulation of his Five Elements Qi Rhythm Art.

Any single one of these methods would be enough to make ordinary Foundation Establishment Cultivators red-eyed with envy.

But when compared to Liu Jinke, even when all these methods were stacked together, they still paled in comparison!

Within Liu Jinke’s body, one of his Golden Blood Pellet Beads dissolved, causing a golden mist to gush forth from deep within his flesh.

The very next moment, the injuries across his entire body knit back together. His aura spiked, his spiritual energy was instantly replenished, and his vital essence blood burned vigorously once more.

He didn't need to tap into Dharma Pools, nor did he need talismans for energy conversion, let alone rely on pills for recovery.

This power was fundamentally his Innate Talent; it grew from within him. It was direct and domineering, like a subterranean spring exploding upward to completely wash away any deficit in his body.

This was the quasi-Divine Art tier Golden Liquid Reverting Core Body!

From the very moment Liu Jinke began fighting, it had provided him with full, unyielding support, establishing an overwhelming, almost crushing advantage!

In the martial arena, Ning Zhuo was forced to retreat continuously.

Because its metal axles were being heavily suppressed by the Gold-Swallowing Divine Domain, the speed of the Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot was drastically reduced.

Every movement made by the Hollow-Chested Folded-Blade Guards was noticeably a beat slower, vastly increasing the difficulty for Ning Zhuo to control them.

The swarm of mechanical birds suffered constant casualties under the strikes of the Chaotic Chord Sound-Splitting Trident.

The Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation, formed by the Mystic Weapon Armors, was struggling to achieve anything meaningful.

Meanwhile, although Ning Zhuo’s Fire Element Techniques were domineering and had reached the Golden Core-level - still capable of inflicting burns on Liu Jinke - their damage was ultimately mitigated by the combined effects of the Fire-Warding Pill, the Flame-Evading Blood-Protecting Pill, and his quasi-Divine Art.

"Ning Zhuo won't be able to hold out much longer," Zhao Ni said in a low voice, a look of joy spreading across his face.

Ye Qingming watched the battle silently, carefully observing Ning Zhuo’s expression. The youth's face had grown pale from the intense consumption of his spiritual energy and divine sense. Yet, his gaze remained steady - like a lantern at the bottom of a deep well; no matter how chaotic the ripples on the surface grew, the light at the bottom would not be extinguished.

Liu Jinke grew increasingly exhilarated as he fought. The golden light in his eyes shone so intensely that onlookers found it hard to look at him directly.

"Ning Zhuo!" With a swing of his trident, he sent a Saber Armor flying. His golden blood erupted with power as he closed the distance to within a mere ten feet.

"Kneel and beg for mercy, and I just might spare your life!"

Throughout the entire fight, he had never once intended to take Ning Zhuo’s life. Too many influential figures looked highly upon Ning Zhuo. If he truly killed him, the many powerhouses of the Nanming Stronghold would absolutely never let him go.

Liu Jinke knew full well that the only reason he had received the backing of the various major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak was because Ning Zhuo had schemed against him, thereby turning him into the most convenient blade for those factions to wield.

But he was merely a blade; he was no suicidal operative.

He held exactly zero loyalty to the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak. Deep down, he was acutely aware that, in some twisted way, he actually had to thank Ning Zhuo. If Ning Zhuo hadn't "propped him up," how could he ever have received such an unprecedented level of resources?

Under normal circumstances, he wouldn't even dare to dream of such lavish backing!

Meanwhile, Ning Zhuo calmly analyzed the flow of the battle.

By using Liu Jinke, he was once again tempering his own combat skills.

Opportunities for live combat like this were incredibly rare!

Weapons were ominous and war was perilous; if one walked along the riverbank long enough, their shoes were bound to get wet. Every actual life-and-death battle carried massive risks that could easily end one's life. But in this spar against Liu Jinke, Ning Zhuo knew the threat of death was minuscule.

He was always severely pressed for time, so he deeply cherished every opportunity, striving to extract the maximum possible value from every second!

Although my Five Element Techniques are comprehensive, against an opponent like Liu Jinke, only my Fire Element Techniques combined with my Fire Element Heart Temple are capable of acting as a real deterrent.

The Shouzhuo Wheel Slash Formation is only good for entanglement at this point. It simply cannot break through his defensive systems.

With my musical skills amplifying them, the mechanical bird flock is significantly boosted. But against a peak Golden Core-level opponent, their fundamental quality is still too lacking. They are only fit for a battle of attrition.

Generally speaking, such mechanical constructs relied heavily on overwhelming numbers, but to be truly effective, they also needed to be paired with advanced mechanical arts.

It's my lack of proficiency in mechanical arts holding me back! If I were skilled enough, these mechanical flying birds would be vastly more effective.

If I had known Liu Jinke’s combat power was at this level, I would have foregone the Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment and equipped the Green Bamboo Hundred Transformations Armor instead.

The Great Thousand Mechanical Sound Garment was primarily used to amplify musical techniques and support mechanical constructs. It offered very little utility in close-quarters combat.

The Green Bamboo Hundred Transformations Armor, on the other hand, excelled at melee and mid-range combat.

Then again, I am still unaccustomed to that mechanical battle armor. I simply lack the necessary practice with it.

Ah well. I suppose this is enough practice for today's battle.

With that thought, Ning Zhuo thrust both palms forward.

The next moment, a Fire Dragon soared upward with ferocious momentum, while a barrage of flaming sabers slashed forward, temporarily halting Liu Jinke's advance.

Having bought himself a brief window of time, Ning Zhuo swept his divine sense over his Storage Belt and summoned forth a Mechanical Puppet.

The moment this mechanical puppet appeared, it instantly drew the eyes of countless spectators both inside and outside the arena.

"What is that?"

"Ning Zhuo is finally willing to play his trump card!"

"It’s a Golden Core-level mechanical puppet."

Liu Jinke’s pupils shrank slightly, his gaze locking dead onto the unfamiliar mechanical construct before him.

It was a female mechanism, standing over six feet tall. Her face plate, carved from snow porcelain, was cold, pristine, and entirely flawless. Strands of frost-silk served as her hair, trailing elegantly down her back, with every individual thread gleaming with a cold luster.

She was clad in tricolor enamel armor that shimmered in cascading waves of crimson, sapphire, and pearl-white. The flowing brilliance resembled a wintry sun reflecting off colored glaze - breathtakingly elegant and far from ordinary.

Her primary pair of arms rested naturally at her front, while two secondary pairs of arms extended outward from her ribs. When all six arms fully spread, they resembled a blooming snow lotus - or the rising of six icy moons over the battlefield.

Snow-Colored Maiden Hui!

The radiance of the Gold-Swallowing Divine Domain washed over her, but it failed to extract even a single trace of metallic essence.

Both Snow-Colored Maiden Hui and Snow Pivot Guard Xie had been crafted by Ning Zhuo using ice-sculpting techniques.

Specifically, the primary materials that made up Snow-Colored Maiden Hui’s chassis were Warm-Cold Jade Essence, Purple-Night Ice Crystal, Seven-Rainbow Ice-Silk, and Immaculate Frostbound Branches. Not a single piece of her contained Metal Element materials.

"The Gold-Swallowing Divine Domain has no effect on her!" Liu Jinke’s eyelid twitched. With a low growl, he charged right at Snow-Colored Maiden Hui.

The Chaotic Chord Sound-Splitting Trident crashed down toward her head, whipping up a fierce howl of wind.

Snow-Colored Maiden Hui merely tilted her body as all six of her arms moved in unison!

One hand seized the trident, the second pressed his wrist, the third jabbed his elbow, the fourth slammed into his shoulder, the fifth sealed off his chest, and the sixth slashed like an icy blade straight toward the side of Liu Jinke’s neck.

She possessed none of the tension or hesitation common to living cultivators in close-quarters combat. Every one of her movements was precise to the point of cruelty. Each jade-like hand seemed to calculate its own unique angles, and every single strike landed exactly where it was most awkward for Liu Jinke to exert his strength.

Clang!

Her jade finger flicked the shaft of the trident from the side, instantly detonating a piercing sonic crack.

The ear-piercing metallic screech surged toward Snow-Colored Maiden's face plate, only to be muffled by an icy blue halo that rose from her tricolor enamel armor.

Even before the sonic blast could fully disperse, her second hand had already struck Liu Jinke’s wrist bone.

Innate Talent - Ice Jade Hands!

The tips of all six of her jade hands lit up with a lustrous white radiance simultaneously, with a faint hint of vibrant emerald green gleaming within the light. This chill was not the stillness of death; rather, it resembled the melting snow of early spring - a penetrating cold that concealed a nurturing moisture.

Liu Jinke’s wrist immediately seized up.

Snow-Colored Maiden’s third palm slammed onto his shoulder.

Bang!

His golden armor caved inward.

Her fourth palm struck him square in the chest, the impact scrambling the glow of the beast-maw divine runes etched across his torso.

Her fifth hand seized his arm and viciously wrenched it outward.

And her sixth hand formed a tight fist, smashing solidly into his left cheek.

Thud.

Crack.

Liu Jinke’s entire head snapped to the side. He coughed up a mouthful of golden blood, but before it could even hit the ground, the residual chill of the Ice Jade Hands froze it into a string of fine, golden frost.

Absolute shock flooded Liu Jinke’s face. This was his first time facing Snow-Colored Maiden Hui in battle, and he had been caught completely off guard.

"Raaaaar!" he bellowed, his eyes erupting with blinding golden brilliance. Marshaling every ounce of his strength, he charged directly back at Snow-Colored Maiden Hui.

Liu Jinke was also a specialist in close-quarters combat. He exchanged a flurry of kicks and punches with Snow-Colored Maiden Hui, the two of them locking into a fierce, back-and-forth stalemate.

Ning Zhuo poured the vast majority of his mental focus into Snow-Colored Maiden Hui. His close-combat attainments had recently undergone a series of enhancements; after absorbing the experiences of the demonic souls, his fundamental skills had been significantly solidified.

As a result, his control over Snow-Colored Maiden Hui was now smooth enough to match Liu Jinke head-on.

Snow-Colored Maiden Hui pivoted lightly on her toes, sticking to him like a shadow. Her six arms became a blur, her jade palms chaining together like an overlapping dance of lunar shadows. Roaring with fury, Liu Jinke swung his trident to block. The Chaotic Chord Sound-Splitting Trident smashed against her jade arms again and again, sending out sharp, ear-piercing shrieks of colliding metal.

The impacts forced hairline fractures onto Snow-Colored Maiden's tricolor enamel armor, with the plating on her shoulder even splitting open. Yet she neither retreated nor dodged, fighting on with absolute, reckless abandon.

Catching a momentary lull in the exchange, she simply reached out and brushed a hand over her shoulder.

Innate Talent - Ice Jade Hands!

In an instant, every single crack across her armor was flawlessly repaired.

"Is this... a healing-type mechanism?" Wen Suzhen suddenly sat up straight, an irrepressible flash of shock crossing her eyes for the first time.

The Red-Robed Guest smiled, thinking to himself: This brat Ning Zhuo is actually using a healer to fight Liu Jinke. Once the true role of this mechanism is exposed after the battle, it will be an utterly humiliating, soul-crushing blow to both Liu Jinke and the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak.

The golden blood across Liu Jinke’s shoulder blades instantly condensed into frost. When her palm struck his right rib, the Golden Meridian Garment’s defensive power plummeted in the face of the encroaching frost and physical blows.

Ironically, the lingering medicinal effects of his Iron Bone Stiffness-Setting Pill had now begun to act as a detriment, causing his movements to grow more sluggish by the second.

As the battle dragged on, the layer of golden blood coating Liu Jinke began to freeze completely. Soon, intricate webs of fine frost had spread over his skin, his Golden Meridian Garment, and his Golden Armor, entangling all three layers.

Of course, this profound ice inherently possessed potent healing properties. But the problem was - Liu Jinke didn't need any healing!

Even after fighting for this long, he still had eighty percent of his internal Golden Blood Pellet Beads remaining; he wasn't lacking in supplies or healing in the slightest.

Both shocked and furious, Liu Jinke roared, "Get off me!"

He forcibly burst another Golden Blood Pellet Bead, unleashing a surge of golden mist that managed to blast away a portion of the frost. In the end, a quasi-Divine Art was tyrannical. The chill of Snow-Colored Maiden Hui was ultimately only at the level of an Innate Talent, so it couldn't lock him down completely.

His aura flared wildly as he launched a sweeping blow with his Chaotic Chord Sound-Splitting Trident.

Yet, under Ning Zhuo’s precise manipulation, Snow-Colored Maiden Hui used his own momentum against him, dodging the sweep and sticking closely to his body.

Her six arms blossomed like a lotus - a lotus hiding deadly blades.

Her primary hands pressed firmly against Liu Jinke’s chest, while her secondary arms unleashed a rapid flurry of strikes against his shoulders, elbows, neck, and wrists. Her final strike was an open-palm slam squarely against the beast-maw divine runes on his chest.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Liu Jinke was sent flying backward, his back smashing heavily into the protective light barrier of the arena's formation.

He had actually been so thoroughly suppressed that he was battered all the way to the very edge of the martial arena!

"To think Ning Zhuo was still hiding an mechanism of this caliber. Is this the deep foundation of a major clan?"

"Not even the Gold-Swallowing Divine Domain can suppress this mechanism?"

"That mechanical puppet isn't just at the Golden Core-level; it’s an ice sculpture! That's why it's immune to the suppression of the Gold-Swallowing Divine Domain!"

Qiu Lei’s expression darkened drastically.

Even the billowing cloud qi within Wandering Cloud Old Man's sleeves ceased its swirling, as if frozen solid by the mere appearance of Snow-Colored Maiden Hui.

The Cultivators from the major factions of Flowing Cloud Peak all wore exceptionally ugly expressions.

Ning Zhuo took the opportunity to reposition his Forest-Piercing Azure Python Chariot, regrouping his forces.

He unhurriedly swallowed a few more pills, strummed his zither anew, and commanded his flock of mechanical birds to swarm forward and surround his opponent.

Thanks to Snow-Colored Maiden Hui, Ning Zhuo had instantly turned the tables, thoroughly seizing the initiative in the battle.

Snow-Colored Maiden Hui pressed her relentless assault on Liu Jinke.

She wasn't in any rush to deal lethal damage. Instead, she continuously injected her icy-cold energy directly into the outer layers of Liu Jinke’s golden blood.

Despite Liu Jinke repeatedly shattering the encroaching frost with wild swings and thrusts of his Chaotic Chord Sound-Splitting Trident, he remained firmly at a disadvantage, completely subjugated and battered by Snow-Colored Maiden Hui!
