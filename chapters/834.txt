No matter how the Luo Shu page adapted, its overall strength simply couldn't compare to the opposition; it was being put in an overly difficult position.

A tiny crack suddenly appeared on the surface of the water vortex!

This was an energy fault caused by the stagnation of the formation's operation reaching a critical stage.

Even though this was formed from Ning Zhuo's own spiritual energy, it wouldn't last long.

The page trembled slightly, its form suddenly becoming ethereal and blurry before splitting into ten copies.

Nine fake, one real!

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath. Thoughts rose and fell in his mind, his thinking speed pushed to the extreme by intense mental consumption.

He used the formation-breaking treasures in his hands one after another, throwing them into the water formation.

Seven of the page's phantoms were dispersed in succession, but at the final moment, three remained.

The formation-breaking treasures in Ning Zhuo's hands were exhausted. In the time it would take to retrieve them, the page could completely regroup and recreate ten illusions.

At this moment, Sun Lingtong's heart inevitably hung high in his throat!

He knew that if they failed this time, they would have to find another way to plot for the Luo Shu page. Because the series of changes this time would become data for the Luo Shu's calculations.

Therefore, the difficulty of breaking the water formation and seizing the Luo Shu would increase with each attempt.

"Which of the three is real?"

In this instant, Ning Zhuo's gaze was electric. He knew his predicament - he had to choose one immediately. If he chose the real body, he would succeed. If he chose wrong, he would fall short at the last step.

He didn't have any more time, not even a little bit, to think. Fortunately, at the critical moment, his Master-level attainment triggered his spirituality once again, and a wave of intuition welled up from the bottom of his heart.

But this intuition was quite faint, because while breaking the formation, Ning Zhuo had been constantly bathed in the Luo Shu's Dao Rhyme.

Although the intuition arose, there were other sensations, chaotic and confusing, like looking at flowers through a fog.

Crucially, the experience Ning Zhuo gained from sweeping the formation trials played its part, allowing him to grasp that intuitive sense with incomparable precision.

"Alright, it's you!" Ning Zhuo struck decisively.

Spiritual energy surged out, transforming into a hand possessing all Five Elements spiritual energy. It forcefully charged into the water formation, broke through the formation-within-a-formation, and grabbed directly toward the deepest part of the water vortex.

The hand of Five Elements spiritual energy grabbed the page on the left.

The Luo Shu struggled, its essence manifesting; it was indeed the true body!

Ning Zhuo took a deep breath, mobilizing his divine sense to retract the Five Elements palm.

The Luo Shu page struggled instinctively, actually using Dao Rhyme to erode the Five Elements spiritual energy palm.

The Luo Shu tried to influence and penetrate it with its Dao Rhyme, but Ning Zhuo immediately adjusted it gently, forcing it back to its original form!

The moment the Luo Shu page was extracted, the new formation was free from infringement and instantly stabilized, falling completely under Ning Zhuo's control.

But this situation had been anticipated by Sun and Ning.

Ning Zhuo went all out, pouring his spiritual energy and divine sense into the new formation as if they cost nothing.

The new formation operated at high speed, emitting waves of light halos. The halos spread out, covering the swirling water formation wave after wave.

In this moment, the new formation replaced the Luo Shu page, becoming the core of the swirling water formation!

The collapse of the water formation continued, but under the guidance of the new formation, it was no longer violent and ferocious, but greatly delayed.

Eventually, the swirling water formation was consumed to nothingness amidst continuous attrition, without triggering any explosion.

The new formation was also tattered and broken.
